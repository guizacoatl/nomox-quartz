# Article 17 - Exemptions

## 1.
This Regulation shall neither apply to insurance intermediaries which provide *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(21)|insurance advice]]* with regard to *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(3)|IBIPs]]* nor to *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(5)|investment firms]]* which provide *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(16)|investment advice]]* that are enterprises irrespective of their legal form, including natural persons and self‐employed persons, provided that they employ fewer than three persons.
## 2.
Member States may decide to apply this Regulation to insurance intermediaries which provide *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(21)|insurance advice]]* with regard to *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(3)|IBIPs]]* or *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(5)|investment firms]]* which provide *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(16)|investment advice]]* as referred to in paragraph 1.
## 3.
Member States shall notify the Commission and the *[[2019R2088_EN.2#1.|ESAs]]* of any decision taken pursuant to paragraph 2.
