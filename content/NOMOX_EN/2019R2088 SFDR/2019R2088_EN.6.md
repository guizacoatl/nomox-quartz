# Article 6 - Transparency of the integration of sustainability risks

## 1.
*[[2019R2088_EN.2#(1)|Financial market participants]]* shall include descriptions of the following in pre‐contractual disclosures:
### (a)
the manner in which *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(22)|sustainability risks]]* are integrated into their investment decisions; and
### (b)
the results of the assessment of the likely impacts of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(22)|sustainability risks]]* on the returns of the *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial products]]* they make available.

Where *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(1)|financial market participants]]* deem *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(22)|sustainability risks]]* not to be relevant, the descriptions referred to in the first subparagraph shall include a clear and concise explanation of the reasons therefor.
## 2.
*[[2019R2088_EN.2#(11)|Financial advisers]]* shall include descriptions of the following in pre‐contractual disclosures:
### (a)
the manner in which *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(22)|sustainability risks]]* are integrated into their investment or *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(21)|insurance advice]]*; and
### (b)
the result of the assessment of the likely impacts of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(22)|sustainability risks]]* on the returns of the *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial products]]* they advise on.

Where *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(11)|financial advisers]]* deem *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(22)|sustainability risks]]* not to be relevant, the descriptions referred to in the first subparagraph shall include a clear and concise explanation of the reasons therefor.
## 3.
The information referred to in paragraphs 1 and 2 of this Article shall be disclosed in the following manner:
### (a)
for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(4)|AIFMs]]*, in the disclosures to investors referred to in [[2011L0061_EN.23#1|Article 23(1) of Directive 2011/61/EU]];
### (b)
for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(2)|insurance undertakings]]*, in the provision of information referred to in Article 185(2) of Directive 2009/138/EC or, where relevant, in accordance with [[2016L0097_EN.29#1|Article 29(1) of Directive (EU) 97/2016]];
### (c)
for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(7)|IORPs]]*, in the provision of information referred to in [[2016L2341_EN.41|Article 41 of Directive (EU) 2341/2016]];
### (d)
for managers of qualifying venture capital funds, in the provision of information referred to in [[2013R0345_EN.13#1|Article 13(1) of Regulation (EU) No 345/2013]];
### (e)
for managers of qualifying social entrepreneurship funds, in the provision of information referred to in [[2013R0346_EN.14#1|Article 14(1) of Regulation (EU) No 346/2013]];
### (f)
for manufacturers of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(8)|pension products]]*, in writing in good time before a *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(19)|retail investor]]* is bound by a contract relating to a *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(8)|pension product]]*;
### (g)
for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(15)|UCITS]]* management companies, in the prospectus referred to in Article 69 of Directive 2009/65/EC;
### (h)
for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(5)|investment firms]]* which provide *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(6)|portfolio management]]* or provide *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(16)|investment advice]]*, in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.24#4|Article 24(4) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
#### (i)
for credit institutions which provide *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(6)|portfolio management]]* or provide *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(16)|investment advice]]*, in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.24#4|Article 24(4) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
### (j)
for insurance intermediaries and *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(2)|insurance undertakings]]* which provide *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(21)|insurance advice]]* with regard to *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(3)|IBIPs]]* and for insurance intermediaries which provide *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(21)|insurance advice]]* with regard to *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(8)|pension products]]* exposed to market fluctuations, in accordance with [[2016L0097_EN.29#1|Article 29(1) of Directive (EU) 97/2016]];
### (k)
for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(4)|AIFMs]]* of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(23)|ELTIFs]]*, in the prospectus referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.23|Article 23 of Regulation (EU) 760/2015]];
### (l)
for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(9)|PEPP]]* providers, in the *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(9)|PEPP]]* key information document referred to in [[2019R1238_EN.26|Article 26 of Regulation (EU) 1238/2019]].
