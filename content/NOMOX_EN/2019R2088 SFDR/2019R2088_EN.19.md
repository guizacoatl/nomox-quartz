# Article 19 - Evaluation

## 1.
By 30 December 2022, the Commission shall evaluate the application of this Regulation and shall in particular consider:
### (a)
whether the reference to the average number of employees in [[2019R2088_EN.4#3|Article 4(3)]] and (4) should be maintained, replaced or accompanied by other criteria, and shall consider the benefits and proportionality of the related administrative burden;
### (b)
whether the functioning of this Regulation is inhibited by the lack of data or their suboptimal quality, including indicators on adverse impacts on *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(24)|sustainability factors]]* by investee companies.
## 2.
The evaluation referred to in paragraph 1 shall be accompanied, if appropriate, by a legislative proposal.
