# Article 1 - Subject matter


This Regulation lays down harmonised rules for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(1)|financial market participants]]* and *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(11)|financial advisers]]* on transparency with regard to the integration of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(22)|sustainability risks]]* and the consideration of adverse sustainability impacts in their processes and the provision of sustainability‐related information with respect to *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial products]]*.
