# Article 3 - Transparency of sustainability risk policies

## 1.
*[[2019R2088_EN.2#(1)|Financial market participants]]* shall publish on their websites information about their policies on the integration of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(22)|sustainability risks]]* in their investment decision‐making process.
## 2.
*[[2019R2088_EN.2#(11)|Financial advisers]]* shall publish on their websites information about their policies on the integration of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(22)|sustainability risks]]* in their *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(16)|investment advice]]* or *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(21)|insurance advice]]*.
