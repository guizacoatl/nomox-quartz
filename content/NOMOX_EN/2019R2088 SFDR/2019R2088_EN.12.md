# Article 12 - Review of disclosures

## 1.
*[[2019R2088_EN.2#(1)|Financial market participants]]* shall ensure that any information published in accordance with [[2019R2088_EN.3|Article 3]], 5 or 10 is kept up to date. Where a *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(1)|financial market participant]]* amends such information, a clear explanation of such amendment shall be published on the same website.
## 2.
Paragraph 1 shall apply

mutatis mutandis

to *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(11)|financial advisers]]* with regard to any information published in accordance with Articles [[2019R2088_EN.3|3]] and [[2019R2088_EN.5|5]].
