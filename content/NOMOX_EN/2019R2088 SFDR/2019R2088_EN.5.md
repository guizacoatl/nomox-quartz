# Article 5 - Transparency of remuneration policies in relation to the integration of sustainability risks

## 1.
*[[2019R2088_EN.2#(1)|Financial market participants]]* and *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(11)|financial advisers]]* shall include in their remuneration policies information on how those policies are consistent with the integration of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(22)|sustainability risks]]*, and shall publish that information on their websites.
## 2.
The information referred to in paragraph 1 shall be included in remuneration policies that *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(1)|financial market participants]]* and *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(11)|financial advisers]]* are required to establish and maintain in accordance with sectoral legislation, in particular Directives 2009/65/EC, 2009/138/EC, 2011/61/EU, 2013/36/EU, 2014/65/EU, (EU) 2016/97 and (EU) 2016/2341.
