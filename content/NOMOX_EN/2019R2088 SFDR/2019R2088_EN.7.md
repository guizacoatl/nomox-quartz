# Article 7 - Transparency of adverse sustainability impacts at financial product level

## 1.
By 30 December 2022, for each *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial product]]* where a *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(1)|financial market participant]]* applies point (a) of [[2019R2088_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]] or [[2019R2088_EN.4#3|Article 4(3)]] or (4), the disclosures referred to in [[2019R2088_EN.6#3|Article 6(3)]] shall include the following:
### (a)
a clear and reasoned explanation of whether, and, if so, how a *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial product]]* considers principal adverse impacts on *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(24)|sustainability factors]]*;
### (b)
a statement that information on principal adverse impacts on *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(24)|sustainability factors]]* is available in the information to be disclosed pursuant to [[2019R2088_EN.11#2|Article 11(2)]].

Where information in [[2019R2088_EN.11#2|Article 11(2)]] includes quantifications of principal adverse impacts on *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(24)|sustainability factors]]*, that information may rely on the provisions of the regulatory technical standards adopted pursuant to [[2019R2088_EN.4#6|Article 4(6)]] and (7).
## 2.
Where a *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(1)|financial market participant]]* applies point (b) of [[2019R2088_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], the disclosures referred to in [[2019R2088_EN.6#3|Article 6(3)]] shall include for each *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial product]]* a statement that the *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(1)|financial market participant]]* does not consider the adverse impacts of investment decisions on *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(24)|sustainability factors]]* and the reasons therefor.
