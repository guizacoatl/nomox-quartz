# Article 16 - Pension products covered by Regulations (EC) No 883/2004 and (EC) No 987/2009

## 1.
Member States may decide to apply this Regulation to manufacturers of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(8)|pension products]]* operating national social security schemes which are covered by Regulations (EC) No 883/2004 and (EC) No 987/2009. In such cases, manufacturers of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(8)|pension products]]* as referred to in point (1)(d) of [[2019R2088_EN.2|Article 2]] of this Regulation shall include manufacturers of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(8)|pension products]]* operating national social security schemes and of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(8)|pension products]]* referred to in point (8) of [[2019R2088_EN.2|Article 2]] of this Regulation. In such case, the definition of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(8)|pension product]]* in point (8) of [[2019R2088_EN.2|Article 2]] of this Regulation shall be deemed to include the *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(8)|pension products]]* referred to in the first sentence.
## 2.
Member States shall notify the Commission and the *[[2019R2088_EN.2#1.|ESAs]]* of any decision taken pursuant to paragraph 1.
