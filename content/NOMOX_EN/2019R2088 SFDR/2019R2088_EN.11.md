# Article 11 - Transparency of the promotion of environmental or social characteristics and of sustainable investments in periodic reports

## 1.
Where *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(1)|financial market participants]]* make available a *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial product]]* as referred to in [[2019R2088_EN.8#1|Article 8(1)]] or in [[2019R2088_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]], (2) or (3), they shall include a description of the following in periodic reports:
### (a)
for a *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial product]]* as referred to in [[2019R2088_EN.8#1|Article 8(1)]], the extent to which environmental or social characteristics are met;
### (b)
for a *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial product]]* as referred to in [[2019R2088_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]], (2) or (3):
#### (i)
the overall sustainability‐related impact of the *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial product]]* by means of relevant sustainability indicators; or
#### (ii)
where an index has been designated as a reference benchmark, a comparison between the overall sustainability‐related impact of the *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial product]]* with the impacts of the designated index and of a broad market index through sustainability indicators;
### (c)
for a *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial product]]* subject to [[2020R0852_EN.5|Article 5 of Regulation (EU) 852/2020]], the information required under that Article;
### (d)
for a *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial product]]* subject to [[2020R0852_EN.6|Article 6 of Regulation (EU) 852/2020]], the information required under that Article.
## 2.
The information referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article shall be disclosed in the following manner:
### (a)
for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(4)|AIFMs]]*, in the annual report referred to in [[2011L0061_EN.22|Article 22 of Directive 2011/61/EU]];
### (b)
for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(2)|insurance undertakings]]*, annually in writing in accordance with Article 185(6) of Directive 2009/138/EC;
### (c)
for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(7)|IORPs]]*, in the annual report referred to in [[2016L2341_EN.29|Article 29 of Directive (EU) 2341/2016]];
### (d)
for managers of qualifying venture capital funds, in the annual report referred to in [[2013R0345_EN.12|Article 12 of Regulation (EU) No 345/2013]];
### (e)
for managers of qualifying social entrepreneurship funds, in the annual report referred to in [[2013R0346_EN.13|Article 13 of Regulation (EU) No 346/2013]];
### (f)
for manufacturers of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(8)|pension products]]*, in writing in the annual report or in a report in accordance with national law;
### (g)
for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(15)|UCITS]]* management companies, in the annual report referred to in Article 69 of Directive 2009/65/EC;
### (h)
for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(5)|investment firms]]* which provide *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(6)|portfolio management]]*, in a periodic report as referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.25#6|Article 25(6) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
#### (i)
for credit institutions which provide *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(6)|portfolio management]]*, in a periodic report as referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.25#6|Article 25(6) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
### (j)
for *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(9)|PEPP]]* providers, in the *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(9)|PEPP]]* Benefit Statement referred to in [[2019R1238_EN.36|Article 36 of Regulation (EU) 1238/2019]].
## 3.
For the purposes of paragraph 1 of this Article, *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(1)|financial market participants]]* may use the information in management reports in accordance with [[2013L0034_EN.19|Article 19 of Directive 2013/34/EU]] or the information in non‐financial statements in accordance with Article 19a of that Directive where appropriate.
## 4.
The *[[2019R2088_EN.2#1.|ESAs]]* shall, through the Joint Committee, develop draft regulatory technical standards to specify the details of the content and presentation of the information referred to in points (a) and (b) of paragraph 1.

When developing the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph, the *[[2019R2088_EN.2#1.|ESAs]]* shall take into account the various types of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial products]]*, their characteristics and objectives and the differences between them. The *[[2019R2088_EN.2#1.|ESAs]]* shall update the regulatory technical standards in the light of regulatory and technological developments.

The *[[2019R2088_EN.2#1.|ESAs]]* shall submit the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph to the Commission by 30 December 2020.

Power is delegated to the Commission to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2019R2088_EN.10|10]] to 14 of Regulations (EU) No 1093/2010, (EU) No 1094/2010 and (EU) No 1095/2010.
## 5.
The *[[2019R2088_EN.2#1.|ESAs]]* shall, through the Joint Committee, develop draft regulatory technical standards to specify the details of the content and presentation of the information referred to in points (c) and (d) of paragraph 1.

When developing the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph, the *[[2019R2088_EN.2#1.|ESAs]]* shall take into account the various types of *[[2019R2088_EN.2#(12)|financial products]]*, their characteristics and objectives and the differences between them and, where necessary, shall develop draft amendments to the regulatory technical standards referred to in paragraph 4 of this Article. The draft regulatory technical standards shall take into account the respective dates of application set out in points (a) and (b) of [[2020R0852_EN.27#2|Article 27(2) of Regulation (EU) 852/2020]] in respect of the environmental objectives set out in Article 9 of that Regulation. The *[[2019R2088_EN.2#1.|ESAs]]* shall update the regulatory technical standards in the light of regulatory and technological developments.

The *[[2019R2088_EN.2#1.|ESAs]]* shall submit the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph to the Commission:
### (a)
in respect of the environmental objectives referred to in points (a) and (b) of [[2020R0852_EN.9|Article 9 of Regulation (EU) 852/2020]], by 1 June 2021; and
### (b)
in respect of the environmental objectives referred to in points (c) to (f) of [[2020R0852_EN.9|Article 9 of Regulation (EU) 852/2020]], by 1 June 2022.

Power is delegated to the Commission to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph in accordance with Articles [[2019R2088_EN.10|10]] to 14 of Regulations (EU) No 1093/2010, (EU) No 1094/2010 and (EU) No 1095/2010.
