# Article 15 - Transparency by IORPs and insurance intermediaries

## 1.
*[[2019R2088_EN.2#(7)|IORPs]]* shall publish and maintain the information referred to in Articles [[2019R2088_EN.3|3]] to 7 and the first subparagraph of [[2019R2088_EN.10#1|Article 10(1)]], of this Regulation in accordance with point (f) of [[2016L2341_EN.36#2|Article 36(2) of Directive (EU) 2341/2016]].
## 2.
Insurance intermediaries shall communicate the information referred to in [[2019R2088_EN.3|Article 3]], [[2019R2088_EN.4#5|Article 4(5)]], [[2019R2088_EN.5|Article 5]], [[2019R2088_EN.6|Article 6]] and the first subparagraph of [[2019R2088_EN.10#1|Article 10(1)]], of this Regulation in accordance with [[2016L0097_EN.23|Article 23 of Directive (EU) 97/2016]].
