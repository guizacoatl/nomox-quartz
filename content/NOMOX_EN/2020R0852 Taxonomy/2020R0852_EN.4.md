# Article 4 - Use of the criteria for environmentally sustainable economic activities in public measures, in standards and in labels


Member States and the Union shall apply the criteria set out in [[2020R0852_EN.3|Article 3]] to determine whether an economic activity qualifies as environmentally sustainable for the purposes of any measure setting out requirements for *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(2)|financial market participants]]* or *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(4)|issuers]]* in respect of *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial products]]* or corporate bonds that are made available as environmentally sustainable.
