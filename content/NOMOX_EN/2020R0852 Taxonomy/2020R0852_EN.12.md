# Article 12 - Substantial contribution to the sustainable use and protection of water and marine resources

## 1.
An economic activity shall qualify as contributing substantially to the sustainable use and protection of water and marine resources where that activity either contributes substantially to achieving the *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(22)|good status]]* of bodies of water, including bodies of *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(19)|surface water]]* and *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(20)|groundwater]]* or to preventing the deterioration of bodies of water that already have *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(22)|good status]]*, or contributes substantially to achieving the *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(21)|good environmental status]]* of *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(18)|marine waters]]* or to preventing the deterioration of *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(18)|marine waters]]* that are already in *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(21)|good environmental status]]*, by:
### (a)
protecting the environment from the adverse effects of urban and industrial waste water discharges, including from contaminants of emerging concern such as pharmaceuticals and microplastics, for example by ensuring the adequate collection, treatment and discharge of urban and industrial waste waters;
### (b)
protecting human health from the adverse impact of any contamination of water intended for human consumption by ensuring that it is free from any micro-organisms, parasites and substances that constitute a potential danger to human health as well as increasing people’s access to clean drinking water;
### (c)
improving water management and efficiency, including by protecting and enhancing the status of aquatic *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(13)|ecosystems]]*, by promoting the sustainable use of water through the long-term protection of available water resources, inter alia, through measures such as water reuse, by ensuring the progressive reduction of *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(10)|pollutant]]* emissions into *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(19)|surface water]]* and *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(20)|groundwater]]*, by contributing to mitigating the effects of floods and droughts, or through any other activity that protects or improves the qualitative and quantitative status of water bodies;
### (d)
ensuring the sustainable use of marine *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(14)|ecosystem services]]* or contributing to the *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(21)|good environmental status]]* of *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(18)|marine waters]]*, including by protecting, preserving or restoring the marine environment and by preventing or reducing inputs in the marine environment; or
### (e)
enabling any of the activities listed in points (a) to (d) of this paragraph in accordance with [[2020R0852_EN.16|Article 16]].
## 2.
The Commission shall adopt a delegated act in accordance with [[2020R0852_EN.23|Article 23]] to:
### (a)
supplement paragraph 1 of this Article by establishing technical screening criteria for determining the conditions under which a specific economic activity qualifies as contributing substantially to sustainable use and protection of water and marine resources; and
### (b)
supplement [[2020R0852_EN.17|Article 17]] by establishing, for each relevant environmental objective, technical screening criteria, for determining whether an economic activity in respect of which technical screening criteria have been established pursuant to point (a) of this paragraph causes significant harm to one or more of those objectives.
## 3.
Prior to adopting the delegated act referred to in paragraph 2 of this Article, the Commission shall consult the Platform referred to in [[2020R0852_EN.20|Article 20]] regarding the technical screening criteria referred to in paragraph 2 of this Article.
## 4.
The Commission shall establish the technical screening criteria referred to in paragraph 2 of this Article in one delegated act, taking into account the requirements of [[2020R0852_EN.19|Article 19]].
## 5.
The Commission shall adopt the delegated act referred to in paragraph 2 by 31 December 2021, with a view to ensuring its application from 1 January 2023.
