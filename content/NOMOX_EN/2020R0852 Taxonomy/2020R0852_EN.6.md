# Article 6 - Transparency of financial products that promote environmental characteristics in pre-contractual disclosures and in periodic reports


Where a *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial product]]* as referred to in [[2019R2088_EN.8#1|Article 8(1) of Regulation (EU) 2088/2019]] promotes environmental characteristics, [[2020R0852_EN.5|Article 5]] of this Regulation shall apply

mutatis mutandis

.

The information to be disclosed in accordance with Articles [[2020R0852_EN.6|6]](3) and 11(2) of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 shall be accompanied by the following statement:

‘The “do no significant harm” principle applies only to those investments underlying the *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial product]]* that take into account the EU criteria for environmentally sustainable economic activities.

The investments underlying the remaining portion of this *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial product]]* do not take into account the EU criteria for environmentally sustainable economic activities.’.
