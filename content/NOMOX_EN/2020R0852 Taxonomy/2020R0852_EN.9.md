# Article 9 - Environmental objectives


For the purposes of this Regulation, the following shall be environmental objectives:
### (a)
*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(5)|climate change mitigation]]*;
### (b)
*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(6)|climate change adaptation]]*;
### (c)
the sustainable use and protection of water and marine resources;
### (d)
the transition to a *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(9)|circular economy]]*;
### (e)
*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(12)|pollution]]* prevention and control;
### (f)
the protection and restoration of *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(15)|biodiversity]]* and *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(13)|ecosystems]]*.
