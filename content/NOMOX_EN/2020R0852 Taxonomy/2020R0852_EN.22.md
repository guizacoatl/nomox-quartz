# Article 22 - Measures and penalties


Member States shall lay down the rules on measures and penalties applicable to infringements of Articles [[2020R0852_EN.5|5]], [[2020R0852_EN.6|6]] and [[2020R0852_EN.7|7]]. The measures and penalties provided for shall be effective, proportionate and dissuasive.
