# Article 5 - Transparency of environmentally sustainable investments in pre-contractual disclosures and in periodic reports


Where a *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial product]]* as referred to in [[2020R0852_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]], (2) or (3) of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 invests in an economic activity that contributes to an environmental objective within the meaning of point (17) of Article 2 of that Regulation, the information to be disclosed in accordance with Articles [[2020R0852_EN.6|6]](3) and 11(2) of that Regulation shall include the following:
### (a)
the information on the environmental objective or environmental objectives set out in [[2020R0852_EN.9|Article 9]] of this Regulation to which the investment underlying the *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial product]]* contributes; and
### (b)
a description of how and to what extent the investments underlying the *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial product]]* are in economic activities that qualify as environmentally sustainable under [[2020R0852_EN.3|Article 3]] of this Regulation.

The description referred to in point (b) of the first subparagraph of this Article shall specify the proportion of investments in environmentally sustainable economic activities selected for the *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial product]]*, including details on the proportions of enabling and transitional activities referred to in Article 16 and [[2020R0852_EN.10#2|Article 10(2)]], respectively, as a percentage of all investments selected for the *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial product]]*.
