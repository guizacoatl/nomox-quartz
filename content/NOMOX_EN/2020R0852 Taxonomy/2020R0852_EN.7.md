# Article 7 - Transparency of other financial products in pre-contractual disclosures and in periodic reports


Where a *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial product]]* is not subject to [[2020R0852_EN.8#1|Article 8(1)]] or to [[2020R0852_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]], (2) or (3) of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088, the information to be disclosed in accordance with the provisions of sectoral legislation referred to in Articles [[2020R0852_EN.6|6]](3) and 11(2) of that Regulation shall be accompanied by the following statement:

‘The investments underlying this *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial product]]* do not take into account the EU criteria for environmentally sustainable economic activities.’.
