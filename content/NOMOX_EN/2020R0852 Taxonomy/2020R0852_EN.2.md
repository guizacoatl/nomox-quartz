# Article 2 - Definitions


For the purposes of this Regulation, the following definitions apply:
## (1)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(1)|environmentally sustainable investment]]*’ means an investment in one or several economic activities that qualify as environmentally sustainable under this Regulation;
## (2)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(2)|financial market participant]]*’ means a *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(2)|financial market participant]]* as defined in point (1) of [[2019R2088_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) 2088/2019]] and includes a manufacturer of a pension product to which a Member State has decided to apply that Regulation in accordance with Article 16 of that Regulation;
## (3)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial product]]*’ means a *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial product]]* as defined in point (12) of [[2019R2088_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) 2088/2019]];
## (4)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(4)|issuer]]*’ means an *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(4)|issuer]]* as defined in point (h) of [[2017R1129_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) 1129/2017]] of the European Parliament and of the Council


;
## (5)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(5)|climate change mitigation]]*’ means the process of holding the increase in the global average temperature to well below 2 °C and pursuing efforts to limit it to 1,5 °C above pre-industrial levels, as laid down in the Paris Agreement;
## (6)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(6)|climate change adaptation]]*’ means the process of adjustment to actual and expected climate change and its impacts;
## (7)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(7)|greenhouse gas]]*’ means a *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(7)|greenhouse gas]]* listed in Annex I to Regulation (EU) No 525/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council


;
## (8)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(8)|waste hierarchy]]*’ means the *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(8)|waste hierarchy]]* as laid down in Article 4 of Directive 2008/98/EC;
## (9)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(9)|circular economy]]*’ means an economic system whereby the value of products, materials and other resources in the economy is maintained for as long as possible, enhancing their efficient use in production and consumption, thereby reducing the environmental impact of their use, minimising waste and the release of hazardous substances at all stages of their life cycle, including through the application of the *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(8)|waste hierarchy]]*;
## (10)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(10)|pollutant]]*’ means a substance, vibration, heat, noise, light or other contaminant present in air, water or land which may be harmful to human health or the environment, which may result in damage to material property, or which may impair or interfere with amenities and other legitimate uses of the environment;
## (11)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(11)|soil]]*’ means the top layer of the Earth’s crust situated between the bedrock and the surface, which is composed of mineral particles, organic matter, water, air and living organisms;
## (12)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(12)|pollution]]*’ means:
### (a)
the direct or indirect introduction of *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(10)|pollutants]]* into air, water or land as a result of human activity;
### (b)
in the context of the marine environment, *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(12)|pollution]]* as defined in point 8 of Article 3 of Directive 2008/56/EC;
### (c)
in the context of the water environment, *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(12)|pollution]]* as defined in point 33 of Article 2 of Directive 2000/60/EC;
## (13)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(13)|ecosystem]]*’ means a dynamic complex of plant, animal, and micro-organism communities and their non-living environment interacting as a functional unit;
## (14)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(14)|ecosystem services]]*’ means the direct and indirect contributions of *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(13)|ecosystems]]* to the economic, social, cultural and other benefits that people derive from those *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(13)|ecosystems]]*;
## (15)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(15)|biodiversity]]*’ means the variability among living organisms arising from all sources including terrestrial, marine and other aquatic *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(13)|ecosystems]]* and the ecological complexes of which they are part and includes diversity within species, between species and of *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(13)|ecosystems]]*;
## (16)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(16)|good condition]]*’ means, in relation to an *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(13)|ecosystem]]*, that the *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(13)|ecosystem]]* is in good physical, chemical and biological condition or of a good physical, chemical and biological quality with self-reproduction or self-restoration capability, in which species composition, *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(13)|ecosystem]]* structure and ecological functions are not impaired;
## (17)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(17)|energy efficiency]]*’ means the more efficient use of energy at all the stages of the energy chain from production to final consumption;
## (18)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(18)|marine waters]]*’ means *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(18)|marine waters]]* as defined in point 1 of Article 3 of Directive 2008/56/EC;
## (19)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(19)|surface water]]*’ means *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(19)|surface water]]* as defined in point 1 of Article 2 of Directive 2000/60/EC;
## (20)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(20)|groundwater]]*’ means *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(20)|groundwater]]* as defined in point 2 of Article 2 of Directive 2000/60/EC;
## (21)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(21)|good environmental status]]*’ means *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(21)|good environmental status]]* as defined in point 5 of Article 3 of Directive 2008/56/EC;
## (22)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(22)|good status]]*’ means:
### (a)
for *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(19)|surface water]]*, having both ‘good ecological status’ as defined in point 22 of Article 2 of Directive 2000/60/EC and ‘good *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(19)|surface water]]* chemical status’ as defined in point 24 of Article 2 of that Directive;
### (b)
for *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(20)|groundwater]]*, having both ‘good *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(20)|groundwater]]* chemical status’ as defined in point 25 of Article 2 of Directive 2000/60/EC and ‘good quantitative status’ as defined in point 28 of Article 2 of that Directive;
## (23)
‘*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(23)|good ecological potential]]*’ means *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(23)|good ecological potential]]* as defined in point 23 of Article 2 of Directive 2000/60/EC.
