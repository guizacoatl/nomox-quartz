# Article 16 - Enabling activities


An economic activity shall qualify as contributing substantially to one or more of the environmental objectives set out in [[2020R0852_EN.9|Article 9]] by directly enabling other activities to make a substantial contribution to one or more of those objectives, provided that such economic activity:
### (a)
does not lead to a lock-in of assets that undermine long-term environmental goals, considering the economic lifetime of those assets; and
### (b)
has a substantial positive environmental impact, on the basis of life-cycle considerations.
