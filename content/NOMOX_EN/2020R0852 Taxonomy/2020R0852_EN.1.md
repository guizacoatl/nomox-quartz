# Article 1 - Subject matter and scope

## 1.
This Regulation establishes the criteria for determining whether an economic activity qualifies as environmentally sustainable for the purposes of establishing the degree to which an investment is environmentally sustainable.
## 2.
This Regulation applies to:
### (a)
measures adopted by Member States or by the Union that set out requirements for *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(2)|financial market participants]]* or *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(4)|issuers]]* in respect of *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial products]]* or corporate bonds that are made available as environmentally sustainable;
### (b)
*[[2020R0852_EN.2#(2)|financial market participants]]* that make available *[[2020R0852_EN.2#(3)|financial products]]*;
### (c)
undertakings which are subject to the obligation to publish a non-financial statement or a consolidated non-financial statement pursuant to [[2020R0852_EN.19a|Article 19a]] or [[2013L0034_EN.29a|Article 29a of Directive 2013/34/EU]] of the European Parliament and of the Council


, respectively.
