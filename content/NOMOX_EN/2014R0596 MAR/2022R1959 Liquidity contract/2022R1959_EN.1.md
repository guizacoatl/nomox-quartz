> Level 1 reference(s): [[2014R0596_EN.13#12]]# Article 1 - Template for a liquidity contract


For the purposes of entering into a liquidity contract as referred to in [[2014R0596_EN.13#12|Article 13(12) of Regulation (EU) No 596/2014]], issuers of financial instruments admitted to trading on one or more SME growth markets shall use the template set out in the Annex to this Regulation.
