# Article 1 - Cooperation arrangements


The template document to be used by competent authorities of Member States where possible for cooperation arrangements pursuant to [[2021R1783_EN.26#1|Article 26(1)]] or the third subparagraph of [[2014R0596_EN.25#8|Article 25(8) of Regulation (EU) No 596/2014]] is set out in the Annex to this Regulation.
