# Article 38 - Reports


Reports

By 5 December 2028, the Commission shall submit a report to the European Parliament and to the Council on the application of this Regulation, together with a legislative proposal to amend it if appropriate. That report shall assess, inter alia:
### (a)
the appropriateness of introducing common rules on the need for all Member States to provide for administrative sanctions for insider dealing and market manipulation;
### (b)
whether the definition of inside information is sufficient to cover all information relevant for competent authorities to effectively combat market abuse;
### (c)
whether the provision on non-disclosure of inside information relating to intermediate steps in a protracted process in [[2014R0596_EN.17#1|Article 17(1)]] strikes an adequate balance between reducing the burden for *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuers]]* and allowing investors to take informed investment decisions; and
### (d)
the proportionality of the absolute amounts, as expressed in [[2014R0596_EN.30#2|Article 30(2)]], points (j)(iii) and (iv), and their appropriateness in relation to micro, *[[2014R0596_EN.3#4.|small and medium-sized enterprises]]*.

—————

For the purposes of point (a) of the first subparagraph, ESMA shall undertake a mapping exercise of the application of administrative sanctions and, where Member States have decided, pursuant to the second subparagraph of [[2014R0596_EN.30#1|Article 30(1)]], to lay down criminal sanctions as referred to therein for infringements of this Regulation, of the application of such criminal sanctions within Member States. That exercise shall also include any data made available under [[2014R0596_EN.33#1|Article 33(1)]] and (2).

By 5 December 2031, the Commission shall submit a report to the European Parliament and to the Council on the functioning of the cross-market order data surveillance mechanism, its impact on the ability of national competent authorities to ensure effective supervision, how to enforce such mechanism, and the merits of the potential inclusion of *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(36)|systematic internalisers]]* in the scope of the mechanism.

By 5 December 2028, the Commission shall, after consulting ESMA, submit a report to the European Parliament and to the Council on the level of the thresholds set out in [[2014R0596_EN.19|Article 19]](1a), points (a) and (b), in relation to managers’ transactions where the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuer]]*’s shares or debt instruments form part of a collective investment undertaking or provide exposure to a portfolio of assets, with a view to assessing whether that level is appropriate or should be adjusted.

The Commission shall be empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2014R0596_EN.35|Article 35]] adjusting the thresholds in [[2014R0596_EN.19|Article 19]](1a)(a) and (b), if it determines in that report that those thresholds should be adjusted.
