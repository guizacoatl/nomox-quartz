> Level 1 reference(s): [[2014R0596_EN.13#2#c]]# Article 5 - Impact on market liquidity and efficiency


In determining whether a market practice proposed to be established as an AMP complies with the criterion set out in point (c) of [[2014R0596_EN.13#2|Article 13(2) of Regulation (EU) No 596/2014]], competent authorities shall assess the impact the market practice has on at least the following elements:
### (a)
volume traded;
### (b)
number of orders in the order book (order depth);
### (c)
speed of execution of the transactions;
### (d)
volume weighted average price of a single session, daily closing price;
### (e)
bid/offer spread, price fluctuation and volatility;
### (f)
regularity of quotations or transactions.
