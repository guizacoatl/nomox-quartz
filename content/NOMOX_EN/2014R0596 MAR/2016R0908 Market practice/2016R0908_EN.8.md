# Article 8 - Investigation of the market practice


In determining whether a market practice proposed to be established as an AMP complies with the criterion set out in point (f) of [[2014R0596_EN.13#2|Article 13(2) of Regulation (EU) No 596/2014]], competent authorities shall in particular take into account the outcome of any investigation in the markets they monitor that might question the AMP to be established.
