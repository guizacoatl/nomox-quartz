# Article 10 - Unlawful disclosure of inside information

## 1.
For the purposes of this Regulation, unlawful disclosure of inside information arises where a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* possesses inside information and discloses that information to any other *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]*, except where the disclosure is made in the normal exercise of an employment, a profession or duties.

This paragraph applies to any natural or legal *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* in the situations or circumstances referred to in [[2014R0596_EN.8#4|Article 8(4)]].
## 2.
For the purposes of this Regulation the onward disclosure of recommendations or inducements referred to in [[2014R0596_EN.8#2|Article 8(2)]] amounts to unlawful disclosure of inside information under this Article where the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* disclosing the recommendation or inducement knows or ought to know that it was based on inside information.
