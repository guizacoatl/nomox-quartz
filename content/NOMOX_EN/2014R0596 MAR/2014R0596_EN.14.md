# Article 14 - Prohibition of insider dealing and of unlawful disclosure of inside information


A *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* shall not:
### (a)
engage or attempt to engage in insider dealing;
### (b)
recommend that another *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* engage in insider dealing or induce another *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* to engage in insider dealing; or
### (c)
unlawfully disclose inside information.
