# Article 9 - Legitimate behaviour

## 1.
For the purposes of Articles [[2014R0596_EN.8|8]] and [[2014R0596_EN.14|14]], it shall not be deemed from the mere fact that a legal *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* is or has been in possession of inside information that that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* has used that information and has thus engaged in insider dealing on the basis of an acquisition or disposal, where that legal *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]*:
### (a)
has established, implemented and maintained adequate and effective internal arrangements and procedures that effectively ensure that neither the natural *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* who made the decision on its behalf to acquire or dispose of *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* to which the information relates, nor another natural *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* who may have had an influence on that decision, was in possession of the inside information; and
### (b)
has not encouraged, made a recommendation to, induced or otherwise influenced the natural *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* who, on behalf of the legal *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]*, acquired or disposed of *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* to which the information relates.
## 2.
For the purposes of Articles [[2014R0596_EN.8|8]] and [[2014R0596_EN.14|14]], it shall not be deemed from the mere fact that a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* is in possession of inside information that that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* has used that information and has thus engaged in insider dealing on the basis of an acquisition or disposal where that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]*:
### (a)
for the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instrument]]* to which that information relates, is a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(30)|market maker]]* or a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* authorised to act as a counterparty, and the acquisition or disposal of *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* to which that information relates is made legitimately in the normal course of the exercise of its function as a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(30)|market maker]]* or as a counterparty for that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instrument]]*; or
### (b)
is authorised to execute orders on behalf of third parties, and the acquisition or disposal of *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* to which the order relates, is made to carry out such an order legitimately in the normal course of the exercise of that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]*’s employment, profession or duties.
## 3.
For the purposes of Articles [[2014R0596_EN.8|8]] and [[2014R0596_EN.14|14]], it shall not be deemed from the mere fact that a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* is in possession of inside information that that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* has used that information and has thus engaged in insider dealing on the basis of an acquisition or disposal where that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* conducts a transaction to acquire or dispose of *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* and that transaction is carried out in the discharge of an obligation that has become due in good faith and not to circumvent the prohibition against insider dealing and:
### (a)
that obligation results from an order placed or an agreement concluded before the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* concerned possessed inside information; or
### (b)
that transaction is carried out to satisfy a legal or regulatory obligation that arose, before the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* concerned possessed inside information.
## 4.
For the purposes of [[2014R0596_EN.8|Article 8]] and 14, it shall not be deemed from the mere fact that a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* is in possession of inside information that that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* has used that information and has thus engaged in insider dealing, where such *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* has obtained that inside information in the conduct of a public takeover or merger with a company and uses that inside information solely for the purpose of proceeding with that merger or public takeover, provided that at the point of approval of the merger or acceptance of the offer by the shareholders of that company, any inside information has been made public or has otherwise ceased to constitute inside information.

This paragraph shall not apply to *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(31)|stake-building]]*.
## 5.
For the purposes of Articles [[2014R0596_EN.8|8]] and [[2014R0596_EN.14|14]], the mere fact that a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* uses its own knowledge that it has decided to acquire or dispose of *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* in the acquisition or disposal of those *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* shall not of itself constitute use of inside information.
## 6.
Notwithstanding paragraphs 1 to 5 of this Article, an infringement of the prohibition of insider dealing set out in [[2014R0596_EN.14|Article 14]] may still be deemed to have occurred if the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(12)|competent authority]]* establishes that there was an illegitimate reason for the orders to trade, transactions or behaviours concerned.
