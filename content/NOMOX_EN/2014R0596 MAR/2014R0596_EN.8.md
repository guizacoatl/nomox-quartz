> Level 2 reference(s): [[2016R0957_EN.5]]# Article 8 - Insider dealing

## 1.
For the purposes of this Regulation, insider dealing arises where a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* possesses inside information and uses that information by acquiring or disposing of, for its own account or for the account of a third party, directly or indirectly, *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* to which that information relates. The use of inside information by cancelling or amending an order concerning a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instrument]]* to which the information relates where the order was placed before the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* concerned possessed the inside information, shall also be considered to be insider dealing. In relation to auctions of *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(19)|emission allowances]]* or other auctioned products based thereon that are held pursuant to Regulation (EU) No 1031/2010, the use of inside information shall also comprise submitting, modifying or withdrawing a bid by a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* for its own account or for the account of a third party.
## 2.
For the purposes of this Regulation, recommending that another *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* engage in insider dealing, or inducing another *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* to engage in insider dealing, arises where the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* possesses inside information and:
### (a)
recommends, on the basis of that information, that another *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* acquire or dispose of *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* to which that information relates, or induces that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* to make such an acquisition or disposal, or
### (b)
recommends, on the basis of that information, that another *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* cancel or amend an order concerning a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instrument]]* to which that information relates, or induces that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* to make such a cancellation or amendment.
## 3.
The use of the recommendations or inducements referred to in paragraph 2 amounts to insider dealing within the meaning of this Article where the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* using the recommendation or inducement knows or ought to know that it is based upon inside information.
## 4.
This Article applies to any *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* who possesses inside information as a result of:
### (a)
being a member of the administrative, management or supervisory bodies of the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuer]]* or *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(20)|emission allowance market participant]]*;
### (b)
having a holding in the capital of the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuer]]* or *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(20)|emission allowance market participant]]*;
### (c)
having access to the information through the exercise of an employment, profession or duties; or
### (d)
being involved in criminal activities.

This Article also applies to any *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* who possesses inside information under circumstances other than those referred to in the first subparagraph where that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* knows or ought to know that it is inside information.
## 5.
Where the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* is a legal *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]*, this Article shall also apply, in accordance with national law, to the natural *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|persons]]* who participate in the decision to carry out the acquisition, disposal, cancellation or amendment of an order for the account of the legal *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* concerned.
