> Level 2 reference(s): [[2022R1210_EN.1]], [[2022R1210_EN.2]]# Article 18 - Insider lists

## 1.
*[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|Issuers]]* and any *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* acting on their behalf or on their account, shall each:
### (a)
draw up a list of all *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|persons]]* who have access to inside information and who are working for them under a contract of employment, or otherwise performing tasks through which they have access to inside information, such as advisers, accountants or credit rating agencies (insider list);
### (b)
promptly update the insider list in accordance with paragraph 4; and
### (c)
provide the insider list to the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(12)|competent authority]]* as soon as possible upon its request.
## 2.
*[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|Issuers]]* and any *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* acting on their behalf or on their account, shall take all reasonable steps to ensure that any *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* on the insider list acknowledges in writing the legal and regulatory duties entailed and is aware of the sanctions applicable to insider dealing and unlawful disclosure of inside information.

Where another *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* is requested by the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuer]]* to draw up and update the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuer]]*’s insider list, the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuer]]* shall remain fully responsible for complying with this Article. The *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuer]]* shall always retain a right of access to the insider list that the other *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* is drawing up.
## 3.
The insider list shall include at least:
### (a)
the identity of any *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* having access to inside information;
### (b)
the reason for including that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* in the insider list;
### (c)
the date and time at which that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* obtained access to inside information; and
### (d)
the date on which the insider list was drawn up.
## 4.
*[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|Issuers]]* and any *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* acting on their behalf or on their account shall each update their insider list promptly, including the date of the update, in the following circumstances:
### (a)
where there is a change in the reason for including a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* already on the insider list;
### (b)
where there is a new *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* who has access to inside information and needs, therefore, to be added to the insider list; and
### (c)
where a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* ceases to have access to inside information.

Each update shall specify the date and time when the change triggering the update occurred.
## 5.
*[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|Issuers]]* and any *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|person]]* acting on their behalf or on their account shall each retain their insider list for a period of at least five years after it is drawn up or updated.
## 6.
*[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|Issuers]]* whose *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* are admitted to trading on an *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(11)|SME growth market]]* shall be entitled to include in their insider lists only those *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|persons]]* who, due to the nature of their function or position within the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuer]]*, have regular access to inside information.

By way of derogation from the first subparagraph of this paragraph, and where justified by specific national market integrity concerns, Member States may require *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuers]]* whose *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* are admitted to trading on an *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(11)|SME growth market]]* to include in their insider lists all *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|persons]]* referred to in paragraph 1, point (a).

The insider lists referred to in the first and second subparagraphs of this paragraph shall be provided to the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(12)|competent authority]]* as soon as possible upon its request.

## 7.
This Article shall apply to *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuers]]* who have requested or approved admission of their *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* to trading on a *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(6)|regulated market]]* in a Member State or, in the case of an instrument only traded on an *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(7)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(8)|OTF]]*, have approved trading of their *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* on an *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(7)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(8)|OTF]]* or have requested admission to trading of their *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* on an *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(7)|MTF]]* in a Member State.
## 8.
Paragraphs 1 to 5 of this Article shall also apply to:
### (a)
*[[2014R0596_EN.3#(20)|emission allowance market participants]]* in relation to inside information concerning *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(19)|emission allowances]]* that arises in relation to the physical operations of that *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(20)|emission allowance market participant]]*;
### (b)
any auction platform, auctioneer and auction monitor in relation to auctions of *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(19)|emission allowances]]* or other auctioned products based thereon that are held pursuant to Regulation (EU) No 1031/2010.
## 9.
ESMA shall review the implementing technical standards on the alleviated format of the insider lists for *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuers]]* admitted to trading on *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(11)|SME growth markets]]* to extend the use of such a format to all insider lists referred to in paragraph 1 and in paragraph 6, first and second subparagraphs.

ESMA shall submit those draft implementing technical standards to the Commission by 5 September 2025.

Power is conferred on the Commission to adopt the implementing technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
