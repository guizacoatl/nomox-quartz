# Article 7 - Inside information

## 1.
For the purposes of this Regulation, inside information shall comprise the following types of information:
### (a)
information of a precise nature, which has not been made public, relating, directly or indirectly, to one or more *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuers]]* or to one or more *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]*, and which, if it were made public, would be likely to have a significant effect on the prices of those *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* or on the price of related derivative *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]*;
### (b)
in relation to *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(24)|commodity derivatives]]*, information of a precise nature, which has not been made public, relating, directly or indirectly to one or more such derivatives or relating directly to the related *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(15)|spot commodity contract]]*, and which, if it were made public, would be likely to have a significant effect on the prices of such derivatives or related *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(15)|spot commodity contracts]]*, and where this is information which is reasonably expected to be disclosed or is required to be disclosed in accordance with legal or regulatory provisions at the Union or national level, market rules, contract, practice or custom, on the relevant *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(24)|commodity derivatives]]* markets or *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(16)|spot markets]]*;
### (c)
in relation to *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(19)|emission allowances]]* or auctioned products based thereon, information of a precise nature, which has not been made public, relating, directly or indirectly, to one or more such instruments, and which, if it were made public, would be likely to have a significant effect on the prices of such instruments or on the prices of related derivative *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]*;
### (d)
information conveyed by a client or by other *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(13)|persons]]* acting on the client’s behalf or information known by virtue of management of a proprietary account or of a managed fund and relating to pending orders in *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]*, which is of a precise nature, relating, directly or indirectly, to one or more *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(21)|issuers]]* or to one or more *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]*, and which, if it were made public, would be likely to have a significant effect on the prices of those *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]*, the price of related *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(15)|spot commodity contracts]]*, or on the price of related derivative *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]*.
## 2.
For the purposes of paragraph 1, information shall be deemed to be of a precise nature if it indicates a set of circumstances which exists or which may reasonably be expected to come into existence, or an event which has occurred or which may reasonably be expected to occur, where it is specific enough to enable a conclusion to be drawn as to the possible effect of that set of circumstances or event on the prices of the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]* or the related derivative *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instrument]]*, the related *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(15)|spot commodity contracts]]*, or the auctioned products based on the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(19)|emission allowances]]*. In this respect in the case of a protracted process that is intended to bring about, or that results in, particular circumstances or a particular event, those future circumstances or that future event, and also the intermediate steps of that process which are connected with bringing about or resulting in those future circumstances or that future event, may be deemed to be precise information.
## 3.
An intermediate step in a protracted process shall be deemed to be inside information if, by itself, it satisfies the criteria of inside information as referred to in this Article.
## 4.
For the purposes of paragraph 1, information which, if it were made public, would be likely to have a significant effect on the prices of *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]*, derivative *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]*, related *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(15)|spot commodity contracts]]*, or auctioned products based on *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(19)|emission allowances]]* shall mean information a reasonable investor would be likely to use as part of the basis of his or her investment decisions.

In the case of participants in the *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(19)|emission allowance]]* market with aggregate emissions or rated thermal input at or below the threshold set in accordance with the second subparagraph of [[2014R0596_EN.17#2|Article 17(2)]], information about their physical operations shall be deemed not to have a significant effect on the price of *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(19)|emission allowances]]*, of auctioned products based thereon, or of derivative *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(1)|financial instruments]]*.
## 5.
ESMA shall issue guidelines to establish a non-exhaustive indicative list of information which is reasonably expected or is required to be disclosed in accordance with legal or regulatory provisions in Union or national law, market rules, contract, practice or custom, on the relevant *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(24)|commodity derivatives]]* markets or *[[2014R0596_EN.3#(16)|spot markets]]* as referred to in point (b) of paragraph 1. ESMA shall duly take into account specificities of those markets.
