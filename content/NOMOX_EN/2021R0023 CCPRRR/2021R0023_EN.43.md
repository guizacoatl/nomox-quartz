# Article 43 - Bridge CCP: procedural requirements

## 1.
The bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall comply with all of the following requirements:
### (a)
the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall seek the approval of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* for all of the following:
#### (i)
the rules of incorporation of the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
#### (ii)
the members of the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]*, where those members are not directly appointed by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*;
#### (iii)
the responsibilities and remuneration of the members of the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]*, where the remuneration and the responsibilities are not determined by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*; and
#### (iv)
the strategy and risk profile of the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*; and
### (b)
the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall take over the authorisations of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution to provide the services or carry out the activities resulting from the transfer referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.42#1|Article 42(1)]] of this Regulation in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 648/2012.

Notwithstanding point (b) of the first subparagraph and where necessary to meet the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*, the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* may be authorised without complying with Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 for a short period at the beginning of its operation. To that end, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall submit a request for such authorisation to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*. If the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* decides to grant such authorisation, it shall indicate the period for which the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s obligation to comply with the requirements of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 is waived. That period shall not exceed 12 months. During that period, the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall be considered as a qualifying *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* as defined in point (88) of [[2013R0575_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]] for the purposes of that Regulation.

Notwithstanding the period referred to in the second subparagraph, in the case of prudential requirements under Chapter 3 of Title IV of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012, the waiver shall only be for a period of up to three months. It may be extended for one or two further periods of up to three months if necessary to achieve the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*.
## 2.
Subject to any restrictions imposed in accordance with Union or national competition rules, the management of the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall operate the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* with the objective of maintaining continuity of the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(27)|critical functions]]* and selling the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or any of its assets, rights, obligations and liabilities to one or more private sector purchasers. That sale shall take place when market conditions are appropriate, and within the period specified in paragraphs 5 and, where applicable, 6.
## 3.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall take a decision that the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is no longer a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* within the meaning of [[2021R0023_EN.42#2|Article 42(2)]] in any of the following cases:
### (a)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]* are fulfilled;
### (b)
the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* merges with another entity;
### (c)
the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* ceases to meet the requirements laid down in [[2021R0023_EN.42#2|Article 42(2)]];
### (d)
the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or substantially all of its assets, rights, obligations or liabilities have been sold in accordance with paragraphs 2 and 4 of this Article;
### (e)
the period specified in paragraph 5 of this Article or, where applicable, paragraph 6 of this Article expires;
### (f)
the contracts cleared by the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* have been settled, have expired or have been closed out and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s rights and obligations relating to those contracts are thereby completely discharged.
## 4.
Before selling the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or its assets, rights, obligations or liabilities, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall advertise the availability of the elements intended to be sold, and shall ensure that they are marketed openly and transparently, and that they are not materially misrepresented.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall carry out the sale referred to in the first subparagraph on commercial terms and shall not unduly favour or discriminate between potential purchasers.
## 5.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall terminate the operation of a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* two years after the date on which the last transfer from the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution is made.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* terminates the operation of a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, it shall request the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* to withdraw the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s authorisation.
## 6.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may extend the period referred to in paragraph 5 for one or more additional one-year periods where the extension is necessary to achieve the outcomes referred to in points (a) to (d) of paragraph 3.
The decision to extend the period referred to in paragraph 5 shall be reasoned and shall contain a detailed assessment of the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s situation in relation to relevant market conditions and market outlook.
## 7.
Where the operations of a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* are terminated in the circumstances referred to in point (d) or (e) of paragraph 3, the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall be wound up under *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(32)|normal insolvency proceedings]]*.
Unless otherwise provided for in this Regulation, any proceeds generated as a result of the termination of the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall benefit its shareholders.

Where a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is used for the purpose of transferring assets and liabilities of more than one *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, the proceeds referred to in the second subparagraph shall be attributed by reference to the assets and liabilities transferred from each of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* under resolution.
