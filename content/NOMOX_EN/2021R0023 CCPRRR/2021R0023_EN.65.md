# Article 65 - Safeguard for counterparties in partial transfers


The protections provided for in Articles [[2021R0023_EN.66|66]], [[2021R0023_EN.67|67]] and [[2021R0023_EN.68|68]] shall apply in the following circumstances:
### (a)
where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* transfers some but not all of the assets, rights, obligations or liabilities of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution to another entity or, in the application of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tool]]*, from a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to a purchaser; and
### (b)
where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* exercises the powers referred to in point (g) of [[2021R0023_EN.49#1|Article 49(1)]].
