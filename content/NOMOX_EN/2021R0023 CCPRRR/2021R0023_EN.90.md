# Article 90 - Amendment to Directive 2004/25/EC


In [[2021R0023_EN.4#5|Article 4(5)]], the third subparagraph is replaced by the following:

‘Member States shall ensure that Article 5(1) of this Directive does not apply in the case of application of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]*, powers and mechanisms provided for in Title IV of Directive 2014/59/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council


or in Title V of Regulation (EU) 2021/23 of the European Parliament and of the Council


.
