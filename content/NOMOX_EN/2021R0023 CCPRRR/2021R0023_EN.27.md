# Article 27 - General provisions on resolution tools

## 1.
Resolution authorities shall take *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution actions]]* referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.21|Article 21]] by applying any of the following *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* individually or in any combination:
### (a)
the position and loss allocation tools;
### (b)
the write-down and conversion tool;
### (c)
the sale of business tool;
### (d)
the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* tool.
## 2.
In the event of a systemic crisis, a Member State may as a last resort provide *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(30)|extraordinary public financial support]]* by applying government stabilisation tools in accordance with Articles [[2021R0023_EN.45|45]], [[2021R0023_EN.46|46]] and [[2021R0023_EN.47|47]] on the condition of prior and final approval under the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(39)|Union State aid framework]]* and where credible arrangements for the timely and comprehensive recovery of the funds in accordance with paragraph 10 of this Article are provided for.
## 3.
Prior to the application of the tools referred to in paragraph 1, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall enforce:
### (a)
any existing and outstanding rights of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, including any contractual obligations by *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* to meet *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(44)|recovery cash calls]]*, to provide additional resources to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, or to take on positions of defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]*, whether through an auction or other agreed means in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s operating rules;
### (b)
any existing and outstanding contractual obligation committing parties other than *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* to any forms of financial support.

The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may partially enforce the contractual obligations referred to in points (a) and (b) where it is not possible to enforce those contractual obligations in full within a reasonable timeframe.
## 4.
By way of derogation from paragraph 3, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may refrain from enforcing the relevant existing and outstanding obligations either partially or in full to avoid significant adverse effects on the financial system or widespread contagion, or where the application of the tools referred to in paragraph 1 is more appropriate in order to achieve the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]* in a timely manner.
## 5.
In the event that the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* refrains partially or fully from enforcing existing and outstanding obligations as set out in the second subparagraph of paragraph 3 or in paragraph 4 of this Article, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may enforce the remaining obligations within 18 months after the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is considered to be failing or likely to fail in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.22|Article 22]], provided that the reasons for refraining from enforcing those obligations no longer exist. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall notify the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* and other party three to six months before enforcing the remaining obligation. The proceeds from the enforced remaining obligations shall be used to recover the use of public funds.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall, after consultation with the competent authorities and resolution authorities of the affected *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* and any other parties committed by existing and outstanding obligations, determine whether the reasons for refraining from enforcing the existing and outstanding obligations have ceased to exist and whether to enforce remaining obligations. Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* deviates from the views expressed by the authorities consulted, it shall provide duly justified reasons, in writing, for doing so.

The requirement to meet the remaining obligations in the circumstances referred to in this paragraph shall be included in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s rules and other contractual arrangements.
## 6.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to compensate non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* for their losses stemming from the application of loss allocation tools, where those losses are in excess of the losses that the non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* would have borne under their obligations under the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s operating rules, provided that the non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* would have been entitled to the payment of the difference referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.62|Article 62]].
The compensation referred to in the first subparagraph may take the form of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]* or instruments recognising a claim on the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s future profits.

The amount of instruments issued to each affected non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* shall be proportionate to the excess loss referred to in the first subparagraph. It shall take account of any outstanding contractual obligations of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* toward the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and be deducted from any entitlement to the payment of the difference referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.62|Article 62]].

The amount of instruments shall be based on the valuation conducted in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.24#3|Article 24(3)]].
## 7.
Where one of the government stabilisation tools is applied, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall exercise the power to write down and convert any *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]* or other unsecured liabilities before or together with the application of the government stabilisation tool.
Where the application of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tool]]* other than the write-down and conversion tool results in financial losses being borne by *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]*, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall exercise the power to write down and convert any *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]* or other unsecured liabilities immediately before or together with the application of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tool]]*, unless applying a different sequence would minimise deviations from the ‘no creditor worse off’ principle set out in [[2021R0023_EN.60|Article 60]] and better achieve the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*.
## 8.
Where only the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* referred to in points (c) and (d) of paragraph 1 of this Article are applied, and only part of the assets, rights, obligations or liabilities of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution are transferred in accordance with Articles [[2021R0023_EN.40|40]] and [[2021R0023_EN.42|42]], the residual part of that *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall be wound up in accordance with *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(32)|normal insolvency proceedings]]*.
## 9.
National insolvency law rules relating to the voidability or unenforceability of legal acts detrimental to creditors shall not apply to transfers of assets, rights, obligations or liabilities from a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in relation to which *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* or government financial stabilisation tools are applied.
## 10.
Member States shall recover over an appropriate period any public funds used as government financial stabilisation tools as referred to in Section 7 of this Chapter and resolution authorities shall recover any reasonable expenses incurred by them in connection with the application of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* or powers. Such recovery shall, inter alia, come from:
### (a)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, as a preferred creditor, including any of its claims against defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]*;
### (b)
any consideration paid by the purchaser to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, as a preferred creditor prior to the application of [[2021R0023_EN.40#4|Article 40(4)]], where the sale of business tool has been applied;
### (c)
any proceeds generated as a result of the termination of the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, as a preferred creditor prior to the application of [[2021R0023_EN.42#5|Article 42(5)]];
### (d)
any proceeds generated by the application of the public equity support tool referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.46|Article 46]] and the temporary public ownership tool referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.47|Article 47]], including the proceeds generated from their sale.
## 11.
When applying the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]*, resolution authorities shall ensure, on the basis of a valuation that complies with [[2021R0023_EN.25|Article 25]], the restoration of a matched book, the full allocation of losses, the replenishment of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(36)|pre-funded resources]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, and the recapitalisation of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
Resolution authorities shall ensure the replenishment of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(36)|pre-funded resources]]* and the recapitalisation of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* as referred to in the first subparagraph, to an extent sufficient to restore the ability of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to comply with the conditions for authorisation and to continue to carry out the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(27)|critical functions]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, taking into account the operating rules of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.

Notwithstanding the application of other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]*, resolution authorities may apply the tools referred to in Articles [[2021R0023_EN.30|30]] and [[2021R0023_EN.31|31]] to recapitalise the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
