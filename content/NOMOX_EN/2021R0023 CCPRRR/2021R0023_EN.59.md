# Article 59 - Exercise of powers by the resolution authorities


Without prejudice to [[2021R0023_EN.74|Article 74]], resolution authorities shall take *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution actions]]* through executive orders in accordance with national administrative competences and procedures.
