# Article 21 - Resolution objectives

## 1.
When applying the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* and exercising the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(5)|resolution powers]]*, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall have regard to all the following *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*, which are of equal significance and shall balance them as appropriate to the nature and circumstances of each case:
### (a)
to ensure the continuity of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(27)|critical functions]]*, in particular:
#### (i)
the timely settlement of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s obligations to its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* and, where applicable, their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]*;
#### (ii)
continuous access of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* and, where applicable, their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* to securities or cash accounts provided by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and collateral in the form of financial assets held by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (b)
to ensure the continuity of the links with other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(16)|FMIs]]* which, if disrupted, would have a material negative impact on financial stability in the Union or in one or more of its Member States as well as the timely completion of payment, clearing, settlement and record-keeping functions;
### (c)
to avoid a significant adverse effect on the financial system in the Union or in one or more of its Member States, in particular by preventing or mitigating contagion of financial distress to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]*, their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* or to the wider financial system, including other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(16)|FMIs]]*, and by maintaining market discipline and public confidence; and
### (d)
to protect public funds by minimising reliance on *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(30)|extraordinary public financial support]]* and the potential risk of losses for taxpayers.
## 2.
When pursuing the objectives set out in paragraph 1, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall seek to minimise the cost of resolution on all affected stakeholders and avoid destruction of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s value, unless such destruction is necessary to achieve the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*.
