# Article 38 - Assessment and adoption of the business reorganisation plan

## 1.
Within one month of the submission of the business reorganisation plan by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.36#1|Article 36(1)]], the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall assess whether the measures provided for in that plan would reliably restore the long-term viability of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* are satisfied that the plan would restore the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s long-term viability, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall approve the plan.
## 2.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* or the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* are not satisfied that the measures provided for in the plan would restore the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s long-term viability, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall notify the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* of their concerns and require it to resubmit an amended plan addressing those concerns within two weeks of the notification.
## 3.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall assess the resubmitted plan and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall notify the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* within one week of the reception of that plan whether the concerns are appropriately addressed or whether further amendments are required.
## 4.
ESMA shall by 12 February 2023 develop draft regulatory technical standards to specify the criteria that a business reorganisation plan is to fulfil for approval by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* pursuant to paragraph 1.
The Commission is empowered to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
