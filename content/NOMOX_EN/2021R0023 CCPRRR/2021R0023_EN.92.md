# Article 92 - Amendment to Directive (EU) 2017/1132


Directive (EU) 2017/1132 is amended as follows:
## (1)
in [[2021R0023_EN.84|Article 84]], paragraph 3 is replaced by the following:

‘3.   Member States shall ensure that [[2021R0023_EN.49|Article 49]], [[2021R0023_EN.58#1|Article 58(1)]], [[2021R0023_EN.68#1|Article 68(1)]], (2) and (3), the first subparagraph of [[2021R0023_EN.70#2|Article 70(2)]], Articles [[2021R0023_EN.72|72]] to 75, 79, 80 and 81 of this Directive do not apply in the case of application of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]*, powers and mechanisms provided for in Title IV of Directive 2014/59/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council


or in Title V of Regulation (EU) 2021/23 of the European Parliament and of the Council


.

Directive 2014/59/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council of 15 May 2014 establishing a framework for the recovery and resolution of credit institutions and investment firms and amending Council Directive 82/891/EEC and Directives 2001/24/EC, 2002/47/EC, 2004/25/EC, 2005/56/EC, 2007/36/EC, 2011/35/EU, 2012/30/EU and 2013/36/EU, and Regulations (EU) No 1093/2010 and (EU) No 648/2012, of the European Parliament and of the Council."


Regulation (EU) 2021/23 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 16 December 2020 on a framework for the recovery and resolution of central counterparties and amending Regulations (EU) No 1095/2010, (EU) No 648/2012, (EU) No 600/2014, (EU) No 806/2014 and (EU) 2015/2365 and Directives 2002/47/EC, 2004/25/EC, 2007/36/EC, 2014/59/EU and (EU) 2017/1132 (OJ L 022, 22.1.2021, p. 1).’;"
## (2)
[[2021R0023_EN.86a|Article 86a]] is amended as follows:
### (a)
point (b) of paragraph 3 is replaced by the following:
the company is subject to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]*, powers and mechanisms provided for in Title IV of Directive 2014/59/EU or in Title V of Regulation (EU) 2021/23.’;
### (b)
point (c) of paragraph 4 is replaced by the following:
the subject of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(48)|crisis prevention measures]]* as defined in point (101) of [[2014L0059_EN.2#1|Article 2(1) of Directive 2014/59/EU]] or in point (48) of [[2021R0023_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]].’;
## (3)
in [[2021R0023_EN.87|Article 87]], paragraph 4 is replaced by the following:

‘4.   Member States shall ensure that this Chapter does not apply to companies which are the subject of the application of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]*, powers and mechanisms provided for in Title IV of Directive 2014/59/EU or in Title V of Regulation (EU) 2021/23.’;
## (4)
[[2021R0023_EN.120|Article 120]] is amended as follows:
### (a)
point (b) of paragraph 4 is replaced by the following:
the company is subject to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]*, powers and mechanisms provided for in Title IV of Directive 2014/59/EU or in Title V of Regulation (EU) 2021/23.’;
### (b)
point (c) of paragraph 5 is replaced by the following:
the subject of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(48)|crisis prevention measures]]* as defined in point (101) of [[2014L0059_EN.2#1|Article 2(1) of Directive 2014/59/EU]] or in point (48) of [[2021R0023_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]].’;
## (5)
[[2021R0023_EN.160a|Article 160a]] is amended as follows:
### (a)
point (b) of paragraph 4 is replaced by the following:
the company is subject to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]*, powers and mechanisms provided for in Title IV of Directive 2014/59/EU or in Title V of Regulation (EU) 2021/23.’;
### (b)
point (c) of paragraph 5 is replaced by the following:
the subject of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(48)|crisis prevention measures]]* as defined in point (101) of [[2014L0059_EN.2#1|Article 2(1) of Directive 2014/59/EU]] or in point (48) of [[2021R0023_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]].’.
