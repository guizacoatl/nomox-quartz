# Article 13 - CCPs’ duty to cooperate and provide information

## 1.
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* shall cooperate as necessary in the drawing up of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plans]]* and provide their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*, either directly or through their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*, with all the information necessary to draw up and implement those plans, including the information and analysis specified in Section B of the Annex.
Competent authorities shall provide resolution authorities with any information referred to in the first subparagraph which is already available to them.
## 2.
Resolution authorities may require *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* to provide them with detailed records of the contracts referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.29|Article 29 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] to which they are a party. Resolution authorities may specify a time limit to provide those records and may specify different time limits for different types of contracts.
## 3.
A *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall exchange information in a timely manner with its competent authorities in order to facilitate the assessment of the risk profiles of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and the interconnectedness with other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(16)|FMIs]]*, other financial institutions and with the financial system in general as referred to in Articles [[2021R0023_EN.9|9]] and [[2021R0023_EN.10|10]]. Competent authorities shall transmit information to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]*, where they deem such information significant.
