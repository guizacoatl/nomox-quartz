# Article 95 - Amendment to Regulation (EU) No 600/2014


In [[2021R0023_EN.54#2|Article 54(2)]], the first subparagraph is replaced by the following:

‘If the Commission assesses that there is no need to exclude exchange-traded *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(46)|derivatives]]* from the scope of Articles [[2021R0023_EN.35|35]] and [[2021R0023_EN.36|36]] in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.52#12|Article 52(12)]], a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venue]]* may, before 11 February 2021, apply to its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* for permission to avail itself of transitional arrangements. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*, taking into account the risks resulting from the application of the access rights under [[2021R0023_EN.35|Article 35]] or 36 as regards exchange-traded *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(46)|derivatives]]* to the orderly functioning of the relevant *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venue]]*, may decide that [[2021R0023_EN.35|Article 35]] or 36 would not apply to the relevant *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venue]]*, respectively, in respect of exchange-traded *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(46)|derivatives]]*, for a transitional period until 3 July 2021. Where such a transitional period is approved, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venue]]* shall not benefit from the access rights under [[2021R0023_EN.35|Article 35]] or 36, as regards exchange-traded *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(46)|derivatives]]* for the duration of that period. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall notify ESMA and, in the case of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, the college of competent authorities for that *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, when a transitional period is approved.’.
