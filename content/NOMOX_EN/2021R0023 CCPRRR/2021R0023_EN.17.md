# Article 17 - Coordination procedure to address or remove impediments to resolvability

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* shall reach a joint decision regarding:
### (a)
the identification of the material impediments to resolvability pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.15#1|Article 15(1)]];
### (b)
the assessment of the measures proposed by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.16#3|Article 16(3)]], as necessary;
### (c)
the alternative measures required pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.16#4|Article 16(4)]].
## 2.
The joint decision on the identification of material impediments to resolvability referred to in point (a) of paragraph 1 of this Article shall be adopted within four months of the submission of the report referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.16#1|Article 16(1)]] to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]*.
## 3.
The joint decision referred to in point (b) of paragraph 1 of this Article shall be adopted within four months of submission of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s proposed measures to remove impediments to resolvability as referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.16#3|Article 16(3)]].
## 4.
The joint decision referred to in point (c) of paragraph 1 of this Article shall be adopted within four months of the communication of the alternative measures to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* as referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.16#4|Article 16(4)]].
## 5.
The joint decisions referred to in paragraph 1 shall be reasoned and notified in writing by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and, where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* deems it relevant, its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(13)|parent undertaking]]*.
## 6.
ESMA may, at the request of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, assist the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* in reaching a joint decision in accordance with point (c) of [[2010R1095_EN.31#2|Article 31(2) of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
## 7.
Where, after four months from the date of transmission of the report provided for in [[2021R0023_EN.16#1|Article 16(1)]], the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* has failed to adopt a joint decision, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall take its own decision on the appropriate measures to be taken in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.16#5|Article 16(5)]]. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall take its decision having taken into account the views of the other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* members expressed during the four-month period.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall notify the decision to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, to its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(13)|parent undertaking]]* where relevant, and to the other members of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* in writing.
## 8.
Where, by the end of the four-month period referred to in paragraph 7 of this Article, a joint decision has not been reached and a simple majority of the voting members disagree with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*’s proposal for a joint decision on a matter referred to in point (j), (l) or (o) of [[2021R0023_EN.16#7|Article 16(7)]] of this Regulation, any of the voting members concerned, based on that majority, may refer that matter to ESMA in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.19|Article 19 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]]. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall await the decision taken by ESMA in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.19#3|Article 19(3) of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] and decide in accordance with the decision of ESMA.
The four-month period shall be deemed to be the conciliation phase within the meaning of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010. ESMA shall take its decision within one month of the referral of the matter to it. The matter shall not be referred to ESMA after the end of the four-month period or after a joint decision has been reached. In the absence of an ESMA decision within one month, the decision of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall apply.
