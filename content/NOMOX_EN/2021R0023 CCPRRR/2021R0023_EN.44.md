# Article 44 - Alternative funding means


The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may enter into contracts to borrow or obtain other forms of financial support, including from *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(36)|pre-funded resources]]* available in any non-depleted *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default funds]]* in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, where necessary to meet temporary liquidity needs in order to ensure the effective application of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]*.
