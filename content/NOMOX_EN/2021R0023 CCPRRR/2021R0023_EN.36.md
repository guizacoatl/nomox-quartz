# Article 36 - Submission of a business reorganisation plan

## 1.
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* shall, within one month after the application of the tools referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.32|Article 32]], conduct a review of the causes of its failure and submit it to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* alongside a business reorganisation plan in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.37|Article 37]]. Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(39)|Union State aid framework]]* is applicable, that plan, including following any amendments in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.38|Article 38]] and as implemented in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.39|Article 39]], shall be compatible with the restructuring plan that the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is required to submit to the Commission in accordance with that framework.
Where necessary for achieving the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may extend the period referred to in the first subparagraph up to a maximum of two months.
## 2.
Where a restructuring plan is required to be notified under the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(39)|Union State aid framework]]*, the submission of the business reorganisation plan shall be without prejudice to the deadline laid down by that framework for the submission of that restructuring plan.
## 3.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall submit the review and the business reorganisation plan, and any revised plan in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.38|Article 38]], to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* and to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]*.
