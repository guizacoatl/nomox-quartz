# Article 42 - Bridge CCP tool

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may transfer to a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* the following:
### (a)
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* issued by a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution;
### (b)
any assets, rights, obligations or liabilities of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution.

The transfer referred to in the first subparagraph may take place without obtaining the consent of the shareholders of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution or any third party other than the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and without complying with any procedural requirements under company or securities law other than those provided for in [[2021R0023_EN.43|Article 43]].
## 2.
The bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall be a legal person that:
### (a)
is controlled by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* and is wholly or partially owned by one or more public authorities which may include the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*; and
### (b)
is established or used for the purpose of receiving and holding some or all of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* issued by a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution or some or all of the assets, rights, obligations and liabilities of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* with a view to maintaining the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(27)|critical functions]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and subsequently selling the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 3.
When applying the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* tool, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall ensure that the total value of liabilities and obligations transferred to the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* does not exceed the total value of the rights and assets transferred from the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution.
## 4.
Subject to [[2021R0023_EN.27#10|Article 27(10)]], any consideration paid by the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall benefit:
### (a)
the owners of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*, where the transfer to the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has been effected by transferring *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* issued by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution from the holders of those instruments to the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (b)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, where the transfer to the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has been effected by transferring some or all of the assets or liabilities of that *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (c)
any non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* that have suffered losses following the application of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* in resolution, proportionate to their losses in resolution.
## 5.
The allocation of any consideration paid by the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in accordance with paragraph 4 of this Article shall be carried out as follows:
### (a)
upon the occurrence of an event covered by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(26)|default waterfall]]* as set out in Articles [[2021R0023_EN.43|43]] and [[2021R0023_EN.45|45]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012, in a reversal of the order in which the losses have been imposed by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(26)|default waterfall]]*; or
### (b)
upon the occurrence of an event not covered by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(26)|default waterfall]]* as set out in Articles [[2021R0023_EN.43|43]] and [[2021R0023_EN.45|45]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012, in a reversal of the order in which the losses were allocated in accordance with any applicable rules of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.

The allocation of any remaining consideration shall be carried out in accordance with the priority of claims under *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(32)|normal insolvency proceedings]]*.
## 6.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may exercise the transfer power referred to in paragraph 1 more than once in order to make supplemental transfers of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* issued by a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or of its assets, rights, obligations or liabilities.
## 7.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may transfer the rights, obligations, assets or liabilities that had been transferred to the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* back to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, or the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* back to their original owners where that transfer is expressly provided for in the instrument by which the transfer referred to in paragraph 1 is made.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* uses the transfer power referred to in the first subparagraph, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution or original owners shall be obliged to take back any such assets, rights, obligations or liabilities, or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*, provided that the conditions in the first subparagraph of this paragraph or in paragraph 8 are met.
## 8.
Where the specific *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*, assets, rights, obligations or liabilities do not fall within the classes of, or meet the conditions for transfer of, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*, assets, rights, obligations or liabilities specified in the instrument by which the transfer was made, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may transfer them from the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* back to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution or the original owners.
## 9.
A transfer referred to in paragraphs 7 and 8 may be made at any time, and shall comply with any other conditions stated in the instrument by which the transfer was made for the relevant purpose.
## 10.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may transfer *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* or assets, rights, obligations or liabilities from the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to a third party.
## 11.
For the purposes of exercising its right to provide services in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 648/2012, a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall be considered to be a continuation of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution and may continue to exercise any such right that was exercised by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution in respect of the assets, rights, obligations or liabilities transferred.
For any other purposes, resolution authorities may require that a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* be considered to be a continuation of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, and be able to continue to exercise any right that was exercised by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution in respect of the assets, rights, obligations or liabilities transferred.
## 12.
The bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall not be prevented from exercising the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s rights of membership and accessing payment and settlement systems and other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(29)|linked FMIs]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]*, provided that it meets the criteria for membership and participation in those systems or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(16)|FMIs]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]*.
Notwithstanding the first subparagraph, the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall not be denied access to payment and settlement systems and other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(16)|FMIs]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]* on the ground that the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* does not possess a rating from a credit rating agency, or that that rating is below the rating levels required to be granted access to those systems or infrastructures or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]*.

Where the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* does not meet the criteria referred to in the first subparagraph, the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* may continue to exercise the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s rights of membership and accessing those systems and other infrastructures and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]* for a period specified by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*. That period shall not exceed 12 months.
## 13.
Shareholders, creditors, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution and other third parties whose assets, rights, obligations or liabilities are not transferred to the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, shall have no rights over, or in relation to, the assets, rights, obligations or liabilities transferred to the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, or against its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]*.
## 14.
The bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have no duty or responsibility to shareholders or creditors of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]* of the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have no liability to those shareholders or creditors for acts and omissions in the discharge of their duties, unless the act or omission is due to gross negligence or serious misconduct in accordance with applicable national law.
