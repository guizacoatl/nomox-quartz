# Article 30 - Reduction of the value of any gains payable by the CCP to non-defaulting clearing members

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may reduce the amount of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s payment obligations to non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* where those obligations arise from gains due in accordance with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s processes for paying *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(42)|variation margin]]* or a payment that has the same economic effect.
## 2.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall calculate any reduction in payment obligations referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article using an equitable allocation mechanism determined in the valuation conducted in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.24#3|Article 24(3)]] and communicated to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* as soon as the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tool]]* is applied. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* shall, without undue delay, communicate the application of such tool to their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* and the way in which such application affects them. The total net gains to be reduced for each *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* shall be proportional to the amounts due from the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 3.
The reduction in the value of gains payable shall take effect and shall be immediately binding on the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and affected *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* from the moment at which the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* takes the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]*.
## 4.
A non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* shall not have any claim in any subsequent proceedings against the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, or its successor entity, arising from the reduction in payment obligations referred to in paragraph 1.
The first subparagraph of this paragraph shall not prevent resolution authorities from requiring the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to reimburse *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]*, where the level of reduction based on the provisional valuation referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.26#1|Article 26(1)]] is found to exceed the level of reduction required based on the definitive valuation referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.26#2|Article 26(2)]].
## 5.
Where a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* reduces only in part the value of gains payable, the residual outstanding payable amount shall still be owed to the non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]*.
## 6.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall include in its operating rules reference to the power to reduce payment obligations referred to in paragraph 1 in addition to any similar arrangements provided for in those operating rules at the recovery stage and shall ensure that contractual arrangements are concluded to allow the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* to exercise its powers under this Article.
