# Article 66 - Protection for financial collateral, set off and netting arrangements


The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall ensure that the application of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tool]]*, other than the position allocation tool referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.29|Article 29]], does not result in transferring some but not all of the rights and liabilities under a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(50)|title transfer financial collateral arrangement]]*, a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(15)|set-off arrangement]]* or a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(47)|netting arrangement]]* between a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution and other parties to the arrangements, or in modifying or terminating the rights and liabilities under those arrangements through the use of ancillary powers.

The arrangements referred to in the first subparagraph shall include any arrangement to which the parties are entitled to set-off or net those rights and liabilities.
