# Article 49 - Ancillary powers

## 1.
Where a power referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.48#1|Article 48(1)]] of this Regulation is exercised, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may also exercise any of the following ancillary powers:
### (a)
subject to [[2021R0023_EN.67|Article 67]], to provide for a transfer to take effect free from any liability or encumbrance affecting the financial instruments, rights, obligations, assets or liabilities transferred;
### (b)
to remove rights to acquire further *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*;
### (c)
to require the relevant authority to discontinue or suspend the admission to trading on a regulated market, or the official listing, of any financial instruments issued by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* pursuant to Directive 2001/34/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council


;
### (d)
to provide for the purchaser or bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, pursuant to Articles [[2021R0023_EN.40|40]] and [[2021R0023_EN.42|42]] respectively, to be treated as if it were the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, for the purposes of any rights or obligations of, or actions taken by, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, including any rights or obligations relating to participation in a market infrastructure;
### (e)
to require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution or the purchaser or bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, where relevant, to provide the other with information and assistance;
### (f)
to provide for the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* which is a recipient of any positions allocated to it by way of the powers in points (o) and (p) of [[2021R0023_EN.48#1|Article 48(1)]] to assume any rights or obligations relating to participation in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in relation to those positions;
### (g)
to cancel or modify the terms of a contract to which the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution is a party or substitute the purchaser or bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, in place of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, as a party;
### (h)
to modify or amend the operating rules of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, including as regards its terms of access to clearing for its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* and other participants;
#### (i)
to transfer the membership of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* from the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution to a purchaser of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.

Any right of compensation provided for in this Regulation shall not be considered to be a liability or an encumbrance for the purposes of point (a) of the first subparagraph.
## 2.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may provide for continuity arrangements necessary to ensure that the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]* is effective and that the business transferred may be operated by the purchaser or bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. Those continuity arrangements may include:
### (a)
the continuity of contracts entered into by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, in order for the purchaser or bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to assume the rights and liabilities of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution relating to any financial instrument, right, obligation, asset or liability that has been transferred and to replace the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, expressly or implicitly, in all relevant contractual documents;
### (b)
the replacement of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution by the purchaser or bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in any legal proceedings relating to any financial instrument, right, obligation, asset or liability that has been transferred.
## 3.
The powers provided for in point (d) of paragraph 1, and point (b) of paragraph 2, of this Article shall not affect:
### (a)
the right of an employee of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to terminate a contract of employment; nor
### (b)
subject to Articles [[2021R0023_EN.55|55]], [[2021R0023_EN.56|56]] and [[2021R0023_EN.57|57]], the exercise of contractual rights of a party to a contract, including the right to terminate, where provided for in the terms of the contract, due to an act or omission by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* prior to the transfer, or by the purchaser or bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* after the transfer.
