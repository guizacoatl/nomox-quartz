# Article 94 - Amendment to Regulation (EU) No 806/2014


[[2021R0023_EN.2|Article 2]] is amended as follows:
### (a)
the first subparagraph is numbered as paragraph 1;
### (b)
the following paragraph is added:

‘2.   This Regulation shall not apply to entities that are also authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]].’.
