# Article 40 - Sale of business tool

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may transfer the following to a purchaser that is not a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*:
### (a)
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* issued by a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution;
### (b)
any assets, rights, obligations or liabilities of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution.

The transfer referred to in the first subparagraph shall take place without obtaining the consent of the shareholders of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, or any third party other than the purchaser and without complying with any procedural requirements under company or securities law other than those provided for in [[2021R0023_EN.41|Article 41]].
## 2.
A transfer made pursuant to paragraph 1 shall be made on commercial terms, having regard to the circumstances, and in accordance with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(39)|Union State aid framework]]*.
For the purposes of the first subparagraph of this paragraph, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall take all reasonable steps to obtain commercial terms that conform to the valuation conducted under [[2021R0023_EN.24#3|Article 24(3)]].
## 3.
Subject to [[2021R0023_EN.27#10|Article 27(10)]], any consideration paid by the purchaser shall benefit:
### (a)
the owners of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* where the sale of business has been effected by transferring *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* issued by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution from the holders of those instruments to the purchaser;
### (b)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, where the sale of business has been effected by transferring some or all of the assets or liabilities of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to the purchaser;
### (c)
any non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* that have suffered losses resulting from the application of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* in resolution, proportionate to their losses in resolution.
## 4.
The allocation of any consideration paid by the purchaser in accordance with paragraph 3 of this Article shall be carried out as follows:
### (a)
upon the occurrence of an event covered by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(26)|default waterfall]]* as set out in Articles [[2021R0023_EN.43|43]] and [[2021R0023_EN.45|45]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012, in a reversal of the order in which the losses have been imposed by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(26)|default waterfall]]*; or
### (b)
upon the occurrence of an event not covered by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(26)|default waterfall]]* as set out in Articles [[2021R0023_EN.43|43]] and [[2021R0023_EN.45|45]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012, in a reversal of the order in which the losses were allocated in accordance with any applicable rules of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.

The allocation of any remaining consideration shall then be carried out in accordance with the priority of claims under *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(32)|normal insolvency proceedings]]*.
## 5.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may exercise the transfer power referred to in paragraph 1 more than once in order to make supplemental transfers of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* issued by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or, as the case may be, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s assets, rights, obligations, or liabilities.
## 6.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may, with the consent of the purchaser, transfer the assets, rights, obligations or liabilities that had been transferred to the purchaser back to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, or the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* back to their original owners.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* uses the transfer power referred to in the first subparagraph, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution or original owners shall be obliged to take back any such assets, rights, obligations or liabilities, or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*.
## 7.
Any transfer made pursuant to paragraph 1 shall take place irrespective of whether the purchaser is authorised to provide the services and carry out the activities resulting from the acquisition.
Where the purchaser is not authorised to provide the services and carry out the activities resulting from the acquisition, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*, in consultation with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*, shall conduct an appropriate due diligence assessment of the purchaser and ensure that the purchaser has the professional and technical capacity to perform the functions of the purchased *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and that it applies for authorisation as soon as practicable and, at the latest, within one month of the application of the sale of business tool. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall ensure that any such application for authorisation is considered in an expedited manner.
## 8.
Where the transfer of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article results in the acquisition of or increase in a qualifying holding referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.31#2|Article 31(2) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall carry out the assessment referred to in that Article within a period that neither delays the application of the sale of business tool nor prevents the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]* from achieving the relevant *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*.
## 9.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* has not completed the assessment referred to in paragraph 8 by the date on which the transfer of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* takes effect, the following shall apply:
### (a)
the transfer of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* shall have immediate legal effect from the date on which they are transferred;
### (b)
during the assessment period and during any divestment period provided for in point (f) of this paragraph, the purchaser’s voting rights attached to those *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* shall be suspended and vested solely in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*, which shall have no obligation to exercise them and, unless the act or omission implies gross negligence or serious misconduct, shall not be liable for exercising or refraining from exercising them;
### (c)
during the assessment period and during any divestment period provided for in point (f) of this paragraph, any penalties provided for in [[2012R0648_EN.22#3|Article 22(3) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] or measures for infringing the requirements for acquisitions or disposals of qualifying holdings provided for in [[2012R0648_EN.30|Article 30 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] shall not apply to that transfer;
### (d)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall notify the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* and the purchaser in writing of the result of its assessment in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.32|Article 32 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] promptly after completing its assessment;
### (e)
where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* does not oppose the transfer, the voting rights attached to those *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* shall be deemed to be fully vested in the purchaser as from the notification referred to in point (d) of this paragraph;
### (f)
where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* opposes the transfer of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*, point (b) shall continue to apply and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may, having taken into account market conditions, establish a divestment period within which the purchaser shall divest such *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*.
## 10.
For the purposes of exercising its right to provide services in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 648/2012, the purchaser shall be considered to be a continuation of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, and may continue to exercise any such right that was exercised by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution in respect of the assets, rights, obligations or liabilities transferred.
## 11.
The purchaser referred to in paragraph 1 shall not be prevented from exercising the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s rights of membership and accessing the payment and settlement systems and other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(29)|linked FMIs]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]* provided that the purchaser meets the criteria for membership or participation in those systems or infrastructures or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]*.
Notwithstanding the first subparagraph, the purchaser shall not be denied access to payment and settlement systems, and other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(29)|linked FMIs]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]*, on the ground that the purchaser does not possess a rating from a credit rating agency, or that that rating is below the rating levels required to be granted access to those systems or infrastructures or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]*.

Where the purchaser does not meet the criteria referred to in the first subparagraph, the purchaser may continue to exercise the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s rights of membership and accessing those systems and other infrastructures and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]* for the period specified by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*. That period shall not exceed 12 months.
## 12.
Unless otherwise provided for in this Regulation, shareholders, creditors, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution and other third parties whose assets, rights, obligations or liabilities are not transferred shall have no rights over, or in relation to, the assets, rights, obligations or liabilities transferred.
