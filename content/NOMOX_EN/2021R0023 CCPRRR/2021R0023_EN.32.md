# Article 32 - Requirement to write down and convert instruments of ownership and debt instruments or other unsecured liabilities

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall apply the write-down and conversion tool in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.33|Article 33]] in respect of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]* issued by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution or other unsecured liabilities in order to absorb losses, recapitalise that *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, or to support the application of the sale of business tool.
## 2.
Based on the valuation carried out in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.24#3|Article 24(3)]], the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall determine the following:
### (a)
the amount by which the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]* or other unsecured liabilities must be written down taking into account any losses that are to be absorbed by the enforcement of any outstanding obligations of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* or other third parties owed to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*; and
### (b)
the amount by which *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]* or other unsecured liabilities must be converted into *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* in order to restore the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* requirements of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or the bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
