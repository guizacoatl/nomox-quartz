# Article 5 - Structural separation

## 1.
The *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]* shall be legally, structurally, operationally and effectively separate from any *[[2023R1616_EN.1#1.|relevant public authority]]* and from the CCP.
## 2.
For the purposes of paragraph 1, the following requirements shall apply:
### (a)
where the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]* is a natural person, the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]* shall not be an employee or contractor of any *[[2023R1616_EN.1#1.|relevant public authority]]* or of the CCP;
### (b)
where the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]* is a legal person, the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]* shall not belong to the same group of companies as the CCP.
