# Article 9 - Impact of group arrangements

## 1.
Where the CCP forms part of a group, the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]*, or the resolution authority where conducting a provisional valuation pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.26#1|Article 26(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], shall take into account the impact of the existing contractual intra-group support arrangements on the value of the assets and liabilities where, on the basis of the circumstances, it is probable that those arrangements will be put into effect.
## 2.
The *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]*, or the resolution authority where conducting a provisional valuation pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.26#1|Article 26(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], shall only take into account the impact of other formal or informal support arrangements within the group where, on the basis of the circumstances, it is probable that those arrangements will remain in place in the context of a group’s stressed financial condition or in resolution.
## 3.
The *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]*, or the resolution authority where conducting a provisional valuation pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.26#1|Article 26(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], shall determine whether the resources of a CCP that is part of a group are available to meet the losses of other group entities.
