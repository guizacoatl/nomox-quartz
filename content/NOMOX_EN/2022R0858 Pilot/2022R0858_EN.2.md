# Article 2 - Definitions


For the purposes of this Regulation, the following definitions apply:
## (1)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(1)|distributed ledger technology]]*’ or ‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(1)|DLT]]*’ means a technology that enables the operation and use of *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(2)|distributed ledgers]]*;
## (2)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(2)|distributed ledger]]*’ means an information repository that keeps records of transactions and that is shared across, and synchronised between, a set of *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(4)|DLT network nodes]]* using a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(3)|consensus mechanism]]*;
## (3)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(3)|consensus mechanism]]*’ means the rules and procedures by which an agreement is reached, among *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(4)|DLT network nodes]]*, that a transaction is validated;
## (4)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(4)|DLT network node]]*’ means a device or process that is part of a network and that holds a complete or partial replica of records of all transactions on a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(2)|distributed ledger]]*;
## (5)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(5)|DLT market infrastructure]]*’ means a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(6)|DLT multilateral trading facility]]*, a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(7)|DLT settlement system]]* or a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(10)|DLT trading and settlement system]]*;
## (6)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(6)|DLT multilateral trading facility]]*’ or ‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(6)|DLT MTF]]*’ means a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(13)|multilateral trading facility]]* that only admits to trading *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(11)|DLT financial instruments]]*;
## (7)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(7)|DLT settlement system]]*’ or ‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(7)|DLT SS]]*’ means a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(8)|settlement]]* system that settles transactions in *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(11)|DLT financial instruments]]* against payment or against delivery, irrespective of whether that *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(8)|settlement]]* system has been designated and notified in accordance with Directive 98/26/EC, and that allows the initial recording of *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(11)|DLT financial instruments]]* or allows the provision of safekeeping services in relation to *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(11)|DLT financial instruments]]*;
## (8)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(8)|settlement]]*’ means *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(8)|settlement]]* as defined in [[2022R0858_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (7), of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014;
## (9)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(9)|settlement fail]]*’ means *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(9)|settlement fail]]* as defined in [[2022R0858_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (15), of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014;
## (10)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(10)|DLT trading and settlement system]]*’ or ‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(10)|DLT TSS]]*’ means a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(6)|DLT MTF]]* or *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(7)|DLT SS]]* that combines services performed by a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(6)|DLT MTF]]* and a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(7)|DLT SS]]*;
## (11)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(11)|DLT financial instrument]]*’ means a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(12)|financial instrument]]* that is issued, recorded, transferred and stored using *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(1)|distributed ledger technology]]*;
## (12)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(12)|financial instrument]]*’ means a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(12)|financial instrument]]* as defined in [[2022R0858_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (15), of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (13)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(13)|multilateral trading facility]]*’ means a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(13)|multilateral trading facility]]* as defined in [[2022R0858_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (22), of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (14)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(14)|central securities depository]]*’ or ‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(14)|CSD]]*’ means a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(14)|central securities depository]]* as defined in [[2022R0858_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (1), of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014;
## (15)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(15)|securities settlement system]]*’ means a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(15)|securities settlement system]]* as defined in [[2022R0858_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (10), of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014;
## (16)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(16)|business day]]*’ means *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(16)|business day]]* as defined in [[2022R0858_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (14), of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014;
## (17)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(17)|delivery versus payment]]* ’ means *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(17)|delivery versus payment]]* as defined in [[2022R0858_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (27), of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014;
## (18)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(18)|credit institution]]*’ means a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(18)|credit institution]]* as defined in [[2022R0858_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (1), of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council


;
## (19)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(19)|investment firm]]*’ means an *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(19)|investment firm]]* as defined in [[2022R0858_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (1), of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (20)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(20)|market operator]]*’ means a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(20)|market operator]]* as defined in [[2022R0858_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (18), of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (21)
‘*[[2022R0858_EN.2#(21)|competent authority]]*’ means one or more competent authorities:
### (a)
designated in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.67|Article 67 of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
### (b)
designated in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.11|Article 11 of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]]; or
### (c)
otherwise designated by a Member State to oversee the application of this Regulation.
