# Article 6 - Requirements and exemptions regarding DLT TSSs

## 1.
An *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(19)|investment firm]]* or *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(20)|market operator]]* operating a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(10)|DLT TSS]]* shall be subject to:
### (a)
the requirements that apply to a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(13)|multilateral trading facility]]* under Regulation (EU) No 600/2014 and Directive 2014/65/EU; and
### (b)
mutatis mutandis

, the requirements that apply to a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(14)|CSD]]* under Regulation (EU) No 909/2014, with the exception of Articles [[2022R0858_EN.9|9]], [[2022R0858_EN.16|16]], [[2022R0858_EN.17|17]], [[2022R0858_EN.18|18]], [[2022R0858_EN.20|20]], [[2022R0858_EN.26|26]], [[2022R0858_EN.27|27]], [[2022R0858_EN.28|28]], [[2022R0858_EN.31|31]], [[2022R0858_EN.42|42]], [[2022R0858_EN.43|43]], [[2022R0858_EN.44|44]], [[2022R0858_EN.46|46]] and [[2022R0858_EN.47|47]] of that Regulation.

The first subparagraph does not apply in respect of those requirements from which the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(19)|investment firm]]* or *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(20)|market operator]]* operating the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(10)|DLT TSS]]* has been exempted under [[2022R0858_EN.4#2|Article 4(2)]] and (3) and [[2022R0858_EN.5#2|Article 5(2)]] to (9), provided that that *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(19)|investment firm]]* or *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(20)|market operator]]* complies with:
### (a)
[[2022R0858_EN.7|Article 7]];
### (b)
[[2022R0858_EN.4#2|Article 4(2)]], (3) and (4) and [[2022R0858_EN.5#2|Article 5(2)]] to (10); and
### (c)
any compensatory measures that the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(21)|competent authority]]* deems appropriate in order to meet the objectives of the provisions in respect of which an exemption has been requested, or in order to ensure investor protection, market integrity or financial stability.
## 2.
A *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(14)|CSD]]* operating a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(10)|DLT TSS]]* shall be subject to:
### (a)
the requirements that apply to a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(14)|CSD]]* under Regulation (EU) No 909/2014; and
### (b)
mutatis mutandis

, the requirements that apply to a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(13)|multilateral trading facility]]* under Regulation (EU) No 600/2014 and Directive 2014/65/EU, with the exception of Articles [[2022R0858_EN.5|5]] to 13 of that Directive.

The first subparagraph does not apply in respect of those requirements from which the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(14)|CSD]]* operating the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(10)|DLT TSS]]* has been exempted under [[2022R0858_EN.4#2|Article 4(2)]] and (3) and [[2022R0858_EN.5#2|Article 5(2)]] to (9), provided that that *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(14)|CSD]]* complies with:
### (a)
[[2022R0858_EN.7|Article 7]];
### (b)
[[2022R0858_EN.4#2|Article 4(2)]], (3) and (4) and [[2022R0858_EN.5#2|Article 5(2)]] to (10); and
### (c)
any compensatory measures that the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(21)|competent authority]]* deems appropriate in order to meet the objectives of the provisions in respect of which an exemption has been requested or in order to ensure investor protection, market integrity or financial stability.
