# Article 17 - Amendment to Regulation (EU) No 909/2014


In [[2014R0909_EN.76#5|Article 76(5) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]], the first subparagraph is replaced by the following:

‘Each of the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(8)|settlement]]* discipline measures referred to in [[2022R0858_EN.7#1|Article 7(1)]] to (13) shall apply from the date of application specified for each *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(8)|settlement]]* discipline measure in the delegated act adopted by the Commission pursuant to [[2022R0858_EN.7#15|Article 7(15)]].’.
