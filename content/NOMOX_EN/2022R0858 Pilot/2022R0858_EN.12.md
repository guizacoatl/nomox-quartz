# Article 12 - Designation of competent authorities

## 1.
The *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(21)|competent authority]]* for an *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(19)|investment firm]]* operating a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(6)|DLT MTF]]* or *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(10)|DLT TSS]]* shall be the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(21)|competent authority]]* designated by the Member State determined in accordance with [[2022R0858_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], points (55)(a)(ii) and (iii), of Directive 2014/65/EU.
## 2.
The *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(21)|competent authority]]* for a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(20)|market operator]]* operating a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(6)|DLT MTF]]* or *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(10)|DLT TSS]]* shall be the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(21)|competent authority]]* designated by the Member State in which the registered office of the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(20)|market operator]]* operating the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(6)|DLT MTF]]* or *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(10)|DLT TSS]]* is situated or, if in accordance with the law of that Member State the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(20)|market operator]]* has no registered office, the Member State in which the head office of the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(20)|market operator]]* operating the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(6)|DLT MTF]]* or *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(10)|DLT TSS]]* is situated.
## 3.
The *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(21)|competent authority]]* for a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(14)|CSD]]* operating a *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(7)|DLT SS]]* or *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(10)|DLT TSS]]* shall be the *[[2022R0858_EN.2#(21)|competent authority]]* designated by the Member State determined in accordance with [[2022R0858_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (23), of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014.
