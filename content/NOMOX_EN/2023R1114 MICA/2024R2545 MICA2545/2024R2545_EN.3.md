> Level 1 reference(s): [[2023R1114_EN.95#11]]# Article 3 - Request for cooperation or exchange of information

## 1.
A requesting authority shall make a request for cooperation or exchange of information in writing, by post or by electronic means. It shall address the request to the contact point designated pursuant to [[2024R2545_EN.2|Article 2]].
## 2.
When requesting cooperation or the exchange of information, a competent authority shall use the form set out in Annex I and shall:
### (a)
specify the details of the relevant information requested;
### (b)
identify, where appropriate, issues relating to the confidentiality of the information requested.
