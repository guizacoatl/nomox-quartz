# Article 10 - Result of the offer to the public and safeguarding arrangements

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|Offerors]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* that set a time limit on their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* shall publish on their website the result of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* within 20 working days of the end of the subscription period.
## 2.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|Offerors]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* that do not set a time limit on their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* shall publish on their website on an ongoing basis, at least monthly, the number of units of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* in circulation.
## 3.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|Offerors]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* that set a time limit on their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* shall have effective arrangements in place to monitor and safeguard the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* or other *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* raised during the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]*. For that purpose, those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offerors]]* shall ensure that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* collected during the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* are kept in custody by one or both of the following:
### (a)
a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]*, where *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* are raised during the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]*;
### (b)
a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]*.
## 4.
When the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* has no time limit, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* shall comply with paragraph 3 of this Article until the right of withdrawal of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|retail holder]]* pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.13|Article 13]] has expired.
