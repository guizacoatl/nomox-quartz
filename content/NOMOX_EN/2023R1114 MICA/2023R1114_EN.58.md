# Article 58 - Specific additional obligations for issuers of e-money tokens

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(43)|Electronic money institutions]]* issuing significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall be subject to:
### (a)
the requirements referred to in Articles [[2023R1114_EN.36|36]], [[2023R1114_EN.37|37]] and [[2023R1114_EN.38|38]] and [[2023R1114_EN.45|Article 45]], (1) to (4) of this Regulation, instead of Article 7 of Directive 2009/110/EC;
### (b)
the requirements referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.35#2|Article 35(2)]], (3) and (5) and [[2023R1114_EN.45#5|Article 45(5)]] of this Regulation, instead of Article 5 of Directive 2009/110/EC.

By way of derogation from [[2023R1114_EN.36#9|Article 36(9)]], the independent audit shall, in respect of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, be mandated every six months as of the date of the decision to classify the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* as significant pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.56|Article 56]] or 57, as applicable.
## 2.
Competent authorities of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member States]]* may require *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(43)|electronic money institutions]]* issuing *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* that are not significant to comply with any requirement referred to in paragraph 1 where necessary to address the risks that those provisions aim to address, such as liquidity risks, operational risks, or risks arising from non-compliance with requirements for management of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(32)|reserve of assets]]*.
## 3.
Articles [[2023R1114_EN.22|22]], [[2023R1114_EN.23|23]] and [[2023R1114_EN.24|24]](3) shall apply to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* denominated in a currency that is not an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]* of a Member State.
