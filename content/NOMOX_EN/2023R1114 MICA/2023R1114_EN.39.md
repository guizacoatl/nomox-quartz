# Article 39 - Right of redemption

## 1.
Holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall have a right of redemption at all times against the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*, and in respect of the reserve assets when *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* are not able to meet their obligations as referred to in Chapter 6 of this Title. *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* shall establish, maintain and implement clear and detailed policies and procedures in respect of such permanent right of redemption.
## 2.
Upon request by a holder of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of such token shall redeem either by paying an amount in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]*, other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(44)|electronic money]]*, equivalent to the market value of the assets referenced by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* held or by delivering the assets referenced by the token. *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* shall establish a policy on such permanent right of redemption setting out:
### (a)
the conditions, including thresholds, periods and timeframes, for holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* to exercise such right of redemption;
### (b)
the mechanisms and procedures to ensure the redemption of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*, including in stressed market circumstances, as well as in the context of the implementation of the recovery plan set out in [[2023R1114_EN.46|Article 46]] or, in the case of an orderly redemption of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*, under [[2023R1114_EN.47|Article 47]];
### (c)
the valuation, or the principles of valuation, of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and of the reserve assets when the right of redemption is exercised by the holder of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*, including by using the valuation methodology set out in [[2023R1114_EN.36#11|Article 36(11)]];
### (d)
the conditions for settlement of the redemption; and
### (e)
measures that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* take to adequately manage increases or decreases in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(32)|reserve of assets]]* in order to avoid any adverse impacts on the market of the reserve assets.

Where *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]*, when selling an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, accept a payment in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(44)|electronic money]]*, denominated in an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]*, they shall always provide an option to redeem the token in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(44)|electronic money]]*, denominated in the same *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]*.
## 3.
Without prejudice to [[2023R1114_EN.46|Article 46]], the redemption of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall not be subject to a fee.
