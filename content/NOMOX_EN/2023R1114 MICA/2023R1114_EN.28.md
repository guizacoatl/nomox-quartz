# Article 28 - Publication of the crypto-asset white paper


An *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall publish on its website the approved *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.17#1|Article 17(1)]] or [[2023R1114_EN.21#1|Article 21(1)]] and, where applicable, the modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.25|Article 25]]. The approved *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall be publicly accessible by the starting date of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or the admission to trading of that token. The approved *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and, where applicable, the modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall remain available on the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*’s website for as long as the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* is held by the public.
