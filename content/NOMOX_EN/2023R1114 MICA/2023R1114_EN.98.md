# Article 98 - Cooperation with other authorities


Where an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*, person seeking admission to trading, an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* or a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* engages in activities other than those covered by this Regulation, the competent authorities shall cooperate with the authorities responsible for the supervision or oversight of such other activities pursuant to Union or national law, including tax authorities and relevant supervisory authorities of third countries.
