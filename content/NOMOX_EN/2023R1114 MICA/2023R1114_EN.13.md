# Article 13 - Right of withdrawal

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|Retail holders]]* who purchase *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* either directly from an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or from a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* placing *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* shall have a right of withdrawal.

*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|Retail holders]]* shall have a period of 14 calendar days within which to withdraw from their agreement to purchase *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* without incurring any fees or costs and without being required to give reasons. The period of withdrawal shall begin from the date of the agreement of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|retail holder]]* to purchase those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*.
## 2.
All payments received from a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|retail holder]]* including, if applicable, any charges, shall be reimbursed without undue delay and in any event no later than 14 days from the date on which the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* placing *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* is informed of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|retail holder]]*’s decision to withdraw from the agreement to purchase those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*.

Such reimbursement shall be carried out using the same means of payment as that used by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|retail holder]]* for the initial transaction, unless the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|retail holder]]* expressly agrees otherwise and provided that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|retail holder]]* does not incur any fees or costs as a result of such reimbursement.
## 3.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|Offerors]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* shall provide information on the right of withdrawal referred to in paragraph 1 in their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper.
## 4.
The right of withdrawal referred to in paragraph 1 shall not apply where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* have been admitted to trading prior to their purchase by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|retail holder]]*.
## 5.
Where *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offerors]]* have set a time limit on their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.10|Article 10]], the right of withdrawal shall not be exercised after the end of the subscription period.
