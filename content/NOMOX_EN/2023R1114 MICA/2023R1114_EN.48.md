# Article 48 - Requirements for the offer to the public or admission to trading of e-money tokens

## 1.
A person shall not make an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or seek the admission to trading of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*, within the Union, unless that person is the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* and:
### (a)
is authorised as a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]* or as an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(43)|electronic money institution]]*; and
### (b)
has notified a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* and has published that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.51|Article 51]].

Notwithstanding the first subparagraph, upon the written consent of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*, other persons may *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or seek the admission to trading of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*. Those persons shall comply with Articles [[2023R1114_EN.50|50]] and [[2023R1114_EN.53|53]].
## 2.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|E-money tokens]]* shall be deemed to be *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(44)|electronic money]]*.

An *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* that references an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]* of a Member State shall be deemed to be offered to the public in the Union.
## 3.
Titles II and III of Directive 2009/110/EC shall apply with respect to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* unless otherwise stated in this Title.
## 4.
Paragraph 1 of this Article shall not apply to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* exempted in accordance with Article 9(1) of Directive 2009/110/EC.
## 5.
This Title, with the exception of paragraph 7 of this Article and [[2023R1114_EN.51|Article 51]], shall not apply in respect of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* exempt pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.1#4|Article 1(4)]] and (5) of Directive 2009/110/EC.
## 6.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall, at least 40 working days before the date on which they intend to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* or seek their admission to trading, notify their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of that intention.
## 7.
Where paragraph 4 or 5 applies, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall draw up a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and notify such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.51|Article 51]].
