# Article 1 - Subject matter

## 1.
This Regulation lays down uniform requirements for the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* and admission to trading on a trading platform of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, as well as requirements for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*.
## 2.
In particular, this Regulation lays down the following:
### (a)
transparency and disclosure requirements for the issuance, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* and admission of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* to trading on a trading platform for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* (‘admission to trading’);
### (b)
requirements for the authorisation and supervision of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, as well as for their operation, organisation and governance;
### (c)
requirements for the protection of holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* in the issuance, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* and admission to trading of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*;
### (d)
requirements for the protection of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*;
### (e)
measures to prevent insider dealing, unlawful disclosure of inside information and market manipulation related to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, in order to ensure the integrity of markets in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*.
