# Article 74 - Orderly wind-down of crypto-asset service providers


*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* that provide the services referred to in Articles [[2023R1114_EN.75|75]] to 79 shall have in place a plan that is appropriate to support an orderly wind-down of their activities under applicable national law, including the continuity or recovery of any critical activities performed by those service providers. That plan shall demonstrate the ability of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* to carry out an orderly wind-down without causing undue economic harm to their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*.
