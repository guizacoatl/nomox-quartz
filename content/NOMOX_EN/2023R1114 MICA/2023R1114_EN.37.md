# Article 37 - Custody of reserve assets

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall establish, maintain and implement custody policies, procedures and contractual arrangements that ensure at all times that:
### (a)
the reserve assets are not encumbered nor pledged as a financial collateral arrangement as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (a), of Directive 2002/47/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council;
### (b)
the reserve assets are held in custody in accordance with paragraph 6 of this Article;
### (c)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* have prompt access to the reserve assets to meet any requests for redemption from the holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*;
### (d)
concentrations of the custodians of reserve assets are avoided;
### (e)
risk of concentration of reserve assets is avoided.
## 2.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* that issue two or more *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* in the Union shall have a custody policy in place for each pool of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(32)|reserve of assets]]*. Different *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* that have issued the same *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall operate and maintain a single custody policy.
## 3.
The reserve assets shall be held in custody by no later than five working days after the date of issuance of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* by one or more of the following:
### (a)
a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]*, where the reserve assets take the form of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*;
### (b)
a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]*, for all types of reserve assets;
### (c)
an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firm]]* that provides the ancillary service of safekeeping and administration of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]* for the account of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* as referred to in Section B, point (1), of Annex I to Directive 2014/65/EU, where the reserve assets take the form of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]*.
## 4.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall exercise all due skill, care and diligence in the selection, appointment and review of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]* appointed as custodians of the reserve assets as referred to in paragraph 3. The custodian shall be a legal person different from the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*.

*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall ensure that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]* appointed as custodians of the reserve assets as referred to in paragraph 3 have the necessary expertise and market reputation to act as custodians of such reserve assets, taking into account the accounting practices, safekeeping procedures and internal control mechanisms of those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]*. The contractual arrangements between the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and the custodians shall ensure that the reserve assets held in custody are protected against claims of the custodians’ creditors.
## 5.
The custody policies and procedures referred to in paragraph 1 shall set out the selection criteria for the appointment of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]* as custodians of the reserve assets and the procedure for reviewing such appointment.

*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall review the appointment of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]* as custodians of the reserve assets on a regular basis. For the purpose of that review, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall evaluate their exposures to such custodians, taking into account the full scope of their relationship with them, and monitor the financial conditions of such custodians on an ongoing basis.
## 6.
Custodians of the reserve assets as referred to in paragraph 4 shall ensure that the custody of those reserve assets is carried out in the following manner:
### (a)
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* shall hold in custody *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* in an account opened in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]*’ books;
### (b)
for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]* that can be held in custody, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]* shall hold in custody all *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]* that can be registered in a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]* account opened in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]*’ or investments firms’ books and all *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]* that can be physically delivered to such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]*;
### (c)
for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* that can be held in custody, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* shall hold in custody the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* included in the reserve assets or the means of access to such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, where applicable, in the form of private cryptographic keys;
### (d)
for other assets, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* shall verify the ownership of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and shall maintain a record of those reserve assets for which they are satisfied that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* own those reserve assets.
For the purposes of point (a) of the first subparagraph, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* shall ensure that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* are registered in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]*’ books on a segregated account in accordance with the provisions of national law transposing Article 16 of Commission Directive 2006/73/EC. That account shall be opened in the name of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* for the purposes of managing the reserve assets of each *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, so that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* held in custody can be clearly identified as belonging to each *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(32)|reserve of assets]]*.

For the purposes of point (b) of the first subparagraph, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]* shall ensure that all *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]* that can be registered in a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]* account opened in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]*’ books and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]*’ books are registered in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]*’ and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]*’ books on segregated accounts in accordance with the provisions of national law transposing Article 16 of Directive 2006/73/EC. The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]* account shall be opened in the name of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* for the purposes of managing the reserve assets of each *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, so that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]* held in custody can be clearly identified as belonging to each *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(32)|reserve of assets]]*.

For the purposes of point (c) of the first subparagraph, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* shall open a register of positions in the name of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* for the purposes of managing the reserve assets of each *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, so that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* held in custody can be clearly identified as belonging to each *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(32)|reserve of assets]]*.

For the purposes of point (d) of the first subparagraph, the assessment whether *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* own the reserve assets shall be based on information or documents provided by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and, where available, on external evidence.
## 7.
The appointment of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]* as custodians of the reserve assets as referred to in paragraph 4 of this Article shall be evidenced by a contractual arrangement as referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.34#5|Article 34(5)]], second subparagraph. Those contractual arrangements shall, amongst others, regulate the flow of information necessary to enable the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]* to perform their functions as custodians.
## 8.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]* appointed as custodians in accordance with paragraph 4 shall act honestly, fairly, professionally, independently and in the interest of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and the holders of such tokens.
## 9.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]* appointed as custodians in accordance with paragraph 4 shall not carry out activities with regard to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* that might create conflicts of interest between those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]*, the holders of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and themselves unless all of the following conditions are met:
### (a)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firms]]* have functionally and hierarchically separated the performance of their custody tasks from their potentially conflicting tasks;
### (b)
the potential conflicts of interest have been properly identified, monitored, managed and disclosed by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* to the holders of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*, in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.32|Article 32]].
## 10.
In the case of a loss of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instrument]]* or a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* held in custody pursuant to paragraph 6, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firm]]* that lost that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instrument]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* shall compensate, or make restitution, to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* with a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instrument]]* or a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* of an identical type or the corresponding value without undue delay. The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firm]]* concerned shall not be liable for compensation or restitution where it can prove that the loss has occurred as a result of an external event beyond its reasonable control, the consequences of which were unavoidable despite all reasonable efforts to the contrary.
