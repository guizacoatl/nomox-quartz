# Article 16 - Authorisation

## 1.
A person shall not make an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]*, or seek the admission to trading, of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, within the Union, unless that person is the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* and is:
### (a)
a legal person or other undertaking that is established in the Union and has been authorised in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.21|Article 21]] by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*; or
### (b)
a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]* that complies with [[2023R1114_EN.17|Article 17]].

Notwithstanding the first subparagraph, upon the written consent of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, other persons may *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or seek the admission to trading of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*. Those persons shall comply with Articles [[2023R1114_EN.27|27]], [[2023R1114_EN.29|29]] and [[2023R1114_EN.40|40]].

For the purposes of point (a) of the first subparagraph, other undertakings may issue *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* only if their legal form ensures a level of protection for third parties’ interests equivalent to that afforded by legal persons and if they are subject to equivalent prudential supervision appropriate to their legal form.
## 2.
Paragraph 1 shall not apply where:
### (a)
over a period of 12 months, calculated at the end of each calendar day, the average outstanding value of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* issued by an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* never exceeds EUR 5 000 000 , or the equivalent amount in another *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]*, and the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* is not linked to a network of other exempt *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]*; or
### (b)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* is addressed solely to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(30)|qualified investors]]* and the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* can only be held by such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(30)|qualified investors]]*.

Where this paragraph applies, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall draw up a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper as provided for in [[2023R1114_EN.19|Article 19]] and notify that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and, upon request, any marketing communications, to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*.
## 3.
The authorisation granted by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* to a person referred to in paragraph 1, first subparagraph, point (a), shall be valid for the entire Union and shall allow an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]*, throughout the Union, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* for which it has been authorised, or to seek an admission to trading of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*.
## 4.
The approval granted by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*’s *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper under [[2023R1114_EN.17#1|Article 17(1)]] or [[2023R1114_EN.21#1|Article 21(1)]] or of the modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper under [[2023R1114_EN.25|Article 25]] shall be valid for the entire Union.
