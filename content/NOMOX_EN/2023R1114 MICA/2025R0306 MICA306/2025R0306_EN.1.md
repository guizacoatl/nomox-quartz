# Article 1 - Designation of a contact point


Competent authorities shall designate a contact point for receiving the applications for authorisation as a crypto-asset service provider in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.62|Article 62 of Regulation (EU) 1114/2023]].

Competent authorities shall keep the contact details of the designated contact point up-to-date and shall make those contact details public on their websites.
