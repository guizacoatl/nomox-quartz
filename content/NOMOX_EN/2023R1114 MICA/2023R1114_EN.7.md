# Article 7 - Marketing communications

## 1.
Any marketing communications relating to an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* other than an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*, or to the admission to trading of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]*, shall comply with all of the following requirements:
### (a)
the marketing communications are clearly identifiable as such;
### (b)
the information in the marketing communications is fair, clear and not misleading;
### (c)
the information in the marketing communications is consistent with the information in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper, where such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper is required pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.4|Article 4]] or 5;
### (d)
the marketing communications clearly state that a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper has been published and clearly indicate the address of the website of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*, the person seeking admission to trading, or the operator of the trading platform for the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* concerned, as well as a telephone number and an email address to contact that person;
### (e)
the marketing communications contain the following clear and prominent statement:

‘This *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* marketing communication has not been reviewed or approved by any *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* in any Member State of the European Union. The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* is solely responsible for the content of this *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* marketing communication.’.

Where the marketing communication is prepared by the person seeking admission to trading or the operator of a trading platform, then, instead of ‛*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*’, a reference to ‘person seeking admission to trading’ or ‘operator of the trading platform’ shall be included in the statement referred to in the first subparagraph, point (e).
## 2.
Where a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper is required pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.4|Article 4]] or 5, no marketing communications shall be disseminated prior to the publication of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper. The ability of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*, the person seeking admission to trading or the operator of a trading platform, to conduct market soundings shall not be affected.
## 3.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the Member State where the marketing communications are disseminated shall have the power to assess compliance with paragraph 1 in respect of those marketing communications.

Where necessary, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* shall assist the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the Member State where the marketing communications are disseminated with assessing the consistency of the marketing communications with the information in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper.
## 4.
The use of any of the supervisory and investigatory powers set out in [[2023R1114_EN.94|Article 94]] in relation to the enforcement of this Article by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member State]]* shall be notified without undue delay to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*, the person seeking admission to trading or the operator of the trading platform for the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*.
