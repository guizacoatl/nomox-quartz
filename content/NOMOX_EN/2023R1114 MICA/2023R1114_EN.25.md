# Article 25 - Modification of published crypto-asset white papers for asset-referenced tokens

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall notify the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* of any intended change of their business model likely to have a significant influence on the purchase decision of any holders or prospective holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*, which occurs after the authorisation pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.21|Article 21]] or after the approval of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.17|Article 17]], as well as in the context of [[2023R1114_EN.23|Article 23]]. Such changes include, amongst others, any material modifications to:
### (a)
the governance arrangements, including reporting lines to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]* and risk management framework;
### (b)
the reserve assets and the custody of the reserve assets;
### (c)
the rights granted to the holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*;
### (d)
the mechanism through which an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* is issued and redeemed;
### (e)
the protocols for validating the transactions in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*;
### (f)
the functioning of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]*’ proprietary *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(1)|distributed ledger technology]]*, where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* are issued, transferred and stored using such a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(1)|distributed ledger technology]]*;
### (g)
the mechanisms to ensure the liquidity of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*, including the liquidity management policy and procedures for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.45|Article 45]];
### (h)
the arrangements with third-party entities, including for managing the reserve assets and the investment of the reserve, the custody of reserve assets, and, where applicable, the distribution of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* to the public;
#### (i)
the complaints-handling procedures;
### (j)
the money laundering and terrorist financing risk assessment and general policies and procedures related thereto.

*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall notify the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* at least 30 working days before the intended changes take effect.
## 2.
Where any intended change as referred to in paragraph 1 has been notified to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]*, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall draw up a draft modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and shall ensure that the order of the information appearing therein is consistent with that of the original *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper.

The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall notify the draft modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*.

The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall electronically acknowledge receipt of the draft modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper as soon as possible, and at the latest five working days from receipt thereof.

The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall grant approval of, or refuse to approve, the draft modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper within 30 working days of acknowledgement of receipt thereof. During the examination of the draft modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* may request any additional information, explanations or justifications concerning the draft modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper. When the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* makes such request, the time limit of 30 working days shall commence only when the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* has received the additional information requested.
## 3.
Where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* considers that the modifications to a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper are potentially relevant for the smooth operation of payment systems, monetary policy transmission and monetary sovereignty, it shall consult the ECB and, where applicable, the central bank referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.20#4|Article 20(4)]]. The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* may also consult EBA and ESMA in such cases.

The ECB or the relevant central bank and, where applicable, EBA and ESMA, shall provide an opinion within 20 working days of receipt of the consultation referred to in the first subparagraph.
## 4.
Where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* approves the modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper, it may require the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*:
### (a)
to put in place mechanisms to ensure the protection of holders of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, when a potential modification of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*’s operations can have a material effect on the value, stability, or risks of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or the reserve assets;
### (b)
to take any appropriate corrective measures to address concerns related to market integrity, financial stability or the smooth operation of payment systems.

The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall require the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* to take any appropriate corrective measures to address concerns related to the smooth operation of payment systems, monetary policy transmission, or monetary sovereignty, if such corrective measures are proposed by the ECB or, where applicable, the central bank referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.20#4|Article 20(4)]] in the consultations referred to in paragraph 3 of this Article.

Where the ECB or the central bank referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.20#4|Article 20(4)]] has proposed different measures than the ones required by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]*, the measures proposed shall be combined or, if not possible, the more stringent measure shall be required.
## 5.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall communicate the modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper to ESMA, the single points of contact of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member States]]*, EBA, the ECB and, where applicable, the central bank of the Member State concerned within two working days of granting approval.

ESMA shall make the modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper available in the register referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.109|Article 109]] without undue delay.
