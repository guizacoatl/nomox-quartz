> Level 1 reference(s): [[2023R1114_EN.22#6]]# Article 1 - Subject matter

## 1.
This Regulation specifies the methodology to estimate the quarterly average number and average aggregate value of transactions per day that are associated to uses of an asset-referenced token as a means of exchange within a *[[2025R0298_EN.2#(1)|single currency area]]*, in accordance with [[2025R0298_EN.22#1|Article 22(1)]], point (d), of Regulation (EU) 2023/1114.
## 2.
In accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.58#3|Article 58(3) of Regulation (EU) 1114/2023]], this Regulation also applies
mutatis mutandis

to e-money tokens denominated in a currency that is not an official currency of a Member State.
