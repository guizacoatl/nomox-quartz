# Article 55 - Recovery and redemption plans


Title III, Chapter 6 shall apply

mutatis mutandis

to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*.

By way of derogation from [[2023R1114_EN.46#2|Article 46(2)]], the date by which the recovery plan is to be notified to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall, in respect of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, be within six months of the date of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or admission to trading.

By way of derogation from [[2023R1114_EN.47#3|Article 47(3)]], the date by which the redemption plan is to be notified to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall, in respect of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, be within six months of the date of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or admission to trading.
