# Article 21 - Grant or refusal of the authorisation

## 1.
Competent authorities shall, within 25 working days of receipt of the opinions referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.20#5|Article 20(5)]], take a fully reasoned decision granting or refusing authorisation to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]* and, within five working days of taking that decision, notify it to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*. Where an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]* is authorised, its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall be deemed to be approved.
## 2.
Competent authorities shall refuse authorisation where there are objective and demonstrable grounds that:
### (a)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]* might pose a threat to its effective, sound and prudent management and business continuity and to the adequate consideration of the interest of its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* and the integrity of the market;
### (b)
members of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]* do not meet the criteria set out in [[2023R1114_EN.34#2|Article 34(2)]];
### (c)
shareholders and members, whether direct or indirect, that have *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(36)|qualifying holdings]]* do not meet the criteria of sufficiently good repute set out in [[2023R1114_EN.34#4|Article 34(4)]];
### (d)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]* fails to meet or is likely to fail to meet any of the requirements of this Title;
### (e)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*’s business model might pose a serious threat to market integrity, financial stability, the smooth operation of payment systems, or exposes the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* or the sector to serious risks of money laundering and terrorist financing.
## 3.
EBA and ESMA shall, by 30 June 2024, jointly issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1093_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]] and [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]], respectively, on the assessment of the suitability of the members of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and of the shareholders and members, whether direct or indirect, that have *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(36)|qualifying holdings]]* in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*.
## 4.
Competent authorities shall also refuse authorisation if the ECB or, where applicable, the central bank gives a negative opinion under [[2023R1114_EN.20#5|Article 20(5)]] on the grounds of a risk posed to the smooth operation of payment systems, monetary policy transmission, or monetary sovereignty.
## 5.
Competent authorities shall, within two working days of granting authorisation, communicate to the single point of contact of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member States]]*, to ESMA, to EBA, to the ECB and, where applicable, to the central bank referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.20#4|Article 20(4)]], the information specified in [[2023R1114_EN.109#3|Article 109(3)]].

ESMA shall make such information available in the register, under [[2023R1114_EN.109#3|Article 109(3)]], by the starting date of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or admission to trading.
## 6.
Competent authorities shall inform EBA, ESMA, the ECB and, where applicable, the central bank referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.20#4|Article 20(4)]], of all requests for authorisations refused, and provide the underlying reasoning for the decision and, where applicable, an explanation for any deviation from the opinions referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.20#5|Article 20(5)]].
