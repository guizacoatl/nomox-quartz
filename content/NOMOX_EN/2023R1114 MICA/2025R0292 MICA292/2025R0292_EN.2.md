> Level 1 reference(s): [[2023R1114_EN.107#3]]# Article 2 - Transfers of personal data


Where competent authorities rely on an administrative arrangement pursuant to [[2025R0292_EN.46#3|Article 46(3)]], point (b), of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 for the transfer of personal data to supervisory authorities of third countries, that arrangement shall be annexed to the cooperation arrangement entered into in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.107#1|Article 107(1) of Regulation (EU) 1114/2023]].
