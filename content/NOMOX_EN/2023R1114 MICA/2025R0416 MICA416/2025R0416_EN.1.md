# Article 1 - Content, standards and format of data relating to orders in crypto-assets

## 1.
Crypto-asset service providers operating a trading platform for crypto-assets shall keep at the disposal of the competent authority, or give the competent authority access to the details of each order in crypto-assets advertised through their systems set out in Articles [[2025R0416_EN.2|2]] to 15, as specified in the format laid down in Tables 2 and 3 of the Annex, insofar as they pertain to the order concerned.
## 2.
The data referred to in paragraph 1 shall be in a JSON format in accordance with the methodology laid out in standard ISO 20022.
