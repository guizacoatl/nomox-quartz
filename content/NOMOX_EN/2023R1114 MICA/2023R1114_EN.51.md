> Level 2 reference(s): [[2025R0422_EN.4]], [[2025R0422_EN.6]], [[2025R0422_EN.5]], [[2025R0422_EN.0]], [[2025R0422_EN.2]], [[2025R0422_EN.3]], [[2025R0422_EN.1]]# Article 51 - Content and form of the crypto-asset white paper for e-money tokens

## 1.
A *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper for an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* shall contain all of the following information, as further specified in Annex III:
### (a)
information about the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*;
### (b)
information about the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*;
### (c)
information about the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* or its admission to trading;
### (d)
information on the rights and obligations attached to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*;
### (e)
information on the underlying technology;
### (f)
information on the risks;
### (g)
information on the principal adverse impacts on the climate and other environment-related adverse impacts of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(3)|consensus mechanism]]* used to issue the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*.

The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall also include the identity of the person other than the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* that offers the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* to the public or seeks its admission to trading pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.48#1|Article 48(1)]], second subparagraph, and the reason why that particular person offers that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* or seeks its admission to trading.
## 2.
All the information listed in paragraph 1 shall be fair, clear and not misleading. The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall not contain material omissions and shall be presented in a concise and comprehensible form.
## 3.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall contain the following clear and prominent statement on the first page:

‘This *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper has not been approved by any *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* in any Member State of the European Union. The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* is solely responsible for the content of this *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper.’.
## 4.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall contain a clear warning that:
### (a)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* is not covered by the investor compensation schemes under Directive 97/9/EC;
### (b)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* is not covered by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(50)|deposit]]* guarantee schemes under Directive 2014/49/EU.
## 5.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall contain a statement from the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*. That statement, which shall be inserted after the statement referred to in paragraph 3, shall confirm that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper complies with this Title and that, to the best of the knowledge of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]*, the information presented in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper is complete, fair, clear and not misleading and that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper makes no omission likely to affect its import.
## 6.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall contain a summary, inserted after the statement referred to in paragraph 5, which shall in brief and non-technical language provide key information about the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* or the intended admission to trading of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*. The summary shall be easily understandable and presented and laid out in a clear and comprehensive format, using characters of readable size. The summary of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall provide appropriate information about the characteristics of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* concerned in order to help prospective holders of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* to make an informed decision.

The summary shall contain a warning that:
### (a)
it should be read as an introduction to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper;
### (b)
the prospective holder should base any decision to purchase the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* on the content of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper as a whole and not on the summary alone;
### (c)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* does not constitute an offer or solicitation to purchase *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]* and that any such offer or solicitation can be made only by means of a prospectus or other offer documents pursuant to the applicable national law;
### (d)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper does not constitute a prospectus as referred to in Regulation (EU) 2017/1129 or any other offer document pursuant to Union or national law.

The summary shall state that holders of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* have a right of redemption at any time and at par value as well as the conditions for redemption.
## 7.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall contain the date of its notification and a table of contents.
## 8.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall be drawn up in an official language of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* or in a language customary in the sphere of international finance.

Where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* is also offered in a Member State other than the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall also be drawn up in an official language of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member State]]* or in a language customary in the sphere of international finance.
## 9.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall be made available in a machine-readable format.
## 10.
ESMA, in cooperation with EBA, shall develop draft implementing technical standards to establish standard forms, formats and templates for the purposes of paragraph 9.

ESMA shall submit the draft implementing technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph to the Commission by 30 June 2024.

Power is conferred on the Commission to adopt the implementing technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
## 11.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall notify their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper to their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* at least 20 working days before the date of their publication.

Competent authorities shall not require prior approval of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white papers before their publication.
## 12.
Any significant new factor, any material mistake or any material inaccuracy that is capable of affecting the assessment of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* shall be described in a modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper drawn up by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]*, notified to the competent authorities and published on the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]*’ websites.
## 13.
Before offering the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* to the public in the Union or seeking an admission to trading of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* shall publish a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper on its website.
## 14.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* shall together with the notification of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper pursuant to paragraph 11 of this Article provide the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* with the information referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.109#4|Article 109(4)]]. The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall communicate to ESMA, within five working days of receipt of the information from the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*, the information specified in [[2023R1114_EN.109#4|Article 109(4)]].

The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall also communicate to ESMA any modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and any withdrawal of the authorisation of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*.

ESMA shall make such information available in the register, under [[2023R1114_EN.109#4|Article 109(4)]], by the starting date of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or admission to trading or, in the case of a modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper, or withdrawal of the authorisation, without undue delay.
## 15.
ESMA, in cooperation with EBA, shall develop draft regulatory technical standards on the content, methodologies and presentation of the information referred to in paragraph 1, point (g), in respect of the sustainability indicators in relation to adverse impacts on the climate and other environment‐related adverse impacts.

When developing the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph, ESMA shall consider the various types of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(3)|consensus mechanisms]]* used to validate transactions in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, their incentive structures and the use of energy, renewable energy and natural resources, the production of waste, and greenhouse gas emissions. ESMA shall update the regulatory technical standards in the light of regulatory and technological developments.

ESMA shall submit the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph to the Commission by 30 June 2024.

Power is delegated to the Commission to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
