# Article 44 - Voluntary classification of asset-referenced tokens as significant asset-referenced tokens

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|Applicant issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* may indicate in their application for authorisation pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.18|Article 18]], or in their notification pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.17|Article 17]], that they wish for their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* to be classified as significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*. In that case, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall immediately notify such request of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]* to EBA, to the ECB and, in the cases referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.43#4|Article 43(4)]], to the central bank of the Member State concerned.

In order for an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* to be classified as significant under this Article, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall demonstrate, through a detailed programme of operations referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.17#1|Article 17(1)]], point (b)(i), and [[2023R1114_EN.18#2|Article 18(2)]], point (d), that it is likely to fulfil at least three of the criteria set out in [[2023R1114_EN.43#1|Article 43(1)]].
## 2.
EBA shall, within 20 working days of the notification referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article, prepare a draft decision containing its opinion based on the programme of operations whether the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* fulfils or is likely to fulfil at least three of the criteria set out in [[2023R1114_EN.43#1|Article 43(1)]] and notify that draft decision to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*’s *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*, to the ECB and, in the cases referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.43#4|Article 43(4)]], second subparagraph, to the central bank of the Member State concerned.

Competent authorities of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*, the ECB and, where applicable, the central bank of the Member State concerned, shall have 20 working days from the date of notification of that draft decision to provide observations and comments in writing. EBA shall duly consider those observations and comments before adopting a final decision.
## 3.
EBA shall take its final decision on whether to classify an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* as a significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* within 60 working days of the notification referred to in paragraph 1 and immediately notify that decision to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]* of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* and its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]*.
## 4.
Where *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* have been classified as significant pursuant to a decision of EBA taken in accordance with paragraph 3 of this Article, the supervisory responsibilities with respect to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall be transferred from the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* to EBA on the date of the decision of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* to grant the authorisation referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.21#1|Article 21(1)]] or on the date of approval of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.17|Article 17]].
