# Article 75 - Providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]* shall conclude an agreement with their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* to specify their duties and their responsibilities. Such an agreement shall include at least the following:
### (a)
the identity of the parties to the agreement;
### (b)
the nature of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset service]]* provided and a description of that service;
### (c)
the custody policy;
### (d)
the means of communication between the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* and the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*, including the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*’s authentication system;
### (e)
a description of the security systems used by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*;
### (f)
the fees, costs and charges applied by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*;
### (g)
the applicable law.
## 2.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]* shall keep a register of positions, opened in the name of each *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*, corresponding to each *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*’s rights to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*. Where relevant, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* shall record as soon as possible in that register any movements following instructions from their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*. In such cases, their internal procedures shall ensure that any movement affecting the registration of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* is evidenced by a transaction regularly registered in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*’s register of positions.
## 3.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]* shall establish a custody policy with internal rules and procedures to ensure the safekeeping or the control of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, or the means of access to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*.

The custody policy referred to in the first subparagraph shall minimise the risk of a loss of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*’ *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or the rights related to those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or the means of access to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* due to fraud, cyber threats or negligence.

A summary of the custody policy shall be made available to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* at their request in an electronic format.
## 4.
Where applicable, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]* shall facilitate the exercise of the rights attached to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*. Any event likely to create or modify the rights of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* shall immediately be recorded in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*’s register of positions.

Where there are changes to the underlying *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(1)|distributed ledger technology]]* or any other event likely to create or modify a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*’s rights, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* shall be entitled to any *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or any rights newly created on the basis and to the extent of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*’s positions at the time of the occurrence of that change or event, except when a valid agreement signed with the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]* pursuant to paragraph 1 prior to that change or event expressly provides otherwise.
## 5.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]* shall provide their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*, at least once every three months and at the request of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* concerned, with a statement of position of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* recorded in the name of those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*. That statement of position shall be made in an electronic format. The statement of position shall identify the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* concerned, their balance, their value and the transfer of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* made during the period concerned.

*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]* shall provide their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* as soon as possible with any information about operations on *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* that require a response from those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*.
## 6.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]* shall ensure that necessary procedures are in place to return *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* held on behalf of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*, or the means of access, as soon as possible to those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*.
## 7.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]* shall segregate holdings of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* from their own holdings and ensure that the means of access to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* is clearly identified as such. They shall ensure that, on the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(2)|distributed ledger]]*, their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*’ *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* are held separately from their own *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*.

The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* held in custody shall be legally segregated from the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*’s estate in the interest of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* in accordance with applicable law, so that creditors of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* have no recourse to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* held in custody by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*, in particular in the event of insolvency.

*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service provider]]* shall ensure that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* held in custody are operationally segregated from the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*’s estate.
## 8.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]* shall be liable to their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* for the loss of any *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or of the means of access to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* as a result of an incident that is attributable to them. The liability of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* shall be capped at the market value of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* that was lost, at the time the loss occurred.

Incidents not attributable to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* include any event in respect of which the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* demonstrates that it occurred independently of the provision of the relevant service, or independently of the operations of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*, such as a problem inherent in the operation of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(2)|distributed ledger]]* that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* does not control.
## 9.
If *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]* make use of other *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* of that service, they shall only make use of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* authorised in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.59|Article 59]].

*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]* and that make use of other *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* of that service shall inform their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* thereof.
