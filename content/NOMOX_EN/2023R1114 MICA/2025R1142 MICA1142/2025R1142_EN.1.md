# Article 1 - Definitions


For the purposes of this Regulation, the following definitions apply:
## (1)
‘connected person’ means any of the persons referred to in [[2025R1142_EN.72#1|Article 72(1)]], points (a)(i) to (a)(iv), of Regulation (EU) 2023/1114;
## (2)
‘remuneration’ means any form of payment or other financial or non-financial benefits provided directly or indirectly by crypto-asset service providers in connection with the provision of crypto-asset services to clients;
## (3)
‘group’ means a group as defined in [[2025R1142_EN.2|Article 2]], point (11), of Directive 2013/34/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council
