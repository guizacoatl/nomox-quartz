# Article 30 - Ongoing information to holders of asset-referenced tokens

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall in a clear, accurate and transparent manner disclose, in a publicly and easily accessible place on their website, the amount of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* in circulation, and the value and composition of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(32)|reserve of assets]]* referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.36|Article 36]]. Such information shall be updated at least monthly.
## 2.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall publish as soon as possible in a publicly and easily accessible place on their website a brief, clear, accurate and transparent summary of the audit report, as well as the full and unredacted audit report, in relation to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(32)|reserve of assets]]* referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.36|Article 36]].
## 3.
Without prejudice to [[2023R1114_EN.88|Article 88]], *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall as soon as possible and in a clear, accurate and transparent manner disclose, in a publicly and easily accessible place, on their website any event that has or is likely to have a significant effect on the value of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or on the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(32)|reserve of assets]]* referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.36|Article 36]].
