# Article 70 - Safekeeping of clients’ crypto-assets and funds

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* that hold *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* belonging to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* or the means of access to such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* shall make adequate arrangements to safeguard the ownership rights of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*, especially in the event of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*’s insolvency, and to prevent the use of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*’ *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* for their own account.
## 2.
Where their business models or the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* require holding *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*’ *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* shall have adequate arrangements in place to safeguard the ownership rights of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* and prevent the use of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*’ *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* for their own account.
## 3.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* shall, by the end of the business day following the day on which *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*’ *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* were received, place those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* with a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]* or a central bank.

*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* shall take all necessary steps to ensure that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*’ *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* held with a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]* or a central bank are held in an account separately identifiable from any accounts used to hold *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* belonging to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*.
## 4.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* may themselves, or through a third party, provide *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(41)|payment services]]* related to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset service]]* they offer provided that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* itself, or the third party, is authorised to provide those services under Directive (EU) 2015/2366.

Where *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(41)|payment services]]* are provided, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* shall inform their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* of all of the following:
### (a)
the nature and terms and conditions of those services, including references to the applicable national law and to the rights of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*;
### (b)
whether those services are provided by them directly or by a third party.
## 5.
Paragraphs 2 and 3 of this Article shall not apply to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* that are *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(43)|electronic money institutions]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(46)|payment institutions]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]*.
