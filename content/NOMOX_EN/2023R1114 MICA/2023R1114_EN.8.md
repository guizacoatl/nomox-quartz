# Article 8 - Notification of the crypto-asset white paper and of the marketing communications

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|Offerors]]*, persons seeking admission to trading, or operators of trading platforms for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall notify their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*.
## 2.
Marketing communications shall, upon request, be notified to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* and to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member State]]*, when addressing prospective holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* in those Member States.
## 3.
Competent authorities shall not require prior approval of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white papers, nor of any marketing communications relating thereto, before their respective publication.
## 4.
The notification of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper referred to in paragraph 1 shall be accompanied by an explanation of why the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* described in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper should not be considered to be:
### (a)
a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* excluded from the scope of this Regulation pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.2#4|Article 2(4)]];
### (b)
an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*; or
### (c)
an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*.
## 5.
The elements referred in paragraphs 1 and 4 shall be notified to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* at least 20 working days before the date of publication of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper.
## 6.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|Offerors]]* and persons seeking admission to trading of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall, together with the notification referred to in paragraph 1, provide the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* with a list of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member States]]*, if any, where they intend to offer their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* to the public or intend to seek admission to trading. They shall also inform the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* of the starting date of the intended *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or intended admission to trading and of any change to that date.

The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* shall notify the single point of contact of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member States]]* of the intended *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or the intended admission to trading and communicate to that single point of contact the corresponding *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper within five working days of receipt of the list of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member States]]* referred to in the first subparagraph.
## 7.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* shall communicate to ESMA the information referred to in paragraphs 1, 2 and 4 as well as the starting date of the intended *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or intended admission to trading and of any change to that date. It shall communicate such information within five working days of receipt thereof from the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or the person seeking admission to trading.

ESMA shall make the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper available in the register, under [[2023R1114_EN.109#2|Article 109(2)]], by the starting date of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or admission to trading.
