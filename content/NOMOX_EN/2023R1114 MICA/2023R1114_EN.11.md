# Article 11 - Rights of offerors and persons seeking admission to trading of crypto-assets other than asset-referenced tokens or e-money tokens

## 1.
After publication of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.9|Article 9]] and, where applicable, of the modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.12|Article 12]], *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offerors]]* may offer *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* throughout the Union and such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* may be admitted to trading on a trading platform for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* in the Union.
## 2.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|Offerors]]* and persons seeking admission to trading of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* that have published a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.9|Article 9]] and, where applicable, a modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.12|Article 12]], shall not be subject to any further information requirements with regard to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or the admission to trading of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]*.
