# Article 102 - Precautionary measures

## 1.
Where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member State]]* has clear and demonstrable grounds for suspecting that there are irregularities in the activities of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or person seeking admission to trading of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*, or a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*, it shall notify the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* and ESMA thereof.

Where the irregularities referred to in the first subparagraph concern an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*, or a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset service]]* related to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member State]]* shall also notify EBA.
## 2.
Where, despite the measures taken by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*, the irregularities referred to in paragraph 1 persist, amounting to an infringement of this Regulation, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member State]]*, after informing the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*, ESMA and, where appropriate, EBA, shall take appropriate measures in order to protect *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* and holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, in particular *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|retail holders]]*. Such measures include preventing the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*, person seeking admission to trading, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* or the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* from conducting further activities in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member State]]*. The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall inform ESMA and, where appropriate, EBA thereof without undue delay. ESMA, and, where involved, EBA, shall inform the Commission accordingly without undue delay.
## 3.
Where a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* disagrees with any of the measures taken by a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member State]]* pursuant to paragraph 2 of this Article, it may bring the matter to the attention of ESMA. [[2010R1095_EN.19#4|Article 19(4) of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] shall apply in such situations

mutatis mutandis

.

By way of derogation from the first subparagraph of this paragraph, where the measures referred to in paragraph 2 of this Article concern an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*, or a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset service]]* related to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member State]]* may bring the matter to the attention of EBA. [[2010R1093_EN.19#4|Article 19(4) of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]] shall apply in such situations

mutatis mutandis

.
