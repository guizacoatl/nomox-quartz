# Article 106 - Coordination with ESMA or EBA

## 1.
ESMA or, for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, EBA, shall perform a facilitating and coordinating role in relation to measures taken by competent authorities pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.105|Article 105]]. ESMA or, for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, EBA, shall ensure that measures taken by a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* are justified and proportionate and that a consistent approach is taken by competent authorities, where appropriate.
## 2.
After receiving notification in accordance with Article 105(3) of any measure to be taken pursuant to that Article, ESMA or, for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, EBA, shall issue an opinion on whether the prohibition or restriction is justified and proportionate. If ESMA or, for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, EBA, considers that the taking of a measure by other competent authorities is necessary to address the risk, it shall state this in its opinion. The opinion shall be published on the website of ESMA or, for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, EBA.
## 3.
Where a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* proposes to take, or takes or declines to take measures contrary to an opinion issued by ESMA or EBA pursuant to paragraph 2, it shall immediately publish on its website a notice fully explaining its reasons therefor.
