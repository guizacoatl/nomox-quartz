# Article 59 - Authorisation

## 1.
A person shall not provide *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]*, within the Union, unless that person is:
### (a)
a legal person or other undertaking that has been authorised as *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.63|Article 63]]; or
### (b)
a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]*, central securities depository, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firm]]*, market operator, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(43)|electronic money institution]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(47)|UCITS management company]]*, or an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(48)|alternative investment fund manager]]* that is allowed to provide *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.60|Article 60]].
## 2.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* authorised in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.63|Article 63]] shall have a registered office in a Member State where they carry out at least part of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]*. They shall have their place of effective management in the Union and at least one of the directors shall be resident in the Union.
## 3.
For the purposes of paragraph 1, point (a), other undertakings that are not legal persons shall only provide *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* if their legal form ensures a level of protection for third parties’ interests equivalent to that afforded by legal persons and if they are subject to equivalent prudential supervision appropriate to their legal form.
## 4.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* authorised in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.63|Article 63]] shall at all times meet the conditions for their authorisation.
## 5.
A person who is not a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* shall not use a name, or a corporate name, or issue marketing communications or undertake any other process suggesting that it is a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* or that is likely to create confusion in that respect.
## 6.
Competent authorities that grant authorisations in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.63|Article 63]] shall ensure that such authorisations specify the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* are authorised to provide.
## 7.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* shall be allowed to provide *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* throughout the Union, either through the right of establishment, including through a branch, or through the freedom to provide services. *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* that provide *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* on a cross-border basis shall not be required to have a physical presence in the territory of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member State]]*.
## 8.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* seeking to add *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* to their authorisation as referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.63|Article 63]] shall request the competent authorities that granted their initial authorisation for an extension of their authorisation by complementing and updating the information referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.62|Article 62]]. The request for extension shall be processed in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.63|Article 63]].
