# Article 101 - Data protection


With regard to the processing of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(45)|personal data]]* within the framework of this Regulation, competent authorities shall carry out their tasks for the purposes of this Regulation in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679.

The processing of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(45)|personal data]]* by EBA and ESMA for the purposes of this Regulation shall be carried out in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2018/1725.
