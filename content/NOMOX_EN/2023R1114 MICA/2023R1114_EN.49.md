# Article 49 - Issuance and redeemability of e-money tokens

## 1.
By way of derogation from Article 11 of Directive 2009/110/EC, in respect of the issuance and redeemability of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* only the requirements set out in this Article shall apply to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*.
## 2.
Holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall have a claim against the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*.
## 3.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall issue *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* at par value and on the receipt of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]*.
## 4.
Upon request by a holder of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* shall redeem it, at any time and at par value, by paying in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]*, other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(44)|electronic money]]*, the monetary value of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* held to the holder of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*.
## 5.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall prominently state the conditions for redemption in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper as referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.51#1|Article 51(1)]], first subparagraph, point (d).
## 6.
Without prejudice to [[2023R1114_EN.46|Article 46]], the redemption of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall not be subject to a fee.
