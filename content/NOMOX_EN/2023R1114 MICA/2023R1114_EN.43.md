# Article 43 - Classification of asset-referenced tokens as significant asset-referenced tokens

## 1.
The criteria for classifying *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* as significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall be the following, as further specified by the delegated acts adopted pursuant to paragraph 11:
### (a)
the number of holders of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* is larger than 10 million;
### (b)
the value of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* issued, its market capitalisation or the size of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(32)|reserve of assets]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* is higher than EUR 5 000 000 000 ;
### (c)
the average number and average aggregate value of transactions in that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* per day during the relevant period, is higher than 2,5 million transactions and EUR 500 000 000  respectively;
### (d)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* is a provider of core platform services designated as a gatekeeper in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2022/1925 of the European Parliament and of the Council;
### (e)
the significance of the activities of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* on an international scale, including the use of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* for payments and remittances;
### (f)
the interconnectedness of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* with the financial system;
### (g)
the fact that the same *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* issues at least one additional *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*, and provides at least one *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset service]]*.
## 2.
EBA shall classify *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* as significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* where at least three of the criteria set out in paragraph 1 of this Article are met:
### (a)
during the period covered by the first report of information as referred to in paragraph 4 of this Article, following authorisation pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.21|Article 21]] or after approval of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.17|Article 17]]; or
### (b)
during the period covered by at least two consecutive reports of information as referred to in paragraph 4 of this Article.
## 3.
Where several *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* issue the same *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, the fulfilment of the criteria set out in paragraph 1 shall be assessed after aggregating the data from those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]*.
## 4.
Competent authorities of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*’s *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* shall report to EBA and the ECB information relevant for the assessment of the fulfilment of the criteria set out in paragraph 1 of this Article, including, if applicable, the information received under [[2023R1114_EN.22|Article 22]], at least twice a year.

Where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* is established in a Member State whose *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]* is not the euro, or where an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]* of a Member State that is not the euro is referenced by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, competent authorities shall transmit the information referred to in the first subparagraph also to the central bank of that Member State.
## 5.
Where EBA concludes that an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* fulfils the criteria set out in paragraph 1 in accordance with paragraph 2, EBA shall prepare a draft decision to classify the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* as a significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* and notify that draft decision to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*’s *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*, to the ECB and, in the cases referred to in paragraph 4, second subparagraph, to the central bank of the Member State concerned.

*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*, their competent authorities, the ECB and, where applicable, the central bank of the Member State concerned shall have 20 working days from the date of notification of EBA’s draft decision to provide observations and comments in writing. EBA shall duly consider those observations and comments before adopting a final decision.
## 6.
EBA shall take its final decision on whether to classify an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* as a significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* within 60 working days of the date of notification referred to in paragraph 5 and immediately notify that decision to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* and its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]*.
## 7.
Where an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* has been classified as significant pursuant to a decision of EBA taken in accordance with paragraph 6, the supervisory responsibilities with respect to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of that significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall be transferred from the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*’s *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* to EBA within 20 working days of the date of notification of that decision.

EBA and the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall cooperate in order to ensure the smooth transition of supervisory competences.
## 8.
EBA shall annually reassess the classification of significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* on the basis of the available information, including from the reports referred to in paragraph 4 or the information received under [[2023R1114_EN.22|Article 22]].

Where EBA concludes that certain *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* no longer fulfil the criteria set out in paragraph 1 in accordance with paragraph 2, EBA shall prepare a draft decision to no longer classify the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* as significant and notify that draft decision to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*, to the ECB and, in the cases referred to in paragraph 4, second subparagraph, to the central bank of the Member State concerned.

*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*, their competent authorities, the ECB and the central bank referred in paragraph 4 shall have 20 working days from the date of notification of that draft decision to provide observations and comments in writing. EBA shall duly consider those observations and comments before adopting a final decision.
## 9.
EBA shall take its final decision on whether to no longer classify an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* as significant within 60 working days from the date of the notification referred to in paragraph 8 and immediately notify that decision to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]*.
## 10.
Where an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* is no longer classified as significant pursuant to a decision of EBA taken in accordance with paragraph 9, the supervisory responsibilities with respect to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall be transferred from EBA to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*’s *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* within 20 working days from the date of notification of that decision.

EBA and the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall cooperate in order to ensure the smooth transition of supervisory competences.
## 11.
The Commission shall adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.139|Article 139]] to supplement this Regulation by further specifying the criteria set out in paragraph 1 for an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* to be classified as significant and determine:
### (a)
the circumstances under which the activities of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* are deemed significant on an international scale outside the Union;
### (b)
the circumstances under which *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* shall be considered to be interconnected with the financial system;
### (c)
the content and format of information provided by competent authorities to EBA and the ECB under paragraph 4 of this Article and [[2023R1114_EN.56#3|Article 56(3)]].
