# Article 64 - Withdrawal of authorisation of a crypto-asset service provider

## 1.
Competent authorities shall withdraw the authorisation of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* if the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* does any of the following:
### (a)
has not used its authorisation within 12 months of the date of the authorisation;
### (b)
has expressly renounced its authorisation;
### (c)
has not provided *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* for nine consecutive months;
### (d)
has obtained its authorisation by irregular means, such as by making false statements in its application for authorisation;
### (e)
no longer meets the conditions under which the authorisation was granted and has not taken the remedial action requested by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* within the specified timeframe;
### (f)
fails to have in place effective systems, procedures and arrangements to detect and prevent money laundering and terrorist financing in accordance with Directive (EU) 2015/849;
### (g)
has seriously infringed this Regulation, including the provisions relating to the protection of holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*, or market integrity.
## 2.
Competent authorities may withdraw authorisation as a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* in any of the following situations:
### (a)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* has infringed the provisions of national law transposing Directive (EU) 2015/849;
### (b)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* has lost its authorisation as a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(46)|payment institution]]* or its authorisation as an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(43)|electronic money institution]]*, and that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* has failed to remedy the situation within 40 calendar days.
## 3.
Where a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* withdraws an authorisation as a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*, it shall notify ESMA and the single points of contact of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member States]]* without undue delay. ESMA shall make such information available in the register referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.109|Article 109]].
## 4.
Competent authorities may limit the withdrawal of authorisation to a particular *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset service]]*.
## 5.
Before withdrawing an authorisation as a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*, competent authorities shall consult the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of another Member State where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* concerned is:
### (a)
a subsidiary of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* authorised in that other Member State;
### (b)
a subsidiary of the parent undertaking of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* authorised in that other Member State;
### (c)
controlled by the same natural or legal persons who control a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* authorised in that other Member State.
## 6.
Before withdrawing an authorisation as a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*, competent authorities may consult the authority competent for supervising compliance of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* with the rules on anti-money laundering and counter-terrorist financing.
## 7.
EBA, ESMA and any *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member State]]* may at any time request that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* examine whether the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* still complies with the conditions under which the authorisation was granted, when there are grounds to suspect it may no longer be the case.
## 8.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* shall establish, implement and maintain adequate procedures ensuring the timely and orderly transfer of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*’ *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* to another *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* when an authorisation is withdrawn.
