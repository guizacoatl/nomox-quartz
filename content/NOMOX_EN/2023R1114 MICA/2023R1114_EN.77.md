# Article 77 - Exchange of crypto-assets for funds or other crypto-assets

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* exchanging *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* or other *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* shall establish a non-discriminatory commercial policy that indicates, in particular, the type of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* they agree to transact with and the conditions that shall be met by such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*.
## 2.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* exchanging *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* or other *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* shall publish a firm price of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or a method for determining the price of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* that they propose to exchange for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* or other *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, and any applicable limit determined by that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* on the amount to be exchanged.
## 3.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* exchanging *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* or other *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* shall execute *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* orders at the prices displayed at the time when the order for exchange is final. *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* shall inform their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* of the conditions for their order to be deemed final.
## 4.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* exchanging *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* or other *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* shall publish information about the transactions concluded by them, such as transaction volumes and prices.
