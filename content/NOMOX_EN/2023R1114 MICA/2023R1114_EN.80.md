# Article 80 - Reception and transmission of orders for crypto-assets on behalf of clients

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* receiving and transmitting orders for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* shall establish and implement procedures and arrangements that provide for the prompt and proper transmission of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* orders for execution on a trading platform for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or to another *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*.
## 2.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* receiving and transmitting orders for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* shall not receive any remuneration, discount or non-monetary benefit in return for routing orders received from *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* to a particular trading platform for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or to another *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*.
## 3.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* receiving and transmitting orders for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* shall not misuse information relating to pending *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* orders, and shall take all reasonable steps to prevent the misuse of such information by any of their employees.
