> Level 1 reference(s): [[2023R1114_EN.17#8]]# Article 9 - Approval of the crypto-asset white paper

## 1.
The competent authority shall notify the credit institution of its final decision regarding the approval of the crypto-asset white paper within 10 working days from the receipt of the new crypto-asset white paper referred to in [[2025R0296_EN.8#3|Article 8(3)]] or, where no changes to the crypto-asset white paper are requested, 10 working days from a positive opinion by the ECB or, where applicable, the relevant central bank as referred to in [[2025R0296_EN.17#5|Article 17(5)]], first subparagraph, of Regulation (EU) 2023/1114, or following the expiration of the period of 20 working days laid down in [[2025R0296_EN.17#5|Article 17(5)]], second subparagraph, of that Regulation without any opinion issued by the ECB or the relevant central bank.
## 2.
The crypto-asset white paper shall not be published until the competent authority of the home Member State has approved it.
