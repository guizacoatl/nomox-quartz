# Article 5 - Notification of completeness of a crypto-asset white paper

## 1.
Where, after the process set out in [[2025R0296_EN.4|Article 4]] is completed, the competent authority assesses the crypto-asset white paper as complete, it shall notify the credit institution of the completeness of the crypto-asset white paper. The notification shall set the date at which the crypto-asset white paper is deemed complete.
## 2.
Where, after the process set out in [[2025R0296_EN.4|Article 4]] is completed, the competent authority assesses the crypto-asset white paper as incomplete, it shall reject the application for approval of the crypto-asset white paper and notify its decision to the credit institution.
