# Article 9 - Publication of the crypto-asset white paper and of the marketing communications

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|Offerors]]* and persons seeking admission to trading of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall publish their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white papers and, where applicable, their marketing communications, on their website, which shall be publicly accessible, at a reasonable time in advance of, and in any event before the starting date of, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or the admission to trading of those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*. The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white papers and, where applicable, the marketing communications, shall remain available on the website of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offerors]]* or persons seeking admission trading for as long as the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* are held by the public.
## 2.
The published *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white papers and, where applicable, the marketing communications, shall be identical to the version notified to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.8|Article 8]] or, where applicable, to the version modified in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.12|Article 12]].
