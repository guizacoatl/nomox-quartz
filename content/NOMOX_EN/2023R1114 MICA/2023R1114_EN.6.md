## 1.
A *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall contain all of the following information, as further specified in Annex I:
### (a)
information about the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or the person seeking admission to trading;
### (b)
information about the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*, if different from the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or person seeking admission to trading;
### (c)
information about the operator of the trading platform in cases where it draws up the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper;
### (d)
information about the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* project;
### (e)
information about the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* or its admission to trading;
### (f)
information about the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]*;
### (g)
information on the rights and obligations attached to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]*;
### (h)
information on the underlying technology;
#### (i)
information on the risks;
### (j)
information on the principal adverse impacts on the climate and other environment-related adverse impacts of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(3)|consensus mechanism]]* used to issue the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]*.

In cases where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper is not drawn up by the persons referred to in the first subparagraph, points (a), (b) and (c), the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall also include the identity of the person that drew up the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and the reason why that particular person drew it up.
## 2.
All of the information listed in paragraph 1 shall be fair, clear and not misleading. The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall not contain material omissions and shall be presented in a concise and comprehensible form.
## 3.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall contain the following clear and prominent statement on the first page:

‘This *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper has not been approved by any *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* in any Member State of the European Union. The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* is solely responsible for the content of this *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper.’.

Where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper is drawn up by the person seeking admission to trading or by an operator of a trading platform, then, instead of ‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*’, a reference to ‘person seeking admission to trading’ or ‘operator of the trading platform’ shall be included in the statement referred to in the first subparagraph.
## 4.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall not contain any assertions as regards the future value of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]*, other than the statement referred to in paragraph 5.
## 5.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall contain a clear and unambiguous statement that:
### (a)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* may lose its value in part or in full;
### (b)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* may not always be transferable;
### (c)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* may not be liquid;
### (d)
where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* concerns a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(9)|utility token]]*, that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(9)|utility token]]* may not be exchangeable against the good or service promised in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper, especially in the case of a failure or discontinuation of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* project;
### (e)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* is not covered by the investor compensation schemes under Directive 97/9/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council;
### (f)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* is not covered by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(50)|deposit]]* guarantee schemes under Directive 2014/49/EU.
## 6.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall contain a statement from the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*, the person seeking admission to trading or the operator of the trading platform. That statement, which shall be inserted after the statement referred to in paragraph 3, shall confirm that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper complies with this Title and that, to the best of the knowledge of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]*, the information presented in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper is fair, clear and not misleading and the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper makes no omission likely to affect its import.
## 7.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall contain a summary, inserted after the statement referred to in paragraph 6, which shall in brief and non-technical language provide key information about the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* or the intended admission to trading. The summary shall be easily understandable and presented and laid out in a clear and comprehensive format, using characters of readable size. The summary of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall provide appropriate information about the characteristics of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* concerned in order to help prospective holders of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* to make an informed decision.

The summary shall contain a warning that:
### (a)
it should be read as an introduction to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper;
### (b)
the prospective holder should base any decision to purchase the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* on the content of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper as a whole and not on the summary alone;
### (c)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* does not constitute an offer or solicitation to purchase *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]* and that any such offer or solicitation can be made only by means of a prospectus or other offer documents pursuant to the applicable national law;
### (d)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper does not constitute a prospectus as referred to in Regulation (EU) 2017/1129 of the European Parliament and of the Council or any other offer document pursuant to Union or national law.
## 8.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall contain the date of its notification and a table of contents.
## 9.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall be drawn up in an official language of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*, or in a language customary in the sphere of international finance.

Where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* is also offered in a Member State other than the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall also be drawn up in an official language of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member State]]*, or in a language customary in the sphere of international finance.
## 10.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall be made available in a machine-readable format.
## 11.
ESMA, in cooperation with EBA, shall develop draft implementing technical standards to establish standard forms, formats and templates for the purposes of paragraph 10.

ESMA shall submit the draft implementing technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph to the Commission by 30 June 2024.

Power is conferred on the Commission to adopt the implementing technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
## 12.
ESMA, in cooperation with EBA, shall develop draft regulatory technical standards on the content, methodologies and presentation of the information referred to in paragraph 1, first subparagraph, point (j), in respect of the sustainability indicators in relation to adverse impacts on the climate and other environment‐related adverse impacts.

When developing the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph, ESMA shall consider the various types of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(3)|consensus mechanisms]]* used to validate transactions in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, their incentive structures and the use of energy, renewable energy and natural resources, the production of waste and greenhouse gas emissions. ESMA shall update those regulatory technical standards in the light of regulatory and technological developments.

ESMA shall submit the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph to the Commission by 30 June 2024.

Power is delegated to the Commission to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
