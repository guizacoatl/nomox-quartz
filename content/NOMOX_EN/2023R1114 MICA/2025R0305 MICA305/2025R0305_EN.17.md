> Level 1 reference(s): [[2023R1114_EN.62#5]]# Article 17 - Transfer services


For the purposes of [[2025R0305_EN.62#2|Article 62(2)]], point (r), of Regulation (EU) 2023/1114, applicants that intend to provide transfer services for crypto-assets on behalf of clients shall provide to the competent authority all of the following information:
### (a)
details on the types of crypto-assets for which the applicant intends to provide transfer services;
### (b)
a detailed description of the arrangements put in place by the applicant to comply with [[2023R1114_EN.82|Article 82 of Regulation (EU) 1114/2023]], including detailed information on the applicant’s arrangements and deployed ICT and human resources to address risks promptly, efficiently and thoroughly during the provision of transfer services for crypto-assets on behalf of clients, taking into account potential operational failures and cybersecurity risks;
### (c)
where available, a description of the applicant’s insurance policy, including on the insurance’s coverage of detriment to client’s crypto-assets that may result from cyber security risks;
### (d)
arrangements to ensure that clients are adequately informed about the policies, procedures and arrangements referred to in point (b).
