# Article 12 - Modification of published crypto-asset white papers and of published marketing communications

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|Offerors]]*, persons seeking admission to trading or operators of a trading platform for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall modify their published *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white papers and, where applicable, their published marketing communications, whenever there is a significant new factor, material mistake or material inaccuracy that is capable of affecting the assessment of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*. That requirement shall apply for the duration of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or for as long as the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* is admitted to trading.
## 2.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|Offerors]]*, persons seeking admission to trading or operators of a trading platform for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall notify their modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white papers and, where applicable, modified marketing communications, and the intended publication date, to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*, including the reasons for such modification, at least seven working days before their publication.
## 3.
On the date of publication, or earlier if required by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]*, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*, the person seeking admission to trading or the operator of the trading platform shall immediately inform the public on its website of the notification of a modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper with the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* and shall provide a summary of the reasons for which it has notified a modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper.
## 4.
The order of the information in a modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and, where applicable, in modified marketing communications, shall be consistent with that of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper or marketing communications published in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.9|Article 9]].
## 5.
Within five working days of receipt of the modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and, where applicable, of the modified marketing communications, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* shall notify the modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and, where applicable, the modified marketing communications to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member States]]* referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.8#6|Article 8(6)]] and communicate the notification and the date of publication to ESMA.

ESMA shall make the modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper available in the register, under [[2023R1114_EN.109#2|Article 109(2)]], upon publication.
## 6.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|Offerors]]*, persons seeking admission to trading or operators of trading platforms for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall publish the modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and, where applicable, the modified marketing communications, including the reasons for such modification, on their website in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.9|Article 9]].
## 7.
The modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and, where applicable, the modified marketing communications, shall be time-stamped. The most recent modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and, where applicable, the modified marketing communications shall be marked as the applicable version. All modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white papers and, where applicable, modified marketing communications shall remain available for as long as the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* are held by the public.
## 8.
Where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* concerns a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(9)|utility token]]* providing access to goods and services that do not yet exist or are not yet in operation, changes made in the modified *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and, where applicable, the modified marketing communications, shall not extend the time limit of 12 months referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.4#6|Article 4(6)]].
## 9.
Older versions of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and the marketing communications shall remain publicly available on the website of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offerors]]*, persons seeking admission to trading, or operators of trading platforms, for at least 10 years after the date of publication of those older versions, with a prominent warning stating that they are no longer valid and with a hyperlink to the dedicated section on the website where the most recent version of those documents is published.
