# Article 10 - Entrustment of tasks among the college members

## 1.
The members of the college shall exchange views on a possible voluntary entrustment of tasks among them under [[2025R0297_EN.119#5|Article 119(5)]], point (c), of Regulation (EU) 2023/1114.
## 2.
The entrustment of the tasks referred to in paragraph 1 may also include all or some of the tasks mentioned in [[2025R0297_EN.119#7|Article 119(7)]], points (b) to (e), of Regulation (EU) 2023/1114.
## 3.
The agreement referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.119#6|Article 119(6) of Regulation (EU) 1114/2023]] shall specify a description of the arrangements on entrustment of tasks referred to in paragraph 1, where relevant.
