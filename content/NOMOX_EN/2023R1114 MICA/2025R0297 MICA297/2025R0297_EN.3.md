> Level 1 reference(s): [[2023R1114_EN.119#8]]# Article 3 - Reference period and transactions

## 1.
The reference period referred to in Articles [[2025R0297_EN.1|1]] and [[2025R0297_EN.2|2]] shall be the most recent six-month period covered by the reporting obligation referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.22#1|Article 22(1) of Regulation (EU) 1114/2023]].
## 2.
For the purpose of [[2025R0297_EN.1#2|Article 1(2)]] and (4), and of [[2025R0297_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (b), ‘transaction’ shall mean any change of the natural or legal person entitled to an asset-referenced token or an e-money token as a result of the transfer of that token from one distributed ledger address or account to another.
