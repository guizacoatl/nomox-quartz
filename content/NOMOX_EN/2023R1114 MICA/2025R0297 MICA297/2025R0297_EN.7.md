> Level 1 reference(s): [[2023R1114_EN.119#8]]# Article 7 - Establishment and update of a contact list

## 1.
The chair of the college shall maintain a contact list of the members of the college, including full contact details, and communicate it to the members of the college.
## 2.
The members of the college shall provide their contact details to the chair of the college and shall inform the chair of any changes in those details without undue delay.
## 3.
Any updated version of the contact list shall be communicated by the chair of the college to the members of the college without undue delay.
