# Article 54 - Investment of funds received in exchange for e-money tokens


*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|Funds]]* received by *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* in exchange for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* and safeguarded in accordance with Article 7(1) of Directive 2009/110/EC shall comply with the following:
### (a)
at least 30 % of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* received is always deposited in separate accounts in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institutions]]*;
### (b)
the remaining *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* received are invested in secure, low-risk assets that qualify as highly liquid *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]* with minimal market risk, credit risk and concentration risk, in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.38#1|Article 38(1)]] of this Regulation, and are denominated in the same *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]* as the one referenced by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*.
