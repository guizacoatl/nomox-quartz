# Article 78 - Execution of orders for crypto-assets on behalf of clients

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* executing orders for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* shall take all necessary steps to obtain, while executing orders, the best possible result for their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* taking into account factors of price, costs, speed, likelihood of execution and settlement, size, nature, conditions of custody of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or any other consideration relevant to the execution of the order.

Notwithstanding the first subparagraph, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* executing orders for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* shall not be required to take the necessary steps as referred to in the first subparagraph in cases where they execute orders for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* following specific instructions given by its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*.
## 2.
To ensure compliance with paragraph 1, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* executing orders for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* shall establish and implement effective execution arrangements. In particular, they shall establish and implement an order execution policy to allow them to comply with paragraph 1. The order execution policy shall, amongst others, provide for the prompt, fair and expeditious execution of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* orders and prevent the misuse by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*’ employees of any information relating to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* orders.
## 3.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* executing orders for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* shall provide appropriate and clear information to their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* on their order execution policy referred to in paragraph 2 and any significant change thereto. That information shall explain clearly, in sufficient detail and in a way that can be easily understood by *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*, how *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* orders are to be executed by *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*. *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* shall obtain prior consent from each *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* regarding the order execution policy.
## 4.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* executing orders for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* shall be able to demonstrate to their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*, at their request, that they have executed their orders in accordance with their order execution policy and shall be able to demonstrate to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]*, at the latter’s request, their compliance with this Article.
## 5.
Where the order execution policy provides for the possibility that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* orders might be executed outside a trading platform, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* executing orders for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* shall inform their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* about that possibility and shall obtain the prior express consent of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* before proceeding to execute their orders outside a trading platform, either in the form of a general agreement or with respect to individual transactions.
## 6.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* executing orders for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* shall monitor the effectiveness of their order execution arrangements and order execution policy in order to identify and, where appropriate, correct any deficiencies in that respect. In particular, they shall assess, on a regular basis, whether the execution venues included in the order execution policy provide for the best possible result for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* or whether they need to make changes to their order execution arrangements. *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* executing orders for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* shall notify *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* with whom they have an ongoing *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* relationship of any material changes to their order execution arrangements or order execution policy.
