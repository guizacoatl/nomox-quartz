# Article 81 - Providing advice on crypto-assets and providing portfolio management of crypto-assets

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(25)|providing portfolio management of crypto-assets]]* shall assess whether the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* are suitable for their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* or prospective *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*, taking into consideration their knowledge and experience in investing in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, their investment objectives, including risk tolerance, and their financial situation including their ability to bear losses.
## 2.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]* shall, in good time before *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]*, inform prospective *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* whether the advice is:
### (a)
provided on an independent basis;
### (b)
based on a broad or on a more restricted analysis of different *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, including whether the advice is limited to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* issued or offered by entities having *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(31)|close links]]* with the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* or any other legal or economic relationships, such as contractual relationships, that risk impairing the independence of the advice provided.
## 3.
Where a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]* informs the prospective *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* that advice is provided on an independent basis, it shall:
### (a)
assess a sufficient range of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* available on the market which must be sufficiently diverse to ensure that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*’s investment objectives can be suitably met and which must not be limited to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* issued or provided by:
#### (i)
that same *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*;
#### (ii)
entities having *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(31)|close links]]* with that same *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*; or
#### (iii)
other entities with which that same *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* has such close legal or economic relationships, such as contractual relationships, as to pose a risk of impairing the independent basis of the advice provided;
### (b)
not accept and retain fees, commissions or any monetary or non-monetary benefits paid or provided by any third party or a person acting on behalf of a third party in relation to the provision of the service to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*.

Notwithstanding point (b) of the first subparagraph, minor non-monetary benefits that are capable of enhancing the quality of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* provided to a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* and that are of such a scale and nature that they do not impair compliance with a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*’s obligation to act in the best interests of its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* shall be permitted in cases where they are clearly disclosed to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*.
## 4.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]* shall also provide prospective *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* with information on all costs and related charges, including the cost of advice, where applicable, the cost of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* recommended or marketed to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* and how the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* is permitted to pay for the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, also encompassing any third-party payments.
## 5.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(25)|providing portfolio management of crypto-assets]]* shall not accept and retain fees, commissions or any monetary or non-monetary benefits paid or provided by an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*, person seeking admission to trading, or any third party, or a person acting on behalf of a third party, in relation to the provision of portfolio management of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* to their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*.
## 6.
Where a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* informs a prospective *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* that its advice is provided on a non-independent basis, that provider may receive inducements subject to the conditions that the payment or benefit:
### (a)
is designed to enhance the quality of the relevant service to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*; and
### (b)
does not impair compliance with the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*’s obligation to act honestly, fairly and professionally in accordance with the best interests of its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*.

The existence, nature and amount of the payment or benefit referred to in paragraph 4, or, where the amount cannot be ascertained, the method of calculating that amount, shall be clearly disclosed to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*, in a manner that is comprehensive, accurate and understandable, prior to the provision of the relevant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset service]]*.
## 7.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]* shall ensure that natural persons giving advice or information about *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, or a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset service]]*, on their behalf possess the necessary knowledge and competence to fulfil their obligations. Member States shall publish the criteria to be used for assessing such knowledge and competence.
## 8.
For the purposes of the suitability assessment referred to in paragraph 1, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(25)|providing portfolio management of crypto-assets]]* shall obtain from their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* or prospective *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* the necessary information regarding their knowledge of, and experience in, investing, including in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, their investment objectives, including risk tolerance, their financial situation including their ability to bear losses, and their basic understanding of the risks involved in purchasing *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, so as to enable *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* to recommend to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* or prospective *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* whether or not the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* are suitable for them and, in particular, are in accordance with their risk tolerance and ability to bear losses.
## 9.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]* or portfolio management of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* shall warn *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* or prospective *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* that:
### (a)
the value of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* might fluctuate;
### (b)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* might be subject to full or partial losses;
### (c)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* might not be liquid;
### (d)
where applicable, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* are not covered by the investor compensation schemes under Directive 97/9/EC;
### (e)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* are not covered by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(50)|deposit]]* guarantee schemes under Directive 2014/49/EU.
## 10.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]* or portfolio management of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* shall establish, maintain and implement policies and procedures to enable them to collect and assess all information necessary to conduct the assessment referred to in paragraph 1 for each *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*. They shall take all reasonable steps to ensure that the information collected about their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* or prospective *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* is reliable.
## 11.
Where *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* do not provide the information required pursuant to paragraph 8, or where *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]* or portfolio management of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* consider that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* are not suitable for their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*, they shall not recommend such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, nor begin the provision of portfolio management of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*.
## 12.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]* or portfolio management of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* shall regularly review for each *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* the suitability assessment referred to in paragraph 1 at least every two years after the initial assessment made in accordance with that paragraph.
## 13.
Once the suitability assessment referred to in paragraph 1 or its review under paragraph 12 has been performed, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]* shall provide *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* with a report on suitability specifying the advice given and how that advice meets the preferences, objectives and other characteristics of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*. That report shall be made and communicated to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* in an electronic format. That report shall, as a minimum:
### (a)
include an updated information on the assessment referred to in paragraph 1; and
### (b)
provide an outline of the advice given.

The report on suitability referred to in the first subparagraph shall make clear that the advice is based on the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*’s knowledge and experience in investing in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*’s investment objectives, risk tolerance, financial situation and ability to bear losses.
## 14.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(25)|providing portfolio management of crypto-assets]]* shall provide periodic statements to their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*, in an electronic format, of the portfolio management activities carried out on their behalf. Those periodic statements shall contain a fair and balanced review of the activities undertaken and of the performance of the portfolio during the reporting period, an updated statement of how the activities undertaken meet the preferences, objectives and other characteristics of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*, as well as an updated information on the suitability assessment referred to in paragraph 1 or its review under paragraph 12.

The periodic statement referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph shall be provided every three months, except in cases where a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* has access to an online system where up-to-date valuations of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*’s portfolio and updated information on the suitability assessment referred to in paragraph 1 can be accessed, and the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* has evidence that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* has accessed a valuation at least once during the relevant quarter. Such online system shall be deemed an electronic format.
## 15.
ESMA shall, by 30 December 2024, issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] specifying:
### (a)
the criteria for the assessment of knowledge and competence in accordance with paragraph 7;
### (b)
the information referred to in paragraph 8; and
### (c)
the format of the periodic statement referred to in paragraph 14.
