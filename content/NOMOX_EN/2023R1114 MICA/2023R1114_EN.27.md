# Article 27 - Obligation to act honestly, fairly and professionally in the best interest of the holders of asset-referenced tokens

## 1.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall act honestly, fairly and professionally and shall communicate with the holders and prospective holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* in a fair, clear and not misleading manner.
## 2.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall act in the best interests of the holders of such tokens and shall treat them equally, unless any preferential treatment is disclosed in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper and, where applicable, the marketing communications.
