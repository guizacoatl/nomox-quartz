# Article 24 - Withdrawal of the authorisation

## 1.
Competent authorities shall withdraw the authorisation of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* in any of the following situations:
### (a)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* has ceased to engage in business for six consecutive months, or has not used its authorisation for 12 consecutive months;
### (b)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* has obtained its authorisation by irregular means, such as by making false statements in the application for authorisation referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.18|Article 18]] or in any *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper modified in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.25|Article 25]];
### (c)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* no longer meets the conditions under which the authorisation was granted;
### (d)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* has seriously infringed the provisions of this Title;
### (e)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* has been subject to a redemption plan;
### (f)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* has expressly renounced its authorisation or has decided to cease operations;
### (g)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*’s activity poses a serious threat to market integrity, financial stability, the smooth operation of payment systems or exposes the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* or the sector to serious risks of money laundering and terrorist financing.

The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall notify its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of any of the situations referred to in the first subparagraph, points (e) and (f).
## 2.
Competent authorities shall also withdraw the authorisation of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* when the ECB or, where applicable, the central bank referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.20#4|Article 20(4)]], issues an opinion that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* poses a serious threat to the smooth operation of payment systems, monetary policy transmission or monetary sovereignty.
## 3.
Competent authorities shall limit the amount of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* to be issued or impose a minimum denomination amount in respect of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* when the ECB or, where applicable, the central bank referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.20#4|Article 20(4)]], issues an opinion that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* poses a threat to the smooth operation of payment systems, monetary policy transmission or monetary sovereignty, and specify the applicable limit or minimum denomination amount.
## 4.
The relevant competent authorities shall notify the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, without delay, of the following situations:
### (a)
a third-party entity as referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.34#5|Article 34(5)]], first subparagraph, point (h), of this Regulation has lost its authorisation as a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]* as referred to in [[2013L0036_EN.8|Article 8 of Directive 2013/36/EU]], as a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* as referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.59|Article 59]] of this Regulation, as a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(46)|payment institution]]*, or as an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(43)|electronic money institution]]*;
### (b)
the members of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*’s *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]* or shareholders or members, whether direct or indirect, that have *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(36)|qualifying holdings]]* in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* have infringed the provisions of national law transposing Directive (EU) 2015/849.
## 5.
Competent authorities shall withdraw the authorisation of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* where they are of the opinion that the situations referred to in paragraph 4 of this Article affect the good repute of the members of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]* of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* or the good repute of any shareholders or members, whether direct or indirect, that have *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(36)|qualifying holdings]]* in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*, or if there is an indication of a failure of the governance arrangements or internal control mechanisms as referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.34|Article 34]].

When the authorisation is withdrawn, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall implement the procedure under [[2023R1114_EN.47|Article 47]].
## 6.
Competent authorities shall, within two working days of withdrawing authorisation, communicate to ESMA the withdrawal of the authorisation of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*. ESMA shall make the information on such withdrawal available in the register referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.109|Article 109]] without undue delay.
