# Article 23 - Restrictions on the issuance of asset-referenced tokens used widely as a means of exchange

## 1.
Where, for an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, the estimated quarterly average number and average aggregate value of transactions per day associated to its uses as a means of exchange within a single currency area is higher than 1 million transactions and EUR 200 000 000 , respectively, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* shall:
### (a)
stop issuing that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*; and
### (b)
within 40 working days of reaching that threshold, submit a plan to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* to ensure that the estimated quarterly average number and average aggregate value of those transactions per day is kept below 1 million transactions and EUR 200 000 000  respectively.
## 2.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall use the information provided by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*, its own estimates, or the estimates provided by the ECB or, where applicable, by the central bank referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.20#4|Article 20(4)]], whichever is higher, in order to assess whether the threshold referred to in paragraph 1 is reached.
## 3.
Where several *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* issue the same *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, the criteria referred in paragraph 1 shall be assessed by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* after aggregating the data from all *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]*.
## 4.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* shall submit the plan referred to in paragraph 1, point (b), for approval to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]*. Where necessary, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall require modifications, such as imposing a minimum denomination amount, in order to ensure a timely decrease of the use as a means of exchange of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*.
## 5.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall only allow the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* to issue the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* again when it has evidence that the estimated quarterly average number and average aggregated value of transactions per day associated to its uses as a means of exchange within a single currency area is lower than 1 million transactions and EUR 200 000 000  respectively.
