# Article 65 - Cross-border provision of crypto-asset services

## 1.
A *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* that intends to provide *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* in more than one Member State shall submit the following information to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*:
### (a)
a list of the Member States in which the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* intends to provide *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]*;
### (b)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* intends to provide on a cross-border basis;
### (c)
the starting date of the intended provision of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]*;
### (d)
a list of all other activities provided by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* not covered by this Regulation.
## 2.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* shall, within 10 working days of receipt of the information referred to in paragraph 1, communicate that information to the single points of contact of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member States]]*, to ESMA and to EBA.
## 3.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the Member State that granted authorisation shall inform the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* concerned of the communication referred to in paragraph 2 without delay.
## 4.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* may begin to provide *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* in a Member State other than its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]* from the date of receipt of the communication referred to in paragraph 3 or at the latest from the 15th calendar day after having submitted the information referred to in paragraph 1.
