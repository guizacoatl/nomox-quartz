# Article 144 - Amendment to Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010


In [[2010R1093_EN.1#2|Article 1(2) of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]], the first subparagraph is replaced by the following:
‘The Authority shall act within the powers conferred by this Regulation and within the scope of Directive 2002/87/EC, Directive 2008/48/EC, Directive 2009/110/EC, Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 (

*2
), Directive 2013/36/EU, Directive 2014/49/EU (

*4
), Directive 2014/92/EU, Directive (EU) 2015/2366 (

*6
), Regulation (EU) 2023/1114 of the European Parliament and of the Council and, to the extent that those acts apply to credit and financial institutions and the competent authorities that supervise them, within the relevant parts of Directive 2002/65/EC, including all directives, regulations, and decisions based on those acts, and of any further legally binding Union act which confers tasks on the Authority. The Authority shall also act in accordance with Council Regulation (EU) No 1024/2013 (

*8

).
