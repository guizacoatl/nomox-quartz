# Article 5 - Admission to trading of crypto-assets other than asset-referenced tokens or e-money tokens

## 1.
A person shall not seek admission to trading of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* other than an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* within the Union unless that person:
### (a)
is a legal person;
### (b)
has drawn up a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper in respect of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.6|Article 6]];
### (c)
has notified the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.8|Article 8]];
### (d)
has published the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.9|Article 9]];
### (e)
has drafted the marketing communications, if any, in respect of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.7|Article 7]];
### (f)
has published the marketing communications, if any, in respect of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.9|Article 9]];
### (g)
complies with the requirements for persons seeking admission to trading laid down in [[2023R1114_EN.14|Article 14]].
## 2.
When a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* is admitted to trading on the initiative of the operator of a trading platform and a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper has not been published in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.9|Article 9]] in the cases required by this Regulation, the operator of that trading platform for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* shall comply with the requirements set out in paragraph 1 of this Article.
## 3.
By way of derogation from paragraph 1, a person seeking admission to trading of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* other than an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* and the respective operator of the trading platform may agree in writing that it shall be the operator of the trading platform who is required to comply with all or part of the requirements referred to in paragraph 1, points (b) to (g).

The agreement in writing referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph shall clearly state that the person seeking admission to trading is required to provide the operator of the trading platform with all necessary information to enable that operator to satisfy the requirements referred to in paragraph 1, points (b) to (g), as applicable.
## 4.
Paragraph 1, points (b), (c) and (d), shall not apply where:
### (a)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* is already admitted to trading on another trading platform for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* in the Union; and
### (b)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper is drawn up in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.6|Article 6]], updated in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.12|Article 12]], and the person responsible for drawing up such white paper consents to its use in writing.
