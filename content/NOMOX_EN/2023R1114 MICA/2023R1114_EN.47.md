# Article 47 - Redemption plan

## 1.
An *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall draw up and maintain an operational plan to support the orderly redemption of each *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, which is to be implemented upon a decision by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* is unable or likely to be unable to fulfil its obligations, including in the case of insolvency or, where applicable, resolution or in the case of withdrawal of authorisation of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*, without prejudice to the commencement of a crisis prevention measure or crisis management measure as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], points (101) and (102), respectively, of Directive 2014/59/EU or a resolution action as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.2|Article 2]], point (11), of Regulation (EU) 2021/23 of the European Parliament and of the Council.
## 2.
The redemption plan shall demonstrate the ability of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* to carry out the redemption of the outstanding *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* issued without causing undue economic harm to its holders or to the stability of the markets of the reserve assets.

The redemption plan shall include contractual arrangements, procedures and systems, including the designation of a temporary administrator in accordance with applicable law, to ensure the equitable treatment of all holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and to ensure that holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* are paid in a timely manner with the proceeds from the sale of the remaining reserve assets.

The redemption plan shall ensure the continuity of any critical activities that are necessary for the orderly redemption and that are performed by *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* or by any third-party entity.
## 3.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall notify the redemption plan to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* within six months of the date of authorisation pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.21|Article 21]] or within six months of the date of approval of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.17|Article 17]]. The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall require amendments to the redemption plan where necessary to ensure its proper implementation and shall notify its decision requesting those amendments to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* within 40 working days of the date of notification of that plan. That decision shall be implemented by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* within 40 working days of the date of notification of that decision. The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* shall regularly review and update the redemption plan.
## 4.
Where applicable, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall notify the redemption plan to the resolution authority and prudential supervisory authority of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*.

The resolution authority may examine the redemption plan with a view to identifying any actions in the redemption plan that might adversely impact the resolvability of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*, and may make recommendations to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* in respect thereof.
## 5.
EBA shall issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1093_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]] to specify:
### (a)
the content of the redemption plan and the periodicity for review, taking into account the size, complexity and nature of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* and the business model of its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*; and
### (b)
the triggers for implementation of the redemption plan.
