# Article 4 - Offers to the public of crypto-assets other than asset-referenced tokens or e-money tokens

## 1.
A person shall not make an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* other than an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* in the Union unless that person:
### (a)
is a legal person;
### (b)
has drawn up a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper in respect of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.6|Article 6]];
### (c)
has notified the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.8|Article 8]];
### (d)
has published the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.9|Article 9]];
### (e)
has drafted the marketing communications, if any, in respect of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.7|Article 7]];
### (f)
has published the marketing communications, if any, in respect of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.9|Article 9]];
### (g)
complies with the requirements for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offerors]]* laid down in [[2023R1114_EN.14|Article 14]].
## 2.
Paragraph 1, points (b), (c), (d) and (f), shall not apply to any of the following offers to the public of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*:
### (a)
an offer to fewer than 150 natural or legal persons per Member State where such persons are acting on their own account;
### (b)
over a period of 12 months, starting with the beginning of the offer, the total consideration of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* in the Union does not exceed EUR 1 000 000 , or the equivalent amount in another *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]* or in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*;
### (c)
an offer of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* addressed solely to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(30)|qualified investors]]* where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* can only be held by such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(30)|qualified investors]]*.
## 3.
This Title shall not apply to offers to the public of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* where any of the following apply:
### (a)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* is offered for free;
### (b)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* is automatically created as a reward for the maintenance of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(2)|distributed ledger]]* or the validation of transactions;
### (c)
the offer concerns a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(9)|utility token]]* providing access to a good or service that exists or is in operation;
### (d)
the holder of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* has the right to use it only in exchange for goods and services in a limited network of merchants with contractual arrangements with the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*.

For the purposes of point (a) of the first subparagraph, a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* shall not be considered to be offered for free where purchasers are required to provide, or to undertake to provide, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(45)|personal data]]* to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* in exchange for that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]*, or where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* receives from prospective holders of that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* any fees, commissions, or monetary or non-monetary benefits in exchange for that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]*.

Where, for each 12-month period starting from the beginning of the initial *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]*, the total consideration of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* in the circumstances referred to in the first subparagraph, point (d), in the Union exceeds EUR 1 000 000 , the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* shall send a notification to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* containing a description of the offer and explaining why the offer is exempt from this Title pursuant to the first subparagraph, point (d).

Based on the notification referred to in the third subparagraph, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall take a duly justified decision where it considers that the activity does not qualify for an exemption as a limited network under the first subparagraph, point (d), and shall inform the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* accordingly.
## 4.
The exemptions listed in paragraphs 2 and 3 shall not apply where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*, or another person acting on the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*’s behalf, makes known in any communication its intention to seek admission to trading of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* other than an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*.
## 5.
Authorisation as a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.59|Article 59]] is not required for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]* or for providing transfer services for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* in relation to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* whose offers to the public are exempt pursuant to paragraph 3 of this Article, unless:
### (a)
there exists another *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the same *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* and that offer does not benefit from the exemption; or
### (b)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* offered is admitted to a trading platform.
## 6.
Where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* other than an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* concerns a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(9)|utility token]]* providing access to goods and services that do not yet exist or are not yet in operation, the duration of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* as described in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall not exceed 12 months from the date of publication of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper.
## 7.
Any subsequent *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* other than an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* shall be deemed a separate *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* to which the requirements of paragraph 1 apply, without prejudice to the possible application of paragraph 2 or 3 to the subsequent *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]*.

No additional *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper shall be required for any subsequent *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* other than an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* so long as a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper has been published in accordance with Articles [[2023R1114_EN.9|9]] and [[2023R1114_EN.12|12]], and the person responsible for drawing up such white paper consents to its use in writing.
## 8.
Where an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* other than an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* is exempt from the obligation to publish a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper under paragraph 2 or 3, but a white paper is nevertheless drawn up voluntarily, this Title shall apply.
