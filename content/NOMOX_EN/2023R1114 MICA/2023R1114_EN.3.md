# Article 3 - Definitions

## 1.
For the purposes of this Regulation, the following definitions apply:
## (1)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(1)|distributed ledger technology]]*’ or ‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(1)|DLT]]*’ means a technology that enables the operation and use of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(2)|distributed ledgers]]*;
## (2)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(2)|distributed ledger]]*’ means an information repository that keeps records of transactions and that is shared across, and synchronised between, a set of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(4)|DLT network nodes]]* using a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(3)|consensus mechanism]]*;
## (3)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(3)|consensus mechanism]]*’ means the rules and procedures by which an agreement is reached, among *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(4)|DLT network nodes]]*, that a transaction is validated;
## (4)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(4)|DLT network node]]*’ means a device or process that is part of a network and that holds a complete or partial replica of records of all transactions on a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(2)|distributed ledger]]*;
## (5)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]*’ means a digital representation of a value or of a right that is able to be transferred and stored electronically using *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(1)|distributed ledger technology]]* or similar technology;
## (6)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*’ means a type of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* that is not an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|electronic money token]]* and that purports to maintain a stable value by referencing another value or right or a combination thereof, including one or more official currencies;
## (7)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|electronic money token]]*’ or ‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*’ means a type of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* that purports to maintain a stable value by referencing the value of one *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]*;
## (8)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]*’ means an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]* of a country that is issued by a central bank or other monetary authority;
## (9)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(9)|utility token]]*’ means a type of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* that is only intended to provide access to a good or a service supplied by its *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*;
## (10)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*’ means a natural or legal person, or other undertaking, who issues *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*;
## (11)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*’ means an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* who applies for authorisation to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or seeks the admission to trading of those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*;
## (12)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]*’ means a communication to persons in any form, and by any means, presenting sufficient information on the terms of the offer and the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* to be offered so as to enable prospective holders to decide whether to purchase those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*;
## (13)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*’ means a natural or legal person, or other undertaking, or the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*, who offers *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* to the public;
## (14)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]*’ means *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.4|Article 4]], point (25), of Directive (EU) 2015/2366;
## (15)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*’ means a legal person or other undertaking whose occupation or business is the provision of one or more *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* on a professional basis, and that is allowed to provide *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.59|Article 59]];
## (16)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset service]]*’ means any of the following services and activities relating to any *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]*:
### (a)
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]*;
### (b)
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(18)|operation of a trading platform for crypto-assets]]*;
### (c)
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(19)|exchange of crypto-assets for funds]]*;
### (d)
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(20)|exchange of crypto-assets for other crypto-assets]]*;
### (e)
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(21)|execution of orders for crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]*;
### (f)
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(22)|placing of crypto-assets]]*;
### (g)
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(23)|reception and transmission of orders for crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]*;
### (h)
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]*;
#### (i)
providing portfolio management on *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*;
### (j)
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(26)|providing transfer services for crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]*;
## (17)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]*’ means the safekeeping or controlling, on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*, of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or of the means of access to such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, where applicable in the form of private cryptographic keys;
## (18)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(18)|operation of a trading platform for crypto-assets]]*’ means the management of one or more multilateral systems, which bring together or facilitate the bringing together of multiple third-party purchasing and selling interests in *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, in the system and in accordance with its rules, in a way that results in a contract, either by exchanging *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* or by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(20)|exchange of crypto-assets for other crypto-assets]]*;
## (19)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(19)|exchange of crypto-assets for funds]]*’ means the conclusion of purchase or sale contracts concerning *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* with *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(14)|funds]]* by using proprietary capital;
## (20)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(20)|exchange of crypto-assets for other crypto-assets]]*’ means the conclusion of purchase or sale contracts concerning *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* with *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* for other *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* by using proprietary capital;
## (21)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(21)|execution of orders for crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]*’ means the conclusion of agreements, on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]*, to purchase or sell one or more *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or the subscription on behalf of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* for one or more *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, and includes the conclusion of contracts to sell *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* at the moment of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or admission to trading;
## (22)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(22)|placing of crypto-assets]]*’ means the marketing, on behalf of or for the account of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or a party related to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]*, of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* to purchasers;
## (23)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(23)|reception and transmission of orders for crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]*’ means the reception from a person of an order to purchase or sell one or more *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or to subscribe for one or more *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* and the transmission of that order to a third party for execution;
## (24)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(24)|providing advice on crypto-assets]]*’ means offering, giving or agreeing to give personalised recommendations to a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*, either at the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*’s request or on the initiative of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* providing the advice, in respect of one or more transactions relating to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, or the use of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]*;
## (25)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(25)|providing portfolio management of crypto-assets]]*’ means managing portfolios in accordance with mandates given by *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* on a discretionary *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*-by-*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]* basis where such portfolios include one or more *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*;
## (26)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(26)|providing transfer services for crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]*’ means providing services of transfer, on behalf of a natural or legal person, of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* from one *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(2)|distributed ledger]]* address or account to another;
## (27)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]*’ means the body or bodies of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or person seeking admission to trading, or of a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*, which are appointed in accordance with national law, which are empowered to set the entity’s strategy, objectives and overall direction, and which oversee and monitor management decision-making in the entity and include the persons who effectively direct the business of the entity;
## (28)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]*’ means a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (1), of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 and authorised under Directive 2013/36/EU;
## (29)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firm]]*’ means an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firm]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (2), of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 and authorised under Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (30)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(30)|qualified investors]]*’ means persons or entities that are listed in Section I, points (1) to (4), of Annex II to Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (31)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(31)|close links]]*’ means *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(31)|close links]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (35), of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (32)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(32)|reserve of assets]]*’ means the basket of reserve assets securing the claim against the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*;
## (33)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*’ means:
### (a)
where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or person seeking admission to trading of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* has its registered office in the Union, the Member State where that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or person has its registered office;
### (b)
where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or person seeking admission to trading of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* has no registered office in the Union but does have one or more branches in the Union, the Member State chosen by that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or person from among the Member States where it has branches;
### (c)
where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or person seeking admission to trading of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* is established in a third country and has no branch in the Union, either the Member State where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* are intended to be offered to the public for the first time or, at the choice of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or person seeking admission to trading, the Member State where the first application for admission to trading of those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* is made;
### (d)
in the case of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*, the Member State where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* has its registered office;
### (e)
in the case of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, the Member State where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* is authorised as a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]* under Directive 2013/36/EU or as an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(43)|electronic money institution]]* under Directive 2009/110/EC;
### (f)
in the case of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*, the Member State where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* has its registered office;
## (34)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member State]]*’ means the Member State where an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or person seeking admission to trading has made an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* or is seeking admission to trading, or where a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* provides *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]*, where different from the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*;
## (35)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]*’ means one or more authorities:
### (a)
designated by each Member State in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.93|Article 93]] concerning *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offerors]]*, persons seeking admission to trading of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* other than *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* and *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*, or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]*;
### (b)
designated by each Member State for the application of Directive 2009/110/EC concerning *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*;
## (36)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(36)|qualifying holding]]*’ means any direct or indirect holding in an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or in a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* which represents at least 10 % of the capital or of the voting rights, as set out in Articles [[2023R1114_EN.9|9]] and [[2023R1114_EN.10|10]] of Directive 2004/109/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council, respectively, taking into account the conditions for the aggregation thereof laid down in [[2023R1114_EN.12#4|Article 12(4)]] and (5) of that Directive, or which makes it possible to exercise a significant influence over the management of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or the management of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* in which that holding subsists;
## (37)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|retail holder]]*’ means any natural person who is acting for purposes which are outside that person’s trade, business, craft or profession;
## (38)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(38)|online interface]]*’ means any software, including a website, part of a website or an application, that is operated by or on behalf of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(13)|offeror]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*, and which serves to give holders of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* access to their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* and to give *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|clients]]* access to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]*;
## (39)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(39)|client]]*’ means any natural or legal person to whom a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* provides *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]*;
## (40)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(40)|matched principal trading]]*’ means *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(40)|matched principal trading]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (38), of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (41)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(41)|payment services]]*’ means *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(41)|payment services]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.4|Article 4]], point (3), of Directive (EU) 2015/2366;
## (42)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(42)|payment service provider]]*’ means a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(42)|payment service provider]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.4|Article 4]], point (11), of Directive (EU) 2015/2366;
## (43)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(43)|electronic money institution]]*’ means an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(43)|electronic money institution]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.2|Article 2]], point (1), of Directive 2009/110/EC;
## (44)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(44)|electronic money]]*’ means *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(44)|electronic money]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.2|Article 2]], point (2), of Directive 2009/110/EC;
## (45)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(45)|personal data]]*’ means *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(45)|personal data]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.4|Article 4]], point (1), of Regulation (EU) 2016/679;
## (46)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(46)|payment institution]]*’ means a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(46)|payment institution]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.4|Article 4]], point (4), of Directive (EU) 2015/2366;
## (47)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(47)|UCITS management company]]*’ means a management company as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (b), of Directive 2009/65/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council;
## (48)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(48)|alternative investment fund manager]]*’ means an AIFM as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (b), of Directive 2011/61/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council;
## (49)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instrument]]*’ means *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(49)|financial instruments]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (15), of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (50)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(50)|deposit]]*’ means a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(50)|deposit]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (3), of Directive 2014/49/EU;
## (51)
‘*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(51)|structured deposit]]*’ means a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(51)|structured deposit]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (43), of Directive 2014/65/EU.
## 2.
The Commission shall adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.139|Article 139]] to supplement this Regulation by further specifying technical elements of the definitions laid down in paragraph 1 of this Article, and to adjust those definitions to market developments and technological developments.
