# Article 18 - Application for authorisation

## 1.
Legal persons or other undertakings that intend to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* or seek the admission to trading of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall submit their application for an authorisation referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.16|Article 16]] to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*.
## 2.
The application referred to in paragraph 1 shall contain all of the following information:
### (a)
the address of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*;
### (b)
the legal entity identifier of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*;
### (c)
the articles of association of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*, where applicable;
### (d)
a programme of operations, setting out the business model that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]* intends to follow;
### (e)
a legal opinion that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* does not qualify as either of the following:
#### (i)
a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* excluded from the scope of this Regulation pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.2#4|Article 2(4)]]; or
#### (ii)
an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*;
### (f)
a detailed description of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*’s governance arrangements as referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.34#1|Article 34(1)]];
### (g)
where cooperation arrangements with specific *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service providers]]* exist, a description of their internal control mechanisms and procedures to ensure compliance with the obligations in relation to the prevention of money laundering and terrorist financing under Directive (EU) 2015/849;
### (h)
the identity of the members of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*;
#### (i)
proof that the persons referred to in point (h) are of sufficiently good repute and possess the appropriate knowledge, skills and experience to manage the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*;
### (j)
proof that any shareholder or member, whether direct or indirect, that has a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(36)|qualifying holding]]* in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]* is of sufficiently good repute;
### (k)
a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper as referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.19|Article 19]];
### (l)
the policies and procedures referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.34#5|Article 34(5)]], first subparagraph;
### (m)
a description of the contractual arrangements with the third-party entities as referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.34#5|Article 34(5)]], second subparagraph;
### (n)
a description of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*’s business continuity policy referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.34#9|Article 34(9)]];
### (o)
a description of the internal control mechanisms and risk management procedures referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.34#10|Article 34(10)]];
### (p)
a description of the systems and procedures in place to safeguard the availability, authenticity, integrity and confidentiality of data as referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.34#11|Article 34(11)]];
### (q)
a description of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*’s complaints-handling procedures as referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.31|Article 31]];
### (r)
where applicable, a list of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member States]]* where the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]* intends to offer the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* to the public or intends to seek admission to trading of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*.
## 3.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* that have already been authorised in respect of one *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall not be required to submit, for the purposes of authorisation in respect of another *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, any information that was previously submitted by them to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* where such information would be identical. When submitting the information listed in paragraph 2, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* shall expressly confirm that any information not resubmitted is still up-to-date.
## 4.
The *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* shall promptly, and in any event within two working days of receipt of an application pursuant to paragraph 1, acknowledge receipt thereof in writing to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*.
## 5.
For the purposes of paragraph 2, points (i) and (j), the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall provide proof of all of the following:
### (a)
for all members of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]*, the absence of a criminal record in respect of convictions or the absence of penalties imposed under the applicable commercial law, insolvency law and financial services law, or in relation to anti-money laundering and counter-terrorist financing, to fraud or to professional liability;
### (b)
that the members of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* collectively possess the appropriate knowledge, skills and experience to manage the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* and that those persons are required to commit sufficient time to perform their duties;
### (c)
for all shareholders and members, whether direct or indirect, that have *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(36)|qualifying holdings]]* in the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(11)|applicant issuer]]*, the absence of a criminal record in respect of convictions and the absence of penalties imposed under the applicable commercial law, insolvency law and financial services law, or in relation to anti-money laundering and counter-terrorist financing, to fraud or to professional liability.
## 6.
EBA, in close cooperation with ESMA and the ECB, shall develop draft regulatory technical standards to further specify the information referred to in paragraph 2.

EBA shall submit the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph to the Commission by 30 June 2024.

Power is delegated to the Commission to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1093_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1093_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010.
## 7.
EBA, in close cooperation with ESMA, shall develop draft implementing technical standards to establish standard forms, templates and procedures for the information to be included in the application in order to ensure uniformity across the Union.

EBA shall submit the draft implementing technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph to the Commission by 30 June 2024.

Power is conferred on the Commission to adopt the implementing technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with [[2010R1093_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]].
