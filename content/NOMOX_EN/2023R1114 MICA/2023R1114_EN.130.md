# Article 130 - Supervisory measures by EBA

## 1.
Where EBA finds that an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of a significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* has committed an infringement as listed in Annex V, it may take one or more of the following measures:
### (a)
adopt a decision requiring the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* to cease the conduct constituting the infringement;
### (b)
adopt a decision imposing fines or periodic penalty payments pursuant to Articles [[2023R1114_EN.131|131]] and [[2023R1114_EN.132|132]];
### (c)
adopt a decision requiring the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* to transmit supplementary information, where necessary for the protection of holders of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, in particular *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|retail holders]]*;
### (d)
adopt a decision requiring the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* to suspend an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* for a maximum period of 30 consecutive working days on any single occasion where it has reasonable grounds for suspecting that this Regulation has been infringed;
### (e)
adopt a decision prohibiting an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* where it finds that this Regulation has been infringed or where it has reasonable grounds for suspecting that it will be infringed;
### (f)
adopt a decision requiring the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* operating a trading platform for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* that has admitted to trading the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* to suspend trading of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* for a maximum of 30 consecutive working days on any single occasion where it has reasonable grounds for suspecting that this Regulation has been infringed;
### (g)
adopt a decision prohibiting trading of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* on a trading platform for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* where it finds that this Regulation has been infringed;
### (h)
adopt a decision requiring the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* to amend its marketing communications, where it finds that the marketing communications do not comply with [[2023R1114_EN.29|Article 29]];
#### (i)
adopt a decision to suspend or prohibit marketing communications where there are reasonable grounds for suspecting that this Regulation has been infringed;
### (j)
adopt a decision requiring the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* to disclose all material information which might have an effect on the assessment of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* offered to the public or admitted to trading in order to ensure consumer protection or the smooth operation of the market;
### (k)
issue warnings that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* fails to fulfil its obligations under this Regulation;
### (l)
withdraw the authorisation of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*;
### (m)
adopt a decision requiring the removal of a natural person from the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*;
### (n)
require the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* under its supervision to introduce a minimum denomination amount in respect of that significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or to limit the amount of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* issued, in accordance with Article 23(4) and [[2023R1114_EN.24#3|Article 24(3)]].
## 2.
Where EBA finds that an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of a significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* has committed an infringement as listed in Annex VI, it may take one or more of the following measures:
### (a)
adopt a decision requiring the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* to cease the conduct constituting the infringement;
### (b)
adopt a decision imposing fines or periodic penalty payments pursuant to Articles [[2023R1114_EN.131|131]] and [[2023R1114_EN.132|132]];
### (c)
adopt a decision requiring the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* to transmit supplementary information where necessary for the protection of holders of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*, in particular *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(37)|retail holders]]*;
### (d)
adopt a decision requiring the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* to suspend an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* for a maximum period of 30 consecutive working days on any single occasion where it has reasonable grounds for suspecting that this Regulation has been infringed;
### (e)
adopt a decision prohibiting an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(12)|offer to the public]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* where it finds that this Regulation has been infringed or where it has reasonable grounds for suspecting that it will be infringed;
### (f)
adopt a decision requiring the relevant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* operating a trading platform for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* that has admitted to trading significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* to suspend trading of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* for a maximum of 30 consecutive working days on any single occasion where it has reasonable grounds for suspecting that this Regulation has been infringed;
### (g)
adopt a decision prohibiting trading of significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* on a trading platform for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* where it finds that this Regulation has been infringed;
### (h)
adopt a decision requiring the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* to disclose all material information which might have an effect on the assessment of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* offered to the public or admitted to trading in order to ensure consumer protection or the smooth operation of the market;
#### (i)
issue warnings that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* fails to fulfil its obligations under this Regulation;
### (j)
require the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* under its supervision to introduce a minimum denomination amount in respect of that significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* or to limit the amount of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* issued, as a result of the application of [[2023R1114_EN.58#3|Article 58(3)]].
## 3.
When taking the measures referred to in paragraph 1 or 2, EBA shall take into account the nature and seriousness of the infringement, having regard to:
### (a)
the duration and frequency of the infringement;
### (b)
whether financial crime has been occasioned, facilitated or is otherwise attributable to the infringement;
### (c)
whether the infringement has revealed serious or systemic weaknesses in the procedures, policies and risk management measures of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*;
### (d)
whether the infringement has been committed intentionally or negligently;
### (e)
the degree of responsibility of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* responsible for the infringement;
### (f)
the financial strength of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*, or of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*, responsible for the infringement, as indicated by the total turnover of the responsible legal person or the annual income and net assets of the responsible natural person;
### (g)
the impact of the infringement on the interests of holders of significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*;
### (h)
the importance of the profits gained, losses avoided by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* responsible for the infringement or the losses for third parties caused by the infringement, insofar as they can be determined;
#### (i)
the level of cooperation of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* responsible for the infringement with EBA, without prejudice to the need to ensure disgorgement of profits gained or losses avoided by that person;
### (j)
previous infringements by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* responsible for the infringement;
### (k)
measures taken by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or by the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* after the infringement to prevent the repetition of such an infringement.
## 4.
Before taking any of the measures as referred to in paragraph 1, points (d) to (g), and point (j), EBA shall inform ESMA and, where the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* are referencing the euro or an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]* of a Member State that is not the euro, the ECB or the central bank of the Member State concerned issuing that *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]*, respectively.
## 5.
Before taking any of the measures as referred to in paragraph 2, EBA shall inform the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* and the central bank of the Member State whose *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(8)|official currency]]* the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* is referencing.
## 6.
EBA shall notify any measure taken pursuant to paragraph 1 or 2 to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of the significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* responsible for the infringement without undue delay and shall communicate that measure to the competent authorities concerned as well as to the Commission. EBA shall publicly disclose any such decision on its website within 10 working days of the date of adoption of such decision, unless such disclosure would seriously jeopardise financial stability or cause disproportionate damage to the parties involved. Such disclosure shall not contain *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(45)|personal data]]*.
## 7.
The disclosure to the public referred to in paragraph 6 shall include the following statements:
### (a)
a statement affirming the right of the person responsible for the infringement to appeal the decision before the Court of Justice;
### (b)
where relevant, a statement affirming that an appeal has been lodged and specifying that such an appeal does not have suspensive effect;
### (c)
a statement asserting that it is possible for EBA’s Board of Appeal to suspend the application of the contested decision in accordance with [[2010R1093_EN.60#3|Article 60(3) of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]].
