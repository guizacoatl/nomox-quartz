# Article 146 - Amendment to Directive 2013/36/EU


In Annex I to Directive 2013/36/EU, point 15 is replaced by the following:

‘15.
Issuing *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(44)|electronic money]]* including *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(44)|electronic-money]]* tokens as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]], point (7), of Regulation (EU) 2023/1114 of the European Parliament and of the Council.
## 16.
Issuance of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]], point (6), of Regulation (EU) 2023/1114.
## 17.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|Crypto-asset services]]* as defined in [[2023R1114_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]], point (16), of Regulation (EU) 2023/1114.
