# Article 69 - Information to competent authorities


*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* shall notify their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* without delay of any changes to their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]*, prior to the exercise of activities by any new members, and shall provide their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* with all of the necessary information to assess compliance with [[2023R1114_EN.68|Article 68]].
