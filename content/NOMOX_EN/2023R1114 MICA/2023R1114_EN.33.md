# Article 33 - Notification of changes to management body


*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|Issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* shall notify immediately their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of any changes to their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]*, and shall provide their *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* with all of the necessary information to assess compliance with [[2023R1114_EN.34#2|Article 34(2)]].
