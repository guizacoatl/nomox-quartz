# Article 117 - Supervisory responsibilities of EBA with respect to issuers of significant asset-referenced tokens and issuers of significant e-money tokens

## 1.
Where an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* has been classified as significant in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.43|Article 43]] or 44, the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* shall carry out its activities under the supervision of EBA.

Without prejudice to the powers of national competent authorities under paragraph 2 of this Article, EBA shall exercise the powers of competent authorities conferred by Articles [[2023R1114_EN.22|22]] to 25, 29, 33 [[2023R1114_EN.34#7|Article 34(7)]] and (12), [[2023R1114_EN.35#3|Article 35(3)]] and (5), Article 36(10) and Articles [[2023R1114_EN.41|41]], [[2023R1114_EN.42|42]], [[2023R1114_EN.46|46]] and [[2023R1114_EN.47|47]] as regards *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*.
## 2.
Where an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of a significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* also provides *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* or issues *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* that are not significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]*, those services and activities shall remain under the supervision of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(33)|home Member State]]*.
## 3.
Where an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* has been classified as significant in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.43|Article 43]], EBA shall conduct a supervisory reassessment to ensure that the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* complies with Title III.
## 4.
Where an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* issued by an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(43)|electronic money institution]]* has been classified as significant in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.56|Article 56]] or 57, EBA shall supervise the compliance of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of such significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* with Articles [[2023R1114_EN.55|55]] and [[2023R1114_EN.58|58]].

For the purposes of the supervision of compliance with Articles [[2023R1114_EN.55|55]] and [[2023R1114_EN.58|58]], EBA shall exercise the powers of the competent authorities conferred on them by Articles [[2023R1114_EN.22|22]] and [[2023R1114_EN.23|23]], [[2023R1114_EN.24#3|Article 24(3)]], [[2023R1114_EN.35#3|Article 35(3)]] and (5), Article 36(10) and Articles [[2023R1114_EN.46|46]] and [[2023R1114_EN.47|47]], as regards *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(43)|electronic money institutions]]* issuing significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*.
## 5.
EBA shall exercise its supervisory powers as provided in paragraphs 1 to 4 in close cooperation with the other competent authorities responsible for supervising the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]*, in particular:
### (a)
the prudential supervisory authority, including, where applicable, the ECB under Regulation (EU) No 1024/2013;
### (b)
relevant competent authorities under national law transposing Directive 2009/110/EC, where applicable;
### (c)
the competent authorities referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.20#1|Article 20(1)]].
