# Article 148 - Transposition of amendments to Directives 2013/36/EU and (EU) 2019/1937

## 1.
Member States shall adopt and publish, by 30 December 2024, the laws, regulations and administrative provisions necessary to comply with Articles [[2023R1114_EN.146|146]] and [[2023R1114_EN.147|147]].
## 2.
Member States shall communicate to the Commission, EBA and ESMA the text of the main measures of national law that they adopt in the field covered by [[2023R1114_EN.116|Article 116]].
