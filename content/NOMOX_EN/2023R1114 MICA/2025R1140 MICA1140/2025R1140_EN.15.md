> Level 1 reference(s): [[2023R1114_EN.68#10]]# Article 15 - Identification of crypto-assets


When providing information to competent authorities under Articles [[2025R1140_EN.6|6]] and [[2025R1140_EN.7|7]], a crypto-asset service provider shall identify the crypto-assets that are the subject of the recorded order or *[[2025R1140_EN.1#(1)|transaction]]*, or used as a means of payment, by using a digital token identifier that is compliant with the ISO 24165 standard or an equivalent unique identifier approved by ESMA at Union level, which meets all of the following characteristics:
### (a)
is unique;
### (b)
is neutral;
### (c)
is reliable;
### (d)
is open source;
### (e)
is scalable;
### (f)
is accessible;
### (g)
is available at a reasonable cost basis; and
### (h)
is subject to an appropriate governance framework.
