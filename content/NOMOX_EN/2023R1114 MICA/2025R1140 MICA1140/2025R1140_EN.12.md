> Level 1 reference(s): [[2023R1114_EN.68#10]]# Article 12 - Recording of orders and transactions executed via trading platforms or service providers to which Regulation (EU) 2023/1114 does not apply

## 1.
Where a crypto-asset service provider executes an order or a *[[2025R1140_EN.1#(1)|transaction]]* on behalf of a client through a trading platform for crypto-assets or a service provider to which Regulation (EU) 2023/1114 does not apply, the crypto-asset service provider shall record the details of the order or *[[2025R1140_EN.1#(1)|transaction]]* as if it had executed the order or *[[2025R1140_EN.1#(1)|transaction]]* itself.
## 2.
The crypto-asset service provider shall record the information referred to in paragraph 1 in the fields specified in Table 2 of Section 2 and in Table 3 of Section 3 of the Annex, where those fields are applicable to the order or *[[2025R1140_EN.1#(1)|transaction]]* in question.
