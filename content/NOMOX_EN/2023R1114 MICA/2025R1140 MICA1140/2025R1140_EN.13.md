> Level 1 reference(s): [[2023R1114_EN.68#10]]# Article 13 - Recording of reception and transmission of orders to entities to which Regulation (EU) 2023/1114 does not apply

## 1.
Where a crypto-asset service provider transmits an order to an entity to which Regulation (EU) 2023/1114 does not apply, the crypto-asset service provider shall record the details of the transmitted order in the fields specified in Table 2 of Section 2 of the Annex, to the extent those fields are applicable to the order or *[[2025R1140_EN.1#(1)|transaction]]* in question.
## 2.
Where the order is aggregated for several clients, the information referred to in [[2025R1140_EN.9|Article 9]] and 14, as applicable, shall be recorded for each client.
