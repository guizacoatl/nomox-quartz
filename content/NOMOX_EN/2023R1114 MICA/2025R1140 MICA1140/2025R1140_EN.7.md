> Level 1 reference(s): [[2023R1114_EN.68#10]]# Article 7 - Record keeping of transactions

## 1.
Crypto-asset service providers shall, immediately after having undertaken a *[[2025R1140_EN.1#(1)|transaction]]*, record the details set out in the second and third columns of Table 3 of Section 3 and Table 4 of Section 4 of the Annex.
## 2.
Where competent authorities request any of the details referred to in paragraph 1 in accordance with [[2025R1140_EN.94#1|Article 94(1)]], points (a) or (d), or [[2025R1140_EN.94#3|Article 94(3)]], point (a), of Regulation (EU) 2023/1114, the operators of trading platforms for crypto-assets shall provide such details as set out in the fourth column of Table 3 of Section 3 of the Annex.
