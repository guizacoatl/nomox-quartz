# Article 125 - Exchange of information

## 1.
In order to carry out EBA’s supervisory responsibilities under [[2023R1114_EN.117|Article 117]] and without prejudice to [[2023R1114_EN.96|Article 96]], EBA and the competent authorities shall provide each other with the information required for the purposes of carrying out their duties under this Regulation without undue delay. For that purpose, the competent authorities and EBA shall exchange any information related to:
### (a)
an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of a significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or a person controlling or being directly or indirectly controlled by an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of a significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]*;
### (b)
a third party as referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.34#5|Article 34(5)]], first subparagraph, point (h), with which an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of a significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* has a contractual arrangement;
### (c)
a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]*, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(28)|credit institution]]* or *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(29)|investment firm]]* ensuring the custody of the reserve assets in accordance with [[2023R1114_EN.37|Article 37]];
### (d)
an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of a significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* or a person controlling or being directly or indirectly controlled by an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of a significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*;
### (e)
a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(42)|payment service provider]]* that provides *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(41)|payment services]]* in relation to significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*;
### (f)
a natural or legal person in charge of distributing significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* on behalf of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* of significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*;
### (g)
a *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|crypto-asset service provider]]* *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(17)|providing custody and administration of crypto-assets on behalf of clients]]*, in relation to significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* or significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*;
### (h)
a trading platform for *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* on which a significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced token]]* or a significant *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* has been admitted to trading;
#### (i)
the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(27)|management body]]* of the persons referred to in points (a) to (h).
## 2.
A *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(35)|competent authority]]* may refuse to act on a request to exchange information as provided for in paragraph 1 of this Article or a request for cooperation in carrying out an investigation or an on-site inspection as provided for in Articles [[2023R1114_EN.123|123]] and [[2023R1114_EN.124|124]], respectively, only where:
### (a)
complying with the request is likely to adversely affect its own investigation, enforcement activities or, where applicable, criminal investigation;
### (b)
judicial proceedings have already been initiated in respect of the same actions and against the same natural or legal persons before the courts of the Member State addressed;
### (c)
a final judgment has already been delivered in relation to such natural or legal person for the same actions in the Member State addressed.
