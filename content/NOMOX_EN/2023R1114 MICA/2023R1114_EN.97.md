# Article 97 - Promotion of convergence on the classification of crypto-assets

## 1.
By 30 December 2024, the ESAs shall jointly issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1093_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]], [[2010R1094_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1094/2010]] and [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] to specify the content and form of the explanation accompanying the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-asset]]* white paper referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.8#4|Article 8(4)]] and of the legal opinions on the qualification of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(6)|asset-referenced tokens]]* referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.17#1|Article 17(1)]], point (b)(ii), and [[2023R1114_EN.18#2|Article 18(2)]], point (e). The guidelines shall include a template for the explanation and the opinion and a standardised test for the classification of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*.
## 2.
The ESAs shall, in accordance with [[2010R1093_EN.29|Article 29 of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]], [[2010R1094_EN.29|Article 29 of Regulation (EU) No 1094/2010]] and [[2010R1095_EN.29|Article 29 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]], respectively, promote discussion among competent authorities on the classification of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, including on the classification of those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* that are excluded from the scope of this Regulation pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.2#3|Article 2(3)]]. The ESAs shall also identify the sources of potential divergences in the approaches of the competent authorities to the classification of those *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* and shall, to the extent possible, promote a common approach thereto.
## 3.
Competent authorities of the home or the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(34)|host Member States]]* may request ESMA, EIOPA or EBA, as appropriate, for an opinion on the classification of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]*, including those that are excluded from the scope of this Regulation pursuant to [[2023R1114_EN.2#3|Article 2(3)]]. ESMA, EIOPA or EBA, as applicable, shall provide such opinion in accordance with [[2010R1093_EN.29|Article 29 of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]], [[2010R1094_EN.29|Article 29 of Regulation (EU) No 1094/2010]] and [[2010R1095_EN.29|Article 29 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]], as applicable, within 15 working days of receipt of the request from the competent authorities.
## 4.
The ESAs shall jointly draw up an annual report based on the information contained in the register referred to in [[2023R1114_EN.109|Article 109]] and on the results of their work referred to in paragraphs 2 and 3 of this Article, identifying difficulties in the classification of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(5)|crypto-assets]]* and divergences in the approaches of the competent authorities.
