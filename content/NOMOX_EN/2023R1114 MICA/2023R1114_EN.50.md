# Article 50 - Prohibition of granting interest

## 1.
Notwithstanding Article 12 of Directive 2009/110/EC, *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuers]]* of *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]* shall not grant interest in relation to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*.
## 2.
*[[2023R1114_EN.3#(15)|Crypto-asset service providers]]* shall not grant interest when providing *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(16)|crypto-asset services]]* related to *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money tokens]]*.
## 3.
For the purposes of paragraphs 1 and 2, any remuneration or any other benefit related to the length of time during which a holder of an *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* holds such *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* shall be treated as interest. That includes net compensation or discounts, with an effect equivalent to that of interest received by the holder of the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]*, directly from the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(10)|issuer]]* or from third parties, and directly associated to the *[[2023R1114_EN.3#(7)|e-money token]]* or from the remuneration or pricing of other products.
