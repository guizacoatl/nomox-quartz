# Article 5 - Information upon request


Information to be provided by a relevant competent authority on an ad hoc basis pursuant to [[2012R0236_EN.11#2|Article 11(2) of Regulation (EU) No 236/2012]] shall include all requested information specified by ESMA that has not previously been submitted by the competent authority in accordance with [[2012R0826_EN.4|Article 4]] of this Regulation.
