# Article 1 - Subject matter


This Regulation lays down regulatory technical standards specifying the following:
### (a)
the details of the information on net short positions to be provided to the competent authorities and disclosed to the public by a natural or legal person pursuant to [[2012R0236_EN.9#5|Article 9(5) of Regulation (EU) No 236/2012]];
### (b)
the details of the information to be provided to the European Securities and Markets Authority (hereinafter ‘ESMA’) by the competent authority pursuant to [[2012R0236_EN.11#3|Article 11(3) of Regulation (EU) No 236/2012]];
### (c)
the method for calculation of turnover to determine the principal venue for the trading of a share pursuant to [[2012R0236_EN.16#3|Article 16(3) of Regulation (EU) No 236/2012]].
