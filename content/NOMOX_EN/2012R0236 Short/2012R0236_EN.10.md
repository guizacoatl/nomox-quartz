# Article 10 - Application of notification and disclosure requirements


The notification and disclosure requirements under Articles [[2012R0236_EN.5|5]], [[2012R0236_EN.6|6]], [[2012R0236_EN.7|7]] and [[2012R0236_EN.8|8]] apply to natural or legal persons domiciled or established within the Union or in a third country.
