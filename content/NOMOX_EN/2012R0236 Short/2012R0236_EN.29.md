# Article 29 - ESMA’s powers in emergency situations relating to sovereign debt


In the case of an emergency situation relating to sovereign debt or sovereign credit default swaps, Articles [[2012R0236_EN.18|18]] and [[2012R0236_EN.38|38]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010 shall apply.
