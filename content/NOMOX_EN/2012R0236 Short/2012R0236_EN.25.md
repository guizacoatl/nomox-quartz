# Article 25 - Notice of restrictions

## 1.
A competent authority shall publish on its website notice of any decision to impose or renew any measure referred to in Articles [[2012R0236_EN.18|18]] to 23.
## 2.
The notice shall specify at least details of:
### (a)
the measures imposed including the instruments and classes of transactions to which they apply and their duration;
### (b)
the reasons why the competent authority believes it is necessary to impose the measures including the evidence supporting those reasons.
## 3.
A measure under Articles [[2012R0236_EN.18|18]] to 23 shall take effect when the notice is published or at a time specified in the notice that is after its publication and shall only apply in relation to a transaction entered into after the measure takes effect.
