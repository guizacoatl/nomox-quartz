# Article 30 - Further specification of adverse events or developments


The Commission shall be empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2012R0236_EN.42|Article 42]] specifying criteria and factors to be taken into account by the competent authorities and by ESMA in determining in which cases the adverse events or developments referred to in Articles [[2012R0236_EN.18|18]] to 21 and [[2012R0236_EN.27|Article 27]] and the threats referred to in point (a) of [[2012R0236_EN.28#2|Article 28(2)]] arise.
