# Article 8 - Notification to competent authorities of uncovered positions in sovereign credit default swaps


Where a competent authority suspends restrictions in accordance with [[2012R0236_EN.14#2|Article 14(2)]], a natural or legal person who has an uncovered position in a sovereign credit default swap shall notify the relevant competent authority where such a position reaches or falls below the relevant notification thresholds for the sovereign issuer, as specified in accordance with [[2012R0236_EN.7|Article 7]].
