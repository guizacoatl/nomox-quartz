# Article 5 - Assignment to risk categories


For the purposes of Articles [[2022R2115_EN.3|3]] and [[2022R2115_EN.4|4]], crowdfunding service providers shall assign the individual loans to the relevant risk category set out in the risk-management framework on the basis of sound and well-defined criteria and taking into account all the relevant factors that may have unfavourable effects on the performance of the loans.
