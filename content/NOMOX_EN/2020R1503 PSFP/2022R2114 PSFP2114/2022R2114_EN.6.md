# Article 6 - Simulation of the ability to bear losses in addition to the online calculation tool


In addition to the on-line tool referred to in [[2022R2114_EN.5#1|Article 5(1)]], crowdfunding service providers may offer prospective non-sophisticated investors the possibility to simulate their ability to bear losses through a different method, provided that the crowdfunding service provider provides the prospective non-sophisticated investors with appropriate information about the method used to simulate the ability to bear losses.
