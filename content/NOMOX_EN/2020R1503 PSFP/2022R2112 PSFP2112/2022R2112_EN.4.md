# Article 4 - Time limit suspension in case of missing information


Where the competent authority requires the prospective crowdfunding service provider to provide missing information in accordance with [[2020R1503_EN.12#4|Article 12(4) of Regulation (EU) 1503/2020]], the time limit for the assessment of whether the application is complete in accordance with that Article shall be suspended from the date such information is requested until the date it is received.
