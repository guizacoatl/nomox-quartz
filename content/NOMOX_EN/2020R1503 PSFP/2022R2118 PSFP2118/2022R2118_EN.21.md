# Article 21 - Transparency and disclosure to investors

## 1.
The management body of the crowdfunding service provider shall inform and update its staff about the contingency fund’s policies and procedures in a clear and consistent way, at least to the level needed to carry out the duties of the contingency fund.
## 2.
The policies, procedures and organisational arrangements that the crowdfunding service provider shall have in place in accordance with [[2022R2118_EN.6#7|Article 6(7)]], point (c) of Regulation (EU) 2020/1503 shall be consistently reflected in the contingency fund policy referred to in [[2022R2118_EN.6#5|Article 6(5)]], point (b), of that Regulation.
