# Article 2 - Standard format and language

## 1.
Crowdfunding service providers shall ensure that clients are able to submit complaints by electronic means, using the standard template set out in the Annex.
## 2.
Crowdfunding service providers shall ensure that clients are able to file complaints in any of the languages referred to in [[2022R2117_EN.1#4|Article 1(4)]].
