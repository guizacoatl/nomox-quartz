> Level 1 reference(s): [[2020R1503_EN.23]]# Article 1 - Key investment information sheet model

## 1.
When providing the information in the key investment information sheet referred to in [[2020R1503_EN.23|Article 23 of Regulation (EU) 1503/2020]], crowdfunding service providers shall use the model laid down in the Annex to this Regulation.
## 2.
The information referred to in paragraph 1 shall be made available as soon as the relevant crowdfunding offer is published by the crowdfunding service provider.
