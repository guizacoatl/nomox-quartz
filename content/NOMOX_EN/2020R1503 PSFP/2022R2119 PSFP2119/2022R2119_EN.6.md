# Article 6 - Types of main risks associated with a crowdfunding offer

## 1.
The types of main risks which are associated with a crowdfunding offer shall be disclosed in the key investment information sheet relating to that offer in accordance with the instructions set out in Part C of the Annex. Where relevant, other risks shall also be disclosed.
## 2.
The description of the risks associated with a crowdfunding offer shall be of relevance to that specific offer and shall be prepared solely for the benefit of prospective investors and shall not give general statements on investment risks or limit the liability of the project owner or any persons acting on their behalf.
