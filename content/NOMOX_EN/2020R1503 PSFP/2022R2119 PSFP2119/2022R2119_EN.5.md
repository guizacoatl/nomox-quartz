# Article 5 - Use of hyperlinks in the key investment information sheet model

## 1.
The key investment information sheet may contain hyperlinks as set out in the model laid down in the Annex.
## 2.
The hyperlinks shall be complementary to the information given and shall not replace that information, except as otherwise provided in the model.
## 3.
The hyperlinks shall be consistent with the information provided elsewhere in the key investment information sheet and the external resources referenced in the hyperlinks shall be freely and easily accessible.
