# Article 13 - Scope of authorisation

## 1.
The competent authorities that granted an authorisation notified under [[2020R1503_EN.12#10|Article 12(10)]] shall ensure that such authorisation specifies the crowdfunding services which the crowdfunding service provider is authorised to provide.
## 2.
A crowdfunding service provider seeking authorisation to extend its business to additional crowdfunding services not foreseen at the time of the authorisation granted under [[2020R1503_EN.12|Article 12]] shall submit a request for extension of its authorisation to the competent authorities that granted the crowdfunding service provider its authorisation under [[2020R1503_EN.12|Article 12]] by complementing and updating the information referred to in [[2020R1503_EN.12#2|Article 12(2)]]. The request for extension shall be processed in accordance with [[2020R1503_EN.12#4|Article 12(4)]] to (11).
