# Article 33 - Cooperation with other authorities


Where a crowdfunding service provider engages in activities other than those covered by the authorisation referred to in [[2020R1503_EN.12|Article 12]], the competent authorities shall cooperate with the authorities responsible for the oversight of such other activities as provided for in the relevant Union or national law.
