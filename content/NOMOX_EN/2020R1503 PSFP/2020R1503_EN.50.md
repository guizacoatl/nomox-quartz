# Article 50 - Transposition of amendment of Directive (EU) 2019/1937

## 1.
Member States shall adopt, publish and apply, by 10 November 2021, the laws, regulations and administrative provisions necessary to comply with [[2020R1503_EN.47|Article 47]]. However, if that date precedes the date of transposition referred to in [[2019L1937_EN.26#1|Article 26(1) of Directive (EU) 1937/2019]], the adoption, publication and application of such laws, regulations and administrative provisions shall be postponed until the date of transposition referred to in [[2019L1937_EN.26#1|Article 26(1) of Directive (EU) 1937/2019]].
## 2.
Member States shall communicate to the Commission and to ESMA the text of the main provisions of national law which they adopt in the field covered by [[2020R1503_EN.47|Article 47]].
