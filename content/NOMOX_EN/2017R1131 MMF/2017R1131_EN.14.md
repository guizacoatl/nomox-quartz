# Article 14 - Eligible repurchase agreements


A *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(4)|repurchase agreement]]* shall be eligible to be entered into by an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* provided that all of the following conditions are fulfilled:
### (a)
it is used on a temporary basis, for no more than seven working days, only for liquidity management purposes and not for investment purposes other than as referred to in point (c);
### (b)
the counterparty receiving assets transferred by the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* as collateral under the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(4)|repurchase agreement]]* is prohibited from selling, investing, pledging or otherwise transferring those assets without the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*'s prior consent;
### (c)
the cash received by the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* as part of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(4)|repurchase agreement]]* is able to be:
#### (i)
placed on deposits in accordance with point (f) of Article 50(1) of Directive 2009/65/EC; or
#### (ii)
invested in assets referred to in [[2017R1131_EN.15#6|Article 15(6)]], but shall not otherwise be invested in eligible assets as referred to in [[2017R1131_EN.9|Article 9]], transferred or otherwise reused;
### (d)
the cash received by the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* as part of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(4)|repurchase agreement]]* that is not centrally cleared through a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(24)|CCP]]* authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] or recognised in accordance with Article 25 of that Regulation does not exceed 10 % of its assets;
### (da)
the cash received by the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* as part of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(4)|repurchase agreement]]* that is centrally cleared through a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(24)|CCP]]* authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] or recognised in accordance with Article 25 of that Regulation does not exceed 15 % of its assets;
### (e)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* has the right to terminate the agreement at any time upon giving prior notice of no more than two working days.
