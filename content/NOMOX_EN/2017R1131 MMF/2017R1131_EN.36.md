# Article 36 - Transparency

## 1.
An *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall indicate clearly which type of *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* it is in accordance with [[2017R1131_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]] and whether it is a short-term or a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(15)|standard MMF]]* in any external document, report, statement, advertisement, letter or any other written evidence issued by it or by the manager of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*, addressed to or intended for distribution to prospective investors, unit-holders, or shareholders.
## 2.
The *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(23)|manager of an MMF]]* shall, at least weekly, make all of the following information available to the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*'s investors:
### (a)
the maturity breakdown of the portfolio of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*;
### (b)
the credit profile of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*;
### (c)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(19)|WAM]]* and *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(20)|WAL]]* of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*;
### (d)
details of the 10 largest holdings in the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*, including the name, country, maturity and asset type, and the counterparty in the case of repurchase and *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreements]]*;
### (e)
the total value of the assets of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*;
### (f)
the net yield of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.
## 3.
Any document of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* used for marketing purposes shall clearly include all of the following statements:
### (a)
that the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* is not a guaranteed investment;
### (b)
that an investment in *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMFs]]* is different from an investment in deposits, with particular reference to the risk that the principal invested in an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* is capable of fluctuation;
### (c)
that the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* does not rely on external support for guaranteeing the liquidity of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* or stabilising the NAV per unit or share;
### (d)
that the risk of loss of the principal is to be borne by the investor.
## 4.
No communication by the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* or by the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(23)|manager of an MMF]]* to investors or potential investors shall in any way suggest that an investment in the units or shares of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* is guaranteed.
## 5.
Investors in an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall be clearly informed of the method or methods used by the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* to value the assets of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* and calculate the NAV.

*[[2017R1131_EN.2#(11)|Public debt CNAV MMFs]]* and *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(12)|LVNAV MMFs]]* shall explain clearly to investors and potential investors any use of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(10)|amortised cost method]]* or of rounding or both.
