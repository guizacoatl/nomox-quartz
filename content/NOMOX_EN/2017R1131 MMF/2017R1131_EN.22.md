# Article 22 - Delegated acts for the credit quality assessment


The Commission shall adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2017R1131_EN.45|Article 45]] in order to supplement this Regulation by specifying the following points:
### (a)
the criteria for the validation of the credit quality assessment methodology, as referred to in [[2017R1131_EN.19#3|Article 19(3)]];
### (b)
the criteria for quantification of the credit risk, and of the relative risk of default of an issuer and of the instrument, as referred to in point (a) of [[2017R1131_EN.20#2|Article 20(2)]];
### (c)
the criteria for establishing qualitative indicators on the issuer of the instrument, as referred to in point (b) of [[2017R1131_EN.20#2|Article 20(2)]];
### (d)
the meaning of material change as referred to in point (d) of [[2017R1131_EN.19#4|Article 19(4)]].
