# Article 1 - Subject matter and scope

## 1.
This Regulation lays down rules for *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|money market funds]]* (*[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMFs]]*) established, managed or marketed in the Union, concerning the financial instruments eligible for investment by a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*, the portfolio of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*, the valuation of the assets of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*, and the reporting requirements in relation to an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.

This Regulation applies to collective investment undertakings that:
### (a)
require authorisation as UCITS or are authorised as UCITS under Directive 2009/65/EC or are AIFs under Directive 2011/61/EU;
### (b)
invest in *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(1)|short-term assets]]*; and
### (c)
have distinct or cumulative objectives offering returns in line with money market rates or preserving the value of the investment.
## 2.
Member States shall not add any additional requirements in the field covered by this Regulation.
