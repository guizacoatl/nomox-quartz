# Article 33 - Issue and redemption price

## 1.
The units or shares of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall be issued or redeemed at a price that is equal to the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*'s NAV per unit or share, notwithstanding permitted fees or charges as specified in the prospectus of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.
## 2.
By way of derogation from paragraph 1:
### (a)
the units or shares of a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(11)|public debt CNAV MMF]]* may be issued or redeemed at a price that is equal to that *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*'s constant NAV per unit or share;
### (b)
the units or shares of a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(12)|LVNAV MMF]]* may be issued or redeemed at a price that is equal to that *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*'s constant NAV per unit or share, but only where the constant NAV per unit or share calculated in accordance with [[2017R1131_EN.32#1|Article 32(1)]], (2) and (3) does not deviate from the NAV per unit or share calculated in accordance with [[2017R1131_EN.30|Article 30]] by more than 20 basis points.

In relation to point (b), when the constant NAV per unit or share calculated in accordance with [[2017R1131_EN.32#1|Article 32(1)]], (2) and (3) deviates from the NAV per unit or share calculated in accordance with [[2017R1131_EN.30|Article 30]] by more than 20 basis points, the following redemption or subscription shall be undertaken at a price that is equal to the NAV per unit or share calculated in accordance with [[2017R1131_EN.30|Article 30]].

Potential investors shall, prior to the conclusion of the contract, be clearly warned in writing by the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(23)|manager of an MMF]]* of the circumstances in which the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(12)|LVNAV MMF]]* will no longer redeem or subscribe at a constant NAV per unit or share.
