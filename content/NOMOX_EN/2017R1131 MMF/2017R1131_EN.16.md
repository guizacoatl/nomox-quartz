# Article 16 - Eligible units or shares of MMFs

## 1.
An *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* may acquire the units or shares of any other *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* (‘*[[2017R1131_EN.2#1.|targeted MMF]]*’) provided that all of the following conditions are fulfilled:
### (a)
no more than 10 % of the assets of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#1.|targeted MMF]]* are able, according to its fund rules or instruments of incorporation, to be invested in aggregate in units or shares of other *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMFs]]*;
### (b)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#1.|targeted MMF]]* does not hold units or shares in the acquiring *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.

An *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* whose units or shares have been acquired shall not invest in the acquiring *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* during the period in which the acquiring *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* holds units or shares in it.
## 2.
An *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* may acquire the units or shares of other *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMFs]]*, provided that no more than 5 % of its assets are invested in units or shares of a single *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.
## 3.
An *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* may, in aggregate, invest no more than 17,5 % of its assets in units or shares of other *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMFs]]*.
## 4.
Units or shares of other *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMFs]]* shall be eligible for investment by an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* provided that all of the following conditions are fulfilled:
### (a)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#1.|targeted MMF]]* is authorised under this Regulation;
### (b)
where the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#1.|targeted MMF]]* is managed, whether directly or under a delegation, by the same manager as that of the acquiring *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* or by any other company to which the manager of the acquiring *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* is linked by common management or control, or by a substantial direct or indirect holding, the manager of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#1.|targeted MMF]]*, or that other company, is prohibited from charging subscription or redemption fees on account of the investment by the acquiring *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* in the units or shares of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#1.|targeted MMF]]*;
### (c)
where an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* invests 10 % or more of its assets in units or shares of other *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMFs]]*:
#### (i)
the prospectus of that *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall disclose the maximum level of the management fees that may be charged to the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* itself and to the other *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMFs]]* in which it invests; and
#### (ii)
the annual report shall indicate the maximum proportion of management fees charged to the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* itself and to the other *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMFs]]* in which it invests.
## 5.
Paragraphs 2 and 3 of this Article shall not apply to an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* that is an AIF authorised in accordance with [[2017R1131_EN.5|Article 5]], where all of the following conditions are met:
### (a)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* is marketed solely through an employee savings scheme governed by national law and which has only natural persons as investors;
### (b)
the employee savings scheme referred to in point (a) only allows investors to redeem their investment subject to restrictive redemption terms which are laid down in national law, whereby redemptions may only take place in certain circumstances that are not linked to market developments.

By way of derogation from paragraphs 2 and 3 of this Article, an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* that is a UCITS authorised in accordance with [[2017R1131_EN.4#2|Article 4(2)]] may acquire units or shares in other *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMFs]]* in accordance with [[2017R1131_EN.55|Article 55]] or 58 of Directive 2009/65/EC under the following conditions:
### (a)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* is marketed solely through an employee savings scheme governed by national law and which has only natural persons as investors;
### (b)
the employee savings scheme referred to in point (a) only allows investors to redeem their investment subject to restrictive redemption terms which are laid down in national law, whereby redemptions may only take place in certain circumstances that are not linked to market developments.
## 6.
*[[2017R1131_EN.2#(14)|Short-term MMFs]]* may only invest in units or shares of other *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(14)|short-term MMFs]]*.
## 7.
*[[2017R1131_EN.2#(15)|Standard MMFs]]* may invest in units or shares of *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(14)|short-term MMFs]]* and *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(15)|standard MMFs]]*.
