# Article 38 - Supervision by the competent authorities

## 1.
The competent authorities shall supervise compliance with this Regulation on an ongoing basis.
## 2.
The *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(17)|competent authority of the MMF]]* or, where relevant, the competent authority of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(23)|manager of an MMF]]* shall be responsible for supervising compliance with Chapters II to VII.
## 3.
The *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(17)|competent authority of the MMF]]* shall be responsible for supervising compliance with the obligations set out in the fund rules or in the instruments of incorporation, and the obligations set out in the prospectus, which shall be consistent with this Regulation.
## 4.
The competent authority of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(23)|manager of an MMF]]* shall be responsible for supervising the adequacy of the arrangements and organisation of the manager of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* so that the manager of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* is in a position to comply with the obligations and rules which relate to the constitution and functioning of all the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMFs]]* that it manages.
## 5.
Competent authorities shall monitor UCITS and AIFs established or marketed in their territories to verify that they do not use the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* designation or suggest that they are an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* unless they comply with this Regulation.
