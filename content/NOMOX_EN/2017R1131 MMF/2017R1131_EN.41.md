# Article 41 - Specific measures

## 1.
Without prejudice to powers vested in competent authorities in accordance with Directive 2009/65/EC or 2011/61/EU, as applicable, the competent authority of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* or the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(23)|manager of an MMF]]* shall, while respecting the principle of proportionality, take the appropriate measures referred to in paragraph 2 where an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* or the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(23)|manager of an MMF]]*:
### (a)
fails to comply with any of the requirements regarding asset composition, in infringement of Articles [[2017R1131_EN.9|9]] to 16;
### (b)
fails to comply with any of the portfolio requirements, in infringement of [[2017R1131_EN.17|Article 17]], 18, 24 or 25;
### (c)
has obtained authorisation through false statements or any other irregular means, in infringement of [[2017R1131_EN.4|Article 4]] or 5;
### (d)
uses the designation ‘*[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|money market fund]]*’, ‘*[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*’ or of another designation that suggests that a UCITS or AIF is an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*, in infringement of [[2017R1131_EN.6|Article 6]];
### (e)
fails to comply with any of the requirements regarding the internal credit quality assessment, in infringement of [[2017R1131_EN.19|Article 19]] or 20;
### (f)
fails to comply with any of the governance, documentation or transparency requirements, in infringement of [[2017R1131_EN.21|Article 21]], 23, 26, 27, 28 or 36;
### (g)
fails to comply with any of the requirements regarding valuation, in infringement of [[2017R1131_EN.29|Article 29]], 30, 31, 32, 33 or 34.
## 2.
In the cases referred to in paragraph 1, the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(17)|competent authority of the MMF]]* shall, as appropriate:
### (a)
take measures to ensure that the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* or the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(23)|manager of an MMF]]* concerned complies with the relevant provisions;
### (b)
withdraw an authorisation granted in accordance with [[2017R1131_EN.4|Article 4]] or 5.
