# Article 35 - External support

## 1.
A *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall not receive external support.
## 2.
External support means direct or indirect support offered to an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* by a third party, including a sponsor of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*, that is intended for or in effect would result in guaranteeing the liquidity of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* or stabilising the NAV per unit or share of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.

External support shall include:
### (a)
cash injections from a third party;
### (b)
purchase by a third party of assets of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* at an inflated price;
### (c)
purchase by a third party of units or shares of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* in order to provide liquidity to the fund;
### (d)
issuance by a third party of any kind of explicit or implicit guarantee, warranty or letter of support for the benefit of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*;
### (e)
any action by a third party the direct or indirect objective of which is to maintain the liquidity profile and the NAV per unit or share of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.
