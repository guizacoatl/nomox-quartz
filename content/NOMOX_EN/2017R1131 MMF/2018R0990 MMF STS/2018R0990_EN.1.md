# Article 1 - Amendment to Regulation (EU) 2017/1131

In [[2017R1131_EN.11#1|Article 11(1) of Regulation (EU) 1131/2017]], point (c) is replaced by the following:

‘(c) a simple, transparent and standardised (STS) securitisation, as determined in accordance with the criteria and conditions laid down in Articles [[2018R0990_EN.20|20]], [[2018R0990_EN.21|21]] and [[2018R0990_EN.22|22]] of Regulation (EU) 2017/2402 of the European Parliament and of the Council (

*1

), or an STS ABCP, as determined in accordance with the criteria and conditions laid down in Articles [[2018R0990_EN.24|24]], [[2018R0990_EN.25|25]] and [[2018R0990_EN.26|26]] of that Regulation.
