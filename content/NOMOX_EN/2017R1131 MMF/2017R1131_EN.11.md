# Article 11 - Eligible securitisations and ABCPs

## 1.
Both a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisation]]* and an ABCP shall be considered to be eligible for investment by an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* provided that the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisation]]* or ABCP is sufficiently liquid, has received a favourable assessment pursuant to Articles [[2017R1131_EN.19|19]] to 22, and is any of the following:
### (a)
a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisation]]* referred to in Article 13 of Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2015/61;
### (b)
an ABCP issued by an ABCP programme which:
#### (i)
is fully supported by a regulated *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(16)|credit institution]]* that covers all liquidity, credit and material dilution risks, as well as ongoing transaction costs and ongoing programme-wide costs related to the ABCP, if necessary to guarantee the investor the full payment of any amount under the ABCP;
#### (ii)
is not a re-*[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisation]]* and the exposures underlying the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisation]]* at the level of each ABCP transaction do not include any *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisation]]* position;
#### (iii)
does not include a synthetic *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisation]]* as defined in point (11) of [[2013R0575_EN.242|Article 242 of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]];
### (c)
a simple, transparent and standardised (STS) *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisation]]*, as determined in accordance with the criteria and conditions laid down in Articles [[2017R1131_EN.20|20]], [[2017R1131_EN.21|21]] and [[2017R1131_EN.22|22]] of Regulation (EU) 2017/2402 of the European Parliament and of the Council, or an STS ABCP, as determined in accordance with the criteria and conditions laid down in Articles [[2017R1131_EN.24|24]], [[2017R1131_EN.25|25]] and [[2017R1131_EN.26|26]] of that Regulation.
## 2.
A *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(14)|short-term MMF]]* may invest in the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* or ABCPs referred to in paragraph 1 provided any of the following conditions is fulfilled, as applicable:
### (a)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(18)|legal maturity]]* at issuance of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* referred to in point (a) of paragraph 1 is 2 years or less and the time remaining until the next interest rate reset date is 397 days or less;
### (b)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(18)|legal maturity]]* at issuance or *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(21)|residual maturity]]* of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* or ABCPs referred to in points (b) and (c) of paragraph 1 is 397 days or less;
### (c)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* referred to in points (a) and (c) of paragraph 1 are amortising instruments and have a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(20)|WAL]]* of 2 years or less.
## 3.
A *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(15)|standard MMF]]* may invest in the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* or ABCPs referred to in paragraph 1 provided any of the following conditions is fulfilled, as applicable:
### (a)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(18)|legal maturity]]* at issuance or *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(21)|residual maturity]]* of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* and ABCPs referred to in points (a), (b) and (c) of paragraph 1 is 2 years or less and the time remaining until the next interest rate reset date is 397 days or less;
### (b)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* referred to in points (a) and (c) of paragraph 1 are amortising instruments and have a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(20)|WAL]]* of 2 years or less.
## 4.
The Commission shall adopt, by 6 months from the date of entry into force of the proposed Regulation on STS *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]*, a delegated act in accordance with [[2017R1131_EN.45|Article 45]] amending this Article by introducing a cross-reference to the criteria identifying STS *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* and ABCPs in the corresponding provisions of that Regulation. The amendment shall become effective at the latest by 6 months after the date of entry into force of that delegated act or from the date of application of the corresponding provisions in the proposed Regulation on STS *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]*, whichever is the later.

For the purposes of the first subparagraph, the criteria identifying STS *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* and ABCPs shall include at least the following:
### (a)
requirements relating to the simplicity of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisation]]*, including its true sale character and the respect of standards relating to the underwriting of the exposures;
### (b)
requirements relating to standardisation of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisation]]*, including risk retention requirements;
### (c)
requirements relating to the transparency of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisation]]*, including the provision of information to potential investors;
### (d)
for ABCPs, in addition to points (a), (b) and (c), requirements relating to the sponsor and to the sponsor support of the ABCP programme.
