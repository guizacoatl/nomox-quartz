# Article 8 - General principles

## 1.
Where an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* comprises more than one investment compartment, each compartment shall be regarded as a separate *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* for the purposes of Chapters II to VII.
## 2.
*[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMFs]]* authorised as UCITS shall not be subject to the obligations concerning investment policies of UCITS laid down in Articles [[2017R1131_EN.49|49]] to 50a, [[2017R1131_EN.51#2|Article 51(2)]], and Articles [[2017R1131_EN.52|52]] to 57 of Directive 2009/65/EC, unless explicitly specified otherwise in this Regulation.
