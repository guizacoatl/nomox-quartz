# Article 17 - Diversification

## 1.
An *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall invest no more than:
### (a)
5 % of its assets in *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(2)|money market instruments]]*, *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* and ABCPs issued by the same body;
### (b)
10 % of its assets in deposits made with the same *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(16)|credit institution]]*, unless the structure of the banking sector in the Member State in which the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* is domiciled is such that there are insufficient viable *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(16)|credit institutions]]* to meet that diversification requirement and it is not economically feasible for the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* to make deposits in another Member State, in which case up to 15 % of its assets may be deposited with the same *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(16)|credit institution]]*.
## 2.
By way of derogation from point (a) of paragraph 1, a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(13)|VNAV MMF]]* may invest up to 10 % of its assets in *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(2)|money market instruments]]*, *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* and ABCPs issued by the same body provided that the total value of such *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(2)|money market instruments]]*, *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* and ABCPs held by the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(13)|VNAV MMF]]* in each issuing body in which it invests more than 5 % of its assets does not exceed 40 % of the value of its assets.
## 3.
Until the date of application of the delegated act referred to in [[2017R1131_EN.11#4|Article 11(4)]], the aggregate of all of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*'s exposures to *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* and ABCPs shall not exceed 15 % of the assets of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.

As from the date of application of the delegated act referred to in [[2017R1131_EN.11#4|Article 11(4)]], the aggregate of all of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*'s exposures to *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* and ABCPs shall not exceed 20 % of the assets of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*, whereby up to 15 % of the assets of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* may be invested in *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* and ABCPs that do not comply with the criteria for the identification of STS *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* and ABCPs.
## 4.
The aggregate risk exposure to the same counterparty of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* stemming from derivative transactions which fulfil the conditions set out in [[2017R1131_EN.13|Article 13]] and which are not centrally cleared through a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(24)|CCP]]* authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] or recognised in accordance with Article 25 of that Regulation, shall not exceed 5 % of the assets of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.
## 5.
The aggregate amount of cash provided to the same counterparty of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* in *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreements]]* that are not centrally cleared through a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(24)|CCP]]* authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] or recognised in accordance with Article 25 of that Regulation shall not exceed 15 % of the assets of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.

Where a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]* is centrally cleared through a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(24)|CCP]]* authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] or recognised in accordance with Article 25 of that Regulation, the cash provided by an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* as part of each *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]* shall not exceed 15 % of the assets of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.
## 6.
Notwithstanding the individual limits laid down in paragraphs 1 and 4, an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall not combine, where to do so would result in an investment of more than 15 % of its assets in a single body, any of the following:
### (a)
investments in *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(2)|money market instruments]]*, *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|securitisations]]* and ABCPs issued by that body;
### (b)
deposits made with that body;
### (c)
financial derivative instruments that are not centrally cleared through a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(24)|CCP]]* authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] or recognised in accordance with Article 25 of that Regulation, giving counterparty risk exposure to that body.

By way of derogation from the diversification requirement provided for in the first subparagraph, where the structure of the financial market in the Member State in which the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* is domiciled is such that there are insufficient viable financial institutions to meet that diversification requirement and it is not economically feasible for the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* to use financial institutions in another Member State, the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* may combine the types of investments referred to in points (a) to (c) up to a maximum investment of 20 % of its assets in a single body.
## 7.
By way of derogation from point (a) of paragraph 1, the competent authority of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* may authorise an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* to invest, in accordance with the principle of risk-spreading, up to 100 % of its assets in different *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(2)|money market instruments]]* issued or guaranteed separately or jointly by the Union, the national, regional and local administrations of the Member States or their central banks, the European Central Bank, the European Investment Bank, the European Investment Fund, the European Stability Mechanism, the European Financial Stability Facility, a central authority or central bank of a third country, the International Monetary Fund, the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development, the Council of Europe Development Bank, the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development, the Bank for International Settlements, or any other relevant international financial institution or organisation to which one or more Member States belong.

The first subparagraph shall only apply where all of the following requirements are met:
### (a)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* holds *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(2)|money market instruments]]* from at least six different issues by the issuer;
### (b)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* limits the investment in *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(2)|money market instruments]]* from the same issue to a maximum of 30 % of its assets;
### (c)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* makes express reference, in its fund rules or instruments of incorporation, to all administrations, institutions or organisations referred to in the first subparagraph that issue or guarantee separately or jointly *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(2)|money market instruments]]* in which it intends to invest more than 5 % of its assets;
### (d)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* includes a prominent statement in its prospectus and marketing communications drawing attention to the use of the derogation and indicating all administrations, institutions or organisations referred to in the first subparagraph that issue or guarantee separately or jointly *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(2)|money market instruments]]* in which it intends to invest more than 5 % of its assets.
## 8.
Notwithstanding the individual limits laid down in paragraph 1, an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* may invest no more than 10 % of its assets in bonds issued by a single *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(16)|credit institution]]* that has its registered office in a Member State and is subject by law to special public supervision designed to protect bond-holders. In particular, sums deriving from the issue of those bonds shall be invested in accordance with the law in assets which, during the whole period of validity of the bonds, are capable of covering claims attaching to the bonds and which, in the event of failure of the issuer, would be used on a priority basis for the reimbursement of the principal and payment of the accrued interest.

Where an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* invests more than 5 % of its assets in the bonds referred to in the first subparagraph issued by a single issuer, the total value of those investments shall not exceed 40 % of the value of the assets of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.
## 9.
Notwithstanding the individual limits laid down in paragraph 1, an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* may invest no more than 20 % of its assets in bonds issued by a single *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(16)|credit institution]]* where the requirements set out in point (f) of [[2017R1131_EN.10#1|Article 10(1)]] or point (c) of Article 11(1) of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2015/61 are met, including any possible investment in assets referred to in paragraph 8 of this Article.

Where an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* invests more than 5 % of its assets in the bonds referred to in the first subparagraph issued by a single issuer, the total value of those investments shall not exceed 60 % of the value of the assets of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*, including any possible investment in assets referred to in paragraph 8, respecting the limits set out therein.
## 10.
Companies which are included in the same group for the purposes of consolidated accounts under Directive 2013/34/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council or in accordance with recognised international accounting rules, shall be regarded as a single body for the purpose of calculating the limits referred to in paragraphs 1 to 6 of this Article.
