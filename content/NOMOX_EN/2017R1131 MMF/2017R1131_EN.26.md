# Article 26 - MMF credit ratings


An *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* that solicits or finances an external credit rating shall do so in accordance with Regulation (EC) No 1060/2009. The *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* or the manager of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall clearly indicate in the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*'s prospectus, and in all communication to investors in which the external credit rating is mentioned, that the rating was solicited or financed by the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* or by the manager of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.
