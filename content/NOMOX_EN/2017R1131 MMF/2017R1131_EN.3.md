# Article 3 - Types of MMFs

## 1.
*[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMFs]]* shall be set up as one of the following types:
### (a)
a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(13)|VNAV MMF]]*;
### (b)
a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(11)|public debt CNAV MMF]]*;
### (c)
a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(12)|LVNAV MMF]]*.
## 2.
The authorisation of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall explicitly state the type of *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*, from those set out in paragraph 1.
