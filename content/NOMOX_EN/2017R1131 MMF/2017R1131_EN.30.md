# Article 30 - Calculation of NAV per unit or share

## 1.
An *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall calculate a NAV per unit or share as the difference between the sum of all assets of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* and the sum of all liabilities of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* valued in accordance with *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(8)|mark-to-market]]* or *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(9)|mark-to-model]]*, or both, divided by the number of outstanding units or shares of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.
## 2.
The NAV per unit or share shall be rounded to the nearest basis point or its equivalent when the NAV is published in a currency unit.
## 3.
The NAV per unit or share of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall be calculated and published at least daily on the public section of the website of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.
