# Article 29 - Valuation of MMFs

## 1.
The assets of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall be valued on at least a daily basis.
## 2.
The assets of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall be valued by using *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(8)|mark-to-market]]* whenever possible.
## 3.
When using mark- to-market:
### (a)
the asset of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall be valued at the more prudent side of bid and offer unless the asset can be closed out at mid-market;
### (b)
only good quality market data shall be used; such data shall be assessed on the basis of all of the following factors:
#### (i)
the number and quality of the counterparties;
#### (ii)
the volume and turnover in the market of the asset of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*;
#### (iii)
the issue size and the portion of the issue that the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* plans to buy or sell.
## 4.
Where use of *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(8)|mark-to-market]]* is not possible or the market data is not of sufficient quality, an asset of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* shall be valued conservatively by using *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(9)|mark-to-model]]*.

The model shall accurately estimate the intrinsic value of the asset of an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*, based on all of the following up-to-date key factors:
### (a)
the volume and turnover in the market of that asset;
### (b)
the issue size and the portion of the issue that the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* plans to buy or sell;
### (c)
market risk, interest rate risk, credit risk attached to the asset.

When using *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(9)|mark-to-model]]*, the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(10)|amortised cost method]]* shall not be used.
## 5.
A valuation carried out in accordance with paragraphs 2, 3, 4, 6 and 7 shall be communicated to the competent authorities.
## 6.
Notwithstanding paragraphs 2, 3 and 4, the assets of *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(11)|public debt CNAV MMFs]]* may additionally be valued by using the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(10)|amortised cost method]]*.
## 7.
By way of derogation from paragraphs 2 and 4, in addition to the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(8)|mark-to-market]]* referred to in paragraphs 2 and 3 and the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(9)|mark-to-model]]* referred to in paragraph 4, the assets of *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(12)|LVNAV MMFs]]* that have a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(21)|residual maturity]]* of up to 75 days may be valued by using the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(10)|amortised cost method]]*.

The *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(10)|amortised cost method]]* shall only be used for valuing an asset of a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(12)|LVNAV MMF]]* in circumstances where the price of that asset calculated in accordance with paragraphs 2, 3 and 4 does not deviate from the price of that asset calculated in accordance with the first subparagraph of this paragraph by more than 10 basis points. In the event of such a deviation, the price of that asset shall be calculated in accordance with paragraphs 2, 3 and 4.
