# Article 42 - Powers and competences of ESMA

## 1.
ESMA shall have the powers necessary to carry out the tasks attributed to it by this Regulation.
## 2.
ESMA's powers in accordance with Directives 2009/65/EC and 2011/61/EU shall be exercised also with respect to this Regulation and in compliance with Regulation (EC) No 45/2001.
## 3.
For the purpose of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010, this Regulation shall be included under any further legally binding Union act which confers tasks on the Authority referred to in [[2010R1095_EN.1#2|Article 1(2) of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
