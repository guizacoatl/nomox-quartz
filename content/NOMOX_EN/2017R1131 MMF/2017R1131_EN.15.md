# Article 15 - Eligible reverse repurchase agreements

## 1.
A *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]* shall be eligible to be entered into by an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* provided that all of the following conditions are fulfilled:
### (a)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* has the right to terminate the agreement at any time upon giving prior notice of no more than two working days;
### (b)
the market value of the assets received as part of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]* is at all times at least equal to the value of the cash paid out.
## 2.
The assets received by an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* as part of a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]* shall be *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(2)|money market instruments]]* that fulfil the requirements set out in [[2017R1131_EN.10|Article 10]].

The assets received by an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* as part of a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]* shall not be sold, reinvested, pledged or otherwise transferred.
## 3.
*[[2017R1131_EN.2#(7)|Securitisations]]* and ABCPs shall not be received by an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* as part of a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]*.
## 4.
The assets received by an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* as part of a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]* shall be sufficiently diversified with a maximum exposure to a given issuer of 15 % of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*'s NAV, except where those assets take the form of *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(2)|money market instruments]]* that fulfil the requirements of [[2017R1131_EN.17#7|Article 17(7)]]. In addition, the assets received by an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* as part of a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]* shall be issued by an entity that is independent from the counterparty and is expected not to display a high correlation with the performance of the counterparty.
## 5.
An *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* that enters into a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]* shall ensure that it is able to recall the full amount of cash at any time on either an accrued basis or a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(8)|mark-to-market]]* basis. When the cash is recallable at any time on a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(8)|mark-to-market]]* basis, the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(8)|mark-to-market]]* value of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]* shall be used for the calculation of the NAV of the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]*.
## 6.
By way of derogation from paragraph 2 of this Article, an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* may receive as part of a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]* liquid *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(3)|transferable securities]]* or *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(2)|money market instruments]]* other than those that fulfil the requirements set out in [[2017R1131_EN.10|Article 10]] provided that those assets comply with one of the following conditions:
### (a)
they are issued or guaranteed by the Union, a central authority or central bank of a Member State, the European Central Bank, the European Investment Bank, the European Stability Mechanism or the European Financial Stability Facility provided that a favourable assessment has been received pursuant to Articles [[2017R1131_EN.19|19]] to 22;
### (b)
they are issued or guaranteed by a central authority or central bank of a third country, provided that a favourable assessment has been received pursuant to Articles [[2017R1131_EN.19|19]] to 22.
The assets received as part of a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]* in accordance with the first subparagraph of this paragraph shall be disclosed to *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* investors, in accordance with [[2015R2365_EN.13|Article 13 of Regulation (EU) 2365/2015]] of the European Parliament and of the Council.

The assets received as part of a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(5)|reverse repurchase agreement]]* in accordance with the first subparagraph of this paragraph shall fulfil the requirements of [[2017R1131_EN.17#7|Article 17(7)]].
## 7.
The Commission shall be empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2017R1131_EN.45|Article 45]] to supplement this Regulation by specifying quantitative and qualitative liquidity requirements applicable to assets referred to in paragraph 6 and quantitative and qualitative credit quality requirements applicable to assets referred to in point (a) of paragraph 6 of this Article.

For those purposes, the Commission shall take into account the report referred to in [[2013R0575_EN.509#3|Article 509(3) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]].

The Commission shall adopt the delegated act referred to in the first subparagraph no later than 21 January 2018.
