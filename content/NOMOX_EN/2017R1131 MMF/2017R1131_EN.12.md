# Article 12 - Eligible deposits with credit institutions


A deposit with a *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(16)|credit institution]]* shall be eligible for investment by an *[[2017R1131_EN.2#2.|MMF]]* provided that all of the following conditions are fulfilled:
### (a)
the deposit is repayable on demand or is able to be withdrawn at any time;
### (b)
the deposit matures in no more than 12 months;
### (c)
the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(16)|credit institution]]* has its registered office in a Member State or, where the *[[2017R1131_EN.2#(16)|credit institution]]* has its registered office in a third country, it is subject to prudential rules considered equivalent to those laid down in Union law in accordance with the procedure laid down in [[2013R0575_EN.107#4|Article 107(4) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]].
