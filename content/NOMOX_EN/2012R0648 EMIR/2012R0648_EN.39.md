# Article 39 - Segregation and portability

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall keep separate records and accounts that shall enable it, at any time and without delay, to distinguish in accounts with the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* the assets and positions held for the account of one *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* from the assets and positions held for the account of any other *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* and from its own assets.
## 2.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall offer to keep separate records and accounts enabling each *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* to distinguish in accounts with the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* the assets and positions of that *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* from those held for the accounts of its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* (‘*[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|omnibus client segregation]]*’).
## 3.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall offer to keep separate records and accounts enabling each *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* to distinguish in accounts with the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* the assets and positions held for the account of a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|client]]* from those held for the account of other *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* (‘*[[2012R0648_EN.2#3.|individual client segregation]]*’). Upon request, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall offer *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* the possibility to open more accounts in their own name or for the account of their *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*.
## 4.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* shall keep separate records and accounts that enable it to distinguish both in accounts held with the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and in its own accounts its assets and positions from the assets and positions held for the account of its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* at the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 5.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* shall offer its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*, at least, the choice between *[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|omnibus client segregation]]* and *[[2012R0648_EN.2#3.|individual client segregation]]* and inform them of the costs and level of protection referred to in paragraph 7 associated with each option. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|client]]* shall confirm its choice in writing.
## 6.
When a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|client]]* opts for *[[2012R0648_EN.2#3.|individual client segregation]]*, any margin in excess of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|client]]*’s requirement shall also be posted to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and distinguished from the margins of other *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* or *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* and shall not be exposed to losses connected to positions recorded in another account.
## 7.
*[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* and *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* shall publicly disclose the levels of protection and the costs associated with the different levels of segregation that they provide and shall offer those services on reasonable commercial terms. Details of the different levels of segregation shall include a description of the main legal implications of the respective levels of segregation offered including information on the insolvency law applicable in the relevant jurisdictions.
## 8.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have a right of use relating to the margins or default fund contributions collected via a security financial collateral arrangement, within the meaning of Article 2(1)(c) of Directive 2002/47/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 6 June 2002 on financial collateral arrangements provided that the use of such arrangements is provided for in its operating rules. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* shall confirm its acceptance of the operating rules in writing. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall publicly disclose that right of use, which shall be exercised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.47|Article 47]].
## 9.
The requirement to distinguish assets and positions with the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in accounts is satisfied where:
### (a)
the assets and positions are recorded in separate accounts;
### (b)
the netting of positions recorded on different accounts is prevented;
### (c)
the assets covering the positions recorded in an account are not exposed to losses connected to positions recorded in another account.
## 10.
Assets refer to collateral held to cover positions and include the right to the transfer of assets equivalent to that collateral or the proceeds of the realisation of any collateral, but does not include default fund contributions.
## 11.
Member States' national insolvency laws shall not prevent a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* from acting in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.48#5|Article 48(5)]], (6) and (7) with regard to the assets and positions recorded in accounts as referred to in paragraphs 2 to 5 of this Article.
