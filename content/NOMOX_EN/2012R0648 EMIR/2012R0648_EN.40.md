# Article 40 - Exposure management


A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall measure and assess its liquidity and credit exposures to each *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* and, where relevant, to another *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* with which it has concluded an *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangement]]*, on a near to real-time basis. A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have access in a timely manner and on a non-discriminatory basis to the relevant pricing sources to effectively measure its exposures. This shall be done on a reasonable cost basis.

Without prejudice to [[2012R0648_EN.1#4|Article 1(4)]] and (5), and with the objective of facilitating central *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* by public sector entities, ESMA shall, by 25 June 2026, issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] specifying the method to be used by *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* authorised under [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]] of this Regulation for the calculation of exposures and of the contributions, if any, to the financial resources of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* by public sector entities participating in such *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*, duly taking account of the mandate of public sector entities.
