# Article 4 - Transitional provision


By way of derogation from [[2014R0484_EN.2#2|Article 2(2)]], during the period from the date of application of this Regulation until 31 December 2014, CCPs shall report the information referred to in that paragraph at the latest by fifteen working days after the reference day, or earlier where possible.
