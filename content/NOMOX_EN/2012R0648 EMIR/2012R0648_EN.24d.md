# Article 24d - Decision making within the Board of Supervisors


Where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* submits draft decisions to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors pursuant to Articles [[2012R0648_EN.25|25]](2), 25(2a), 25(2b), 25(2c), 25(5), 25p, 85(6), 89(3b) of this Regulation and additionally only for Tier 2 *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* in accordance with Articles [[2012R0648_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648_EN.44|44]], [[2012R0648_EN.46|46]], [[2012R0648_EN.50|50]] and [[2012R0648_EN.54|54]] of this Regulation, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors shall decide on those draft decisions in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.44|Article 44 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] within 10 working days.

Where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* submits draft decisions to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors pursuant to Articles other than those referred to in the first subparagraph, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors shall decide on those draft decisions in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.44|Article 44 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] within three working days.
