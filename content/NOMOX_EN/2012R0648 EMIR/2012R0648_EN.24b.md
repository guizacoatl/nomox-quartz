# Article 24b - Consultation of central banks of issue

## 1.
With regard to supervisory assessments conducted in relation to, and decisions to be taken pursuant to, Articles [[2012R0648_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648_EN.44|44]], [[2012R0648_EN.46|46]], [[2012R0648_EN.50|50]] and [[2012R0648_EN.54|54]] in relation to Tier 2 *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall consult the central banks of issue referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.25#3|Article 25(3)]], point (f). Each central bank of issue may respond. Where the central bank of issue decides to respond, it shall do so within 10 working days of receipt of the draft decision. In emergency situations, that period shall not exceed 24 hours. Where a central bank of issue proposes amendments or objects to assessments related to, or draft decisions pursuant to Articles [[2012R0648_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648_EN.44|44]], [[2012R0648_EN.46|46]], [[2012R0648_EN.50|50]] and [[2012R0648_EN.54|54]], it shall provide full and detailed reasons, in writing. Upon conclusion of the period for consultation, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall duly consider the response and any amendments proposed by the central banks of issue and provide its assessment to the central bank of issue.
## 2.
Where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* does not reflect in its draft decision the amendments proposed by a central bank of issue, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall inform that central bank of issue in writing stating its full reasons for not taking into account the amendments proposed by that central bank of issue, providing an explanation for any deviations from those amendments. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall submit to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors the responses received and the amendments proposed by central banks of issue and its explanations for not taking them into account together with its draft decision.
## 3.
With regard to decisions to be taken pursuant to Articles [[2012R0648_EN.25|25]](2c) and 85(6), the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall seek the agreement of the central banks of issue referred to in point (f) of [[2012R0648_EN.25#3|Article 25(3)]] for matters relating to the currencies they issue. The agreement of each central bank of issue shall be deemed to be given, unless the central bank of issue proposes amendments or objects within 10 working days of the transmission of the draft decision. Where a central bank of issue proposes amendments or objects to a draft decision, it shall provide full and detailed reasons, in writing. Where a central bank of issue proposes amendments with respect to matters relating to the currency it issues, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* may only submit to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors the draft decision as amended with respect to those matters. Where a central bank of issue objects with respect to matters relating to the currency it issues, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall not include those matters in the draft decision it submits to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors for adoption.
