# Article 17c - Central database

## 1.
ESMA shall establish and maintain a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* providing access to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* and ESMA (‘*[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|registered recipients]]*’), as well as to the members of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] for the relevant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* where required under a relevant article, to all documents registered within the database for the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, and to the other recipients identified under this Regulation. ESMA shall ensure that the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* performs the functions under this Article.

ESMA shall announce the establishment of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* on its website.
## 2.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall submit the applications referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]], [[2012R0648_EN.15#1|Article 15(1)]], second subparagraph, Article 49 and [[2012R0648_EN.49a|Article 49a]] via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*. An acknowledgement of receipt shall be sent via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* within two working days of submission of the application.

A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall upload to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* promptly all documents it is required to provide under the authorisation processes referred to in Articles [[2012R0648_EN.14|14]] and [[2012R0648_EN.15|15]] or validation processes referred to in Articles [[2012R0648_EN.49|49]] and [[2012R0648_EN.49a|49a]], as applicable. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|registered recipients]]* shall upload promptly all documents they receive from the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in relation to an application referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph unless the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has already uploaded such documents.

A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have access to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* as regards the documents it submitted to that *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* or the documents transmitted to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* through that *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* by any of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|registered recipients]]* or the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]].
## 3.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall submit its request for an opinion as referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.17b|Article 17b]] via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*.
## 4.
Questions submitted to, or information requested from, a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* by ESMA, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* or the members of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] during periods for assessment under Articles [[2012R0648_EN.17|17]], [[2012R0648_EN.17a|17a]], [[2012R0648_EN.17b|17b]], [[2012R0648_EN.49|49]] and [[2012R0648_EN.49a|49a]] shall be submitted and answered by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*.
## 5.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall notify the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* concerned via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* where a decision, report or other measure has been taken, as applicable, pursuant to Articles [[2012R0648_EN.14|14]], [[2012R0648_EN.15|15]], [[2012R0648_EN.15a|15a]], [[2012R0648_EN.17|17]], [[2012R0648_EN.17a|17a]], [[2012R0648_EN.17b|17b]], [[2012R0648_EN.20|20]], [[2012R0648_EN.21|21]] and [[2012R0648_EN.30|30]] to 33, 35, 37, 41, 49, 49a, 51 and 54 and of any decisions that the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* voluntarily decides to share with the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*.
## 6.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* shall be designed to automatically inform the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|registered recipients]]* when changes have been made to its content, including the uploading, deletion or replacement of documents, submission of questions and requests for information.
## 7.
Members of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall have access to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* for the performance of their tasks pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.24a#7|Article 24a(7)]]. The Chair of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* may limit access to some of the documents for the members of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.24a#2|Article 24a(2)]], point (c) and point (d)(ii), where justified based on confidentiality concerns.
