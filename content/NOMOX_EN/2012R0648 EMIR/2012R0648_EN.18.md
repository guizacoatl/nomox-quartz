# Article 18 - College

## 1.
Within 30 calendar days of the submission of the notification referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.17#2|Article 17(2)]], second subparagraph, point (a), the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall establish a college to facilitate the exercise of the tasks referred to in Articles [[2012R0648_EN.15|15]], [[2012R0648_EN.17|17]], [[2012R0648_EN.17a|17a]], [[2012R0648_EN.20|20]], [[2012R0648_EN.21|21]], [[2012R0648_EN.30|30]], [[2012R0648_EN.31|31]], [[2012R0648_EN.32|32]], [[2012R0648_EN.35|35]], [[2012R0648_EN.37|37]], [[2012R0648_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648_EN.49|49]], [[2012R0648_EN.51|51]] and [[2012R0648_EN.54|54]]. That college shall be co-chaired and managed by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* and any of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.24a#2|Article 24a(2)]], point (b) (the ‘*[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|co-chairs]]*’).
## 2.
The college shall consist of:
### (a)
the Chair or any of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* referred to in points (a) and (b) of [[2012R0648_EN.24a#2|Article 24a(2)]];
### (b)
the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*;
### (c)
the competent authorities responsible for the supervision of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* which are established in the three Member States with the largest contributions to the default fund of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.42|Article 42]] of this Regulation on an aggregate basis over a one-year period, including, where relevant, the ECB in the framework of the tasks concerning the prudential supervision of credit institutions within the single supervisory mechanism conferred upon it in accordance with Council Regulation (EU) No 1024/2013;
### (ca)
the competent authorities responsible for the supervision of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, other than those referred to in point (c), subject to the consent of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*. Those competent authorities shall request the consent of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* to participate in the college, justifying the request based on their assessment of the impact that the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s financial distress could have on the financial stability of their respective Member State. Where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* does not grant the request, it shall provide full and detailed reasons in writing;
### (d)
the competent authorities responsible for the supervision of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(4)|trading venues]]* served by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (e)
the competent authorities supervising *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* with which *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangements]]* have been established;
### (f)
the competent authorities supervising central securities depositories to which the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is linked;
### (g)
the relevant members of the ESCB responsible for the oversight of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and the relevant members of the ESCB responsible for the oversight of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* with which *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangements]]* have been established;
### (h)
the central banks of issue of the most relevant Union currencies of the financial instruments cleared;
#### (i)
the central banks of issue of the Union currencies of the financial instruments cleared or to be cleared by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* other than those referred to in point (h), subject to the consent of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*. Those central banks of issue shall request the consent of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* to participate in the college, justifying the request based on their assessment of the impact that the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s financial distress could have on their respective currency of issue. Where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* does not grant the request, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall provide full and detailed reasons in writing.

The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall publish on its website a list of the members of the college. That list shall be updated by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* without undue delay after any change to the composition of the college. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall notify that list to ESMA within 30 calendar days of the college's establishment or change in its composition. Upon reception of the notification by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*, ESMA shall publish on its website without undue delay the list of the members of that college.
## 3.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of a Member State which is not a member of the college may request from the college any information relevant for the performance of its supervisory duties.
## 4.
The college shall, without prejudice to the responsibilities of competent authorities under this Regulation, ensure:
### (a)
the preparation of the opinion referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.19|Article 19]];
### (b)
the exchange of information, including requests for information pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.84|Article 84]];
### (c)
agreement on the voluntary entrustment of tasks among its members;
### (d)
the coordination of supervisory examination programmes based on a risk assessment of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*; and
### (e)
the determination of procedures and contingency plans to address emergency situations, as referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.24|Article 24]].

The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|co-chairs]]* shall decide the dates of the college meetings and establish the agenda of such meetings.

In order to facilitate the performance of the tasks assigned to colleges pursuant to the first subparagraph of this paragraph, members of the college referred to in paragraph 2 shall be entitled to contribute to the setting of the agenda of the college meetings, in particular by adding points to the agenda of a meeting taking into consideration the outcome of the work carried out by the Joint Monitoring Mechanism.
## 5.
The establishment and functioning of the college shall be based on a written agreement between all its members.

That agreement shall determine the practical arrangements for the functioning of the college, including detailed rules on:
#### (i)
voting procedures as referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.19#3|Article 19(3)]];
#### (ii)
the procedures for setting the agenda of college meetings;
#### (iii)
the frequency of the college meetings;
#### (iv)
the format and scope of the information to be provided by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*'s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* to the college members, especially with regard to the information to be provided in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.21#4|Article 21(4)]];
#### (v)
the appropriate minimum timeframes for the assessment of the relevant documentation by the college members;
#### (vi)
the modalities of communication between college members;

The agreement may also determine tasks to be entrusted to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*, ESMA or another member of the college. In the event of a disagreement between the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|co-chairs]]*, the final decision shall be taken by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*, who shall provide ESMA with a reasoned explanation of its decision.
## 6.
In order to ensure the consistent and coherent functioning of colleges across the Union, ESMA shall, in cooperation with the ESCB, develop draft regulatory technical standards specifying the conditions under which the Union currencies referred to in point (h) of paragraph 2 are to be considered as the most relevant and the details of the practical arrangements referred to in paragraph 5.

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 2 January 2021.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
