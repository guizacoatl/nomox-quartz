# Article 59 - Notification of ESMA decisions relating to registration

## 1.
Where ESMA adopts a registration decision or a decision refusing or withdrawing registration, it shall notify the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* within five working days with a fully reasoned explanation of its decision.

ESMA shall, without undue delay, notify the relevant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* referred to in Article 57(1) of its decision.
## 2.
ESMA shall communicate any decision taken in accordance with paragraph 1 to the Commission.
## 3.
ESMA shall publish on its website a list of trade repositories registered in accordance with this Regulation. That list shall be updated within five working days following the adoption of a decision under paragraph 1.
