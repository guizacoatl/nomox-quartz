# Article 7 - Implementation through contractual arrangements


Euro area NCBs shall take the necessary measures to implement Articles [[2025R1734_EN.2|2]] to 6 through contractual arrangements with eligible CCPs where relevant, by the date referred to in [[2025R1734_EN.8#2|Article 8(2)]].
