# Article 6 - Penalties in relation to safeguards and discretionary measures

## 1.
Unless decided otherwise by the Governing Council, the respective euro area NCB shall apply penalties in the following cases:
### (a)
where a CCP’s access to the CCP credit facility is limited in accordance with [[2025R1734_EN.5|Article 5]] and the CCP exceeds the restricted level of access; or
### (b)
where a CCP resorts to the CCP credit facility in breach of [[2025R1734_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]], point (a).
## 2.
The penalties referred to in paragraph 1 shall take the form of penalty interest rates, which shall be calculated in line with:
### (a)
Annex I, Part II, [[2025R1734_EN.12a#3|Article 12a(3)]], point (a), to Guideline (EU) 2022/912 (ECB/2022/8), if the case set out in paragraph 1, point (a) or point (b), occurs for the first time within any 12-month period;
### (b)
Annex I, Part II, [[2025R1734_EN.12a#3|Article 12a(3)]], point (b), to Guideline (EU) 2022/912 (ECB/2022/8), if the case set out in paragraph 1, point (a) or point (b), occurs for at least the second time within the same 12-month period.
## 3.
The penalty interest rates referred to in paragraph 2 shall be applied to:
### (a)
in the case set out in paragraph 1, point (a), the amount used under the CCP credit facility in excess of the restricted level of access set as a permitted limit; or
### (b)
in the case set out in paragraph 1, point (b), the full amount used under the CCP credit facility.
