# Article 2 - Requirements relating to safeguards on financial soundness

## 1.
In order to safeguard their financial soundness, eligible CCPs shall meet the following requirements on an ongoing basis:
### (a)
the capital requirements pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
### (b)
the margin requirements pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.41|Article 41 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
### (c)
the requirements in relation to pre-funded financial resources pursuant to Articles [[2025R1734_EN.42|42]] and [[2025R1734_EN.43|43]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012.
## 2.
In the event of a default of a clearing member of a CCP where the CCP conducts its default management process in accordance with Articles [[2025R1734_EN.45|45]] and [[2025R1734_EN.48|48]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 and as a result uses the margins posted by the defaulted clearing member and the pre-funded financial resources referred to in Articles [[2025R1734_EN.42|42]] and [[2025R1734_EN.43|43]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012, the following shall apply:
### (a)
the CCP shall have sufficient pre-funded financial resources, including the margins posted by the defaulted clearing member and the pre-funded financial resources, as set out in Articles [[2025R1734_EN.42|42]] and [[2025R1734_EN.43|43]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012, to cover credit losses incurred as a result of the default management process;
### (b)
on the basis of the assessment conducted in accordance with [[2025R1734_EN.4|Article 4]], the Eurosystem central banks may decide to temporarily waive the requirement set out in paragraph 1, point (c), where the CCP presents a plan to replenish pre-funded financial resources as set out in Articles [[2025R1734_EN.42|42]] and [[2025R1734_EN.43|43]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 in a credible and timely manner.
## 3.
No CCP deemed to be failing or likely to fail as determined under [[2021R0023_EN.22|Article 22 of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]] of the European Parliament and of the Council

shall be considered financially sound.
