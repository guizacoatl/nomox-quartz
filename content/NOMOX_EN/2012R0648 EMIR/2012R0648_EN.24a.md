# Article 24a - CCP Supervisory Committee

## 1.
ESMA shall establish a permanent internal committee pursuant to [[2010R1095_EN.41|Article 41 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] for the purposes of preparing draft decisions for adoption by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors and carrying out the tasks set out in paragraphs 7, 9 and 10 of this Article (‘*[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]*’).
## 2.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall be composed of:
### (a)
the Chair, who shall be voting;
### (b)
two *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]*, who shall be voting;
### (c)
the competent authorities of Member States referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.22|Article 22]] of this Regulation with an authorised *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, who shall be voting; where a Member State has designated several competent authorities, each of the designated competent authorities of this Member State may decide to appoint one representative for the purposes of participation pursuant to this point, however, for the voting procedures set out in [[2012R0648_EN.24c|Article 24c]], the representatives of the respective Member State shall together be considered as one voting member;
### (d)
The following central banks of issue:
#### (i)
where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* convenes in relation to third-country *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*, in respect of the preparation of all decisions pertaining to Articles referred to in paragraph 10 of this Article in relation to Tier 2 *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* and to [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]](2a), the central banks of issue referred to in point (f) of [[2012R0648_EN.25#3|Article 25(3)]] that have requested membership of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]*, who shall be non-voting;
#### (ii)
where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* convenes in relation to *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]], in the context of discussions pertaining to paragraph 7 of this Article, the central banks of issue of the Union currencies of the financial instruments cleared by authorised *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* that have requested membership of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]*, who shall be non-voting.

Membership for the purpose of subpoints (i) and (ii) shall be granted automatically upon a one-off written request addressed to the Chair.
## 3.
The Chair may invite as observers to the meetings of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]*, where appropriate, members of the colleges referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]], representatives from the relevant authorities of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*, where known, and representatives from the relevant Union institutions and bodies.
## 4.
Meetings of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall be convened by its Chair at his or her own initiative or at the request of any of its voting members. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall meet at least five times a year.
## 5.
The Chair and the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall be full-time, independent professionals. They shall be appointed by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors on the basis of merit, skills, knowledge of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]*, post-trading, prudential supervision and financial matters, as well as of experience relevant to *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* supervision and regulation, following an open selection procedure.

Before the appointment of the Chair and of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]*, and up to one month after the selection by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors, which shall submit its shortlist of selected candidates respecting gender balance to the European Parliament, the European Parliament, after having heard the selected candidates, shall approve or reject them.

Where the Chair or any of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* no longer fulfils the conditions required for the performance of his or her duties or has been found guilty of serious misconduct, the Council may, on a proposal from the Commission which has been approved by the European Parliament, adopt an implementing decision to remove him or her from office. The Council shall act by qualified majority.

The European Parliament or the Council may inform the Commission that they consider the conditions for the removal of the Chair or one of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* to be fulfilled, to which the Commission shall respond.

The term of office of the Chair and the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall be five years and may be extended once.
## 6.
The Chair and the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall not hold any office at national, Union, or international level. They shall act independently and objectively in the sole interest of the Union as a whole and shall neither seek nor take instructions from Union institutions or bodies, from any government of a Member State or from any other public or private body.

Neither Member States, the Union institutions or bodies, nor any other public or private body shall seek to influence the Chair and the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* in the performance of their tasks.

In accordance with the Staff Regulations referred to in [[2010R1095_EN.68|Article 68 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]], the Chair and the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall, after leaving service, continue to be bound by the duty to behave with integrity and discretion as regards the acceptance of certain appointments or benefits.
## 7.
In relation to *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* authorised or applying for authorisation in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]], the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall, for the purpose of [[2012R0648_EN.23a|Article 23a]], prepare decisions and carry out the tasks entrusted to ESMA in [[2012R0648_EN.23a|Article 23a]] and in the following points:
### (a)
at least annually, conduct a peer review analysis of the supervisory activities of all competent authorities in relation to the authorisation and the supervision of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.30|Article 30 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]];
### (b)
at least annually, initiate and coordinate Union-wide assessments of the resilience of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* to adverse market developments in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.32#2|Article 32(2) of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]], taking into account, where possible, the aggregate effect of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* recovery and resolution arrangements on Union financial stability;
### (ba)
at least annually, discuss and identify supervisory priorities for *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]] of this Regulation in order to feed into the preparation of Union-wide strategic supervisory priorities by ESMA in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.29a|Article 29a of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]];
### (bb)
consider, in cooperation with EBA, EIOPA, and the ECB in carrying out its tasks within a single supervisory mechanism under Regulation (EU) No 1024/2013, any cross-border risks arising from *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*’ activities, including due to *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*’ interconnectedness, interlinkages and concentration risks due to such cross-border connections;
### (bc)
prepare draft opinions for adoption by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors in accordance with Articles [[2012R0648_EN.17|17]] and [[2012R0648_EN.17b|17b]], draft validations for adoption by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.49|Article 49]] and draft decisions for adoption by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.49a|Article 49a]];
### (bd)
provide input to the competent authorities pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.17a|Article 17a]];
### (be)
inform the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors where a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* does not comply or does not intend to comply with ESMA’s opinions or with any conditions or recommendations contained therein, including the reasoning from the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*, in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.17|Article 17]](3c) and [[2012R0648_EN.17b#4|Article 17b(4)]];
### (c)
promote the regular exchange and discussion among competent authorities designated in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.22#1|Article 22(1)]] of this Regulation in relation to:
#### (i)
relevant supervisory activities and decisions that have been adopted by the competent authorities referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.22|Article 22]] when carrying out their duties in accordance with this Regulation regarding the authorisation and supervision of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* established in their territory;
#### (ii)
draft decisions submitted to ESMA by a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* in accordance with the first subparagraph of [[2012R0648_EN.23a#2|Article 23a(2)]];
#### (iii)
draft decisions submitted to ESMA by a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* on a voluntary basis in accordance with the second subparagraph of [[2012R0648_EN.23a#2|Article 23a(2)]];
#### (iv)
relevant market developments, including situations or events which impact or are likely to impact the prudential or financial soundness or the resilience of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]] or their *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]*;
### (d)
be informed of and discuss all opinions and recommendations adopted by colleges pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.19|Article 19]] of this Regulation, in order to contribute to the consistent and coherent functioning of the colleges and to foster coherence in the application of this Regulation among them.

For the purposes of points (a) to (d) of the first subparagraph, competent authorities shall provide ESMA with all relevant information and documentation without undue delay.

ESMA shall on a yearly basis report to the Commission on the cross-border risks arising from *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*’ activities referred to in the first subparagraph, point (bb).
## 8.
Where the activities or the exchange referred to in points (a) to (d) of paragraph 7 expose a lack of convergence and coherence in the application of this Regulation, ESMA shall issue the necessary guidelines or recommendations pursuant to [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] or opinions pursuant to [[2010R1095_EN.29|Article 29 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]]. Where an assessment referred to in point (b) of paragraph 7 exposes shortcomings in the resilience of one or more *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*, ESMA shall issue the necessary recommendations pursuant to [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
## 9.
In addition, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* may:
### (a)
based on its activities in accordance with points (a) to (d) of paragraph 7, request the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors to consider whether the adoption of guidelines, recommendations and opinions by ESMA is necessary in order to address a lack of convergence and coherence in the application of this Regulation among competent authorities and colleges. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors shall duly consider such requests and provide an appropriate response;
### (b)
submit opinions to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors on decisions to be taken in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.44|Article 44 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]], with the exception of decisions referred to in Articles [[2012R0648_EN.17|17]] and [[2012R0648_EN.19|19]] of that Regulation, relating to tasks conferred on the competent authorities referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.22|Article 22]] of this Regulation.
## 10.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall, in relation to third-country *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*, prepare draft decisions to be taken by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors and carry out the tasks entrusted to ESMA in Articles [[2012R0648_EN.25|25]], [[2012R0648_EN.25a|25a]], [[2012R0648_EN.25b|25b]] and [[2012R0648_EN.25f|25f]] to 25q and 85(6).
## 11.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall, in relation to third-country *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*, share with the third-country *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.25c|Article 25c]] the agendas of its meetings before those meetings take place, the minutes of its meetings, the complete draft decisions it submits to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors and the final decisions adopted by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors.
## 12.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall be supported by dedicated staff from ESMA, possessing sufficient knowledge, skills and experience, in order to:
### (a)
prepare the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* meetings;
### (b)
prepare the analyses necessary for the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* to carry out its tasks;
### (c)
support the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* in its international cooperation at administrative level.
## 13.
For the purposes of this Regulation, ESMA shall ensure structural separation between the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* and other functions referred to in Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
