# Article 71 - Withdrawal of registration

## 1.
Without prejudice to [[2012R0648_EN.73|Article 73]], ESMA shall withdraw the registration of a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]*:
### (a)
expressly renounces the registration or has provided no services for the preceding six months;
### (b)
obtained the registration by making false statements or by any other irregular means;
### (c)
no longer meets the conditions under which it was registered.
## 2.
ESMA shall, without undue delay, notify the relevant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* referred to in Article 57(1) of a decision to withdraw the registration of a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]*.
## 3.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of a Member State in which the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* performs its services and activities and which considers that one of the conditions referred to in paragraph 1 has been met, may request ESMA to examine whether the conditions for the withdrawal of registration of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* concerned are met. Where ESMA decides not to withdraw the registration of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* concerned, it shall provide full reasons.
## 4.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* referred to in paragraph 3 shall be the authority designated under [[2012R0648_EN.22|Article 22]].
