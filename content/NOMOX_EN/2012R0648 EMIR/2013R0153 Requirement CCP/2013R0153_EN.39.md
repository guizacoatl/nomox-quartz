# Article 39 - Financial instruments


For the purposes of [[2012R0648_EN.46#1|Article 46(1) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], financial instruments, bank guarantees and gold that meet the conditions set out in Annex I shall be considered as, highly liquid collateral.

Until 7 September 2024, for the purposes of [[2012R0648_EN.46#1|Article 46(1) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], public guarantees that meet the conditions set out in Annex I shall be considered as highly liquid collateral.
