> Level 1 reference(s): [[2012R0648_EN.41]]# Article 25 - Time horizon for the calculation of historical volatility

## 1.
A CCP shall ensure that according to its model methodology and its validation process established in accordance with Chapter XII, *[[2013R0153_EN.1#(5)|initial margins]]* cover at least with the *[[2013R0153_EN.1#(2)|confidence interval]]* defined in [[2013R0153_EN.24|Article 24]] and for the *[[2013R0153_EN.1#(8)|liquidation period]]* defined in [[2013R0153_EN.26|Article 26]] the exposures resulting from historical volatility calculated based on data covering at least the latest 12 months.

A CCP shall ensure that the data used for calculating historical volatility capture a full range of market conditions, including periods of stress.
## 2.
A CCP may use any other time horizon for the calculation of historical volatility provided that the use of such time horizon results in margin requirements at least as high as those obtained with the time period defined in paragraph 1.
## 3.
Margin parameters for financial instruments without a historical observation period shall be based on conservative assumptions. A CCP shall promptly adapt the calculation of the required *[[2013R0153_EN.1#(4)|margins]]* based on the analysis of the price history of the new financial instruments.
