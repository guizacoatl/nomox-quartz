> Level 1 reference(s): [[2012R0648_EN.47]]# Article 43 - Highly liquid financial instruments


For the purposes of [[2012R0648_EN.47#1|Article 47(1) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], debt instruments can be considered highly liquid, bearing minimal credit and market risk if they are debt instruments meeting each of the conditions set out in Annex II.
