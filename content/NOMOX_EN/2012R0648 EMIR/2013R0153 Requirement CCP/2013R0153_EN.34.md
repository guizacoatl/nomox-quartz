# Article 34 - Concentration risk

## 1.
A CCP shall closely monitor and control the concentration of its liquidity risk exposure, including its exposures to the entities listed in [[2013R0153_EN.32#4|Article 32(4)]] and to entities in the same group.
## 2.
A CCP’s liquidity risk management framework shall include the application of exposure and concentration limits.
## 3.
A CCP shall define processes and procedures for breaches of concentration limits.
