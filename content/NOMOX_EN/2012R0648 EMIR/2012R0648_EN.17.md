# Article 17 - Procedure for granting and refusing authorisation

## 1.
The applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall submit an application for authorisation as referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.14#1|Article 14(1)]] or an application for an extension of an existing authorisation as referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.15#1|Article 15(1)]] in an electronic format via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*. The application shall be immediately shared via that *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* with the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*, ESMA and the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]].

The applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall provide all information necessary to demonstrate that it has established, at the time of the initial authorisation, all the necessary arrangements to meet the requirements laid down in this Regulation. Where a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is applying for an extension of an existing authorisation pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.15|Article 15]], it shall provide all information necessary to demonstrate that, at the time such an extension is granted, it will have established all additional arrangements to meet any requirements laid down in this Regulation in respect of such an extension.

In accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.17c|Article 17c]], an acknowledgement of receipt of the application shall be sent via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* within two working days of submission of that application under the first subparagraph of this paragraph.
## 2.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall, following the acknowledgement of receipt referred to in paragraph 1, third subparagraph, notify the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* whether the application contains the documents and information required.

The notification shall be sent within:
### (a)
20 working days of the acknowledgment of the receipt, where the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has applied for an authorisation pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.14#1|Article 14(1)]]; or
### (b)
10 working days of the acknowledgment of the receipt, where the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has applied for an extension of an existing authorisation pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.15#1|Article 15(1)]].
Where, during the applicable period specified under the second subparagraph of this paragraph, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* decides that not all documents or information required pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.14#6|Article 14(6)]] and (7) or [[2012R0648_EN.15#3|Article 15(3)]] and (4) have been submitted, it shall request the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to submit such additional documents or information, via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*. The application for authorisation or the application for the extension of authorisation shall be rejected where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* decides that the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has failed to comply with any such request. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall inform the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* thereof via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*.
## 3.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall conduct a risk assessment of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s compliance with the relevant requirements laid down in this Regulation within the period specified under the second subparagraph (the ‘*[[2012R0648_EN.2#3.|risk assessment period]]*’).

The risk assessment shall be carried out within:
### (a)
80 working days of the confirmation set out in paragraph 2, second subparagraph, point (a), where an application is made under [[2012R0648_EN.14#1|Article 14(1)]]; or
### (b)
40 working days of the confirmation set out in paragraph 2, second subparagraph, point (b), where an application is made under [[2012R0648_EN.15#1|Article 15(1)]].
By the end of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#3.|risk assessment period]]*, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall submit its draft decision and report to ESMA and the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*.

Following receipt of the draft decision and report referred to in the third subparagraph of this paragraph, and on the basis of the findings therein, the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] shall within 15 working days adopt an opinion pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.19|Article 19]] determining whether the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* complies with the requirements laid down in this Regulation and transmit it to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* and ESMA in an electronic format via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*.

The college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] may include in its opinion any conditions or recommendations it considers necessary to mitigate any shortcomings in the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s risk management.

Following receipt of the draft decision and report referred to in the third subparagraph of this paragraph, ESMA shall within 15 working days adopt an opinion determining whether the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* complies with the requirements laid down in this Regulation in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.23a#1|Article 23a(1)]], point (e), [[2012R0648_EN.23a#2|Article 23a(2)]], and [[2012R0648_EN.24a#7|Article 24a(7)]], first subparagraph, point (bc), and transmit it to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* and the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]].

ESMA may include in its opinion any conditions or recommendations it considers necessary to mitigate any shortcomings in the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s risk management in relation to identified cross-border risks or risks to the financial stability of the Union.
## 3a.
Without prejudice to the opinion referred to in paragraph 3, sixth subparagraph, of this Article, following receipt of the draft decision and report referred to in paragraph 3, third subparagraph, of this Article, ESMA may also provide an opinion in accordance with Article 23a and [[2012R0648_EN.24a#7|Article 24a(7)]] on that draft decision to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* where necessary to promote a consistent and coherent application of a relevant article, within 15 working days of receipt of the draft decision.

Where the draft decision submitted to ESMA in accordance with paragraph 3 of this Article shows a lack of convergence or coherence in the application of this Regulation, ESMA shall issue guidelines or recommendations to promote the necessary consistency or coherence in the application of this Regulation pursuant to [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].

The adopted opinions of ESMA and the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] shall be submitted in an electronic format via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*, to the respective recipients.
## 3b.
During the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#3.|risk assessment period]]*, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*, through the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*:
### (a)
may submit questions to, and request complementary information from, the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (b)
shall coordinate and submit questions from ESMA or any member of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] to the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*; and
### (c)
shall share with ESMA and the members of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] all answers provided by the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.

Where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* has not provided the requested information to ESMA or any member of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] within 10 working days of submission of the request, ESMA or any member of that college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] may submit its request directly to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*.

Where the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has not responded to the questions referred to in the first subparagraph within the deadline set by the authority requesting the information, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*, after consulting the requesting authority, may decide to extend once the relevant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#3.|risk assessment period]]* by a maximum of 10 working days in total if, in its view or in the view of the requesting authority, any of the questions is material for the assessment. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall inform the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*, of the extension provided. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* may take a decision on the application in the absence of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s response.
## 3c.
Within 10 working days of receipt of the opinions of ESMA and of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]], adopted under paragraph 3, fourth and sixth subparagraphs, respectively, of this Article, and, where issued, the opinion of ESMA adopted under paragraph 3a, first subparagraph, of this Article, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall adopt its decision and transmit it to ESMA and the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*.

Where the decision of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* does not reflect the opinion of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]], including any conditions or recommendations contained therein, it shall contain a fully reasoned explanation of any significant deviation from that opinion or conditions or recommendations.

Where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* does not comply or does not intend to comply with an opinion of ESMA or with any conditions or recommendations included therein, ESMA shall inform the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* of Supervisors in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.24a|Article 24a]]. The information shall also include the reasoning from the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* for non-compliance or for its intention not to comply.
## 4.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall, after duly considering the opinions of ESMA and of the college provided for in paragraphs 3 and 3a of this Article, including any conditions or recommendations contained therein, decide to grant authorisation as referred to in Article 14 and [[2012R0648_EN.15#1|Article 15(1)]], second subparagraph, only where it is fully satisfied that the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*:
### (a)
complies with the requirements laid down in this Regulation, including, where applicable, for the provision of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* services or activities for non-financial instruments; and
### (b)
is notified as a system pursuant to Directive 98/26/EC.

Where a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* applies for an extension of an existing authorisation pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.15|Article 15]], ESMA, the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] and the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* may rely on part of the assessment previously made pursuant to this Article to the extent that the application for extension will not result in a change or otherwise affect the previous assessment for that part. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall confirm to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* that there is no change to the underlying facts of that part of the assessment.

The applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall not be authorised where:
### (a)
the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* has decided not to grant the authorisation; or
### (b)
all members of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]], excluding the authorities of the Member State where the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is established, reach a joint opinion by mutual agreement, pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.19#1|Article 19(1)]], that the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is not to be authorised.

The joint opinion referred to in the third subparagraph, point (b), of this paragraph, shall state in writing the full and detailed reasons why the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] considers that the requirements laid down in this Regulation or in other Union law are not met.

Where such a joint opinion has not been reached by mutual agreement and a majority of two-thirds of the members of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] have expressed a negative opinion, any of the competent authorities concerned, based on that majority, may, within 30 calendar days of the adoption of that negative opinion, refer the matter to ESMA in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.19|Article 19 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].

The decision to refer the matter to ESMA shall state in writing the full and detailed reasons for which the relevant members of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] consider that the requirements laid down in this Regulation or other Union law are not met. In that case, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall defer its decision on authorisation and await any decision on authorisation that ESMA may take in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.19#3|Article 19(3) of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]]. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall take its decision in conformity with ESMA’s decision. The matter shall not be referred to ESMA after the end of the 30-day period referred to in the fifth subparagraph of this paragraph.

Where all members of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]], excluding the authorities of the Member State where the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is established, reach a joint opinion by mutual agreement, pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.19#1|Article 19(1)]], that the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is not to be authorised, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* may refer the matter to ESMA in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.19|Article 19 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].

The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of the Member State where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is established shall transmit the decision to the other competent authorities concerned.

The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall, without undue delay after taking a decision whether to grant or refuse authorisation under paragraph 3c, inform the applicant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in writing of its decision via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*, together with a fully reasoned explanation.
## 5.
ESMA shall act in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.17|Article 17 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] in the event that the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* has not applied the provisions of this Regulation, or has applied them in a way which appears to be in breach of Union law.

ESMA may investigate an alleged breach or non-application of Union law upon request from any member of the college or on its own initiative, after having informed the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*.
## 6.
While performing their duties, any action taken by any member of the college shall not, directly or indirectly, discriminate against any Member State or *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(16)|group]]* of Member States as a venue for *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* services in any currency.
