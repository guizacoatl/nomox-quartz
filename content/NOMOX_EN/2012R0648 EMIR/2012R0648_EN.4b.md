# Article 4b - Post-trade risk reduction services

## 1.
Without prejudice to risk-mitigation techniques under [[2012R0648_EN.11|Article 11]], the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]] shall not apply to an *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contract]]* that is initiated and concluded as the result of an eligible post-trade risk reduction (‘*[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]*’) exercise (‘*[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR transaction]]*’) carried out pursuant to paragraphs 2 to 4 of this Article.
## 2.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR transaction]]* shall only be exempted from the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]] where:
### (a)
the entity performing the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise (‘*[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* service provider’) complies with the requirements set out under paragraphs 3 and 4 of this Article; and
### (b)
each participant in the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise complies with the requirements under paragraph 3 of this Article.
## 3.
An eligible *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise shall:
### (a)
be performed by an entity authorised in accordance with Article 7 of the Directive 2014/65/EU that is independent of the counterparties to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contracts]]* included in the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise;
### (b)
achieve a reduction in risk in each of the portfolios submitted to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise;
### (c)
be accepted in full and, as a result, the participants in the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise shall not be able to choose which trades to execute under the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise;
### (d)
be open for participation only to the entities that initially submitted a portfolio to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise;
### (e)
be market risk neutral;
### (f)
not contribute to price formation;
### (g)
take the form of a compression, rebalancing or optimisation exercise or a combination thereof;
### (h)
be executed on a bilateral or multilateral basis.
## 4.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* service provider shall:
### (a)
comply with the pre-agreed rules of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise, including methods and algorithms in prescheduled cycles, and act in a reasonable, transparent and non-discriminatory manner;
### (b)
ensure that entities participating in a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise have no influence over the result of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise;
### (c)
undertake regular compression exercises where *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercises result in new *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR transactions]]*;
### (d)
keep complete and accurate records of all transactions executed pursuant to a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise, including:
#### (i)
information on transactions entered into as part of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise;
#### (ii)
transactions resulting from the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise either as modified transactions or as new transactions; and
#### (iii)
the overall change in the risk of the different portfolios included in the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise;
### (e)
upon request make available, without undue delay, the records referred to in point (d) to the relevant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* and to ESMA; and
### (f)
monitor the transactions resulting from the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise in order to ensure, to the extent possible, that the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise does not result in any misuse or circumvention of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation.
## 5.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* which has authorised the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* service provider in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.7|Article 7 of Directive 2014/65/EU]] shall, before a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR transaction]]* resulting from a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise performed by that *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* service provider is able to be exempted from the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation in accordance with paragraph 1, do the following without undue delay:
### (a)
notify the name of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* service provider to ESMA; and
### (b)
share with ESMA its assessment of how the requirements referred to in paragraphs 3 and 4 are complied with by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* service provider.

The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* referred to in the first subparagraph shall, at least on an annual basis, confirm to ESMA that the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* service provider continues to comply with the requirements referred to in paragraphs 3 and 4 or that the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* service provider is no longer providing *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* services, as applicable.

ESMA shall transmit the information received under the first and second subparagraphs of this paragraph to the authorities of each Member State with supervisory powers in relation to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]].

The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph shall, without undue delay, notify ESMA where a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* service provider no longer complies with the requirements referred to in paragraphs 3 and 4. Upon such notification, ESMA shall remove the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* service provider from the list referred to in the fifth subparagraph of this paragraph. From the date when the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* service provider has been removed from that list, *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR transactions]]* resulting from a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise performed by that *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* service provider shall no longer be exempted from the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation in accordance with paragraph 1.

ESMA shall, on a yearly basis, publish a list of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* service providers notified to ESMA under the first subparagraph, point (a).
## 6.
ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards to further specify the elements and requirements set out in paragraphs 3 and 4 and the following other conditions or characteristics of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercises:
### (a)
what constitutes market risk neutrality in a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise;
### (b)
the required risk reduction in submitted portfolios;
### (c)
the possible inclusion of mixed portfolios containing both cleared and uncleared transactions in the same *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise and the conditions under which such inclusion would be allowed;
### (d)
requirements regarding the management of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* exercise;
### (e)
requirements for different types of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|PTRR]]* services;
### (f)
the process for monitoring the application of the exemption granted; and
### (g)
the criteria to apply when assessing whether the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation is circumvented.

ESMA shall submit the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph to the Commission by 25 December 2025.

Power is delegated to the Commission to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
