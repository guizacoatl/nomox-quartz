# Article 84 - Exchange of information

## 1.
Competent authorities, ESMA, and other relevant authorities shall, without undue delay, provide one another with the information required for the purposes of carrying out their duties.
## 2.
Competent authorities, ESMA, other relevant authorities and other bodies or natural and legal persons receiving confidential information in the exercise of their duties under this Regulation shall use it only in the course of their duties.
## 3.
Competent authorities shall communicate information to the relevant members of the ESCB where such information is relevant for the exercise of their duties.
