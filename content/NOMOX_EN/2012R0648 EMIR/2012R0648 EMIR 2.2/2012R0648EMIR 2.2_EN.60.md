# Article 60 - Exercise of the powers referred to in Articles 61 to 63


The powers conferred on ESMA or any official of or other person authorised by ESMA by Articles [[2012R0648EMIR 2.2_EN.61|61]] to 63 shall not be used to require the disclosure of information or documents which are subject to legal privilege.
