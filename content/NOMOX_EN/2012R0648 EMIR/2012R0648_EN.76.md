# Article 76 - Cooperation arrangements


Relevant authorities of third countries that do not have any *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* established in their jurisdiction may contact ESMA with a view to establishing cooperation arrangements to access information on *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]* contracts held in Union trade repositories.

ESMA may establish cooperation arrangements with those relevant authorities regarding access to information on *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]* contracts held in Union trade repositories that these authorities need to fulfil their respective responsibilities and mandates, provided that guarantees of professional secrecy exist, including the protection of business secrets shared by the authorities with third parties.
