# Article 20 - Withdrawal of authorisation

## 1.
Without prejudice to [[2012R0648_EN.22#3|Article 22(3)]], a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall withdraw authorisation, in full or in part, where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*:
### (a)
has not made use of the authorisation within 12 months;
### (b)
has not made use of an authorisation for a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* service or activity in a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(6)|class of derivatives]]*, securities, other financial instruments or non-financial instruments, within 12 months of the date when the authorisation was granted or of the date when the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* last offered such *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* service or activity;
### (c)
expressly renounces the authorisation;
### (d)
has provided no services or performed no activity for the preceding 12 months in a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(6)|class of derivatives]]*, securities, other financial instruments or non-financial instruments covered by an authorisation;
### (e)
has obtained authorisation by making false statements or by any other irregular means;
### (f)
is no longer in compliance with the conditions under which authorisation was granted and has not taken the remedial action within the set timeframe; or
### (g)
has seriously and systematically infringed any of the requirements laid down in this Regulation.
## 2.
Where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* withdraws the authorisation of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* pursuant to paragraph 1, it may limit such withdrawal of authorisation to a particular *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* service or activity in one or more classes of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]*, securities, other financial instruments or non-financial instruments.
## 3.
Before the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* takes a decision to withdraw the authorisation of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in full or in part, including for one or more *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* services or activities in one or more classes of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]*, securities, other financial instruments or non-financial instruments under paragraph 1, it shall, in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.17b|Article 17b]], request the opinion of ESMA and the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] on the necessity of withdrawing the authorisation, in full or in part, of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, except where a decision is required urgently.
## 4.
ESMA or any member of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] may, at any time, request that the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* examine whether the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* remains in compliance with the conditions under which the authorisation was granted.
## 5.
Where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* takes a decision to withdraw the authorisation of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in full or in part, including for one or more *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* services or activities in one or more classes of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]*, securities, other financial instruments or non-financial instruments, that decision shall take effect throughout the Union and the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall inform the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, via the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]*, without undue delay.
