# Article 24 - Emergency situations

## 1.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* or any other relevant authority shall inform ESMA, the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]], the relevant members of the ESCB, the Commission and other relevant authorities without undue delay of any emergency situation relating to a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, including:
### (a)
situations or events which impact, or are likely to impact, the prudential or financial soundness or the resilience of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]], their *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* or their *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*;
### (b)
where a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* intends to activate its recovery plan pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.9|Article 9 of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* has taken an early intervention measure pursuant to Article 18 of that Regulation or a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* has required a total or partial removal of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(29)|senior management]]* or *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|board]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* pursuant to Article 19 of that Regulation;
### (c)
where there are developments in financial markets, or other markets where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* provides *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* services, which may have an adverse effect on market liquidity, the transmission of monetary policy, the smooth operation of payment systems or the stability of the financial system in any of the Member States where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or one of its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* are established.
## 2.
In an emergency situation, information shall be provided and updated without undue delay to enable the members of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] to analyse the impact of that emergency situation in particular on their *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* and their *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*. The members of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] may forward the information to the public bodies responsible for the financial stability of their markets, subject to the obligation of professional secrecy set out in [[2012R0648_EN.83|Article 83]]. The obligation of professional secrecy in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.83|Article 83]] shall apply to those bodies receiving that information.
## 3.
In the event of an emergency situation at one or more *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* that has or is likely to have destabilising effects on cross-border markets, ESMA shall coordinate competent authorities, the resolution authorities designated pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.3#1|Article 3(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]] and the colleges referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] of this Regulation to build a coordinated response to emergency situations relating to a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and ensure effective information sharing among competent authorities, the colleges referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] of this Regulation and resolution authorities.
## 4.
In an emergency situation, except where a resolution authority is taking or has taken a resolution action in relation to a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.21|Article 21 of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], ad hoc meetings of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]*, to coordinate the responses of competent authorities:
### (a)
may be convened by the Chair of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]*;
### (b)
shall be convened by the Chair of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]*, upon the request of two members of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]*.
## 5.
Any of the following authorities shall also be invited to the ad hoc meeting referred to in paragraph 4, where relevant, having regard to the issues to be discussed at that meeting:
### (a)
the relevant central banks of issue;
### (b)
the relevant competent authorities for the supervision of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]*, including, where relevant, the ECB in the framework of the tasks concerning the prudential supervision of credit institutions within the single supervisory mechanism conferred upon it in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1024/2013;
### (c)
the relevant competent authorities for the supervision of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(4)|trading venues]]*;
### (d)
the relevant competent authorities for the supervision of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* where they are known;
### (e)
the relevant resolution authorities designated pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.3#1|Article 3(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]];
### (f)
any member of the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]], that is not already covered by points (a) to (d) of this paragraph.
## 6.
Where an ad hoc meeting of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* is convened pursuant to paragraph 4, the Chair of that Committee shall inform EBA, EIOPA, the ESRB, the Single Resolution *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|Board]]* established under Regulation (EU) No 806/2014 of the European Parliament and of the Council and the Commission thereof who shall also be invited to participate in that meeting upon their request.
Where a meeting is held following an emergency situation as specified in paragraph 1, point (c), the Chair of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall invite the relevant central banks of issue to participate in that meeting.
## 7.
ESMA may require all relevant competent authorities to provide it with the necessary information to carry out its coordination function provided for in this Article.

Where a relevant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* has the requested information, it shall forward it to ESMA without undue delay.

Where a relevant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* does not have the requested information, it shall require the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]], their *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* or their *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*, connected financial market infrastructures or related third parties to whom those *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* have outsourced operational functions or activities, as relevant and applicable, to provide it with that information, and shall inform ESMA thereof. Once the relevant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* receives the requested information, it shall forward it to ESMA without undue delay.

Instead of requiring the information referred to in the third subparagraph, the relevant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* may allow ESMA to require that information directly from the relevant entity. ESMA shall forward all information received from that entity to the relevant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* without undue delay.

Where ESMA has not received the information it required in accordance with the first subparagraph within 48 hours, it may, by simple request, require authorised *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*, their *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* and their *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*, connected financial market infrastructures and related third parties to whom those *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* have outsourced operational functions or activities to provide it with that information without undue delay. ESMA shall forward all information received from such entities to the relevant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* without undue delay.
## 8.
ESMA may, upon the proposal of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]*, issue recommendations pursuant to [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] addressed to one or more competent authorities recommending them to adopt temporary or permanent supervisory decisions in line with the requirements set out in [[2012R0648_EN.16|Article 16]] and in Titles IV and V of this Regulation to avoid or mitigate significant adverse effects on the financial stability of the Union. ESMA may issue such recommendations only where more than one *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]] is affected or where Union-wide events are destabilising cross-border cleared markets.
