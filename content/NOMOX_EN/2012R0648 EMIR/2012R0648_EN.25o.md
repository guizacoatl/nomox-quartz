# Article 25o - Amendments to Annexes III and IV


In order to take account of amendments to [[2012R0648_EN.16|Article 16]] and Titles IV and V, the Commission is empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.82|Article 82]] to ensure that the infringements under Annex III correspond to the requirements under [[2012R0648_EN.16|Article 16]] and Titles IV and V.

In order to take account of developments on financial markets the Commission is empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.82|Article 82]] concerning measures to amend Annex IV.
