# Article 7 - Reporting on aggregate thresholds for assessing compliance with the active account

## 1.
Every six months, counterparties shall report to their competent authority the information referred to in Table 1 and Table 2 of Annex II to this Regulation.
## 2.
The information referred to in Table 2 of Annex II to this Regulation shall be reported at the level of the counterparty. However, where the counterparty belongs to a group subject to consolidated supervision in the Union as referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.7a#2|Article 7a(2) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], the information referred to in Table 2 of Annex II to this Regulation shall also be reported at the level of any subsidiaries, within and outside the Union.
