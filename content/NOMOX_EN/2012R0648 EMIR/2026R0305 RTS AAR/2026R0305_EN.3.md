# Article 3 - Stress-testing of the operational conditions of the active account


The stress testing referred to in [[2026R0305_EN.7a#4|Article 7a(4)]], fourth subparagraph of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 shall consist of technical and functional tests that verify the operational capacity and the functioning of the IT connectivity with the CCP, directly or indirectly, with the clearing member or client providing client clearing services in accordance with [[2026R0305_EN.1|Article 1]].

Those technical and functional tests shall demonstrate to the competent authority that the account of the counterparty can withstand a substantial increase in clearing activity as referred to in [[2026R0305_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], points (d) and (e).

The tests shall take place annually.
