# Article 9 - Reporting on the representativeness obligation

## 1.
Every six months, counterparties shall report to the competent authority:
### (a)
the most relevant subcategories, as referred to in Articles [[2026R0305_EN.4|4]](2), 5(2) and 6(2) of this Regulation;
### (b)
the number of trades cleared, in each of the most relevant subcategory referred to Articles [[2026R0305_EN.4|4]](2), 5(2) and 6(2), per class of derivative contracts and per reference period at clearing services of substantial systemic importance as specified pursuant to [[2026R0305_EN.25|Article 25]](2c) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012;
### (c)
the number of trades cleared, based on the average of the 12 previous months, in each of the most relevant subcategory referred to in Articles [[2026R0305_EN.4|4]](2), 5(2) and 6(2), per class of derivative contracts and per reference period at an authorised CCP;
### (d)
the duration of the reference period referred to in Articles [[2026R0305_EN.4|4]](3) and 5(3) and [[2026R0305_EN.6#3|Article 6(3)]] and (4).
## 2.
Counterparties shall report to the competent authority when the number of trades cleared in a subcategory of the derivative contracts referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.7a#6|Article 7a(6) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] exceeds half of that counterparty’s total trades of the previous 12 months.
## 3.
For the purposes of paragraph 1, counterparties shall use for each class of derivatives the Tables set out in Annex III to this Regulation, as appropriate.
