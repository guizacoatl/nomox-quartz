# Article 8 - Reporting on the operational conditions of the active account

## 1.
Every six months, counterparties shall provide their competent authority with a written statement confirming that they comply with Articles [[2026R0305_EN.1|1]], [[2026R0305_EN.2|2]] and [[2026R0305_EN.3|3]] of this Regulation.
## 2.
The counterparties referred to in the paragraph 1 shall keep at the disposal of their competent authority the documentation necessary to prove that the counterparties comply with Articles [[2026R0305_EN.1|1]], [[2026R0305_EN.2|2]] and [[2026R0305_EN.3|3]] of this Regulation.
