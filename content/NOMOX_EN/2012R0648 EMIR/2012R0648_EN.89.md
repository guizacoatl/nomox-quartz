# Article 89 - Transitional provisions

## 1.
Until 18 June 2021, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation set out in [[2012R0648_EN.4|Article 4]] shall not apply to *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contracts]]* that are objectively measurable as reducing investment risks that directly relate to the financial solvency of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(10)|pension scheme arrangements]]*, and to entities established to provide compensation to members of such arrangements in case of default.

The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation set out in [[2012R0648_EN.4|Article 4]] shall not apply to *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contracts]]* as referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph entered into by *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(10)|pension scheme arrangements]]* from 17 August 2018 until 16 June 2019.

The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contracts]]*, which would otherwise be subject to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation under [[2012R0648_EN.4|Article 4]], entered into by those entities during this period shall be subject to the requirements laid down in [[2012R0648_EN.11|Article 11]].
## 2.
In relation to *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(10)|pension scheme arrangements]]* referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.2#10#c|Article 2(10)(c)]] and (d) the exemption referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article shall be granted by the relevant *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* for types of entities or types of arrangements. After receiving the request, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall notify ESMA and EIOPA. Within 30 calendar days of receipt of the notification ESMA, after consulting EIOPA, shall issue an opinion assessing compliance of the type of entities or the type of arrangements with [[2012R0648_EN.2#10#c|Article 2(10)(c)]] or (d) as well as the reasons why an exemption is justified due to difficulties in meeting the variation margin requirements. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall only grant an exemption where it is fully satisfied that the type of entities or the type of arrangements complies with [[2012R0648_EN.2#10#c|Article 2(10)(c)]] or (d) and that they encounter difficulties in meeting the variation margin requirements. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall adopt a decision within ten working days of receipt of ESMA’s opinion, taking due account of that opinion. If the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* does not agree with ESMA’s opinion, it shall give full reasons in its decision and shall explain any significant deviation therefrom.

ESMA shall publish on its website a list of types of entities and types of arrangements referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.2#10#c|Article 2(10)(c)]] and (d) which has been granted an exemption in accordance with the first subparagraph. To further strengthen consistency in supervisory outcomes, ESMA shall conduct a peer review of the entities included on the list every year in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.30|Article 30 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
## 3.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* that has been authorised in its Member State of establishment to provide *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* services in accordance with the national law of that Member State before all the regulatory technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648_EN.4|4]], [[2012R0648_EN.5|5]] and [[2012R0648_EN.8|8]] to 11, 16, 18, 25, 26, 29, 34, 41, 42, 44, 45, 46, 47, 49, 56 and 81 are adopted by the Commission, shall apply for authorisation under [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]] for the purposes of this Regulation within six months of the date of entry into force of all the regulatory technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648_EN.16|16]], [[2012R0648_EN.25|25]], [[2012R0648_EN.26|26]], [[2012R0648_EN.29|29]], [[2012R0648_EN.34|34]], [[2012R0648_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648_EN.42|42]], [[2012R0648_EN.44|44]], [[2012R0648_EN.45|45]], [[2012R0648_EN.47|47]] and [[2012R0648_EN.49|49]].

A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* established in a third country, which has been recognised to provide *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* services in a Member State in accordance with the national law of that Member State before all the regulatory technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648_EN.16|16]], [[2012R0648_EN.26|26]], [[2012R0648_EN.29|29]], [[2012R0648_EN.34|34]], [[2012R0648_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648_EN.42|42]], [[2012R0648_EN.44|44]], [[2012R0648_EN.45|45]], [[2012R0648_EN.47|47]] and [[2012R0648_EN.49|49]] are adopted by the Commission, shall apply for recognition under [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]] for the purposes of this Regulation within six months of the date of entry into force of all the regulatory technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648_EN.16|16]], [[2012R0648_EN.26|26]], [[2012R0648_EN.29|29]], [[2012R0648_EN.34|34]], [[2012R0648_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648_EN.42|42]], [[2012R0648_EN.44|44]], [[2012R0648_EN.45|45]], [[2012R0648_EN.47|47]] and [[2012R0648_EN.49|49]].
## 3a.
ESMA shall not exercise its powers pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]](2a), (2b) and (2c) until the date of entry into force of the delegated acts referred to in the second subparagraph of [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]](2a) and in [[2012R0648_EN.25a#3|Article 25a(3)]] and, in relation to *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* for which ESMA has not adopted a recognition decision pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]] before 1 January 2020, until the date of entry into force of the relevant implementing act referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.25#6|Article 25(6)]].
## 3b.
ESMA shall establish and manage a college pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.25c|Article 25c]] for all *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* recognised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]] before 1 January 2020 within four months from the date of entry into force of the delegated act referred to in the second subparagraph of [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]](2a).
## 3c.
ESMA shall review the recognition decisions adopted pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.25#1|Article 25(1)]] before the date of entry into force of the delegated acts referred to in the second subparagraph of [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]](2a) and in [[2012R0648_EN.25a#3|Article 25a(3)]] within 18 months from the date of entry into force of the delegated act referred to in the second subparagraph of [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]](2a), in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.25#5|Article 25(5)]].

Where, following the review referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph, ESMA determines that a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* recognised before 1 January 2020 should be classified as a Tier 2 *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]](2a), ESMA shall set an appropriate adaptation period which shall not exceed 18 months within which the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* must comply with the requirements referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]](2b). ESMA may extend the adaptation period up to an additional six months upon the reasoned request of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or any of the competent authorities responsible for the supervision of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* established in the Union, where such extension is justified by exceptional circumstances and by the impact on the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* established in the Union.
## 4.
Until a decision is made under this Regulation on the authorisation or recognition of a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, the respective national rules on authorisation and recognition of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* shall continue to apply and the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall continue to be supervised by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of its Member State of establishment or recognition.
## 5.
Where a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* authorised a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to clear a given *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(6)|class of derivatives]]* in accordance with the national law of its Member State before all the regulatory technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648_EN.16|16]], [[2012R0648_EN.26|26]], [[2012R0648_EN.29|29]], [[2012R0648_EN.34|34]], [[2012R0648_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648_EN.42|42]], [[2012R0648_EN.45|45]], [[2012R0648_EN.47|47]] and [[2012R0648_EN.49|49]] are adopted by the Commission, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of that Member State shall notify ESMA of that authorisation within one month of the date of entry into force of the regulatory technical standards under [[2012R0648_EN.5#1|Article 5(1)]].

Where a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* recognised a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* established in a third country to provide *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* services in accordance with the national law of its Member State before all the regulatory technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648_EN.16|16]], [[2012R0648_EN.26|26]], [[2012R0648_EN.29|29]], [[2012R0648_EN.34|34]], [[2012R0648_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648_EN.42|42]], [[2012R0648_EN.45|45]], [[2012R0648_EN.47|47]] and [[2012R0648_EN.49|49]] are adopted by the Commission, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of that Member State shall notify ESMA of that recognition within one month of the date of entry into force of the regulatory technical standards under [[2012R0648_EN.5#1|Article 5(1)]].
## 5a.
During the transitional period set out in [[2013R0575_EN.497|Article 497 of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]], a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* referred to in that Article shall include in the information it shall report in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.50c#1|Article 50c(1)]] of this Regulation the total amount of initial margin, as defined in point (140) of [[2013R0575_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]], it has received from its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* where both of the following conditions are met:
### (a)
the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* does not have a default fund;
### (b)
the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* does not have in place a binding arrangement with its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* that allows it to use all or part of the initial margin received from those *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* as if they were pre-funded contributions.
## 6.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* that has been authorised or registered in its Member State of establishment to collect and maintain the records of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]* in accordance with the national law of that Member State before all the regulatory and implementing technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648_EN.9|9]], [[2012R0648_EN.56|56]] and [[2012R0648_EN.81|81]] are adopted by the Commission, shall apply for registration under [[2012R0648_EN.55|Article 55]] within six months of the date of entry into force of those regulatory and implementing technical standards.

A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* established in a third country, which is allowed to collect and maintain the records of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]* in a Member State in accordance with the national law of that Member State before all the regulatory and implementing technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648_EN.9|9]], [[2012R0648_EN.56|56]] and [[2012R0648_EN.81|81]] are adopted by the Commission, shall apply for recognition under [[2012R0648_EN.77|Article 77]] within six months of the date of entry into force of those regulatory and implementing technical standards.
## 7.
Until a decision is made under this Regulation on the registration or recognition of a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]*, the respective national rules on authorisation, registration and recognition of trade repositories shall continue to apply and the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* shall continue to be supervised by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of its Member State of establishment or recognition.
## 8.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* that has been authorised or registered in its Member State of establishment to collect and maintain the records of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]* in accordance with the national law of that Member State before the regulatory and implementing technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648_EN.56|56]] and [[2012R0648_EN.81|81]] are adopted by the Commission, can be used to meet the reporting requirement under [[2012R0648_EN.9|Article 9]] until the time a decision is made on the registration of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* under this Regulation.

A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* established in a third country which has been allowed to collect and maintain the records of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]* in accordance with the national law of a Member State before all the regulatory and implementing technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648_EN.56|56]] and [[2012R0648_EN.81|81]] are adopted by the Commission, can be used to meet the reporting requirement under [[2012R0648_EN.9|Article 9]] until the time a decision is made on the recognition of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* under this Regulation.
## 9.
Notwithstanding [[2012R0648_EN.81#3#f|Article 81(3)(f)]], where no international agreement is in place between a third country and the Union as referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.75|Article 75]], a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* may make the necessary information available to the relevant authorities of that third country until 17 August 2013 provided that it notifies ESMA.
## 10.
Where a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* or a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|client]]* of another *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, or has established indirect *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* arrangements, before 24 December 2024, it shall become subject to [[2012R0648_EN.26#1|Article 26(1)]] on 25 December 2026.

By way of derogation from [[2012R0648_EN.37#1|Article 37(1)]], a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* can allow other *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* or *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* houses that were its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]*, directly or indirectly, as of 31 December 2023 to remain its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* until 25 December 2026 at the latest.
## 11.
Until 25 December 2025 or 30 days after the announcement referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.17c#1|Article 17c(1)]], second subparagraph, whichever date is earlier, the exchange of information, the submission of information and documentation, and notifications that are required to use the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|central database]]* shall be carried out through the use of alternative arrangements.
## 12.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* authorised under [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]] that has entered into an *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangement]]* in financial instruments other than transferable securities, as defined in [[2012R0648_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (44), of Directive 2014/65/EU, and money-market instruments with another *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* authorised under [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]] or a third-country *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* recognised under [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]] before 24 December 2024 shall seek approval from its competent authorities in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.54|Article 54]] before 25 December 2026.

An *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangement]]* established between a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* authorised under [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]] and a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* that is neither authorised under [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]] or recognised under [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]] shall be discontinued before 25 June 2025. If the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* with which that *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangement]]* is established becomes authorised under [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]] or recognised under [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]] before 25 June 2025, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* that are party to that *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangement]]* shall seek approval from their competent authorities in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.54|Article 54]] before 25 June 2027.
## 13.
By way of derogation from [[2012R0648_EN.11#3|Article 11(3)]], fourth and fifth subparagraphs, and [[2012R0648_EN.11|Article 11]](12a), until EBA has publicly announced that it has set up its central validation function, the validation of pro forma models shall be carried out by competent authorities.
