# Article 25e - Exercise of the powers referred to in Articles 25f to 25h


The powers conferred on ESMA or any official of or other person authorised by them by Articles [[2012R0648_EN.25f|25f]] to 25h shall not be used to require the disclosure of information or documents which are subject to legal privilege.
