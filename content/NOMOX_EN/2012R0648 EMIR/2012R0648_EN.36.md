# Article 36 - General provisions

## 1.
When providing services to its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]*, and where relevant, to their *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*, a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall act fairly and professionally in accordance with the best interests of such *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* and *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* and sound risk management.
## 2.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have accessible, transparent and fair rules for the prompt handling of complaints.
