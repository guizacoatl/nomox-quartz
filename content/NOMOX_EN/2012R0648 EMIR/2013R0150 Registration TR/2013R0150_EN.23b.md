# Article 23b - Extension of registration

The application for an extension of an existing registration under Regulation (EU) 2015/2365 of the European Parliament and of the Council shall contain the information specified in [[2013R0150_EN.1|Article 1]], except paragraph 2, point (k), Articles [[2013R0150_EN.2|2]] and [[2013R0150_EN.5|5]], [[2013R0150_EN.7|Article 7]], except paragraph 2, point (d), [[2013R0150_EN.8#b|Article 8(b)]], [[2013R0150_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]], points (b) and (e), [[2013R0150_EN.11|Article 11]], [[2013R0150_EN.12#2|Article 12(2)]], [[2013R0150_EN.13|Article 13]], [[2013R0150_EN.14#2|Article 14(2)]], [[2013R0150_EN.15|Article 15]], [[2013R0150_EN.16|Article 16]], except point (c), Articles [[2013R0150_EN.17|17]] to 23a and [[2013R0150_EN.23c|Article 23c]].
