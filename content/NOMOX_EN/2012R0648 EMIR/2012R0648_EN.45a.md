# Article 45a - Temporary restrictions in the case of a significant non-default event

## 1.
In the case of a significant non-default event as defined in point (9) of [[2021R0023_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* may require the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to refrain from any of the following actions for a period specified by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*, that cannot exceed five years:
### (a)
making a dividend distribution or give an irrevocable commitment to make a dividend distribution, except for rights to dividends specifically referred to in Regulation (EU) 2021/23 as a form of compensation;
### (b)
buy-back of ordinary shares;
### (c)
creating an obligation to pay variable remuneration as defined by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s remuneration policy pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.26#5|Article 26(5)]] of this Regulation, discretionary pension benefits or severance packages to *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(29)|senior management]]* as defined in point 29 of [[2012R0648_EN.2|Article 2]] of this Regulation.

The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall not restrict the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* from undertaking any of the actions referred to in the first subparagraph, if the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is legally obliged to undertake that action and the obligation predates the events pursuant to the first subparagraph.
## 2.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* may decide to waive the restrictions in paragraph 1 where it deems that waiving those restrictions would not reduce the quantity or availability of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s own resources, in particular own resources available for use as a recovery measure.
## 3.
ESMA shall by 12 February 2022 draft guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] further specifying the circumstances in which the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* may require the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to refrain from undertaking any of the actions referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article.
