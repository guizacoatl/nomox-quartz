# Article 58 - Examination of the application

## 1.
ESMA shall, within 40 working days from the notification referred to in the third subparagraph of [[2012R0648_EN.56#2|Article 56(2)]], examine the application for registration based on the compliance of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* with Articles [[2012R0648_EN.78|78]] to 81 and shall adopt a fully reasoned registration decision or decision refusing registration.
## 2.
A decision issued by ESMA pursuant to paragraph 1 shall take effect on the fifth working day following its adoption.
