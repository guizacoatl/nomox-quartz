# Article 89 - Transitional provisions

## 1.
For three years after the entry into force of this Regulation, the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation set out in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.4|Article 4]] shall not apply to *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contracts]]* that are objectively measurable as reducing investment risks directly relating to the financial solvency of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(10)|pension scheme arrangements]]* as defined in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#10|Article 2(10)]]. The transitional period shall also apply to entities established for the purpose of providing compensation to members of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(10)|pension scheme arrangements]]* in case of a default.
The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contracts]]*, which would otherwise be subject to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.4|Article 4]], entered into by those entities during this period shall be subject to the requirements laid down in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.11|Article 11]].
## 2.
In relation to *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(10)|pension scheme arrangements]]* referred to in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#10#c|Article 2(10)(c)]] and (d) the exemption referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article shall be granted by the relevant *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* for types of entities or types of arrangements. After receiving the request, the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall notify ESMA and EIOPA. Within 30 calendar days of receipt of the notification ESMA, after consulting EIOPA, shall issue an opinion assessing compliance of the type of entities or the type of arrangements with [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#10#c|Article 2(10)(c)]] or (d) as well as the reasons why an exemption is justified due to difficulties in meeting the variation margin requirements. The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall only grant an exemption where it is fully satisfied that the type of entities or the type of arrangements complies with [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#10#c|Article 2(10)(c)]] or (d) and that they encounter difficulties in meeting the variation margin requirements. The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall adopt a decision within ten working days of receipt of ESMA’s opinion, taking due account of that opinion. If the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* does not agree with ESMA’s opinion, it shall give full reasons in its decision and shall explain any significant deviation therefrom.
ESMA shall publish on its website a list of types of entities and types of arrangements referred to in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#10#c|Article 2(10)(c)]] and (d) which has been granted an exemption in accordance with the first subparagraph. To further strengthen consistency in supervisory outcomes, ESMA shall conduct a peer review of the entities included on the list every year in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.30|Article 30 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
## 3.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* that has been authorised in its Member State of establishment to provide *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* services in accordance with the national law of that Member State before all the regulatory technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.4|4]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.5|5]] and [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.8|8]] to 11, 16, 18, 25, 26, 29, 34, 41, 42, 44, 45, 46, 47, 49, 56 and 81 are adopted by the Commission, shall apply for authorisation under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.14|Article 14]] for the purposes of this Regulation within six months of the date of entry into force of all the regulatory technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.16|16]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.25|25]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.26|26]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.29|29]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.34|34]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.42|42]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.44|44]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.45|45]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.47|47]] and [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.49|49]].
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* established in a third country, which has been recognised to provide *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* services in a Member State in accordance with the national law of that Member State before all the regulatory technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.16|16]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.26|26]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.29|29]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.34|34]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.42|42]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.44|44]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.45|45]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.47|47]] and [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.49|49]] are adopted by the Commission, shall apply for recognition under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.25|Article 25]] for the purposes of this Regulation within six months of the date of entry into force of all the regulatory technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.16|16]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.26|26]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.29|29]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.34|34]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.42|42]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.44|44]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.45|45]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.47|47]] and [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.49|49]].
## 4.
Until a decision is made under this Regulation on the authorisation or recognition of a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, the respective national rules on authorisation and recognition of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* shall continue to apply and the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall continue to be supervised by the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of its Member State of establishment or recognition.
## 5.
Where a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* authorised a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to clear a given *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(6)|class of derivatives]]* in accordance with the national law of its Member State before all the regulatory technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.16|16]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.26|26]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.29|29]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.34|34]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.42|42]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.45|45]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.47|47]] and [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.49|49]] are adopted by the Commission, the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of that Member State shall notify ESMA of that authorisation within one month of the date of entry into force of the regulatory technical standards under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.5#1|Article 5(1)]].
Where a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* recognised a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* established in a third country to provide *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* services in accordance with the national law of its Member State before all the regulatory technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.16|16]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.26|26]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.29|29]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.34|34]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.41|41]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.42|42]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.45|45]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.47|47]] and [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.49|49]] are adopted by the Commission, the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of that Member State shall notify ESMA of that recognition within one month of the date of entry into force of the regulatory technical standards under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.5#1|Article 5(1)]].
## 6.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* that has been authorised or registered in its Member State of establishment to collect and maintain the records of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]* in accordance with the national law of that Member State before all the regulatory and implementing technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.9|9]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.56|56]] and [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.81|81]] are adopted by the Commission, shall apply for registration under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.55|Article 55]] within six months of the date of entry into force of those regulatory and implementing technical standards.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* established in a third country, which is allowed to collect and maintain the records of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]* in a Member State in accordance with the national law of that Member State before all the regulatory and implementing technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.9|9]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.56|56]] and [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.81|81]] are adopted by the Commission, shall apply for recognition under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.77|Article 77]] within six months of the date of entry into force of those regulatory and implementing technical standards.
## 7.
Until a decision is made under this Regulation on the registration or recognition of a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]*, the respective national rules on authorisation, registration and recognition of trade repositories shall continue to apply and the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* shall continue to be supervised by the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of its Member State of establishment or recognition.
## 8.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* that has been authorised or registered in its Member State of establishment to collect and maintain the records of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]* in accordance with the national law of that Member State before the regulatory and implementing technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.56|56]] and [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.81|81]] are adopted by the Commission, can be used to meet the reporting requirement under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.9|Article 9]] until the time a decision is made on the registration of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* under this Regulation.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* established in a third country which has been allowed to collect and maintain the records of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]* in accordance with the national law of a Member State before all the regulatory and implementing technical standards under Articles [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.56|56]] and [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.81|81]] are adopted by the Commission, can be used to meet the reporting requirement under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.9|Article 9]] until the time a decision is made on the recognition of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* under this Regulation.
## 9.
Notwithstanding [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.81#3#f|Article 81(3)(f)]], where no international agreement is in place between a third country and the Union as referred to in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.75|Article 75]], a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(2)|trade repository]]* may make the necessary information available to the relevant authorities of that third country until 17 August 2013 provided that it notifies ESMA.
