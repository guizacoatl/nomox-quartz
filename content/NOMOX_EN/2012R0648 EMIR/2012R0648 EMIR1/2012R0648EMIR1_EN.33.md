# Article 33 - Conflicts of interest

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall maintain and operate effective written organisational and administrative arrangements to identify and manage any potential conflicts of interest between itself, including its managers, employees, or any person with direct or indirect *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(23)|control]]* or *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(24)|close links]]*, and its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* or their *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* known to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. It shall maintain and implement adequate procedures aiming at resolving possible conflicts of interest.
## 2.
Where the organisational or administrative arrangements of a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to manage conflicts of interest are not sufficient to ensure, with reasonable confidence, that risks of damage to the interests of a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* or *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|client]]* are prevented, it shall clearly disclose the general nature or sources of conflicts of interest to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* before accepting new transactions from that *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]*. Where the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|client]]* is known to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall inform the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|client]]* and the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* whose *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|client]]* is concerned.
## 3.
Where the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(21)|parent undertaking]]* or a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(22)|subsidiary]]*, the written arrangements shall also take into account any circumstances, of which the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is or should be aware, which may give rise to a conflict of interest arising as a result of the structure and business activities of other undertakings with which it has a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(21)|parent undertaking]]* or a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(22)|subsidiary]]* relationship.
## 4.
The written arrangements established in accordance with paragraph 1 shall include the following:
### (a)
the circumstances which constitute or may give rise to a conflict of interest entailing a material risk of damage to the interests of one or more *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* or *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*;
### (b)
procedures to be followed and measures to be adopted in order to manage such conflict.
## 5.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall take all reasonable steps to prevent any misuse of the information held in its systems and shall prevent the use of that information for other business activities. A natural person who has a close link to a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or a legal person that has a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(21)|parent undertaking]]* or a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(22)|subsidiary]]* relationship with a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall not use confidential information recorded in that *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* for any commercial purposes without the prior written consent of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|client]]* to whom such confidential information belongs.
