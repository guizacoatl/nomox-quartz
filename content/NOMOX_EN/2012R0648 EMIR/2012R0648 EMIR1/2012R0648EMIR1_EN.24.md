# Article 24 - Emergency situations


The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* or any other authority shall inform ESMA, the college, the relevant members of the ESCB and other relevant authorities without undue delay of any emergency situation relating to a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, including developments in financial markets, which may have an adverse effect on market liquidity and the stability of the financial system in any of the Member States where the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or one of its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* are established.
