# Article 51 - Interoperability arrangements

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* may enter into an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangement]]* with another *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* where the requirements laid down in Articles [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.52|52]], [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.53|53]] and [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.54|54]] are fulfilled.
## 2.
When establishing an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangement]]* with another *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* for the purpose of providing services to a particular *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]*, the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have non-discriminatory access, both to the data that it needs for the performance of its functions from that particular *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]*, to the extent that the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* complies with the operational and technical requirements established by the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]*, and to the relevant settlement system.
## 3.
Entering into an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangement]]* or accessing a data feed or a settlement system referred to in paragraphs 1 and 2 shall be rejected or restricted, directly or indirectly, only in order to *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(23)|control]]* any risk arising from that arrangement or access.
