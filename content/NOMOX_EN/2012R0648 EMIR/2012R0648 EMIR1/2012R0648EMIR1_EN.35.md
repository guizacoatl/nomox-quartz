# Article 35 - Outsourcing

## 1.
Where a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* outsources operational functions, services or activities, it shall remain fully responsible for discharging all of its obligations under this Regulation and shall ensure at all times that:
### (a)
outsourcing does not result in the delegation of its responsibility;
### (b)
the relationship and obligations of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* towards its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* or, where relevant, towards their *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* are not altered;
### (c)
the conditions for authorisation of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* do not effectively change;
### (d)
outsourcing does not prevent the exercise of supervisory and oversight functions, including on-site access to acquire any relevant information needed to fulfil those mandates;
### (e)
outsourcing does not result in depriving the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* from the necessary systems and *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(23)|controls]]* to manage the risks it faces;
### (f)
the service provider implements equivalent business continuity requirements to those that the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* must fulfil under this Regulation;
### (g)
the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* retains the necessary expertise and resources to evaluate the quality of the services provided and the organisational and *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* adequacy of the service provider, and to supervise the outsourced functions effectively and manage the risks associated with the outsourcing and supervises those functions and manages those risks on an ongoing basis;
### (h)
the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has direct access to the relevant information of the outsourced functions;
#### (i)
the service provider cooperates with the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* in connection with the outsourced activities;
### (j)
the service provider protects any confidential information relating to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* and *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* or, where that service provider is established in a third country, ensures that the data protection standards of that third country, or those set out in the agreement between the parties concerned, are comparable to the data protection standards in effect in the Union.

A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall not outsource major activities linked to risk management unless such outsourcing is approved by the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*.
## 2.
The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall require the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to allocate and set out its rights and obligations, and those of the service provider, clearly in a written agreement.
## 3.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall make all information necessary to enable the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* to assess the compliance of the performance of the outsourced activities with this Regulation available on request.
