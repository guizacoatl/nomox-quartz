# Article 53 - Provision of margins among CCPs

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall distinguish in accounts the assets and positions held for the account of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* with whom it has entered into an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangement]]*.
## 2.
If a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* that enters into an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangement]]* with another *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* only provides initial margins to that *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under a security financial collateral arrangement, the receiving *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have no right of use over the margins provided by the other *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 3.
Collateral received in the form of financial instruments shall be deposited with operators of securities settlement systems notified under Directive 98/26/EC.
## 4.
The assets referred to in paragraphs 1 and 2 shall be available to the receiving *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* only in case of default of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* which has provided the collateral in the context of an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangement]]*.
## 5.
In case of default of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* which has received the collateral in the context of an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangement]]*, the collateral referred to in paragraphs 1 and 2 shall be readily returned to the providing *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
