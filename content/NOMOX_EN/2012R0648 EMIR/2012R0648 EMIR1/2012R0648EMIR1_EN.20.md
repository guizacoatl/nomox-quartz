# Article 20 - Withdrawal of authorisation

## 1.
Without prejudice to [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.22#3|Article 22(3)]], the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall withdraw authorisation where the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*:
### (a)
has not made use of the authorisation within 12 months, expressly renounces the authorisation or has provided no services or performed no activity for the preceding six months;
### (b)
has obtained authorisation by making false statements or by any other irregular means;
### (c)
is no longer in compliance with the conditions under which authorisation was granted and has not taken the remedial action requested by the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* within a set time frame;
### (d)
has seriously and systematically infringed any of the requirements laid down in this Regulation.
## 2.
Where the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* considers that one of the circumstances referred to in paragraph 1 applies, it shall, within five working days, notify ESMA and the members of college accordingly.
## 3.
The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall consult the members of the college on the necessity to withdraw the authorisation of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, except where a decision is required urgently.
## 4.
Any member of the college may, at any time, request that the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* examine whether the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* remains in compliance with the conditions under which authorisation was granted.
## 5.
The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* may limit the withdrawal to a particular service, activity, or class of financial instruments.
## 6.
The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall send ESMA and the members of the college its fully reasoned decision, which shall take into account the reservations of the members of the college.
## 7.
The decision on the withdrawal of authorisation shall take effect throughout the Union.
