# Article 37 - Participation requirements

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall establish, where relevant per type of product cleared, the categories of admissible *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* and the admission criteria, upon the advice of the risk committee pursuant to [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.28#3|Article 28(3)]]. Such criteria shall be non-discriminatory, transparent and objective so as to ensure fair and open access to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and shall ensure that *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* have sufficient financial resources and operational capacity to meet the obligations arising from participation in a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. Criteria that restrict access shall be permitted only to the extent that their objective is to *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(23)|control]]* the risk for the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 2.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall ensure that the application of the criteria referred to in paragraph 1 is met on an ongoing basis and shall have timely access to the information relevant for such assessment. A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall conduct, at least once a year, a comprehensive review of compliance with this Article by its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]*.
## 3.
*[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|Clearing members]]* that clear transactions on behalf of their *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* shall have the necessary additional financial resources and operational capacity to perform this activity. The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s rules for *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* shall allow it to gather relevant basic information to identify, monitor and manage relevant concentrations of risk relating to the provision of services to *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*. *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|Clearing members]]* shall, upon request, inform the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* about the criteria and arrangements they adopt to allow their *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* to access the services of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. Responsibility for ensuring that *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* comply with their obligations shall remain with *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]*.
## 4.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have objective and transparent procedures for the suspension and orderly exit of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* that no longer meet the criteria referred to in paragraph 1.
## 5.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* may only deny access to *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* meeting the criteria referred to in paragraph 1 where duly justified in writing and based on a comprehensive risk analysis.
## 6.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* may impose specific additional obligations on *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]*, such as the participation in auctions of a defaulting *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]*’s position. Such additional obligations shall be proportional to the risk brought by the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* and shall not restrict participation to certain categories of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]*.
