# Article 38 - Transparency

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* shall publicly disclose the prices and fees associated with the services provided. They shall disclose the prices and fees of each service provided separately, including discounts and rebates and the conditions to benefit from those reductions. A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall allow its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* and, where relevant, their *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* separate access to the specific services provided.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall account separately for costs and revenues of the services provided and shall disclose that information to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*.
## 2.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall disclose to *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* and *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* the risks associated with the services provided.
## 3.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall disclose to its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* and to its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* the price information used to calculate its end-of-day exposures to its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]*.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall publicly disclose the volumes of the cleared transactions for each class of instruments cleared by the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* on an aggregated basis.
## 4.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall publicly disclose the operational and technical requirements relating to the communication protocols covering content and message formats it uses to interact with third parties, including the operational and technical requirements referred to in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.7|Article 7]].
## 5.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall publicly disclose any breaches by *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* of the criteria referred to in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.37#1|Article 37(1)]] and the requirements laid down in paragraph 1 of this Article, except where the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*, after consulting ESMA, considers that such disclosure would constitute a threat to financial stability or to market confidence or would seriously jeopardise the financial markets or cause disproportionate damage to the parties involved.
