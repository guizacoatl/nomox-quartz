# Article 28 - Risk committee

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall establish a risk committee, which shall be composed of representatives of its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]*, *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(27)|board]]* and representatives of its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*. The risk committee may invite employees of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and external independent experts to attend risk-committee meetings in a non-voting capacity. Competent authorities may request to attend risk-committee meetings in a non-voting capacity and to be duly informed of the activities and decisions of the risk committee. The advice of the risk committee shall be independent of any direct influence by the management of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. None of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(16)|groups]]* of representatives shall have a majority in the risk committee.
## 2.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall clearly determine the mandate, the governance arrangements to ensure its independence, the operational procedures, the admission criteria and the election mechanism for risk-committee members. The governance arrangements shall be publicly available and shall, at least, determine that the risk committee is chaired by an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(28)|independent member]]* of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(27)|board]]*, reports directly to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(27)|board]]* and holds regular meetings.
## 3.
The risk committee shall advise the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(27)|board]]* on any arrangements that may impact the risk management of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, such as a significant change in its risk model, the default procedures, the criteria for accepting *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]*, the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* of new classes of instruments, or the outsourcing of functions. The advice of the risk committee is not required for the daily operations of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. Reasonable efforts shall be made to consult the risk committee on developments impacting the risk management of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in emergency situations.
## 4.
Without prejudice to the right of competent authorities to be duly informed, the members of the risk committee shall be bound by confidentiality. Where the chairman of the risk committee determines that a member has an actual or potential conflict of interest on a particular matter, that member shall not be allowed to vote on that matter.
## 5.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall promptly inform the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of any decision in which the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(27)|board]]* decides not to follow the advice of the risk committee.
