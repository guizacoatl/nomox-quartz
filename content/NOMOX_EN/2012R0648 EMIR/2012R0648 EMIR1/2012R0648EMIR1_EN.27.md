# Article 27 - Senior management and the board

## 1.
The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(29)|senior management]]* of a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall be of sufficiently good repute and shall have sufficient experience so as to ensure the sound and prudent management of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 2.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(27)|board]]*. At least one third, but no less than two, of the members of that *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(27)|board]]* shall be independent. Representatives of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* shall be invited to *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(27)|board]]* meetings for matters relevant to Articles [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.38|38]] and [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.39|39]]. The compensation of the independent and other non-executive members of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(27)|board]]* shall not be linked to the business performance of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
The members of a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(27)|board]]*, including its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]*, shall be of sufficiently good repute and shall have adequate expertise in financial services, risk management and *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* services.
## 3.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall clearly determine the roles and responsibilities of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(27)|board]]* and shall make the minutes of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(27)|board]]* meetings available to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* and auditors.
