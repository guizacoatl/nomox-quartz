# Article 5 - Clearing obligation procedure

## 1.
Where a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* authorises a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to clear a class of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]* under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.14|Article 14]] or 15, it shall immediately notify ESMA of that authorisation.
In order to ensure consistent application of this Article, ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards specifying the details to be included in the notifications referred to in the first subparagraph.

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 30 September 2012.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the second subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
## 2.
Within six months of receiving notification in accordance with paragraph 1 or accomplishing a procedure for recognition set out in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.25|Article 25]], ESMA shall, after conducting a public consultation and after consulting the ESRB and, where appropriate, the competent authorities of third countries, develop and submit to the Commission for endorsement draft regulatory technical standards specifying the following:
### (a)
the class of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]* that should be subject to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation referred to in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.4|Article 4]];
### (b)
the date or dates from which the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation takes effect, including any phase in and the categories of counterparties to which the obligation applies; and
### (c)
the minimum remaining maturity of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contracts]]* referred to in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.4#1#b|Article 4(1)(b)]](ii).

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
## 3.
ESMA shall, on its own initiative, after conducting a public consultation and after consulting the ESRB and, where appropriate, the competent authorities of third countries, identify, in accordance with the criteria set out in points (a), (b) and (c) of paragraph 4 and notify to the Commission the classes of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]* that should be subject to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation provided in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.4|Article 4]], but for which no *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has yet received authorisation.
Following the notification, ESMA shall publish a call for a development of proposals for the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* of those classes of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]*.
## 4.
With the overarching aim of reducing systemic risk, the draft regulatory technical standards for the part referred to in paragraph 2(a) shall take into consideration the following criteria:
### (a)
the degree of standardisation of the contractual terms and operational processes of the relevant class of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]*;
### (b)
the volume and liquidity of the relevant class of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]*;
### (c)
the availability of fair, reliable and generally accepted pricing information in the relevant class of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]*.

In preparing those draft regulatory technical standards, ESMA may take into consideration the interconnectedness between counterparties using the relevant classes of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]*, the anticipated impact on the levels of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(11)|counterparty credit risk]]* between counterparties as well as the impact on competition across the Union.

In order to ensure consistent application of this Article, ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards further specifying the criteria referred to in points (a), (b) and (c) of the first subparagraph.

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 30 September 2012.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt regulatory technical standards referred to in the third subparagraph of this paragraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
## 5.
The draft regulatory technical standards for the part referred to in paragraph 2(b) shall take into consideration the following criteria:
### (a)
the expected volume of the relevant class of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]*;
### (b)
whether more than one *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* already clear the same class of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]*;
### (c)
the ability of the relevant *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* to handle the expected volume and to manage the risk arising from the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* of the relevant class of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]*;
### (d)
the type and number of counterparties active, and expected to be active within the market for the relevant class of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]*;
### (e)
the period of time a counterparty subject to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation needs in order to put in place arrangements to clear its *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contracts]]* through a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (f)
the risk management and the legal and operational capacity of the range of counterparties that are active in the market for the relevant class of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]* and that would be captured by the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation pursuant to [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]].
## 6.
If a class of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contracts]]* no longer has a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* which is authorised or recognised to clear those contracts under this Regulation, it shall cease to be subject to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation referred to in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.4|Article 4]], and paragraph 3 of this Article shall apply.
