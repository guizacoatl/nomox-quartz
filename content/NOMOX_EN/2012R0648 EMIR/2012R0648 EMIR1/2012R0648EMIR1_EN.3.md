# Article 3 - Intragroup transactions

## 1.
In relation to a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(9)|non-financial counterparty]]*, an intragroup transaction is an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contract]]* entered into with another counterparty which is part of the same *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(16)|group]]* provided that both counterparties are included in the same consolidation on a full basis and they are subject to an appropriate centralised risk evaluation, measurement and *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(23)|control]]* procedures and that counterparty is established in the Union or, if it is established in a third country, the Commission has adopted an implementing act under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.13#2|Article 13(2)]] in respect of that third country.
## 2.
In relation to a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(8)|financial counterparty]]*, an intragroup transaction is any of the following:
### (a)
an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contract]]* entered into with another counterparty which is part of the same *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(16)|group]]*, provided that the following conditions are met:
#### (i)
the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(8)|financial counterparty]]* is established in the Union or, if it is established in a third country, the Commission has adopted an implementing act under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.13#2|Article 13(2)]] in respect of that third country;
#### (ii)
the other counterparty is a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(8)|financial counterparty]]*, a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(18)|financial holding company]]*, a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(17)|financial institution]]* or an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(19)|ancillary services undertaking]]* subject to appropriate prudential requirements;
#### (iii)
both counterparties are included in the same consolidation on a full basis; and
#### (iv)
both counterparties are subject to appropriate centralised risk evaluation, measurement and *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(23)|control]]* procedures;
### (b)
an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contract]]* entered into with another counterparty where both counterparties are part of the same institutional protection scheme, referred to in Article 80(8) of Directive 2006/48/EC, provided that the condition set out in point (a)(ii) of this paragraph is met;
### (c)
an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contract]]* entered into between credit institutions affiliated to the same central body or between such credit institution and the central body, as referred to in Article 3(1) of Directive 2006/48/EC; or
### (d)
an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contract]]* entered into with a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(9)|non-financial counterparty]]* which is part of the same *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(16)|group]]* provided that both counterparties are included in the same consolidation on a full basis and they are subject to an appropriate centralised risk evaluation, measurement and *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(23)|control]]* procedures and that counterparty is established in the Union or in a third-country jurisdiction for which the Commission has adopted an implementing act as referred to in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.13#2|Article 13(2)]] in respect of that third country.
## 3.
For the purposes of this Article, counterparties shall be considered to be included in the same consolidation when they are both either:
### (a)
included in a consolidation in accordance with Directive 83/349/EEC or International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) adopted pursuant to Regulation (EC) No 1606/2002 or, in relation to a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(16)|group]]* the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(21)|parent undertaking]]* of which has its head office in a third country, in accordance with generally accepted accounting principles of a third country determined to be equivalent to IFRS in accordance with Regulation (EC) No 1569/2007 (or accounting standards of a third country the use of which is permitted in accordance with Article 4 of that Regulation); or
### (b)
covered by the same consolidated supervision in accordance with Directive 2006/48/EC or Directive 2006/49/EC or, in relation to a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(16)|group]]* the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(21)|parent undertaking]]* of which has its head office in a third country, the same consolidated supervision by a third-country *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* verified as equivalent to that governed by the principles laid down in Article 143 of Directive 2006/48/EC or in Article 2 of Directive 2006/49/EC.
