# Article 40 - Exposure management


A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall measure and assess its liquidity and credit exposures to each *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* and, where relevant, to another *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* with which it has concluded an *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(12)|interoperability arrangement]]*, on a near to real-time basis. A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have access in a timely manner and on a non-discriminatory basis to the relevant pricing sources to effectively measure its exposures. This shall be done on a reasonable cost basis.
