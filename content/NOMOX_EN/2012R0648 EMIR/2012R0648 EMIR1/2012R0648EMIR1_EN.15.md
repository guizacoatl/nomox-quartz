# Article 15 - Extension of activities and services

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* wishing to extend its business to additional services or activities not covered by the initial authorisation shall submit a request for extension to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*. The offering of *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* services for which the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has not already been authorised shall be considered to be an extension of that authorisation.
The extension of authorisation shall be made in accordance with the procedure set out under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.17|Article 17]].
## 2.
Where a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* wishes to extend its business into a Member State other than that where it is established, the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall immediately notify the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of that other Member State.
