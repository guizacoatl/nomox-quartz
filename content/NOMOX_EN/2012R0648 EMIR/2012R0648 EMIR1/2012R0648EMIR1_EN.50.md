# Article 50 - Settlement

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall, where practical and available, use central bank money to settle its transactions. Where central bank money is not used, steps shall be taken to strictly limit cash settlement risks.
## 2.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall clearly state its obligations with respect to deliveries of financial instruments, including whether it has an obligation to make or receive delivery of a financial instrument or whether it indemnifies participants for losses incurred in the delivery process.
## 3.
Where a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has an obligation to make or receive deliveries of financial instruments, it shall eliminate principal risk through the use of delivery-versus-payment mechanisms to the extent possible.
