# Article 30 - Shareholders and members with qualifying holdings

## 1.
The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall not authorise a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* unless it has been informed of the identities of the shareholders or members, whether direct or indirect, natural or legal persons, that have *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(20)|qualifying holdings]]* and of the amounts of those holdings.
## 2.
The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall refuse to authorise a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* where it is not satisfied as to the suitability of the shareholders or members that have *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(20)|qualifying holdings]]* in the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, taking into account the need to ensure the sound and prudent management of a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 3.
Where *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(24)|close links]]* exist between the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and other natural or legal persons, the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall grant authorisation only where those links do not prevent the effective exercise of the supervisory functions of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*.
## 4.
Where the persons referred to in paragraph 1 exercise an influence which is likely to be prejudicial to the sound and prudent management of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall take appropriate measures to terminate that situation, which may include the withdrawal of the authorisation of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 5.
The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall refuse authorisation where the laws, regulations or administrative provisions of a third country governing one or more natural or legal persons with which the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(24)|close links]]*, or difficulties involved in their enforcement, prevent the effective exercise of the supervisory functions of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*.
