# Article 43 - Other financial resources

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall maintain sufficient pre-funded available financial resources to cover potential losses that exceed the losses to be covered by margin requirements laid down in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.41|Article 41]] and the default fund as referred to in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.42|Article 42]]. Such pre-funded financial resources shall include dedicated resources of the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, shall be freely available to the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and shall not be used to meet the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* required under [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.16|Article 16]].
## 2.
The default fund referred to in [[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.42|Article 42]] and the other financial resources referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article shall at all times enable the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to withstand the default of at least the two *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* to which it has the largest exposures under extreme but plausible market conditions.
## 3.
A *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* may require non-defaulting *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* to provide additional funds in the event of a default of another *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]*. The *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* of a *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have limited exposures toward the *[[2012R0648EMIR1_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
