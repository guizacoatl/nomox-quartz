# Article 45 - Default waterfall

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall use the margins posted by a defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* prior to other financial resources in covering losses.
## 2.
Where the margins posted by the defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* are not sufficient to cover the losses incurred by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall use the default fund contribution of the defaulting member to cover those losses.
## 3.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall use contributions to the default fund of the non-defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* and any other financial resources referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.43#1|Article 43(1)]] only after having exhausted the contributions of the defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]*.
## 4.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall use dedicated own resources before using the default fund contributions of non-defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]*. A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall not use the margins posted by non-defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* to cover the losses resulting from the default of another *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]*.
## 5.
In order to ensure consistent application of this Article, ESMA, shall, after consulting the relevant competent authorities and the members of the ESCB, develop draft regulatory technical standards specifying the methodology for calculation and maintenance of the amount of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s own resources to be used in accordance with paragraph 4.

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 30 September 2012.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
