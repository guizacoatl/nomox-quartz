# Article 24e - Accountability

## 1.
The European Parliament or the Council may invite the Chair and the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* to make a statement while fully respecting their independence. The Chair and the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall make that statement before the European Parliament and answer any questions put by its Members whenever so requested.
## 2.
The Chair and the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall report in writing on the main activities of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* to the European Parliament and to the Council where requested and at least 15 days before making the statement referred to in paragraph 1.
## 3.
The Chair and the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(28)|independent members]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#1.|CCP Supervisory Committee]]* shall report any relevant information requested by the European Parliament on an ad hoc and confidential basis. That report shall not cover confidential information relating to individual *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*.
