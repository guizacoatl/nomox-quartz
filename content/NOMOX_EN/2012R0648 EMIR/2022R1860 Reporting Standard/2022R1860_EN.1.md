# Article 1 - Data standards and formats of derivative reports


The details of a derivative contract to be reported pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.9|Article 9 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] shall be provided in accordance with the standards and formats laid down in Tables 1, 2 and 3 of the Annex to this Regulation and in a common electronic and machine-readable form and in a common XML template in accordance with the ISO 20022 methodology.
