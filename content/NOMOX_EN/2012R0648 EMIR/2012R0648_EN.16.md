## 1.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have a permanent and available initial *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* of at least EUR 7,5 million to be authorised pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]].
## 2.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(25)|capital]]*, including retained earnings and *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(26)|reserves]]*, shall be proportionate to the risk stemming from the activities of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. It shall at all times be sufficient to ensure an orderly winding-down or restructuring of the activities over an appropriate time span and an adequate protection of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* against credit, counterparty, market, operational, legal and business risks which are not already covered by specific financial resources as referred to in Articles [[2012R0648_EN.41|41]] to 44.
## 3.
In order to ensure consistent application of this Article, EBA shall, in close cooperation with the ESCB and after consulting ESMA, develop draft regulatory technical standards specifying requirements regarding the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(25)|capital]]*, retained earnings and *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(26)|reserves]]* of a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* referred to in paragraph 2.

EBA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 30 September 2012.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1093_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1093_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010.
