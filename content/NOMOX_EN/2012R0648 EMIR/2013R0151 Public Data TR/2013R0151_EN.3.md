# Article 3 - Third country authorities

## 1.
In relation to a relevant authority of a third country that has entered into an international agreement with the Union as referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.75|Article 75 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], a trade repository shall provide access to the data, taking account of the third country authority’s mandate and responsibilities and in line with the provisions of the relevant international agreement.
## 2.
In relation to a relevant authority of a third country that has entered into a cooperation arrangement with ESMA as referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.76|Article 76 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], a trade repository shall provide access to the data, taking account of the third country authority’s mandate and responsibilities and in line with the provisions of the relevant cooperation arrangement.
## 3.
In relation to a relevant authority of a third country for which the Commission has adopted an implementing act determining that the legal framework fulfils the conditions set out in [[2012R0648_EN.76a#2|Article 76a(2) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], a trade repository shall provide access to the data, taking account of the third country authority’s mandate and responsibilities.
