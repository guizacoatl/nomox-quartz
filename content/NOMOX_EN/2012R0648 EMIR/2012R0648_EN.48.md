# Article 48 - Default procedures

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall have detailed procedures in place to be followed where a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* does not comply with the participation requirements of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* laid down in [[2012R0648_EN.37|Article 37]] within the time limit and in accordance with the procedures established by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall set out in detail the procedures to be followed in the event the default of a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* is not declared by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. Those procedures shall be reviewed annually.
## 2.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall take prompt action to contain losses and liquidity pressures resulting from defaults and shall ensure that the closing out of any *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]*’s positions does not disrupt its operations or expose the non-defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* to losses that they cannot anticipate or *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(23)|control]]*.
## 3.
Where a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* considers that the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* will not be able to meet its future obligations, it shall promptly inform the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* before the default procedure is declared or triggered. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall promptly communicate that information to ESMA, to the relevant members of the ESCB and to the authority responsible for the supervision of the defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]*.
## 4.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall verify that its default procedures are enforceable. It shall take all reasonable steps to ensure that it has the legal powers to liquidate the proprietary positions of the defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* and to transfer or liquidate the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*’ positions of the defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]*.
## 5.
Where assets and positions are recorded in the records and accounts of a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* as being held for the account of a defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.39#2|Article 39(2)]], the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall, at least, contractually commit itself to trigger the procedures for the transfer of the assets and positions held by the defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* for the account of all its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* to another *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* designated by all of those *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*, and shall transfer such assets and positions unless all *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* object to such transfer before that transfer is concluded and without the consent of the defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]*. That other *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* shall be obliged to accept those assets and positions only where it has previously entered into a contractual relationship with those *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* by which it has committed itself to do so. If the transfer to that other *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* has not taken place for any reason within a predefined transfer period specified in its operating rules, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* may take all steps permitted by its rules to actively manage its risks in relation to those positions, including liquidating the assets and positions held by the defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* for the account of its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*.
## 6.
Where assets and positions are recorded in the records and accounts of a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* as being held for the account of a defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]*’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|client]]* in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.39#3|Article 39(3)]], the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall, at least, contractually commit itself to trigger the procedures for the transfer of the assets and positions held by the defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* for the account of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|client]]* to another *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* designated by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|client]]*, on the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|client]]*’s request and without the consent of the defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]*. That other *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* shall be obliged to accept these assets and positions only where it has previously entered into a contractual relationship with the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|client]]* by which it has committed itself to do so. If the transfer to that other *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* has not taken place for any reason within a predefined transfer period specified in its operating rules, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* may take all steps permitted by its rules to actively manage its risks in relation to those positions, including liquidating the assets and positions held by the defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* for the account of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|client]]*.
## 7.
*[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|Clients]]*’ collateral distinguished in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.39#2|Article 39(2)]] and (3) shall be used exclusively to cover the positions held for their account. Any balance owed by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* after the completion of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]*’s default management process by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall be readily returned to those *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* when they are known to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or, if they are not, to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* for the account of its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]*.
## 8.
In the event of default of a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* and where such default results in the transfer in full or in part of the assets and positions held by *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* from the defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* towards another *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* in accordance with paragraphs 5 and 6, that other *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* may, for three months from the date of that transfer, rely on the due diligence performed by the defaulting *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* pursuant to Section 4 of Chapter II of Directive (EU) 2015/849 for the purpose of complying with the requirements of that Directive.

Where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing member]]* to whom the transfer of assets and positions, as referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph, has been made is subject to Regulation (EU) No 575/2013, it shall comply with the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* requirements for exposures of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(14)|clearing members]]* towards *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(15)|clients]]* under that Regulation within a period agreed with its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*, which shall not exceed three months from the date of that transfer.
