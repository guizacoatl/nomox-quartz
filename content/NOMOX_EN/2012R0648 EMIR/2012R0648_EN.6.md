# Article 6 - Public register

## 1.
ESMA shall establish, maintain and keep up to date a public register in order to identify the classes of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]* subject to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation correctly and unequivocally. The public register shall be available on ESMA’s website.
## 2.
The register shall include:
### (a)
the classes of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]* that are subject to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.4|Article 4]];
### (b)
the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* that are authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.17|Article 17]] or recognised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]] and the date of authorisation or recognition respectively, indicating the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* that are authorised or recognised for the purpose of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation;
### (c)
the dates from which the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation takes effect, including any phased-in implementation;
### (d)
the classes of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]* identified by ESMA in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.5#3|Article 5(3)]];

—————
### (f)
the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* that have been notified to ESMA by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* for the purpose of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* obligation and the date of notification of each of them;
### (g)
the proportion, as of the end of the calendar year, of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]* contracts cleared in *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* authorised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.14|Article 14]] compared with *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(5)|derivatives]]* contracts cleared in third-country *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* recognised in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.25|Article 25]], presented on an aggregated basis and per asset class.
## 3.
Where a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is no longer authorised or recognised in accordance with this Regulation to clear a specific class of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]*, ESMA shall immediately remove that *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* from the public register in relation to that class of *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivatives]]*.
## 4.
In order to ensure consistent application of this Article, ESMA may develop draft regulatory technical standards specifying the details to be included in the public register referred to in paragraph 1.

ESMA shall submit any such draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 30 September 2012.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
