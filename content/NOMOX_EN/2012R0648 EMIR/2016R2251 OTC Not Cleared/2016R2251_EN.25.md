# Article 25 - Minimum transfer amount

## 1.
By way of derogation from [[2016R2251_EN.2#2|Article 2(2)]], counterparties may provide in their risk management procedures that no collateral is collected from a counterparty where the amount due from the last collection of collateral is equal to or lower than the amount agreed by the counterparties (‘*[[2016R2251_EN.1#1.|minimum transfer amount]]*’).

The *[[2016R2251_EN.1#1.|minimum transfer amount]]* shall not exceed EUR 500 000  or the equivalent amount in another currency.
## 2.
Where counterparties agree on a *[[2016R2251_EN.1#1.|minimum transfer amount]]*, the amount of collateral due shall be calculated as the sum of:
### (a)
the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]* due from its last collection calculated in accordance with [[2016R2251_EN.10|Article 10]], including any excess collateral;
### (b)
the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* due from its last collection calculated in accordance with [[2016R2251_EN.11|Article 11]], including any excess collateral.
## 3.
Where the amount of collateral due exceeds the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#1.|minimum transfer amount]]* agreed by the counterparties, the collecting counterparty shall collect the full amount of collateral due without deduction of the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#1.|minimum transfer amount]]*.
## 4.
Counterparties may agree on separate *[[2016R2251_EN.1#1.|minimum transfer amounts]]* for initial and *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margins]]*, provided that the sum of those *[[2016R2251_EN.1#1.|minimum transfer amounts]]* is equal to or lower than EUR 500 000  or the equivalent amount in another currency.
## 5.
Where counterparties agree on separate *[[2016R2251_EN.1#1.|minimum transfer amounts]]* in accordance with paragraph 4, the collecting counterparty shall collect the full amount of initial or *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]* due without any deduction of those *[[2016R2251_EN.1#1.|minimum transfer amounts]]* where the amount of initial or variation collateral due exceeds the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#1.|minimum transfer amount]]*.
