# Article 39 - Calculation of aggregate average notional amount

## 1.
For the purposes of Articles [[2016R2251_EN.36|36]] and [[2016R2251_EN.37|37]], the aggregate average notional amount referred to shall be calculated as the average of the total gross notional amount that meets all of the following conditions:
### (a)
that are recorded on the last business day of March, April and May of 2016 with respect to counterparties referred to in point (a) of [[2016R2251_EN.36#1|Article 36(1)]];
### (b)
that are recorded on the last business day of March, April and May of the year referred to in each of the points in [[2016R2251_EN.36#1|Article 36(1)]];
### (c)
it includes all the entities of the group;
### (d)
it includes all the non-centrally cleared *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|OTC]]* derivative contracts of the group;
### (e)
it includes all the intragroup non-centrally cleared *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|OTC]]* derivative contracts of the group, counting each one of them once.
## 2.
For the purpose of paragraph 1, UCITS authorised in accordance with Directive 2009/65/EC and alternative investment funds managed by alternative investment fund managers authorised or registered in accordance with Directive 2011/61/EU shall be considered distinct entities and treated separately, where the following conditions are met:
### (a)
the funds are distinct segregated pools of assets for the purposes of the fund's insolvency or bankruptcy;
### (b)
the segregated pools of assets are not collateralised, guaranteed or otherwise financially supported by other investment funds or their managers.
