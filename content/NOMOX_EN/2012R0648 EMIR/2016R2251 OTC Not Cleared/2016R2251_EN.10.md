# Article 10 - Calculation of variation margin


The amount of *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]* to be collected by a counterparty shall be the aggregation of the values calculated in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.11#2|Article 11(2) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] of all contracts in the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|netting set]]*, minus the value of all *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]* previously collected, minus the net value of each contract in the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|netting set]]* at the point of entry into the contract, and plus the value of all *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]* previously posted.
