# Article 9 - Frequency of calculation and determination of the calculation date

## 1.
Counterparties shall calculate *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]* in accordance with [[2016R2251_EN.10|Article 10]] at least on a daily basis.
## 2.
Counterparties shall calculate *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* in accordance with [[2016R2251_EN.11|Article 11]] no later than the business day following one of these events:
### (a)
where a new non-centrally cleared *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|OTC]]* derivative contract is executed or added to the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|netting set]]*;
### (b)
where an existing non-centrally cleared *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|OTC]]* derivative contract expires or is removed from the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|netting set]]*;
### (c)
where an existing non-centrally cleared *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|OTC]]* derivative contract triggers a payment or a delivery other than the posting and collecting of margins;
### (d)
where the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* is calculated in accordance with the standardised approach referred to in [[2016R2251_EN.11#1|Article 11(1)]] and an existing contract is reclassified in terms of the asset category referred to in paragraph 1 of Annex IV as a result of reduced time to maturity;
### (e)
where no calculation has been performed in the preceding 10 business days.
## 3.
For the purpose of determining the calculation date for initial and *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]*, the following shall apply:
### (a)
where two counterparties are located in the same time-zone, the calculation shall be based on the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|netting set]]* of the previous business day;
### (b)
where two counterparties are not located in the same time-zone, the calculation shall be based on the transactions in the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|netting set]]* which are entered into before 16.00 of the previous business day of the time zone where it is first 16.00.
