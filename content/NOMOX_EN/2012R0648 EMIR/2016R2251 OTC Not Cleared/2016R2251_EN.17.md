# Article 17 - Diversification, hedging and risk offsets across underlying classes

## 1.
*[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|Initial margin]]* models shall only include non-centrally cleared *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|OTC]]* derivative contracts within the same *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|netting set]]*. *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|Initial margin]]* models may provide for diversification, hedging and risk offsets arising from the risks of the contracts within the same *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|netting set]]*, provided that the diversification, hedging or risk offset is only carried out within the same underlying asset class as referred to in paragraph 2.
## 2.
For the purposes of paragraph 1, diversification, hedging and risk offsets may only be carried out within the following underlying asset classes:
### (a)
interest rates, currency and inflation;
### (b)
equity;
### (c)
credit;
### (d)
commodities and gold;
### (e)
other.
