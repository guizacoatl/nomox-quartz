# Article 15 - Confidence interval and MPOR

## 1.
The assumed variations in the value of the non-centrally cleared *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|OTC]]* derivative contracts within the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|netting set]]* for the calculation of *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margins]]* using an *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* model shall be based on a one-tailed 99 percent confidence interval over a MPOR of at least 10 days.
## 2.
The MPOR for the calculation of *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margins]]* using an *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* model referred to in paragraph 1 shall include:
### (a)
the period that may elapse from the last margin exchange of *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]* to the default of the counterparty;
### (b)
the estimated period needed to replace each of the non-centrally cleared *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|OTC]]* derivative contracts within the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|netting set]]* or hedge the risks arising from them, taking into account the level of liquidity of the market where those types of contracts are traded, the total volume of the non-centrally cleared *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|OTC]]* derivative contracts in that market and the number of participants in that market.
