# Article 12 - Provision of variation margin

## 1.
The posting counterparty shall provide the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]* as follows:
### (a)
within the same business day of the calculation date determined in accordance with [[2016R2251_EN.9#3|Article 9(3)]];
### (b)
where the conditions in paragraph 2 are met, within 2 business days of the calculation date determined in accordance with [[2016R2251_EN.9#3|Article 9(3)]].
## 2.
The provision of *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]* in accordance with paragraph 1(b) may only be applied to the following:
### (a)
*[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|netting sets]]* comprising derivative contracts not subject to *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* requirements in accordance with this Regulation, where the posting counterparty has provided, at or before the calculation date of the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]*, an advance amount of eligible collateral calculated in the same manner as that applicable to *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margins]]* in accordance with [[2016R2251_EN.15|Article 15]], for which the collecting counterparty has used a margin period of risk (‘MPOR’) at least equal to the number of days in between and including the calculation date and the collection date;
### (b)
*[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|netting sets]]* comprising contracts subject to *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* requirements in accordance with this Regulation, where the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* has been adjusted in one of the following ways:
#### (i)
by increasing the MPOR referred to in [[2016R2251_EN.15#2|Article 15(2)]] by the number of days in between, and including, the calculation date determined in accordance with [[2016R2251_EN.9#3|Article 9(3)]] and the collection date determined in accordance with paragraph 1 of this Article;
#### (ii)
by increasing the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* calculated in accordance with the standardised approach referred to in [[2016R2251_EN.11|Article 11]] using an appropriate methodology taking into account a MPOR that is increased by the number of days in between, and including, the calculation date determined in accordance with [[2016R2251_EN.9#3|Article 9(3)]] and the collection date determined in accordance with paragraph 2 of this Article.

For the purposes of point (a), in case no mechanism for segregation is in place between the two counterparties, those counterparties may offset the amounts to be provided.
## 3.
In the event of a dispute over the amount of *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]* due, counterparties shall provide, in the same time frame referred to in paragraph 1, at least the part of the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]* amount that is not being disputed.
