# Article 20 - Treatment of collected initial margins

## 1.
The collecting counterparty shall not rehypothecate, repledge nor otherwise reuse the collateral collected as *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]*.
## 2.
Notwithstanding paragraph 1, a third-party holder may use the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* received in cash for reinvestment purposes.
