# Article 1 - Definitions


For the purposes of this Regulation, the following definitions apply:
## (1)
‘*[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]*’ means the collateral collected by a counterparty to cover its current and potential future exposure in the interval between the last collection of margin and the liquidation of positions or hedging of market risk following a default of the other counterparty;
## (2)
‘*[[2016R2251_EN.1#(2)|variation margin]]*’ means the collateral collected by a counterparty to reflect the results of the daily marking-to-market or marking-to-model of outstanding contracts referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.11#2|Article 11(2) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (3)
‘*[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|netting set]]*’ means a set of non-centrally cleared over-the-counter (‘*[[2016R2251_EN.1#(3)|OTC]]*’) derivative contracts between two counterparties that is subject to a legally enforceable bilateral netting agreement.
