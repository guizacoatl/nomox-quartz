# Article 13 - Provision of initial margin

## 1.
The posting counterparty shall provide the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* in accordance with Section 5.
## 2.
The posting counterparty shall provide the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* within the same business day of the calculation date determined in accordance with [[2016R2251_EN.9#3|Article 9(3)]].
## 3.
In the event of a dispute over the amount of *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* due, counterparties shall provide at least the part of the *[[2016R2251_EN.1#(1)|initial margin]]* amount that is not being disputed within the same business day of the calculation date determined in accordance with [[2016R2251_EN.9#3|Article 9(3)]].
