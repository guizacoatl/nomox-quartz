# Article 31 - Information to competent authorities

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall notify its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of any changes to its management, and shall provide the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* with all the information necessary to assess compliance with [[2012R0648_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]] and the second subparagraph of [[2012R0648_EN.27#2|Article 27(2)]].

Where the conduct of a member of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|board]]* is likely to be prejudicial to the sound and prudent management of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall take appropriate measures, which may include removing that member from the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(27)|board]]*.
## 2.
Any natural or legal person or such persons acting in concert (the ‘*[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]*’), who have taken a decision either to acquire, directly or indirectly, a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(20)|qualifying holding]]* in a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or to further increase, directly or indirectly, such a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(20)|qualifying holding]]* in a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* as a result of which the proportion of the voting rights or of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* held would reach or exceed 10 %, 20 %, 30 % or 50 % or so that the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* would become its *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(22)|subsidiary]]* (the ‘*[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]*’), shall first notify in writing the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in which they are seeking to acquire or increase a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(20)|qualifying holding]]*, indicating the size of the intended holding and relevant information, as referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.32#4|Article 32(4)]].

Any natural or legal person who has taken a decision to dispose, directly or indirectly, of a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(20)|qualifying holding]]* in a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* (the ‘proposed vendor’) shall first notify the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* in writing thereof, indicating the size of such holding. Such a person shall likewise notify the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* where it has taken a decision to reduce a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(20)|qualifying holding]]* so that the proportion of the voting rights or of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* held would fall below 10 %, 20 %, 30 % or 50 % or so that the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* would cease to be that person’s *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(22)|subsidiary]]*.

The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall, promptly and in any event within two working days of receipt of the notification referred to in this paragraph and of the information referred to in paragraph 3 of this Article, acknowledge receipt in writing thereof to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]* or vendor and share the information with ESMA and the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]].

Within 60 working days of the date of the written acknowledgement of receipt of the notification and all documents required to be attached to the notification on the basis of the list referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.32#4|Article 32(4)]] and unless extended in accordance with this Article, (‘the assessment period’), the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall carry out the assessment provided for in [[2012R0648_EN.32#1|Article 32(1)]] (‘the assessment’). The college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] shall issue an opinion pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.19|Article 19]] and ESMA shall issue an opinion pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.24a#7|Article 24a(7)]], first subparagraph, point (bc), and in accordance with the procedure under [[2012R0648_EN.17b|Article 17b]] during the assessment period.

The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall inform the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]* or vendor of the date of the expiry of the assessment period at the time of acknowledging receipt.
## 3.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall, on its own behalf and where requested by ESMA or the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]], without undue delay during the assessment period, where necessary, but no later than on the 50th working day of the assessment period, request such further information that is necessary to complete the assessment. Such request shall be made in writing and shall specify the additional information needed.

The assessment period shall be interrupted for the period between the date of request for information by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* and the receipt of a response thereto by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]*. The interruption shall not exceed 20 working days. Any further requests by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* for completion or clarification of the information shall be at its discretion but may not result in an interruption of the assessment period.
## 4.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* may extend the interruption referred to in the second subparagraph of paragraph 3 up to 30 working days where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]* or vendor is either:
### (a)
situated or regulated outside the Union;
### (b)
a natural or legal person not subject to supervision under this Regulation or Directive 73/239/EEC, Council Directive 92/49/EEC of 18 June 1992 on the coordination of laws, regulations and administrative provisions relating to direct insurance other than life assurance or Directives 2002/83/EC, 2003/41/EC, 2004/39/EC, 2005/68/EC, 2006/48/EC, 2009/65/EC or 2011/61/EU.
## 5.
Where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]*, upon completion of the assessment, decides to oppose the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]*, it shall, within two working days, and not exceeding the assessment period, inform the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]* in writing and provide the reasons for that decision. The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* shall notify the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18|Article 18]] accordingly. Subject to national law, an appropriate statement of the reasons for the decision may be made accessible to the public at the request of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]*. However, Member States may allow a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* to make such disclosure in the absence of a request by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]*.
## 6.
Where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* does not oppose the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]* within the assessment period, it shall be deemed to be approved.
## 7.
The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* may fix a maximum period for concluding the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]* and extend it where appropriate.
## 8.
Member States shall not impose requirements for notification to, and approval by, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of direct or indirect acquisitions of voting rights or *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* that are more stringent than those set out in this Regulation.
