# Article 7 - Access to a CCP

## 1.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* that has been authorised to clear *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(7)|OTC derivative contracts]]* shall accept *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(3)|clearing]]* such contracts on a non-discriminatory and transparent basis, including as regards collateral requirements and fees related to access, regardless of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]*. This in particular shall ensure that a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]* has the right to non-discriminatory treatment in terms of how contracts traded on that *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]* are treated in terms of:
### (a)
collateral requirements and netting of economically equivalent contracts, where the inclusion of such contracts in the close-out and other netting procedures of a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* based on the applicable insolvency law would not endanger the smooth and orderly functioning, the validity or enforceability of such procedures; and
### (b)
cross-margining with correlated contracts cleared by the same *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under a risk model that complies with [[2012R0648_EN.41|Article 41]].

A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* may require that a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]* comply with the operational and technical requirements established by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, including the risk-management requirements.
## 2.
A *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall accede to or refuse a formal request for access by a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]* within three months of such a request.
## 3.
Where a *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* refuses access under paragraph 2, it shall provide the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]* with full reasons for such refusal.
## 4.
Save where the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]* and that of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* refuse access, the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall, subject to the second subparagraph, grant access within three months of a decision acceding to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]*’s formal request in accordance with paragraph 2.

The *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(13)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]* and that of the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* may refuse access to the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* following a formal request by the *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]* only where such access would threaten the smooth and orderly functioning of the markets or would adversely affect systemic risk.
## 5.
ESMA shall settle any dispute arising from a disagreement between competent authorities in accordance with its powers under [[2010R1095_EN.19|Article 19 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
## 6.
The conditions laid down in paragraph 1 regarding non-discriminatory treatment in terms of how contracts traded on that *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(4)|trading venue]]* are treated in terms of collateral requirements and netting of economically equivalent contracts and cross-margining with correlated contracts cleared by the same *[[2012R0648_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall be further specified by the technical standards adopted pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.35#6#e|Article 35(6)(e) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]].
