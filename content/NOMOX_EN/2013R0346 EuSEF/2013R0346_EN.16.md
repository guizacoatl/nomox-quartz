# Article 16 - Managers of qualifying social entrepreneurship funds shall inform the competent authority of the home Member State where they intend to market:

### (a)
a new qualifying social entrepreneurship fund; or
### (b)
an existing qualifying social entrepreneurship fund in a Member State not mentioned in the list referred to in point (d) of [[2013R0346_EN.15#1|Article 15(1)]].
