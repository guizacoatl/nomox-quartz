# Article 2 - 1.


This Regulation applies to managers of collective investment undertakings as defined in point (a) of [[2013R0346_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]] that meet the following conditions:
### (a)
their assets under management in total do not exceed the threshold referred to in point (b) of [[2011L0061_EN.3#2|Article 3(2) of Directive 2011/61/EU]];
### (b)
they are established in the Union;
### (c)
they are subject to registration with the competent authorities of their home Member State in accordance with point (a) of [[2011L0061_EN.3#3|Article 3(3) of Directive 2011/61/EU]]; and
### (d)
they manage portfolios of qualifying social entrepreneurship funds.
## 2.
Articles [[2013R0346_EN.3|3]] to 6, Articles [[2013R0346_EN.10|10]] and [[2013R0346_EN.13|13]], points (d), (e) and (f) of [[2013R0346_EN.14#1|Article 14(1)]], Articles [[2013R0346_EN.15a|15a]] to 20, the second subparagraph of Article 21(3) and Articles [[2013R0346_EN.22|22]] and [[2013R0346_EN.22a|22a]] of this Regulation shall apply to managers of collective investment undertakings authorised under [[2011L0061_EN.6|Article 6 of Directive 2011/61/EU]] that manage portfolios of qualifying social entrepreneurship funds and intend to use the designation ‘EuSEF’ in relation to the marketing of those funds in the Union.
## 3.
Where managers of qualifying social entrepreneurship funds are external managers and are registered in accordance with [[2013R0346_EN.15|Article 15]], they may additionally manage undertakings for collective investment in transferable securities (UCITS), subject to authorisation under Directive 2009/65/EC.
