# Article 29 - This Regulation shall enter into force on the twentieth day following that of its publication in the
 Official Journal of the European Union .

It shall apply from the 22 July 2013, except for [[2013R0346_EN.3#2|Article 3(2)]], [[2013R0346_EN.9#5|Article 9(5)]], Article 10(2) and [[2013R0346_EN.14#4|Article 14(4)]], which shall apply from 15 May 2013.
