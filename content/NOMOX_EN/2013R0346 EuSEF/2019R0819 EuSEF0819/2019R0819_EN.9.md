> Level 1 reference(s): [[2013R0346_EN.14#1#d]]# Article 9 - Information on positive social impact

## 1.
The information referred to in [[2013R0346_EN.14#1#d|Article 14(1)(d) of Regulation (EU) No 346/2013]] shall specify targeted products and services to be provided by the qualifying portfolio undertakings in which the qualifying social entrepreneurship fund invests.
## 2.
Where the information referred to in [[2013R0346_EN.14#1#d|Article 14(1)(d) of Regulation (EU) No 346/2013]] contains information on projections of positive social impacts, it shall describe the assumptions on the basis of which those projections are calculated.
## 3.
Where the information referred to in [[2013R0346_EN.14#1#d|Article 14(1)(d) of Regulation (EU) No 346/2013]] contains information on past performance in terms of positive social impact, it shall contain a copy of the most recent annual report, or a summary of the relevant information contained in the annual report referred to in [[2013R0346_EN.13#2|Article 13(2) of Regulation (EU) No 346/2013]].
