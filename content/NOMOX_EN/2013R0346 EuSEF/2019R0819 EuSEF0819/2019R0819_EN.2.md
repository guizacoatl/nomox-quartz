# Article 2 - Conflicts of interest policy

## 1.
A manager of a qualifying social entrepreneurship fund shall establish, implement and maintain a written conflicts of interest policy that is appropriate to the size and organisational structure of that manager given the nature, scale and complexity of its business.
## 2.
The conflicts of interest policy referred to in paragraph 1 shall identify, in line with [[2019R0819_EN.1|Article 1]], the circumstances that may give rise to a conflict of interest and shall specify the measures to be adopted and the procedures to be followed on an ongoing basis.
