### CHAPTER I - SUBJECT MATTER, SCOPE AND DEFINITIONS
[[2013R0346_EN.1|Article 1]] - This Regulation lays down uniform requirements and conditions for managers of collective investment undertakings that wish to use the designation ‘EuSEF’ in relation to the marketing of qualifying social entrepreneurship funds in the Union, thereby contributing to the smooth functioning of the internal market.
