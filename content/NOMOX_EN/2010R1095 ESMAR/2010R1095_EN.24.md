# Article 24 - Permanent capacity to respond to systemic risks

## 1.
The Authority shall ensure it has specialised and ongoing capacity to respond effectively to the materialisation of systemic risks as referred to in Articles [[2010R1095_EN.22|22]] and [[2010R1095_EN.23|23]], in particular with respect to institutions that pose a systemic risk.
## 2.
The Authority shall fulfil the tasks conferred upon it in this Regulation and in the legislation referred to in [[2010R1095_EN.1#2|Article 1(2)]], and shall contribute to ensuring a coherent and coordinated crisis management and resolution regime in the Union.
