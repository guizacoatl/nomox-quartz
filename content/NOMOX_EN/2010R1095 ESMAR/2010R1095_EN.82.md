# Article 82 - Entry into force


This Regulation shall enter into force on the day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union .

It shall apply from 1 January 2011, with the exception of Article 76 and [[2010R1095_EN.77#1|Article 77(1)]] and (2), which shall apply as from the date of its entry into force.

The Authority shall be established on 1 January 2011.
