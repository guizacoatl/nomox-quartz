# Article 26 - European system of national Investor Compensation Schemes

## 1.
The Authority shall contribute to strengthening the European system of national Investor Compensation Schemes (ICS) by acting under the powers conferred to it in this Regulation to ensure the correct application of Directive 97/9/EC with the aim of ensuring that national Investor Compensation Schemes are adequately funded by contributions from the concerned *[[2010R1095_EN.4#(1)|financial market participants]]*, including where appropriate *[[2010R1095_EN.4#(1)|financial market participants]]* headquartered in third-countries, and provide a high level of protection to all investors in a harmonised framework throughout the Union.
## 2.
[[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16]] concerning the Authority’s powers to adopt guidelines and recommendations shall apply to Investor Compensation Schemes.
## 3.
The Authority may develop regulatory and *[[2010R1095_EN.4#4.|implementing technical standards]]* as specified in the legislative acts referred to in [[2010R1095_EN.1#2|Article 1(2)]] in accordance with the procedure laid down in Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to 15.
## 4.
The review of this Regulation provided for in [[2010R1095_EN.81|Article 81]] shall in particular examine the convergence of the European system of national Investor Compensation Schemes.
