# Article 11 - Exercise of the delegation

## 1.
The power to adopt *[[2010R1095_EN.4#4.|regulatory technical standards]]* referred to in [[2010R1095_EN.10|Article 10]] shall be conferred on the Commission for a period of 4 years from 16 December 2010. The Commission shall draw up a report in respect of the delegated power not later than 6 months before the end of the four-year period. The delegation of power shall be automatically extended for periods of an identical duration, unless the European Parliament or the Council revokes it in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.14|Article 14]].
## 2.
As soon as it adopts a *[[2010R1095_EN.4#4.|regulatory technical standard]]*, the Commission shall notify it simultaneously to the European Parliament and to the Council.
## 3.
The power to adopt *[[2010R1095_EN.4#4.|regulatory technical standards]]* is conferred on the Commission subject to the conditions laid down in Articles [[2010R1095_EN.12|12]] to 14.
