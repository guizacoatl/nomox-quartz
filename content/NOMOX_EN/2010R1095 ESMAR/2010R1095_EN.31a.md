# Article 31a - Information exchange on fitness and propriety


The Authority shall, together with the European Supervisory Authority (European Banking Authority) and with the European Supervisory Authority (European Insurance and Occupational Pensions Authority), establish a system for the exchange of information relevant to the assessment of the fitness and propriety of holders of qualifying holdings, directors and key function holders of *[[2010R1095_EN.4#(1)|financial market participant]]* by *[[2010R1095_EN.4#(3)|competent authorities]]* in accordance with the legislative acts referred to in [[2010R1095_EN.1#2|Article 1(2)]].
