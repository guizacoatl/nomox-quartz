# Article 23 - Identification and measurement of systemic risk

## 1.
The Authority shall, in consultation with the ESRB, develop criteria for the identification and measurement of systemic risk and an adequate stress-testing regime which includes an evaluation of the potential for systemic risk posed by, or to, *[[2010R1095_EN.4#(1)|financial market participants]]* to increase in situations of stress, including potential environmental-related systemic risk. The *[[2010R1095_EN.4#(1)|financial market participants]]* that may pose a systemic risk shall be subject to strengthened supervision, and where necessary, the recovery and resolution procedures referred to in [[2010R1095_EN.25|Article 25]].
## 2.
The Authority shall take fully into account the relevant international approaches when developing the criteria for the identification and measurement of systemic risk posed by *[[2010R1095_EN.4#(1)|financial market participants]]*, including those established by the Financial Stability Board, the International Monetary Fund and the Bank for International Settlements.
