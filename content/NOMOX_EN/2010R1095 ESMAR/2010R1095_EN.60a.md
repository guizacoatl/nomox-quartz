# Article 60a - Exceeding of competence by the Authority


Any natural or legal person may send reasoned advice to the Commission if that person is of the opinion that the Authority has exceeded its competence, including by failing to respect the principle of proportionality referred to in [[2010R1095_EN.1#5|Article 1(5)]], when acting under Articles [[2010R1095_EN.16|16]] and [[2010R1095_EN.16b|16b]], and that is of direct and individual concern to that person.
