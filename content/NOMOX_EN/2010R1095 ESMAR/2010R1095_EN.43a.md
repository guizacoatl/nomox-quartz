# Article 43a - Transparency of decisions adopted by the Board of Supervisors


Notwithstanding [[2010R1095_EN.70|Article 70]], within six weeks of each meeting of the Board of Supervisors, the Authority shall, at least, provide the European Parliament with a comprehensive and meaningful record of the proceedings of that meeting that enables a full understanding of the discussions, including an annotated list of decisions. Such record shall not reflect discussions within the Board of Supervisors relating to individual *[[2010R1095_EN.4#(1)|financial market participants]]*, unless otherwise provided for in [[2010R1095_EN.75#3|Article 75(3)]] or in the legislative acts referred to in [[2010R1095_EN.1#2|Article 1(2)]].
