# Article 6 - Composition


The Authority shall comprise:
## (1)
a Board of Supervisors, which shall exercise the tasks set out in [[2010R1095_EN.43|Article 43]];
## (2)
a Management Board, which shall exercise the tasks set out in [[2010R1095_EN.47|Article 47]];
## (3)
a Chairperson, who shall exercise the tasks set out in [[2010R1095_EN.48|Article 48]];
## (4)
an Executive Director, who shall exercise the tasks set out in [[2010R1095_EN.53|Article 53]];
## (5)
a Board of Appeal, which shall exercise the tasks set out in [[2010R1095_EN.60|Article 60]].
