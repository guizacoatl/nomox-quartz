# Article 57 - Sub-Committees

## 1.
For the purposes of [[2010R1095_EN.56|Article 56]], a Sub-Committee on Financial Conglomerates to the Joint Committee shall be established.
## 2.
The Sub-Committee shall be composed of the individuals referred to in [[2010R1095_EN.55#1|Article 55(1)]], and one high-level representative from the current staff of the relevant competent authority from each Member State.
## 3.
The Sub-Committee shall elect a Chairperson from among its members, who shall also be a member of the Joint Committee.
## 4.
The Joint Committee may establish further Sub Committees.
