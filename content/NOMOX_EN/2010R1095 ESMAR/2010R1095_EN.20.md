# Article 20 - Settlement of disagreements between competent authorities across sectors


The Joint Committee shall, in accordance with the procedure laid down in Article 19 and [[2010R1095_EN.56|Article 56]], settle cross-sectoral disagreements that may arise between *[[2010R1095_EN.4#(3)|competent authorities]]* as defined in [[2010R1095_EN.4#2|Article 4(2)]] of this Regulation, of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010 and of Regulation (EU) No 1094/2010 respectively.
