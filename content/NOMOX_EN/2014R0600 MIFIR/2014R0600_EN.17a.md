# Article 17a - Tick sizes

## 1.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(12)|Systematic internalisers]]*’ quotes, price improvements on those quotes and execution prices shall comply with the tick sizes set in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.49|Article 49 of Directive 2014/65/EU]].
## 2.
The requirements laid down in [[2014R0600_EN.15#2|Article 15(2)]] of this Regulation and in [[2014L0065_EN.49|Article 49 of Directive 2014/65/EU]] shall not prevent *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(12)|systematic internalisers]]* from matching orders at midpoint within the current bid and offer prices.
