# Article 39a - Prohibition of receiving payment for order flow

## 1.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|Investment firms]]* acting on behalf of retail *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|clients]]*, as defined in [[2014R0600_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (11), of Directive 2014/65/EU, or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(8)|professional clients]]* as referred to in Section II of Annex II to that Directive shall not receive any fee, commission or non-monetary benefit from any third party for executing orders from those *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|clients]]* on a particular execution venue or for forwarding orders of those *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|clients]]* to any third party for their execution on a particular execution venue (‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#1.|payment for order flow]]*’).

The first subparagraph shall not apply to rebates or discounts on the transaction fees of execution venues, where permitted under the approved and public tariff structure of a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* in the Union or of a third-country *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]*, where they exclusively benefit the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|client]]*. Such discounts or rebates shall not result in a monetary benefit to the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firm]]*.
## 2.
A Member State in which, before 28 March 2024, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* acting on behalf of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|clients]]* are established which receive a fee, a commission or a non-monetary benefit from any third party for executing orders from those *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|clients]]* on a particular execution venue or for forwarding orders of those *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|clients]]* to any third party for their execution on a particular execution venue, may exempt *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* under its jurisdiction from the prohibition laid down in paragraph 1 until 30 June 2026 where those *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* provide investment services to *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|clients]]* domiciled or established in that Member State.

To apply the exemption referred to in the first subparagraph, a Member State which fulfils the condition laid down in the first subparagraph shall notify ESMA by 29 September 2024 to that effect. ESMA shall maintain a list of Member States using that exemption. The list shall be made available to the public and updated regularly.
