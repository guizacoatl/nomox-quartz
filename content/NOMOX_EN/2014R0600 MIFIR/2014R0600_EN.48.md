# Article 48 - Register


ESMA shall keep a register of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(42)|third-country firms]]* allowed to provide investment services or perform investment activities in the Union in accordance with [[2014R0600_EN.46|Article 46]]. The register shall be publicly accessible on the website of ESMA and shall contain information on the services or activities which the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(42)|third-country firms]]* are permitted to provide or perform and the reference of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* responsible for their supervision in the third country.
