# Article 27h - Organisational requirements for CTPs

## 1.
A *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* shall, in accordance with the conditions for authorisation referred to in Article 27db:
### (a)
collect all data transmitted by *[[2014R0600_EN.2#1.|data contributors]]* in relation to the asset class for which it is authorised;
### (b)
collect fees from users, while providing access, free of charge, to the consolidated tape to retail investors, academics, civil society organisations and competent authorities;
### (c)
in the case of the consolidated tape for shares and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]*, redistribute part of its revenue in accordance with paragraph 6;
### (d)
disseminate *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36b)|core market data]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36c)|regulatory data]]* to users as a continuous electronic live data stream on non-discriminatory terms as close to real time as technically possible;
### (e)
ensure that the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36b)|core market data]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36c)|regulatory data]]* are easily accessible, machine-readable and usable for all users, including retail investors;
### (f)
have systems in place that can effectively check the completeness of the data transmitted by *[[2014R0600_EN.2#1.|data contributors]]*, identify obvious errors, and request the re-submission of data;
### (g)
where the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* is controlled by a group of economic operators, have a compliance system in place to ensure that the operation of the consolidated tape does not result in a distortion of competition.

For the purposes of the first subparagraph, point (d), a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* for shares and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]* shall not publish the market identifier code when disseminating the European best bid and offer as close as to real time as technically possible to the public.
## 2.
A *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* shall adopt, publish on its website and regularly update service level standards covering all of the following:
### (a)
an inventory of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#1.|data contributors]]* from whom data are received;
### (b)
modes and speed of delivery of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36b)|core market data]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36c)|regulatory data]]* to users;
### (c)
measures taken to ensure operational continuity in the provision of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36b)|core market data]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36c)|regulatory data]]*.
## 3.
A *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* shall have sound security arrangements in place designed to guarantee the security of the means of transfer of data between the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#1.|data contributors]]* and the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* and between the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* and the users and to minimise the risk of data corruption and unauthorised access. The *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* shall maintain adequate resources and have back-up facilities in place to offer and maintain its services at all times.
## 4.
For each of the asset classes referred to in Article 27da(1), a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* shall publish a list of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* that are covered by the consolidated tape, indicating their identifying reference data.

The *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* shall offer access, free of charge, to its list, and shall ensure that the list is regularly reviewed and updated, in order to offer a comprehensive view of all the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* covered by the consolidated tape.
## 5.
A *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* for *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* other than shares and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]* may redistribute to *[[2014R0600_EN.2#1.|data contributors]]* part of the revenue generated by the consolidated tape.
## 6.
A *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* for shares and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]* shall redistribute part of the revenue generated by the consolidated tape, as indicated in the reasoned decision referred to in Article 27db(3), to *[[2014R0600_EN.2#1.|data contributors]]* meeting one or more of the following criteria (the ‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#6.|revenue redistribution scheme]]*’):
### (a)
the data contributor is a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(13)|regulated market]]* or an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(8a)|SME growth market]]* whose annual trading volume of shares represents 1 % or less of the annual trading volume of shares in the Union (‘small *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]*’);
### (b)
the data contributor is a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* that provided initial admission to trading of shares or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]* on 27 March 2019 or thereafter;
### (c)
the data are transmitted by a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* and pertain to transactions in shares and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]* that have been concluded on a trading system that provides pre-trade transparency, where those transactions did not result from orders that were subject to a waiver from pre-trade transparency pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (c).
## 7.
For the purposes of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#6.|revenue redistribution scheme]]*, the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* shall take into account the following trading volume (the ‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#7.|relevant trading volume]]*’):
### (a)
for the purposes of paragraph 6, point (a), the total annual trading volume generated by that *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]*;
### (b)
for the purposes of paragraph 6, point (b):
#### (i)
in the case of small *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]*, their total annual trading volume;
#### (ii)
in the case of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]* other than small *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]*, the trading volume pertaining to the shares and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]* referred to in that point;
### (c)
for the purposes of paragraph 6, point (c), the volume pertaining to the shares and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]* referred to in that point.

The *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* shall determine the amount of the revenue to be redistributed to *[[2014R0600_EN.2#1.|data contributors]]* under the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#6.|revenue redistribution scheme]]* by multiplying the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#7.|relevant trading volume]]* by the weighting assigned to each criterion laid down in paragraph 6, as specified in the regulatory technical standards adopted pursuant to paragraph 8.

If *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]* meet more than one of the criteria laid down in paragraph 6, the amounts resulting from the calculation referred to in the second subparagraph of this paragraph shall be added cumulatively.
## 8.
ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards to:
### (a)
specify the weighting assigned to each criterion laid down in paragraph 6;
### (b)
further specify the method for calculating the amount of the revenue to be redistributed to *[[2014R0600_EN.2#1.|data contributors]]* as referred to in paragraph 7, second subparagraph;
### (c)
specify the criteria under which the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* can, where the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* proves that a data contributor has seriously and repeatedly breached the data requirements referred to in Articles [[2014R0600_EN.22a|22a]], [[2014R0600_EN.22b|22b]] and [[2014R0600_EN.22c|22c]], temporarily suspend the participation of that data contributor in the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#6.|revenue redistribution scheme]]*, and specify the conditions under which the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* is to:
#### (i)
resume revenue redistribution; and
#### (ii)
where there was no breach of those requirements, provide that data contributor with the revenue retained plus interest.

For the purposes of the first subparagraph, point (a), of this paragraph, the criterion laid down in paragraph 6, point (a), shall have a higher weighting than the criterion laid down in point (b) of that paragraph, and the criterion laid down in point (b) of that paragraph shall have a higher weighting than the criterion laid down in point (c) of that paragraph.

ESMA shall submit the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph to the Commission by 29 December 2024.

Power is delegated to the Commission to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
