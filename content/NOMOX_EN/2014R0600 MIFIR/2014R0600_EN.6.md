> Level 2 reference(s): [[2017R0587_EN.12]], [[2017R0587_EN.14]]# Article 6 - Post-trade transparency requirements for trading venues in respect of shares, depositary receipts, ETFs, certificates and other similar financial instruments

## 1.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|Market operators]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* operating a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* shall make public the price, volume and time of the transactions executed in respect of shares, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(25)|depositary receipts]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(27)|certificates]]* and other similar *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* traded on that *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]*. *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|Market operators]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* operating a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* shall make details of all such transactions public as close to real-time as is technically possible.
## 2.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|Market operators]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* operating a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* shall give access, on reasonable commercial terms and on a non-discriminatory basis, to the arrangements they employ for making public the information under paragraph 1 of this Article to *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* which are obliged to publish the details of their transactions in shares, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(25)|depositary receipts]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(27)|certificates]]* and other similar *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.20|Article 20]].
