# Article 9 - Contractual terms

## 1.
The market data agreement shall specify in a clear and concise manner the terms and conditions for the provision of market data and allow the client to easily understand the obligations and rights in that agreement.
## 2.
The market data agreement shall use clear and comprehensible definitions and terms and shall use the terminology of the market data policy as set out in [[2025R1156_EN.18|Article 18]].
