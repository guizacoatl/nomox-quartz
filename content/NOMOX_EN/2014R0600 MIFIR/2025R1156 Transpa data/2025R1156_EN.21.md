# Article 21 - Format for publication of market data policy

## 1.
Market data providers shall publish the market data policy by using the template set out in Annex I. That template shall not be used for any other information.
## 2.
In the market data policy market data providers shall provide information in a consistent manner and with the same level of granularity and ensure that offers to market data clients can be easily compared. Information on pre- and post-trade data shall be provided separately.
