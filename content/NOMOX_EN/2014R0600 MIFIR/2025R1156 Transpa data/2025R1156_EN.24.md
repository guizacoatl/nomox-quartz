# Article 24 - Content of delayed market data


Market operators and investment firms operating a trading venue, APAs and systematic internalisers shall make available to the public the delayed market data from all the systems operated, in accordance with the following criteria:
### (a)
the delayed pre-trade market data shall contain the current best bid and offer prices available and the depth of trading interest at those best bid and offer prices;
### (b)
the delayed post-trade market data shall contain all the relevant fields for the purpose of post-trade transparency, as specified in Commission Delegated Regulations (EU) 2017/587


and (EU) 2017/583


, and no other field.
