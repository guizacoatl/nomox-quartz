# Article 37 - Non-discriminatory access to and obligation to licence benchmarks

## 1.
Where the value of any *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]* is calculated by reference to a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]*, a person with proprietary rights to the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* shall ensure that *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(31)|CCPs]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]* are permitted, for the purposes of trading and clearing, non-discriminatory access to:
### (a)
relevant price and data feeds and information on the composition, methodology and pricing of that *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* for the purposes of clearing and trading; and
### (b)
licences.

A licence including access to information shall be granted on a fair, reasonable and non-discriminatory basis within three months following the request by a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(31)|CCP]]* or a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]*.

Access shall be given at a reasonable commercial price taking into account the price at which access to the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* is granted or the intellectual property rights are licensed on equivalent terms to another *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(31)|CCP]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]* or any related persons for the purposes of clearing and trading. Different prices can be charged to different *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(31)|CCPs]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]* or any related persons only where objectively justified having regard to reasonable commercial grounds such as the quantity, scope or field of use demanded.
## 2.
Where a new *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* is developed after
3 January 2018

the obligation to licence starts no later than 30 months after a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]* referencing that *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* commenced trading or was admitted to trading. Where a person with proprietary rights to a new *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* owns an existing *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]*, that person shall establish that compared to any such existing *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* the new *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* meets the following cumulative criteria:
### (a)
the new *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* is not a mere copy or adaptation of any such existing *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* and the methodology, including the underlying data, of the new *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* is meaningfully different from any such existing *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]*; and
### (b)
the new *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* is not a substitute for any such existing *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]*.

This paragraph shall be without prejudice to the application of competition rules and, in particular, [[2014R0600_EN.101|Article 101]] and 102 TFEU.
## 3.
No *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(31)|CCP]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* or related entity may enter into an agreement with any provider of a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* the effect of which would be either:
### (a)
to prevent any other *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(31)|CCP]]* or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* from obtaining access to such information or rights as referred to in paragraph 1; or
### (b)
to prevent any other *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(31)|CCP]]* or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* from obtaining access to such a licence, as referred to in paragraph 1.
## 4.
ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards to specify:
### (a)
the information through licensing to be made available under paragraph 1(a) for the sole use of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(31)|CCP]]* or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]*;
### (b)
other conditions under which access is granted, including confidentiality of information provided;
### (c)
the standards guiding how a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]* may be proven to be new in accordance with paragraph 2(a) and (b).

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 3 July 2015.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
