> Level 2 reference(s): [[2017R0583_EN.8]], [[2017R0583_EN.12]], [[2017R0583_EN.7]]# Article 21 - Post-trade disclosure by investment firms in respect of bonds, structured finance products, emission allowances and derivatives


Post-trade disclosure by *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* in respect of bonds, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(28)|structured finance products]]*, emission allowances and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(29)|derivatives]]*
## 1.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|Investment firms]]* which, either on own account or on behalf of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|clients]]*, conclude transactions in bonds, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(28)|structured finance products]]* and emission allowances traded on a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(32a)|OTC derivatives]]* as referred to in [[2014R0600_EN.8a#2|Article 8a(2)]], shall make public the volume and price of those transactions and the time at which they were concluded. That information shall be made public through an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APA]]*.
## 2.
Each individual transaction shall be made public once through a single *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APA]]*.
## 3.
The information which is made public in accordance with paragraph 1 and the time-limits within which it is published shall comply with the requirements adopted pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.10|Article 10]], including the regulatory technical standards adopted pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.11#4|Article 11(4)]], points (a) and (b), and [[2014R0600_EN.11a#3|Article 11a(3)]], points (a) and (b).
## 4.
With regard to bonds, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(28)|structured finance products]]* and emission allowances traded on a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* may defer the publication of the price or volume on the same conditions as laid down in [[2014R0600_EN.11|Article 11]].
## 4a.
With regard to *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(32a)|OTC derivatives]]* as referred to in [[2014R0600_EN.8a#2|Article 8a(2)]], *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* may defer the publication of the price or volume on the same conditions as laid down in [[2014R0600_EN.11a|Article 11a]].
## 5.
ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards in such a way as to enable the publication of information required pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.27g|Article 27g]] to specify the following:
### (a)
the identifiers for the different types of transactions published in accordance with this Article, distinguishing between those determined by factors linked primarily to the valuation of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* and those determined by other factors;
### (b)
the application of the obligation under paragraph 1 to transactions involving the use of those *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* for collateral, lending or other purposes where the exchange of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* is determined by factors other than the current market valuation of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]*.

—————

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 3 July 2015.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
