# Article 16 - Obligations of competent authorities


The competent authorities shall check that *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(12)|systematic internalisers]]* comply with the conditions for order execution laid down in [[2014R0600_EN.15#1|Article 15(1)]] and with the conditions for price improvement laid down in [[2014R0600_EN.15#2|Article 15(2)]].
