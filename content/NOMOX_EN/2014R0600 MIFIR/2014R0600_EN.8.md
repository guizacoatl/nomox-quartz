> Level 2 reference(s): [[2017R0583_EN.5]], [[2017R0583_EN.2]]# Article 8 - Pre-trade transparency requirements for trading venues in respect of bonds, structured finance products and emission allowances


Pre-trade transparency requirements for *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]* in respect of bonds, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(28)|structured finance products]]* and emission allowances
## 1.
When applying a central limit order book or a periodic auction trading system, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operators]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* operating a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* shall make public current bid and offer prices and the depth of trading interests at those prices which are advertised through their systems in respect of bonds, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(28)|structured finance products]]* and emission allowances. Those *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operators]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* shall make that information available to the public on a continuous basis during normal trading hours.
## 2.
The transparency requirements referred to in paragraph 1 shall be calibrated for different types of trading systems.
