> Level 2 reference(s): [[2017R0587_EN.3]]# Article 3 - Pre-trade transparency requirements for trading venues in respect of shares, depositary receipts, ETFs, certificates and other similar financial instruments

## 1.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|Market operators]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* operating a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* shall make public current bid and offer prices and the depth of trading interests at those prices which are advertised through their systems for shares, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(25)|depositary receipts]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(27)|certificates]]* and other similar *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* traded on a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]*. That requirement shall also apply to *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(33)|actionable indication of interests]]*. *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|Market operators]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* operating a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* shall make that information available to the public on a continuous basis during normal trading hours.
## 2.
The transparency requirements referred to in paragraph 1 shall be calibrated for different types of trading systems including order-book, quote-driven, hybrid and periodic auction trading systems.
## 3.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|Market operators]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* operating a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* shall give access, on reasonable commercial terms and on a non-discriminatory basis, to the arrangements they employ for making public the information referred to in paragraph 1 to *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* which are obliged to publish their quotes in shares, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(25)|depositary receipts]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(27)|certificates]]* and other similar *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.14|Article 14]].
