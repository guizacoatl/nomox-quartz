# Article 10 - Post-trade transparency requirements for trading venues in respect of bonds, structured finance products, emission allowances and derivatives

## 1.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|Market operators]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* operating a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* shall make public the price, volume and time of transactions executed in respect of bonds, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(28)|structured finance products]]* and emission allowances traded on a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]*. Those requirements shall also apply to transactions executed in respect of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(32)|exchange-traded derivatives]]* and in respect of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(32a)|OTC derivatives]]* as referred to in [[2014R0600_EN.8a#2|Article 8a(2)]]. *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|Market operators]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* operating a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* shall make details of all such transactions public as close to real time as technically possible.
## 2.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|Market operators]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* operating a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* shall give access, on reasonable commercial terms and on a non-discriminatory basis, to their arrangements for making public the information pursuant to paragraph 1 of this Article to *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* which are obliged, pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.21|Article 21]], to publish the details of their transactions in bonds, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(28)|structured finance products]]*, emission allowances and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(32a)|OTC derivatives]]* as referred to in [[2014R0600_EN.8a#2|Article 8a(2)]].
## 3.
Information relating to a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(50)|package transaction]]* shall be made available in respect of each component as close to real time as technically possible, having regard to the need to allocate prices to particular *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]*, and shall include a flag to identify that the component belongs to a package.

Where a component of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(50)|package transaction]]* is eligible for deferred publication pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.11|Article 11]] or 11a, information on the component shall be made available after the period of deferral for the transaction has lapsed.
