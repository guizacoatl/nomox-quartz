# Article 2 - Definitions

## 1.
For the purposes of this Regulation, the following definitions apply:
## (1)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firm]]*’ means an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firm]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (2)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(2)|investment services and activities]]*’ means *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(2)|investment services and activities]]* defined in Article 4(1)(2) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (3)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(3)|ancillary services]]*’ means *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(3)|ancillary services]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(3) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (4)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(4)|execution of orders on behalf of clients]]*’ means execution on behalf of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|clients]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(5) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (5)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(5)|dealing on own account]]*’ means *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(5)|dealing on own account]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(6) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (6)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(6)|market maker]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(6)|market maker]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(7) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (7)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|client]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|client]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(9) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (8)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(8)|professional client]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(8)|professional client]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(10) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (8a)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(8a)|SME growth market]]*’ means an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(8a)|SME growth market]]* as defined in [[2014R0600_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (12), of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (9)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(15) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (10)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operator]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operator]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(18) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (11)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(11)|multilateral system]]*’ means any system or facility in which multiple third-party buying and selling trading interests in *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* are able to interact;
## (12)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(12)|systematic internaliser]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(12)|systematic internaliser]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(20) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (13)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(13)|regulated market]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(13)|regulated market]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(21) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (14)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(14)|multilateral trading facility]]*’ or ‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(14)|MTF]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(14)|multilateral trading facility]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(22) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (15)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(15)|organised trading facility]]*’ or ‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(15)|OTF]]*’ means an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(15)|organised trading facility]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(23) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (16)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(24) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (16a)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16a)|designated publishing entity]]*’ means an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firm]]* responsible for making transactions public through an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APA]]* in accordance with Article 20(1) and [[2014R0600_EN.21#1|Article 21(1)]];
## (17)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(17)|liquid market]]*’ means:
### (a)
for the purposes of Articles [[2014R0600_EN.9|9]], [[2014R0600_EN.11|11]] and [[2014R0600_EN.11a|11a]]:
#### (i)
as regards bonds, a market in which there are ready and willing buyers and sellers on a continuous basis, where the market is assessed according to the issuance size of the bond;
#### (ii)
as regards a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]* or a class of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]* other than those referred to in point (i), a market in which there are ready and willing buyers and sellers on a continuous basis, where the market is assessed in accordance with the following criteria, taking into consideration the specific market structures of the particular *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]* or of the particular class of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]*:
— the average frequency and size of transactions over a range of market conditions, having regard to the nature and life cycle of products within the class of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]*,
— the number and type of market participants, including the ratio of market participants to traded *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* in a particular product,
— the average size of spreads, where available,
— the issuance size, where appropriate;
### (b)
for the purposes of Articles [[2014R0600_EN.4|4]], [[2014R0600_EN.5|5]] and [[2014R0600_EN.14|14]], a market for a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]* that is traded daily, where the market is assessed according to the following criteria:
#### (i)
the market capitalisation of that *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]*;
#### (ii)
the average daily number of transactions in that *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]*;
#### (iii)
the average daily turnover for that *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]*;
## (18)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* as defined in point (26) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]] and, for the authorisation and supervision of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services providers]]*, ESMA, with the exception of those *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36)|approved reporting mechanisms]]* (*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36)|ARMs]]*) and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|approved publication arrangements]]* (*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APAs]]*) with a derogation in accordance with paragraph 3 of this Article;
## (19)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(19)|credit institution]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(19)|credit institution]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council;
## (20)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(20)|branch]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(20)|branch]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(30) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (21)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(21)|close links]]*’ means *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(21)|close links]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(35) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (22)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(22)|management body]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(22)|management body]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(36) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (22a)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(22a)|senior management]]*’ means *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(22a)|senior management]]* as defined in point (37) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
## (23)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(23)|structured deposit]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(23)|structured deposit]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(43) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (24)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(24)|transferable securities]]*’ means *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(24)|transferable securities]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(44) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (25)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(25)|depositary receipts]]*’ means *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(25)|depositary receipts]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(45) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (26)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|exchange-traded fund]]*’ or ‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETF]]*’ means an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|exchange-traded fund]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(46) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (27)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(27)|certificates]]*’ means those securities which are negotiable on the capital market and which in case of a repayment of investment by the issuer are ranked above shares but below unsecured bond instruments and other similar instruments;
## (28)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(28)|structured finance products]]*’ means those securities created to securitise and transfer credit risk associated with a pool of financial assets entitling the security holder to receive regular payments that depend on the cash flow from the underlying assets;
## (29)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(29)|derivatives]]*’ means those *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* defined in point (44)(c) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]]; and referred to in Annex I, Section C (4) to (10) thereto;
## (30)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(30)|commodity derivatives]]*’ means those *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* defined in point (44)(c) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]]; which relate to a commodity or an underlying referred to in Section C(10) of Annex I to Directive 2014/65/EU; or in points (5), (6), (7) and (10) of Section C of Annex I thereto;
## (31)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(31)|CCP]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(31)|CCP]]* within the meaning of [[2012R0648_EN.2#1|Article 2(1) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (32)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(32)|exchange-traded derivative]]*’ means a derivative that is traded on a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(13)|regulated market]]* or on a third-country market considered to be equivalent to a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(13)|regulated market]]* in accordance with [[2014R0600_EN.28|Article 28]] of this Regulation, and as such does not fall within the definition of an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(32a)|OTC derivative]]* as defined in [[2012R0648_EN.2#7|Article 2(7) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (32a)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(32a)|OTC derivative]]*’ means an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(32a)|OTC derivative]]* as defined in [[2014R0600_EN.2|Article 2]], point (7), of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012;
## (33)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(33)|actionable indication of interest]]*’ means a message from one member or participant to another within a trading system in relation to available trading interest that contains all necessary information to agree on a trade;
## (34)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|approved publication arrangement]]*’ or ‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APA]]*’ means a person authorised under this Regulation to provide the service of publishing trade reports on behalf of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* pursuant to Articles [[2014R0600_EN.20|20]] and [[2014R0600_EN.21|21]];
## (35)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|consolidated tape provider]]*’ or ‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]*’ means a person authorised in accordance with Title IVa, Chapter 1, of this Regulation to provide the service of collecting data from *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APAs]]*, and of consolidating those data into a continuous electronic live data stream providing *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36b)|core market data]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36c)|regulatory data]]*;
## (36)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36)|approved reporting mechanism]]*’ or ‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36)|ARM]]*’ means a person authorised under this Regulation to provide the service of reporting details of transactions to competent authorities or to ESMA on behalf of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]*;
## (36a)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]*’ means a person referred to in points (34) to (36) and a person referred to in [[2014R0600_EN.27b#2|Article 27b(2)]];
## (36b)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36b)|core market data]]*’ means:
### (a)
all of the following data on a given share or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETF]]* at any given timestamp:
#### (i)
for continuous order books, the European best bid and offer with the corresponding volume;
#### (ii)
for auction trading systems, the price at which the trading algorithm would be best satisfied and the volume potentially executed at that price by participants in that system;
#### (iii)
the transaction price and volume executed at that price;
#### (iv)
for transactions, the type of trading system and the applicable waivers and deferrals;
#### (v)
except for the information referred to in points (i) and (ii), the market identifier code uniquely identifying the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* and, for other execution venues, the identifier code identifying the type of execution venue;
#### (vi)
the standardised instrument identifier that applies across execution venues;
#### (vii)
the timestamp information on the following, as applicable:
— the execution of the transaction and any amendment thereto,
— the entry of the best bids and offers into the order book,
— the indication, in an auction trading system, of the prices or volumes,
— the publication by the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]* of the elements listed in the first, second and third indents,
— the dissemination of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36b)|core market data]]*;
### (b)
all of the following data on a given bond or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(32a)|OTC derivative]]* at any given timestamp:
#### (i)
the transaction price and quantity or size executed at that price;
#### (ii)
the market identifier code uniquely identifying the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* and, for other execution venues, the identifier code identifying the type of execution venue;
#### (iii)
for bonds, the standardised instrument identifier that applies across execution venues;
#### (iv)
for *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(32a)|OTC derivatives]]*, the identifying reference data as referred to in [[2014R0600_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]], second subparagraph;
#### (v)
the timestamp information on the following:
— the execution of the transaction and any amendment thereto,
— the publication of the transaction by the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]*,
— the dissemination of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36b)|core market data]]*;
#### (vi)
the type of trading system and the applicable waivers and deferrals;
## (36c)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36c)|regulatory data]]*’ means data related to the status of systems matching orders in *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* and data related to the trading status of individual *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]*;
## (37)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(37)|home Member State]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(37)|home Member State]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(55) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (38)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(38)|host Member State]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(38)|host Member State]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(56) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (39)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(39)|benchmark]]*’ means any rate, index or figure, made available to the public or published that is periodically or regularly determined by the application of a formula to, or on the basis of the value of one or more underlying assets or prices, including estimated prices, actual or estimated interest rates or other values, or surveys and by reference to which the amount payable under a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]* or the value of a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]* is determined.
## (40)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(40)|interoperability arrangement]]*’ means an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(40)|interoperability arrangement]]* as defined in [[2012R0648_EN.2#12|Article 2(12) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (41)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(41)|third-country financial institution]]*’ means an entity, the head office of which is established in a third country, that is authorised or licensed under the law of that third country to carry out any of the services or activities listed in Directive 2013/36/EU, Directive 2014/65/EU; Directive 2009/138/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council, Directive 2009/65/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council (
## 5
), Directive 2003/41/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council or Directive 2011/61/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council (
## 7
);
## (42)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(42)|third-country firm]]*’ means a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(42)|third-country firm]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(57) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (43)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(43)|wholesale energy product]]*’ means *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(43)|wholesale energy products]]* as defined in [[2011R1227_EN.2#4|Article 2(4) of Regulation (EU) No 1227/2011]] of the European Parliament and of the Council;
## (44)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(44)|agricultural commodity derivatives]]*’ means derivative contracts relating to products listed in Article 1 of, and Annex I, Parts I to XX and XXIV/1 to, Regulation (EU) No 1308/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council;
## (45)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(45)|liquidity fragmentation]]*’ means a situation in which:
### (a)
participants in a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* are unable to conclude a transaction with one or more other participants in that venue because of the absence of clearing arrangements to which all participants have access; or
### (b)
a clearing member or its *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|clients]]* would be forced to hold their positions in a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]* in more than one *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(31)|CCP]]* which would limit the potential for the netting of financial exposures;
## (46)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(46)|sovereign debt]]*’ means *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(46)|sovereign debt]]* as defined in Article 4(1)(61) of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (47)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(47)|portfolio compression]]*’ means a risk reduction service in which two or more counterparties wholly or partially terminate some or all of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(29)|derivatives]]* submitted by those counterparties for inclusion in the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(47)|portfolio compression]]* and replace the terminated *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(29)|derivatives]]* with another derivative whose combined notional value is less than the combined notional value of the terminated *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(29)|derivatives]]*;
## (48)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(48)|exchange for physical]]*’ means a transaction in a derivative contract or other *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]* contingent on the simultaneous execution of an equivalent quantity of an underlying physical asset;
## (49)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(49)|package order]]*’ means an order priced as a single unit:
### (a)
for the purpose of executing an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(48)|exchange for physical]]*; or
### (b)
in two or more *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* for the purpose of executing a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(50)|package transaction]]*;
## (50)
‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(50)|package transaction]]*’ means:
### (a)
an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(48)|exchange for physical]]*; or
### (b)
a transaction involving the execution of two or more component transactions in *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* and which fulfils all of the following criteria:
#### (i)
the transaction is executed between two or more counterparties;
#### (ii)
each component of the transaction bears meaningful economic or financial risk related to all the other components;
#### (iii)
the execution of each component is simultaneous and contingent upon the execution of all the other components.
## 2.
The Commission shall be empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2014R0600_EN.50|Article 50]] to specify certain technical elements of the definitions laid down in paragraph 1 to adjust them to market developments.
## 3.
The Commission is empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2014R0600_EN.50|Article 50]], specifying criteria to identify those *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36)|ARMs]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APAs]]* that, by way of derogation from this Regulation on account of their limited relevance for the internal market, are subject to authorisation and supervision by a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* of a Member State as defined in point (26) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]].

When adopting the delegated act, the Commission shall take into account one or more of the following elements:
### (a)
the extent to which the services are provided to *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* authorised in one Member State only;
### (b)
the number of trade reports or transactions;
### (c)
whether the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36)|ARM]]* or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APA]]* is part of a group of financial market participants operating cross border.

Where an entity is supervised by ESMA for any services provided in its capacity as a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]* under this Regulation, none of its activities as an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36)|ARM]]* or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APA]]* shall be excluded from ESMA supervision under any delegated act adopted pursuant to this paragraph.
