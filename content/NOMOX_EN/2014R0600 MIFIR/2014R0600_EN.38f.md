# Article 38f - Professional secrecy


The obligation of professional secrecy referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.76|Article 76 of Directive 2014/65/EU]] shall apply to ESMA and all persons who work or who have worked for ESMA or for any other person to whom ESMA has delegated tasks, including auditors and experts contracted by ESMA.
