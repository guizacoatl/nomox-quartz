# Article 27b - Requirement for authorisation

## 1.
The operation of an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APA]]*, a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* or an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36)|ARM]]* as a regular occupation or business shall be subject to prior authorisation by ESMA in accordance with this Title.

By way of derogation from the first subparagraph of this paragraph, an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APA]]* or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36)|ARM]]* identified in accordance with the delegated act referred to in [[2014R0600_EN.2#3|Article 2(3)]] shall be subject to prior authorisation and supervision by the relevant national *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* in accordance with this Title.
## 2.
An *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firm]]* or a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operator]]* operating a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* may also provide the services of an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APA]]*, a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* or an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36)|ARM]]*, subject to the prior verification by ESMA or the relevant national *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* that the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firm]]* or the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operator]]* complies with this Title. The provision of those services shall be included in their authorisation.
## 3.
ESMA shall establish a register of all *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services providers]]* in the Union. The register shall be publicly available and shall contain information on the services for which the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]* is authorised and it shall be updated on a regular basis.

Where ESMA, or a national *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* where relevant, has withdrawn an authorisation in accordance with [[2014R0600_EN.27e|Article 27e]], that withdrawal shall be published in the register for a period of five years.
## 4.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|Data reporting services providers]]* shall provide their services under the supervision of ESMA or the national *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* where relevant. ESMA, or the national *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* where relevant, shall regularly review the compliance of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services providers]]* with this Title. ESMA, or the national *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* where relevant, shall monitor that *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services providers]]* comply at all times with the conditions for initial authorisation established under this Title.
