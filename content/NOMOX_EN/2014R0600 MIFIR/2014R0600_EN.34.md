# Article 34 - Register of derivatives subject to the trading obligation


ESMA shall publish and maintain on its website a register specifying, in an exhaustive and unequivocal manner, the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(29)|derivatives]]* that are subject to the obligation to trade on the venues referred to in [[2014R0600_EN.28#1|Article 28(1)]], the venues where they are admitted to trading or traded, and the dates from which the obligation takes effect.
