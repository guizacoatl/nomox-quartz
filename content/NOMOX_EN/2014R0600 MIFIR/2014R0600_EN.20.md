> Level 2 reference(s): [[2017R0587_EN.15]], [[2017R0587_EN.12]], [[2017R0587_EN.13]]# Article 20 - Post-trade disclosure by investment firms, including systematic internalisers, in respect of shares, depositary receipts, ETFs, certificates and other similar financial instruments

## 1.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|Investment firms]]* which, either on own account or on behalf of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|clients]]*, conclude transactions in shares, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(25)|depositary receipts]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(27)|certificates]]* and other similar *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* traded on a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]*, shall make public the volume and price of those transactions and the time at which they were concluded. That information shall be made public through an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APA]]*.
## 1a.
Each individual transaction shall be made public once through a single *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APA]]*.
## 2.
The information which is made public in accordance with paragraph 1 of this Article and the time-limits within which it is published shall comply with the requirements adopted pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.6|Article 6]], including the regulatory technical standards adopted in accordance with [[2014R0600_EN.7#2#a|Article 7(2)(a)]]. Where the measures adopted pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.7|Article 7]] provide for deferred publication for certain categories of transaction in shares, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(25)|depositary receipts]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(27)|certificates]]* and other similar *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* traded on a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]*, that possibility shall also apply to those transactions when undertaken outside *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]*.
## 3.
ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards to specify the following:
### (a)
identifiers for the different types of transactions published under this Article, distinguishing between those determined by factors linked primarily to the valuation of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* and those determined by other factors;
### (b)
the application of the obligation under paragraph 1 to transactions involving the use of those *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* for collateral, lending or other purposes where the exchange of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* is determined by factors other than the current market valuation of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instrument]]*.

—————

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 3 July 2015.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
