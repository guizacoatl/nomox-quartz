# Article 27e - Withdrawal of authorisation

## 1.
ESMA, or the national *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* where relevant, may withdraw the authorisation of a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]* where the latter:
### (a)
does not make use of the authorisation within 12 months, expressly renounces the authorisation or has provided no services for the preceding six months;
### (b)
obtained the authorisation by making false statements or by any other irregular means;
### (c)
no longer meets the conditions under which it was authorised;
### (d)
has seriously and systematically infringed this Regulation.
## 2.
ESMA shall, where relevant, without undue delay, notify the national *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* in the Member State where the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]* is established of a decision to withdraw the authorisation of a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]*.
## 3.
A *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]* from which authorisation is to be withdrawn shall ensure orderly substitution, including the transfer of data to other *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services providers]]*, the provision of due notice to its *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(7)|clients]]* and the redirection of reporting flows to other *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services providers]]* before the withdrawal.
