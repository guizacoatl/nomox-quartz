# Article 8b - Pre-trade transparency requirements for trading venues in respect of package orders

## 1.
When applying a central limit order book or a periodic auction trading system, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operators]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* operating a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]* shall make public current bid and offer prices and the depth of trading interests at those prices which are advertised through their systems in respect of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(49)|package orders]]* composed of bonds, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(28)|structured finance products]]*, emission allowances or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(29)|derivatives]]*. Those *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operators]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* shall make that information available to the public on a continuous basis during normal trading hours.
## 2.
The transparency requirements referred to in paragraph 1 shall be calibrated for different types of trading systems.
