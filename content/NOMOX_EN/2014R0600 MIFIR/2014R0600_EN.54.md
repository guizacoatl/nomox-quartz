# Article 54 - Transitional provisions

## 1.
Third‐country firms may continue to provide services and activities in Member States, in accordance with national regimes, until three years after the adoption by the Commission of a decision in relation to the relevant third country in accordance with [[2014R0600_EN.47|Article 47]]. Services and activities not covered by such a decision may continue to be provided in accordance with national regime.

—————
## 3.
The provisions of the delegated acts adopted pursuant to Regulation (EU) No 600/2014 as applicable before 28 March 2024 shall continue to apply until the date of application of the delegated acts adopted pursuant to Regulation (EU) No 600/2014 as applicable from that date.
