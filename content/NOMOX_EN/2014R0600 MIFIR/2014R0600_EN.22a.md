# Article 22a - Transmission of data to the CTP

## 1.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|Trading venues]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APAs]]* (‘*[[2014R0600_EN.2#1.|data contributors]]*’) shall, with regard to shares, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(26)|ETFs]]* and bonds that are traded on a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venue]]*, and with regard to *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(32a)|OTC derivatives]]* as referred to in [[2014R0600_EN.8a#2|Article 8a(2)]], transmit to the data centre of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]*, as close to real time as technically possible, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36c)|regulatory data]]* and the data required pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]], without prejudice to [[2014R0600_EN.4|Article 4]], and pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.6#1|Article 6(1)]], Article 10(1) and Articles [[2014R0600_EN.20|20]] and [[2014R0600_EN.21|21]], and, where regulatory technical standards are adopted pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.22b#3|Article 22b(3)]], point (d), in accordance with the requirements specified therein. Those data shall be transmitted in a harmonised format, through a high-quality transmission protocol.
## 2.
An *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firm]]* operating an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(8a)|SME growth market]]*, or a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operator]]*, whose annual trading volume of shares represents 1 % or less of the annual trading volume of shares in the Union shall not be required to transmit its data to the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* where:
### (a)
that *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operator]]* is not a part of a group comprising or having *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(21)|close links]]* with an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firm]]* or a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operator]]* whose annual trading volume of shares represents more than 1 % of the annual trading volume of shares in the Union; or
### (b)
the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(13)|regulated market]]* or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(8a)|SME growth market]]* operated by that *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operator]]* accounts for more than 85 % of the annual trading volume of shares that were initially admitted to trading on that *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(13)|regulated market]]* or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(8a)|SME growth market]]*.
## 3.
Notwithstanding paragraph 2, an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firm]]* operating an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(8a)|SME growth market]]*, or a *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operator]]*, which meets the conditions laid down in that paragraph may decide to transmit data to the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* in accordance with paragraph 1, provided that it notifies ESMA and the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* accordingly. Such an *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operator]]* shall start transmitting data to the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* within 30 working days of the date of the notification to ESMA.
## 4.
ESMA shall publish on its website and keep up to date a list of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(1)|investment firms]]* operating *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(8a)|SME growth markets]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(10)|market operators]]* that meet the conditions laid down in paragraph 2, indicating which of them have decided to apply paragraph 3.
## 5.
Each *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* shall choose, from among the types of transmission protocols that the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#1.|data contributors]]* offer to other users, which transmission protocol is to be used for the direct transmission of the data referred to in paragraph 1 to the data centre of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]*.
## 6.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#1.|Data contributors]]* shall not receive any remuneration for transmitting the data referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article or the transmission protocol referred to in paragraph 5 of this Article, other than the revenue received pursuant to [[2014R0600_EN.27h#5|Article 27h(5)]], (6) and (7).
## 7.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#1.|Data contributors]]* shall, where applicable, apply the deferrals laid down in Articles [[2014R0600_EN.7|7]], [[2014R0600_EN.11|11]] and [[2014R0600_EN.11a|11a]], Article 20(2) and [[2014R0600_EN.21#4|Article 21(4)]] to the data to be transmitted to the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]*.
## 8.
Where the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTP]]* considers the quality of the data to be insufficient, it shall notify the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* of the data contributor accordingly. The *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* shall take the necessary measures in accordance with Article 38g of this Regulation and Articles [[2014R0600_EN.69|69]] and [[2014R0600_EN.70|70]] of Directive 2014/65/EU.
