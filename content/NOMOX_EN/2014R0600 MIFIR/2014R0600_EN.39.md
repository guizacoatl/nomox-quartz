# Article 39 - Market monitoring

## 1.
In accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.9#2|Article 9(2) of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]], ESMA shall monitor the market for *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* which are marketed, distributed or sold in the Union.
## 2.
In accordance with [[2010R1093_EN.9#2|Article 9(2) of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]], EBA shall monitor the market for *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(23)|structured deposits]]* which are marketed, distributed or sold in the Union.
## 3.
Competent authorities shall monitor the market for *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(23)|structured deposits]]* which are marketed, distributed or sold in or from their Member State.
