# Article 38n - Authorisation and supervisory fees

## 1.
ESMA shall charge fees to the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services providers]]* in accordance with this Regulation and in accordance with the delegated acts adopted pursuant to paragraph 3 of this Article. Those fees shall fully cover ESMA’s necessary expenditure relating to the authorisation and supervision of *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services providers]]* and the reimbursement of any costs that the competent authorities may incur carrying out work pursuant to this Regulation, in particular as a result of any delegation of tasks in accordance with [[2014R0600_EN.38o|Article 38o]].
## 2.
The amount of an individual fee charged to a particular *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]* shall cover all administrative costs incurred by ESMA for the authorisation and supervisory activities relating to that provider. It shall be proportionate to the turnover of the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]*.
## 3.
The Commission shall adopt a delegated act in accordance with [[2014R0600_EN.50|Article 50]] supplementing this Regulation by 1 October 2021 to specify the type of fees, the matters for which fees are due, the amount of the fees and the manner in which they are to be paid.
