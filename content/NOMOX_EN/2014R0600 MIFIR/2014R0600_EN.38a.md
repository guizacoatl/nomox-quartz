# Article 38a - Exercise of ESMA’s powers


The powers conferred on ESMA or any official of or other person authorised by ESMA by Articles [[2014R0600_EN.38b|38b]] to 38e shall not be used to require the disclosure of information or documents which are subject to legal privilege.
