# Article 22 - Providing information for the purposes of transparency and other calculations

## 1.
In order to carry out calculations for determining the requirements for the pre- and post-trade transparency and the trading obligation regimes referred to in Articles [[2014R0600_EN.3|3]] to 11a, 14 to 21 and 32 which are applicable to *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(9)|financial instruments]]*, and in order to prepare reports to the Commission in accordance with [[2014R0600_EN.4#4|Article 4(4)]], [[2014R0600_EN.7#1|Article 7(1)]], [[2014R0600_EN.9#2|Article 9(2)]], Article 11(3) and [[2014R0600_EN.11a#1|Article 11a(1)]], ESMA and competent authorities may require information from:
### (a)
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]*;
### (b)
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APAs]]*; and
### (c)
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTPs]]*.
## 2.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|Trading venues]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APAs]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTPs]]* shall store the necessary data for a sufficient period.
## 3.
ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards to specify the content and frequency of data requests and the formats and the timeframe in which *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APAs]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTPs]]* are to respond to data requests referred to in paragraph 1, the type of data that is to be stored, and the minimum period for which *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(16)|trading venues]]*, *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(34)|APAs]]* and *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(35)|CTPs]]* are to store data in order to be able to respond to data requests in accordance with paragraph 2.

Power is delegated to the Commission to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
