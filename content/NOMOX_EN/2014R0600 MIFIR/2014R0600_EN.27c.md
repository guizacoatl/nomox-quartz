# Article 27c - Authorisation of data reporting services providers

## 1.
*[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|Data reporting services providers]]* shall be authorised by ESMA, or the national *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* where relevant, for the purposes of this Title where:
### (a)
the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]* is a legal person established in the Union; and
### (b)
the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]* meets the requirements laid down in this Title.
## 2.
The authorisation referred to in paragraph 1 shall specify the data reporting service which the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]* is authorised to provide. Where an authorised *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]* seeks to extend its business to additional data reporting services, it shall submit a request to ESMA, or the national *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* where relevant, for extension of that authorisation.
## 3.
An authorised *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]* shall comply at all times with the conditions for authorisation referred to in this Title. An authorised *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]* shall, without undue delay, notify ESMA, or the national *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(18)|competent authority]]* where relevant, of any material changes to the conditions for authorisation.
## 4.
The authorisation referred to in paragraph 1 shall be effective and valid for the entire territory of the Union, and shall allow the *[[2014R0600_EN.2#(36a)|data reporting services provider]]* to provide the services for which it has been authorised throughout the Union.
