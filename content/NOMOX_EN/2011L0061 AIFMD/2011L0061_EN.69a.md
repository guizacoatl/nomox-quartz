# Article 69a - Assessment of the passport regime


Before the entry into force of the delegated acts referred to in [[2011L0061_EN.67#6|Article 67(6)]] pursuant to which the rules set out in Article 35 and Articles [[2011L0061_EN.37|37]] to 41 become applicable, the Commission shall submit a report to the European Parliament and to the Council, taking into account the result of an assessment of the passport regime provided in this Directive including the extension of that regime to non-EU AIFMs. That report shall be accompanied, where appropriate, by a legislative proposal.
