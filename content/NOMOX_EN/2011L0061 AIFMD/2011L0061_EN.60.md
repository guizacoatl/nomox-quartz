# Article 60 - Disclosure of derogations


Where a Member State makes use of a derogation or option provided by Articles [[2011L0061_EN.6|6]], [[2011L0061_EN.9|9]], [[2011L0061_EN.21|21]], [[2011L0061_EN.22|22]], [[2011L0061_EN.28|28]] and [[2011L0061_EN.43|43]] and [[2011L0061_EN.61#5|Article 61(5)]], it shall inform the Commission thereof as well as of any subsequent changes. The Commission shall make the information public on a web-site or by other easily accessible means.
