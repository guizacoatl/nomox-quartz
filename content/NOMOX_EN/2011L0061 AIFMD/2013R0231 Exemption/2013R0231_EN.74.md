> Level 1 reference(s): [[2011L0061_EN.19]]# Article 74 - Frequency of valuation of assets held by open-ended AIFs

## 1.
The valuation of financial instruments held by open-ended AIFs shall take place every time the net asset value per unit or share is calculated pursuant to [[2013R0231_EN.72#1|Article 72(1)]].
## 2.
The valuation of other assets held by open-ended AIFs shall take place at least once a year, and every time there is evidence that the last determined value is no longer fair or proper.
