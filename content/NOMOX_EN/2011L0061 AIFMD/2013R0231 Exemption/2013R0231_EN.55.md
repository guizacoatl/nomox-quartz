# Article 55 - Grandfathering clause


Articles [[2013R0231_EN.51|51]] to 54 shall apply in relation to new securitisations issued on or after 1 January 2011. Articles [[2013R0231_EN.51|51]] to 54 shall, after 31 December 2014, apply in relation to existing securitisations where new underlying exposures are added or substituted after that date.
