## 1.
Leverage shall be considered to be employed on a substantial basis for the purposes of [[2011L0061_EN.24#4|Article 24(4) of Directive 2011/61/EU]] when the exposure of an AIF as calculated according to the commitment method under [[2013R0231_EN.8|Article 8]] of this Regulation exceeds three times its net asset value.
## 2.
Where the requirements referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article are fulfilled, AIFMs shall provide information in accordance with [[2011L0061_EN.24#4|Article 24(4) of Directive 2011/61/EU]] to the competent authorities of their home Member States in accordance with the principles laid down in [[2013R0231_EN.110#3|Article 110(3)]] of this Regulation.
