> Level 1 reference(s): [[2011L0061_EN.20]]# Article 81 - Consent and notification of sub-delegation

## 1.
A subdelegation shall become effective where the AIFM demonstrates its consent to it in writing.

A general consent given in advance by the AIFM shall not be deemed consent in accordance with point (a) of [[2011L0061_EN.20#4|Article 20(4) of Directive 2011/61/EU]].
## 2.
Pursuant to point (b) of [[2011L0061_EN.20#4|Article 20(4) of Directive 2011/61/EU]], the notification shall contain details of the delegate, the name of the competent authority where the sub-delegate is authorised or registered, the delegated functions, the AIFs affected by the sub-delegation, a copy of the written consent by the AIFM and the intended effective date of the sub-delegation.
