> Level 1 reference(s): [[2011L0061_EN.12]], [[2011L0061_EN.18]]# Article 58 - Electronic data processing

## 1.
AIFMs shall make appropriate and sufficient arrangements for suitable electronic systems so as to permit the timely and proper recording of each portfolio transaction or subscription or, where relevant, redemption order.
## 2.
AIFMs shall ensure a high standard of security during the electronic data processing and integrity and confidentiality of the recorded information, as appropriate.
