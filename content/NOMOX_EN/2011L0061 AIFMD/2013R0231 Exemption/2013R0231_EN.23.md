# Article 23 - Fair treatment of investors in the AIF

## 1.
The AIFM shall ensure that its decision-making procedures and its organisational structure, referred to in [[2013R0231_EN.57|Article 57]], ensure fair treatment of investors.
## 2.
Any preferential treatment accorded by an AIFM to one or more investors shall not result in an overall material disadvantage to other investors.
