# Article 2 - With regard to the use made of the passport, the competent authorities shall provide the following information:

### (a)
the number of EU AIFMs authorised in accordance with [[2011L0061_EN.7|Article 7 of Directive 2011/61/EU]];
### (b)
the number of EU AIFMs that market units or shares of EU AIFs pursuant to [[2011L0061_EN.32|Article 32 of Directive 2011/61/EU]], the number of EU AIFs and compartments of AIFs marketed pursuant to [[2011L0061_EN.32|Article 32 of Directive 2011/61/EU]], and the breakdown by home and host Member State;
### (c)
the number of EU AIFMs that manage EU AIFs pursuant to [[2011L0061_EN.33|Article 33 of Directive 2011/61/EU]], the number of EU AIFs managed pursuant to [[2011L0061_EN.33|Article 33 of Directive 2011/61/EU]] and the breakdown by home and host Member State;
### (d)
the number of EU AIFMs that manage EU AIFs established in other Member States pursuant to [[2011L0061_EN.33|Article 33 of Directive 2011/61/EU]] by establishing a branch and the number of EU AIFM that manage directly EU AIFs established in other Member States pursuant to [[2011L0061_EN.33|Article 33 of Directive 2011/61/EU]].
