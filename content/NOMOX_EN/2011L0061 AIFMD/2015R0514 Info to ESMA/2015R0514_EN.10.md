# Article 10 - With regard to the management of EU AIFs by non-EU AIFMs in accordance with applicable national regimes, competent authorities shall provide the following information:

### (a)
the legal provisions under which such management is allowed, including a description of the specific conditions applicable;
### (b)
the number of non-EU AIFMs that manage EU AIFs in the jurisdiction of the competent authority and the number of EU AIFs managed;
### (c)
the number of requests for information addressed by the competent authority to non-EU AIFMs in relation to the management of EU AIFs.
