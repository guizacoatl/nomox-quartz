# Article 4 - Access to details of SFTs


A trade repository shall provide the entities listed in [[2015R2365_EN.12#2|Article 12(2) of Regulation (EU) 2365/2015]] with direct and immediate access, including where delegation under [[2010R1095_EN.28|Article 28 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] exists, to the details of SFTs in an electronic and machine-readable form in accordance with Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2019/357


For the purposes of the first subparagraph, a trade repository shall use an XML format and a template developed in accordance with the ISO 20022 methodology.
