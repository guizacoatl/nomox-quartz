# Article 5 - Registration of a trade repository

## 1.
A *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* shall register with *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* for the purposes of [[2015R2365_EN.4|Article 4]] under the conditions and the procedure set out in this Article.
## 2.
To be eligible to be registered under this Article, a *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* shall be a legal person *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(5)|established]]* in the Union, apply procedures to verify the completeness and correctness of the details reported to it under [[2015R2365_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], and meet the requirements laid down in Articles [[2015R2365_EN.78|78]], [[2015R2365_EN.79|79]] and [[2015R2365_EN.80|80]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012. For the purposes of this Article, references in Articles [[2015R2365_EN.78|78]] and [[2015R2365_EN.80|80]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 to [[2015R2365_EN.9|Article 9]] thereof shall be construed as references to [[2015R2365_EN.4|Article 4]] of this Regulation.
## 3.
The registration of a *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* shall be effective for the entire territory of the Union.
## 4.
A registered *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* shall comply at all times with the conditions for registration. A *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* shall, without undue delay, notify *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* of any material changes to the conditions for registration.
## 5.
A *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* shall submit to *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* either of the following:
### (a)
an application for registration;
### (b)
an application for an extension of registration for the purposes of [[2015R2365_EN.4|Article 4]] of this Regulation in the case of a *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* already registered under Title VI, Chapter 1 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012.
## 6.
*[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall assess whether the application is complete within 20 working days of receipt of the application.

Where the application is not complete, *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall set a deadline by which the *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* is to provide additional information.

After assessing an application as complete, *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall notify the *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* accordingly.
## 7.
In order to ensure consistent application of this Article, *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall develop draft regulatory technical standards specifying the details of all of the following:
### (a)
the procedures referred to in paragraph 2 of this Article and which are to be applied by trade repositories in order to verify the completeness and correctness of the details reported to them under [[2015R2365_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]];
### (b)
the application for registration referred to in point (a) of paragraph 5;
### (c)
a simplified application for an extension of registration referred to in point (b) of paragraph 5 in order to avoid duplicate requirements.

*[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 13 January 2017.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
## 8.
In order to ensure uniform conditions of application of paragraphs 1 and 2, *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall develop draft implementing technical standards specifying the format of both of the following:
### (a)
the application for registration referred to in point (a) of paragraph 5;
### (b)
the application for an extension of registration referred to in point (b) of paragraph 5.

With regard to point (b) of the first subparagraph, *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall develop a simplified format to avoid duplicate procedures.

*[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall submit those draft implementing technical standards to the Commission by 13 January 2017.

Power is conferred on the Commission to adopt the implementing technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
