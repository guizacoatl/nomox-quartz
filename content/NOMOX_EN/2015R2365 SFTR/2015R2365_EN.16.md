# Article 16 - Designation and powers of competent authorities

## 1.
For the purpose of this Regulation, competent authorities shall comprise the following:
### (a)
for financial *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(2)|counterparties]]*, competent authorities or national competent authorities within the meaning of Regulations (EU) No 648/2012, (EU) No 1024/2013 and (EU) No 909/2014 and of Directives 2003/41/EC, 2009/65/EC, 2011/61/EU, 2013/36/EU and 2014/65/EU, and the supervisory authorities within the meaning of Directive 2009/138/EC;
### (b)
for non-financial *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(2)|counterparties]]*, the competent authorities designated in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.10#5|Article 10(5) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
### (c)
for the purpose of Articles [[2015R2365_EN.13|13]] and [[2015R2365_EN.14|14]] of this Regulation, concerning UCITS management companies and UCITS investment companies, the competent authorities designated in accordance with Article 97 of Directive 2009/65/EC;
### (d)
for the purpose of Articles [[2015R2365_EN.13|13]] and [[2015R2365_EN.14|14]] of this Regulation, concerning AIFMs, the competent authorities designated in accordance with [[2011L0061_EN.44|Article 44 of Directive 2011/61/EU]].
## 2.
The competent authorities shall exercise the powers conferred on them by the provisions referred to in paragraph 1 and shall supervise compliance with the obligations laid down in this Regulation.
## 3.
The competent authorities referred to in points (c) and (d) of paragraph 1 of this Article shall monitor UCITS management companies, UCITS investment companies and AIFMs *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(5)|established]]* in their territories to verify that they do not use *[[2015R2365_EN.3#6.|SFTs]]* and *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(18)|total return swaps]]*, unless they comply with Articles [[2015R2365_EN.13|13]] and [[2015R2365_EN.14|14]].
