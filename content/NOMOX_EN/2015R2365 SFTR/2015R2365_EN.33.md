# Article 33 - Entry into force and application

## 1.
This Regulation shall enter into force on the twentieth day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union .
## 2.
This Regulation shall apply from 12 January 2016, with the exception of:
### (a)
[[2015R2365_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], which shall apply:
#### (i)
12 months after the date of entry into force of the delegated act adopted by the Commission pursuant to [[2015R2365_EN.4#9|Article 4(9)]] for financial *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(2)|counterparties]]* referred to in points (3)(a) and (b) of [[2015R2365_EN.3|Article 3]] and third-country entities referred to in point (3)(i) of [[2015R2365_EN.3|Article 3]] which would require authorisation or registration in accordance with the legislation referred to in points (3)(a) and (b) of [[2015R2365_EN.3|Article 3]] if they were *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(5)|established]]* in the Union;
#### (ii)
15 months after the date of entry into force of the delegated act adopted by the Commission pursuant to [[2015R2365_EN.4#9|Article 4(9)]] for financial *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(2)|counterparties]]* referred to in points (3)(g) and (h) of [[2015R2365_EN.3|Article 3]] and third-country entities referred to in point (3)(i) of [[2015R2365_EN.3|Article 3]] which would require authorisation or registration in accordance with the legislation referred to in points (3)(g) and (h) of [[2015R2365_EN.3|Article 3]] if they were *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(5)|established]]* in the Union;
#### (iii)
18 months after the date of entry into force of the delegated act adopted by the Commission pursuant to [[2015R2365_EN.4#9|Article 4(9)]] for financial *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(2)|counterparties]]* referred to in points (3)(c) to (f) of [[2015R2365_EN.3|Article 3]] and third-country entities referred to point (3)(i) of [[2015R2365_EN.3|Article 3]] which would require authorisation or registration in accordance with the legislation referred to in points (3)(c) to (f) of [[2015R2365_EN.3|Article 3]] if they were *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(5)|established]]* in the Union; and
#### (iv)
21 months after the date of entry into force of the delegated act adopted by the Commission pursuant to [[2015R2365_EN.4#9|Article 4(9)]] for non-financial *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(2)|counterparties]]*;
### (b)
[[2015R2365_EN.13|Article 13]], which shall apply from 13 January 2017;
### (c)
[[2015R2365_EN.14|Article 14]], which shall apply from 13 July 2017 in the case of collective investment undertakings subject to Directive 2009/65/EC or Directive 2011/61/EU that are constituted before 12 January 2016;
### (d)
[[2015R2365_EN.15|Article 15]], which shall apply from 13 July 2016, including for *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(15)|collateral arrangements]]* existing on that date.
