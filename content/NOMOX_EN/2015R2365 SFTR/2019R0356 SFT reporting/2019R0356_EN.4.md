# Article 4 - Reporting of reuse of collateral

## 1.
A counterparty receiving one or several financial instruments as collateral in a SFT shall specify the complete and accurate details of any reuse of each of those financial instruments in Fields 7, 8 and 9 of Table 4 of the Annex.
## 2.
A counterparty receiving cash as collateral in a SFT shall specify the complete and accurate details of all cash collateral reinvestment for each currency in Fields 11, 12 and 13 of Table 4 of the Annex.
