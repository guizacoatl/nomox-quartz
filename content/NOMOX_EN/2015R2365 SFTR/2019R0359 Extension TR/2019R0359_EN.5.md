# Article 5 - Organisational chart

## 1.
An application for registration as a trade repository shall contain the organisational chart detailing the organisational structure of the applicant, including that of any ancillary services.
## 2.
That chart shall include information about the identity of the person responsible for each significant role, including senior management and persons who direct the activities of any branches.
