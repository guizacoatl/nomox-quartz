# Article 10 - Withdrawal of registration

## 1.
Without prejudice to [[2012R0648_EN.73|Article 73 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall withdraw the registration of a *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* where the *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]*:
### (a)
expressly renounces the registration or has provided no services for the preceding six months;
### (b)
obtained the registration by making false statements or by other irregular means;
### (c)
no longer meets the conditions under which it was registered.
## 2.
*[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall, without undue delay, notify the relevant competent authority referred to in Article 6(1) of a decision to withdraw the registration of a *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]*.
## 3.
The competent authority of a Member State in which the *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* performs its services and activities and which considers that one of the conditions referred to in paragraph 1 has been met, may request *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* to examine whether the conditions for the withdrawal of registration of the *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* concerned are met. Where *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* decides not to withdraw the registration of the *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* concerned, it shall provide full reasons for its decision.
## 4.
The competent authority referred to in paragraph 3 of this Article shall be the authority designated under points (a) and (b) of [[2015R2365_EN.16#1|Article 16(1)]] of this Regulation.
