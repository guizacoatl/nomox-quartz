# Article 28 - Sanctions and other measures for the purpose of Articles 13 and 14


Sanctions and other measures *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(5)|established]]* in accordance with Directives 2009/65/EC and 2011/61/EU shall be applicable to infringements of Articles [[2015R2365_EN.13|13]] and [[2015R2365_EN.14|14]] of this Regulation.
