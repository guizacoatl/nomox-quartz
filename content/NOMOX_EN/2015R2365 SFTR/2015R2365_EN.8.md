# Article 8 - Notification of ESMA decisions relating to registration or extension of registration

## 1.
Where *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* adopts a decision as referred to in [[2015R2365_EN.7#1|Article 7(1)]] or withdraws the registration as referred to in [[2015R2365_EN.10#1|Article 10(1)]], it shall notify the *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* within five working days with a fully reasoned explanation for its decision.

*[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall, without undue delay, notify the competent authority as referred to in Article 6(1) of its decision.
## 2.
*[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall communicate any decision taken in accordance with paragraph 1 to the Commission.
## 3.
*[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall publish on its website a list of trade repositories registered in accordance with this Regulation. That list shall be updated within five working days of the adoption of a decision under paragraph 1.
