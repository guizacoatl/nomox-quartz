# Article 1 - Subject matter


This Regulation lays down rules on the transparency of *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(11)|securities financing transactions]]* (*[[2015R2365_EN.3#6.|SFTs]]*) and of *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(12)|reuse]]*.
