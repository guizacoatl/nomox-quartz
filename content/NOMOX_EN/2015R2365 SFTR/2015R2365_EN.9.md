# Article 9 - Powers of ESMA

## 1.
The powers conferred on *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* in accordance with Articles [[2015R2365_EN.61|61]] to 68, 73 and 74 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012, in conjunction with Annexes I and II thereto, shall also be exercised with respect to this Regulation. References to [[2015R2365_EN.81#1|Article 81(1)]] and (2) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 in Annex I to that Regulation shall be construed as references to [[2015R2365_EN.12#1|Article 12(1)]] and (2) of this Regulation respectively.
## 2.
The powers conferred on *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* or any official of or other person authorised by *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* by Articles [[2015R2365_EN.61|61]], [[2015R2365_EN.62|62]] and [[2015R2365_EN.63|63]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 shall not be used to require the disclosure of information or documents which are subject to legal privilege.
