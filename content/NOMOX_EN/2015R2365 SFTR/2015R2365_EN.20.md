# Article 20 - Indirect access to data between authorities


*[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* may conclude cooperation arrangements with relevant authorities of third countries that need to fulfil their respective responsibilities and mandates regarding mutual exchange of information on *[[2015R2365_EN.3#6.|SFTs]]* made available to *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* by Union trade repositories in accordance with [[2015R2365_EN.12#2|Article 12(2)]] and on *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(11)|SFT]]* data collected and maintained by third-country authorities, provided that guarantees of professional secrecy exist, including with regard to the protection of business secrets shared by the authorities with third parties.
