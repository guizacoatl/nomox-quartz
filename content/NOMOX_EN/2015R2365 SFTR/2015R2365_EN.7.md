# Article 7 - Examination of the application

## 1.
*[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* shall, within 40 working days of the notification referred to in [[2015R2365_EN.5#6|Article 5(6)]], examine the application for registration, or for an extension of registration, based on the compliance of the *[[2015R2365_EN.3#(1)|trade repository]]* with this Chapter and shall adopt a fully reasoned decision accepting or refusing registration or an extension of registration.
## 2.
A decision issued by *[[2015R2365_EN.3#5.|ESMA]]* pursuant to paragraph 1 shall take effect on the fifth working day following its adoption.
