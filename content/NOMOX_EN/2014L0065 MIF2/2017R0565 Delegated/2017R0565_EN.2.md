# Article 2 - Definitions


For the purposes of this Regulation, the following definitions shall apply:
## (1)
‘*[[2017R0565_EN.2#(1)|relevant person]]*’ in relation to an investment firm, means any of the following:
### (a)
a director, partner or equivalent, manager or tied agent of the firm;
### (b)
a director, partner or equivalent, or manager of any tied agent of the firm;
### (c)
an employee of the firm or of a tied agent of the firm, as well as any other natural person whose services are placed at the disposal and under the control of the firm or a tied agent of the firm and who is involved in the provision by the firm of investment services and activities;
### (d)
a natural person who is directly involved in the provision of services to the investment firm or to its tied agent under an *[[2017R0565_EN.2#(3)|outsourcing]]* arrangement for the purpose of the provision by the firm of investment services and activities;
## (2)
‘*[[2017R0565_EN.2#(2)|financial analyst]]*’ means a *[[2017R0565_EN.2#(1)|relevant person]]* who produces the substance of investment research;
## (3)
‘*[[2017R0565_EN.2#(3)|outsourcing]]*’ means an arrangement of any form between an investment firm and a service provider by which that service provider performs a process, a service or an activity which would otherwise be undertaken by the investment firm itself;
## (3a)
‘*[[2017R0565_EN.2#(3a)|person with whom a relevant person has a family relationship]]*’ means any of the following:
### (a)
the spouse of the *[[2017R0565_EN.2#(1)|relevant person]]* or any partner of that person considered by national law as equivalent to a spouse;
### (b)
a dependent child or stepchild of the *[[2017R0565_EN.2#(1)|relevant person]]*;
### (c)
any other relative of the *[[2017R0565_EN.2#(1)|relevant person]]* who has shared the same household as that person for at least one year on the date of the personal transaction concerned;
## (4)
‘*[[2017R0565_EN.2#(4)|securities financing transaction]]*’ means security financing transaction as defined in [[2015R2365_EN.3#11|Article 3(11) of Regulation (EU) 2365/2015]] of the European Parliament and of the Council;
## (5)
‘*[[2017R0565_EN.2#(5)|remuneration]]*’ means all forms of payments or financial or non-financial benefits provided directly or indirectly by firms to *[[2017R0565_EN.2#(1)|relevant persons]]* in the provision of investment or ancillary services to clients;
## (6)
‘*[[2017R0565_EN.2#(6)|commodity]]*’ means any goods of a fungible nature that are capable of being delivered, including metals and their ores and alloys, agricultural products, and energy such as electricity;
## (7)
‘*[[2017R0565_EN.2#(7)|sustainability preferences]]*’ means a client’*[[2017R0565_EN.2#1.|s or potential client]]*’s choice as to whether and, if so, to what extent, one or more of the following financial instruments shall be integrated into his or her investment:
### (a)
a financial instrument for which the client or potential client determines that a minimum proportion shall be invested in environmentally sustainable investments as defined in [[2017R0565_EN.2|Article 2]], point (1), of Regulation (EU) 2020/852 of the European Parliament and of the Council;
### (b)
a financial instrument for which the client or potential client determines that a minimum proportion shall be invested in sustainable investments as defined in [[2017R0565_EN.2|Article 2]], point (17), of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 of the European Parliament and of the Council;
### (c)
a financial instrument that considers principal adverse impacts on *[[2017R0565_EN.2#(8)|sustainability factors]]* where qualitative or quantitative elements demonstrating that consideration are determined by the client or potential client;
## (8)
‘*[[2017R0565_EN.2#(8)|sustainability factors]]*’ means *[[2017R0565_EN.2#(8)|sustainability factors]]* as defined in [[2017R0565_EN.2|Article 2]], point (24), of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088;
## (9)
‘*[[2017R0565_EN.2#(9)|sustainability risks]]*’ means *[[2017R0565_EN.2#(9)|sustainability risks]]* as defined in [[2017R0565_EN.2|Article 2]], point (22), of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088.
