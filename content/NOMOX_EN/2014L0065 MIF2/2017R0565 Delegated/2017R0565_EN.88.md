> Level 1 reference(s): [[2014L0065_EN.64#1]], [[2014L0065_EN.65#1]]# Article 88 - Unbundling and disaggregating market data

## 1.
APAs and CTPs shall make market data available without being bundled with other services.
## 2.
Prices for market data shall be charged on the basis of the level of market data disaggregation provided for in [[2014R0600_EN.12#1|Article 12(1) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]] as further specified in Articles of Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2017/572.
