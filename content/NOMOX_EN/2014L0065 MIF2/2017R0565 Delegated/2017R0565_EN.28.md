> Level 1 reference(s): [[2014L0065_EN.16#2]]# Article 28 - Scope of personal transactions

For the purposes of Article 29 and [[2017R0565_EN.37|Article 37]], a personal transaction shall be a trade in a financial instrument effected by or on behalf of a *[[2017R0565_EN.2#(1)|relevant person]]*, where at least one of the following criteria are met:
### (a)
the *[[2017R0565_EN.2#(1)|relevant person]]* is acting outside the scope of the activities he carries out in his professional capacity;
### (b)
the trade is carried out for the account of any of the following persons:
#### (i)
the *[[2017R0565_EN.2#(1)|relevant person]]*;
#### (ii)
any person with whom he has a family relationship, or with whom he has close links;
#### (iii)
a person in respect of whom the *[[2017R0565_EN.2#(1)|relevant person]]* has a direct or indirect material interest in the outcome of the trade, other than obtaining a fee or commission for the execution of the trade.
