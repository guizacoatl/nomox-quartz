# Article 1 - Subject-matter and scope

## 1.
Chapter II, and Sections 1 to 4, [[2017R0565_EN.64#4|Article 64(4)]], [[2017R0565_EN.65|Article 65]] and Sections 6 to 8 of Chapter III and, to the extent they relate to those provisions, Chapter I and Chapter VIII of this Regulation shall apply to management companies when providing services in accordance with Article 6(4) of Directive 2009/65/EC and [[2011L0061_EN.6#6|Article 6(6) of Directive 2011/61/EU]] of the European Parliament and of the Council.
## 2.
References to investment firms shall encompass credit institutions and references to financial instruments shall encompass structured deposits in relation to all the requirements referred to in [[2017R0565_EN.1#3|Article 1(3)]] and 1(4) of Directive 2014/65/EU and their implementing provisions as set out under this Regulation.
