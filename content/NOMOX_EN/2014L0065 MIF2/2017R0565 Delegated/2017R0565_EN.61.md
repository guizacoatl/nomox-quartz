# Article 61 - Reporting obligations in respect of eligible counterparties

The requirements applicable to reports for retail and professional clients under Articles [[2017R0565_EN.49|49]] and [[2017R0565_EN.59|59]] shall apply unless investment firms enter into agreements with eligible counterparties to determine content and timing of reporting.
