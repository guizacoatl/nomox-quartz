# Article 87 - Per user fees

## 1.
APAs and CTPs shall charge for the use of market data on the basis of the use made by individual end-users of the market data (‘*[[2017R0565_EN.2#1.|per user basis]]*’). APAs and CTPs shall have arrangements in place to ensure that each individual use of market data is charged only once.
## 2.
By way of derogation from paragraph 1, APAs and CTPs may decide not to make market data available on a *[[2017R0565_EN.2#1.|per user basis]]* where to charge on a *[[2017R0565_EN.2#1.|per user basis]]* is disproportionate to the cost of making market data available, having regard to the scale and scope of the market data.
## 3.
APAs or CTPs shall provide grounds for the refusal to make market data available on a *[[2017R0565_EN.2#1.|per user basis]]* and shall publish those grounds on their webpage.
