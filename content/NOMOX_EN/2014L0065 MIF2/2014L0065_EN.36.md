# Article 36 - Access to regulated markets

## 1.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* from other Member States which are authorised to execute *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* orders or to deal on own account have the right of membership or have access to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated markets]]* established in their territory by means of any of the following arrangements:
### (a)
directly, by setting up branches in the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member States]]*;
### (b)
by becoming remote members of or having remote access to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* without having to be established in the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*, where the trading procedures and systems of the market in question do not require a physical presence for conclusion of transactions on the market.
## 2.
Member States shall not impose any additional regulatory or administrative requirements, in respect of matters covered by this Directive, on *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* exercising the right conferred by paragraph 1.
