# Article 3 - Transitional provision


For the purposes of [[2022R0466_EN.1|Article 1]], ESMA shall perform the initial assessment of the derogation criteria listed in [[2022R0466_EN.2|Article 2]]. Such initial assessment shall be based on data related to the first 6 months of 2021.
