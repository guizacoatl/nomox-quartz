# Article 52 - Suspension and removal of financial instruments from trading on a regulated market

## 1.
Without prejudice to the right of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* under [[2014L0065_EN.69#2|Article 69(2)]] to demand suspension or removal of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* from trading, a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* may suspend or remove from trading a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* which no longer complies with the rules of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* unless such suspension or removal would be likely to cause significant damage to the investors’ interests or the orderly functioning of the market.
## 2.
Member States shall require that a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* that suspends or removes from trading a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* also suspends or removes the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* as referred to in points (4) to (10) of Section C of Annex I that relate or are referenced to that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* where necessary to support the objectives of the suspension or removal of the underlying *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]*. The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* shall make public its decision on the suspension or removal of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* and of any related derivative and communicate the relevant decisions to its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]*.

The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]*, in whose jurisdiction the suspension or removal originated, shall require that other *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated markets]]*, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTFs]]*, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTFs]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(20)|systematic internalisers]]*, which fall under its jurisdiction and trade the same *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* as referred to in points (4) to (10) of Section C of Annex I to this Directive that relate or are referenced to that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]*, also suspend or remove that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* from trading, where the suspension or removal is due to suspected market abuse, a take-over bid or the non-disclosure of inside information about the issuer or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* infringing Articles [[2014L0065_EN.7|7]] and [[2014L0065_EN.17|17]] of Regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 except where such suspension or removal could cause significant damage to the investors’ interests or the orderly functioning of the market.

Each notified *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* shall communicate its decision to ESMA and other competent authorities, including an explanation if the decision was not to suspend or remove from trading the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* as referred to in points (4) to (10) of Section C of Annex I that relate or are referenced to that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]*.

The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* shall immediately make public and communicate to ESMA and the competent authorities of the other Member States such a decision.

The notified competent authorities of the other Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated markets]]*, other *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTFs]]*, other *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTFs]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(20)|systematic internalisers]]*, which fall under their jurisdiction and trade the same *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* referred to in points (4) to (10) of Section C of Annex I to this Directive that relate or are referenced to that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]*, also suspend or remove that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* from trading, where the suspension or removal is due to suspected market abuse, a take-over bid or the non-disclosure of inside information about the issuer or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* infringing Articles [[2014L0065_EN.7|7]] and [[2014L0065_EN.17|17]] of Regulation (EU) No 596/2014 except where such suspension or removal could cause significant damage to the investors’ interests or the orderly functioning of the market.

This paragraph applies also when the suspension from trading of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* as referred to in points (4) to (10) of Section C of Annex I that relate or are referenced to that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* is lifted.

The notification procedure referred to in this paragraph shall also apply in the case where the decision to suspend or remove from trading a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* as referred to in points (4) to (10) of Section C of Annex I that relate or are referenced to that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* is taken by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* pursuant to points (m) and (n) of [[2014L0065_EN.69#2|Article 69(2)]].

In order to ensure that the obligation to suspend or remove from trading such *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* is applied proportionately, ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards to further specify the cases in which the connection between a derivative relating or referenced to a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* suspended or removed from trading and the original *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* implies that the derivative are also to be suspended or removed from trading, in order to achieve the objective of the suspension or removal of the underlying *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]*.

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 3 July 2015.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
## 3.
ESMA shall develop draft implementing technical standards to determine the format and timing of the communications and publications referred to in paragraph 2.

ESMA shall submit those draft implementing technical standards to the Commission by 3 January 2016.

Power is conferred on the Commission to adopt the implementing technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
## 4.
The Commission shall be empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.89|Article 89]] to specify the list of circumstances constituting significant damage to the investors’ interests and the orderly functioning of the market referred to in paragraphs 1 and 2.
