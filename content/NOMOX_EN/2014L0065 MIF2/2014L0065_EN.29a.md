# Article 29a - Services provided to professional clients

## 1.
The requirements laid down in point (c) of [[2014L0065_EN.24#4|Article 24(4)]] shall not apply to services provided to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(10)|professional clients]]* except for *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(4)|investment advice]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(8)|portfolio management]]*.
## 2.
The requirements laid down in the third subparagraph of [[2014L0065_EN.25#2|Article 25(2)]] and in [[2014L0065_EN.25#6|Article 25(6)]] shall not apply to services provided to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(10)|professional clients]]*, unless those *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* inform the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* either in *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(62a)|electronic format]]* or on paper that they wish to benefit from the rights provided for in those provisions.
## 3.
Member States shall ensure that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* keep a record of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* communications referred to in paragraph 2.
