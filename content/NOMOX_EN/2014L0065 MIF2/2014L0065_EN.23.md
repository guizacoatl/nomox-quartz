# Article 23 - Conflicts of interest

## 1.
Member States shall require *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* to take all appropriate steps to identify and to prevent or manage conflicts of interest between themselves, including their managers, employees and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]*, or any person directly or indirectly linked to them by control and their *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* or between one *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* and another that arise in the course of providing any investment and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]*, or combinations thereof, including those caused by the receipt of inducements from third parties or by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*’s own remuneration and other incentive structures.
## 2.
Where organisational or administrative arrangements made by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.16#3|Article 16(3)]] to prevent conflicts of interest from adversely affecting the interest of its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* are not sufficient to ensure, with reasonable confidence, that risks of damage to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* interests will be prevented, the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* shall clearly disclose to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* the general nature and/or sources of conflicts of interest and the steps taken to mitigate those risks before undertaking business on its behalf.
## 3.
The disclosure referred to in paragraph 2 shall:
### (a)
be made in a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(62)|durable medium]]*; and
### (b)
include sufficient detail, taking into account the nature of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]*, to enable that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* to take an informed decision with respect to the service in the context of which the conflict of interest arises.
## 4.
The Commission shall be empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.89|Article 89]] to:
### (a)
define the steps that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* might reasonably be expected to take to identify, prevent, manage and disclose conflicts of interest when providing various investment and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* and combinations thereof;
### (b)
establish appropriate criteria for determining the types of conflict of interest whose existence may damage the interests of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* or potential *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*.
