# Article 5 - Format of the publications and communications by competent authorities

## 1.
Competent authorities shall publish the decision referred to in point (c) of [[2017R1005_EN.1|Article 1]] on a website in the format set out in Table 3 of the Annex.
## 2.
Competent authorities shall communicate the decisions referred to in points (d) and (e) of [[2017R1005_EN.1|Article 1]] in a standard machine-readable format using the formats set out in Tables 3 and 4 of the Annex, respectively.
