# Article 3 - Format of the publication and communication by trading venue operators

## 1.
Trading venue operators shall publish the decisions referred to in point (a) of [[2017R1005_EN.1|Article 1]] on their websites in the format set out in Table 2 of the Annex.
## 2.
Trading venue operators shall communicate the decisions referred to in point (a) of [[2017R1005_EN.1|Article 1]] to the relevant competent authority in a standard machine readable format approved by that competent authority using the format set out in Table 2 of the Annex.
