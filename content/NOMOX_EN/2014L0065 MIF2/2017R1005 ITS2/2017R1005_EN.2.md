# Article 2 - Definition of the term ‘trading venue operator’


For the purposes of this Regulation, ‘trading venue operator’ means any of the following:
### (a)
a market operator operating a regulated market, an MTF or an OTF;
### (b)
an investment firm operating an MTF or an OTF.
