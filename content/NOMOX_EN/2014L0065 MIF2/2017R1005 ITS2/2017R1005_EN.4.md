# Article 4 - Timing of the publications and communications by trading venue operators

## 1.
Trading venue operators shall publish the decisions referred to in point (a) of [[2017R1005_EN.1|Article 1]] immediately.
## 2.
Trading venue operators shall not publish the decisions referred to in point (a) of [[2017R1005_EN.1|Article 1]] by other means prior to their publication in accordance with [[2017R1005_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]].
## 3.
Trading venue operators shall communicate the decisions referred to in point (a) of [[2017R1005_EN.1|Article 1]] to the relevant competent authority simultaneously with its publication or immediately thereafter.
