# Article 6 - Timing of the publications and communications by competent authorities

## 1.
Competent authorities shall publish the decision referred to in point (c) of [[2017R1005_EN.1|Article 1]] immediately.
## 2.
Competent authorities shall communicate the decision referred to in point (d) of [[2017R1005_EN.1|Article 1]] simultaneously with its publication or immediately thereafter.
## 3.
A notified competent authority shall communicate the decision referred to in point (e) of [[2017R1005_EN.1|Article 1]] without undue delay upon receipt of the communication referred to in point (d) of [[2017R1005_EN.1|Article 1]].
