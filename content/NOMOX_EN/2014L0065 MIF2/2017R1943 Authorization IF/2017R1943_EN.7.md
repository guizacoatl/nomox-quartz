# Article 7 - General requirements

## 1.
The information to be provided to the competent authority of the home Member State, as set out in Articles [[2017R1943_EN.1|1]] and [[2017R1943_EN.6|6]], shall refer to both the head office of the firm and its branches and tied agents.
## 2.
The information to be provided to the competent authority of the home Member State, as set out in Articles [[2017R1943_EN.2|2]] to 5, shall refer to the head office of the firm.
