# Article 19 - Specific requirements for MTFs

## 1.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]*, in addition to meeting the requirements laid down in Articles [[2014L0065_EN.16|16]] and [[2014L0065_EN.18|18]], shall establish and implement non-discretionary rules for the execution of orders in the system.
## 2.
Member States shall require that the rules referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.18#3|Article 18(3)]] governing access to an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* comply with the conditions established in [[2014L0065_EN.53#3|Article 53(3)]].
## 3.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* to have arrangements:
### (a)
to be adequately equipped to manage the risks to which it is exposed, to implement appropriate arrangements and systems to identify all significant risks to its operation, and to put in place effective measures to mitigate those risks;
### (b)
to have effective arrangements to facilitate the efficient and timely finalisation of the transactions executed under its systems; and
### (c)
to have available, at the time of authorisation and on an ongoing basis, sufficient financial resources to facilitate its orderly functioning, having regard to the nature and extent of the transactions concluded on the market and the range and degree of the risks to which it is exposed.
## 4.
Member States shall ensure that Articles [[2014L0065_EN.24|24]] and [[2014L0065_EN.25|25]], [[2014L0065_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]], (2) and (4) to (10) and [[2014L0065_EN.28|Article 28]] are not applicable to the transactions concluded under the rules governing an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* between its members or participants or between the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* and its members or participants in relation to the use of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]*. However, the members of or participants in the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* shall comply with the obligations provided for in Articles [[2014L0065_EN.24|24]], [[2014L0065_EN.25|25]], [[2014L0065_EN.27|27]] and [[2014L0065_EN.28|28]] with respect to their *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* when, acting on behalf of their *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]*, they execute their orders through the systems of an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]*.
## 5.
Member States shall not allow *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* to execute *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* orders against proprietary capital, or to engage in *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(38)|matched principal trading]]*.
