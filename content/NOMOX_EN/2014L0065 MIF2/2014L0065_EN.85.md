# Article 85 - Powers for host Member States

## 1.
*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|Host Member States]]* shall provide that the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* may, for statistical purposes, require all *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* with branches within their territories to report to them periodically on the activities of those branches.
## 2.
In discharging their responsibilities under this Directive, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member States]]* shall provide that the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* may require branches of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* to provide the information necessary for the monitoring of their compliance with the standards set by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* that apply to them for the cases provided for in [[2014L0065_EN.35#8|Article 35(8)]]. Those requirements may not be more stringent than those which the same Member State imposes on established firms for the monitoring of their compliance with the same standards.
