# Article 26 - Provision of services through the medium of another investment firm


Member States shall allow an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* receiving an instruction to provide investment or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* on behalf of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* through the medium of another *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* to rely on *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* information transmitted by the latter *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*. The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* which mediates the instructions will remain responsible for the completeness and accuracy of the information transmitted.

The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* which receives an instruction to undertake services on behalf of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* in that way shall also be able to rely on any recommendations in respect of the service or transaction that have been provided to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* by another *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*. The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* which mediates the instructions will remain responsible for the suitability for the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* of the recommendations or advice provided.

The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* which receives *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* instructions or orders through the medium of another *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* shall remain responsible for concluding the service or transaction, based on any such information or recommendations, in accordance with the relevant provisions of this Title.
