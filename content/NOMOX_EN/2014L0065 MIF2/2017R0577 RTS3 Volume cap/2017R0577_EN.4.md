# Article 4 - Format of the data requests


Trading venues, APAs and CTPs shall submit the data referred to in [[2017R0577_EN.2|Article 2]] in a common XML format and, where available, in compliance with any other specifications in terms of content and format defined to facilitate an efficient and automated process of data delivery as well as its consolidation with similar data from other sources.
