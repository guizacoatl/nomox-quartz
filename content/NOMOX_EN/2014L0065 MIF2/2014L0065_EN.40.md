# Article 40 - Obligation to provide information


A *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(57)|third-country firm]]* intending to obtain authorisation for the provision of any investment services or the performance of investment activities with or without any *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* in the territory of a Member State through a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* shall provide the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of that Member State with the following:
### (a)
the name of the authority responsible for its supervision in the third country concerned. When more than one authority is responsible for supervision, the details of the respective areas of competence shall be provided;
### (b)
all relevant details of the firm (name, legal form, registered office and address, members of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(36)|management body]]*, relevant shareholders) and a programme of operations setting out the investment services and/or activities as well as the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* to be provided and the organisational structure of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]*, including a description of any outsourcing to third parties of essential operating functions;
### (c)
the name of the persons responsible for the management of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* and the relevant documents to demonstrate compliance with requirements laid down in [[2014L0065_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]];
### (d)
information about the initial capital at free disposal of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]*.
