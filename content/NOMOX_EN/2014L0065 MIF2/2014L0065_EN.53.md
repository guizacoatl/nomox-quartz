# Article 53 - Access to a regulated market

## 1.
Member States shall require a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* to establish, implement and maintain transparent and non-discriminatory rules, based on objective criteria, governing access to or membership of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*.
## 2.
The rules referred to in paragraph 1 shall specify any obligations for the members or participants arising from:
### (a)
the constitution and administration of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*;
### (b)
rules relating to transactions on the market;
### (c)
professional standards imposed on the staff of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institutions]]* that are operating on the market;
### (d)
the conditions established, for members or participants other than *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institutions]]*, under paragraph 3;
### (e)
the rules and procedures for the clearing and settlement of transactions concluded on the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*.
## 3.
*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|Regulated markets]]* may admit as members or participants *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]*, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institutions]]* authorised under Directive 2013/36/EU and other persons who:
### (a)
are of sufficient good repute;
### (b)
have a sufficient level of trading ability, competence and experience;
### (c)
have, where applicable, adequate organisational arrangements;
### (d)
have sufficient resources for the role they are to perform, taking into account the different financial arrangements that the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* may have established in order to guarantee the adequate settlement of transactions.
## 4.
Member States shall ensure that, for the transactions concluded on a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*, members and participants are not obliged to apply to each other the obligations laid down in Articles [[2014L0065_EN.24|24]], [[2014L0065_EN.25|25]], [[2014L0065_EN.27|27]] and [[2014L0065_EN.28|28]]. However, the members or participants of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* shall apply the obligations provided for in Articles [[2014L0065_EN.24|24]], [[2014L0065_EN.25|25]], [[2014L0065_EN.27|27]] and [[2014L0065_EN.28|28]] with respect to their *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* when they, acting on behalf of their *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]*, execute their orders on a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*.
## 5.
Member States shall ensure that the rules on access to or membership of or participation in the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* provide for the direct or remote participation of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institutions]]*.
## 6.
Member States shall, without further legal or administrative requirements, allow *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated markets]]* from other Member States to provide appropriate arrangements on their territory so as to facilitate access to and trading on those markets by remote members or participants established in their territory.

The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* shall communicate to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* the Member State in which it intends to provide such arrangements. The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* shall communicate that information to the Member State in which the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* intends to provide such arrangements within 1 month. ESMA may request access to that information in accordance with the procedure and under the conditions set out in [[2010R1095_EN.35|Article 35 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].

The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* shall, on the request of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* and, without undue delay, communicate the identity of the members or participants of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* established in that Member State.
## 7.
Member States shall require the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* to communicate, on a regular basis, the list of the members or participants of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*.
