# Article 15 - Initial capital endowment

Member States shall ensure that the competent authorities do not grant authorisation unless the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* has sufficient initial capital in accordance with the requirements of [[2019L2034_EN.9|Article 9 of Directive (EU) 2034/2019]] of the European Parliament and of the Council, having regard to the nature of the investment service or activity in question.
