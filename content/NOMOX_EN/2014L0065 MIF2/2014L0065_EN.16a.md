# Article 16a - Exemptions from product governance requirements


An *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* shall be exempted from the requirements set out in the second to fifth subparagraphs of [[2014L0065_EN.16#3|Article 16(3)]] and in [[2014L0065_EN.24#2|Article 24(2)]], where the investment service it provides relates to bonds with no other embedded derivative than a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(44a)|make-whole clause]]* or where the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* are marketed or distributed exclusively to eligible counterparties.
