# Article 1 - Most relevant market in terms of liquidity

For the purposes of this Regulation, the most relevant market in terms of liquidity for a share or a depositary receipt shall be considered to be the most relevant market in terms of liquidity as referred to in [[2014R0600_EN.4#1#a|Article 4(1)(a) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]] and specified in Article 4 of Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2017/587.
