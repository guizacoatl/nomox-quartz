# Article 16 - Assessment of factors


Competent authorities shall set the spot month and *[[2022R1302_EN.2#(4)|other months]]*’ position limits for an agricultural commodity derivative or a critical or significant commodity derivative by taking the baseline figure determined in accordance with Articles [[2022R1302_EN.11|11]], [[2022R1302_EN.13|13]] and [[2022R1302_EN.15|15]] and adjusting it according to the potential impact of the factors referred to in Articles [[2022R1302_EN.18|18]] to 21 on the integrity of the market for that derivative and for its underlying commodity to either of the following limits:
### (a)
between 5 % and 35 %;
### (b)
between 2,5 % and 35 %, for any derivative contract with an underlying that qualifies as food intended for human consumption with a total combined open interest in spot month and *[[2022R1302_EN.2#(4)|other months]]*’ contracts exceeding 50 000 *[[2022R1302_EN.2#(5)|lots]]* over a consecutive 3-month period.
