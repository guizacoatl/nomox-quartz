# Article 5 - Significant volumes

## 1.
An agricultural commodity derivative shall be considered to be traded in a significant volume on a trading venue when the trading in the agricultural commodity derivative on that trading venue over a consecutive three month period exceeds an average daily open interest of 20 000 *[[2022R1302_EN.2#(5)|lots]]* in the spot month and *[[2022R1302_EN.2#(4)|other months]]* combined.
## 2.
The trading venue where the largest volume of trading in commodity derivatives based on the same underlying and sharing the same characteristics takes place shall be the trading venue that over 1 year has the largest average daily open interest.
