# Article 6 - OTC contracts economically equivalent to commodity derivatives traded on trading venues


An OTC derivative shall be considered economically equivalent to a commodity derivative traded on a trading venue where it has identical contractual specifications, terms and conditions, excluding different *[[2022R1302_EN.2#(5)|lot]]* size specifications, delivery dates diverging by less than 1 calendar day and different post trade risk management arrangements.
