# Article 17 - New and less liquid agricultural commodity derivatives

## 1.
By way of derogation from [[2022R1302_EN.16|Article 16]], for agricultural commodity derivatives traded on a trading venue with a total combined open interest in spot month and *[[2022R1302_EN.2#(4)|other months]]*’ contracts not exceeding 20 000 *[[2022R1302_EN.2#(5)|lots]]* over a consecutive 3-month period, competent authorities shall set the spot month and *[[2022R1302_EN.2#(4)|other months]]*’ limit of positions held in those commodity derivatives at 10 000 *[[2022R1302_EN.2#(5)|lots]]*.
## 2.
The trading venue shall notify the competent authority when the total open interest of any commodity derivative referred to in paragraph 1 reaches 20 000 *[[2022R1302_EN.2#(5)|lots]]* over a consecutive 3-month period. Competent authorities shall review the position limit upon receiving such notifications.
