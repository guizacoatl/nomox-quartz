# Article 10 - Designation to identify an applicable waiver


Transaction reports shall identify the applicable waiver pursuant to [[2017R0590_EN.4|Article 4]] or [[2014R0600_EN.9|Article 9 of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]] under which the executed transaction has taken place in accordance with field 61 of Table 2 of Annex I to this Regulation.
