# Article 3 - Meaning of execution of a transaction

## 1.
An investment firm shall be deemed to have executed a transaction within the meaning of [[2017R0590_EN.2|Article 2]], where it provides any of the following services or performs any of the following activities that result in a transaction:
### (a)
reception and transmission of orders in relation to one or more financial instruments;
### (b)
execution of orders on behalf of clients;
### (c)
dealing on own account;
### (d)
making an investment decision in accordance with a discretionary mandate given by a client;
### (e)
transfer of financial instruments to or from accounts.
## 2.
An investment firm shall not be deemed to have executed a transaction where it has transmitted an order in accordance with [[2017R0590_EN.4|Article 4]].
