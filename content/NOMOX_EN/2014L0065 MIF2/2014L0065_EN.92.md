# Article 92 - Amendments to Directive 2011/61/EU


Directive 2011/61/EU is amended as follows:
## (1)
in point (r) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], the following point is added:
a Member State, other than the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]*, in which an EU AIFM provides the services referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.6#4|Article 6(4)]];’;
## (2)
[[2014L0065_EN.33|Article 33]] is amended as follows:
### (a)
the title is replaced by the following:

‘

Conditions for managing EU AIFs established in other Member States and for providing services in other Member States

’;
### (b)
paragraphs 1 and 2 are replaced by the following:

‘1.

Member States shall ensure that an authorised EU AIFM may, directly or by establishing a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]*:
### (a)
manage EU AIFs established in another Member State, provided that the AIFM is authorised to manage that type of AIF;
### (b)
provide in another Member State the services referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.6#4|Article 6(4)]] for which it has been authorised.
## 2.
An AIFM intending to provide the activities and services referred to in paragraph 1 for the first time shall communicate the following information to the competent authorities of its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]*:
### (a)
the Member State in which it intends to manage AIFs directly or to establish a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]*, and/or to provide the services referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.6#4|Article 6(4)]];
### (b)
a programme of operations stating in particular the services which it intends to perform and/or identifying the AIFs that it intends to manage.’.
