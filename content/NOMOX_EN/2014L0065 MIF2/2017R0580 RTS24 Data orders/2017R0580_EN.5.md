# Article 5 - Validity period and order restrictions

## 1.
Operators of trading venues shall keep a record of the validity periods and order restrictions that are listed in fields 10 and 11 of Table 2 of the Annex.
## 2.
Records of the dates and times in respect of validity periods shall be maintained as specified in field 12 of Table 2 of the Annex for each validity period.
