# Article 13 - Trading phases and indicative auction price and volume

## 1.
Operators of trading venues shall maintain a record of the order details as specified in Section K of Table 2 of the Annex.
## 2.
Where competent authorities request details referred to in Section K pursuant to [[2017R0580_EN.1|Article 1]], the details referred to in fields 9 and 15 to 18 of Table 2 of the Annex shall also be considered as details pertaining to the order concerned by that request.
