# Article 4 - Format


Investment firms shall publish the information required in accordance with [[2017R0576_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]] and 3(2) on their websites, by filling in the templates set out in Annex II, in a machine-readable electronic format, available for downloading by the public and the information required in accordance with [[2017R0576_EN.3#3|Article 3(3)]] shall be published on their websites in an electronic format available for downloading by the public.
