> Level 1 reference(s): [[2014R0600_EN.22#1]]# Article 18 - Reference to competent authorities

The competent authority for a specific financial instrument responsible for performing the calculations and ensuring the publication of the information referred to in Articles [[2017R0587_EN.4|4]], [[2017R0587_EN.7|7]], [[2017R0587_EN.11|11]] and [[2017R0587_EN.17|17]] shall be the competent authority of the most relevant market in terms of liquidity in [[2014R0600_EN.26|Article 26 of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]] and specified in Article 16 of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2017/590.
