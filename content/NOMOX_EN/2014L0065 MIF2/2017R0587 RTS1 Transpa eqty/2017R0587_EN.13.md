# Article 13 - Application of post-trade transparency to certain types of transactions executed outside a trading venue

The obligation in [[2014R0600_EN.20#1|Article 20(1) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]] shall not apply to the following:
### (a)
excluded transactions listed under Article 2(5) of Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2017/590 where applicable.
