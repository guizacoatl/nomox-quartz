# Article 8 - Reports by external auditors


Member States shall require investment firms to ensure that their external auditors report at least annually to the competent authority of the home Member State of the firm on the adequacy of the firm's arrangements under [[2017L0593_EN.16#8|Article 16(8)]], (9) and (10) of Directive 2014/65/EU and this Chapter.
