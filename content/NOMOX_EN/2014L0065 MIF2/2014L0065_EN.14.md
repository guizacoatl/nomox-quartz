# Article 14 - Membership of an authorised investor compensation scheme


The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* shall verify that any entity seeking authorisation as an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* meets its obligations under Directive 97/9/EC at the time of authorisation.

The obligation laid down in the first paragraph shall be met in relation to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(43)|structured deposits]]* where the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(43)|structured deposit]]* is issued by a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]* which is a member of a deposit guarantee scheme recognised under Directive 2014/49/EU.
