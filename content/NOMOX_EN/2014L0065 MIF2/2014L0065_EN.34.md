# Article 34 - Freedom to provide investment services and activities

## 1.
Member States shall ensure that any *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* authorised and supervised by the competent authorities of another Member State in accordance with this Directive, and in respect of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institutions]]* in accordance with Directive 2013/36/EU, may freely provide investment services and/or perform investment activities as well as *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* within their territories, provided that such services and activities are covered by its authorisation. *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|Ancillary services]]* may only be provided together with an investment service and/or activity.

Member States shall not impose any additional requirements on such an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]* in respect of the matters covered by this Directive.
## 2.
Any *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* wishing to provide services or activities within the territory of another Member State for the first time, or which wishes to change the range of services or activities so provided, shall communicate the following information to the competent authorities of its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]*:
### (a)
the Member State in which it intends to operate;
### (b)
a programme of operations stating in particular the investment services and/or activities as well as *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* which it intends to provide in the territory of that Member State and whether it intends to do so through the use of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]*, established in its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]*. Where an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* intends to use *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]*, the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* shall communicate to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* the identity of those *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]*.

Where an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* intends to use *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* established in its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]*, in the territory of the Member States in which it intends to provide services the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* shall, within one month from receipt of all the information, communicate to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* designated as contact point in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.79#1|Article 79(1)]] the identity of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* that the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* intends to use to provide *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(2)|investment services and activities]]* in that Member State. The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* shall publish such information. ESMA may request access to that information in accordance with the procedure and under the conditions set out in [[2010R1095_EN.35|Article 35 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
## 3.
The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* shall, within one month of receiving the information, forward it to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* designated as contact point in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.79#1|Article 79(1)]]. The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* may then start to provide the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(2)|investment services and activities]]* concerned in the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]*.
## 4.
In the event of a change in any of the particulars communicated in accordance with paragraph 2, an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* shall give written notice of that change to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* at least one month before implementing the change. The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* shall inform the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* of that change.
## 5.
Any *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]* wishing to provide investment services or activities as well as *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* in accordance with paragraph 1 through *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* shall communicate to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* the identity of those *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]*.

Where the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]* intends to use *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* established in its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* in the territory of the Member States in which it intends to provide services, the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]* shall, within one month from the receipt of all the information, communicate to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* designated as contact point in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.79#1|Article 79(1)]] the identity of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* that the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]* intends to use to provide services in that Member State. The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* shall publish such information.
## 6.
Member States shall, without further legal or administrative requirement, allow *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]* operating *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTFs]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTFs]]* from other Member States to provide appropriate arrangements on their territory so as to facilitate access to and trading on those markets by remote users, members or participants established in their territory.
## 7.
The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* shall communicate to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* the Member State in which it intends to provide such arrangements. The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* shall communicate, within one month, that information to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the Member State in which the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* intends to provide such arrangements.

The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* shall, on the request of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* and without undue delay, communicate the identity of the remote members or participants of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* established in that Member State.
## 8.
ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards to specify the information to be notified in accordance with paragraphs 2, 4, 5 and 7.

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 3 July 2015.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
## 9.
ESMA shall develop draft implementing technical standards to establish standard forms, templates and procedures for the transmission of information in accordance with paragraphs 2, 3, 4, 5 and 7.

ESMA shall submit those draft implementing technical standards to the Commission by 31 December 2016.

Power is conferred on the Commission to adopt the implementing technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
