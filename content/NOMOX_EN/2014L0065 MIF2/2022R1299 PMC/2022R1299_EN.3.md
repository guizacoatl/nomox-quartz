# Article 3 - Review and reporting of accountability levels

## 1.
Trading venues shall, on an annual basis, evaluate the adequacy and effectiveness of the accountability levels established pursuant to [[2022R1299_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]].
## 2.
A trading venue shall communicate to its competent authority the methodology used for setting the accountability levels referred to in [[2022R1299_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]].
## 3.
A trading venue shall inform its competent authority on an annual basis of the number of instances where accountability levels have been exceeded, of any requests for additional information made in accordance with [[2022R1299_EN.2#3|Article 2(3)]] and of any actions taken in accordance with [[2022R1299_EN.2#4|Article 2(4)]].
