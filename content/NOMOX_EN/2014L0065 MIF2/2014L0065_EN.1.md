# Article 1 - Scope

## 1.
This Directive shall apply to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]*, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]*, and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(57)|third-country firms]]* providing investment services or performing investment activities through the establishment of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* in the Union.
## 2.
This Directive establishes requirements in relation to the following:
### (a)
authorisation and operating conditions for *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]*;
### (b)
provision of investment services or activities by *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(57)|third-country firms]]* through the establishment of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]*;
### (c)
authorisation and operation of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated markets]]*; and

—————
### (e)
supervision, cooperation and enforcement by competent authorities.
## 3.
The following provisions shall also apply to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institutions]]* authorised under Directive 2013/36/EU, when providing one or more investment services and/or performing investment activities:
### (a)
[[2014L0065_EN.2#2|Article 2(2)]], Article 9(3) and Articles [[2014L0065_EN.14|14]] and [[2014L0065_EN.16|16]] to 20,
### (b)
Chapter II of Title II excluding second subparagraph of [[2014L0065_EN.29#2|Article 29(2)]],
### (c)
Chapter III of Title II excluding [[2014L0065_EN.34#2|Article 34(2)]] and (3) and [[2014L0065_EN.35#2|Article 35(2)]] to (6) and (9),
### (d)
Articles [[2014L0065_EN.67|67]] to 75 and Articles [[2014L0065_EN.80|80]], [[2014L0065_EN.85|85]] and [[2014L0065_EN.86|86]].
## 4.
The following provisions shall also apply to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institutions]]* authorised under Directive 2013/36/EU when selling or advising *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* in relation to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(43)|structured deposits]]*:
### (a)
[[2014L0065_EN.9#3|Article 9(3)]], [[2014L0065_EN.14|Article 14]], and [[2014L0065_EN.16#2|Article 16(2)]], (3) and (6);
### (b)
Articles [[2014L0065_EN.23|23]] to 26, Article 28 and [[2014L0065_EN.29|Article 29]], excluding the second subparagraph of paragraph 2 thereof, and [[2014L0065_EN.30|Article 30]]; and
### (c)
Articles [[2014L0065_EN.67|67]] to 75.
## 5.
[[2014L0065_EN.17#1|Article 17(1)]] to (6) shall also apply to members or participants of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated markets]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTFs]]* who are not required to be authorised under this Directive pursuant to points (a), (e), (i) and (j) of [[2014L0065_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]].
## 6.
Articles [[2014L0065_EN.57|57]] and [[2014L0065_EN.58|58]] shall also apply to persons exempt under [[2014L0065_EN.2|Article 2]].
