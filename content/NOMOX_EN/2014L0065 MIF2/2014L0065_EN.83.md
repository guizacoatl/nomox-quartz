# Article 83 - Refusal to cooperate


A *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* may refuse to act on a request for cooperation in carrying out an investigation, on-the-spot verification or supervisory activity as provided for in [[2014L0065_EN.84|Article 84]] or to exchange information as provided for in [[2014L0065_EN.81|Article 81]] only where:
### (a)
judicial proceedings have already been initiated in respect of the same actions and the same persons before the authorities of the Member State addressed;
### (b)
final judgment has already been delivered in the Member State addressed in respect of the same persons and the same actions.

In the case of such a refusal, the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* shall notify the requesting *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* and ESMA accordingly, providing as detailed information as possible.
