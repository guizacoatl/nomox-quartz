# Article 4 - Definitions

## 1.
For the purposes of this Directive, the following definitions apply:
## (1)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*’ means any legal person whose regular occupation or business is the provision of one or more investment services to third parties and/or the performance of one or more investment activities on a professional basis.

Member States may include in the definition of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* undertakings which are not legal persons, provided that:
### (a)
their legal status ensures a level of protection for third parties’ interests equivalent to that afforded by legal persons; and
### (b)
they are subject to equivalent prudential supervision appropriate to their legal form.

However, where a natural person provides services involving the holding of third party funds or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(44)|transferable securities]]*, that person may be considered to be an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* for the purposes of this Directive and of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014 only if, without prejudice to the other requirements imposed in this Directive, in Regulation (EU) No 600/2014, and in Directive 2013/36/EU, that person complies with the following conditions:
### (a)
the ownership rights of third parties in instruments and funds must be safeguarded, especially in the event of the insolvency of the firm or of its proprietors, seizure, set-off or any other action by creditors of the firm or of its proprietors;
### (b)
the firm must be subject to rules designed to monitor the firm’s solvency and that of its proprietors;
### (c)
the firm’s annual accounts must be audited by one or more persons empowered, under national law, to audit accounts;
### (d)
where the firm has only one proprietor, that person must make provision for the protection of investors in the event of the firm’s cessation of business following the proprietor’s death or incapacity or any other such event;
## (2)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(2)|investment services and activities]]*’ means any of the services and activities listed in Section A of Annex I relating to any of the instruments listed in Section C of Annex I.

The Commission shall adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.89|Article 89]] measures specifying:
### (a)
the derivative contracts referred to in Section C.6 of Annex I that have the characteristics of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(58)|wholesale energy products]]* that must be physically settled and C.6 energy derivative contracts;
### (b)
the derivative contracts referred to in Section C.7 of Annex I that have the characteristics of other derivative *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]*;
### (c)
the derivative contracts referred to in Section C.10 of Annex I that have the characteristics of other derivative *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]*, having regard to whether, inter alia, they are traded on a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*, an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*;
## (3)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]*’ means any of the services listed in Section B of Annex I;
## (4)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(4)|investment advice]]*’ means the provision of personal recommendations to a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]*, either upon its request or at the initiative of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*, in respect of one or more transactions relating to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]*;
## (5)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(5)|execution of orders on behalf of clients]]*’ means acting to conclude agreements to buy or sell one or more *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* on behalf of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* and includes the conclusion of agreements to sell *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* issued by an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]* at the moment of their issuance;
## (6)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(6)|dealing on own account]]*’ means trading against proprietary capital resulting in the conclusion of transactions in one or more *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]*;
## (7)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(7)|market maker]]*’ means a person who holds himself out on the financial markets on a continuous basis as being willing to deal on own account by buying and selling *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* against that person’s proprietary capital at prices defined by that person;
## (8)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(8)|portfolio management]]*’ means managing portfolios in accordance with mandates given by *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* on a discretionary *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]*-by-*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* basis where such portfolios include one or more *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]*;
## (8a)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(8a)|switching of financial instruments]]*’ means selling a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* and buying another *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* or exercising a right to make a change with regard to an existing *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]*;
## (9)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]*’ means any natural or legal person to whom an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* provides investment or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]*;
## (10)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(10)|professional client]]*’ means a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* meeting the criteria laid down in Annex II;
## (11)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(11)|retail client]]*’ means a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* who is not a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(10)|professional client]]*;
## (12)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(12)|SME growth market]]*’ means a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* that is registered as an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(12)|SME growth market]]* in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.33|Article 33]];
## (13)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(13)|small and medium-sized enterprises]]*’ for the purposes of this Directive, means companies that had an average market capitalisation of less than EUR 200 000 000  on the basis of end-year quotes for the previous three calendar years;
## (14)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(14)|limit order]]*’ means an order to buy or sell a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* at its specified price limit or better and for a specified size;
## (15)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]*’ means those instruments specified in Section C of Annex I, including such instruments issued by means of distributed ledger technology;
## (16)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(16)|C6 energy derivative contracts]]*’ means options, futures, swaps, and any other derivative contracts mentioned in Section C.6 of Annex I relating to coal or oil that are traded on an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* and must be physically settled;
## (17)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(17)|money-market instruments]]*’ means those classes of instruments which are normally dealt in on the money market, such as treasury bills, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(47)|certificates]]* of deposit and commercial papers and excluding instruments of payment;
## (18)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]*’ means a person or persons who manages and/or operates the business of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* and may be the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* itself;
## (19)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(19)|multilateral system]]*’ means a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(19)|multilateral system]]* as defined in [[2014L0065_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (11), of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014;
## (20)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(20)|systematic internaliser]]*’ means an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* which, on an organised, frequent and systematic basis, deals on own account in equity instruments by executing *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* orders outside a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*, an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*, without operating a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(19)|multilateral system]]*, or which opts in to the status of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(20)|systematic internaliser]]*;
## (21)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*’ means a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(19)|multilateral system]]* operated and/or managed by a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]*, which brings together or facilitates the bringing together of multiple third-party buying and selling interests in *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* – in the system and in accordance with its non-discretionary rules – in a way that results in a contract, in respect of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* admitted to trading under its rules and/or systems, and which is authorised and functions regularly and in accordance with Title III of this Directive;
## (22)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|multilateral trading facility]]*’ or ‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]*’ means a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(19)|multilateral system]]*, operated by an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]*, which brings together multiple third-party buying and selling interests in *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* – in the system and in accordance with non-discretionary rules – in a way that results in a contract in accordance with Title II of this Directive;
## (23)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|organised trading facility]]*’ or ‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*’ means a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(19)|multilateral system]]* which is not a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* and in which multiple third-party buying and selling interests in bonds, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(48)|structured finance products]]*, emission allowances or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* are able to interact in the system in a way that results in a contract in accordance with Title II of this Directive;
## (24)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(24)|trading venue]]*’ means a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*, an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*;
## (25)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(25)|liquid market]]*’ means a market for a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* or a class of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]*, where there are ready and willing buyers and sellers on a continuous basis, assessed in accordance with the following criteria, taking into consideration the specific market structures of the particular *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* or of the particular class of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]*:
### (a)
the average frequency and size of transactions over a range of market conditions, having regard to the nature and life cycle of products within the class of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]*;
### (b)
the number and type of market participants, including the ratio of market participants to traded instruments in a particular product;
### (c)
the average size of spreads, where available;
## (26)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]*’ means the authority, designated by each Member State in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.67|Article 67]], unless otherwise specified in this Directive;
## (27)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]*’ means a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]* as defined in point (1) of [[2013R0575_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]];
## (28)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(28)|UCITS management company]]*’ means a management company as defined in point (b) of Article 2(1) of Directive 2009/65/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council;
## (29)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]*’ means a natural or legal person who, under the full and unconditional responsibility of only one *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* on whose behalf it acts, promotes investment and/or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* or prospective *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]*, receives and transmits instructions or orders from the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* in respect of investment services or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]*, places *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* or provides advice to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* or prospective *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* in respect of those *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* or services;
## (30)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]*’ means a place of business other than the head office which is a part of an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*, which has no legal personality and which provides investment services and/or activities and which may also perform *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* for which the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* has been authorised; all the places of business set up in the same Member State by an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* with headquarters in another Member State shall be regarded as a single *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]*;
## (31)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(31)|qualifying holding]]*’ means a direct or indirect holding in an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* which represents 10 % or more of the capital or of the voting rights, as set out in Articles [[2014L0065_EN.9|9]] and [[2014L0065_EN.10|10]] of Directive 2004/109/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council, taking into account the conditions regarding aggregation thereof laid down in [[2014L0065_EN.12#4|Article 12(4)]] and (5) of that Directive, or which makes it possible to exercise a significant influence over the management of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* in which that holding subsists;
## (32)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(32)|parent undertaking]]*’ means a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(32)|parent undertaking]]* within the meaning of [[2014L0065_EN.2#9|Article 2(9)]] and 22 of Directive 2013/34/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council;
## (33)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(33)|subsidiary]]*’ means a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(33)|subsidiary]]* undertaking within the meaning of Articles [[2014L0065_EN.2|2]](10) and 22 of Directive 2013/34/EU, including any *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(33)|subsidiary]]* of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(33)|subsidiary]]* undertaking of an ultimate *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(32)|parent undertaking]]*;
## (34)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(34)|group]]*’ means a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(34)|group]]* as defined in [[2013L0034_EN.2#11|Article 2(11) of Directive 2013/34/EU]];
## (35)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(35)|close links]]*’ means a situation in which two or more natural or legal persons are linked by:
### (a)
participation in the form of ownership, direct or by way of control, of 20 % or more of the voting rights or capital of an undertaking;
### (b)
‘control’ which means the relationship between a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(32)|parent undertaking]]* and a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(33)|subsidiary]]*, in all the cases referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.22#1|Article 22(1)]] and (2) of Directive 2013/34/EU, or a similar relationship between any natural or legal person and an undertaking, any *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(33)|subsidiary]]* undertaking of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(33)|subsidiary]]* undertaking also being considered to be a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(33)|subsidiary]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(32)|parent undertaking]]* which is at the head of those undertakings;
### (c)
a permanent link of both or all of them to the same person by a control relationship;
## (36)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(36)|management body]]*’ means the body or bodies of an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*, a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]*, or a data reporting services provider as defined in point (36a) of Article 2(1) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014, which are appointed in accordance with national law, which are empowered to set the entity’s strategy, objectives and overall direction, and which oversee and monitor management decision-making and include persons who effectively direct the business of the entity.

Where this Directive refers to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(36)|management body]]* and, pursuant to national law, the managerial and supervisory functions of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(36)|management body]]* are assigned to different bodies or different members within one body, the Member State shall identify the bodies or members of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(36)|management body]]* responsible in accordance with its national law, unless otherwise specified by this Directive;
## (37)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(37)|senior management]]*’ means natural persons who exercise executive functions within an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*, a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]*, or a data reporting services provider as defined in point (36a) of Article 2(1) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014, and who are responsible and accountable to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(36)|management body]]* for the day-to-day management of the entity, including for the implementation of the policies concerning the distribution of services and products to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* by the firm and its personnel;
## (38)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(38)|matched principal trading]]*’ means a transaction where the facilitator interposes itself between the buyer and the seller to the transaction in such a way that it is never exposed to market risk throughout the execution of the transaction, with both sides executed simultaneously, and where the transaction is concluded at a price where the facilitator makes no profit or loss, other than a previously disclosed commission, fee or charge for the transaction;
## (39)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(39)|algorithmic trading]]*’ means trading in *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* where a computer algorithm automatically determines individual parameters of orders such as whether to initiate the order, the timing, price or quantity of the order or how to manage the order after its submission, with limited or no human intervention, and does not include any system that is only used for the purpose of routing orders to one or more *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(24)|trading venues]]* or for the processing of orders involving no determination of any trading parameters or for the confirmation of orders or the post-trade processing of executed transactions;
## (40)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(40)|high-frequency algorithmic trading technique]]*’ means an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(39)|algorithmic trading]]* technique characterised by:
### (a)
infrastructure intended to minimise network and other types of latencies, including at least one of the following facilities for algorithmic order entry: co-location, proximity hosting or high-speed *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(41)|direct electronic access]]*;
### (b)
system-determination of order initiation, generation, routing or execution without human intervention for individual trades or orders; and
### (c)
high message intraday rates which constitute orders, quotes or cancellations;
## (41)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(41)|direct electronic access]]*’ means an arrangement where a member or participant or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(24)|trading venue]]* permits a person to use its trading code so the person can electronically transmit orders relating to a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* directly to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(24)|trading venue]]* and includes arrangements which involve the use by a person of the infrastructure of the member or participant or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]*, or any connecting system provided by the member or participant or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]*, to transmit the orders (direct market access) and arrangements where such an infrastructure is not used by a person (sponsored access);
## (42)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(42)|cross-selling practice]]*’ means the offering of an investment service together with another service or product as part of a package or as a condition for the same agreement or package;
## (43)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(43)|structured deposit]]*’ means a deposit as defined in point (3) of [[2014L0049_EN.2#1|Article 2(1) of Directive 2014/49/EU]] of the European Parliament and of the Council
, which is fully repayable at maturity on terms under which interest or a premium will be paid or is at risk, according to a formula involving factors such as:
### (a)
an index or combination of indices, excluding variable rate deposits whose return is directly linked to an interest rate index such as Euribor or Libor;
### (b)
a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* or combination of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]*;
### (c)
a commodity or combination of commodities or other physical or non-physical non-fungible assets; or
### (d)
a foreign exchange rate or combination of foreign exchange rates;
## (44)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(44)|transferable securities]]*’ means those classes of securities which are negotiable on the capital market, with the exception of instruments of payment, such as:
### (a)
shares in companies and other securities equivalent to shares in companies, partnerships or other entities, and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(45)|depositary receipts]]* in respect of shares;
### (b)
bonds or other forms of securitised debt, including *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(45)|depositary receipts]]* in respect of such securities;
### (c)
any other securities giving the right to acquire or sell any such *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(44)|transferable securities]]* or giving rise to a cash settlement determined by reference to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(44)|transferable securities]]*, currencies, interest rates or yields, commodities or other indices or measures;
## (44a)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(44a)|make-whole clause]]*’ means a clause that aims to protect the investor by ensuring that, in the event of early redemption of a bond, the issuer is required to pay to the investor holding the bond an amount equal to the sum of the net present value of the remaining coupon payments expected until maturity and the principal amount of the bond to be redeemed;
## (45)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(45)|depositary receipts]]*’ means those securities which are negotiable on the capital market and which represent ownership of the securities of a non-domiciled issuer while being able to be admitted to trading on a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* and traded independently of the securities of the non-domiciled issuer;
## (46)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(46)|exchange-traded fund]]*’ means a fund of which at least one unit or share class is traded throughout the day on at least one *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(24)|trading venue]]* and with at least one *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(7)|market maker]]* which takes action to ensure that the price of its units or shares on the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(24)|trading venue]]* does not vary significantly from its net asset value and, where applicable, from its indicative net asset value;
## (47)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(47)|certificates]]*’ means *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(47)|certificates]]* as defined in Article 2(1)(27) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014;
## (48)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(48)|structured finance products]]*’ means *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(48)|structured finance products]]* as defined in Article 2(1)(28) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014;
## (49)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]*’ means *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* as defined in Article 2(1)(29) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014;
## (50)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(50)|commodity derivatives]]*’ means *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(50)|commodity derivatives]]* as defined in Article 2(1)(30) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014;
## (51)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(51)|CCP]]*’ means a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(51)|CCP]]* as defined in [[2012R0648_EN.2#1|Article 2(1) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];

## (55)
‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]*’ means:
### (a)
in the case of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]*:
#### (i)
if the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* is a natural person, the Member State in which its head office is situated;
#### (ii)
if the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* is a legal person, the Member State in which its registered office is situated;
