# Article 6 - Scope of authorisation

## 1.
The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* shall ensure that the authorisation specifies the investment services or activities which the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* is authorised to provide. The authorisation may cover one or more of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* set out in Section B of Annex I. Authorisation shall in no case be granted solely for the provision of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]*.
## 2.
An *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* seeking authorisation to extend its business to additional investment services or activities or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* not foreseen at the time of initial authorisation shall submit a request for extension of its authorisation.
## 3.
The authorisation shall be valid for the entire Union and shall allow an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* to provide the services or perform the activities, for which it has been authorised, throughout the Union, either through the right of establishment, including through a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]*, or through the freedom to provide services.
