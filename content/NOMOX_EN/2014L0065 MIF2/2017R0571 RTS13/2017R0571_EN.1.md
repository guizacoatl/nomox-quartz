# Article 1 - Information to competent authorities

## 1.
An applicant seeking authorisation to provide data reporting services shall submit to the competent authority the information set out in Articles [[2017R0571_EN.2|2]], [[2017R0571_EN.3|3]] and [[2017R0571_EN.4|4]] and the information regarding all the organisational requirements set out in Chapters II and III.
## 2.
A data reporting services provider shall promptly inform the competent authority of its home Member State of any material change to the information provided at the time of the authorisation and thereafter.
