# Article 21 - Regular review of conditions for initial authorisation

## 1.
Member States shall require that an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* authorised in their territory comply at all times with the conditions for initial authorisation established in Chapter I.
## 2.
Member States shall require competent authorities to establish the appropriate methods to monitor that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* comply with their obligation under paragraph 1. They shall require *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* to notify the competent authorities of any material changes to the conditions for initial authorisation.

ESMA may develop guidelines regarding the monitoring methods referred to in this paragraph.
