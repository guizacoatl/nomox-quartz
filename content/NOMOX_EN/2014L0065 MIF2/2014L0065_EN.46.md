# Article 46 - Requirements relating to persons exercising significant influence over the management of the regulated market

## 1.
Member States shall require the persons who are in a position to exercise, directly or indirectly, significant influence over the management of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* to be suitable.
## 2.
Member States shall require the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*:
### (a)
to provide the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* with, and to make public, information regarding the ownership of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* and/or the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]*, and in particular, the identity and scale of interests of any parties in a position to exercise significant influence over the management;
### (b)
to inform the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of and to make public any transfer of ownership which gives rise to a change in the identity of the persons exercising significant influence over the operation of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*.
## 3.
The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* shall refuse to approve proposed changes to the controlling interests of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* and/or the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* where there are objective and demonstrable grounds for believing that they would pose a threat to the sound and prudent management of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*.
