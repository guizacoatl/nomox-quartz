# Article 1 - Conditions on the denial of access by a CCP

## 1.
A CCP shall assess whether granting access would create any of the risks specified in Articles [[2017R0581_EN.2|2]], [[2017R0581_EN.3|3]] and [[2017R0581_EN.4|4]] and may deny access only if, after making all reasonable efforts to manage its risks, the CCP concludes that there are significant undue risks that cannot be managed.
## 2.
If a CCP denies access, it shall identify which risks specified in Articles [[2017R0581_EN.2|2]], [[2017R0581_EN.3|3]] and [[2017R0581_EN.4|4]] would result from granting access and explain why those risks cannot be managed.
