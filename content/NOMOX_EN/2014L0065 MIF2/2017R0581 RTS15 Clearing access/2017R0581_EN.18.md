# Article 18 - Notification procedure from the trading venue to its competent authority regarding an extension of the transitional period


Where a trading venue wishes to continue not to be bound by [[2014R0600_EN.36|Article 36 of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]] for a further thirty months, it shall submit a notification to its competent authority and ESMA in written form, using Forms 4.1 and 4.2 set out in the Annex to this Regulation.
