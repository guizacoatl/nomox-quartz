# Article 15 - Notification procedure from the CCP to its competent authority


Where a CCP applies for permission to avail itself of transitional arrangements referred to in [[2014R0600_EN.35#5|Article 35(5) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]], it shall submit a notification to its competent authority in written form, using Form 1 set out in the Annex to this Regulation.
