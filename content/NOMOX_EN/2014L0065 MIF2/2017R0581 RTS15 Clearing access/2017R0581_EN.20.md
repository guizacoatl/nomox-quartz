# Article 20 - Approval and verification method by ESMA

## 1.
For the purposes of verification in accordance with [[2014R0600_EN.36#6#d|Article 36(6)(d) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]], the trading venue shall submit to ESMA on request all facts and figures on which the calculation is based.
## 2.
When verifying the submitted annual notional amount figures, ESMA shall also consider relevant post-trade data and annual statistics.
## 3.
ESMA shall approve or reject the opt-out within three months of the reception of all relevant information for the notification in accordance with either [[2017R0581_EN.16|Article 16]] or 17, including the information specified in [[2017R0581_EN.19|Article 19]].
