# Article 21 - Entry into force and application


This Regulation shall enter into force on the twentieth day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union .

It shall apply from 3 January 2018.

However, Articles [[2017R0581_EN.15|15]], [[2017R0581_EN.16|16]], [[2017R0581_EN.17|17]], [[2017R0581_EN.19|19]] and [[2017R0581_EN.20|20]] shall apply from the entry into force of this Regulation.
