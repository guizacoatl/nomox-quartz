# Article 17 - Notification procedure from the trading venue to its competent authority regarding the initial transitional period


Where a trading venue does not wish to be bound by [[2014R0600_EN.36|Article 36 of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]], it shall submit a notification to its competent authority and ESMA in written form, using Forms 3.1 and 3.2 set out in the Annex to this Regulation.
