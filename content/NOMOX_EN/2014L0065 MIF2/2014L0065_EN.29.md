# Article 29 - Obligations of investment firms when appointing tied agents

## 1.
Member States shall allow an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* to appoint *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* for the purposes of promoting the services of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*, soliciting business or receiving orders from *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* or potential *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* and transmitting them, placing *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* and providing advice in respect of such *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* and services offered by that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*.
## 2.
Member States shall require that where an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* decides to appoint a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]* it remains fully and unconditionally responsible for any action or omission on the part of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]* when acting on behalf of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*. Member States shall require the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* to ensure that a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]* discloses the capacity in which he is acting and the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* which he is representing when contacting or before dealing with any *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* or potential *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]*.

Member States may allow, in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.16#6|Article 16(6)]], (8) and (9), *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* registered in their territory to hold money and/or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* on behalf and under the full responsibility of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* for which they are acting within their territory or, in the case of a cross border operation, in the territory of a Member State which allows a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]* to hold *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* money.

Member States shall require the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* to monitor the activities of their *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* so as to ensure that they continue to comply with this Directive when acting through *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]*.
## 3.
*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|Tied agents]]* shall be registered in the public register in the Member State where they are established. ESMA shall publish on its website references or hyperlinks to the public registers established under this Article by the Member States that decide to allow *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* to appoint *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]*.

Member States shall ensure that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* are only admitted to the public register if it has been established that they are of sufficiently good repute and that they possess the appropriate general, commercial and professional knowledge and competence so as to be able to deliver the investment service or ancillary service and to communicate accurately all relevant information regarding the proposed service to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* or potential *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]*.

Member States may decide that, subject to appropriate control, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* can verify whether the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* which they have appointed are of sufficiently good repute and possess the knowledge and competence referred to in the second subparagraph.

The register shall be updated on a regular basis. It shall be publicly available for consultation.
## 4.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* appointing *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* take adequate measures in order to avoid any negative impact that the activities of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]* not covered by the scope of this Directive could have on the activities carried out by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]* on behalf of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*.

Member States may allow competent authorities to collaborate with *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institutions]]*, their associations and other entities in registering *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* and in monitoring compliance of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* with the requirements of paragraph 3. In particular, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* may be registered by an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]* or their associations and other entities under the supervision of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]*.
## 5.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* appoint only *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* entered in the public registers referred to in paragraph 3.
## 6.
Member States may adopt or retain provisions that are more stringent than those set out in this Article or add further requirements for *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* registered within their jurisdiction.
