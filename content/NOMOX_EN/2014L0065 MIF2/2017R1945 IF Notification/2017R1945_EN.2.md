# Article 2 - Submission of the application

## 1.
An applicant seeking authorisation as an investment firm in accordance with Title II of Directive 2014/65/EU shall submit to the competent authority its application by filling in the template set out in Annex I.
## 2.
The applicant shall notify the competent authority of the information on all members of its management body by filling in the template set out in Annex II.
