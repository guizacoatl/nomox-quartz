# Article 6 - Communication of the decision


The competent authority shall inform the applicant of its decision to grant or not the authorisation in paper form, by electronic means or both, within the 6-month period referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.7#3|Article 7(3) of Directive 2014/65/EU]].
