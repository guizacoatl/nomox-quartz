# Article 1 - Designation of a contact point


Competent authorities shall designate a contact point for handling all information received from applicants seeking authorisation as an investment firm in accordance to Title II of Directive 2014/65/EU. The contact details on the designated contact point shall be made public and regularly updated on the competent authorities' websites.
