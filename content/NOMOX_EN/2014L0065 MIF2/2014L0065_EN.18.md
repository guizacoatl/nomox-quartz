# Article 18 - Trading process and finalisation of transactions in an MTF and an OTF

## 1.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*, in addition to meeting the organisational requirements laid down in [[2014L0065_EN.16|Article 16]], establish transparent rules and procedures for fair and orderly trading and establish objective criteria for the efficient execution of orders. They shall have arrangements for the sound management of the technical operations of the facility, including the establishment of effective contingency arrangements to cope with risks of systems disruption.
## 2.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* establish transparent rules regarding the criteria for determining the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* that can be traded under its systems.

Member States shall require that, where applicable, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* provide, or are satisfied that there is access to, sufficient publicly available information to enable its users to form an investment judgement, taking into account both the nature of the users and the types of instruments traded.
## 3.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* establish, publish and maintain and implement transparent and non-discriminatory rules, based on objective criteria, governing access to its facility.
## 4.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* have arrangements to identify clearly and manage the potential adverse consequences for the operation of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*, or for the members or participants and users, of any conflict of interest between the interest of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]*, the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*, their owners or the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* and the sound functioning of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*.
## 5.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* comply with Articles [[2014L0065_EN.48|48]] and [[2014L0065_EN.49|49]] and have in place all the necessary effective systems, procedures and arrangements to do so.
## 6.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* clearly inform its members or participants of their respective responsibilities for the settlement of the transactions executed in that facility. Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* have put in place the necessary arrangements to facilitate the efficient settlement of the transactions concluded under the systems of that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*.
## 7.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTFs]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTFs]]* have at least three materially active members or users, each having the opportunity to interact with all the others in respect to price formation.
## 8.
Where a transferable security that has been admitted to trading on a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* is also traded on an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* without the consent of the issuer, the issuer shall not be subject to any obligation relating to initial, ongoing or ad hoc financial disclosure with regard to that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*.
## 9.
Member States shall require that any *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* comply immediately with any instruction from its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* pursuant to [[2014L0065_EN.69#2|Article 69(2)]] to suspend or remove a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instrument]]* from trading.
## 10.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operators]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* provide the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* with a detailed description of the functioning of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*, including, without prejudice to [[2014L0065_EN.20#1|Article 20(1)]], (4) and (5), any links to or participation by a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*, an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]*, an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* or a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(20)|systematic internaliser]]* owned by the same *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]*, and a list of their members, participants and/or users. Competent authorities shall make that information available to ESMA on request. Every authorisation to an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* as an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* and an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* shall be notified to ESMA. ESMA shall establish a list of all *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTFs]]* and *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTFs]]* in the Union. The list shall contain information on the services an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* or an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* provides and entail the unique code identifying the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]* and the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* for use in reports in accordance with Articles [[2014L0065_EN.6|6]], [[2014L0065_EN.10|10]] and [[2014L0065_EN.26|26]] of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014. It shall be updated on a regular basis. ESMA shall publish and keep up-to-date that list on its website.
## 11.
ESMA shall develop draft implementing technical standards to determine the content and format of the description and notification referred to in paragraph 10.

ESMA shall submit those draft implementing technical standards to the Commission by 3 January 2016.

Power is conferred on the Commission to adopt the implementing technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
