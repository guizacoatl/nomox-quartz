# Article 20 - Specific requirements for OTFs

## 1.
Member States shall require that an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* and a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* establishes arrangements preventing the execution of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* orders in an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* against the proprietary capital of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* or from any entity that is part of the same *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(34)|group]]* or legal person as the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]*.
## 2.
Member States shall permit an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* to engage in *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(38)|matched principal trading]]* in bonds, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(48)|structured finance products]]*, emission allowances and certain *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* only where the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* has consented to the process.

An *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* shall not use *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(38)|matched principal trading]]* to execute *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* orders in an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* in *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* pertaining to a class of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(49)|derivatives]]* that has been declared subject to the clearing obligation in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.5|Article 5 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]].

An *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* shall establish arrangements ensuring compliance with the definition of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(38)|matched principal trading]]* in point (38) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]].
## 3.
Member States shall permit an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* to engage in *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(6)|dealing on own account]]* other than *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(38)|matched principal trading]]* only with regard to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(61)|sovereign debt]]* instruments for which there is not a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(25)|liquid market]]*.
## 4.
Member States shall not allow the operation of an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* and of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(20)|systematic internaliser]]* to take place within the same legal entity. An *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* shall not connect with a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(20)|systematic internaliser]]* in a way which enables orders in an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* and orders or quotes in a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(20)|systematic internaliser]]* to interact. An *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* shall not connect with another *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* in a way which enables orders in different *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTFs]]* to interact.
## 5.
Member States shall not prevent an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* from engaging another *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* to carry out market making on that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* on an independent basis.

For the purposes of this Article, an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* shall not be deemed to be carrying out market making on an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* on an independent basis if it has *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(35)|close links]]* with the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*.
## 6.
Member States shall require that the execution of orders on an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* is carried out on a discretionary basis.

An *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* shall exercise discretion only in either or both of the following circumstances:
### (a)
when deciding to place or retract an order on the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* they operate;
### (b)
when deciding not to match a specific *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* order with other orders available in the systems at a given time, provided it is in compliance with specific instructions received from a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* and with its obligations in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.27|Article 27]].

For the system that crosses *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* orders the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* may decide if, when and how much of two or more orders it wants to match within the system. In accordance with paragraphs 1, 2, 4 and 5 and without prejudice to paragraph 3, with regard to a system that arranges transactions in non-equities, the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* operating the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* may facilitate negotiation between *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]* so as to bring together two or more potentially compatible trading interest in a transaction.

That obligation shall be without prejudice to Articles [[2014L0065_EN.18|18]] and [[2014L0065_EN.27|27]].
## 7.
The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* may require, either when an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* requests to be authorised for the operation of an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* or on ad-hoc basis, a detailed explanation why the system does not correspond to and cannot operate as a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(22)|MTF]]*, or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(20)|systematic internaliser]]*, a detailed description as to how discretion will be exercised, in particular when an order to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* may be retracted and when and how two or more *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|client]]* orders will be matched within the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*. In addition, the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* of an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]* shall provide the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* with information explaining its use of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(38)|matched principal trading]]*. The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* shall monitor an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*’s or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]*’s engagement in *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(38)|matched principal trading]]* to ensure that it continues to fall within the definition of such trading and that its engagement in *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(38)|matched principal trading]]* does not give rise to conflicts of interest between the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(18)|market operator]]* and its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(9)|clients]]*.
## 8.
Member States shall ensure that Articles [[2014L0065_EN.24|24]], [[2014L0065_EN.25|25]], [[2014L0065_EN.27|27]] and [[2014L0065_EN.28|28]] are applied to the transactions concluded on an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(23)|OTF]]*.
