# Article 35 - Establishment of a branch

## 1.
Member States shall ensure that investment services and/or activities as well as *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* may be provided within their territories in accordance with this Directive and with Directive 2013/36/EU through the right of establishment, whether by the establishment of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* or by the use of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]* established in a Member State outside its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]*, provided that those services and activities are covered by the authorisation granted to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]* in the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]*. *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|Ancillary services]]* may only be provided together with an investment service and/or activity.

Member States shall not impose any additional requirements save those allowed under paragraph 8, on the organisation and operation of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* in respect of the matters covered by this Directive.
## 2.
Member States shall require any *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* wishing to establish a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* within the territory of another Member State or to use *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* established in another Member State in which it has not established a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]*, first to notify the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* and to provide it with the following information:
### (a)
the Member States within the territory of which it plans to establish a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* or the Member States in which it has not established a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* but plans to use *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* established there;
### (b)
a programme of operations setting out, inter alia, the investment services and/or activities as well as the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* to be offered;
### (c)
where established, the organisational structure of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* and indicating whether the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* intends to use *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* and the identity of those *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]*;
### (d)
where *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agents]]* are to be used in a Member State in which an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* has not established a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]*, a description of the intended use of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]*(s) and an organisational structure, including reporting lines, indicating how the agent(s) fit into the corporate structure of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*;
### (e)
the address in the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* from which documents may be obtained;
### (f)
the names of those responsible for the management of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* or of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]*.

Where an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* uses a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]* established in a Member State outside its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]*, such *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]* shall be assimilated to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]*, where one is established, and shall in any event be subject to the provisions of this Directive relating to branches.
## 3.
Unless the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* has reason to doubt the adequacy of the administrative structure or the financial situation of an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*, taking into account the activities envisaged, it shall, within three months of receiving all the information, communicate that information to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* designated as contact point in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.79#1|Article 79(1)]] and inform the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* concerned accordingly.
## 4.
In addition to the information referred to in paragraph 2, the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* shall communicate details of the accredited compensation scheme of which the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* is a member in accordance with Directive 97/9/EC to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]*. In the event of a change in the particulars, the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* shall inform the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* accordingly.
## 5.
Where the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* refuses to communicate the information to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]*, it shall give reasons for its refusal to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* concerned within three months of receiving all the information.
## 6.
On receipt of a communication from the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]*, or failing such communication from the latter at the latest after two months from the date of transmission of the communication by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]*, the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* may be established and commence business.
## 7.
Any *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]* wishing to use a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]* established in a Member State outside its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* to provide investment services and/or activities as well as *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(3)|ancillary services]]* in accordance with this Directive shall notify the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* and provide it with the information referred to in paragraph 2.

Unless the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* has reason to doubt the adequacy of the administrative structure or the financial situation of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]*, it shall, within three months of receiving all the information, communicate that information to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* designated as contact point in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.79#1|Article 79(1)]] and inform the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]* concerned accordingly.

Where the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* refuses to communicate the information to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]*, it shall give reasons for its refusal to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]* concerned within three months of receiving all the information.

On receipt of a communication from the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]*, or failing such communication from the latter at the latest after two months from the date of transmission of the communication by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]*, the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]* can commence business. Such *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(29)|tied agent]]* shall be subject to the provisions of this Directive relating to branches.
## 8.
The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the Member State in which the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* is located shall assume responsibility for ensuring that the services provided by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* within its territory comply with the obligations laid down in Articles [[2014L0065_EN.24|24]], [[2014L0065_EN.25|25]], [[2014L0065_EN.27|27]] and [[2014L0065_EN.28|28]], of this Directive and Articles [[2014R0600_EN.14|14]] to [[2014R600_EN.26|26]] of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014 and the measures adopted pursuant thereto by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* where allowed in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.24#12|Article 24(12)]].

The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the Member State in which the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* is located shall have the right to examine *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* arrangements and to request such changes as are strictly needed to enable the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* to enforce the obligations under Articles [[2014L0065_EN.24|24]], [[2014L0065_EN.25|25]], [[2014L0065_EN.27|27]] and [[2014L0065_EN.28|28]] of this Directive and Articles [[2014R0600_EN.14|14]] to [[2014R600_EN.26|26]] of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014 and measures adopted pursuant thereto with respect to the services and/or activities provided by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* within its territory.
## 9.
Each Member State shall provide that, where an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* authorised in another Member State has established a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]* within its territory, the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]*, in the exercise of its responsibilities and after informing the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]*, may carry out on-site inspections in that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(30)|branch]]*.
## 10.
In the event of a change in any of the information communicated in accordance with paragraph 2, an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* shall give written notice of that change to the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]* at least one month before implementing the change. The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(56)|host Member State]]* shall also be informed of that change by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(55)|home Member State]]*.
## 11.
ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards to specify the information to be notified in accordance with paragraphs 2, 4, 7 and 10.

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 3 July 2015.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
## 12.
ESMA shall develop draft implementing technical standards to establish standard forms, templates and procedures for the transmission of information in accordance with paragraphs 2, 3, 4, 7 and 10.

ESMA shall submit those draft implementing technical standards to the Commission by 3 January 2016.

Power is conferred on the Commission to adopt the implementing technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
