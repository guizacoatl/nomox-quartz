# Article 95 - Transitional provisions

## 1.
Until
3 January 2021

:
### (a)
the clearing obligation set out in [[2012R0648_EN.4|Article 4 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] and the risk mitigation techniques set out in [[2014L0065_EN.11#3|Article 11(3)]] thereof shall not apply to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(16)|C6 energy derivative contracts]]* entered into by non-financial counterparties that meet the conditions in [[2012R0648_EN.10#1|Article 10(1) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] or by non-financial counterparties that shall be authorised for the first time as *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* as from
3 January 2018

; and
### (b)
such *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(16)|C6 energy derivative contracts]]* shall not be considered to be OTC derivative contracts for the purposes of the clearing threshold set out in [[2012R0648_EN.10|Article 10 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]].

*[[2014L0065_EN.4#(16)|C6 energy derivative contracts]]* benefiting from the transitional regime set out in the first subparagraph shall be subject to all other requirements laid down in Regulation (EU) No 648/2012.
## 2.
The exemption referred to in paragraph 1 shall be granted by the relevant *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]*. The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* shall notify ESMA of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(16)|C6 energy derivative contracts]]* which have been granted an exemption in accordance with paragraph 1 and ESMA shall publish on its website a list of those *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(16)|C6 energy derivative contracts]]*.
