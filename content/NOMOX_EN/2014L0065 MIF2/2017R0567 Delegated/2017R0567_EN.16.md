# Article 16 - Size specific to the instrument

For the purposes of [[2014R0600_EN.18#6|Article 18(6) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]], the size specific to the instrument in respect of instruments traded on request for quote, voice, hybrid or other trading forms shall be as set out in Annex III to Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2017/583
