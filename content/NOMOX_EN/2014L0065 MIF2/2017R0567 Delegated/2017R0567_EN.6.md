# Article 6 - Obligation to provide market data on a reasonable commercial basis

## 1.
For the purposes of making market data containing the information set out in Articles [[2017R0567_EN.3|3]], [[2017R0567_EN.4|4]] and [[2017R0567_EN.6|6]] to 11, 15 and 18 of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014 available to the public on a reasonable commercial basis, market operators and investment firms operating a trading venue and systematic internalisers shall, in accordance with Articles [[2017R0567_EN.13|13]](1), 15(1) and 18(8) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014, comply with the obligations set out in Articles [[2017R0567_EN.7|7]] to 11 of this Regulation.
## 2.
Articles [[2017R0567_EN.7|7]] and [[2017R0567_EN.8|8]](2), 9, 10(2) and 11 shall not apply to market operators or investment firms operating trading venues or to systematic internalisers that make market data available to the public free of charge.
