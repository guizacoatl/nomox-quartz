# Article 12 - Obligation for systematic internalisers to make quotes public on a regular and continuous basis during normal trading hours

For the purposes of [[2014R0600_EN.15#1|Article 15(1) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]], a systematic internaliser shall be considered to make public its quotes on a regular and continuous basis during normal trading hours only where the systematic internaliser makes the quotes available at all times during the hours which the systematic internaliser has established and published in advance as its normal trading hours.
