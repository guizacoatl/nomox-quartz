# Article 11 - Notification of proposed acquisitions

## 1.
Member States shall require any natural or legal person or such persons acting in concert (the ‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#1.|proposed acquirer]]*’), who have taken a decision either to acquire, directly or indirectly, a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(31)|qualifying holding]]* in an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or to further increase, directly or indirectly, such a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(31)|qualifying holding]]* in an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* as a result of which the proportion of the voting rights or of the capital held would reach or exceed 20 %, 30 % or 50 % or so that the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* would become its *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(33)|subsidiary]]* (the ‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#1.|proposed acquisition]]*’), first to notify in writing the competent authorities of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* in which they are seeking to acquire or increase a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(31)|qualifying holding]]*, indicating the size of the intended holding and relevant information, as referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.13#4|Article 13(4)]].

Member States shall require any natural or legal person who has taken a decision to dispose, directly or indirectly, of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(31)|qualifying holding]]* in an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* first to notify in writing the competent authorities, indicating the size of the intended holding. Such a person shall likewise notify the competent authorities if he has taken a decision to reduce his *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(31)|qualifying holding]]* so that the proportion of the voting rights or of the capital held would fall below 20 %, 30 % or 50 % or so that the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* would cease to be his *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(33)|subsidiary]]*.

Member States need not apply the 30 % threshold where, in accordance with point (a) of Article 9(3) of Directive 2004/109/EC, they apply a threshold of one-third.

In determining whether the criteria for a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(31)|qualifying holding]]* referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.10|Article 10]] and in this Article are fulfilled, Member States shall not take into account voting rights or shares which *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institutions]]* may hold as a result of providing the underwriting of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* and/or placing of *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* on a firm commitment basis included under point 6 of Section A of Annex I, provided that those rights are, on the one hand, not exercised or otherwise used to intervene in the management of the issuer and, on the other, disposed of within one year of acquisition.
## 2.
The relevant competent authorities shall work in full consultation with each other when carrying out the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#2.|assessment]]* provided for in [[2014L0065_EN.13#1|Article 13(1)]] (the ‘*[[2014L0065_EN.4#2.|assessment]]*’) if the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#1.|proposed acquirer]]* is one of the following:
### (a)
a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]*, assurance undertaking, insurance undertaking, reinsurance undertaking, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(28)|UCITS management company]]* authorised in another Member State or in a sector other than that in which the acquisition is proposed;
### (b)
the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(32)|parent undertaking]]* of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]*, assurance undertaking, insurance undertaking, reinsurance undertaking, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(28)|UCITS management company]]* authorised in another Member State or in a sector other than that in which the acquisition is proposed; or
### (c)
a natural or legal person controlling a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(27)|credit institution]]*, assurance undertaking, insurance undertaking, reinsurance undertaking, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* or *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(28)|UCITS management company]]* authorised in another Member State or in a sector other than that in which the acquisition is proposed.

The competent authorities shall, without undue delay, provide each other with any information which is essential or relevant for the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#2.|assessment]]*. In that regard, the competent authorities shall communicate to each other upon request all relevant information and shall communicate on their own initiative all essential information. A decision by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* that has authorised the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* in which the acquisition is proposed shall indicate any views or reservations expressed by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* responsible for the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#1.|proposed acquirer]]*.
## 3.
Member States shall require that, if an *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* becomes aware of any acquisitions or disposals of holdings in its capital that cause holdings to exceed or fall below any of the thresholds referred to in the first subparagraph of paragraph 1, that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firm]]* is to inform the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* without delay.

At least once a year, *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* shall also inform the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the names of shareholders and members possessing *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(31)|qualifying holdings]]* and the sizes of such holdings as shown, for example, by the information received at annual general meetings of shareholders and members or as a result of compliance with the regulations applicable to companies whose *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(44)|transferable securities]]* are admitted to trading on a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*.
## 4.
Member States shall require that competent authorities take measures similar to those referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.10#3|Article 10(3)]] in respect of persons who fail to comply with the obligation to provide prior information in relation to the acquisition or increase of a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(31)|qualifying holding]]*. If a holding is acquired despite the opposition of the competent authorities, the Member States shall, regardless of any other sanctions to be adopted, provide either for exercise of the corresponding voting rights to be suspended, for the nullity of the votes cast or for the possibility of their annulment.
