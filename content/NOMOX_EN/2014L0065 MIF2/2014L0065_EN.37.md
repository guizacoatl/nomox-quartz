# Article 37 - Access to CCP, clearing and settlement facilities and right to designate settlement system

## 1.
Without prejudice to Titles III, IV or V of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012, Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* from other Member States have the right of direct and indirect access to *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(51)|CCP]]*, clearing and settlement systems in their territory for the purposes of finalising or arranging the finalisation of transactions in *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]*.

Member States shall require that direct and indirect access of those *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(1)|investment firms]]* to such facilities be subject to the same non-discriminatory, transparent and objective criteria as apply to local members or participants. Member States shall not restrict the use of those facilities to the clearing and settlement of transactions in *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* undertaken on a *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(24)|trading venue]]* in their territory.
## 2.
Member States shall require that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated markets]]* in their territory offer all their members or participants the right to designate the system for the settlement of transactions in *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(15)|financial instruments]]* undertaken on that *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]*, subject to the following conditions:
### (a)
such links and arrangements between the designated settlement system and any other system or facility as are necessary to ensure the efficient and economic settlement of the transaction in question;
### (b)
agreement by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* responsible for the supervision of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* that technical conditions for settlement of transactions concluded on the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* through a settlement system other than that designated by the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* are such as to allow the smooth and orderly functioning of financial markets.

That *[[2014L0065_EN.4#2.|assessment]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(21)|regulated market]]* shall be without prejudice to the competencies of the national central banks as overseers of settlement systems or other supervisory authorities with competence in relation to such systems. The *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* shall take into account the oversight/supervision already exercised by those institutions in order to avoid undue duplication of control.
