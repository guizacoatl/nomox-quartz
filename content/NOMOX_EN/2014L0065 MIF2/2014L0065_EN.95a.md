# Article 95a - Transitional provision on the authorisation of credit institution referred to in point (1)(b) of Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013


Competent authorities shall inform the *[[2014L0065_EN.4#(26)|competent authority]]* referred to in [[2013L0036_EN.8|Article 8 of Directive 2013/36/EU]] where the envisaged total assets of an undertaking which has applied for authorisation pursuant to Title II of this Directive before 25 December 2019 in order to carry out the activities referred to in points (3) and (6) of Section A of Annex I are equal to or exceed EUR 30 billion, and notify the applicant thereof.
