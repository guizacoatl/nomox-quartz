# Article 3 - Obligations of CCPs

## 1.
A CCP shall open and maintain any of the accounts referred to in [[2017R2154_EN.4#4|Article 4(4)]] in accordance with the request of the clearing member.
## 2.
A CCP that holds the assets and positions of several indirect clients in an account as referred to in [[2017R2154_EN.4#4#b|Article 4(4)(b)]] shall keep separate records of the positions of each indirect client, calculate the margins in respect of each indirect client and collect the sum of those margins on a gross basis, based on the information referred to in [[2017R2154_EN.4#3|Article 4(3)]].
## 3.
A CCP shall identify, monitor and manage any material risks arising from the provision of indirect clearing services that could affect the resilience of the CCP to adverse market developments.
