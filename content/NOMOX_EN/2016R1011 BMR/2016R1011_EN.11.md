> Level 2 reference(s): [[2018R1638_EN.0]]# Article 11 - Input data

## 1.
The *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(5)|provision of a benchmark]]* shall be governed by the following requirements in respect of its *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*:
### (a)
the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* shall be sufficient to represent accurately and reliably the market or economic reality that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is intended to measure.

The *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* shall be *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(15)|transaction data]]*, if available and appropriate. If *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(15)|transaction data]]* is not sufficient or is not appropriate to represent accurately and reliably the market or economic reality that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is intended to measure, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* which is not *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(15)|transaction data]]* may be used, including estimated prices, quotes and committed quotes, or other values;
### (b)
the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* referred to in point (a) shall be verifiable;
### (c)
the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall draw up and publish clear guidelines regarding the types of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*, the priority of use of the different types of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* and the exercise of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(13)|expert judgement]]*, to ensure compliance with point (a) and the methodology;
### (d)
where a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is based on *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* from *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]*, the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall obtain, where appropriate, the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* from a reliable and representative panel or sample of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* so as to ensure that the resulting *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is reliable and representative of the market or economic reality that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is intended to measure;
### (e)
the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall not use *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* from a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributor]]* if the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* has any indication that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributor]]* does not adhere to the code of conduct referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.15|Article 15]], and in such a case shall obtain representative publicly available data.
## 2.
*[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|Administrators]]* shall ensure that their controls in respect of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* include:
### (a)
criteria that determine who may contribute *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* to the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* and a process for selecting *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]*;
### (b)
a process for evaluating a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributor]]*'s *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* and for stopping the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributor]]* from providing further *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*, or applying other penalties for non-compliance against the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributor]]*, where appropriate; and
### (c)
a process for validating *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*, including against other indicators or data, to ensure its integrity and accuracy.
## 3.
Where the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is contributed from a front office function, meaning any department, division, group, or personnel of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* or any of its affiliates that performs any pricing, trading, sales, marketing, advertising, solicitation, structuring, or brokerage activities, the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall:
### (a)
obtain data from other sources that corroborate that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*; and
### (b)
ensure that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* have in place adequate internal oversight and verification procedures.
## 4.
Where an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* considers that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* does not represent the market or economic reality that a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is intended to measure, that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall, within a reasonable time period, either change the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*, the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* or the methodology in order to ensure that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* does represent such market or economic reality, or else cease to provide that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*.
## 5.
ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards to specify further how to ensure that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* is appropriate and verifiable, as required under points (a) and (b) of paragraph 1, as well as the internal oversight and verification procedures of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributor]]* that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* has to ensure are in place, in compliance with point (b) of paragraph 3, in order to ensure the integrity and accuracy of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*. However, the ESMA draft regulatory technical standards shall not cover or apply to *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]* of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(27)|non-significant benchmarks]]*.

ESMA shall take into account the different types of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* and sectors as set out in this Regulation, the nature of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*, the characteristics of the underlying market or economic reality and the principle of proportionality, the vulnerability of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* to manipulation as well as the international convergence of supervisory practice in relation to *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]*.

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 1 April 2017.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with the procedure laid down in Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
## 6.
ESMA may issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]], addressed to *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]* of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(27)|non-significant benchmarks]]* to specify the elements referred to in paragraph 5 of this Article.
