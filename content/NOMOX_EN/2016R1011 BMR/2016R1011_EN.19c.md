# Article 19c - Exclusions for EU Paris-aligned Benchmarks

## 1.
The Commission is empowered to adopt a delegated act in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.49|Article 49]] in order to supplement this Regulation by identifying, in respect of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23b)|EU Paris-aligned Benchmarks]]*, the sectors to be excluded because they do not have measurable carbon emission reduction targets with specific deadlines that are aligned with the objectives of the Paris Agreement. The Commission shall adopt that delegated act by 1 January 2021 and update it every three years.
## 2.
When drawing up the delegated act referred to in paragraph 1, the Commission shall take into account the work of the TEG.
