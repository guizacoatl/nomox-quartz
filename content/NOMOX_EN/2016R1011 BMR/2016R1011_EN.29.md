# Article 29 - Use of a benchmark

## 1.
A *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(17)|supervised entity]]* may use a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or a combination of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* in the Union if the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is provided by an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(28)|located]]* in the Union and included in the register referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.36|Article 36]] or is a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* which is included in the register referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.36|Article 36]].
## 1a.
A *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(17)|supervised entity]]* may also use the replacement for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* designated in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.23b|Article 23b]] or [[2016R1011_EN.23c|Article 23c]].
## 2.
Where the object of a prospectus to be published under Directive 2003/71/EC or Directive 2009/65/EC is transferable securities or other investment products that reference a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, the issuer, offeror, or person asking for admission to trade on a regulated market shall ensure that the prospectus also includes clear and prominent information stating whether the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is provided by an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* included in the register referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.36|Article 36]] of this Regulation.
