# Article 8 - Record-keeping requirements

## 1.
An *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall keep records of:
### (a)
all *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*, including the use of such data;
### (b)
the methodology used for the determination of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*;
### (c)
any exercise of judgement or discretion by the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* and, where applicable, by *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(12)|assessors]]*, in the determination of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, including the reasoning for said judgement or discretion;
### (d)
the disregard of any *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*, in particular where it conformed to the requirements of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* methodology, and the rationale for such disregard;
### (e)
other changes in or deviations from standard procedures and methodologies, including those made during periods of market stress or disruption;
### (f)
the identities of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(11)|submitters]]* and of the natural persons employed by the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* for the determination of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*;
### (g)
all documents relating to any complaint, including those submitted by a complainant; and
### (h)
telephone conversations or electronic communications between any person employed by the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* and *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(11)|submitters]]* in respect of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*.
## 2.
An *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall keep the records set out in paragraph 1 for at least five years in such a form that it is possible to replicate and fully understand the determination of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* and enable an audit or evaluation of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*, calculations, judgements and discretion. Records of telephone conversation or electronic communications recorded in accordance with point (h) of paragraph 1 shall be provided to the persons involved in the conversation or communication upon request and shall be kept for a period of three years.
