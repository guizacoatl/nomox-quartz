# Article 28 - Changes to and cessation of a benchmark

## 1.
An *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall publish, together with the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* statement referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.27|Article 27]], a procedure concerning the actions to be taken by the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* in the event of changes to or the cessation of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* which may be used in the Union in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.29#1|Article 29(1)]]. The procedure may be drafted, where applicable, for families of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* and shall be updated and published whenever a material change occurs.
## 2.
Supervised entities other than an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* as referred to in paragraph 1 that use a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* shall produce and maintain robust written plans setting out the actions that they would take in the event that a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* materially changes or ceases to be provided. Where feasible and appropriate, such plans shall designate one or several alternative *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* that could be referenced to substitute the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* that would no longer be provided, indicating the reasons for the suitability of such alternative *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]*. The supervised entities shall, upon request and without undue delay, provide the relevant competent authority with those plans and any updates and shall reflect them in their contractual relationship with clients.
