# Article 23b - Replacement of a benchmark by Union law

## 1.
This Article shall apply to:
### (a)
*[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* designated as critical by an implementing act adopted pursuant to point (a) or (c) of [[2016R1011_EN.20#1|Article 20(1)]];
### (b)
*[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* based on the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(8)|contribution of input data]]* if their cessation or wind-down would significantly disrupt the functioning of financial markets in the Union; and
### (c)
third-country *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* if their cessation or wind-down would significantly disrupt the functioning of financial markets in the Union or pose a systemic risk to the financial system in the Union.
## 2.
The Commission may designate one or more replacements for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* provided that any of the following events has occurred:
### (a)
the competent authority for the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* has issued a public statement, or has published information, in which it is announced that that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* no longer reflects the underlying market or economic reality; in the case of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* designated as critical by an implementing act adopted pursuant to point (a) or (c) of [[2016R1011_EN.20#1|Article 20(1)]], the competent authority shall make such an announcement only where, following the exercise of the powers set out in [[2016R1011_EN.23|Article 23]], the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* still does not reflect the underlying market or economic reality;
### (b)
the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, or a person acting on behalf of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*, has issued a public statement, or has published information, or such public statement has been made or such information has been published, in which it is announced that that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* will commence the orderly wind-down of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or will cease to provide that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or certain tenors or certain currencies for which that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is calculated permanently or indefinitely, provided that, at the time of the issuance of the statement or the publication of the information, there is no successor *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* that will continue to provide that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*;
### (c)
the competent authority for the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or any entity with insolvency or resolution authority over such *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* has issued a public statement, or has published information, in which it is stated that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* will commence the orderly wind-down of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or will cease to provide that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or certain tenors or certain currencies for which that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is calculated permanently or indefinitely, provided that, at the time of the issuance of the statement or the publication of the information, there is no successor *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* that will continue to provide that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*; or
### (d)
the competent authority for the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* withdraws or suspends the authorisation in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.35|Article 35]] or the recognition in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.32#8|Article 32(8)]] or requires the cessation of the endorsement in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.33#6|Article 33(6)]], provided that, at the time of the withdrawal or suspension or the cessation of endorsement, there is no successor *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* that will continue to provide that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* and its *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* will commence the orderly wind-down of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or will cease to provide that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or certain tenors or certain currencies for which that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is calculated permanently or indefinitely.
## 3.
For the purposes of paragraph 2 of this Article, the replacement for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* shall replace all references to that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in contracts and *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instruments]]* as referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.23a|Article 23a]] where those contracts and *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instruments]]* contain:
### (a)
no fallback provision; or
### (b)
no suitable fallback provisions.
## 4.
For the purpose of point (b) of paragraph 3, a fallback provision shall be deemed unsuitable if:
### (a)
it does not provide for a permanent replacement for the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in cessation; or
### (b)
its application requires consent from third parties that has been denied; or
### (c)
it provides for a replacement for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* which no longer reflects or significantly diverges from the underlying market or the economic reality that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in cessation is intended to measure, and its application could have an adverse impact on financial stability.
## 5.
The replacement for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* agreed as a contractual fallback rate no longer reflects or significantly diverges from the underlying market or the economic reality that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in cessation is intended to measure, and could have an adverse impact on financial stability, where:
### (a)
that has been established by the relevant national authority on the basis of a horizontal assessment of a specific type of contractual arrangement that has been performed following a motivated request of at least one interested party, and after having consulted the relevant stakeholders;
### (b)
following an assessment in accordance with point (a), one of the parties to the contract or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instrument]]* has objected to the contractually agreed fallback provision at the latest three months before the cessation of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*; and
### (c)
following an objection pursuant to point (b), the parties to the contract or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instrument]]* have not agreed on an alternative replacement for the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* at the latest one working day before the cessation of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*.
## 6.
For the purposes of point (c) of paragraph 4, the relevant national authority shall, without undue delay, inform the Commission and ESMA of its assessment referred to in point (a) of paragraph 5. Where entities in more than one Member State could be affected by the assessment, the relevant authorities of all those Member States shall conduct the assessment jointly.
## 7.
Member States shall designate a relevant authority that is in the position to conduct the assessment referred to in point (a) of paragraph 5. Member States shall inform the Commission and ESMA of the designation of the relevant authorities by 14 August 2021.
## 8.
The Commission shall adopt implementing acts to designate one or more replacements for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in accordance with the examination procedure referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.50#2|Article 50(2)]] where any of the events referred to in paragraph 2 of this Article have occurred.
## 9.
An implementing act as referred to in paragraph 8 shall include the following:
### (a)
the replacement or replacements for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*;
### (b)
the spread adjustment, including the method for determining such spread adjustment, that is to be applied to the replacement for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in cessation on the date of the replacement for each particular term to account for the effects of the transition or change from the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* to be wound down to its replacement;
### (c)
the corresponding essential conforming changes that are associated with and reasonably necessary for the use or application of a replacement for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*; and
### (d)
the date from which the replacement or replacements for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* applies.
## 10.
When adopting an implementing act as referred to in paragraph 8, the Commission shall take into account available recommendations on the replacement for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, the corresponding conforming changes and the spread adjustment made by the central bank responsible for the currency area in which the relevant *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is being wound down, or by the alternative reference rate working group operating under the auspices of the public authorities or the central bank. Before adopting the implementing act, the Commission shall conduct a public consultation and shall take into account the recommendations of other relevant stakeholders, including the competent authority of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* and ESMA.
## 11.
Notwithstanding point (c) of paragraph (5) of this Article, a replacement for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* designated by the Commission in accordance with paragraph 2 of this Article shall not apply where all parties or the required majority of parties to a contract or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instrument]]* referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.23a|Article 23a]] have agreed to apply a different replacement for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* whether before or after the date of application of the implementing act referred to in paragraph 8 of this Article.
