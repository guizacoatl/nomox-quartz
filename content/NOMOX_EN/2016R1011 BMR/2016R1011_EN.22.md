# Article 22 - Mitigation of market power of critical benchmark administrators


Without prejudice to the application of Union competition law, when providing a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]*, the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall take adequate steps to ensure that licences of, and information relating to, the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* are provided to all users on a fair, reasonable, transparent and non-discriminatory basis.
