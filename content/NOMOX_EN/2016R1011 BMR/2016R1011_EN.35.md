# Article 35 - Withdrawal or suspension of authorisation or registration

## 1.
A competent authority may withdraw or suspend the authorisation or registration of an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* where the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*:
### (a)
expressly renounces the authorisation or registration or has provided no *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* for the preceding 12 months;
### (b)
has obtained the authorisation or registration, or has endorsed a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, by making false statements or by any other irregular means;
### (c)
no longer meets the conditions under which it was authorised or registered; or
### (d)
has seriously or repeatedly infringed the provisions of this Regulation.
## 2.
The competent authority shall notify ESMA of its decision within five working days of the adoption of said decision.

ESMA shall promptly update the register provided for in [[2016R1011_EN.36|Article 36]].
## 3.
Following the adoption of a decision to suspend the authorisation or registration of an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*, and where cessation of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* would result in a force majeure event, or frustrate or otherwise breach the terms of any *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(18)|financial contract]]* or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instrument]]*, or the rules of any *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(19)|investment fund]]*, which references that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, as specified in the delegated act adopted pursuant to [[2016R1011_EN.51#6|Article 51(6)]], the provision of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in question may be permitted by the relevant competent authority of the Member State where the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* is *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(28)|located]]* until the decision of suspension has been withdrawn. During that period of time, the use of such *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* by supervised entities shall be permitted only for *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(18)|financial contracts]]*, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instruments]]* and *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(19)|investment funds]]* that already reference the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*.
## 4.
Following the adoption of a decision to withdraw the authorisation or registration of an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*, [[2016R1011_EN.28#2|Article 28(2)]] shall apply.
