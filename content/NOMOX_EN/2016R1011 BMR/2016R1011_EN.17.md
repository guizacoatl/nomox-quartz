# Article 17 - Regulated-data benchmarks

## 1.
[[2016R1011_EN.11#1#d|Article 11(1)(d)]] and (e), [[2016R1011_EN.11#2|Article 11(2)]] and (3), [[2016R1011_EN.14#1|Article 14(1)]] and (2), and Articles [[2016R1011_EN.15|15]] and [[2016R1011_EN.16|16]] shall not apply to the provision of and the contribution to *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(24)|regulated-data benchmarks]]*. [[2016R1011_EN.8#1#a|Article 8(1)(a)]] shall not apply to the provision of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(24)|regulated-data benchmarks]]* with reference to *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* that are contributed entirely and directly as specified in point (24) of [[2016R1011_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]].
## 2.
Articles [[2016R1011_EN.24|24]] and [[2016R1011_EN.25|25]] or [[2016R1011_EN.26|Article 26]] shall, as applicable, apply to the provision of, and the contribution to, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(24)|regulated-data benchmarks]]* that are used directly or indirectly within a combination of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* as a reference for *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instruments]]* or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(18)|financial contracts]]* or for measuring the performance of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(19)|investment funds]]*, having a total value of up to EUR 500 billion, on the basis of all the range of maturities or tenors of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, where applicable.
