# Article 55 - Notification of benchmarks referenced and their administrators


When a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is referenced in a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instrument]]* covered by [[2014R0596_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 596/2014]], the notifications under Article 4(1) of that Regulation shall include the name of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* referenced and its *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*.
