# Article 2 - Specific disclosure requirements for regulated-data benchmarks


In addition to the information to be included pursuant to [[2018R1643_EN.1|Article 1]], for a regulated-data benchmark or, where applicable, family of regulated-data benchmarks, the benchmark statement shall state at least the following in its description of the input data:
### (a)
the sources of the input data used;
### (b)
for each source, the relevant type, as listed in Article 3(1)(24) of Regulation (EU) 2016/1011.
