# Article 36 - Register of administrators and benchmarks

## 1.
ESMA shall establish and maintain a public register that contains the following information:
### (a)
the identities of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]* authorised or registered pursuant to [[2016R1011_EN.34|Article 34]] and the competent authorities responsible for the supervision thereof;
### (b)
the identities of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]* that comply with the conditions laid down in [[2016R1011_EN.30#1|Article 30(1)]], the list of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* referred to in point (c) of [[2016R1011_EN.30#1|Article 30(1)]] and the third country competent authorities responsible for the supervision thereof;
### (c)
the identities of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]* that acquired recognition in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.32|Article 32]], the list of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.32#7|Article 32(7)]] and, where applicable, the third country competent authorities responsible for the supervision thereof;
### (d)
the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* that are endorsed in accordance with the procedure laid down in [[2016R1011_EN.33|Article 33]], the identities of their *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]*, and the identities of the endorsing *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]* or endorsing supervised entities.
## 2.
The register referred to in paragraph 1 shall be publicly accessible on the website of ESMA and shall be updated promptly, as necessary.
