# Article 48a - Exercise of the powers by ESMA


The powers conferred on ESMA, on any official of ESMA or on any other person authorised by ESMA by Articles [[2016R1011_EN.48b|48b]] to 48d shall not be used to require the disclosure of information or documents that are subject to legal privilege.
