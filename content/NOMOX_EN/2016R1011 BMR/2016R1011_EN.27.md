## 1.
Within two weeks of the inclusion of an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* in the register referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.36|Article 36]], the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall publish, by means that ensure fair and easy access, a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* statement for each *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or, where applicable, for each *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(4)|family of benchmarks]]*, that may be used in the Union in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.29|Article 29]].

Where that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* begins providing a new *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(4)|family of benchmarks]]* that may be used in the Union in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.29|Article 29]], the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall publish, within two weeks and by means that ensure a fair and easy access, a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* statement for each new *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or, where applicable, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(4)|family of benchmarks]]*.

The *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall review and, where necessary, update the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* statement for each *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(4)|family of benchmarks]]* in the event of any changes to the information to be provided under this Article and at least every two years.

The *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* statement shall:
### (a)
clearly and unambiguously define the market or economic reality measured by the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* and the circumstances in which such measurement may become unreliable;
### (b)
lay down technical specifications that clearly and unambiguously identify the elements of the calculation of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in relation to which discretion may be exercised, the criteria applicable to the exercise of such discretion and the position of the persons that can exercise discretion, and how such discretion may be subsequently evaluated;
### (c)
provide notice of the possibility that factors, including external factors beyond the control of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*, may necessitate changes to, or the cessation of, the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*; and
### (d)
advise users that changes to, or the cessation of, the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* may have an impact upon the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(18)|financial contracts]]* and *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instruments]]* that reference the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or the measurement of the performance of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(19)|investment funds]]*.
## 2.
A *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* statement shall contain at least:
### (a)
the definitions for all key terms relating to the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*;
### (b)
the rationale for adopting the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* methodology and procedures for the review and approval of the methodology;
### (c)
the criteria and procedures used to determine the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, including a description of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*, the priority given to different types of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*, the minimum data needed to determine a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, the use of any models or methods of extrapolation and any procedure for rebalancing the constituents of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*'s *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(1)|index]]*;
### (d)
the controls and rules that govern any exercise of judgement or discretion by the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* or any *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]*, to ensure consistency in the use of such judgement or discretion;
### (e)
the procedures which govern the determination of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in periods of stress or periods where *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(15)|transaction data]]* sources may be insufficient, inaccurate or unreliable and the potential limitations of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in such periods;
### (f)
the procedures for dealing with errors in *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* or in the determination of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, including when a re-determination of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is required; and
### (g)
the identification of potential limitations of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, including its operation in illiquid or fragmented markets and the possible concentration of inputs.
## 2a.
By 30 April 2020, for each of the requirements referred to in paragraph 2, the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* statement shall contain an explanation of how ESG factors are reflected in each *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(4)|family of benchmarks]]* provided and published. For those *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* or families of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* that do not pursue ESG objectives, it shall be sufficient for *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]* to clearly state in the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* statement that they do not pursue such objectives.
Where no *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23a)|EU Climate Transition Benchmark]]* or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23b)|EU Paris-aligned Benchmark]]* is available in the portfolio of that individual *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*, or the individual *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* has no *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* that pursue ESG objectives or take into account ESG factors, this shall be stated in the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* statements of all *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* provided by that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*. For significant equity and bond *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]*, as well as for *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23a)|EU Climate Transition Benchmarks]]* and *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23b)|EU Paris-aligned Benchmarks]]*, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]* shall disclose in their *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* statements details on whether or not and to what extent a degree of overall alignment with the target of reducing carbon emissions or the attainment of the objectives of the Paris Agreement is ensured in accordance with the disclosure rules for financial products in [[2019R2088_EN.9#3|Article 9(3) of Regulation (EU) 2088/2019]] of the European Parliament and of the Council.

By 31 December 2021, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]* shall, for each *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or, where applicable, each *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(4)|family of benchmarks]]*, with the exception of interest rate and foreign exchange *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]*, include in their *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* statement an explanation of how their methodology aligns with the target of carbon emission reductions or attains the objectives of the Paris Agreement.
## 2b.
The Commission is empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.49|Article 49]] to supplement this Regulation by further specifying the information to be provided in the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* statement pursuant to paragraph 2a of this Article, as well as the standard format to be used for references to ESG factors to enable market participants to make well-informed choices and to ensure the technical feasibility of compliance with that paragraph.
## 3.
ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards to specify further the contents of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* statement and the cases in which an update of such statement is required.

ESMA shall distinguish between the different types of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* and sectors as set out in this Regulation and shall take into account the principle of proportionality.

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 1 April 2017.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with the procedure laid down in Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
