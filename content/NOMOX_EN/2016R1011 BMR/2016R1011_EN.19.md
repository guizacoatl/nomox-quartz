# Article 19 - Commodity benchmarks

## 1.
The specific requirements laid down in Annex II shall apply instead of the requirements of Title II, with the exception of [[2016R1011_EN.10|Article 10]], to the provision of, and contribution to, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23)|commodity benchmarks]]*, unless the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in question is a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(24)|regulated-data benchmark]]* or is based on submissions by *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* the majority of which are supervised entities.

Articles [[2016R1011_EN.24|24]], [[2016R1011_EN.25|25]] and [[2016R1011_EN.26|26]] shall not apply to the provision of, and contribution to, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23)|commodity benchmarks]]*.
## 2.
Where a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23)|commodity benchmark]]* is a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]* and the underlying asset is gold, silver or platinum, the requirements of Title II shall apply instead of Annex II.
