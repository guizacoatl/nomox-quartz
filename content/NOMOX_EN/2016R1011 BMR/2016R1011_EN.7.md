# Article 7 - Accountability framework requirements

## 1.
An *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall have in place an accountability framework, covering record-keeping, auditing and review, and a complaints process, that provides evidence of compliance with the requirements of this Regulation.
## 2.
An *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall designate an internal function with the necessary capability to review and report on the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*'s compliance with the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* methodology and this Regulation.
## 3.
For *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmarks]]*, an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall appoint an independent external auditor to review and report on the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*'s compliance with the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* methodology and this Regulation, at least annually.
## 4.
Upon the request of the relevant competent authority, an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall provide to the relevant competent authority the details of the reviews and reports provided for in paragraph 2. Upon the request of the relevant competent authority or any user of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall publish the details of the audits provided for in paragraph 3.
