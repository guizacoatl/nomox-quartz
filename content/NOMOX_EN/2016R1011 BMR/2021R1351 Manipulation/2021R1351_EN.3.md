# Article 3 - Input data integrity policy


The adequate systems and effective controls referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.14#1|Article 14(1) of Regulation (EU) 1011/2016]] shall be documented in an input data integrity policy indicating:
### (a)
the risk of benchmark manipulation;
### (b)
a general description of the adequate systems and effective controls, including their compliance with the requirements laid down in [[2021R1351_EN.1|Article 1]];
### (c)
a general description of the training of the employees of the administrator involved in the operation of the adequate systems and effective controls, as referred to in [[2021R1351_EN.2|Article 2]];
### (d)
the name and contact details of the persons responsible for the adequate systems and effective controls.
