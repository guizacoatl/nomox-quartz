# Article 19a - EU Climate Transition Benchmarks and EU Paris-aligned Benchmarks

## 1.
The requirements laid down in Annex III shall apply to the provision of, and contribution to, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23a)|EU Climate Transition Benchmarks]]* and *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23b)|EU Paris-aligned Benchmarks]]*, in addition to the requirements of Titles II, III and IV.
## 2.
The Commission is empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.49|Article 49]] to supplement this Regulation by laying down the minimum standards for *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23a)|EU Climate Transition Benchmarks]]* and *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23b)|EU Paris-aligned Benchmarks]]* to specify:
### (a)
the criteria for the choice of the underlying assets, including, where applicable, any criteria for excluding assets;
### (b)
the criteria and method for the weighting of the underlying assets in the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*;
### (c)
the determination of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23c)|decarbonisation trajectory]]* for *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23a)|EU Climate Transition Benchmarks]]*.
## 3.
*[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|Benchmark]]* *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]* which provide an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23a)|EU Climate Transition Benchmark]]* or an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23b)|EU Paris-aligned Benchmark]]* shall comply with this Regulation by 30 April 2020.
