# Article 57 - Amendments to Directive 2008/48/EC


Directive 2008/48/EC is amended as follows:
## (1)
In [[2016R1011_EN.5#1|Article 5(1)]], the following subparagraph is inserted after the second subparagraph:
‘Where the credit agreement references a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* as defined in point 3 of [[2016R1011_EN.3#1|Article 3(1) of Regulation (EU) 1011/2016]] of the European Parliament and of the Council, the name of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* and of its *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* and the potential implications on the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(21)|consumer]]* shall be provided by the creditor, or where applicable, by the credit intermediary, to the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(21)|consumer]]* in a separate document, which may be annexed to the Standard European *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(21)|Consumer]]* Credit Information form.
## (2)
In [[2016R1011_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]], the following subparagraph is inserted after the second subparagraph:

‘By 1 July 2018 Member States shall adopt and publish the provisions necessary to comply with the third subparagraph of [[2016R1011_EN.5#1|Article 5(1)]] and shall communicate them to the Commission. They shall apply those provisions from 1 July 2018.’.
