# Article 9 - Complaints-handling mechanism

## 1.
An *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall have in place and publish procedures for receiving, investigating and retaining records concerning complaints made, including about the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*'s *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* determination process.
## 2.
Such a complaints-handling mechanism shall ensure that:
### (a)
the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* makes available the complaints-handling policy through which complaints may be submitted on whether a specific *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* determination is representative of market value, on a proposed change to the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* determination process, on an application of the methodology in relation to a specific *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* determination, and on other decisions in relation to the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* determination process;
### (b)
complaints are investigated in a timely and fair manner and the outcome of the investigation is communicated to the complainant within a reasonable period of time, unless such communication would be contrary to objectives of public policy or to Regulation (EU) No 596/2014; and
### (c)
the inquiry is conducted independently of any personnel who may be or may have been involved in the subject-matter of the complaint.
