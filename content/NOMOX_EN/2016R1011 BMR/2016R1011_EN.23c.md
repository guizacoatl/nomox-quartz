# Article 23c - Replacement of a benchmark by national law

## 1.
The national competent authority of a Member State where the majority of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* is *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(28)|located]]* may designate one or more replacements for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* as referred to in point (b) of [[2016R1011_EN.20#1|Article 20(1)]], provided that any of the following events has occurred:
### (a)
the competent authority for the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* has issued a public statement, or has published information, in which it is announced that that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* no longer reflects the underlying market or economic reality; the competent authority shall make such an announcement only where, following the exercise of the powers set out in [[2016R1011_EN.23|Article 23]], the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* still does not reflect the underlying market or economic reality;
### (b)
the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, or a person acting on behalf of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*, has issued a public statement, or has published information, or such public statement has been made or such information has been published, in which it is announced that that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* will commence the orderly wind-down of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or will cease to provide that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or certain tenors or certain currencies for which that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is calculated permanently or indefinitely, provided that, at the time of the issuance of the statement or the publication of the information, there is no successor *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* that will continue to provide that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*;
### (c)
the competent authority for the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or any entity with insolvency or resolution authority over such *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* has issued a public statement, or has published information, in which it is stated that that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* will commence the orderly wind-down of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or will cease to provide that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or certain tenors or certain currencies for which that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is calculated permanently or indefinitely, provided that, at the time of the issuance of the statement or the publication of the information, there is no successor *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* that will continue to provide that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*; or
### (d)
the competent authority for the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* withdraws or suspends the authorisation in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.35|Article 35]], provided that, at the time of the withdrawal or suspension, there is no successor *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* that will continue to provide that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* and its *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* will commence the orderly wind-down of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or will cease to provide that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or certain tenors or certain currencies for which that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is calculated permanently or indefinitely.
## 2.
Where a Member State designates one or more replacements for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in accordance with paragraph 1, the competent authority of that Member State shall immediately notify the Commission and ESMA thereof.
## 3.
The replacement for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* shall replace all references to that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in contracts and *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instruments]]* as referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.23a|Article 23a]] where both of the following conditions are fulfilled:
### (a)
those contracts or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instruments]]* reference the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in cessation on the date on which the national law designating the replacement for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* becomes applicable; and
### (b)
those contracts or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instruments]]* contain no fallback provision or contain a fallback provision that does not provide for a permanent replacement for the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* in cessation.
## 4.
A replacement for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* designated by a competent authority in accordance with paragraph 1 of this Article shall not apply where all parties or the required majority of the parties to a contract or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instrument]]* as referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.23a|Article 23a]] have agreed to apply a different replacement for a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* whether before or after the date of application of the relevant provision of national law.
