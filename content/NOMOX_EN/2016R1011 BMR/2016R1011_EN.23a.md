# Article 23a - Scope of the statutory replacement of a benchmark


This Chapter applies to:
### (a)
any contract, or any *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instrument]]* as defined in Directive 2014/65/EU, that references a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* and is subject to the law of one of the Member States; and
### (b)
any contract, the parties to which are all established in the Union, that references a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* and that is subject to the law of a third country and where that law does not provide for the orderly wind-down of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*.
