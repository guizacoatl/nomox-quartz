# Article 24 - Significant benchmarks

## 1.
A *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* which does not fulfil any of the conditions laid down in [[2016R1011_EN.20#1|Article 20(1)]] is significant when:
### (a)
it is used directly or indirectly within a combination of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* as a reference for *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instruments]]* or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(18)|financial contracts]]* or for measuring the performance of investments funds having a total average value of at least EUR 50 billion on the basis of all the range of maturities or tenors of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, where applicable, over a period of six months; or
### (b)
it has no or very few appropriate market-led substitutes and, in the event that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* ceases to be provided or is provided on the basis of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* no longer fully representative of the underlying market or economic reality or unreliable *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*, there would be a significant and adverse impact on market integrity, financial stability, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(21)|consumers]]*, the real economy or the financing of households or businesses in one or more Member States.
## 2.
The Commission shall be empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.49|Article 49]] in order to review the calculation method used to determine the threshold referred to in point (a) of paragraph 1 of this Article in the light of market, price and regulatory developments as well as the appropriateness of the classification of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* with a total value of *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instruments]]*, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(18)|financial contracts]]* or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(19)|investment funds]]* referencing them that is close to that threshold. Such review shall take place at least every two years as from 1 January 2018.
## 3.
An *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall immediately notify its competent authority when its *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(26)|significant benchmark]]* falls below the threshold mentioned in point (a) of paragraph 1.
