# Article 1 - Scope


This Regulation does not cover or apply to supervised contributors which contribute data only for non-significant benchmarks.

The requirements imposed under this Regulation are without prejudice to those imposed under Articles [[2018R1640_EN.11|11]] and [[2018R1640_EN.15|15]] of the Regulation (EU) 2016/1011 and the regulatory technical standards adopted under Article 11(5) and [[2016R1011_EN.15#6|Article 15(6) of Regulation (EU) 1011/2016]]
