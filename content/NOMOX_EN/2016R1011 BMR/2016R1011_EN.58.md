# Article 58 - Amendments to Directive 2014/17/EU


Directive 2014/17/EU is amended as follows:
## (1)
In the second subparagraph of [[2016R1011_EN.13#1|Article 13(1)]], the following point is inserted:
where contracts that reference a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* as defined in point (3) of [[2016R1011_EN.3#1|Article 3(1) of Regulation (EU) 1011/2016]] of the European Parliament and of the Council are available, the names of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* and of their *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]* and the potential implications on the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(21)|consumer]]*;
## (2)
In [[2016R1011_EN.42#2|Article 42(2)]], the following subparagraph is inserted after the first subparagraph:

‘By 1 July 2018, Member States shall adopt and publish the provisions necessary to comply with point (ea) of the second subparagraph of [[2016R1011_EN.13#1|Article 13(1)]] and shall communicate them to the Commission. They shall apply those provisions from 1 July 2018.’;
## (3)
In [[2016R1011_EN.43#1|Article 43(1)]], the following subparagraph is added:

‘Point (ea) of the second subparagraph of [[2016R1011_EN.13#1|Article 13(1)]] shall not apply to credit agreements existing before 1 July 2018.’.
