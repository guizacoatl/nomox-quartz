# Article 19d - Endeavour to provide EU Climate Transition Benchmarks


By 1 January 2022, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]* which are *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(28)|located]]* in the Union and which provide *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(26)|significant benchmarks]]* determined on the basis of the value of one or more underlying assets or prices shall endeavour to provide one or more *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(23a)|EU Climate Transition Benchmarks]]*.
