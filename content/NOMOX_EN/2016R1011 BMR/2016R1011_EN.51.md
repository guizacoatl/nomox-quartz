# Article 51 - Transitional provisions

## 1.
An *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]* providing a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* on 30 June 2016 shall apply for authorisation or registration in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.34|Article 34]] by 1 January 2020.
## 2.
By 1 January 2020, the competent authority of the Member State where an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]* applying for authorisation in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.34|Article 34]] is *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(28)|located]]* shall have the power to decide to register that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]* as an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* even if it is not a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(17)|supervised entity]]*, under the following conditions:
### (a)
the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]* does not provide a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]*;
### (b)
the competent authority is aware, on a reasonable basis, that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(1)|index]]* or indices provided by the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]* are not widely used, within the meaning of this Regulation, in the Member State where the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]* is *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(28)|located]]* as well as in other Member States.

The competent authority shall notify ESMA of its decision adopted in accordance with the first subparagraph.

The competent authority shall keep evidence of the reasons for its decision adopted in accordance with the first subparagraph, in such a form that it is possible to fully understand the evaluations of the competent authority that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(1)|index]]* or indices provided by the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]* are not widely used, including any market data, judgement or other information, as well as information received from the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]*.
## 3.
An *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]* may continue to provide an existing *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* which may be used by supervised entities until 1 January 2020 or, where the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]* submits an application for authorisation or registration in accordance with paragraph 1, unless and until such authorisation or registration is refused.
## 4.
Where an existing *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* does not meet the requirements of this Regulation, but ceasing or changing that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* to fulfil the requirements of this Regulation would result in a force majeure event, frustrate or otherwise breach the terms of any *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(18)|financial contract]]* or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instrument]]* or the rules of any *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(19)|investment fund]]*, which references that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*, the use of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* shall be permitted by the competent authority of the Member State where the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]* is *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(28)|located]]*. No *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instruments]]*, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(18)|financial contracts]]*, or measurements of the performance of an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(19)|investment fund]]* shall add a reference to such an existing *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* after 1 January 2020.
## 4a.
An *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]* may continue to provide an existing *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* that has been recognised as a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]* by an implementing act adopted by the Commission in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.20|Article 20]] until 31 December 2021 or, where the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]* submits an application for authorisation in accordance with paragraph 1, unless and until such authorisation is refused.
## 4b.
An existing *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* that has been recognised as a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]* by an implementing act adopted by the Commission in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.20|Article 20]] may be used for existing and new *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instruments]]*, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(18)|financial contracts]]*, or for measuring the performance of an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(19)|investment fund]]* until 31 December 2021 or, where the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(2)|index provider]]* submits an application for authorisation in accordance with paragraph 1, unless and until such authorisation is refused.
## 5.
Unless the Commission has adopted an equivalence decision as referred to in paragraph (2) or (3) of [[2016R1011_EN.30|Article 30]], an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* has been recognised pursuant to [[2016R1011_EN.32|Article 32]] or a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* has been endorsed pursuant to [[2016R1011_EN.33|Article 33]], the use in the Union by supervised entities of a third-country *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* shall be permitted only for *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instruments]]*, *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(18)|financial contracts]]* and measurements of the performance of an *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(19)|investment fund]]* that already reference that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* or which add a reference to such *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* before 31 December 2023.

The first subparagraph shall not apply to *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* provided by *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrators]]* who relocate from the Union to a third country during the transitional period. The competent authority shall notify ESMA in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.35|Article 35]]. ESMA shall draw up a list of third-country *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmarks]]* to which the first subparagraph does not apply.
## 6.
The Commission shall be empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.49|Article 49]] concerning measures to determine the conditions on which the relevant competent authority may assess whether the cessation or the changing of an existing *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* to conform with the requirements of this Regulation could reasonably result in a force majeure event, frustrate or otherwise breach the terms of any *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(18)|financial contract]]* or *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(16)|financial instrument]]* or the rules of any *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(19)|investment fund]]* which references such *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*.
