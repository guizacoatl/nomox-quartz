# Article 52 - Deadline for updating the prospectuses and key information documents


[[2016R1011_EN.29#2|Article 29(2)]] is without prejudice to outstanding prospectuses approved under Directive 2003/71/EC prior to 1 January 2018. For prospectuses approved prior to 1 January 2018 under Directive 2009/65/EC, the underlying documents shall be updated at the first occasion or at the latest within 12 months after that date.
