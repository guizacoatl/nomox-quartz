# Article 10 - Exclusions for EU Climate Transition Benchmarks

## 1.
Administrators of EU Climate Transition Benchmarks shall disclose in their methodology whether and how they exclude companies.
## 2.
By 31 December 2022, administrators of EU Climate Transition Benchmarks shall comply with the requirements set out in [[2020R1818_EN.12#1|Article 12(1)]], points (a), (b) and (c), and [[2020R1818_EN.12#2|Article 12(2)]].
