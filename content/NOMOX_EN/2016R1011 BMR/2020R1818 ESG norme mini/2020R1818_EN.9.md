# Article 9 - Baseline reduction of GHG intensity or absolute GHG emissions for EU Climate Transition Benchmarks


The GHG intensity or, where applicable, absolute GHG emissions for EU Climate Transition Benchmarks, including Scope 1, 2 and 3 GHG emissions, shall be at least 30 % lower than the GHG intensity or absolute GHG emissions of the investable universe.

For the purposes of the first subparagraph, Scope 3 GHG emissions shall be construed in accordance with the phase-in implementation period set out in [[2020R1818_EN.5|Article 5]].
