# Article 1 - General requirements

## 1.
An administrator located in a third country shall when applying for recognition pursuant to [[2016R1011_EN.32|Article 32 of Regulation (EU) 1011/2016]] provide the information listed in the Annex.
## 2.
Where the applicant has left out any of the required information, the application shall include an explanation as to why that information has not been provided.
