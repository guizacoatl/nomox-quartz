# Article 23 - Mandatory contribution to a critical benchmark

## 1.
This Article shall apply to *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmarks]]* based on submissions by *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* the majority of which are supervised entities.
## 2.
*[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|Administrators]]* of one or more *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmarks]]* shall, every two years, submit to their competent authority an assessment of the capability of each *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]* they provide to measure the underlying market or economic reality.
## 3.
A *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(10)|supervised contributor]]* to a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]* that intends to cease contributing *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* shall promptly notify the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* thereof in writing. The *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall thereupon inform without undue delay its competent authority.

The competent authority of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]* *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall inform the competent authority of that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(10)|supervised contributor]]*, and where applicable ESMA, thereof without undue delay. The *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall submit to its competent authority an assessment of the implications on the capability of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]* to measure the underlying market or economic reality, as soon as possible but no later than 14 days after the notification made by the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(10)|supervised contributor]]*.
## 4.
Upon receipt of the assessment referred to in paragraphs 2 and 3 of this Article, the competent authority of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall, where applicable, promptly inform ESMA or the college established under [[2016R1011_EN.46|Article 46]] and shall on the basis of that assessment make its own assessment of the capability of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* to measure the underlying market and economic reality, taking into account the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*’s procedure for cessation of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* established in accordance with [[2016R1011_EN.28#1|Article 28(1)]].
## 5.
From the date on which the competent authority of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* is notified of the intention of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributor]]* to cease contributing *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* and until such time as the assessment referred to in paragraph 4 is complete, it shall have the power to require the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* which made the notification in accordance with paragraph 3 to continue contributing *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]*, in any event for a period of no more than four weeks, without imposing an obligation on supervised entities to either trade or commit to trade.
## 6.
In the event that the competent authority, after the period specified in paragraph 5 and on the basis of its own assessment referred to in paragraph 4, considers that the representativeness of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]* is put at risk, it shall have the power to:
### (a)
require supervised entities selected in accordance with paragraph 7 of this Article, including entities that are not yet *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* to the relevant *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]*, to contribute *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* to the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* in accordance with the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*'s methodology, the code of conduct referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.15|Article 15]] and other rules. Such requirement shall be in place for an appropriate period of time not exceeding 12 months from the date on which the initial decision requiring mandatory contribution was taken pursuant to paragraph 5 or, for those entities that are not yet *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]*, from the date on which the decision requiring mandatory contribution is taken under this point;
### (b)
extend the period of mandatory contribution by an appropriate period of time not exceeding 12 months, following a review under paragraph 9 of any measures adopted pursuant to point (a) of this paragraph;
### (c)
determine the form in which, and the time by which, any *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* is to be contributed without imposing an obligation on supervised entities to either trade or commit to trade;
### (d)
require the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* to change the methodology, the code of conduct referred to in [[2016R1011_EN.15|Article 15]] or other rules of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]*.

The maximum period of mandatory contribution under points (a) and (b) of the first subparagraph shall not exceed five years.
## 7.
For the purposes of paragraph 6, supervised entities that are to be required to contribute *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* shall be selected by the competent authority of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]*, with the close cooperation of the competent authorities of the supervised entities, on the basis of the size of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(17)|supervised entity]]*'s actual and potential participation in the market that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* intends to measure.
## 8.
The competent authority of a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(10)|supervised contributor]]* that has been required to contribute to a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* through measures taken in accordance with point (a), (b) or (c) of paragraph 6 shall cooperate with the competent authority of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* in the enforcement of such measures.
## 9.
By the end of the period referred to in point (a) of the first subparagraph of paragraph 6, the competent authority of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall review the measures adopted under paragraph 6. It shall revoke any of them if it considers that:
### (a)
the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* are likely to continue contributing *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* for at least one year if the measure were revoked, which shall be evidenced by at least:
#### (i)
a written commitment by the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* to the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* and the competent authority to continue contributing *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* to the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]* for at least one year if the measure were revoked;
#### (ii)
a written report by the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* to the competent authority providing evidence for its assessment that the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]*'s continued viability can be assured once mandatory contribution has been revoked;
### (b)
the provision of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is able to continue once the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* mandated to contribute *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* have ceased contributing;
### (c)
an acceptable substitute *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is available and users of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]* can switch to this substitute at minimal costs which shall be evidenced by at least a written report by the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* detailing the means of transition to a substitute *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* and the ability and costs to users of transitioning to this *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*; or
### (d)
no appropriate alternative *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* can be identified and the cessation of contributions from the relevant supervised entities would weaken the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* to such an extent to require the cessation of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]*.
## 10.
In the event that a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(25)|critical benchmark]]* is to be ceased to be provided, each *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(10)|supervised contributor]]* to that *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* shall contribute *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* for a period of time determined by the competent authority, but not exceeding the maximum five year period laid down in the second subparagraph of paragraph 6.
## 11.
The *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall notify the relevant competent authority in the event that any *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(9)|contributors]]* breach the requirements set out in paragraph 6 as soon as reasonably possible.
## 12.
In the event that a *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(3)|benchmark]]* is recognised as critical in accordance with the procedure laid down in [[2016R1011_EN.20#2|Article 20(2)]], (3), (4) and (5), the competent authority of the *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(6)|administrator]]* shall have the power to require *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(14)|input data]]* in accordance with paragraph 5, and points (a), (b) and (c) of paragraph 6, of this Article only from *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(10)|supervised contributors]]* *[[2016R1011_EN.3#(28)|located]]* in its Member State.
