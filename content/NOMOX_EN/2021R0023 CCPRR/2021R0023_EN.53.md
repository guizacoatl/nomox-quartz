# Article 53 - Power in respect of assets, contracts, rights, liabilities, obligations and instruments of ownership of persons located in or governed by the law of third countries

## 1.
Where a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]* concerns assets or contracts of persons located in a third country or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*, rights, obligations or liabilities governed by the law of a third country, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may require that:
### (a)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution and the recipient of those assets, contracts, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*, rights, obligations or liabilities take all necessary steps to ensure that the action becomes effective;
### (b)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution holds the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*, assets or rights or discharges the liabilities or obligations on behalf of the recipient until the action becomes effective;
### (c)
the reasonable expenses of the recipient properly incurred in carrying out any action required under points (a) and (b) of this paragraph are reimbursed in any of the ways referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.27#10|Article 27(10)]].
## 2.
For the purposes of paragraph 1 of this Article, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to ensure the inclusion of a provision in its contracts and other agreements with *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* and holders of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]* located in or governed by the law of third countries by which they agree to be bound by any action in respect of their assets, contracts, rights, obligations and liabilities taken by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*, including the application of Articles [[2021R0023_EN.28|28]], [[2021R0023_EN.32|32]], [[2021R0023_EN.55|55]], [[2021R0023_EN.56|56]] and [[2021R0023_EN.57|57]].
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to ensure the inclusion of such a provision in its contracts and other agreements with holders of other liabilities located in or governed by the law of third countries. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to provide it with a reasoned legal opinion by an independent legal expert confirming the legal enforceability and effectiveness of such provisions.
## 3.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]* referred to in paragraph 1 does not become effective, that action shall be void in relation to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*, assets, rights, obligations or liabilities concerned.
