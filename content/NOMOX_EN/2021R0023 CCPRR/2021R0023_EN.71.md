# Article 71 - Decision of the resolution authority

## 1.
After a notification from the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.70#3|Article 70(3)]], the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall determine whether any *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]* is needed.
## 2.
The decision whether or not to take *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]* in relation to a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall contain information on the following:
### (a)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*’s assessment of whether the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* meets the conditions for resolution; and
### (b)
any action that the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* intends to take, including the decision to apply for winding up, the appointment of an administrator or any other measure under applicable *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(32)|normal insolvency proceedings]]*.
