# Article 72 - Procedural obligations of resolution authorities

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall notify the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution actions]]* it intends to take. That notification shall also indicate whether the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution actions]]* deviate from the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plan]]*.
As soon as practicable after taking a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]*, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall notify all of the following:
### (a)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution;
### (b)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]*;
### (c)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(34)|designated national macroprudential authority]]* and the ESRB;
### (d)
the Commission, ECB, and EIOPA; and
### (e)
the operators of the systems covered by Directive 98/26/EC in which the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution participates.
## 2.
The notification referred to in the second subparagraph of paragraph 1 shall include a copy of any order or instrument by which the relevant action is taken and indicate the date from which the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]* is effective.
The notification to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* pursuant to second subparagraph of paragraph 1 shall provide the reasons for any deviation from the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plan]]*.
## 3.
A copy of the order or instrument by which the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]* is taken, or a notice summarising the effects of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]* and, if applicable, the terms and period of suspension or restriction referred to in Articles [[2021R0023_EN.55|55]], [[2021R0023_EN.56|56]] and [[2021R0023_EN.57|57]] of this Regulation, shall be published on all of the following:
### (a)
the website of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*;
### (b)
the website of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*, if different from the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*, and the website of ESMA;
### (c)
the website of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution; and
### (d)
where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution are admitted to trading on a regulated market, the means used for the disclosure of regulated information concerning the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution in accordance with Article 21(1) of Directive 2004/109/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council


.
## 4.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]* are not admitted to trading on a regulated market, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall ensure that the documents providing proof of the order referred to in paragraph 3 are sent to the holders of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* and creditors of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution that are known through the registers or databases of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution which are available to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*.
