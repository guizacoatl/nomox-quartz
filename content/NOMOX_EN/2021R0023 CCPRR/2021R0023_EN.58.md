# Article 58 - Power to exercise control over the CCP

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may exercise control over the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution to:
### (a)
manage the activities and services of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, exercising the powers of its shareholders and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]*;
### (b)
consult the risk committee;
### (c)
manage and dispose of the assets and property of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution.

The control referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph may be exercised directly by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* or indirectly by a special manager appointed by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.50#1|Article 50(1)]].
## 2.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* exercises control over the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall not be deemed to be a shadow director or de facto director under national law.
