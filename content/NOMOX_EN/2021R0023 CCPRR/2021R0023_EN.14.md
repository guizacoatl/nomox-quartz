# Article 14 - Coordination procedure for resolution plans

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall transmit to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* a draft *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plan]]*, the information provided in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.13|Article 13]] and any additional information relevant to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]*.
## 2.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* shall reach a joint decision regarding the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plan]]* and any changes thereto within a four-month period from the date of the transmission of that plan by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* referred to in paragraph 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall ensure that ESMA is provided with all the information that is relevant to its role in accordance with this Article.
## 3.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may, in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.4#4|Article 4(4)]], decide to involve third-country authorities when drawing up and reviewing the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plan]]*, provided that they meet the confidentiality requirements laid down in [[2021R0023_EN.73|Article 73]] and are from jurisdictions in which any of the following entities are established:
### (a)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(13)|parent undertaking]]*, where applicable;
### (b)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* where their contribution to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default fund]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is, on an aggregate basis over a one-year period, higher than those of the third Member State with the largest contributions as referred to in point (c) of [[2012R0648_EN.18#2|Article 18(2) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
### (c)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s subsidiaries, where applicable;
### (d)
other providers of critical services to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (e)
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(21)|interoperable CCPs]]*.
## 4.
ESMA may, at the request of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*, assist the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* in reaching a joint decision in accordance with point (c) of [[2010R1095_EN.31#2|Article 31(2) of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
## 5.
Where, after four months from the date of transmission of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plan]]*, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* has failed to reach a joint decision, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall make its own decision on the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plan]]*. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall make its decision taking into account the views of the other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* members expressed during the four-month period. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall notify in writing the decision to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and to the other members of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]*.
## 6.
Where, by the end of the four-month period referred to in paragraph 5 of this Article, a joint decision has not been reached and a simple majority of the voting members disagree with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*’s proposal for a joint decision on a matter in relation to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plan]]*, any of the voting members concerned, based on that majority, may refer that matter to ESMA in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.19|Article 19 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]]. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall await the decision taken by ESMA in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.19#3|Article 19(3) of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] and decide in accordance with the decision of ESMA.
The four-month period shall be deemed to be the conciliation phase within the meaning of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010. ESMA shall take its decision within one month of the referral of the matter to it. The matter shall not be referred to ESMA after the end of the four-month period or after a joint decision has been reached. In the absence of an ESMA decision within one month, the decision of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall apply.
## 7.
Where a joint decision is taken pursuant to paragraph 1 and any *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* or competent ministry considers under paragraph 6 that the subject matter of the disagreement impinges on the fiscal responsibilities of its Member State, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall initiate a reassessment of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plan]]*.
