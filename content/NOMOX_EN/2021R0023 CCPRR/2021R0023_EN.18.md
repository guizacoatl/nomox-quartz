# Article 18 - Early intervention measures

## 1.
Where a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* infringes, or is likely to infringe in the near future, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* and prudential requirements of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012, or poses a risk to financial stability in the Union or in one or more of its Member States, or where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* has determined that there are other indications of an emerging crisis situation that could affect the operations of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, in particular, its ability to provide clearing services, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* may:
### (a)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to update the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.9#6|Article 9(6)]] of this Regulation, where the circumstances that required early intervention are different from the assumptions set out in the initial *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]*;
### (b)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to implement one or more of the arrangements or measures set out in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* within a specific timeframe. Where the plan is updated pursuant to point (a), those arrangements or measures shall include any updated arrangements or measures;
### (c)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to identify the causes of the infringement or likely infringement as mentioned in paragraph 1 and draw up an action programme, including suitable measures and timeframes;
### (d)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to convene a meeting of its shareholders or, if the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* fails to comply with that requirement, convene the meeting itself. In both cases the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall set the agenda, including the decisions to be considered for adoption by the shareholders;
### (e)
require one or more members of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]* to be removed or replaced where any of those persons is found unfit to perform their duties pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.27|Article 27 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
### (f)
require changes to the business strategy of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (g)
require changes to the legal or operational structures of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (h)
provide the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* with all the information necessary to update the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plan]]* in order to prepare for the possible resolution of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and the valuation of its assets and liabilities in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.24|Article 24]] of this Regulation, including any information acquired through on-site inspections;
#### (i)
require, where necessary and in accordance with paragraph 4, the implementation of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s recovery measures;
### (j)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to abstain from the implementation of certain recovery measures where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* has determined that the implementation of those measures may have an adverse effect on financial stability in the Union or in one or more of its Member States;
### (k)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to replenish its financial resources in a timely manner in order to comply or maintain compliance with its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* and prudential requirements;
### (l)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to instruct *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* to invite their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* to participate directly in auctions organised by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* when the nature of the auction justifies this exceptional participation. *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|Clearing members]]* shall inform their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* comprehensively about the auction following the instructions received from the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. In particular, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall specify the deadline after which it will not be possible to participate in the auction. *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|Clients]]* shall directly inform the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* before this deadline of their willingness to participate in the auction. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall then facilitate the bidding process for those *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]*. A *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|client]]* shall only be authorised to participate in the auction if it is able to demonstrate to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* that it has set up the appropriate contractual relationship with a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* to execute and clear the transactions that may result from the auction;
### (m)
restrict or prohibit any remuneration of equity and instruments treated as equity to the fullest extent possible without triggering an event of default, including dividend payments and buybacks by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, and it may restrict, prohibit or freeze any payments of variable remuneration as defined by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s remuneration policy pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.26#5|Article 26(5) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], discretionary pension benefits or severance packages to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]* as defined in point 29 of [[2012R0648_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]].
## 2.
For each of the measures referred to in paragraph 1, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall set an appropriate deadline and evaluate the effectiveness of those measures once they have been taken.
## 3.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall only apply the measures in points (a) to (m) of paragraph 1 after taking account of the impact of those measures in other Member States where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* operates or provides services and after informing the relevant competent authorities, in particular where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s operations are critical or important for local financial markets, including the places in which *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]*, linked *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(16)|FMIs]]* are established.
## 4.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall apply the measure in point (i) of paragraph 1 only where that measure is in the public interest and is necessary to achieve any of the following objectives:
### (a)
to maintain the financial stability in the Union or in one or more of its Member States;
### (b)
to maintain the continuity of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(27)|critical functions]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and access to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(27)|critical functions]]* on a transparent and non-discriminatory basis;
### (c)
to maintain or restore the financial resilience of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.

The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall not apply the measure in point (i) of paragraph 1 in relation to measures involving the transfer of property, rights or liabilities of another *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 5.
Where a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* uses contributions to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default fund]]* of the non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.45#3|Article 45(3) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], it shall inform the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* without undue delay and explain whether that event reflects weaknesses or problems of that *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 6.
Where the conditions referred to in paragraph 1 are met, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall notify ESMA and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* and consult the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]* on the envisaged measures provided for in paragraph 1.
Following those notifications and the consultation of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]*, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall decide whether to apply any of the measures provided for in paragraph 1. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall notify the decision on the measures to be taken to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]*, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* and ESMA.
## 7.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*, following the notification of the first subparagraph of paragraph 6 of this Article, may require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to contact potential purchasers in order to prepare for its resolution, subject to the conditions laid down in [[2021R0023_EN.41|Article 41]] and the confidentiality provisions laid down in [[2021R0023_EN.73|Article 73]].
## 8.
ESMA shall, by 12 February 2022, issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] to promote the consistent application of the triggers for the use of the measures referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article.
