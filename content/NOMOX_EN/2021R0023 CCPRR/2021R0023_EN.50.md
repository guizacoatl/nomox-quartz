# Article 50 - Special management

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may appoint one or more special managers to replace the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution. The special manager shall be of sufficiently good repute and shall have adequate expertise in financial services, risk management and clearing services in accordance with the second subparagraph of [[2012R0648_EN.27#2|Article 27(2) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]].
## 2.
The special manager shall have all the powers of the shareholders and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. The special manager shall only exercise those powers under the control of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may limit the actions of the special manager or require prior consent for certain acts.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall make public the appointment referred to in paragraph 1 and the terms and conditions attached to that appointment.
## 3.
The special manager shall be appointed for no more than one year. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may renew that period where necessary to achieve the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*.
## 4.
The special manager shall take all the measures necessary to promote the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]* and implement *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution actions]]* taken by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*. In the event of inconsistency or conflict, that statutory duty shall override any other duty of management in accordance with the statutes of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or national law.
## 5.
The special manager shall draw up reports for the appointing *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* at regular intervals set by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* and at the beginning and the end of the mandate. Those reports shall describe in detail the financial situation of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and state the reasons for the measures taken.
## 6.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may remove the special manager at any time. It shall in any case remove the special manager in the following cases:
### (a)
where the special manager is failing to perform its duties in accordance with the terms and conditions set out by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*;
### (b)
where the objectives of resolution would be better achieved by removing or replacing that special manager; or
### (c)
where the conditions for the appointment are no longer fulfilled.
