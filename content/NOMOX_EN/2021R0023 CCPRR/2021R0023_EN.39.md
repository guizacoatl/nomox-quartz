# Article 39 - Implementation and monitoring of the business reorganisation plan

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall implement the business reorganisation plan and shall submit a report to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* as requested and, at least, every six months on its progress in implementing that plan.
## 2.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*, in agreement with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*, may require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to revise the plan where necessary to achieve the aim referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.37#1|Article 37(1)]].
