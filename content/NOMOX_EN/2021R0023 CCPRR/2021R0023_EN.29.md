# Article 29 - Termination of contracts – partial or full

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may terminate some or all of the following contracts of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution:
### (a)
the contracts with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* in default;
### (b)
the contracts of the affected clearing service or asset class;
### (c)
the other contracts of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution.

The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall terminate the contracts referred to in point (a) of the first subparagraph of this paragraph only where the transfer of the assets and positions resulting from those contracts has not taken place within the meaning of [[2021R0023_EN.48#5|Article 48(5)]] and (6) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012.

When using the power under the first subparagraph, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall terminate contracts referred to under each of points (a), (b) and (c) of the first subparagraph in a similar way, without discriminating between counterparties to those contracts, with the exception of those contractual obligations that cannot be enforced in a reasonable timeframe.
## 2.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall give notice to all relevant *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* of the date on which any contract referred to in paragraph 1 is terminated.
## 3.
Prior to the termination of any of the contracts referred to in paragraph 1, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall take the following steps:
### (a)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution to value each contract, and update the account balances of each *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]*;
### (b)
determine the net amount payable by or to each *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]*, taking account of any due but unpaid *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(42)|variation margin]]*, including *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(42)|variation margin]]* due as a result of the contract valuations referred to in point (a); and
### (c)
notify each *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* of the determined net amounts and require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to pay or collect them accordingly.

The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* shall, without undue delay, communicate the application of such tool to their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* and the way in which such application affects them.
## 4.
The valuation referred in point (a) of paragraph 3 shall be based, as far as possible, on a fair market price determined on the basis of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s own rules and arrangements, unless the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* deems necessary the use of another appropriate price discovery method.
## 5.
Where a non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* is unable to pay the net amount determined in accordance with paragraph 3 of this Article, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may, having regard to [[2021R0023_EN.21|Article 21]] of this Regulation, require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to place the non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* in default and use its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(41)|initial margin]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default fund]]* contribution in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.45|Article 45 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]].
## 6.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* has terminated one or more contracts of the types referred to in paragraph 1, it may temporarily prevent the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* from clearing any new contract of the same type as the one terminated.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may allow the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to resume the clearing of those types of contracts only where the following conditions are met:
### (a)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* complies with the requirements of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012; and
### (b)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* issues and publishes a notice to that effect using the means referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.72#3|Article 72(3)]].
## 7.
ESMA shall by 12 February 2022 issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] further specifying the methodology to be used by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* for determining the valuation referred in point (a) of paragraph 3 of this Article.
