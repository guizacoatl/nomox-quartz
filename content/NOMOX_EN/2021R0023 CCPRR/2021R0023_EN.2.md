# Article 2 - Definitions


For the purposes of this Regulation the following definitions apply:
## (1)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* as defined in point (1) of [[2012R0648_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (2)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]*’ means the college established pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.4|Article 4]];
## (3)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*’ means an authority designated by a Member State in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.3|Article 3]];
## (4)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tool]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tool]]* as set out in [[2021R0023_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]];
## (5)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(5)|resolution power]]*’ means any of the powers laid down in Articles [[2021R0023_EN.48|48]] to 58;
## (6)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*’ mean the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]* laid down in [[2021R0023_EN.21|Article 21]];
## (7)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*’ means an authority designated by a Member State in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.22|Article 22 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (8)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(8)|default event]]*’ means a scenario in which the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has declared in default:
### (a)
one or more *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* in accordance with the procedure set out in [[2012R0648_EN.48|Article 48 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]]; or
### (b)
one or more *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(21)|interoperable CCPs]]* in accordance with the relevant contractual arrangements or with the procedure set out in [[2012R0648_EN.52|Article 52 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (9)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(9)|non-default event]]*’ means a scenario in which losses are incurred by a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* for any reason other than a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(8)|default event]]*, including but not limited to, business, custody, investment, legal or operational failures or fraud, including failures resulting from cyber-attacks;
## (10)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plan]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plan]]* for a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* drawn up in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.12|Article 12]];
## (11)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]*’ means a decision to place a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.22|Article 22]], the application of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tool]]*, or the exercise of one or more *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(5)|resolution powers]]*;
## (12)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* as defined in point 14 of [[2012R0648_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (13)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(13)|parent undertaking]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(13)|parent undertaking]]* as defined in point (15)(a) of [[2013R0575_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]];
## (14)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(14)|third-country CCP]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* the head office of which is established in a third country;
## (15)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(15)|set-off arrangement]]*’ means an arrangement under which two or more claims or obligations owed between the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution and a counterparty can be set off against each other;
## (16)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(16)|financial market infrastructure]]*’ or ‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(16)|FMI]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, a central securities depository, a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(38)|trade repository]]*, a payment system or another system defined and designated by a Member State under point (a) of Article 2 of Directive 98/26/EC;
## (17)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venue]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venue]]* as defined in point 4 of [[2012R0648_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (18)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|client]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|client]]* as defined in point 15 of [[2012R0648_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (19)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(19)|O-SIIs]]*’ means other systemically important institutions as referred to in [[2013L0036_EN.131#3|Article 131(3) of Directive 2013/36/EU]];
## (20)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(20)|indirect client]]*’ means an undertaking which has established indirect clearing arrangements with a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* within the meaning of the second subparagraph of [[2012R0648_EN.4#3|Article 4(3) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (21)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(21)|interoperable CCP]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* with which an interoperability arrangement has been established;
## (22)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* drawn up and maintained by a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.9|Article 9]];
## (23)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]*’ means the administrative or supervisory *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]*, or both, set up pursuant to national company law and in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.27#2|Article 27(2) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (24)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]*’ means the college referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.18#1|Article 18(1) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (25)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(25)|capital]]*’ means *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* as defined in point 25 of [[2012R0648_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (26)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(26)|default waterfall]]*’ means *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(26)|default waterfall]]* in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.45|Article 45 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (27)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(27)|critical functions]]*’ means activities, services or operations provided to third parties external to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* the discontinuance of which is likely to lead to the disruption of services that are essential to the real economy or to disrupt financial stability in one or more Member States due to the size, market share, external and internal interconnectedness, complexity or cross-border activities of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, with particular regard to the substitutability of those activities, services or operations;
## (28)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]* as defined in point 16 of [[2012R0648_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (29)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(29)|linked FMI]]*’ means an *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(16)|FMI]]* with which the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has entered into contractual arrangements, including interoperability arrangements;
## (30)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(30)|extraordinary public financial support]]*’ means State aid within the meaning of Article 107(1) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union (TFEU), or any other public financial support at supra-national level, which, if provided for at national level, would constitute State aid, that is provided in order to preserve or restore the viability, liquidity or solvency of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
## (31)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(31)|financial contracts]]*’ means contracts and agreements as defined in point 100 of [[2014L0059_EN.2#1|Article 2(1) of Directive 2014/59/EU]];
## (32)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(32)|normal insolvency proceedings]]*’ means collective insolvency proceedings which entail the partial or total divestment of a debtor and the appointment of a liquidator or an administrator normally applicable to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* under national law and either specific to those institutions or generally applicable to any natural or legal person;
## (33)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*’ means shares, other instruments that confer ownership, instruments that are convertible into or give the right to acquire shares or other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*, and instruments representing interests in shares or other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*;
## (34)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(34)|designated national macroprudential authority]]*’ means the authority entrusted with the conduct of macroprudential policy referred to in Recommendation B1 of the Recommendation of the European Systemic Risk *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|Board]]* (ESRB) of 22 December 2011 on the macroprudential mandate of national authorities (ESRB/2011/3);
## (35)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default fund]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default fund]]* maintained by a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.42|Article 42 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (36)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(36)|pre-funded resources]]*’ means resources which are held by and freely available to the relevant legal person;
## (37)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]*’ means the person or persons who effectively direct the business of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, and the executive member or members of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]*;
## (38)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(38)|trade repository]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(38)|trade repository]]* as defined in point 2 of [[2012R0648_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] or in point 1 of [[2015R2365_EN.3|Article 3 of Regulation (EU) 2365/2015]] of the European Parliament and of the Council


;
## (39)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(39)|Union State aid framework]]*’ means the framework established by Articles [[2021R0023_EN.107|107]], [[2021R0023_EN.108|108]] and [[2021R0023_EN.109|109]] TFEU and regulations and all Union acts, including guidelines, communications and notices, made or adopted pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.108#4|Article 108(4)]] or [[2021R0023_EN.109|Article 109]] TFEU;
## (40)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]*’ means bonds or other forms of unsecured transferable debt, instruments creating or acknowledging a debt, and instruments giving rights to acquire *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]*;
## (41)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(41)|initial margin]]*’ means margins collected by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to cover potential future exposure to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* providing the margin and, where relevant, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(21)|interoperable CCPs]]* in the interval between the last margin collection and the liquidation of positions following a default of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* or of an *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(21)|interoperable CCP]]*;
## (42)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(42)|variation margin]]*’ means margins collected or paid out to reflect current exposures resulting from actual changes in market prices;
## (43)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(43)|resolution cash call]]*’ means a request for cash resources to be provided by *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, additional to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(36)|pre-funded resources]]*, based on statutory powers available to a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.31|Article 31]];
## (44)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(44)|recovery cash call]]*’ means a request for cash resources, other than a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(43)|resolution cash call]]*, to be provided by *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, additional to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(36)|pre-funded resources]]*, based on contractual arrangements laid out in the operating rules of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
## (45)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(45)|transfer powers]]*’ means the powers specified in point (c) and (d) of [[2021R0023_EN.48#1|Article 48(1)]] to transfer shares, other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]*, assets, rights, obligations or liabilities, or any combination of those items from a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution to a recipient;
## (46)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(46)|derivative]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(46)|derivative]]* as defined in point 5 of [[2012R0648_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (47)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(47)|netting arrangement]]*’ means an arrangement under which a number of claims or obligations can be converted into a single net claim, including a close-out *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(47)|netting arrangement]]* under which, on the occurrence of an enforcement event (however or wherever defined), the obligations of the parties are accelerated so as to become immediately due or are terminated, and in either case are converted into or replaced by a single net claim, including a close-out netting provision as defined in point (n)(i) of Article 2(1) of Directive 2002/47/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council


and netting as defined in point (k) of Article 2 of Directive 98/26/EC;
## (48)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(48)|crisis prevention measure]]*’ means the exercise of powers to require a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to take measures to remedy deficiencies in its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* under [[2021R0023_EN.10#8|Article 10(8)]] and (9), the exercise of powers to address or remove impediments to resolvability under [[2021R0023_EN.16|Article 16]], or the application of an early intervention measure under [[2021R0023_EN.18|Article 18]];
## (49)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(49)|termination right]]*’ means a right to terminate a contract, a right to accelerate, close out, set-off or net obligations or any similar provision that suspends, modifies or extinguishes an obligation of a party to the contract or a provision that prevents an obligation under the contract from arising that would otherwise arise;
## (50)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(50)|title transfer financial collateral arrangement]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(50)|title transfer financial collateral arrangement]]* as defined in point (b) of Article 2(1) of Directive 2002/47/EC;
## (51)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(51)|covered bond]]*’ means a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(51)|covered bond]]* as defined in point (1) of [[2019L2162_EN.3|Article 3 of Directive (EU) 2162/2019]] of the European Parliament and of the Council


;
## (52)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(52)|third-country resolution proceedings]]*’ means an action under the law of a third country to manage the failure of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(14)|third-country CCP]]* that is comparable, in terms of objectives and anticipated results, to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution actions]]* under this Regulation;
## (53)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(53)|relevant national authorities]]*’ means the resolution authorities, competent authorities or competent ministries designated in accordance with this Regulation or pursuant to [[2014L0059_EN.3|Article 3 of Directive 2014/59/EU]] or other authorities in Member States with powers in relation to assets, rights, obligations or liabilities of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(14)|third-country CCPs]]* providing clearing services in their jurisdiction;
## (54)
‘*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(54)|relevant third-country authority]]*’ means a third-country authority responsible for carrying out functions comparable to those of resolution authorities or competent authorities pursuant to this Regulation.
