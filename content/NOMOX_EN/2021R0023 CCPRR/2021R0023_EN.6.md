# Article 6 - Cooperation between authorities

## 1.
Competent authorities, resolution authorities and ESMA shall cooperate closely for the purposes of this Regulation. In particular, during the recovery phase, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* and the members of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]* should cooperate and communicate effectively with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*, to enable the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* to act in a timely manner.
## 2.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and the resolution authorities of its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* shall cooperate closely with the aim of ensuring that there are no impediments to resolution.
## 3.
Competent authorities and resolution authorities shall cooperate with ESMA for the purposes of this Regulation in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
Competent authorities and resolution authorities shall, without delay, provide ESMA with all the information necessary to carry out its duties in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.35|Article 35 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
