# Article 5 - ESMA Resolution Committee

## 1.
ESMA shall create a resolution committee (the ‘ESMA Resolution Committee’) pursuant to [[2010R1095_EN.41|Article 41 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] for the purpose of preparing the decisions entrusted to ESMA in this Regulation, except for the decisions to be adopted pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.11|Article 11]] of this Regulation.
The ESMA Resolution Committee shall also promote the drawing up and coordination of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plans]]* and develop methods for the resolution of failing *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*.
## 2.
The ESMA Resolution Committee shall be composed of the authorities designated pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]].
Authorities referred to in points (i) and (v) of [[2010R1093_EN.4#2|Article 4(2) of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]] shall be invited to participate in the ESMA Resolution Committee as observers.
## 3.
For the purposes of this Regulation, ESMA shall cooperate with the European Supervisory Authority (European Insurance and Occupational Pensions Authority) (EIOPA) established by Regulation (EU) No 1094/2010 of the European Parliament and of the Council

and EBA within the framework of the Joint Committee of the European Supervisory Authorities established under [[2010R1093_EN.54|Article 54 of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]], [[2010R1094_EN.54|Article 54 of Regulation (EU) No 1094/2010]] and [[2010R1095_EN.54|Article 54 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
## 4.
For the purposes of this Regulation, ESMA shall ensure structural separation between the ESMA Resolution Committee and other functions referred to in Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
