# Article 35 - Removal of procedural obstacles for write-down and conversion

## 1.
Where [[2021R0023_EN.32#1|Article 32(1)]] is applied, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to maintain at all times a sufficient amount of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* to ensure that the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* may issue sufficient new *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* and that the issuance of or conversion into *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* could be carried out effectively.
## 2.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall apply the write-down and conversion tool regardless of any provisions in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s instruments of incorporation or statutes, including with respect to pre-emption rights for shareholders or requirements for the consent of shareholders to an increase of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(25)|capital]]*.
