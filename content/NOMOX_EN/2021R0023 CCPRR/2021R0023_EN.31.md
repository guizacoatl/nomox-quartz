# Article 31 - Resolution cash call

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may require non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* to make a contribution in cash to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* up to twice the amount equivalent to their contribution to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default fund]]*. This obligation to make a contribution in cash shall also be included in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s rules and other contractual arrangements as a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(43)|resolution cash call]]* reserved to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* taking *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]*. Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* calls for an amount in excess of the contribution to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default fund]]*, it shall do so after assessing the impact of this tool on non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* and the financial stability of Member States, in cooperation with the resolution authorities of non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]*.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* operates multiple *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default funds]]* and the tool is applied to address a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(8)|default event]]*, the amount of the contribution in cash referred to in the first subparagraph shall refer to the contribution of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default fund]]* of the affected clearing service or asset class.

Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* operates multiple *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default funds]]* and the tool is applied to address a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(9)|non-default event]]*, the amount of the contribution in cash referred to in the first subparagraph shall refer to the sum of the contributions of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* to all *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default funds]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.

The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may exercise the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(43)|resolution cash call]]* regardless of whether all contractual obligations requiring cash contributions from non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* have been exhausted.

The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall determine the amount of the cash contribution of each non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* in proportion to its contribution to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default fund]]* up to the limit referred in the first subparagraph.

The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to reimburse *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* the possible excess amount of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(43)|resolution cash call]]* where the level of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(43)|resolution cash call]]* applied based on a provisional valuation according to [[2021R0023_EN.26#1|Article 26(1)]] is found to exceed the required level based on the definitive valuation referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.26#2|Article 26(2)]].
## 2.
If a non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* does not pay the required amount, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to place that *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* in default and use its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(41)|initial margin]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default fund]]* contribution in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.45|Article 45 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] up to the required amount.
