# Article 16 - Addressing or removing impediments to resolvability

## 1.
Where, following the assessment in [[2021R0023_EN.15|Article 15]], the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*, in coordination with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* in accordance with the procedure set out in [[2021R0023_EN.17|Article 17]], concludes that there are material impediments to the resolvability of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*, in cooperation with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*, shall prepare and submit a report to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]*.
The report referred to in the first subparagraph shall analyse the material impediments to the effective application of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* and the exercise of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(5)|resolution powers]]* in relation to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, consider their impact on the business model of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and recommend targeted measures to remove those impediments.
## 2.
The requirement laid down in [[2021R0023_EN.14|Article 14]] for *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution colleges]]* to reach a joint decision on *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plans]]* shall be suspended following the submission of the report referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article until the measures to remove the material impediments to resolvability have been accepted by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* pursuant to paragraph 3 of this Article or alternative measures have been decided pursuant to paragraph 4 of this Article.
## 3.
Within four months of the date of receipt of the report submitted in accordance with paragraph 1 of this Article, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall propose to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* possible measures to address or remove the material impediments identified in the report. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall communicate to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* any measure proposed by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* shall assess, in accordance with point (b) of [[2021R0023_EN.17#1|Article 17(1)]], whether those measures effectively address or remove those impediments.
## 4.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* in coordination with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* in accordance with the procedure set out in [[2021R0023_EN.17|Article 17]] concludes that the measures proposed by a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in accordance with paragraph 3 of this Article would not effectively reduce or remove the impediments identified in the report, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall identify alternative measures which it shall communicate to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* for joint decision in accordance with point (c) of [[2021R0023_EN.17#1|Article 17(1)]].
The alternative measures referred to in the first subparagraph shall take into account the following:
### (a)
the threat to financial stability of those material impediments to the resolvability of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (b)
the likely effect of the alternative measures on:
#### (i)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, including its business model and operational efficiency;
#### (ii)
its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]*, and to the extent the information is available, their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(20)|indirect clients]]*, including where they have been designated as *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(19)|O-SIIs]]*;
#### (iii)
any *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(29)|linked FMIs]]*;
#### (iv)
financial markets, including *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]*, served by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
#### (v)
the financial system in any Member State or the Union as a whole; and
#### (vi)
the internal market; and
### (c)
the effects on the provision of integrated clearing services for different products and portfolio margining across asset classes.

For the purposes of points (a) and (b) of the second subparagraph, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall consult the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* and, where appropriate, relevant designated national macroprudential authorities.
## 5.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall notify the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in writing, either directly or indirectly through the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*, of the alternative measures to take in order to achieve the objective of removing impediments to resolvability. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall justify why the measures proposed by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* would not be able to remove the material impediments to resolvability and how the alternative measures would be effective in doing so.
## 6.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall propose within one month a plan to comply with the alternative measures, with a reasonable timeframe for the implementation of the plan. If deemed necessary by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may shorten or extend the proposed timeframe.
## 7.
For the purposes of paragraph 4, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may, after consulting the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* and while allowing for a reasonable timeframe for implementation:
### (a)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to revise or draw up service agreements, whether intra-*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]* or with third parties, to cover the provision of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(27)|critical functions]]*;
### (b)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to limit its maximum individual and aggregate uncovered exposures;
### (c)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to make changes to how it collects and holds margins pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.41|Article 41 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
### (d)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to make changes to the composition and number of its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default funds]]* referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.42|Article 42 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
### (e)
impose on the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* specific or regular additional information requirements;
### (f)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to divest specific assets;
### (g)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to limit or cease specific existing or proposed activities;
### (h)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to make changes to its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]*, operating rules and other contractual arrangements;
#### (i)
restrict or prevent the development of new or existing business lines or provision of new or existing services;
### (j)
require changes to legal or operational structures of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or any *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]* entity directly or indirectly under its control to ensure that *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(27)|critical functions]]* may be legally and operationally separated from other functions through the application of resolutions tools;
### (k)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to operationally and financially segregate its different clearing services so as to isolate some specific asset classes from other asset classes and where deemed appropriate, to restrict netting sets covering different asset classes;
### (l)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to set up a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(13)|parent undertaking]]* in the Union;
### (m)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to issue liabilities that can be written down and converted or to set aside other financial resources to increase the capacity for loss absorption, recapitalisation and the replenishment of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(36)|pre-funded resources]]*;
### (n)
require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to take other steps to enable *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(25)|capital]]*, other liabilities and contracts to be able to absorb losses, to recapitalise the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or to replenish *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(36)|pre-funded resources]]*. Actions considered may include in particular attempting to renegotiate any liability the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has issued or to revise contractual terms, with a view to ensuring that any decision of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* to write down, convert or restructure that liability, instrument or contract would be effected under the law of the jurisdiction governing that liability or instrument;
### (o)
where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is a subsidiary, coordinate with relevant authorities with a view to requiring the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(13)|parent undertaking]]* to set up a separate holding company to control the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, where that measure is necessary in order to facilitate the resolution of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and to avoid the adverse effects that the application of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* and the exercise of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(5)|resolution powers]]* could have on other entities of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]*;
### (p)
restrict or prohibit interoperability links of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* where such a restriction or prohibition is necessary in order to avoid adverse effects on the achievement of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*.
