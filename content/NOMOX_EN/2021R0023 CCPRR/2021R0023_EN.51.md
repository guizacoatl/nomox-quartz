# Article 51 - Power to require the provision of services and facilities

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may require a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, any entity belonging to the same *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]* as the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or any of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* to provide any services or facilities that are necessary to enable a purchaser or bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to operate effectively the business transferred to it.
The first subparagraph shall apply regardless of whether an entity in the same *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]* as the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or one of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* has entered into *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(32)|normal insolvency proceedings]]* or is itself under resolution.
## 2.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* may enforce obligations imposed, pursuant to paragraph 1, by resolution authorities in other Member States where those powers are exercised in relation to entities belonging to the same *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]* as the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution, or in relation to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* of that *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 3.
The services and facilities referred to in paragraph 1 shall not include any form of financial support.
## 4.
The services and facilities provided pursuant to paragraph 1 shall be provided:
### (a)
on the same commercial terms on which they were provided to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* immediately before the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]* was taken, where an agreement for the purposes of providing those services and facilities exists; or
### (b)
on reasonable commercial terms, where there is no agreement for the purposes of providing those services and facilities or where that agreement has expired.
