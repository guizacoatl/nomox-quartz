# Article 46 - Public equity support tool

## 1.
Public financial support may be provided for the recapitalisation of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in exchange for *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*.
## 2.
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* subject to the public equity support tool shall be managed on a commercial and professional basis.
## 3.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* referred to in paragraph 1 shall be sold to a private purchaser as soon as commercial and financial circumstances allow.
