# Article 28 - Objective and scope of the position and loss allocation tools

## 1.
Resolution authorities shall apply the position allocation tool in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.29|Article 29]] and the loss allocation tools in accordance with Articles [[2021R0023_EN.30|30]] and [[2021R0023_EN.31|31]].
## 2.
Resolution authorities shall apply the tools referred to in paragraph 1 in respect of contracts relating to clearing services and the collateral related to those services posted to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 3.
Resolution authorities shall apply the position allocation tool referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.29|Article 29]] in order to rematch the book of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* where relevant.
Resolution authorities shall apply the loss allocation tools referred to in Articles [[2021R0023_EN.30|30]] and [[2021R0023_EN.31|31]] for any of the following purposes:
### (a)
to cover the losses of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* assessed in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.25|Article 25]];
### (b)
to restore the ability of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to meet payment obligations as they fall due;
### (c)
to achieve the outcome referred to in points (a) and (b) in relation to a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (d)
to support the transfer of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s business by way of the sale of business tool to a solvent third party.

The loss allocation tool referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.30|Article 30]] may be applied by the resolution authorities in relation to losses arising from a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(8)|default event]]* and in relation to losses arising from a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(9)|non-default event]]*. If the loss allocation tool referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.30|Article 30]] is applied in relation to losses arising from a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(9)|non-default event]]*, it shall only be applied up to a cumulative amount equivalent to the non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]*’ contribution to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default funds]]* and distributed among *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* proportionally to their contributions to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(35)|default funds]]*.
## 4.
Resolution authorities shall not apply the loss allocation tools referred to in Articles [[2021R0023_EN.30|30]] and [[2021R0023_EN.31|31]] of this Regulation with regard to the entities referred to in Articles [[2021R0023_EN.1|1]](4) and 1(5) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012.
