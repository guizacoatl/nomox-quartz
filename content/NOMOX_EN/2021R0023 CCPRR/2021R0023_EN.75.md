# Article 75 - Restrictions on other proceedings

## 1.
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(32)|Normal insolvency proceedings]]* shall not be commenced in relation to a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* except at the initiative of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* or with its consent in accordance with paragraph 3.
## 2.
Competent authorities and resolution authorities shall be notified without delay of any application for the opening of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(32)|normal insolvency proceedings]]* in relation to a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, irrespective of whether the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is under resolution or a decision has been made public in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.72#3|Article 72(3)]].
## 3.
The authorities responsible for *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(32)|normal insolvency proceedings]]* shall only commence those proceedings after the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* has notified them of its decision not to take any *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]* in relation to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or where no notification has been received within seven days of the notification referred to in paragraph 2.
Where necessary for the effective application of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* and powers, resolution authorities may request the court to apply a stay on any judicial action or proceeding in which a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution is or may become a party for an appropriate period in accordance with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*.
