# Article 69 - Partial transfers: protection of trading, clearing and settlement systems

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall ensure that the application of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tool]]* does not affect the operation of systems and rules of systems covered by Directive 98/26/EC, where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]*:
### (a)
transfers some but not all of the assets, rights, obligations or liabilities of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution to another entity;
### (b)
cancels or amends the terms of a contract to which the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution is a party or substitutes a purchaser or bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* as a party.
## 2.
For the purposes of paragraph 1 of this Article, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall ensure that the application of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* does not result in any of the following outcomes:
### (a)
revoking a transfer order in accordance with Article 5 of Directive 98/26/EC;
### (b)
affecting the enforceability of transfer orders and netting as required by Articles [[2021R0023_EN.3|3]] and [[2021R0023_EN.5|5]] of Directive 98/26/EC;
### (c)
affecting the use of funds, securities or credit facilities as required by Article 4 of Directive 98/26/EC;
### (d)
affecting the protection of collateral security as required by Article 9 of Directive 98/26/EC.
