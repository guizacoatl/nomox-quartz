> Level 2 reference(s): [[2023R0450_EN.1]], [[2023R0450_EN.0]]# Article 20 - Provision of recompense to non-defaulting clearing members

## 1.
Without prejudice to the responsibility of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* to take losses which go beyond the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(26)|default waterfall]]*, where a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* in recovery caused by a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(9)|non-default event]]* has applied the arrangements and measures to reduce the value of any gains payable by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* set out in its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]*, and as a result has not entered into resolution, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* may require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to recompense the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* for their loss through cash payments or, where appropriate, may require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to issue instruments recognising a claim on the future profits of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. The possibility to provide recompense to non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* shall not apply to their contractually committed losses in the default management or recovery phases.
The cash payments or the value of instruments recognising a claim on future profits of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* issued to each affected non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* shall be proportionate to its loss in excess of its contractual commitments. The instruments recognising a claim on future profits of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall entitle the possessor to receive payments from the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* on an annual basis until the loss has been recouped, if possible in full, subject to an appropriate maximum number of years from the date of issuance. If the non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* have passed on the excess losses to their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]*, the non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* shall be obliged to pass the payments received by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* on to their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]*, to the extent that the losses being recompensed are related to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|client]]* positions. An appropriate maximum share of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s annual profits shall be used towards payments relating to those instruments.
## 2.
ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards to specify the order in which recompense must be paid, the appropriate maximum number of years and the appropriate maximum share of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s annual profits referred to in the second subparagraph of paragraph 1.
ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 12 February 2022.

The Commission is empowered to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
