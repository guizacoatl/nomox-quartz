# Article 9 - Recovery plans

## 1.
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* shall draw up and maintain a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* providing for measures to be taken in the case of both default and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(9)|non-default events]]* and combinations of both, in order to restore their financial soundness, without any *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(30)|extraordinary public financial support]]*, and allow them to continue to provide *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(27)|critical functions]]* following a significant deterioration of their financial situation or a risk of breaching their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* and prudential requirements under Regulation (EU) No 648/2012.
## 2.
The measures included in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* shall:
### (a)
comprehensively and effectively address all the risks identified in the different scenarios, including possible uncovered liquidity shortfalls;
### (b)
in the case of losses due to a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(8)|default event]]*, ensure the re-establishment of a matched book and the full allocation of uncovered losses to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]*, and to their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* if those *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* are direct creditors of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, and to shareholders, taking into account the interests of all stakeholders;
### (c)
include loss-absorbing arrangements that are adequate to cover the losses that might arise from all types of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(9)|non-default events]]*; and
### (d)
enable the replenishment of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s financial resources, including its own funds, to a level sufficient in order for the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to meet its obligations under Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 and to support the continued and timely operation of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(27)|critical functions]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 3.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* shall include a framework of indicators based on the risk profile of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, that identify the circumstances under which measures in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* are to be taken. The indicators may be of either a qualitative or a quantitative nature relating to the financial soundness and operational viability of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and should enable recovery measures to be taken early enough to provide sufficient time for the plan to be implemented.
## 4.
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* shall put in place appropriate arrangements for the regular monitoring of the indicators referred to in paragraph 3. *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* shall report to their competent authorities on the outcome of that monitoring. Competent authorities shall transmit information to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]*, where they deem such information significant.
## 5.
ESMA shall, in cooperation with the ESRB, by 12 February 2022, issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] to specify the minimum list of qualitative and quantitative indicators referred to in paragraph 3 of this Article.
## 6.
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* shall include provisions in their operating rules, outlining the procedures to be followed by them where, in order to achieve the goals of the recovery process, they propose to:
### (a)
take measures provided for in their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* despite the fact that the relevant indicators have not been met; or
### (b)
refrain from taking measures provided for in their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* despite the fact that the relevant indicators have been met.

Any decision taken pursuant to this paragraph and its justification shall be notified to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* without delay.
## 7.
Where a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* intends to activate its *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]*, it shall notify the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* of the nature and magnitude of the problems it has identified, setting out all relevant circumstances and indicating the recovery measures or other measures it intends to take to address the situation as well as the envisaged time-frame to restore its financial soundness by use of those measures.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* considers that a recovery measure that the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* intends to take may cause significant adverse effects to the financial system or is unlikely to be effective, it may require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to refrain from taking that measure.

Following the notification received under the second subparagraph of paragraph 6 of this Article, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall immediately assess whether the circumstances require the use of early intervention powers in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.18|Article 18]].
## 8.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall promptly inform the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]*, and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall promptly inform the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]*, of any notification received in accordance with the second subparagraph of paragraph 6 and with the first subparagraph of paragraph 7, and of any subsequent instruction by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* in accordance with the second subparagraph of paragraph 7.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* is informed in accordance with the first subparagraph of paragraph 7 of this Article, it shall restrict or prohibit any remuneration of equity and instruments treated as equity, including dividend payments and buybacks by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, to the fullest extent possible without triggering an event of default, and it may restrict or prohibit any payments of variable remuneration as defined by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s remuneration policy pursuant to [[2012R0648_EN.26#5|Article 26(5) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], discretionary pension benefits or severance packages to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]* as defined in point 29 of [[2012R0648_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]].
## 9.
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* shall, at least annually and in any case after any change to their legal or organisational structure or business or financial situation which could have a material effect on those plans or otherwise necessitate a change to the plans, review, test and, where necessary, update their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plans]]*. Competent authorities may require *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* to update their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plans]]* more frequently.
## 10.
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|Recovery plans]]* shall be drawn up in accordance with Section A of the Annex and take into account all relevant interdependencies within the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]* to which the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* belongs. Competent authorities may require *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* to include additional information in their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plans]]*. Where relevant, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall consult the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(13)|parent undertaking]]*.
## 11.
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|Recovery plans]]* shall:
### (a)
not assume any access to or receipt of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(30)|extraordinary public financial support]]*, central bank emergency liquidity assistance or central bank liquidity assistance provided under non-standard collateralisation, tenor and interest rate terms;
### (b)
consider the interests of all stakeholders that are likely to be affected by the plan, including *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* and, to the extent that the information is available, their direct and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(20)|indirect clients]]*; and
### (c)
ensure that *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* do not have unlimited exposures toward the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and stakeholders’ potential losses and liquidity shortfalls are transparent, measurable, manageable and controllable.
## 12.
ESMA shall, in cooperation with the ESRB, by 12 February 2022 issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] further specifying the range of scenarios to be considered for the purposes of paragraph 1 of this Article. In issuing such guidelines, ESMA shall take into account, where appropriate, supervisory stress testing exercises.
## 13.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is part of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]* and contractual parental or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]* support agreements form part of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]*, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* shall contemplate scenarios in which those agreements cannot be honoured.
## 14.
Following a default or a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(9)|non-default event]]*, a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall use an additional amount of its pre-funded dedicated own resources, prior to the use of the arrangements and measures referred to in point 15 of Section A of the Annex to this Regulation. That amount shall not be lower than 10 % nor higher than 25 % of the risk-based *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* requirements calculated in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.16#2|Article 16(2) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]].
To comply with that requirement, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* may use the amount of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* it holds, in addition to its minimum *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(25)|capital]]* requirements, to comply with the notification threshold referred to in the delegated act adopted on the basis of [[2012R0648_EN.16#3|Article 16(3) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]].
## 15.
ESMA shall, in close cooperation with EBA and after consulting the ESCB, develop draft regulatory technical standards specifying the methodology for calculation and maintenance of the additional amount of pre-funded dedicated own resources to be used in accordance with paragraph 14. When developing those technical standards, ESMA shall take into account all of the following:
### (a)
the structure and the internal organisation of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* and the nature, scope and complexity of their activities;
### (b)
the structure of incentives of the shareholders, management and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* and of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]*;
### (c)
the appropriateness for *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*, depending on the currencies in which the financial instruments they clear are denominated, the currencies accepted as collateral and the risk stemming from their activities, in particular where they do not clear OTC *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(46)|derivatives]]* as defined in point (7) of [[2012R0648_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], to invest that additional amount of dedicated own resources in assets other than those referred to in Article 47(1) of that Regulation; and
### (d)
the rules applying to and the practices of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(14)|third-country CCPs]]*, as well as the international developments concerning the recovery and resolution of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*, in order to preserve the competitiveness of internationally active Union *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*, and the competitiveness of Union *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* compared to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(14)|third-country CCPs]]* providing clearing services in the Union.

Where ESMA concludes, on the basis of the criteria referred to in point (c) of the first subparagraph, that it is appropriate for certain *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* to invest that additional amount of pre-funded dedicated own resources in assets other than those referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.47#1|Article 47(1) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], it shall also specify:
### (a)
the procedure through which, in the event that those resources are not immediately available, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* may resort to recovery measures that require the financial contribution of non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]*;
### (b)
the procedure that *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* shall follow to subsequently reimburse the non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* referred to in point (a) up to the amount to be used in accordance with paragraph 14 of this Article.

ESMA shall submit the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph to the Commission by 12 February 2022.

The Commission is empowered to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
## 16.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall develop adequate mechanisms to involve *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(29)|linked FMIs]]* and stakeholders which would bear losses, incur costs or contribute to cover liquidity shortfalls in the event that the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* was implemented in the process of drawing-up of that plan.
## 17.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall assess, taking into account the advice of the risk committee in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.28#3|Article 28(3) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], and approve the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* before submitting it to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*.
## 18.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* has decided not to follow the advice of the risk committee, it shall promptly inform the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.28#5|Article 28(5) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] and explain its decision in detail to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*.
## 19.
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|Recovery plans]]* shall be integrated in the corporate governance and the overall risk management framework of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
## 20.
The measures set out in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plans]]* that create financial or contractual obligations on *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* and, where relevant, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(20)|indirect clients]]*, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(29)|linked FMIs]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]* shall form part of the operating rules of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*.
## 21.
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* shall ensure that the measures set out in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plans]]* are enforceable at all times in all jurisdictions where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]*, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(29)|linked FMIs]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(17)|trading venues]]* are located.
## 22.
The obligation of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* to include in their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plans]]* the right to make a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(44)|recovery cash call]]* and, if applicable, to reduce the value of any gains payable by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]* to non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* shall not be applicable to the entities referred to in Articles [[2021R0023_EN.1|1]](4) and 1(5) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012.
## 23.
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|Clearing members]]* shall communicate to their *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(18)|clients]]* in a clear and transparent manner if and in what way measures in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* may affect them.
