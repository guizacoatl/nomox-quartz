# Article 19 - Removal of senior management and board

## 1.
Where there is a significant deterioration in the financial situation of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, or the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* infringes its legal requirements, including its operating rules, and other measures taken in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.18|Article 18]] are not sufficient to reverse that situation, competent authorities may require total or partial removal of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* requires complete or partial removal of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, it shall notify ESMA, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]*.
## 2.
The appointment of the new *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* shall be done in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.27|Article 27 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] and be subject to the approval or consent of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*. Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* considers that replacement of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* as referred to in this Article is insufficient, it may appoint one or more temporary administrators to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to replace or to temporarily work with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*. Any temporary administrator shall have the qualifications, ability and knowledge required to carry out his or her functions and be free of any conflict of interests.
