# Article 70 - Notification requirements

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall notify the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* where it considers that it is failing or likely to fail as referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.22#2|Article 22(2)]].
## 2.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall inform the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* of any notifications received under paragraph 1, and of any recovery or other measures in accordance with Title IV that the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* requires the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to take.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* shall inform the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* of any emergency situation referred to in [[2012R0648_EN.24|Article 24 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]] relating to a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and of any notification received in accordance with Article 48 of that Regulation.
## 3.
Where a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* determines that the conditions referred to in points (a) and (b) of [[2021R0023_EN.22#1|Article 22(1)]] or in [[2021R0023_EN.22#3|Article 22(3)]] are met in relation to a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, it shall notify without undue delay the following bodies:
### (a)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* or *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* for that *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (b)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* for the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(13)|parent undertaking]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (c)
the central bank;
### (d)
the competent ministry;
### (e)
the ESRB and the designated national macro-prudential authority; and
### (f)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(2)|resolution college]]* for that *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*.
