# Article 10 - External communication


As far as practicable, the resolution authority of the CCP shall inform the resolution college at least on the following:
### (a)
the coordination of external communication and public statements during a going concern situation, in a situation where the CCP is considered as failing or likely to fail pursuant to [[2023R1192_EN.22|Article 22]], paragraph 3, of Regulation (EU) 2021/23;
### (b)
the level of information to be disclosed.
