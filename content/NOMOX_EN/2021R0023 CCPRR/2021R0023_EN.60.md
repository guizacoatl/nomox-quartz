# Article 60 - ‘No creditor worse off’ principle


Where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* applies one or more *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]*, it shall ensure that shareholders, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* and other creditors do not incur greater losses than they would have incurred had the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* not taken *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]* in relation to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* at the time the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* considered that the conditions for resolution pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.22#1|Article 22(1)]] were met and had the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* instead been wound up under *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(32)|normal insolvency proceedings]]*, following the full application of the applicable contractual obligations and other arrangements in its operating rules.
