# Article 1 - Subject matter


This Regulation lays down rules and procedures relating to the recovery and resolution of central counterparties (*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*) authorised in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 and rules relating to arrangements with third countries in the field of recovery and resolution of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCPs]]*.
