# Article 11 - Coordination procedure for recovery plans

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]* shall examine the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* and, where any member of the college considers that there are material deficiencies in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* or any material impediment to its implementation, that member shall make recommendations to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* with regard to those matters within two months of the transmission of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*.
## 2.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]* shall reach a joint decision on all of the following issues:
### (a)
the review and assessment of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]*;
### (b)
the application of the measures referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.10#7|Article 10(7)]], (8), (9) and (10).
## 3.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]* shall reach a joint decision on the issues referred to in paragraph 2 within four months of the date of the transmission of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* by the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*.
ESMA may, at the request of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* within the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]*, assist the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(24)|supervisory college]]* in reaching a joint decision in accordance with point (c) of [[2010R1095_EN.31#2|Article 31(2) of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
## 4.
Where, after four months from the date of transmission of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]*, the college has failed to reach a joint decision on the issues referred to in paragraph 2, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall make its own decision.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall make the decision referred to in the first subparagraph taking into account the views of the other college members expressed during the four-month period. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall notify in writing that decision to the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* and to the other members of the college.
## 5.
Where, by the end of that four-month period, a joint decision has not been reached and a simple majority of the voting members disagree with the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]*’s proposal for a joint decision on a matter in relation to the assessment of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plans]]* or implementation of the measures pursuant to points (a), (b) and (d) of [[2021R0023_EN.10#10|Article 10(10)]] of this Regulation, any of the voting members concerned, based on that majority, may refer that matter to ESMA in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.19|Article 19 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]]. The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall await the decision taken by ESMA in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.19#3|Article 19(3) of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] and decide in accordance with the decision of ESMA.
## 6.
The four-month period shall be deemed to be the conciliation phase within the meaning of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010. ESMA shall take its decision within one month of the referral of the matter to it. The matter shall not be referred to ESMA after the end of the four-month period or after a joint decision has been reached. In the absence of an ESMA decision within one month, the decision of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* shall apply.
