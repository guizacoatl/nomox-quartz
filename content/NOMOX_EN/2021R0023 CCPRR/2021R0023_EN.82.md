# Article 82 - Specific provisions

## 1.
Member States shall ensure that their laws, regulations and administrative provisions provide for penalties and other administrative measures at least in respect of the following situations:
### (a)
failure to draw up, maintain and update *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plans]]*, in breach of [[2021R0023_EN.9|Article 9]];
### (b)
failure to provide all the information necessary for the development of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plans]]*, in breach of [[2021R0023_EN.13|Article 13]]; and
### (c)
failure of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to notify the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* when the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is failing or likely to fail, in breach of [[2021R0023_EN.70#1|Article 70(1)]].
## 2.
Member States shall ensure that, in the cases referred to in paragraph 1, the administrative penalties and other administrative measures that can be applied include at least the following:
### (a)
a public statement which indicates the natural person, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, or other legal person responsible and the nature of the infringement;
### (b)
an order requiring the natural or legal person responsible to cease the conduct and to desist from a repetition of that conduct;
### (c)
a temporary ban against the members of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or any other natural person, who is held responsible, to exercise functions in a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (d)
in the case of a legal person, administrative fines of up to 10 % of the total annual turnover of that legal person in the preceding business year. Where the legal person is a subsidiary of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(13)|parent undertaking]]*, the relevant turnover shall be the turnover resulting from the consolidated accounts of the ultimate *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(13)|parent undertaking]]* in the preceding business year;
### (e)
in the case of a natural person, administrative fines of up to EUR 5 000 000, or in the Member States where the euro is not the official currency, the corresponding value in the national currency on 11 February 2021; and
### (f)
administrative fines of up to twice the amount of the benefit derived from the infringement where that benefit can be determined.
