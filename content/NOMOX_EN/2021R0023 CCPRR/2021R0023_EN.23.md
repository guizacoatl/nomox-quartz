# Article 23 - General principles regarding resolution

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall take all appropriate measures to apply the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.27|Article 27]] and exercise the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(5)|resolution powers]]* referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.48|Article 48]] in accordance with the following principles:
### (a)
all contractual obligations and other arrangements in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* are enforced, to the extent that they have not been exhausted before entry into resolution, unless the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* determines that in order to achieve the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]* in a timely manner any of the following or both are more appropriate:
#### (i)
to refrain from enforcing certain contractual obligations under the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(22)|recovery plan]]* or otherwise deviate from it;
#### (ii)
to apply *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* or exercise the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(5)|resolution powers]]*;
### (b)
the shareholders of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution bear first losses following the enforcement of all obligations and arrangements referred to in point (a) and in accordance with that point;
### (c)
creditors of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution bear losses after the shareholders in accordance with the order of priority of their claims under *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(32)|normal insolvency proceedings]]*, unless expressly provided otherwise in this Regulation;
### (d)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s creditors of the same class are treated in an equitable manner;
### (e)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s shareholders, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* and other creditors should not incur greater losses than they would have incurred in the circumstances referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.60|Article 60]];
### (f)
the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution are replaced, except where the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* considers that the retention of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]*, in whole or in part, is necessary for the achievement of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*;
### (g)
resolution authorities inform and consult employee representatives in accordance with their national laws, collective agreements or practice;
### (h)
*[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* are applied and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(5)|resolution powers]]* are exercised without prejudice to provisions on the representation of employees in management bodies as provided for in national laws, collective agreements or practice; and
#### (i)
where a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* is part of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]*, resolution authorities take account of the impact on other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]* entities, in particular where such *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]* comprises other *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(16)|FMIs]]*, and on the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(28)|group]]* as a whole.
## 2.
Resolution authorities may take a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]* which deviates from the principles set out in points (d) or (e) of paragraph 1 of this Article where it is justified in the public interest to achieve the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]* and is proportionate to the risk addressed. However, where that deviation results in a shareholder, a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* or any other creditor incurring greater losses than it would have incurred in the circumstances referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.60|Article 60]], the entitlement to payment of the difference under [[2021R0023_EN.62|Article 62]] shall apply.
## 3.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]* of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution shall provide the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* with all necessary assistance for the achievement of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*.
