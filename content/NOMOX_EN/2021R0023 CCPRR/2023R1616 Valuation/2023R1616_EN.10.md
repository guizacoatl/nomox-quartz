# Article 10 - Impact of interoperability arrangements


Where the CCP has entered into interoperability arrangements in accordance with [[2012R0648_EN.54|Article 54 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]*, or the resolution authority where conducting a provisional valuation pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.26#1|Article 26(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], shall take into account the potential impact of such arrangements on the value of the assets and liabilities of the CCP.
