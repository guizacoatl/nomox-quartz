# Article 16 - Selection of the measurement basis

## 1.
When selecting the most appropriate *[[2023R1616_EN.1#8.|measurement basis]]* or bases, the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]*, or the resolution authority where conducting a provisional valuation pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.26#1|Article 26(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], shall take into account the range of resolution actions assessed in accordance with [[2023R1616_EN.15#1|Article 15(1)]].
## 2.
The *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]*, or the resolution authority where conducting a provisional valuation pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.26#1|Article 26(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], shall determine, on the basis of fair, prudent and realistic assumptions, the cash flows that the CCP can expect from its existing assets and liabilities following the adoption of the resolution action or actions identified and discount them at an appropriate rate determined in accordance with paragraph 6.
## 3.
Cash flows shall be determined at the appropriate level of aggregation.
## 4.
Where the resolution actions referred to in [[2023R1616_EN.15#1|Article 15(1)]] require assets and liabilities to be retained by a CCP that continues to be a going concern entity, the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]* shall use the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#4.|hold value]]* as the appropriate *[[2023R1616_EN.1#8.|measurement basis]]*.
The *[[2023R1616_EN.1#4.|hold value]]* may, if considered fair, prudent and realistic by the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]*, or the resolution authority where conducting a provisional valuation pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.26#1|Article 26(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], anticipate a normalisation of market conditions.

The *[[2023R1616_EN.1#4.|hold value]]* shall not be used as the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#8.|measurement basis]]* where assets are transferred to a bridge CCP pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.42|Article 42 of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], or where a sale of business tool pursuant to Article 40 of that Regulation is used.
## 5.
Where the resolution actions referred to in [[2023R1616_EN.15#1|Article 15(1)]] envisage the sale of assets, the expected cash flows shall correspond to the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#5.|disposal values]]* referred to in [[2023R1616_EN.17#5|Article 17(5)]] envisaged for the expected disposal horizon.
## 6.
The discount rates shall be determined having regard to the timing of cash flows, to the risk profile, financing costs and market conditions appropriate to the asset or liability being measured, to the disposal strategy considered and to the financial position of the CCP after resolution.
