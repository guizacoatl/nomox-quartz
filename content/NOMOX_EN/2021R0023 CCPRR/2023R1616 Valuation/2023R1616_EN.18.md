# Article 18 - Methodology for calculating and including a buffer for additional losses

## 1.
The *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]*, or the resolution authority where conducting a provisional valuation pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.26#1|Article 26(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], shall include in the valuation a buffer that reflects facts and circumstances supporting the existence of additional losses of uncertain amount or timing.
The assumptions supporting the calculation of the buffer shall be sufficiently explained and substantiated by the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]*, or the resolution authority where conducting a provisional valuation pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.26#1|Article 26(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]].
## 2.
When determining the amount of the buffer, the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]*, or the resolution authority where conducting a provisional valuation pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.26#1|Article 26(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], shall identify factors that could affect expected cash flows as a result of resolution actions likely to be adopted.
