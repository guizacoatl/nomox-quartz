# Article 7 - Valuation date


The valuation date shall be one of the following dates:
### (a)
a reference date as determined by the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#2.|valuer]]*, or the resolution authority where conducting a provisional valuation pursuant to [[2021R0023_EN.26#1|Article 26(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], and set as close as possible before the expected date of a decision by the resolution authority to take resolution action in relation to the CCP in accordance with [[2021R0023_EN.71|Article 71 of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]] or to exercise the power to write down or convert instruments of ownership and debt instruments or other unsecured liabilities set out in Article 33 of that Regulation;
### (b)
where a definitive valuation required by [[2021R0023_EN.26#2|Article 26(2) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]] is conducted, the *[[2023R1616_EN.1#9.|resolution decision date]]*;
### (c)
in relation to liabilities arising from contracts referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.29#1|Article 29(1) of Regulation (EU) 23/2021]], the date on which those contracts are terminated.
