# Article 48 - General powers

## 1.
The *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall have all the powers necessary to apply the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(4)|resolution tools]]* effectively, including all the following powers:
### (a)
to require any person to provide the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* with any information it requires to decide upon and prepare a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(11)|resolution action]]*, including updates and additional information to that provided in the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(10)|resolution plan]]* or required through on-site inspections;
### (b)
to take control of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution and exercise all the rights and powers conferred upon holders of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* including the rights and powers under the operating rules of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (c)
to transfer *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* issued by a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution;
### (d)
to transfer to another entity, with its consent, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s rights, assets, obligations or liabilities;
### (e)
to reduce, including to reduce to zero, the principal amount of or outstanding amount due in respect of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]* or other unsecured liabilities of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution;
### (f)
to convert *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]* or other unsecured liabilities of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution into *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* of that *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* or of a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* to which assets, rights, obligations or liabilities of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution have been transferred;
### (g)
to cancel *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]* issued by a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution;
### (h)
to reduce, including to reduce to zero, the nominal amount of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]* of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution and to cancel such *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*;
#### (i)
to require a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution to issue new *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(33)|instruments of ownership]]*, including preference shares and contingent convertible instruments;
### (j)
with regards to *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(40)|debt instruments]]* and other liabilities of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*, to amend or alter their maturity, amend the amount of interest payable, or amend the date on which interest becomes payable, including by suspending payment for a temporary period;
### (k)
to close out and terminate *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(31)|financial contracts]]*;
### (l)
to remove or replace the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(23)|board]]* and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(37)|senior management]]* of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution;
### (m)
to require the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(7)|competent authority]]* to assess the buyer of a qualifying holding in a timely manner by way of derogation from the time-limits laid down in [[2012R0648_EN.31|Article 31 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
### (n)
to reduce, including to reduce to zero, the amount of *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(42)|variation margin]]* due to a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution;
### (o)
to transfer open positions and any related assets, including relevant title transfer and security financial collateral arrangements, *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(15)|set-off arrangements]]*, and *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(47)|netting arrangements]]*, from the account of a defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* to a non-defaulting *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing member]]* in a manner consistent with [[2012R0648_EN.48|Article 48 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
### (p)
to enforce any existing and outstanding contractual obligations of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution or, where necessary to achieve the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(6)|resolution objectives]]*, refrain from enforcing such contractual obligations or otherwise deviate from the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*’s operating rules;
### (q)
to enforce any existing and outstanding obligations of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(13)|parent undertaking]]* of the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution including to provide the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* with financial support by way of guarantees or credit lines; and
### (r)
to require *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(12)|clearing members]]* to provide further contributions in cash subject to the limit referred to in [[2021R0023_EN.31|Article 31]].

Resolution authorities may exercise the powers referred to in the first subparagraph individually or in any combination.
## 2.
Unless otherwise provided for in this Regulation and the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(39)|Union State aid framework]]*, the *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(3)|resolution authority]]* shall not be subject to any of the following requirements where it exercises the powers referred to in paragraph 1:
### (a)
requirement to obtain approval or consent from any public or private person;
### (b)
requirements relating to the transfer of financial instruments, rights, obligations, assets or liabilities of a *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]* under resolution or a bridge *[[2021R0023_EN.2#(1)|CCP]]*;
### (c)
requirement to notify any public or private person;
### (d)
requirement to publish any notice or prospectus;
### (e)
requirement to file or register any document with any other authority.
