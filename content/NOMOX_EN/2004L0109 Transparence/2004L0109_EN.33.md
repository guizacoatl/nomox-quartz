# Article 33 - Review


The Commission shall by 30 June 2009 report on the operation of this Directive to the European Parliament and to the Council including the appropriateness of ending the exemption for existing debt securities after the 10-year period as provided for by [[2004L0109_EN.30#4|Article 30(4)]] and its potential impact on the European financial markets.
