# Article 28d - ESMA guidelines


After consulting the European Environment Agency and the European Union Agency for Fundamental Rights, *[[2004L0109_EN.2#9.|ESMA]]* shall issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] on the supervision of sustainability reporting by national competent authorities.
