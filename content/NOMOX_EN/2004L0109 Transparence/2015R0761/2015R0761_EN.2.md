# Article 2 - Aggregation of holdings


For the purpose of calculation of the 5 % threshold referred to in [[2015R0761_EN.9#5|Article 9(5)]] and (6) of Directive 2004/109/EC, holdings under Articles [[2015R0761_EN.9|9]], [[2015R0761_EN.10|10]] and [[2015R0761_EN.13|13]] of that Directive shall be aggregated.
