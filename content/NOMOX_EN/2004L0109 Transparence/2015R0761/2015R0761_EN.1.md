# Article 1 - Subject matter


This Regulation lays down detailed rules for the implementation of [[2015R0761_EN.9|Article 9]](6b), [[2015R0761_EN.13|Article 13]](1a)(a) and (b) and Article 13(4) of Directive 2004/109/EC.
