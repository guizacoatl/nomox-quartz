# Article 28 - Administrative measures and sanctions

## 1.
Without prejudice to the powers of competent authorities in accordance with [[2004L0109_EN.24|Article 24]] and the right of Member States to provide for and impose criminal sanctions, Member States shall lay down rules on administrative measures and sanctions applicable to breaches of the national provisions adopted in transposition of this Directive and shall take all measures necessary to ensure that they are implemented. Those administrative measures and sanctions shall be effective, proportionate and dissuasive.
## 2.
Without prejudice to [[2004L0109_EN.7|Article 7]], Member States shall ensure that where obligations apply to legal entities, in the event of a breach, sanctions can be applied, subject to the conditions laid down in national law, to the members of administrative, management or supervisory bodies of the legal entity concerned, and to other individuals who are responsible for the breach under national law.
