> Level 1 reference(s): [[2004L0109_EN.16#6]], [[2004L0109_EN.12#2]], [[2004L0109_EN.14#1]]# Article 7 - Calendar of trading days

## 1.
For the purposes of [[2007L0014_EN.12#2|Article 12(2)]] and (6), and [[2007L0014_EN.14#1|Article 14(1)]], of Directive 2004/109/EC, the calendar of trading days of the home Member State of the issuer shall apply.
## 2.
Each competent authority shall publish in its Internet site the calendar of trading days of the different regulated markets situated or operating on the territory within its jurisdiction.
