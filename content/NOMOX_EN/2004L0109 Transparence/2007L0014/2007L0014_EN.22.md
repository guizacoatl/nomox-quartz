> Level 1 reference(s): [[2004L0109_EN.23#1]]# Article 22 - Requirements equivalent to Articles 17(2)(a) and 18(2)(a) of Directive 2004/109/EC

A third country shall be deemed to set requirements equivalent to those set out in [[2007L0014_EN.17#2#a|Article 17(2)(a)]] and 18(2)(a) of Directive 2004/109/EC, as far as the content of the information about meetings is concerned, where, under the law of that country, an issuer whose registered office is in that third country is required to provide at least information on the place, time and agenda of meetings.
