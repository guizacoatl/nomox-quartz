# Article 1 - Subject matter


This Directive lays down detailed rules for the implementation of [[2007L0014_EN.2#1#i|Article 2(1)(i)]](ii), the second subparagraph of [[2007L0014_EN.5#3|Article 5(3)]], the second sentence of [[2007L0014_EN.5#4|Article 5(4)]], [[2007L0014_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]], (2) and (4), [[2007L0014_EN.10|Article 10]], [[2007L0014_EN.12#1|Article 12(1)]], (2), (4), (5) and (6), [[2007L0014_EN.12#2#a|Article 12(2)(a)]], [[2007L0014_EN.13#1|Article 13(1)]], [[2007L0014_EN.21#1|Article 21(1)]], [[2007L0014_EN.23#1|Article 23(1)]] and (6) of Directive 2004/109/EC.
