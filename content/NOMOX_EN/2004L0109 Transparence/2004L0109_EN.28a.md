# Article 28a - Breaches


[[2004L0109_EN.28b|Article 28b]] shall apply at least to the following breaches:
### (a)
failure by the issuer to make public, within the required time limit, information required under the national provisions adopted in transposition of Articles [[2004L0109_EN.4|4]], [[2004L0109_EN.5|5]], [[2004L0109_EN.6|6]], [[2004L0109_EN.14|14]] and [[2004L0109_EN.16|16]];
### (b)
failure by the natural or the legal person to notify, within the required time limit, the acquisition or disposal of a major holding in accordance with the national provisions adopted in transposition of Articles [[2004L0109_EN.9|9]], [[2004L0109_EN.10|10]], [[2004L0109_EN.12|12]], [[2004L0109_EN.13|13]] and [[2004L0109_EN.13a|13a]].
