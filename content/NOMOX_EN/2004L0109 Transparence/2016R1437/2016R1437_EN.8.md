# Article 8 - Common format for the delivery of metadata

## 1.
Each OAM shall use an Extensible Markup Language (XML) based format to deliver metadata on regulated information to the EEAP.
## 2.
Each OAM shall deliver metadata on regulated information to the EEAP in the format laid down in Section A of the Annex.
