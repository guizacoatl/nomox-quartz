## 1.
Any *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* in a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* that settles in that system on its own account or on behalf of a third party transactions in *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(35)|transferable securities]]*, *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(37)|money-market instruments]]*, *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(38)|units in collective investment undertakings]]* and *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(39)|emission allowances]]* shall settle such transactions on the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(12)|intended settlement date]]*.
## 2.
As regards transactions in *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(35)|transferable securities]]* referred to in paragraph 1 which are executed on *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(42)|trading venues]]*, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(12)|intended settlement date]]* shall be no later than on the second *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(14)|business day]]* after the trading takes place. That requirement shall not apply to transactions which are negotiated privately but executed on a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(42)|trading venue]]*, to transactions which are executed bilaterally but reported to a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(42)|trading venue]]* or to the first transaction where the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(35)|transferable securities]]* concerned are subject to initial recording in book-entry form pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.3#2|Article 3(2)]].
## 3.
The competent authorities shall ensure that paragraph 1 is applied.

The authorities competent for the supervision of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(42)|trading venues]]* shall ensure that paragraph 2 is applied.
