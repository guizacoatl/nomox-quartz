# Article 56 - Extension of the banking-type ancillary services

## 1.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* that intends to extend the banking-type ancillary services for which it designates a credit institution or that it provides itself in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.54|Article 54]], shall submit a request for extension to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]*.
## 2.
The request for extension shall be subject to the procedure under [[2014R0909_EN.55|Article 55]].
