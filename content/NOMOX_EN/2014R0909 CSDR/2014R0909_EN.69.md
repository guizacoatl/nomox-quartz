# Article 69 - Transitional provisions

## 1.
The competent authorities shall communicate to ESMA those institutions that operate as *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* by 16 December 2014.
## 2.
*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* shall apply for all authorisations that are necessary for the purposes of this Regulation and shall notify the relevant *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(29)|CSD links]]* within six months from the date of entry into force of all the regulatory technical standards adopted under Articles [[2014R0909_EN.17|17]], [[2014R0909_EN.26|26]], [[2014R0909_EN.45|45]], [[2014R0909_EN.47|47]] and [[2014R0909_EN.48|48]], and, where relevant, Articles [[2014R0909_EN.55|55]] and [[2014R0909_EN.59|59]].
## 3.
Within six months from the later of the date of entry into force of the regulatory technical standards adopted under Articles [[2014R0909_EN.12|12]], [[2014R0909_EN.17|17]], [[2014R0909_EN.25|25]], [[2014R0909_EN.26|26]], [[2014R0909_EN.45|45]], [[2014R0909_EN.47|47]] and [[2014R0909_EN.48|48]], and, where relevant, Articles [[2014R0909_EN.55|55]] and [[2014R0909_EN.59|59]] or the implementing decision referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.25#9|Article 25(9)]], a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(2)|third-country CSD]]* shall apply for recognition from ESMA where it intends to provide its services on the basis of [[2014R0909_EN.25|Article 25]].
## 4.
The national rules on authorisation of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* shall continue to apply until the date when a decision is made under this Regulation on the authorisation of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* and of their activities, including *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(29)|CSD links]]*, or until 17 January 2025, whichever is earlier.
## 4a.
The national rules on recognition of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(2)|third-country CSDs]]* shall continue to apply until the date when a decision is made under this Regulation on the recognition of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(2)|third-country CSDs]]* and of their activities, or until 17 January 2027, whichever is earlier.

A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(2)|third-country CSD]]* that provides the core services referred to in Section A, points 1 and 2, of the Annex in relation to *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* constituted under the law of a Member State referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.49#1|Article 49(1)]], second subparagraph, pursuant to the applicable national rules on recognition of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(2)|third-country CSDs]]* shall notify ESMA thereof within two years of 16 January 2024.

ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards to specify the information that the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(2)|third-country CSD]]* is required to provide to ESMA in the notification referred to in the second subparagraph. Such information shall be limited to what is strictly necessary including, where applicable and available:
### (a)
the number of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* to whom the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(2)|third-country CSD]]* provides or intends to provide the services referred to in the second subparagraph;
### (b)
the categories of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* in respect of which the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(2)|third-country CSD]]* provides such services; and
### (c)
the total volume and value of such *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]*.

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 17 January 2025.

Power is delegated to the Commission to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
## 4b.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(2)|third-country CSD]]* that provided the core service referred to in Section A, point 3, of the Annex in relation to *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* constituted under the law of a Member State referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.49#1|Article 49(1)]] second subparagraph, before 17 January 2026 shall submit the notification referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.25|Article 25]](2a) by 17 January 2026.
## 4c.
Where a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* has submitted a complete application for recognition in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.25#4|Article 25(4)]], (5) and (6) before 16 January 2024 but ESMA has not issued a decision in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.25#6|Article 25(6)]] by that date, the national rules on recognition of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* shall continue to apply until the ESMA decision is issued.
## 5.
*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* operated by the entities referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.1#4|Article 1(4)]] shall comply with the requirements of this Regulation at the latest within one year from the date of entry into force of the regulatory technical standards referred to in paragraph 2.
## 6.
The delegated act adopted pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.7#14|Article 7(14)]] as applicable before 16 January 2024 shall continue to apply until the date of application of the delegated act adopted pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.7#5|Article 7(5)]].

The delegated act adopted pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.7#15|Article 7(15)]], points (a), (b) and (g), as applicable before 16 January 2024 shall continue to apply until the date of application of the delegated act adopted pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.7#10|Article 7(10)]].
## 7.
The competent authorities shall establish colleges pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.24a|Article 24a]] within one month of the date of entry into force of the regulatory technical standards adopted under [[2014R0909_EN.24a#13|Article 24a(13)]].
## 8.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* that, in another Member State, provided core services referred to in Section A, points 1 and 2, of the Annex or set up a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]* in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.23|Article 23]] as applicable before 16 January 2024 shall be subject to the procedure set out in [[2014R0909_EN.23#3|Article 23(3)]] to (6) only in relation to:
### (a)
the setting up of a new *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]*;
### (b)
a change in the range of those services.
