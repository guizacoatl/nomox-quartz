# Article 68 - Committee procedure

## 1.
The Commission shall be assisted by the European *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|Securities]]* Committee established by Commission Decision 2001/528/EC. That Committee shall be a committee within the meaning of Regulation (EU) No 182/2011.
## 2.
Where reference is made to this paragraph, [[2011R0182_EN.5|Article 5 of Regulation (EU) No 182/2011]] shall apply.
## 3.
Where reference is made to this paragraph, [[2011R0182_EN.8|Article 8 of Regulation (EU) No 182/2011]], in conjunction with [[2014R0909_EN.5|Article 5]] thereof, shall apply.
