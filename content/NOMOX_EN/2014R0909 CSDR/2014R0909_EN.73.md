# Article 73 - Application of Directive 2014/65/EU and Regulation (EU) No 600/2014


*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* authorised in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.16|Article 16]] of this Regulation shall not require authorisation under Directive 2014/65/EU in order to provide the services explicitly listed in Sections A and B of the Annex to this Regulation.

Where a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* authorised in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.16|Article 16]] of this Regulation provides one or more investment services or carries out one or more investment activities in addition to providing the services explicitly listed in Sections A and B of the Annex to this Regulation, Directive 2014/65/EU with the exception of Articles [[2014R0909_EN.5|5]] to 8, [[2014R0909_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]] to (2) and (4) to (6) and Articles [[2014R0909_EN.10|10]] to 13, and Regulation (EU) No 600/2014 shall apply.
