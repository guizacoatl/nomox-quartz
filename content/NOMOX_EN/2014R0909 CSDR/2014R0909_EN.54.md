# Article 54 - Authorisation and designation to provide banking-type ancillary services

## 1.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall not itself provide any banking-type ancillary services set out in Section C of the Annex unless it has obtained an additional authorisation to provide such services in accordance with this Article.
## 2.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* that intends to settle the cash payments for all or part of its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement systems]]* through its own accounts in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.40#2|Article 40(2)]] or that otherwise intends to provide any banking-type ancillary services referred to in paragraph 1 shall be authorised under the conditions specified in paragraphs 3, 6, 7, 8 and 9a of this Article.
## 2a.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* that intends to settle the cash payments for all or part of its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement systems]]* through accounts opened with a credit institution or with a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.40#2|Article 40(2)]] shall be authorised, under the conditions specified in paragraphs 3 to 9a of this Article, to designate for that purpose one or more:
### (a)
credit institutions authorised in accordance with [[2013L0036_EN.8|Article 8 of Directive 2013/36/EU]]; or
### (b)
*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* authorised to provide banking-type ancillary services pursuant to paragraph 3 of this Article.

An authorisation to designate credit institutions or *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* in accordance with the first subparagraph shall only be used with regard to the banking-type ancillary services referred to in Section C of the Annex for the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* of the cash payments for all or part of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement systems]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* seeking to use the banking-type ancillary services, and not to carry out any other activities.

The credit institutions and *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* authorised to provide banking-type ancillary services designated in accordance with the first subparagraph shall be considered to be *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(43)|settlement agents]]*.
## 3.
Where a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* seeks to provide any banking-type ancillary services from within the same legal entity as the legal entity operating the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* the authorisation referred to in paragraph 2 shall be granted only where the following conditions are met:
### (a)
the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* is authorised as a credit institution as provided for in [[2013L0036_EN.8|Article 8 of Directive 2013/36/EU]];
### (b)
the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* meets the prudential requirements laid down in [[2014R0909_EN.59#1|Article 59(1)]], (3) and (4) and the supervisory requirements laid down in [[2014R0909_EN.60|Article 60]];
### (c)
the authorisation referred to in point (a) of this subparagraph is used only to provide the banking-type ancillary services referred to in Section C of the Annex and not to carry out any other activities;
### (d)
the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* is subject to an additional capital surcharge that reflects the risks, including credit and liquidity risks, resulting from the provision of intra-day credit, inter alia, to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* in a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* or other users of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* services;
### (e)
the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* reports at least monthly to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* and annually as a part of its public disclosure as required under Part Eight of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 on the extent and management of intra-day liquidity risk in accordance with point (j) of [[2014R0909_EN.59#4|Article 59(4)]] of this Regulation;
### (f)
the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* has submitted to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* an adequate recovery plan to ensure continuity of its critical operations, including in situations where liquidity or credit risk crystallises as a result of the provision of banking-type ancillary services.

In the case of conflicting provisions laid down in this Regulation, in Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 and in Directive 2013/36/EU, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* referred to in point (a) of the first subparagraph shall comply with the stricter requirements on prudential supervision. The regulatory technical standards referred to in Articles [[2014R0909_EN.47|47]] and [[2014R0909_EN.59|59]] of this Regulation shall clarify the cases of conflicting provisions.
## 4.
Where all of the following conditions are met, a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* may be authorised to designate a credit institution to provide banking-type ancillary services for the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* of the cash payments for all or part of that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*’s *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement systems]]* pursuant to paragraph 2a, point (a):
### (a)
the credit institution meets the prudential requirements laid down in [[2014R0909_EN.59#1|Article 59(1)]], (3) and (4) and the supervisory requirements laid down in [[2014R0909_EN.60|Article 60]];
### (b)
the credit institution does not itself carry out any of the core services referred to in Section A of the Annex;
### (c)
the authorisation under [[2013L0036_EN.8|Article 8 of Directive 2013/36/EU]] is used only to provide the banking-type ancillary services referred to in Section C of the Annex for the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* of the cash payments for all or part of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement systems]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* seeking to use the banking-type ancillary services, and not to carry out any other activities;
### (d)
the credit institution is subject to an additional capital surcharge that reflects the risks, including credit and liquidity risks, resulting from the provision of intra-day credit, inter alia, to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* in a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* or other users of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* services;
### (e)
the credit institution reports at least monthly to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* and discloses to the public annually as a part of its public disclosure as required under Part Eight of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 on the extent and management of intra-day liquidity risk in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.59#4|Article 59(4)]], point (j), of this Regulation; and
### (f)
the credit institution has submitted to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* an adequate recovery plan to ensure continuity of its critical operations, including in situations where liquidity or credit risk materialises as a result of the provision of banking-type ancillary services from within a separate legal entity.
## 4a.
Where a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* seeks to designate a credit institution or a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* in accordance with paragraph 2a to settle the cash payments for all or part of its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement systems]]*, such cash payments shall not be in a currency of the country where the designating *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* is established.
## 5.
Paragraph 4 shall not apply to credit institutions referred to in point (b) of paragraph 2 that offer to settle the cash payments for part of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*’s *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]*, if the total value of such cash *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* through accounts opened with those credit institutions, calculated over a one-year period, is less than one per cent of the total value of all *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* transactions against cash settled in the books of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* and does not exceed a maximum of EUR 2,5 billion per year.

The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall monitor at least once per year that the threshold defined in the first subparagraph is respected and report its findings to ESMA. Where the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* determines that the threshold has been exceeded, it shall require the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* concerned to seek authorisation in accordance with paragraph 4. The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* concerned shall submit its application for authorisation within six months.
## 6.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* may require a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* to designate more than one credit institution, or to designate a credit institution in addition to providing services itself in accordance with point (a) of paragraph 2 of this Article where it considers that the exposure of one credit institution to the concentration of risks under [[2014R0909_EN.59#3|Article 59(3)]] and (4) is not sufficiently mitigated. The designated credit institutions shall be considered to be *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(43)|settlement agents]]*.
## 7.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* authorised to provide any banking-type ancillary services and a credit institution designated in accordance with point (b) of paragraph 2 shall comply at all times with the conditions necessary for authorisation under this Regulation and shall, without delay, notify the competent authorities of any substantive changes affecting the conditions for authorisation.
## 8.
EBA shall, in close cooperation with ESMA and the members of the ESCB, develop draft regulatory technical standards to determine the additional risk-based capital surcharge referred to in paragraph 3, point (d), and paragraph 4, point (d).

EBA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 18 June 2015.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1093_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1093_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010.
## 9.
EBA shall, in close cooperation with the members of the ESCB and ESMA, develop draft regulatory technical standards to determine the threshold referred to in paragraph 5 and accompanying appropriate risk management and prudential requirements to mitigate risks in relation to the designation of credit institutions in accordance with paragraph 2a. When developing those standards, EBA shall take into account the following:
### (a)
the implications for the market stability that could derive from a change of risk profile of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* and their *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]*, including the systemic importance of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* for the functioning of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* markets;
### (b)
the implications for the credit and liquidity risks for *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]*, for the designated credit institutions involved and for the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* that result from the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* of cash payments through accounts opened with credit institutions that are not subject to paragraph 4;
### (c)
the possibility for *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* to settle cash payments in several currencies;
### (d)
the need to avoid both an unintended shift from *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* in central bank money to *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* in commercial bank money and disincentives to the efforts of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* to settle in central bank money; and
### (e)
the need to ensure a level playing field amongst *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* in the Union.

EBA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 17 January 2025.

Power is delegated to the Commission to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1093_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1093_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010.
