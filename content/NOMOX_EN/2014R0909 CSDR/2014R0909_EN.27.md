# Article 27 - Senior management, management body and shareholders

## 1.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(46)|senior management]]* of a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall be of sufficiently good repute and experience so as to ensure the sound and prudent management of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*.
## 2.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall have a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* of which at least one third, but no less than two, of its members are independent.

For the purposes of this Article, an independent member of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* means a member of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* who has no business, family or other relationship that raises a conflict of interest regarding the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* concerned or its controlling shareholders, its management or its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]*, and who has had no such relationship during the five years preceding their membership of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]*.
## 3.
The remuneration of the independent and other non-executive members of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* shall not be linked to the business performance of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*.
## 4.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* shall be composed of suitable members of sufficiently good repute with an appropriate mix of skills, experience and knowledge of the entity and of the market. The non-executive members of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* shall decide on a target for the representation of the under-represented gender in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* and prepare a policy on how to increase the number of the under-represented gender in order to meet that target. The target, policy and its implementation shall be made public.
## 5.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall clearly determine the role and responsibilities of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* in accordance with the relevant national law. A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall make the minutes of the meetings of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* available to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* and the auditor upon request.
## 6.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall not authorise a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* unless it has been informed of the identities of the shareholders or members, whether direct or indirect, natural or legal persons, that have *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(49)|qualifying holdings]]* in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* and of the amounts of those holdings.
## 7.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall refuse to authorise a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* where it is not satisfied as to the suitability of the shareholders or members that have *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(49)|qualifying holdings]]* in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*, taking into account the need to ensure the sound and prudent management of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*.
## 8.
Where *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(48)|close links]]* exist between the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* and other natural or legal persons, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall grant authorisation only where those links do not prevent the effective exercise of the supervisory functions of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]*.
## 9.
Where the persons referred to in paragraph 6 exercise an influence which is likely to be prejudicial to the sound and prudent management of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall take appropriate measures to put an end to that situation, which may include the withdrawal of the authorisation of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*.
## 10.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall refuse authorisation where the laws, regulations or administrative provisions of a third country governing one or more natural or legal persons with which the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* has *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(48)|close links]]*, or where difficulties involved in the enforcement of those laws, regulations or administrative provisions, prevent the effective exercise of the supervisory functions of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]*.
## 11.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall, without delay:
### (a)
provide the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* with information regarding the ownership of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*, and, in particular, the identity and scale of interests of any person having a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(49)|qualifying holding]]* in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*;
### (b)
make public:
#### (i)
the information provided to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* under point (a); and
#### (ii)
the transfer of ownership rights that results in a change in *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(21)|control]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*.
