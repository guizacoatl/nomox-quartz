# Article 10 - Competent authority


Without prejudice to the oversight by the members of the ESCB referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.12#1|Article 12(1)]], a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall be authorised and supervised by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]*.
