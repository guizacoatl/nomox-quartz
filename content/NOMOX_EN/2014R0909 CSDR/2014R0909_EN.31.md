# Article 31 - Services provided by parties other than CSDs

## 1.
Notwithstanding [[2014R0909_EN.30|Article 30]] and where required by national law, a person other than *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* may be responsible for recording book entries into *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(28)|securities accounts]]* maintained by *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]*.
## 2.
Member States that allow parties other than *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* to provide certain core services referred to in Section A of the Annex in accordance with paragraph 1 shall specify in their national law the requirements that will apply in such a case. Those requirements shall include the provisions of this Regulation which shall apply both to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* and, where relevant, to the other party concerned.
## 3.
Member States that allow parties other than *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* to provide certain core services referred to in Section A of the Annex in accordance with paragraph 1 shall communicate to ESMA all the relevant information concerning the provision of such services, including their relevant national law.

ESMA shall include such information in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* register referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.21|Article 21]].
