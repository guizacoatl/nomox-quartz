# Article 51 - Customised link access

## 1.
Where a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* requests another *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* to establish a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(31)|customised link]]* for having access to the latter, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(5)|receiving CSD]]* shall reject such a request only on the basis of risk considerations. It shall not deny a request on the grounds of loss of market share.
## 2.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(5)|receiving CSD]]* may charge a reasonable commercial fee on a cost-plus basis to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(6)|requesting CSD]]* for making *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(31)|customised link]]* access available, unless otherwise agreed by both parties.
