# Article 16 - Authorisation of a CSD

## 1.
Any legal person that falls within the definition of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall obtain an authorisation from the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the Member State where it is established before commencing its activities.
## 2.
The authorisation shall specify the core services listed in Section A of the Annex and non-banking-type ancillary services permitted under Section B of the Annex, which the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* is authorised to provide.
## 3.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall comply at all times with the conditions necessary for authorisation.
## 4.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* as well as its independent auditors, shall, without undue delay, inform the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of any substantive changes affecting the compliance with the conditions for authorisation.
