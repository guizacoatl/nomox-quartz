# Article 26 - General provisions

## 1.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall have robust governance arrangements, which include a clear organisational structure with well-defined, transparent and consistent lines of responsibility, effective processes to identify, manage, monitor and report the risks to which it is or might be exposed, and adequate remuneration policies and internal *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(21)|control]]* mechanisms, including sound administrative and accounting procedures.
## 2.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall adopt policies and procedures which are sufficiently effective so as to ensure compliance with this Regulation, including compliance of its managers and employees with all the provisions of this Regulation.

Where a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* intends to provide banking-type ancillary services to other *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.54|Article 54]](2a), first subparagraph, point (b), that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall have in place clear rules and procedures addressing potential conflicts of interest and mitigating the risk of discriminatory treatment towards those other *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* and their *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]*.
## 3.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall maintain and operate effective written organisational and administrative arrangements to identify and manage any potential conflicts of interest between its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* or their clients and the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* itself, including:
### (a)
the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*’s managers;
### (b)
the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*’s employees;
### (c)
the members of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*’s *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]*;
### (d)
any person with direct or indirect *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(21)|control]]* over the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*;
### (e)
any person with *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(48)|close links]]* with any of the persons listed in points (a), (b) and (c); and
### (f)
any person with *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(48)|close links]]* with the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* itself.

A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall maintain and implement adequate resolution procedures where possible conflicts of interest occur.
## 4.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall make its governance arrangements and the rules governing its activity available to the public.
## 5.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall have appropriate procedures for its employees to report internally potential infringements of this Regulation through a specific channel.
## 6.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall be subject to regular and independent audits. The results of these audits shall be communicated to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* and made available to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* and, where appropriate taking into account potential conflicts of interest between the members of the user committee and the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*, to the user committee.
## 7.
Where a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* is part of a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(47)|group]]* of undertakings including other *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* or credit institutions referred to in Title IV, it shall adopt detailed policies and procedures specifying how the requirements laid down in this Article apply to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(47)|group]]* and to the different entities in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(47)|group]]*.
## 8.
ESMA shall, in close cooperation with the members of the ESCB, develop draft regulatory technical standards specifying at the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* level and at the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(47)|group]]* level as referred to in paragraph 7:
### (a)
the monitoring tools for the risks of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* referred to in paragraph 1;
### (b)
the responsibilities of the key personnel in respect of the risks of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* referred to in paragraph 1;
### (c)
the potential conflicts of interest referred to in paragraph 3;
### (d)
the audit methods referred to in paragraph 6; and
### (e)
the circumstances in which it would be appropriate, taking into account potential conflicts of interest between the members of the user committee and the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*, to share audit findings with the user committee in accordance with paragraph 6.

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 18 June 2015.

Power is delegated to the Commission to adopt the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
## 9.
EBA shall, in close cooperation with ESMA and the members of the ESCB, develop draft regulatory technical standards to further specify the details of the rules and procedures referred to in paragraph 2, second subparagraph.

EBA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 17 January 2025.

Power is delegated to the Commission to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1093_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1093_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010.
