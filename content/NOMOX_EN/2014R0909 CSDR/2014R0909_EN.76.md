# Article 76 - Entry into force and application

## 1.
This Regulation shall enter into force on the twentieth day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union .
## 2.
[[2014R0909_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]] shall apply from 1 January 2023 to *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(35)|transferable securities]]* issued after that date and from 1 January 2025 to all *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(35)|transferable securities]]*.
## 3.
[[2014R0909_EN.5#2|Article 5(2)]] shall apply from 1 January 2015.

By way of derogation from the first subparagraph of this paragraph, in the case of a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(42)|trading venue]]* that has access to a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.30#5|Article 30(5)]], [[2014R0909_EN.5#2|Article 5(2)]] shall apply at least six months before such a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* outsources its activities to the relevant public entity, and in any event from 1 January 2016.
## 4.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* discipline measures referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.6#1|Article 6(1)]] to (4) shall apply from the date of entry into force of the delegated act adopted by the Commission pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.6#5|Article 6(5)]].
## 5.
Each of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* discipline measures referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.7#1|Article 7(1)]] to (13) shall apply from the date of application specified for each *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* discipline measure in the delegated act adopted by the Commission pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.7#15|Article 7(15)]].

An *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(41)|MTF]]* that complies with the criteria laid down in [[2014L0065_EN.33#3|Article 33(3) of Directive 2014/65/EU]] shall be subject to the second subparagraph of [[2014R0909_EN.7#3|Article 7(3)]] of this Regulation:
### (a)
until the final determination of its application for registration under [[2014L0065_EN.33|Article 33 of Directive 2014/65/EU]]; or
### (b)
where an *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(41)|MTF]]* has not applied for registration under [[2014L0065_EN.33|Article 33 of Directive 2014/65/EU]], until
13 June 2018

.
## 6.
The reporting measures referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]] shall apply from the date of entry into force of the implementing act adopted by the Commission pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.9#3|Article 9(3)]].
## 7.
References in this Regulation to Directive 2014/65/EU and Regulation (EU) No 600/2014 shall, before
3 January 2018

, be read as references to Directive 2004/39/EC in accordance with the correlation table set out in Annex IV to Directive 2014/65/EU in so far as that correlation table contains provisions referring to Directive 2004/39/EC.
