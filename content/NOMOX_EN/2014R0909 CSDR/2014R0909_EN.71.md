# Article 71 - Amendments to Directive 2014/65/EU


Directive 2014/65/EU is amended as follows:
## (1)
in [[2014R0909_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (o) is replaced by the following:
*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* except as provided for in [[2014R0909_EN.73|Article 73 of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] of the European Parliament and of the Council.
## (2)
in [[2014R0909_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], the following point is added:
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|central securities depository]]*’ or ‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*’ means a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|central securities depository]]* as defined in point (1) of [[2014R0909_EN.2#1|Article 2(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]].’;
## (3)
in Section B of Annex I, point (1) is replaced by the following:
Safekeeping and administration of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* for the account of clients, including custodianship and related services such as cash/collateral management and excluding providing and maintaining *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(28)|securities accounts]]* at the top tier level (‘central maintenance service’) referred to in point (2) of Section A of the Annex to the Regulation (EU) No 909/2014.’.
