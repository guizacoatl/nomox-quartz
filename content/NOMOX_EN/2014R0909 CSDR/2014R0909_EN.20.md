# Article 20 - Withdrawal of authorisation

## 1.
Without prejudice to any remedial actions or measures under Title V, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* shall withdraw the authorisation in any of the following circumstances, where the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*:
### (a)
has not made use of the authorisation during 12 months, expressly renounces the authorisation or has provided no services or performed no activity during the preceding six months;
### (b)
has obtained the authorisation by making false statements or by any other unlawful means;
### (c)
no longer complies with the conditions under which authorisation was granted and has not taken the remedial actions requested by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* within a set time-frame;
### (d)
has seriously or systematically infringed the requirements laid down in this Regulation or, where applicable, in Directive 2014/65/EU or Regulation (EU) No 600/2014.
## 2.
From the moment when it becomes aware of one of the circumstances referred to in paragraph 1, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall immediately consult the relevant authorities and, where applicable, the authority referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.67|Article 67 of Directive 2014/65/EU]] on the necessity to withdraw the authorisation.
## 3.
ESMA and any *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(18)|relevant authority]]* and, where applicable, the authority referred to in [[2014L0065_EN.67|Article 67 of Directive 2014/65/EU]] may, at any time, request that the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* examines whether the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* still complies with the conditions under which the authorisation was granted.
## 4.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* may limit the withdrawal of authorisation to a particular service, activity, or financial instrument.
## 5.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall establish, implement and maintain adequate procedures ensuring the timely and orderly *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* and transfer of the assets of clients and *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* to another *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* in the event of a withdrawal of authorisation referred to in paragraph 1. Such procedures shall include the transfer of issuance accounts or similar records evidencing the issuance of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]*, and records linked to the provision of the core services referred to in Section A, points 1 and 2, of the Annex.
