# Article 43 - Legal risks

## 1.
For the purpose of its authorisation and supervision, as well as for the information of its clients, a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall have rules, procedures, and contracts that are clear and understandable for all the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement systems]]* that it operates and all other services that it provides.
## 2.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall design its rules, procedures and contracts so that they are enforceable in all relevant jurisdictions, including in the case of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(26)|default]]* of a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]*.
## 3.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* conducting business in different jurisdictions shall take all reasonable steps to identify and mitigate the risks arising from potential conflicts of law across jurisdictions.
