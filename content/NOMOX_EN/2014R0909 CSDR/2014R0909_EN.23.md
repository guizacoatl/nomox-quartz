# Article 23 - Freedom to provide services in another Member State

## 1.
An authorised *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* may provide services referred to in the Annex within the territory of the Union, including through setting up a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]*, provided that those services are covered by the authorisation.
## 2.
An authorised *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* or a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* that has applied for authorisation pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.17|Article 17]] that intends to provide the core services referred to in Section A, points 1 and 2, of the Annex in relation to *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* constituted under the law of another Member State referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.49#1|Article 49(1)]], second subparagraph, point (a), or to set up a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]* in another Member State shall be subject to the procedure referred to in paragraphs 3 to 9 of this Article. The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* may provide such services only after it has been authorised pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.17|Article 17]] and not earlier than the date applicable in accordance with paragraph 8 of this Article.
## 3.
Any *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* that intends to provide the services referred to in paragraph 2 in relation to *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* constituted under the law of another Member State referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.49#1|Article 49(1)]], second subparagraph, point (a), for the first time, or to change the range of those services provided, shall communicate the following information to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]*:
### (a)
the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]*;
### (b)
a programme of operations stating in particular the services which the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* intends to provide, including the type of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* constituted under the law of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* in respect of which the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* intends to provide such services;
### (c)
the currency or currencies that the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* intends to process;
### (d)
an assessment of the measures the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* intends to take to allow its users to comply with the law of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.49#1|Article 49(1)]], second subparagraph, point (a), in relation to *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(36)|shares]]*.
## 4.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* intending to set up a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]* in another Member State for the first time or to change the range of the core service referred to in Section A, point 1, of the Annex, or of the core service referred to in Section A, point 2, of the Annex, provided through a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]*, shall communicate the following information to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]*:
### (a)
the information referred to in paragraph 3, points (a), (b) and (c);
### (b)
the organisational structure of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]* and the names of the persons responsible for the management of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]*;
### (c)
an assessment of the measures that the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* intends to take to allow its users to comply with the law of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.49#1|Article 49(1)]], second subparagraph, point (a), in relation to *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(36)|shares]]*.
## 5.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* shall communicate the assessment referred to in paragraph 3, point (d), or in paragraph 4, point (c), as applicable, to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* without undue delay. The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* may provide a non-binding opinion on that assessment to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* within one month of receipt of that assessment.
## 6.
Within two months of receipt of the complete information referred to in paragraph 3, points (a), (b) and (c), or paragraph 4, points (a) and (b), as applicable, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* shall communicate that information to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* unless, by taking into account the provision of services envisaged, it has reasons to doubt the adequacy of the administrative structure or the financial situation of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* intending to provide services in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* or the adequacy of the measures the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* intends to take in accordance with paragraph 3, point (d), or in paragraph 4, point (c), as applicable. Within that period, where the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* already provides services to other *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member States]]*, including through a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]*, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* shall also inform the college referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.24a|Article 24a]].

The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* shall without delay inform the relevant authorities of that Member State of any communication received under the first subparagraph.

The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* shall immediately inform the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* of the date of transmission of the communication referred to in the first subparagraph.
## 7.
Where the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* decides in accordance with paragraph 6 not to communicate the information referred to in paragraph 3 or paragraph 4, as applicable, to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]*, it shall provide the reasons for its refusal to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* concerned within two months of receipt of that information and inform the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* and the college referred to in Article 24a of its decision.
## 8.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* may start providing services or set up a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]* as referred to in paragraph 2 at the earliest 15 calendar days after the date of transmission of the communication referred to in paragraph 6, first subparagraph, from the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]*.
## 9.
In the event of a change to the information set out in the documents submitted in accordance with paragraph 3 or paragraph 4, as applicable, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall give written notice of the change to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* at least one month before implementing the change. The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* and the college referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.24a|Article 24a]] shall also be informed of that change without delay by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]*.
## 10.
ESMA may issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] to specify the scope of the assessment that the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* is required to provide under paragraph 3, point (d), and paragraph 4, point (c), of this Article.
