# Article 50 - Standard link access


A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall have the right to become a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* of another *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* and set up a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(30)|standard link]]* with that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.33|Article 33]] and subject to the prior notification of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(29)|CSD link]]* provided under [[2014R0909_EN.19#5|Article 19(5)]].
