# Article 3 - Book-entry form

## 1.
Without prejudice to paragraph 2, any issuer established in the Union that issues or has issued *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(35)|transferable securities]]* which are admitted to trading or traded on *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(42)|trading venues]]*, shall arrange for such *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* to be represented in book-entry form as *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(3)|immobilisation]]* or subsequent to a direct issuance in *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(4)|dematerialised form]]*.
## 2.
Where a transaction in *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(35)|transferable securities]]* takes place on a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(42)|trading venue]]* the relevant *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* shall be recorded in book-entry form in a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* on or before the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(12)|intended settlement date]]*, unless they have already been so recorded.

Where *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(35)|transferable securities]]* are transferred following a financial collateral arrangement as defined in point (a) of Article 2(1) of Directive 2002/47/EC, those *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* shall be recorded in book-entry form in a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* on or before the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(12)|intended settlement date]]*, unless they have already been so recorded.
