# Article 21 - CSD register

## 1.
Decisions taken by competent authorities under Articles [[2014R0909_EN.16|16]], [[2014R0909_EN.19|19]] and [[2014R0909_EN.20|20]] shall be immediately communicated to ESMA.
## 2.
Central banks shall without undue delay inform ESMA of any *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* that they operate.
## 3.
The name of each *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* operating in compliance with this Regulation and to which authorisation or recognition has been granted pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.16|Article 16]], 19 or 25 shall be entered in a register specifying the services and, where applicable, classes of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* for which the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* has been authorised. The register shall include branches operated by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* in other Member States, *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(29)|CSD links]]* and the information required under [[2014R0909_EN.31|Article 31]] where Member States have made use of the possibility provided for in that Article. ESMA shall make the register available on its dedicated website and keep it up to date.
