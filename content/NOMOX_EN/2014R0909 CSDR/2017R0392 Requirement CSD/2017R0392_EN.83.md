> Level 1 reference(s): [[2014R0909_EN.46]]# Article 83 - Concentration limits to individual entities

## 1.
For the purposes of [[2014R0909_EN.46#5|Article 46(5) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]], a CSD shall hold its financial assets in diversified authorised credit institutions or authorised CSDs in order to remain within acceptable concentration limits.
## 2.
For the purposes of [[2014R0909_EN.46#5|Article 46(5) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]], acceptable concentration limits shall be determined based on the following:
### (a)
the geographic distribution of the entities with which the CSD holds its financial assets;
### (b)
the interdependency relationships that the entity holding the financial assets or entities of its group may have with the CSD;
### (c)
the level of credit risk of the entity holding the financial assets.
