> Level 1 reference(s): [[2014R0909_EN.17]]# Article 35 - Capital requirements


An application for authorisation shall include the following information concerning the capital requirements:
### (a)
information demonstrating that the capital of the applicant CSD, including retained earnings and reserves of the applicant CSD, meets the requirements of [[2014R0909_EN.47|Article 47 of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]];
### (b)
the plan referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.47#2|Article 47(2) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] and any updates to that plan, and evidence of its approval by the management body or an appropriate committee of the management body of the applicant CSD.
