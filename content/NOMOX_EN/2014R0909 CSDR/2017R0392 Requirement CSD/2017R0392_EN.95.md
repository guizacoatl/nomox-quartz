# Article 95 - Transitional provisions

## 1.
Information referred to in [[2017R0392_EN.17#2|Article 17(2)]] of this Regulation, shall be provided to the competent authority at the latest six months before the date referred to in [[2017R0392_EN.96#2|Article 96(2)]].
## 2.
Information referred to in [[2017R0392_EN.24#2|Article 24(2)]] of this Regulation shall be provided to the competent authority at the latest six months before the date referred to in [[2017R0392_EN.96#2|Article 96(2)]].
## 3.
Information referred to in points (j) and (r) of [[2017R0392_EN.41|Article 41]] and in points (d), (f), (h), (i), and (j) of [[2017R0392_EN.42#1|Article 42(1)]] of this Regulation shall be provided from the date referred to in [[2017R0392_EN.96#2|Article 96(2)]].
