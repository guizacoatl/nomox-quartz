# Article 56 - Ancillary Services Records

## 1.
A CSD shall keep the types of records specified in Annex II to this Regulation for each of the ancillary services provided by a CSD in accordance with Sections B and C of the Annex to Regulation (EU) No 909/2014, including the end-of-day balances of the cash accounts provided by the CSD or the designated credit institution for each currency.
## 2.
Where a CSD provides ancillary services other than those explicitly mentioned in Sections B or C of the Annex to Regulation (EU) No 909/2014, it shall keep adequate records of those services.
