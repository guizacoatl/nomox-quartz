# Article 37 - Access rules


An application for authorisation shall include a description of the procedures for dealing with the following requests for access:
### (a)
from legal persons intending to become participants in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.33|Article 33 of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] and Chapter XIII of this Regulation;
### (b)
from issuers in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.49|Article 49 of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] and Chapter XIII of this Regulation;
### (c)
from other CSDs in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.52|Article 52 of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] and Chapter XIII of this Regulation;
### (d)
from other market infrastructures in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.53|Article 53 of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] and Chapter XIII of this Regulation.
