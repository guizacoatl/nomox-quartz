> Level 1 reference(s): [[2014R0909_EN.17]]# Article 6 - Information concerning services and activities of the CSD


The applicant CSD shall include the following in the application for authorisation:
### (a)
a detailed description of the services referred to in points (l) to (p) of [[2017R0392_EN.4#2|Article 4(2)]];
### (b)
the procedures to be applied in the provision of the services referred to in point (a).
