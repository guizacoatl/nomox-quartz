# Article 5 - General information concerning policies and procedures

## 1.
An application for authorisation shall specify the following information on the policies and procedures of the applicant CSD referred to in this Chapter:
### (a)
the job titles of the persons responsible for the approval and implementation of the policies and procedures;
### (b)
a description of the measures implementing and monitoring the compliance with the policies and procedures.
## 2.
An application for authorisation shall include a description of the procedures put in place by the applicant CSD pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.65#3|Article 65(3) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]].
