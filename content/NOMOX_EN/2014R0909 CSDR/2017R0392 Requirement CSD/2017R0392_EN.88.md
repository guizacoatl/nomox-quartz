> Level 1 reference(s): [[2014R0909_EN.53#4]], [[2014R0909_EN.52#3]], [[2014R0909_EN.49#5]], [[2014R0909_EN.33#5]]# Article 88 - Receiving and requesting parties

## 1.
For the purposes of this Chapter, a receiving party shall include one of the following entities:
### (a)
a receiving CSD as defined in point (5) of [[2014R0909_EN.2#1|Article 2(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]], in respect of paragraphs 1, 4, 9, 13 and 14 of Article 89 and [[2017R0392_EN.90|Article 90]] of this Regulation;
### (b)
a CSD which receives a request from a participant, an issuer, a central counterparty (CCP) or a trading venue to have access to its services in accordance with Articles [[2017R0392_EN.33|33]](2), 49(2) and 53(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014 in respect of paragraphs 1 to 3, 5 to 8 and 10 to 14 of Article 89 and [[2017R0392_EN.90|Article 90]] of this Regulation;
### (c)
a CCP which receives a request from a CSD to have access to its transaction feeds in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.53#1|Article 53(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] in respect of [[2017R0392_EN.90|Article 90]] of this Regulation;
### (d)
a trading venue which receives a request from a CSD to have access to its transaction feeds in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.53#1|Article 53(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] in respect of [[2017R0392_EN.90|Article 90]] of this Regulation.
## 2.
For the purposes of this Chapter, a requesting party shall include one of the following entities:
### (a)
a requesting CSD as defined in point (6) of [[2014R0909_EN.2#1|Article 2(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] in respect of paragraphs 1, 4, 9 and 13 of Article 89 and [[2017R0392_EN.90|Article 90]] of this Regulation;
### (b)
a participant, an issuer, a CCP or a trading venue which requests access to the securities settlement system operated by a CSD or to other services provided by a CSD in accordance with Articles [[2017R0392_EN.33|33]](2), 49(2) and 53(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014 in respect of paragraphs 1 to 3, 5 to 8 and 10 to 14 of Article 89 and [[2017R0392_EN.90|Article 90]] of this Regulation;
### (c)
a CSD which requests access to the transaction feeds of a CCP in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.53#1|Article 53(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] in respect of [[2017R0392_EN.90|Article 90]] of this Regulation;
### (d)
a CSD which requests access to the transaction feeds of a trading venue in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.53#1|Article 53(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] in respect of [[2017R0392_EN.90|Article 90]] of this Regulation.
