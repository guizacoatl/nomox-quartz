> Level 1 reference(s): [[2014R0909_EN.25#6]]# Article 46 - Content of the application

## 1.
An application for recognition shall include the information set out in Annex I.
## 2.
An application for recognition shall:
### (a)
be provided in a durable medium;
### (b)
be submitted in both paper form and electronic form, the latter using open source formats that may be read easily;
### (c)
be submitted in a language customary in the sphere of international finance, including translations therein where the original documents are not drawn up in a language customary in the sphere of international finance;
### (d)
be provided with a unique reference number for each document included.
## 3.
The applicant CSD shall provide evidence certifying the information included in Annex I.
