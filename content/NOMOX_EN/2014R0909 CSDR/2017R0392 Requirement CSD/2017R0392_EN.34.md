# Article 34 - Investment policy


An application for authorisation shall include evidence demonstrating that:
### (a)
the applicant CSD holds its financial assets in accordance with [[2017R0392_EN.46#1|Article 46(1)]], (2) and (5) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014 and Chapter XI of this Regulation.
### (b)
the investments of the applicant CSD are compliant with [[2014R0909_EN.46#3|Article 46(3) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] and Chapter XI of this Regulation.
