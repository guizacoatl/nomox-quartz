# Article 36 - General provisions


For each *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* it operates a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall have appropriate rules and procedures, including robust accounting practices and *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(21)|controls]]*, to help ensure the integrity of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* issues, and minimise and manage the risks associated with the safekeeping and *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* of transactions in *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]*.
