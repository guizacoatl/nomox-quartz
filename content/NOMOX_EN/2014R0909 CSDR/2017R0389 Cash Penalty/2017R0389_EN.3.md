# Article 3 - Reference price of the transaction

## 1.
The reference price referred to in [[2017R0389_EN.2|Article 2]] shall be equal to the aggregated market value of the financial instruments determined in accordance with [[2017R0389_EN.7|Article 7]] for each business day that the transaction fails to be settled.
## 2.
The reference price referred to in paragraph 1 shall be used to calculate the level of cash penalties for all settlement fails, irrespective of whether the settlement fail is due to a lack of securities or cash.
