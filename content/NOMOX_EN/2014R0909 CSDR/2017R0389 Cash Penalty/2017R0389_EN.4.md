# Article 4 - Criteria for establishing the substantial importance of a CSD


The operations of a CSD in a host Member State shall be considered to be of substantial importance for the functioning of the securities markets and the protection of investors in the host Member State where at least one of the criteria specified in Articles [[2017R0389_EN.5|5]] and [[2017R0389_EN.6|6]] is fulfilled.
