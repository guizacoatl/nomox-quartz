# Article 2 - Calculation of cash penalties


The level of cash penalties referred to in the third subparagraph of [[2014R0909_EN.7#2|Article 7(2) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] for settlement fails of transactions in a given financial instrument shall be calculated by applying the relevant penalty rate set out in the Annex to this Regulation to the reference price of the transaction determined in accordance with [[2017R0389_EN.3|Article 3]] of this Regulation.
