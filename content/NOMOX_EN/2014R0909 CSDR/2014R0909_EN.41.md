# Article 41 - Participant default rules and procedures

## 1.
For each *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* it operates, a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall have effective and clearly defined rules and procedures to manage the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(26)|default]]* of one or more of its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* ensuring that the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* can take timely action to contain losses and liquidity pressures and continue to meet its obligations.
## 2.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall make its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(26)|default]]* rules and relevant procedures available to the public.
## 3.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall undertake with its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* and other relevant stakeholders periodic testing and review of its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(26)|default]]* procedures to ensure that they are practical and effective.
## 4.
In order to ensure consistent application of this Article, ESMA may, in close cooperation with the members of the ESCB, issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
