# Article 27b - Assessment

## 1.
When assessing the notification provided for in [[2014R0909_EN.27a#2|Article 27a(2)]] and the information referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.27a#4|Article 27a(4)]], the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall, in order to ensure the sound and prudent management of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* in which an acquisition is proposed and having regard to the likely influence of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]* on the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*, assess the suitability of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]* and the financial soundness of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]* against all of the following:
### (a)
the reputation and financial soundness of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]*;
### (b)
the reputation, knowledge, skills and experience of any person who will direct the business of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* as a result of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]*;
### (c)
whether the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* will be able to comply and continue to comply with this Regulation;
### (d)
whether there are reasonable grounds to suspect that, in connection with the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]*, money laundering or terrorist financing within the meaning of [[2015L0849_EN.1|Article 1 of Directive (EU) 849/2015]] is being or has been committed or attempted, or that the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]* could increase the risk thereof.

When assessing the financial soundness of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]*, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall pay particular attention to the type of business pursued and envisaged in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* in which the acquisition is proposed.

When assessing the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*’s ability to comply with this Regulation, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall pay particular attention to whether the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(47)|group]]* of which it will become a part has a structure that makes it possible to exercise effective supervision, to effectively exchange information among the competent authorities and to determine the allocation of responsibilities among the competent authorities.
## 2.
The competent authorities may oppose the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]* only where there are reasonable grounds for doing so on the basis of the criteria set out in paragraph 1 or where the information provided by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]* is incomplete.
## 3.
Member States shall neither impose any prior conditions in respect of the level of holding that is to be acquired nor allow their competent authorities to examine the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]* in terms of the economic needs of the market.
## 4.
Member States shall make available to the public a list specifying the information that is necessary to carry out the assessment and that shall be provided to the competent authorities at the time of the notification referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.27a#2|Article 27a(2)]]. The information required shall be proportionate and shall be adapted to the nature of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]* and the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]*. Member States shall not require information that is not relevant for a prudential assessment.
## 5.
Notwithstanding [[2014R0909_EN.27a#2|Article 27a(2)]] to (5), where two or more proposals to acquire or increase *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(49)|qualifying holdings]]* in the same *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* have been notified to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]*, the latter shall treat the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirers]]* in a non-discriminatory manner.
## 6.
The competent authorities shall, without undue delay, provide each other with any information which is essential for or relevant to the assessment. The competent authorities shall, upon request, communicate all relevant information to each other and shall communicate all essential information at their own initiative. A decision by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* that has authorised the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* in which the acquisition is proposed shall indicate any views or reservations expressed by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* responsible for the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]*.
## 7.
ESMA shall, in close cooperation with EBA, issue guidelines in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]] on the assessment of suitability of any person who will direct the business of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*, as well as on the procedural rules and evaluation criteria for the prudential assessment of direct or indirect acquisitions of, and increases in, holdings in *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]*.
