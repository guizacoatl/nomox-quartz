> Level 1 reference(s): [[2014R0909_EN.59]]# Article 9 - General rules on collateral and other equivalent financial resources

## 1.
A CSD-banking service provider shall fulfil the following conditions with regard to collateral:
### (a)
it shall clearly distinguish the collateral from the other securities of the borrowing participant;
### (b)
it shall accept collateral that meets the conditions of [[2017R0390_EN.10|Article 10]], or other types of collateral that meet the requirements of [[2017R0390_EN.11|Article 11]] in the following hierarchy:
#### (i)
firstly accept as collateral all the securities in the account of the borrowing participant that meet the requirements of [[2017R0390_EN.10|Article 10]] and only those;
#### (ii)
secondly accept as collateral all the securities in the account of the borrowing participant that meet the requirements set out in [[2017R0390_EN.11#1|Article 11(1)]] and only those;
#### (iii)
finally accept as collateral all the securities in the account of the borrowing participant that meet the requirements set out in [[2017R0390_EN.11#2|Article 11(2)]], within the limits of available qualifying liquid resources referred to in [[2017R0390_EN.34|Article 34]] with the view to meeting the minimum liquid resources requirement referred to in [[2017R0390_EN.35#3|Article 35(3)]];
### (c)
it shall monitor on at least a daily basis the credit quality, market liquidity and price volatility of each security accepted as collateral and value it in accordance with [[2017R0390_EN.12|Article 12]];
### (d)
it shall specify methodologies related to the haircuts applied to the collateral value in accordance with [[2017R0390_EN.13|Article 13]];
### (e)
it shall ensure that the collateral remains sufficiently diversified to allow its liquidation within the periods referred to in Articles [[2017R0390_EN.10|10]] and [[2017R0390_EN.11|11]] without a significant market impact, in accordance with [[2017R0390_EN.14|Article 14]].
## 2.
Collateral shall be provided by the counterparties under a security financial collateral arrangement as defined in point (c) of Article 2(1) of Directive 2002/47/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council or under a title transfer financial collateral arrangement as defined in point (b) of Article 2(1) of that Directive.
## 3.
A CSD-banking service provider shall fulfil the conditions of [[2017R0390_EN.15|Article 15]] and 16 with regard to other equivalent financial resources.
