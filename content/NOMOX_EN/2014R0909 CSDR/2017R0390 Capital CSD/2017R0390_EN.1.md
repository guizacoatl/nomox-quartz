> Level 1 reference(s): [[2014R0909_EN.47]]# Article 1 - Overview of requirements regarding the capital of a CSD

## 1.
For the purposes of [[2014R0909_EN.47#1|Article 47(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]], a central securities depository (‘CSD’) shall hold at all times, together with retained earnings and reserves, the amount of capital specified in [[2017R0390_EN.3|Article 3]] of this Regulation.
## 2.
The capital requirements referred to in [[2017R0390_EN.3|Article 3]] shall be met with capital instruments that meet the conditions set out in [[2017R0390_EN.2|Article 2]] of this Regulation.
