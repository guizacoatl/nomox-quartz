# Article 3 - Level of capital requirements for a CSD

## 1.
A CSD shall hold capital, together with retained earnings and reserves, which shall be at all times more than or equal to the sum of:
### (a)
the CSD's capital requirements for operational, legal and custody risks, referred to in point (a) of [[2014R0909_EN.47#1|Article 47(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]], calculated in accordance with [[2017R0390_EN.4|Article 4]];
### (b)
the CSD's capital requirements for investment risks, referred to in point (a) of [[2014R0909_EN.47#1|Article 47(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]], calculated in accordance with [[2017R0390_EN.5|Article 5]];
### (c)
the CSD's capital requirements for business risks, referred to in point (a) of [[2014R0909_EN.47#1|Article 47(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]], calculated in accordance with [[2017R0390_EN.6|Article 6]];
### (d)
the CSD's capital requirements for winding-down or restructuring its activities, referred to in point (b) of [[2014R0909_EN.47#1|Article 47(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]], calculated in accordance with [[2017R0390_EN.7|Article 7]].
## 2.
A CSD shall have procedures in place to identify all sources of the risks referred to in paragraph 1.
