# Article 24 - Buy-in agent


A buy-in agent shall not have any conflict of interest in the execution of a buy-in and shall execute the buy-in on the terms most favourable to the failing clearing member, trading venue member or trading party, as applicable, in accordance with [[2014L0065_EN.27|Article 27 of Directive 2014/65/EU]].
