> Level 1 reference(s): [[2014R0909_EN.7#4#a]]# Article 36 - Extension periods


In accordance with point (a) of [[2014R0909_EN.7#4|Article 7(4) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]], the extension period for the financial instruments referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.5#1|Article 5(1) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] shall be increased from four to seven business days for all financial instruments other than shares that have a liquid market as referred to in point (b) of Article 2(1)(17) of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014.
