# Article 19 - Penalty mechanism for settlement fails relating to cleared transactions submitted by CCPs for settlement


With respect to settlement fails relating to cleared transactions submitted by CCPs for settlement, CSDs shall apply Articles [[2018R1229_EN.16|16]], [[2018R1229_EN.17|17]] and [[2018R1229_EN.18|18]].

CCPs may allocate to their clearing members any remaining net amount of penalties, credit or debit, paid pursuant to [[2018R1229_EN.16|Article 16]] and distributed pursuant to [[2018R1229_EN.17#2|Article 17(2)]].

CCPs shall establish an appropriate mechanism in their rules to that effect.
