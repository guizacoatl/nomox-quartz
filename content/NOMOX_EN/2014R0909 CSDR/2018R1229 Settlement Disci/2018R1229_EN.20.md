# Article 20 - CSDs that use a common settlement infrastructure


CSDs that use a common settlement infrastructure, including where some of their services or activities have been outsourced as referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.30#5|Article 30(5) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]], shall jointly establish the penalty mechanism referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.7#2|Article 7(2) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] and jointly manage the modalities for the calculation, application, collection and distribution of cash penalties in accordance with this Regulation.
