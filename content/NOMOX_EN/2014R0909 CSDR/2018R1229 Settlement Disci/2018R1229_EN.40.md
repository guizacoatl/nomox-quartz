# Article 40 - Settlement information for CCPs and trading venues


The settlement information referred to in the second subparagraph of [[2014R0909_EN.7#10|Article 7(10) of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014]] shall include the identification of the relevant transactions, of the participants and of the relevant settlement instructions. That information shall be based on the information in the securities settlement system the CSD operates.
