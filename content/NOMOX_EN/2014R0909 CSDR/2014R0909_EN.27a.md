# Article 27a - Information to competent authorities

## 1.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall notify its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of any changes to its management and provide the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* with all the information necessary to assess its compliance with [[2014R0909_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]] to (5).

Where the conduct of a member of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* is likely to be prejudicial to the sound and prudent management of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall take appropriate measures, which may include removing that member from the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]*.
## 2.
Any natural or legal person or such persons acting in concert (the ‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]*’), who have taken a decision either to acquire, directly or indirectly, a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(49)|qualifying holding]]* in a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* or to further increase, directly or indirectly, such a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(49)|qualifying holding]]* in a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* as a result of which the proportion of the voting rights or of the capital held would reach or exceed 10 %, 20 %, 30 % or 50 % or would lead to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* becoming its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(22)|subsidiary]]* (the ‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]*’), shall first notify the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* in writing thereof, indicating the size of the intended holding and relevant information, as referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.27b#4|Article 27b(4)]].

Any natural or legal person who has taken a decision to dispose, directly or indirectly, of a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(49)|qualifying holding]]* in a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* (the ‘proposed vendor’) shall first notify the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* in writing thereof, indicating the size of such holding. Such a person shall likewise notify the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* where it has taken a decision to reduce a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(49)|qualifying holding]]* so that the proportion of the voting rights or of the capital held would fall below 10 %, 20 %, 30 % or 50 % or so that the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* would cease to be that person’s *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(22)|subsidiary]]*.
## 3.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall, promptly and in any event within two working days of receipt of the notification referred to in paragraph 2 and of the information referred to in paragraph 4, acknowledge receipt in writing thereof to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]* or proposed vendor.

The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall have a maximum of 60 working days after the date of the written acknowledgement of receipt of the notification and all documents required to be attached to the notification on the basis of the list referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.27b#4|Article 27b(4)]] (the ‘assessment period’), to carry out the assessment provided for in [[2014R0909_EN.27b#1|Article 27b(1)]] (the ‘assessment’).

The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* shall inform the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]* or proposed vendor of the date of the expiry of the assessment period at the time of acknowledging the receipt.
## 4.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* may, during the assessment period, but no later than on the 50th working day of the assessment period, request any further information that is necessary to complete the assessment. Such a request shall be made in writing and shall specify the additional information needed.

The assessment period shall be suspended for the period between the date of the request for information by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* and the receipt of a response thereto by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]*. The suspension shall not exceed 20 working days. Any further requests by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* for completion or clarification of the information shall be at its discretion but shall not result in a suspension of the assessment period.
## 5.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* may extend the suspension referred to in paragraph 4, second subparagraph, to up to 30 working days where the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]* is situated or regulated outside the Union or is a natural or legal person not subject to supervision under this Regulation or under Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 or under Directive 2009/65/EC, 2009/138/EC (
## 7
) or 2011/61/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council, or Directive 2013/36/EU or 2014/65/EU.
## 6.
Where the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]*, upon completion of the assessment, decides to oppose the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]*, it shall, within two working days, and not exceeding the assessment period, inform the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]* in writing and provide the reasons for that decision. Subject to national law, an appropriate statement of the reasons for the decision may be made available to the public upon request of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]*. However, a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* may make such disclosure also in the absence of a request by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquirer]]* if so provided for by national law.
## 7.
Where the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* does not oppose the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]* within the assessment period, it shall be deemed to be approved.
## 8.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* may fix a maximum period for concluding the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#2.|proposed acquisition]]* and may extend that period where appropriate.
## 9.
Member States shall not impose requirements for notification to, and approval by, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of direct or indirect acquisitions of voting rights or capital that are more stringent than those set out in this Regulation.
