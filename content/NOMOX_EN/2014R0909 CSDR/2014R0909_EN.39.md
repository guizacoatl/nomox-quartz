# Article 39 - Settlement finality

## 1.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall ensure that the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* it operates offers adequate protection to *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]*. Member States shall designate and notify the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement systems]]* operated by *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* according to the procedures referred to in point (a) of Article 2 of Directive 98/26/EC.
## 2.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall ensure that each *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* that it operates defines the moments of entry and of irrevocability of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(9)|transfer orders]]* in that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* in accordance with Articles [[2014R0909_EN.3|3]] and [[2014R0909_EN.5|5]] of Directive 98/26/EC.
## 3.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall disclose the rules governing the finality of transfers of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* and cash in a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]*.
## 4.
Paragraphs 2 and 3 shall apply without prejudice to the provisions applicable to *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(29)|CSD links]]*, and without prejudice to paragraph 8 of [[2014R0909_EN.48|Article 48]].
## 5.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall take all reasonable steps to ensure that, in accordance with the rules referred to in paragraph 3, finality of transfers of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* and cash referred to in paragraph 3 is achieved either in real time or intra-day and in any case no later than by the end of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(14)|business day]]* of the actual *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* date.
## 6.
Where the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* offers the services referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.40#2|Article 40(2)]], it shall ensure that the cash proceeds of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlements]]* shall be available for recipients to use no later than by the end of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(14)|business day]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(12)|intended settlement date]]*.
## 7.
All *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* transactions against cash between direct *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* in a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* operated by a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* and settled in that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* shall be settled on a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(27)|DVP]]* basis.
