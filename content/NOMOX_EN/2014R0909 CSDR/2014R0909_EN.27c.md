# Article 27c - Derogation for CSDs providing banking-type ancillary services


Articles [[2014R0909_EN.27a|27a]] and [[2014R0909_EN.27b|27b]] shall not apply to a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* which has been authorised pursuant to [[2014R0909_EN.54#3|Article 54(3)]] and is subject to Directive 2013/36/EU.
