# Article 32 - General provisions

## 1.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall have clearly defined goals and objectives that are achievable, such as in the areas of minimum service levels, risk-management expectations and business priorities.
## 2.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall have transparent rules for the handling of complaints.
