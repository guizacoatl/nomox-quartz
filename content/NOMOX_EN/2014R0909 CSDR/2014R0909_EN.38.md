# Article 38 - Protection of securities of participants and those of their clients

## 1.
For each *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* it operates, a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall keep records and accounts that shall enable it, at any time and without delay, to segregate in the accounts with the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* of a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* from those of any other *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* and, if applicable, from the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*’s own assets.
## 2.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall keep records and accounts that enable any *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* to segregate the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* from those of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]*’s clients.
## 3.
## 4.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall keep records and accounts that enable a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* to segregate the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* of any of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]*’s clients, if and as required by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* (‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#4.|individual client segregation]]*’).
## 5.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* shall offer its clients at least the choice between *[[2014R0909_EN.2#3.|omnibus client segregation]]* and *[[2014R0909_EN.2#4.|individual client segregation]]* and inform them of the costs and risks associated with each option.

However, a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* and its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* shall provide individual clients segregation for citizens and residents of, and legal persons established in, a Member State where required under the national law of the Member State under which the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* are constituted as it stands at 17 September 2014. That obligation shall apply as long as the national law is not amended or repealed and its objectives are still valid.
## 6.
*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* and their *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* shall publicly disclose the levels of protection and the costs associated with the different levels of segregation that they provide and shall offer those services on reasonable commercial terms. Details of the different levels of segregation shall include a description of the main legal implications of the respective levels of segregation offered, including information on the insolvency law applicable in the relevant jurisdictions.
## 7.
A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall not use for any purpose *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* that do not belong to it. A *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* may however use *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* of a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* where it has obtained that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]*’s prior express consent. The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall require its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* to obtain any necessary prior consent from their clients.
