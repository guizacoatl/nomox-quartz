# Article 2 - Definitions

## 1.
For the purposes of this Regulation, the following definitions apply:
## (1)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|central securities depository]]*’ or ‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*’ means a legal person that operates a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* referred to in point (3) of Section A of the Annex and provides at least one other core service listed in Section A of the Annex;
## (2)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(2)|third-country CSD]]*’ means any legal entity established in a third country that provides a similar service to the core service referred to in point (3) of Section A of the Annex and performs at least one other core service listed in Section A of the Annex;
## (3)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(3)|immobilisation]]*’ means the act of concentrating the location of physical *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* in a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* in a way that enables subsequent transfers to be made by book entry;
## (4)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(4)|dematerialised form]]*’ means the fact that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* exist only as book entry records;
## (5)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(5)|receiving CSD]]*’ means the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* which receives the request of another *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* to have access to its services through a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(29)|CSD link]]*;
## (6)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(6)|requesting CSD]]*’ means the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* which requests access to the services of another *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* through a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(29)|CSD link]]*;
## (7)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]*’ means the completion of a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* transaction where it is concluded with the aim of discharging the obligations of the parties to that transaction through the transfer of cash or *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]*, or both;
## (8)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]*’ or ‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]*’ means *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* as defined in point (15) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
## (9)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(9)|transfer order]]*’ means *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(9)|transfer order]]* as defined in the second indent of point (i) of Article 2 of Directive 98/26/EC;
## (10)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]*’ means a system under the first, second and third indents of point (a) of Article 2 of Directive 98/26/EC that is not operated by a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(16)|central counterparty]]* whose activity consists of the execution of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(9)|transfer orders]]*;
## (11)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(11)|settlement internaliser]]*’ means any institution, including one authorised in accordance with Directive 2013/36/EU or with Directive 2014/65/EU, which executes *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(9)|transfer orders]]* on behalf of clients or on its own account other than through a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]*;
## (12)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(12)|intended settlement date]]*’ means the date that is entered into the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* as the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* date and on which the parties to a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* transaction agree that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* is to take place;
## (13)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(13)|settlement period]]*’ means the time period between the trade date and the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(12)|intended settlement date]]*;
## (14)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(14)|business day]]*’ means *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(14)|business day]]* as defined in point (n) of Article 2 of Directive 98/26/EC;
## (15)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(15)|settlement fail]]*’ means the non-occurrence of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]*, or partial *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* of a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* transaction on the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(12)|intended settlement date]]*, due to a lack of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* or cash and regardless of the underlying cause;
## (16)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(16)|central counterparty]]*’ or ‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(16)|CCP]]*’ means a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(16)|CCP]]* as defined in point (1) of [[2012R0648_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]];
## (17)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]*’ means the authority designated by each Member State in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.11|Article 11]], unless otherwise specified in this Regulation;
## (18)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(18)|relevant authority]]*’ means any authority referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.12|Article 12]];
## (19)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]*’ means any *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]*, as defined in point (f) of Article 2 of Directive 98/26/EC in a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]*;
## (20)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(20)|participation]]*’ means *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(20)|participation]]* within the meaning of the first sentence of point (2) of [[2013L0034_EN.2|Article 2 of Directive 2013/34/EU]], or the ownership, direct or indirect, of 20 % or more of the voting rights or capital of an undertaking;
## (21)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(21)|control]]*’ means the relationship between two undertakings as described in [[2013L0034_EN.22|Article 22 of Directive 2013/34/EU]];
## (22)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(22)|subsidiary]]*’ means a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(22)|subsidiary]]* undertaking within the meaning of Article 2(10) and [[2013L0034_EN.22|Article 22 of Directive 2013/34/EU]];
## (23)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]*’ means the Member State in which a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* is established;
## (24)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]*’ means the Member State, other than the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]*, in which a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* has a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]* or provides *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* services;
## (25)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]*’ means a place of business other than the head office which is a part of a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*, which has no legal personality and which provides *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* services for which the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* has been authorised;
## (26)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(26)|default]]*’ means, in relation to a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]*, a situation where insolvency proceedings, as defined in [[2014R0909_EN.2|Article 2]], point (j), of Directive 98/26/EC, are opened against a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* or an event defined in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*’s internal rules as constituting a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(26)|default]]*;
## (27)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(27)|delivery versus payment]]*’ or ‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(27)|DVP]]*’ means a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* mechanism which links a transfer of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* with a transfer of cash in a way that the delivery of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* occurs if and only if the corresponding transfer of cash occurs and vice versa;
## (28)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(28)|securities account]]*’ means an account on which *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* may be credited or debited;
## (29)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(29)|CSD link]]*’ means an arrangement between *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* whereby one *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* becomes a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* of another *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* in order to facilitate the transfer of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* from the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* of the latter *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* of the former *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* or an arrangement whereby a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* accesses another *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* indirectly via an intermediary. *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(29)|CSD links]]* include *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(30)|standard links]]*, *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(31)|customised links]]*, *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(32)|indirect links]]*, and *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(33)|interoperable links]]*;
## (30)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(30)|standard link]]*’ means a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(29)|CSD link]]* whereby a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* becomes a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* of another *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* under the same terms and conditions as applicable to any other *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* operated by the latter;
## (31)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(31)|customised link]]*’ means a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(29)|CSD link]]* whereby a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* that becomes a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* of another *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* is provided with additional specific services to the services normally provided by that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* to *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]*;
## (32)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(32)|indirect link]]*’ means an arrangement between a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* and a third party other than a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*, that is a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participant]]* in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* of another *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*. Such link is set up by a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* in order to facilitate the transfer of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* to its *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* from the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* of another *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*;
## (33)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(33)|interoperable link]]*’ means a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(29)|CSD link]]* whereby *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* agree to establish mutual technical solutions for *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement systems]]* that they operate;
## (34)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(34)|international open communication procedures and standards]]*’ means internationally accepted standards for communication procedures, such as standardised messaging formats and data representation, which are available on a fair, open and non-discriminatory basis to any interested party;
## (35)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(35)|transferable securities]]*’ means *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(35)|transferable securities]]* as defined in point (44) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
## (36)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(36)|shares]]*’ means *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* specified in point (44)(a) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
## (37)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(37)|money-market instruments]]*’ means *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(37)|money-market instruments]]* as defined in point (17) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
## (38)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(38)|units in collective investment undertakings]]*’ means *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(38)|units in collective investment undertakings]]* as referred to in point (3) of Section C of Annex I to Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (39)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(39)|emission allowance]]*’ means *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(39)|emission allowance]]* as described in point (11) of Section C of Annex I to Directive 2014/65/EU, excluding derivatives in *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(39)|emission allowances]]*;
## (40)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(40)|regulated market]]*’ means *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(40)|regulated market]]* as defined in point (21) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
## (41)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(41)|multilateral trading facility]]*’ or ‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(41)|MTF]]*’ means *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(41)|multilateral trading facility]]* as defined in point (22) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
## (42)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(42)|trading venue]]*’ means a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(42)|trading venue]]* as defined in point (24) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
## (43)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(43)|settlement agent]]*’ means *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(43)|settlement agent]]* as defined in point (d) of Article 2 of Directive 98/26/EC;
## (44)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(44)|SME growth market]]*’ means an *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(44)|SME growth market]]* as defined in point (12) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
## (45)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]*’ means the body or bodies of a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*, appointed in accordance with national law, which is empowered to set the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*’s strategy, objectives and overall direction, and which oversees and monitors management decision-making and includes persons who effectively direct the business of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*.

Where, according to national law, a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* comprises different bodies with specific functions, the requirements of this Regulation shall apply only to members of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* to whom the applicable national law assigns the respective responsibility;
## (46)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(46)|senior management]]*’ means those natural persons who exercise executive functions within a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* and who are responsible and accountable to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]* for the day-to-day management of that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*;
## (47)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(47)|group]]*’ means a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(47)|group]]* within the meaning of [[2014R0909_EN.2|Article 2]], point (11), of Directive 2013/34/EU;
## (48)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(48)|close links]]*’ means *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(48)|close links]]* as defined in [[2014R0909_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (35), of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (49)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(49)|qualifying holding]]*’ means a direct or indirect holding in a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* which represents at least 10 % of the capital or of the voting rights, as set out in Articles [[2014R0909_EN.9|9]], [[2014R0909_EN.10|10]] and [[2014R0909_EN.11|11]] of Directive 2004/109/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council, or which makes it possible to exercise a significant influence over the management of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*;
## (50)
‘*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(50)|deferred net settlement]]*’ means a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* mechanism whereby cash or *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(9)|transfer orders]]* in relation to *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* transactions of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement system]]* are subject to netting, and whereby *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]*’ net claims and obligations takes place at the end of predefined *[[2014R0909_EN.2#3.|settlement cycles]]* during or at the end of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(14)|business day]]*.
## 2.
The Commission shall be empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.67|Article 67]] concerning measures to further specify the non-banking-type ancillary services set out in points (1) to (4) of Section B of the Annex and the banking-type ancillary services set out in Section C of the Annex.
