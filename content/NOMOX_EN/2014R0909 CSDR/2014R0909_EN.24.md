> Level 2 reference(s): [[2017R0389_EN.0]]# Article 24 - Cooperation between authorities of the home Member State and of the host Member State and peer review

## 1.
Where a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* authorised in one Member State has set up a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]* in another Member State, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* and the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* shall cooperate closely in the performance of their duties provided for in this Regulation, in particular when carrying out on-site inspections in that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]*. The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* and of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* may, in the exercise of their responsibilities, carry out on-site inspections in that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]* after informing the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* or of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* respectively.

The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* may invite staff from the competent authorities of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member States]]* and from ESMA to participate in on-site inspections.

The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* shall transmit to ESMA and to the college referred to in [[2014R0909_EN.24a|Article 24a]] the findings of the on-site inspections and information on any remedial actions or penalties decided on by that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]*.
## 2.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* or of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* may require *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* which provide services in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.23|Article 23]] to report to them periodically on their activities in that *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]*, including for the purpose of collecting statistics. The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* shall, on request from the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]*, provide those periodic reports to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]*.
## 3.
The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* shall, upon the request of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* and without delay, communicate the identity of the issuers established in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* and of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]* holding *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* constituted under the law of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(10)|securities settlement systems]]* operated by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* which provides core services as referred to in Section A, points 1 and 2, of the Annex in relation to *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* constituted under the law of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]*, and any other relevant information concerning the activities of a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* that provides core services in the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* through a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(25)|branch]]*.

## 5.
Where the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* has clear and demonstrable grounds for believing that a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* providing services within its territory in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.23|Article 23]] is in breach of the obligations arising from the provisions of this Regulation, it shall inform the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]*, ESMA and the college referred to in Article 24a of those findings.

Where, despite measures taken by the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]*, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* persists in acting in breach of the obligations arising from the provisions of this Regulation, the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* shall, after informing the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]*, take all the appropriate measures needed in order to ensure compliance with the provisions of this Regulation within the territory of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]*. The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* shall inform ESMA and the college referred to in Article 24a of such measures without undue delay.

The *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(24)|host Member State]]* or the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(17)|competent authority]]* of the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(23)|home Member State]]* may refer the matter to ESMA, which may act in accordance with the powers conferred on it under [[2010R1095_EN.19|Article 19 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
## 6.
Without prejudice to [[2010R1095_EN.30|Article 30 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]], ESMA shall, after consulting the members of the ESCB, organise and conduct, at least every three years, a peer review of the supervision of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* which make use of the freedom to provide services in another Member State in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.23|Article 23]] or to participate in an *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(33)|interoperable link]]*.

In the context of the peer review referred to in the first subparagraph ESMA shall, where appropriate, also request opinions or advice from the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|Securities]]* and Markets Stakeholder *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(47)|Group]]* referred to in [[2010R1095_EN.37|Article 37 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].

## 8.
ESMA shall, in close cooperation with the members of the ESCB, develop draft implementing technical standards to establish standard forms, templates and procedures for the cooperation referred to in paragraphs 1, 3 and 5.

ESMA shall submit those draft implementing technical standards to the Commission by 18 June 2015.

Power is conferred on the Commission to adopt the implementing technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
