# Article 58 - CSD register

## 1.
Decisions taken by competent authorities under Articles [[2014R0909_EN.54|54]], [[2014R0909_EN.56|56]] and [[2014R0909_EN.57|57]] shall be notified to ESMA.
## 2.
ESMA shall introduce in the register, that it is required to make available on its dedicated website in accordance with [[2014R0909_EN.21#3|Article 21(3)]], the following information:
### (a)
the name of each *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* which was subject to a decision under Articles [[2014R0909_EN.54|54]], [[2014R0909_EN.56|56]] and [[2014R0909_EN.57|57]];
### (b)
the name of each designated credit institution;
### (c)
the list of banking-type ancillary services that a designated credit institution or a *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* authorised under [[2014R0909_EN.54|Article 54]] is authorised to provide for the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]*’s *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(19)|participants]]*.
## 3.
The competent authorities shall notify to ESMA those entities that provide banking-type ancillary services according to requirements of national law by 16 December 2014.
