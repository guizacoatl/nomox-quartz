# Article 1 - Subject matter and scope

## 1.
This Regulation lays down uniform requirements for the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* in the Union and rules on the organisation and conduct of central *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|securities]]* depositories (*[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]*) to promote safe, efficient and smooth *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]*.
## 2.
This Regulation applies to the *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(7)|settlement]]* of all *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]* and activities of *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSDs]]* unless otherwise specified in this Regulation.
## 3.
This Regulation is without prejudice to provisions of Union law concerning specific *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(8)|financial instruments]]*, in particular Directive 2003/87/EC.
## 4.
Articles [[2014R0909_EN.10|10]] to 20, 22 to 24 and 27, [[2014R0909_EN.28#6|Article 28(6)]], Article 30(4) and Articles [[2014R0909_EN.46|46]] and [[2014R0909_EN.47|47]], the provisions of Title IV and the requirements to report to competent authorities or relevant authorities or to comply with their orders under this Regulation, do not apply to the members of the ESCB, other Member States’ national bodies performing similar functions, or to other public bodies charged with or intervening in the management of public debt in the Union in relation to any *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(1)|CSD]]* which the aforementioned bodies directly manage under the responsibility of the same *[[2014R0909_EN.2#(45)|management body]]*, which has access to the funds of those bodies and which is not a separate entity.
