# Article 10 - Transition provision


Until 16 April 2027, UCITS that were constituted before 16 April 2026 shall be deemed to comply with this Regulation. Those UCITS may, however, choose to be subject to this Regulation from 16 April 2026, provided that they notify the competent authorities of their home Member State thereof.
