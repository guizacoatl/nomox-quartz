# Article 91 - 1.


UCITS host Member States shall ensure that UCITS are able to market their units within their territories upon notification in accordance with [[2009L0065_EN.93|Article 93]].
## 2.
UCITS host Member States shall not impose any additional requirements or administrative procedures on UCITS as referred to in paragraph 1 in respect of the field governed by this Directive.

—————
## 4.
For the purposes of this Chapter, a UCITS shall include investment compartments thereof.
