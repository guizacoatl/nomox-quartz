> Level 1 reference(s): [[2009L0065_EN.20#1#b]]# Article 3 - Information to be provided under Article 20(1), point (b), of Directive 2009/65/EC


The information that management companies are to provide under [[2024R0911_EN.20#1|Article 20(1)]], point (b), of Directive 2009/65/EC shall contain all the following:
### (a)
a list of investment management or administration functions subject to delegation;
### (b)
the name, address and contact details of the delegate.
