# Article 26b - The Commission shall be empowered to adopt delegated acts in accordance with Article 112a specifying:

### (a)
the particulars that need to be included in the written contract referred to in [[2009L0065_EN.22#2|Article 22(2)]];
### (b)
the conditions for performing the depositary functions pursuant to [[2009L0065_EN.22#3|Article 22(3)]], (4) and (5), including:
#### (i)
the types of financial instrument to be included in the scope of the custody duties of the depositary in accordance with point (a) of [[2009L0065_EN.22#5|Article 22(5)]];
#### (ii)
the conditions subject to which the depositary is able to exercise its custody duties over financial instruments registered with a central depositary;
#### (iii)
the conditions subject to which the depositary is to safekeep the financial instruments issued in a nominative form and registered with an issuer or a registrar, in accordance with point (b) of [[2009L0065_EN.22#5|Article 22(5)]];
### (c)
the due diligence duties of depositaries pursuant to point (c) of [[2009L0065_EN.22a#2|Article 22a(2)]];
### (d)
the segregation obligation pursuant to point (c) of [[2009L0065_EN.22a#3|Article 22a(3)]];
### (e)
the steps to be taken by the third party pursuant to point (d) of [[2009L0065_EN.22a#3|Article 22a(3)]];
### (f)
the conditions subject to which and circumstances in which financial instruments held in custody are to be considered to be lost for the purpose of [[2009L0065_EN.24|Article 24]];
### (g)
what is to be understood by external events beyond reasonable control, the consequences of which would have been unavoidable despite all reasonable efforts to the contrary, pursuant to [[2009L0065_EN.24#1|Article 24(1)]];
### (h)
the conditions for fulfilling the independence requirement referred to in [[2009L0065_EN.25#2|Article 25(2)]].
