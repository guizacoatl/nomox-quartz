> Level 1 reference(s): [[2009L0065_EN.23#5]], [[2009L0065_EN.33#5]]# Article 36 - Scope of the agreement


Member States may allow that the agreement referred to in either [[2010L0043_EN.23#5|Article 23(5)]] or Article 33(5) of Directive 2009/65/EC cover more than one UCITS managed by the management company. In such case, the agreement shall list the UCITS covered.
