# Article 7 - Electronic data processing

## 1.
Member States shall require management companies to make appropriate arrangements for suitable electronic systems so as to permit a timely and proper recording of each portfolio transaction or subscription or redemption order in order to be able to comply with Articles [[2010L0043_EN.14|14]] and [[2010L0043_EN.15|15]].
## 2.
Member States shall require management companies to ensure a high level of security during the electronic data processing as well as integrity and confidentiality of the recorded information, as appropriate.
