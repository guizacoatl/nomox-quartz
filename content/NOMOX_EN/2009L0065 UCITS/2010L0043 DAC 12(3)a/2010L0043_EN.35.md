# Article 35 - Electronic transmission of information


In cases where the parties to the agreement referred to in either [[2010L0043_EN.23#5|Article 23(5)]] or Article 33(5) of Directive 2009/65/EC agree to the use of electronic transmission for part or all of information that flows between them, Member States shall require that such agreement contains provisions ensuring that a record is kept of such information.
