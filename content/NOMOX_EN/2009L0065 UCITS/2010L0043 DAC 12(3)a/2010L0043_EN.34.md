# Article 34 - Applicable law


Member States shall require the parties to the agreement referred to either in Articles [[2010L0043_EN.23|23]](5) or Article 33(5) of Directive 2009/65/EC to specify that the law of the UCITS’ home Member State applies to that agreement.
