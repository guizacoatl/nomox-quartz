> Level 1 reference(s): [[2009L0065_EN.14#1#c]], [[2009L0065_EN.12#1#a]]# Article 6 - Complaints handling

## 1.
Member States shall require management companies to establish, implement and maintain effective and transparent procedures for the reasonable and prompt handling of complaints received from investors.
## 2.
Member States shall require management companies to ensure that each complaint and the measures taken for its resolution are recorded.
## 3.
Investors shall be able to file complaints free of charge. The information regarding procedures referred to in paragraph 1 shall be made available to investors free of charge.
