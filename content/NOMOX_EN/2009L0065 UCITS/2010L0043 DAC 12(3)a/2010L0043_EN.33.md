# Article 33 - Elements related to potential amendments and the termination of the agreement


Member States shall require the parties to the agreement referred to in either [[2010L0043_EN.23#5|Article 23(5)]] or Article 33(5) of Directive 2009/65/EC to include at least the following particulars related to amendments and the termination of the agreement in that agreement:
### (a)
the period of validity of the agreement;
### (b)
the conditions under which the agreement may be amended or terminated;
### (c)
the conditions which are necessary to facilitate transition to another depositary and, in case of such transition the procedure by which the depositary shall send all relevant information to the other depositary.
