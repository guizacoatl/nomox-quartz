# Article 5a - Obligation for investment companies to integrate sustainability risks in the management of UCITS


Member States shall ensure that investment companies integrate *[[2010L0043_EN.3#11.|sustainability risks]]* in the management of UCITS, taking into account the nature, scale and complexity of the business of the investment companies.
