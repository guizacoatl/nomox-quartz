# Article 68 - 1.


An investment company and, for each of the common funds it manages, a management company, shall publish the following:
### (a)
a prospectus;
### (b)
an annual report for each financial year; and
### (c)
a half-yearly report covering the first six months of the financial year.
## 2.
The annual and half-yearly reports shall be published within the following time limits, with effect from the end of the period to which they relate:
### (a)
four months in the case of the annual report; or
### (b)
two months in the case of the half-yearly report.
