# Article 13 - Specific cases

## 1.
Where a new UCITS cannot comply with the requirements contained in Article 10(2)(b) and [[2010R0583_EN.11#1#b|Article 11(1)(b)]], the ongoing charges shall be estimated, based on the expected total of charges.
## 2.
Paragraph 1 shall not apply in the following cases:
### (a)
for funds which charge a fixed all-inclusive fee, where instead that figure shall be displayed;
### (b)
for funds which set a cap or maximum on the amount that can be charged, where instead that figure shall be disclosed so long as the management company gives a commitment to respect the published figure and to absorb any costs that would otherwise cause it to be exceeded.
