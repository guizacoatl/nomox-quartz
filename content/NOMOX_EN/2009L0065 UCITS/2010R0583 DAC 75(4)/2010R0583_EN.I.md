## 1.
The synthetic indicator shall rank the fund on a scale from 1 to 7 on the basis of its volatility record.
## 2.
The scale shall be shown as a sequence of categories denoted by the whole numbers in ascending order from 1 to 7 running from left to right, representing levels of risk and reward, from lowest to highest.
## 3.
It shall be made clear on the scale that lower risk entails potentially lower reward and that higher risk entails potentially higher rewards.
## 4.
The category into which the UCITS falls shall be prominently indicated.
