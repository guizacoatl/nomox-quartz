# Article 17 - Impact and treatment of material changes

## 1.
Where a material change occurs to a UCITS’ objectives and investment policy during the period displayed in the bar chart referred to in [[2010R0583_EN.15|Article 15]], the UCITS’ past performance prior to that material change shall continue to be shown.
## 2.
The period prior to the material change referred to in paragraph 1 shall be indicated on the bar chart and labelled with a clear warning that the performance was achieved under circumstances that no longer apply.
