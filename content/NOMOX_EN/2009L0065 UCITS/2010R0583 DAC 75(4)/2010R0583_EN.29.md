# Article 29 - Risk and reward profile


The narrative explanation of risk factors referred to in [[2010R0583_EN.8#1#b|Article 8(1)(b)]] shall take account of the risks posed by each underlying collective undertaking, to the extent that these are likely to be material to the UCITS as a whole.
