# Article 35 - Past performance

## 1.
The past performance presentation in the key investor information document of the feeder UCITS shall be specific to the feeder UCITS, and shall not reproduce the performance record of the master UCITS.
## 2.
Paragraph 1 shall not apply:
### (a)
where a feeder UCITS shows the past performance of its master UCITS as a benchmark; or
### (b)
where the feeder was launched as a feeder UCITS at a later date than the master UCITS, and where the conditions of [[2010R0583_EN.19|Article 19]] are satisfied, and where a simulated performance is shown for the years before the feeder existed, based on the past performance of the master UCITS; or
### (c)
where the feeder UCITS has a past performance record from before the date on which it began to operate as a feeder, its own record being retained in the bar chart for the relevant years, with the material change labelled as required by [[2010R0583_EN.17#2|Article 17(2)]].
