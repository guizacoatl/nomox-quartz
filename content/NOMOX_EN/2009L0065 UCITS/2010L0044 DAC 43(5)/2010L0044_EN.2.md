# Article 2 - Definitions


For the purpose of this Directive the following definitions shall apply:
## 1.
‘*[[2010L0044_EN.2#1.|rebalancing of the portfolio]]*’ means a significant modification of the composition of the portfolio of a UCITS;
## 2.
‘*[[2010L0044_EN.2#2.|synthetic risk and reward indicators]]*’ means synthetic indicators within the meaning of Article 8 of Commission Regulation (EU) No 583/2010 of 1 July 2010 implementing Directive 2009/65/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council as regards key investor information and conditions to be met when providing key investor information or the prospectus in a durable medium other than paper or by means of a website.
