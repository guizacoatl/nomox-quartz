# Article 33 - Development of common data processing systems

## 1.
In order to facilitate access by the competent authorities of the UCITS host Member States to the information or documents referred to in [[2010L0044_EN.93#1|Article 93(1)]], (2) and (3) of Directive 2009/65/EC, for the purpose of Article 93(7) of that Directive, competent authorities of Member States may coordinate the establishment of sophisticated electronic data processing and central storage systems common to all Member States.
## 2.
The coordination between Member States referred to in paragraph 1 shall take place in the Committee of European Securities Regulators.
