# Article 1 - Subject matter


This Directive lays down detailed rules for the implementation of [[2010L0044_EN.43#5|Article 43(5)]], [[2010L0044_EN.60#6#a|Article 60(6)(a)]] and (c), Articles [[2010L0044_EN.61|61]](3) and 62(4), Article 64(4)(a) and Article 95(1) of Directive 2009/65/EC.
