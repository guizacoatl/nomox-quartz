# Article 113 - 1.


Solely for the purpose of Danish UCITS,

pantebreve

issued in Denmark shall be treated as equivalent to the transferable securities referred to in [[2009L0065_EN.50#1#b|Article 50(1)(b)]].
## 2.
By way of derogation from Articles [[2009L0065_EN.22|22]](1) and 32(1), the competent authorities may authorise those UCITS which, on 20 December 1985, had two or more depositaries in accordance with their national law to maintain that number of depositaries if those authorities have guarantees that the functions to be performed under Article 22(3) and [[2009L0065_EN.32#3|Article 32(3)]] will be performed in practice.
## 3.
By way of derogation from [[2009L0065_EN.16|Article 16]], the Member States may authorise management companies to issue bearer certificates representing the registered securities of other companies.
