# Article 1 - Definitions


For the purpose of this Regulation, the following definitions shall apply:
### (a)
‘link’ means a situation in which two and more natural or legal persons are either linked by a direct or indirect holding in an undertaking which represents 10 % or more of the capital or of the voting rights or which makes it possible to exercise a significant influence over the management of the undertaking in which that holding subsists;
### (b)
‘group link’ means a situation in which two or more undertakings or entities belong to the same group within the meaning of [[2013L0034_EN.2#11|Article 2(11) of Directive 2013/34/EU]] of the European Parliament and of the Council or international accounting standards adopted in accordance with Regulation (EC) No 1606/2002 of the European Parliament and of the Council (
