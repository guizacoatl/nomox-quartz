# Article 15 - Managers of qualifying venture capital funds shall inform the competent authority of the home Member State where they intend to market:

### (a)
a new qualifying venture capital fund; or
### (b)
an existing qualifying venture capital fund in a Member State not mentioned in the list referred to in point (d) of [[2013R0345_EN.14#1|Article 14(1)]].
