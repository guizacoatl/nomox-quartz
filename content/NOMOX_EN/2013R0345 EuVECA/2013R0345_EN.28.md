# Article 28 - This Regulation shall enter into force on the twentieth day following that of its publication in the
 Official Journal of the European Union .

It shall apply from the 22 July 2013, except for [[2013R0345_EN.9#5|Article 9(5)]], which shall apply from 15 May 2013.
