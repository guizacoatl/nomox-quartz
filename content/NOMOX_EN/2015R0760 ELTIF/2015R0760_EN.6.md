# Article 6 - Conditions for granting authorisation as an ELTIF

## 1.
An *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]* shall be authorised as an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* only where its competent authority:
### (a)
is satisfied that the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]* is able to meet all the requirements of this Regulation;
### (b)
has approved the application of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]* authorised in accordance with Directive 2011/61/EU to manage the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, the fund rules or instruments of incorporation, and the choice of the depositary.
## 2.
In the event that an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]* makes an application pursuant to [[2015R0760_EN.5#5|Article 5(5)]] of this Regulation, the competent authority shall authorise the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]* only where it is satisfied that the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]* complies with both the requirements of this Regulation and of Directive 2011/61/EU regarding the authorisation of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]*.
## 3.
The *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]* may refuse to approve the application of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]* to manage an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* only where the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]*:
### (a)
does not comply with this Regulation;
### (b)
does not comply with Directive 2011/61/EU;
### (c)
is not authorised by its competent authority to manage AIFs that follow investment strategies of the type covered by this Regulation; or
### (d)
has not provided the documentation referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.5#2|Article 5(2)]], or any clarification or information requested thereunder.

Before refusing to approve an application, the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]* shall consult the competent authority of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]*.
## 4.
The *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]* shall not grant authorisation as an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* to the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]* that has made an application for authorisation if it is legally prevented from marketing its units or shares in its home Member State.
## 5.
The *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]* shall communicate to the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]* the reason for its refusal to grant authorisation as an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*.
## 6.
An application which has been rejected under this Chapter shall not be resubmitted to the competent authorities of other Member States.
## 7.
Authorisation as an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall not be subject to a requirement that the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* be managed by an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]* authorised in the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(11)|home Member State of the ELTIF]]* or that the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]* pursue or delegate any activities in the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(11)|home Member State of the ELTIF]]*.
