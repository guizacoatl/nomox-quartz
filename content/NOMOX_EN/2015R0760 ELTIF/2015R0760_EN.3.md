# Article 3 - Authorisation and central public register

## 1.
An *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* may only be marketed in the Union when it has been authorised in accordance with this Regulation. Authorisation as an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall be valid for all Member States.
## 2.
Only *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIFs]]* shall be eligible to apply for and to be granted authorisation as an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*.
## 3.
The competent authorities of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* shall, on a quarterly basis, inform ESMA of authorisations granted or withdrawn pursuant to this Regulation and of any changes to the information about an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* that is set out in the central public register referred to in the second subparagraph.

ESMA shall keep an up-to-date central public register identifying for each *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* authorised under this Regulation:
### (a)
the Legal Entity Identifier (LEI) and national code identifier of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, where available;
### (b)
the name and address of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* and, where available, the LEI of that manager;
### (c)
the ISIN codes of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* and each separate unit or share class, where available;
### (d)
the LEI of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]*, where available;
### (e)
the LEI of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]*, where available;
### (f)
the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]* and the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(11)|home Member State of the ELTIF]]*;
### (g)
the Member States where the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* is marketed;
### (h)
whether the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* can be marketed to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]* or can be marketed solely to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(2)|professional investors]]*;
#### (i)
the date of the authorisation of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*;
### (j)
the date on which the marketing of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* commenced;
### (k)
the date of the last update by ESMA of the information about the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*.

The central public register shall be made available in electronic format.
