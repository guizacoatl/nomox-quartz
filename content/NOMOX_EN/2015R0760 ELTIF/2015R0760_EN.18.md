> Level 2 reference(s): [[2024R2759_EN.5]], [[2024R2759_EN.3]], [[2024R2759_EN.2]], [[2024R2759_EN.4]], [[2024R2759_EN.6]], [[2018R0480_EN.2]], [[2024R2759_EN.7]]# Article 18 - Redemption of units or shares of ELTIFs

## 1.
Investors in an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall not be able to request the redemption of their units or shares before the end of the life of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*. Redemptions to investors shall be possible as from the day following the date of the end of the life of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*.

The rules or instruments of incorporation of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall clearly indicate a specific date for the end of the life of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* and may provide for the right to extend temporarily the life of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* and the conditions for exercising such a right.

The rules or instruments of incorporation of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* and disclosures to investors shall lay down the procedures for the redemption of units or shares and the disposal of assets, and state clearly that redemptions to investors shall be possible as from the day following the date of the end of life of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*.
## 2.
By way of derogation from paragraph 1 of this Article, the rules or instruments of incorporation of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* may provide for the possibility of redemptions during the life of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* provided that all of the following conditions are fulfilled:
### (a)
redemptions are not granted before the end of a minimum holding period or before the date specified in [[2015R0760_EN.17#1|Article 17(1)]], point (a);
### (b)
at the time of authorisation and throughout the life of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* is able to demonstrate to the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]* that the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* has in place an appropriate redemption policy and liquidity management tools that are compatible with the long-term investment strategy of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*;
### (c)
the redemption policy of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* clearly indicates the procedures and conditions for redemptions;
### (d)
the redemption policy of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* ensures that redemptions are limited to a percentage of the assets of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]], point (b);
### (e)
the redemption policy of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* ensures that investors are treated fairly and redemptions are granted on a pro rata basis if the requests for redemptions exceed the percentage referred to in point (d) of this subparagraph.

The condition of a minimum holding period referred to in point (a) of the first subparagraph shall not apply to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIFs]]* investing in their *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIFs]]*.
## 3.
The life of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall be consistent with the long-term nature of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* and shall be compatible with the life-cycles of each of the individual assets of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, measured according to the illiquidity profile and economic life-cycle of the asset and the stated investment objective of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*.
## 4.
Investors shall always have the option to be repaid in cash.
## 5.
Repayment in kind out of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*’s assets shall be possible only where all of the following conditions are met:
### (a)
the rules or instruments of incorporation of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* offer that possibility, provided that all investors are treated fairly;
### (b)
the investor asks in writing to be repaid through a share of the assets of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*;
### (c)
no specific rules restrict the transfer of those assets.
## 6.
ESMA shall develop draft regulatory technical standards specifying the circumstances in which the life of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* is considered compatible with the life-cycles of each of the individual assets of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, as referred to in paragraph 3.

ESMA shall also develop draft regulatory technical standards specifying the following:
### (a)
the criteria to determine the minimum holding period referred to in paragraph 2, first subparagraph, point (a);
### (b)
the minimum information to be provided to the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]* under paragraph 2, first subparagraph, point (b);
### (c)
the requirements to be fulfilled by the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* in relation to its redemption policy and liquidity management tools, referred to in paragraph 2, first subparagraph, points (b) and (c); and
### (d)
the criteria to assess the percentage referred to in paragraph 2, first subparagraph, point (d), taking into account amongst others the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*’s expected cash flows and liabilities.

ESMA shall submit the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first and second subparagraphs to the Commission by 10 January 2024.

Power is delegated to the Commission to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first and second subparagraphs in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
