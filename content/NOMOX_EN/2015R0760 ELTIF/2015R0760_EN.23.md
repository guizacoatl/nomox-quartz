# Article 23 - Transparency

## 1.
The units or shares of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall not be marketed in the Union without prior publication of a prospectus.

The units or shares of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall not be marketed to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]* in the Union without prior publication of a key information document in accordance with Regulation (EU) No 1286/2014.
## 2.
The prospectus shall include all information necessary to enable investors to make an informed assessment regarding the investment proposed to them and, in particular, the risks attached thereto.
## 3.
The prospectus shall contain at least the following:
### (a)
a statement setting out how the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*'s investment objectives and strategy for achieving these objectives qualify the fund as long-term in nature;
### (b)
information to be disclosed by collective investment undertakings of the closed-end type in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2017/1129 of the European Parliament and of the Council;
### (c)
information to be disclosed to investors pursuant to [[2011L0061_EN.23|Article 23 of Directive 2011/61/EU]], if it is not already covered under point(b) of this paragraph;
### (d)
a prominent indication of the categories of assets in which the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* is authorised to invest;
### (e)
a prominent indication of the jurisdictions in which the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* is allowed to invest;
### (f)
any other information considered by the competent authorities to be relevant for the purposes of paragraph 2.
## 3a.
The prospectus of a *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* shall contain the following information:
### (a)
a declaration that the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* is a feeder of a *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* and as such permanently invests 85 % or more of its assets in units or shares of that *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]*;
### (b)
the investment objective and policy of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]*, including the risk profile and whether the performance of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* and the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* are identical, or to what extent and for which reasons they differ;
### (c)
a brief description of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]*, its organisation, its investment objective and policy, including the risk profile, and an indication of how the prospectus of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* can be obtained;
### (d)
a summary of the agreement entered into between the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* and the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* or of the internal rules on the conduct of business referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.29#6|Article 29(6)]];
### (e)
how the unit- or shareholders may obtain further information on the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* and the agreement entered into between the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* and the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.29#6|Article 29(6)]];
### (f)
a description of all remuneration or reimbursement of costs payable by the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* by virtue of its investment in units or shares of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]*, as well as of the aggregate charges of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* and the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]*.
## 4.
The prospectus and any other marketing documents shall prominently inform investors about the illiquid nature of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*.

In particular, the prospectus and any other marketing documents shall clearly:
### (a)
inform investors about the long-term nature of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*'s investments;
### (b)
inform investors about the end of the life of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* as well as the option to extend the life of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, where this is provided for, and the conditions thereof;
### (c)
state whether the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* is intended to be marketed to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*;
### (d)
explain the rights of investors to redeem their investment in accordance with [[2015R0760_EN.18|Article 18]] and with the rules or instruments of incorporation of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*;
### (e)
state the frequency and the timing of distributions of proceeds, if any, to investors during the life of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*;
### (f)
advise investors that only a small proportion of their overall investment portfolio should be invested in an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*;
### (g)
describe the hedging policy of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, including a prominent indication that financial derivative instruments may be used only for the purpose of hedging risks inherent to other investments of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, and an indication of the possible impact of the use of financial derivative instruments on the risk profile of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*;
### (h)
inform investors about the risks related to investing in *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(6)|real assets]]*, including infrastructure;
#### (i)
inform investors regularly, at least once a year, of the jurisdictions in which the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* has invested.
## 5.
In addition to the information required under [[2011L0061_EN.22|Article 22 of Directive 2011/61/EU]], the annual report of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall contain the following:
### (a)
a cash flow statement;
### (b)
information on any participation in instruments involving Union budgetary funds;
### (c)
information on the value of the individual qualifying portfolio undertakings and the value of other assets in which the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* has invested, including the value of financial derivative instruments used;
### (d)
information on the jurisdictions in which the assets of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* are located.

Where the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* is marketed to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*, the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* shall include in the annual report of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* a statement on the aggregate charges of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* and the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]*. The annual report of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* shall indicate how the annual report of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* can be obtained.
## 6.
Upon the request of a *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investor]]*, the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* shall provide additional information relating to the quantitative limits that apply to the risk management of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, the methods chosen to that end, and the recent evolution of the main risks and yields of the categories of assets.
