# Article 14 - Rectification of investment positions


In the event that an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* infringes the portfolio composition and diversification requirements laid down in [[2015R0760_EN.13|Article 13]] or the borrowing limits set out in [[2015R0760_EN.16#1|Article 16(1)]], point (a), and the infringement is beyond the control of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]*, the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* shall, within an appropriate period of time, take such measures as are necessary to rectify the position, taking due account of the interests of the investors in the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*.
