# Article 20 - Issuance of new units or shares

## 1.
An *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* may offer new issues of units or shares in accordance with its rules or instruments of incorporation.
## 2.
An *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall not issue new units or shares at a price below their net asset value without a prior offering of those units or shares at that price to existing investors in the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*.
