# Article 2 - Definitions


For the purposes of this Regulation, the following definitions apply:
## (1)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]*’ means aggregate *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* contributions and uncalled committed *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]*, calculated on the basis of amounts investible after deduction of all fees, charges and expenses that are directly or indirectly borne by investors;
## (2)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(2)|professional investor]]*’ means an investor which is considered to be a professional client, or may, on request, be treated as a professional client in accordance with Annex II to Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (3)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investor]]*’ means an investor who is not a *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(2)|professional investor]]*;
## (4)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(4)|equity]]*’ means ownership interest in a qualifying portfolio undertaking, represented by the shares or other forms of participation in the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* of the qualifying portfolio undertaking issued to its investors;
## (5)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(5)|quasi-equity]]*’ means any type of financing instrument where the return on the instrument is linked to the profit or loss of the qualifying portfolio undertaking and where the repayment of the instrument in the event of default is not fully secured;
## (6)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(6)|real asset]]*’ means an asset that has an intrinsic value due to its substance and properties;
## (7)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(7)|financial undertaking]]*’ means any of the following:
### (a)
a credit institution as defined in point (1) of [[2013R0575_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]] of the European Parliament and of the Council;
### (b)
an investment firm as defined in point (1) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
### (c)
an insurance undertaking as defined in point (1) of Article 13 of Directive 2009/138/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council;
### (ca)
a reinsurance undertaking as defined in [[2015R0760_EN.13|Article 13]], point (4), of Directive 2009/138/EC;
### (d)
a financial holding company as defined in point (20) of [[2013R0575_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]];
### (e)
a mixed-activity holding company as defined in point (22) of [[2013R0575_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]];
### (f)
a management company as defined in point (b) of Article 2(1) of Directive 2009/65/EC;
### (g)
an AIFM as defined in point (b) of [[2011L0061_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2011/61/EU]].
## (8)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]*’ means *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]* as defined in point (k) of [[2011L0061_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2011/61/EU]];
## (9)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]*’ means *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]* as defined in point (l) of [[2011L0061_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2011/61/EU]];
## (10)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]*’ means the competent authority of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]* within the meaning of point (h) of [[2011L0061_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2011/61/EU]];
## (11)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(11)|home Member State of the ELTIF]]*’ means the Member State where the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* is authorised;
## (12)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]*’ means the authorised *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]* approved to manage an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, or the internally managed *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* where the legal form of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* permits internal management and where no external AIFM has been appointed;
## (13)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(13)|competent authority of the manager of the ELTIF]]*’ means the competent authority of the home Member State of the AIFM within the meaning of point (q) of [[2011L0061_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2011/61/EU]];
## (14)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(14)|securities lending]]*’ and ‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(14)|securities borrowing]]*’ mean any transaction in which a counterparty transfers securities subject to a commitment that the borrower will return equivalent securities at some future date or when requested to do so by the transferor, that transaction being considered as *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(14)|securities lending]]* for the counterparty transferring the securities and being considered as *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(14)|securities borrowing]]* for the counterparty to which they are transferred;
## (14a)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(14a)|simple, transparent and standardised securitisation]]*’ means a securitisation that complies with the conditions set out in [[2017R2402_EN.18|Article 18 of Regulation (EU) 2402/2017]] of the European Parliament and of the Council;
## (14b)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(14b)|group]]*’ means a *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(14b)|group]]* as defined in [[2015R0760_EN.2|Article 2]], point (11), of Directive 2013/34/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council;
## (15)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(15)|repurchase transaction]]*’ means a *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(15)|repurchase transaction]]* as defined in point (83) of [[2013R0575_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]];
## (16)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(16)|financial instrument]]*’ means a *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(16)|financial instrument]]* as specified in Section C of Annex I to Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (17)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(17)|short selling]]*’ means an activity as defined in point (b) of [[2012R0236_EN.2#1|Article 2(1) of Regulation (EU) No 236/2012]] of the European Parliament and of the Council;
## (18)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(18)|regulated market]]*’ means a *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(18)|regulated market]]* as defined in point (21) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
## (19)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(19)|multilateral trading facility]]*’ means a *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(19)|multilateral trading facility]]* as defined in point (22) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]];
## (20)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]*’ means an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, or an investment compartment thereof, which has been approved to invest at least 85 % of its assets in units or shares of another *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* or investment compartment of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*;
## (21)
‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]*’ means an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, or an investment compartment thereof, in which another *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* invests at least 85 % of its assets in units or shares.
