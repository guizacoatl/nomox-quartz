# Article 5 - Application for authorisation as an ELTIF

## 1.
An application for authorisation as an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall be made to the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]*.

The application for authorisation as an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall include all of the following:
### (a)
the fund rules or instruments of incorporation;
### (b)
the name of the proposed *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]*;
### (c)
the name of the depositary and, where requested by the competent authority of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* that can be marketed to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*, the written agreement with the depositary;
### (d)
where the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* can be marketed to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*, a description of the information to be made available to investors, including a description of the arrangements for dealing with complaints submitted by *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*;
### (e)
where applicable, the following information on the master-feeder structure of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*:
#### (i)
a declaration that the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* is a feeder of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]*;
#### (ii)
the fund rules or instruments of incorporation of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* and the agreement between the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* and the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]*, or the internal rules on the conduct of business, referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.29#6|Article 29(6)]];
#### (iii)
where the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* and the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* have different depositaries, the information-sharing agreement referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.29#7|Article 29(7)]];
#### (iv)
where the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* is established in a Member State other than the home Member State of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]*, an attestation by the competent authority of the home Member State of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* that the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* is an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* provided by the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]*.

The *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]* may request clarification and information as regards the documentation and information provided under the second subparagraph.
## 2.
Only an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]* authorised under Directive 2011/61/EU may apply to the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]* for approval to manage an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* for which authorisation is requested in accordance with paragraph 1. In the event that the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]* is the same as the competent authority of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]*, such an application for approval shall refer to the documentation submitted for authorisation under Directive 2011/61/EU.

Without prejudice to paragraph 1, an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]* that applies to manage an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* established in another Member State shall provide the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]* with the following documentation:
### (a)
the written agreement with the depositary;
### (b)
information on delegation arrangements regarding portfolio and risk management and administration with regard to the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*;
### (c)
information about the investment strategies, the risk profile and other characteristics of AIFs that the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]* is authorised to manage.

The *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]* may ask the competent authority of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]* for clarification and information as regards the documentation referred to in the second subparagraph or an attestation as to whether *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* fall within the scope of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]*'s authorisation to manage AIFs. The competent authority of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]* shall provide an answer within 10 working days from the date on which it received the request submitted by the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]*.
## 3.
Applicants shall be informed within two months from the date of submission of a complete application whether authorisation as an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* has been granted.
## 4.
Any subsequent modifications to the documentation referred to in paragraphs 1 and 2 shall be immediately notified to the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIF]]*.
## 5.
By way of derogation from paragraphs 1 and 2, an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]* the legal form of which permits internal management and the governing body of which chooses not to appoint an external AIFM shall apply simultaneously for authorisation as an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* under this Regulation and as an AIFM under Directive 2011/61/EU.

Without prejudice to [[2011L0061_EN.7|Article 7 of Directive 2011/61/EU]], the application for authorisation as an internally managed *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall include the following:
### (a)
the fund rules or instruments of incorporation;
### (b)
where the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* can be marketed to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*, a description of the information to be made available to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*, including a description of the arrangements for dealing with complaints submitted by *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*.

By way of derogation from paragraph 3, an internally managed *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]* shall be informed within three months from the date of submission of a complete application whether authorisation as an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* has been granted.
