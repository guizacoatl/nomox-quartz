# Article 13 - Portfolio composition and diversification

## 1.
An *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall invest at least 55 % of its *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* in eligible investment assets.
## 2.
An *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall invest no more than:
### (a)
20 % of its *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* in instruments issued by, or loans granted to, any single qualifying portfolio undertaking;
### (b)
20 % of its *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* in a single *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(6)|real asset]]*;
### (c)
20 % of its *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* in units or shares of any single *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, EuVECA, EuSEF, UCITS or *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]* managed by an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]*;
### (d)
10 % of its *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* in assets referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]], point (b), where those assets have been issued by any single body.
## 3.
The aggregate value of *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(14a)|simple, transparent and standardised securitisations]]* in an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* portfolio shall not exceed 20 % of the value of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*.
## 4.
The aggregate risk exposure to a counterparty of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* stemming from over-the-counter (OTC) derivative transactions, repurchase agreements, or reverse repurchase agreements shall not exceed 10 % of the value of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*.
## 5.
By way of derogation from paragraph 2, point (d), an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* may raise the 10 % limit referred to therein to 25 % where bonds are issued by a credit institution that has its registered office in a Member State and that is subject by law to special public supervision designed to protect bond-holders. In particular, sums deriving from the issue of those bonds shall be invested in accordance with the law in assets which, during the whole period of validity of the bonds, are capable of covering claims attaching to the bonds and which, in the event of failure of the issuer, would be used on a priority basis for the reimbursement of the principal and payment of the accrued interest.
## 6.
Companies which are included in the same *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(14b)|group]]* for the purposes of consolidated accounts, as regulated by Directive 2013/34/EU or in accordance with recognised international accounting rules, shall be regarded as a single qualifying portfolio undertaking or a single body for the purpose of calculating the limits referred to in paragraphs 1 to 5 of this Article.
## 7.
The investment limits set out in paragraphs 2 to 4 shall not apply where *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* are marketed solely to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(2)|professional investors]]*. The investment limit set out in paragraph 2, point (c), shall not apply where an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* is a *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]*.
