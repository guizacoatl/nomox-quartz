# Article 22 - Distribution of proceeds and capital

## 1.
An *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* may regularly distribute to investors the proceeds generated by the assets contained in its portfolio. Those proceeds shall comprise:
### (a)
proceeds that the assets are regularly producing;
### (b)
*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* appreciation realised after the disposal of an asset.
## 2.
The proceeds shall not be distributed to the extent that they are required for future commitments of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*.
## 3.
An *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* may reduce its *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* on a pro rata basis in the event of a disposal of an asset during the life of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, provided that such disposal is duly considered by the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* to be in the investors’ interests.
## 4.
The rules or instruments of incorporation of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall specify the distribution policy that the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* will apply during its life.
