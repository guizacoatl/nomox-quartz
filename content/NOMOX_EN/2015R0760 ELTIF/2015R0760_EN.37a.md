# Article 37a - Review of sustainability aspects of ELTIFs


By 11 January 2026, the Commission shall carry out an assessment and submit a report to the European Parliament and to the Council accompanied, where appropriate, by a legislative proposal, regarding at least the following:
### (a)
whether the creation of an optional designation of ‘*[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* marketed as environmentally sustainable’ or ‘green *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*’ is feasible, and in particular:
#### (i)
whether such designation should be reserved to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* that are financial products having sustainable investment as their objective as referred to in [[2019R2088_EN.9|Article 9 of Regulation (EU) 2088/2019]] of the European Parliament and of the Council;
#### (ii)
whether such designation should be reserved to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* that invest all or a significant part of their eligible assets or total assets into sustainable activities and, if so, how the significant part is to be defined;
#### (iii)
whether sustainable activities can be linked to the sustainability criteria set out in the delegated acts adopted pursuant to Articles [[2015R0760_EN.10|10]](3), 11(3), 12(2), 13(2), 14(2) and 15(2) of Regulation (EU) 2020/852 of the European Parliament and of the Council;
### (b)
whether there should be a general obligation for *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* to comply in their investment decisions with the principle of ‘do no significant harm’ within the meaning of [[2019R2088_EN.2a|Article 2a of Regulation (EU) 2088/2019]], or whether that obligation should be limited to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* marketed as environmentally sustainable or green *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]*, in the eventuality that such an optional designation is considered feasible;
### (c)
whether there is any potential to improve the framework for *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* by contributing more significantly to the objectives of the European Green Deal, without undermining the nature of *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]*.
