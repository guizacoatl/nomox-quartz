# Article 12 - Conflicts of interest

## 1.
An *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall not invest in an eligible investment asset in which the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* has or takes a direct or indirect interest, other than by holding units or shares of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]*, EuSEFs, EuVECAs, UCITS or *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIFs]]* that the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* manages.
## 2.
An *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]* managing an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* and undertakings that belong to the same *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(14b)|group]]* as that *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]*, and their staff, may co-invest in that *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* and co-invest with the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* in the same asset provided that the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* has put in place organisational and administrative arrangements designed to identify, prevent, manage and monitor conflicts of interest and provided that such conflicts of interest are adequately disclosed.
