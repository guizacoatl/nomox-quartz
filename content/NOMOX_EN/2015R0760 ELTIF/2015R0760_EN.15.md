# Article 15 - Concentration limits

## 1.
An *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* may acquire no more than 30 % of the units or shares of a single *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, EuVECA, EuSEF, UCITS or of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIF]]* managed by an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFM]]*. That limit shall not apply where *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* are marketed solely to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(2)|professional investors]]*, nor shall it apply to a *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* investing in its *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]*.
## 2.
The concentration limits laid down in Article 56(2) of Directive 2009/65/EC shall apply to investments in the assets referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]], point (b), of this Regulation, except where *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* are marketed solely to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(2)|professional investors]]*.
