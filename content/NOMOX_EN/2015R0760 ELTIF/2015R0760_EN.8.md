# Article 8 - Investment compartments


Where an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* comprises more than one investment compartment, each compartment shall be regarded as a separate *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* for the purposes of this Chapter.
