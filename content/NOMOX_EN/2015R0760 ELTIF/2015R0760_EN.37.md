# Article 37 - Review

## 1.
The Commission shall review the application of this Regulation and shall analyse at least the following elements:
### (a)
the extent to which *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* are marketed in the Union, including whether the AIFMs referred to in [[2011L0061_EN.3#2|Article 3(2) of Directive 2011/61/EU]] might have an interest in marketing *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]*;
### (b)
the application of provisions on the authorisation of *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]*, as set out in Articles [[2015R0760_EN.3|3]] to 6;
### (c)
whether the provisions on the central public register of *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* as laid down in [[2015R0760_EN.3|Article 3]] should be updated;
### (d)
whether the list of eligible assets and investments, the portfolio composition and diversification requirements, the concentration rules and the limits regarding the borrowing of cash should be updated;
### (e)
the impact of the application of the investment limit for eligible investment assets laid down in [[2015R0760_EN.13#1|Article 13(1)]] on asset diversification;
### (f)
whether the provisions concerning conflicts of interest laid down in [[2015R0760_EN.12|Article 12]] should be updated;
### (g)
the application of [[2015R0760_EN.18|Article 18]] and the impact of that application on the redemption policy and the life of *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]*;
### (h)
whether the transparency requirements laid down in Chapter IV are appropriate;
#### (i)
whether the provisions concerning the marketing of units or shares of *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* laid down in Chapter V are appropriate and ensure an effective protection of investors, including *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*;
### (j)
whether *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* have made a significant contribution to achieving Union objectives such as those set out in the European Green Deal and in other priority areas.
## 2.
Based on the review referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article, the Commission shall by 10 April 2030, and after consulting ESMA, submit to the European Parliament and to the Council a report assessing the contribution of this Regulation and of *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* to the completion of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* markets union and to the achievement of the objectives set out in [[2015R0760_EN.1#2|Article 1(2)]]. The report shall be accompanied, where appropriate, by a legislative proposal.
