# Article 10 - Eligible investment assets

## 1.
An asset as referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]], point (a), shall only be eligible for investment by an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* where it falls into one of the following categories:
### (a)
*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(4)|equity]]* or *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(5)|quasi-equity]]* instruments which have been:
#### (i)
issued by a qualifying portfolio undertaking as referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.11|Article 11]] and acquired by the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* from that qualifying portfolio undertaking or from a third party via the secondary market;
#### (ii)
issued by a qualifying portfolio undertaking as referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.11|Article 11]] in exchange for an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(4)|equity]]* or *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(5)|quasi-equity]]* instrument previously acquired by the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* from that qualifying portfolio undertaking or from a third party via the secondary market;
#### (iii)
issued by an undertaking in which a qualifying portfolio undertaking as referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.11|Article 11]] holds a *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* participation in exchange for an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(4)|equity]]* or *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(5)|quasi-equity]]* instrument acquired by the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* in accordance with point (i) or (ii) of this point (a);
### (b)
debt instruments issued by a qualifying portfolio undertaking as referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.11|Article 11]];
### (c)
loans granted by the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* to a qualifying portfolio undertaking as referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.11|Article 11]] with a maturity that does not exceed the life of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*;
### (d)
units or shares of one or several other *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]*, EuVECAs, EuSEFs, UCITS and *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIFs]]* managed by *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFMs]]* provided that those *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]*, EuVECAs, EuSEFs, UCITS and *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIFs]]* invest in eligible investments as referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]] and (2) and have not themselves invested more than 10 % of their assets in any other collective investment undertaking;
### (e)
*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(6)|real assets]]*;
### (f)
*[[2015R0760_EN.2#(14a)|simple, transparent and standardised securitisations]]* where the underlying exposures correspond to one of the following categories:
#### (i)
assets listed in [[2015R0760_EN.1|Article 1]], point (a)(i), (ii) or (iv), of Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2019/1851;
#### (ii)
assets listed in [[2015R0760_EN.1|Article 1]], point (a)(vii) or (viii), of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2019/1851, provided that the proceeds from the securitisation bonds are used for financing or refinancing long-term investments;
### (g)
bonds issued, pursuant to a Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council on European green bonds, by a qualifying portfolio undertaking as referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.11|Article 11]].

The limit laid down in point (d) of the first subparagraph shall not apply to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIFs]]*.
## 2.
For the purpose of determining compliance with the investment limit laid down in [[2015R0760_EN.13#1|Article 13(1)]], investments by *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* in units or shares of *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]*, EuVECAs, EuSEFs, UCITS and *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(8)|EU AIFs]]* managed by *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(9)|EU AIFMs]]* shall only be taken into account to the extent of the amount of the investments of those collective investment undertakings in the eligible investment assets referred to in paragraph 1, first subparagraph, points (a), (b), (c), (e), (f) and (g), of this Article.

For the purpose of determining compliance with the investment limit and the other limits laid down in Article 13 and [[2015R0760_EN.16#1|Article 16(1)]], the assets and the cash borrowing position of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* and of the other collective investment undertakings in which that *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* has invested shall be combined.

The determination of compliance with the investment limit and the other limits laid down in Article 13 and [[2015R0760_EN.16#1|Article 16(1)]] in accordance with this paragraph shall be carried out on the basis of information updated on at least a quarterly basis and, where that information is not available on a quarterly basis, on the basis of the most recent available information.
