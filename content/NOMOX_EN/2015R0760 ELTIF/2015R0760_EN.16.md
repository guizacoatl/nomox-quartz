# Article 16 - Borrowing of cash

## 1.
An *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* may borrow cash provided that such borrowing fulfils all of the following conditions:
### (a)
it represents no more than 50 % of the net asset value of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* in the case of *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* that can be marketed to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*, and no more than 100 % of the net asset value of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* in the case of *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* marketed solely to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(2)|professional investors]]*;
### (b)
it serves the purpose of making investments or providing liquidity, including to pay costs and expenses, provided that the holdings in cash or cash equivalent of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* are not sufficient to make the investment concerned;
### (c)
it is contracted in the same currency as the assets to be acquired with the borrowed cash, or in another currency where currency exposure has been appropriately hedged;
### (d)
it has a maturity no longer than the life of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*.

When borrowing cash, an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* may encumber assets to implement its borrowing strategy.

Borrowing arrangements that are fully covered by investors’ *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* commitments shall not be considered to constitute borrowing for the purposes of this paragraph.
## 2.
The *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* shall specify in the prospectus of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* whether the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* intends to borrow cash as part of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*’s investment strategy and, if so, shall also specify therein the borrowing limits.
## 3.
The borrowing limits to be specified in the prospectus as referred to in paragraph 2 shall only apply as from the date specified in the rules or instruments of incorporation of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*. That date shall be no later than three years after the date on which the marketing of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* commenced.
## 4.
The borrowing limits referred to in paragraph 1, point (a), shall be temporarily suspended where the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* raises additional *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]* or reduces its existing *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(1)|capital]]*. Such suspension shall be limited in time to the period that is strictly necessary taking due account of the interests of the investors in the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* and, in any case, shall last no longer than 12 months.
