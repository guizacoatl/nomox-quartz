# Article 31 - Marketing of units or shares of ELTIFs

## 1.
The manager of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall be able to market the units or shares of that *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* to professional and *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]* in its home Member State upon notification in accordance with [[2011L0061_EN.31|Article 31 of Directive 2011/61/EU]].
## 2.
The manager of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall be able to market the units or shares of that *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* to professional and *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]* in Member States other than in the home Member State of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* upon notification in accordance with [[2011L0061_EN.32|Article 32 of Directive 2011/61/EU]].
## 3.
The manager of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall, in respect of each *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* that it manages, specify to competent authorities whether or not it intends to market the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*.
## 4.
In addition to the documentation and information required pursuant to Articles [[2015R0760_EN.31|31]] and [[2015R0760_EN.32|32]] of Directive 2011/61/EU, the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* shall provide competent authorities with the following:
### (a)
the prospectus of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*; and
### (b)
the key information document of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* in the event that it is marketed to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*.

—————
## 5.
The competences and powers of the competent authorities pursuant to Articles [[2015R0760_EN.31|31]] and [[2015R0760_EN.32|32]] of Directive 2011/61/EU shall be understood to refer also to the marketing of *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]* and to cover the additional requirements laid down in this Regulation.
## 6.
In addition to its powers set out in the first subparagraph of [[2011L0061_EN.31#3|Article 31(3) of Directive 2011/61/EU]], the competent authority of the home Member State of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* shall also prevent the marketing of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* if the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* does not or will not comply with this Regulation.
## 7.
In addition to its powers set out in the first subparagraph of [[2011L0061_EN.32#3|Article 32(3) of Directive 2011/61/EU]], the competent authority of the home Member State of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* shall also refuse the transmission of a complete notification file to the competent authorities of the Member State where the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* is intended to be marketed if the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* does not comply with this Regulation.
