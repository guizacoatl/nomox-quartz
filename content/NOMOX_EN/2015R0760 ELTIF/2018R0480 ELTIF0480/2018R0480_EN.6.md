# Article 6 - Transitional provisions


An ELTIF authorised under Regulation (EU) 2015/760 before the entry into force of this Regulation shall be deemed to fulfil the requirements set out in [[2018R0480_EN.2|Article 2]] of this Regulation.

An ELTIF authorised under Regulation (EU) 2015/760 before the entry into force of this Regulation shall apply Articles [[2018R0480_EN.1|1]], [[2018R0480_EN.3|3]], [[2018R0480_EN.4|4]] and [[2018R0480_EN.5|5]] of this Regulation from 1 May 2019.
