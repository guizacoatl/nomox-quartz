# Article 25a - Accessibility of information on the European single access point

From 10 January 2030, the information referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.3#3|Article 3(3)]], second subparagraph, of this Regulation shall be made accessible on the European single access point (ESAP) established under Regulation (EU) 2023/2859 of the European Parliament and of the Council. For that purpose, the collection body as defined in [[2015R0760_EN.2|Article 2]], point (2), of that Regulation shall be ESMA. ESMA shall draw that information from the information notified by the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(10)|competent authority of the ELTIFs]]* in accordance with [[2015R0760_EN.3#3|Article 3(3)]], first subparagraph, of this Regulation for the purpose of establishment of the central public register referred to in [[2015R0760_EN.3#3|Article 3(3)]], second subparagraph, of this Regulation.

That information shall comply with the following requirements:
### (a)
be submitted in a data extractable format as defined in [[2015R0760_EN.2|Article 2]], point (3), of Regulation (EU) 2023/2859;
### (b)
be accompanied by the following metadata:
#### (i)
all the names of the authorised *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* to which the information relates;
#### (ii)
where available, the legal entity identifier of the authorised *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, as specified pursuant to [[2015R0760_EN.7#4|Article 7(4)]], point (b), of Regulation (EU) 2023/2859;
#### (iii)
the type of information, as classified pursuant to [[2015R0760_EN.7#4|Article 7(4)]], point (c), of that Regulation;
#### (iv)
an indication of whether the information contains personal data.
