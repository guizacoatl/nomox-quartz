# Article 36 - Processing of applications by the Commission


The Commission shall prioritise and streamline its processes for all applications by *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* for financing from the EIB. The Commission shall streamline the delivery of any opinions or contributions on any applications by *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIFs]]* for financing from the EIB.
