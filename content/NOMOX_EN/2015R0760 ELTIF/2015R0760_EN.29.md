# Article 29 - Specific provisions concerning the depositary of an ELTIF marketed to retail investors

## 1.
By way of derogation from [[2011L0061_EN.21#3|Article 21(3) of Directive 2011/61/EU]], the depositary of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* marketed to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]* shall be an entity of the type referred to in Article 23(2) of Directive 2009/65/EC.
## 2.
By way of derogation from the second subparagraph of Article 21(13) and [[2011L0061_EN.21#14|Article 21(14) of Directive 2011/61/EU]], the depositary of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* marketed to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]* shall not be able to discharge itself of liability in the event of a loss of *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(16)|financial instruments]]* held in custody by a third party.
## 3.
The liability of the depositary referred to in [[2011L0061_EN.21#12|Article 21(12) of Directive 2011/61/EU]] shall not be excluded or limited by agreement where the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* is marketed to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*.
## 4.
Any agreement that contravenes paragraph 3 shall be void.
## 5.
The assets held in custody by the depositary of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* shall not be reused by the depositary, or by any third party to whom the custody function has been delegated, for their own account. Reuse comprises any transaction involving assets held in custody including, but not limited to, transferring, pledging, selling and lending.

The assets held in custody by the depositary of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* are only allowed to be reused provided that:
### (a)
the reuse of the assets is executed for the account of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*;
### (b)
the depositary is carrying out the instructions of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]* on behalf of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*;
### (c)
the reuse is for the benefit of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* and in the interests of the unit- or shareholders; and
### (d)
the transaction is covered by high quality and liquid collateral received by the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]* under a title transfer arrangement.

The market value of the collateral referred to in point (d) of the second subparagraph shall at all times amount to at least the market value of the reused assets plus a premium.
## 6.
In the case of a master-feeder structure, the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* shall provide the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* with all documents and information necessary for the latter to meet the requirements of this Regulation. For that purpose, the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* shall enter into an agreement with the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]*.

The agreement referred to in the first subparagraph shall be made available, on request and free of charge, to all unit- or shareholders. In the event that both the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* and the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* are managed by the same *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(12)|manager of the ELTIF]]*, the agreement may be replaced by internal rules on the conduct of business ensuring compliance with the requirements of this paragraph.
## 7.
Where the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* and the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* have different depositaries, those depositaries shall enter into an information-sharing agreement in order to ensure the fulfilment of the duties of both depositaries. The *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* shall not invest in units or shares of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* until such agreement has become effective.

Where they comply with the requirements of this paragraph, neither the depositary of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* nor that of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* shall be found to infringe any rules that restrict the disclosure of information or relate to data protection where such rules are provided for in a contract or in a law, regulation or administrative provision. Such compliance shall not give rise to any liability on the part of such depositary or any person acting on its behalf.

The *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* or, where applicable, the manager of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]*, shall be in charge of communicating to the depositary of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* any information about the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* that is required for the completion of the duties of the depositary of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]*. The depositary of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* shall immediately inform the competent authorities of the home Member State of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]*, of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]* or, where applicable, of the manager and of the depositary of the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]*, of any irregularities it detects with regard to the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(21)|master ELTIF]]* that are deemed to have a negative impact on the *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(20)|feeder ELTIF]]*.
