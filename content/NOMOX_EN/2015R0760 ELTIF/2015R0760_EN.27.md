# Article 27 - Internal assessment process for ELTIFs that can be marketed to retail investors


The manager of an *[[2015R0760_EN.2#3.|ELTIF]]*, the units or shares of which can be marketed to *[[2015R0760_EN.2#(3)|retail investors]]*, shall be subject to the requirements laid down in [[2015R0760_EN.16#3|Article 16(3)]], second to fifth and seventh subparagraphs, of Directive 2014/65/EU and in Article 24(2) of that Directive.
