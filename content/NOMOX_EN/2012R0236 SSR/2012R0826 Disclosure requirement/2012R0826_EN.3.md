> Level 1 reference(s): [[2012R0236_EN.9]]# Article 3 - Public disclosure of information on net short positions in shares


Any public disclosure of a net short position in shares that reaches, or upon having reached, subsequently falls below, a relevant publication threshold in accordance with [[2012R0236_EN.6#1|Article 6(1) of Regulation (EU) No 236/2012]] shall contain the information specified in Table 2 of Annex I to this Regulation.
