# Article 46 - Transitional provision

## 1.
Existing measures falling within the scope of this Regulation, in force before 15 September 2010, may remain applicable until 1 July 2013 provided that they are notified to the Commission by 24 April 2012.
## 2.
Credit default swap transactions resulting in an uncovered position in a sovereign credit default swap that have been concluded before 25 March 2012 or during a suspension of restrictions on uncovered sovereign credit default swaps in accordance with [[2012R0236_EN.14#2|Article 14(2)]] may be held until the maturity date of the credit default swap contract.
