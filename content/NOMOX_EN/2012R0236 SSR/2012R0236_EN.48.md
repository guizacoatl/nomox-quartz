# Article 48 - Entry into force


This Regulation shall enter into force on the day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union .

It shall apply from 1 November 2012.

However, [[2012R0236_EN.2#2|Article 2(2)]], [[2012R0236_EN.3#7|Article 3(7)]], [[2012R0236_EN.4#2|Article 4(2)]], [[2012R0236_EN.7#3|Article 7(3)]], [[2012R0236_EN.9#5|Article 9(5)]], [[2012R0236_EN.11#3|Article 11(3)]] and (4), [[2012R0236_EN.12#2|Article 12(2)]], [[2012R0236_EN.13#4|Article 13(4)]] and (5), [[2012R0236_EN.16#3|Article 16(3)]] and (4), [[2012R0236_EN.17#2|Article 17(2)]], [[2012R0236_EN.23#5|Article 23(5)]], (7) and (8), and Articles [[2012R0236_EN.30|30]], [[2012R0236_EN.42|42]], [[2012R0236_EN.43|43]] and [[2012R0236_EN.44|44]] shall apply from 25 March 2012.
