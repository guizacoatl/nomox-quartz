# Article 4 - Method of calculation of a significant fall in value for derivatives


A significant fall in value for financial instruments falling under the categories of derivatives listed in points (4) to (10) of Section C of Annex I to Directive 2004/39/EC and which have a sole underlying financial instrument that is traded on a trading venue and for which a significant fall in value has been specified in accordance with [[2012R0919_EN.2|Article 2]] or [[2012R0919_EN.3|Article 3]], shall be calculated by reference to the significant fall in value of the underlying financial instrument.
