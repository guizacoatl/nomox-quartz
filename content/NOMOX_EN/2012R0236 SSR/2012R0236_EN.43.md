# Article 43 - Deadline for the adoption of delegated acts


The Commission shall adopt the delegated acts under [[2012R0236_EN.2#2|Article 2(2)]], [[2012R0236_EN.3#7|Article 3(7)]], [[2012R0236_EN.4#2|Article 4(2)]], [[2012R0236_EN.5#4|Article 5(4)]], [[2012R0236_EN.6#4|Article 6(4)]], [[2012R0236_EN.7#3|Article 7(3)]], [[2012R0236_EN.17#2|Article 17(2)]], Article 23(5) and [[2012R0236_EN.30|Article 30]] by 31 March 2012.

The Commission may extend the deadline referred to in the first paragraph by 6 months.
