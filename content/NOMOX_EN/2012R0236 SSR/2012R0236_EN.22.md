# Article 22 - Measures by other competent authorities


Without prejudice to [[2012R0236_EN.26|Article 26]], a competent authority in relation to a financial instrument for which it is not the relevant competent authority may impose or renew a measure under [[2012R0236_EN.18|Article 18]], 19, 20 or 21 only with the consent of the relevant competent authority.
