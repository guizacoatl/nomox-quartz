# Article 17 - ICT-related incident management process

## 1.
Financial entities shall define, establish and implement an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incident]]* management process to detect, manage and notify *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incidents]]*.
## 2.
Financial entities shall record all *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incidents]]* and *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(13)|significant cyber threats]]*. Financial entities shall establish appropriate procedures and processes to ensure a consistent and integrated monitoring, handling and follow-up of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incidents]]*, to ensure that root causes are identified, documented and addressed in order to prevent the occurrence of such incidents.
## 3.
The *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incident]]* management process referred to in paragraph 1 shall:
### (a)
put in place early warning indicators;
### (b)
establish procedures to identify, track, log, categorise and classify *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incidents]]* according to their priority and severity and according to the criticality of the services impacted, in accordance with the criteria set out in [[2022R2554_EN.18#1|Article 18(1)]];
### (c)
assign roles and responsibilities that need to be activated for different *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incident]]* types and scenarios;
### (d)
set out plans for communication to staff, external stakeholders and media in accordance with [[2022R2554_EN.14|Article 14]] and for notification to clients, for internal escalation procedures, including ICT-related customer complaints, as well as for the provision of information to financial entities that act as counterparts, as appropriate;
### (e)
ensure that at least *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(10)|major ICT-related incidents]]* are reported to relevant senior management and inform the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(30)|management body]]* of at least *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(10)|major ICT-related incidents]]*, explaining the impact, response and additional controls to be established as a result of such *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incidents]]*;
### (f)
establish *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incident]]* response procedures to mitigate impacts and ensure that services become operational and secure in a timely manner.
