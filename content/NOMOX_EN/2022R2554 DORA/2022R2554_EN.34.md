# Article 34 - Operational coordination between Lead Overseers

## 1.
To ensure a consistent approach to oversight activities and with a view to enabling coordinated general oversight strategies and cohesive operational approaches and work methodologies, the three *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseers]]* appointed in accordance with [[2022R2554_EN.31#1|Article 31(1)]], point (b), shall set up a JON to coordinate among themselves in the preparatory stages and to coordinate the conduct of oversight activities over their respective overseen *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(23)|critical ICT third-party service providers]]*, as well as in the course of any action that may be needed pursuant to [[2022R2554_EN.42|Article 42]].
## 2.
For the purposes of paragraph 1, the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseers]]* shall draw up a common oversight protocol specifying the detailed procedures to be followed for carrying out the day-to-day coordination and for ensuring swift exchanges and reactions. The protocol shall be periodically revised to reflect operational needs, in particular the evolution of practical oversight arrangements.
## 3.
The *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseers]]* may, on an ad-hoc basis, call on the ECB and ENISA to provide technical advice, share hands-on experience or join specific coordination meetings of the JON.
