# Article 3 - Calculation of the oversight fees

## 1.
For each critical ICT third-party service, the annual oversight fee for a given year (n) shall be the overall annual costs estimated referred to in [[2024R1505_EN.1|Article 1]] adjusted by the turnover coefficient referred to in paragraph 2 based on its applicable turnover for the year n-2.
