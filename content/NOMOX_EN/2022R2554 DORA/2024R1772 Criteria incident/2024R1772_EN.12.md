> Level 1 reference(s): [[2022R2554_EN.19#6]]# Article 12 - Details of major incidents to be shared with other competent authorities


The details of major incidents to be submitted by competent authorities to other competent authorities in accordance with [[2022R2554_EN.19#6|Article 19(6) of Regulation (EU) 2554/2022]] and the notifications to be submitted by EBA, ESMA or EIOPA and the ECB to the relevant competent authorities in other Member States in accordance with Article 19(7) of that Regulation shall contain the same level of information, without any anonymisation, as the notifications and reports of major incidents received from financial entities in accordance with [[2022R2554_EN.19#4|Article 19(4) of Regulation (EU) 2554/2022]].
