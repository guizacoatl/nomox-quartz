> Level 1 reference(s): [[2022R2554_EN.31#2]]# Article 1 - Assessment approach

## 1.
When considering the criteria set out in [[2022R2554_EN.31#2|Article 31(2) of Regulation (EU) 2554/2022]] to designate an ICT third-party service provider that is critical for financial entities, the ESAs shall apply the following approach:
### (a)
as a first step, the ESAs shall assess whether the ICT third-party service provider fulfils all of the ‘step 1’ sub-criteria set out in Articles [[2024R1502_EN.2|2]](1), 3(1), and 5(1);
### (b)
as a second step, for those ICT third-party service providers that fulfil all of the ‘step 1’ sub-criteria referred to in point (a), the ESAs shall carry out their assessment in the light of the ‘step 2’ sub-criteria referred to in Articles [[2024R1502_EN.2|2]](5), 3(4), 4(1), and 5(5).

By way of derogation from the first sub paragraph, for the assessment of the criterion (c) of [[2022R2554_EN.31#2|Article 31(2) of Regulation (EU) 2554/2022]], the first step shall be covered by the assessment to be carried out for the criteria (a), (b) and (d) of [[2022R2554_EN.31#2|Article 31(2) of Regulation (EU) 2554/2022]].
## 2.
After the end of the time period for the submission of a reasoned statement referred to in [[2024R1502_EN.31#5|Article 31(5)]], first subparagraph, of Regulation (EU) 2022/2554, the ESAs, through the Joint Committee and upon recommendation from the Oversight Forum, shall designate an ICT third-party service provider as critical for financial entities if it fulfils all the ‘step 1’ sub-criteria referred to in paragraph 1, point (a), and following a positive outcome of the assessment carried out in relation to the ‘step 2’ sub-criteria referred to in paragraph 1, point (b).
