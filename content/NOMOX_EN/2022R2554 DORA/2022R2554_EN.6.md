# Article 6 - ICT risk management framework

## 1.
Financial entities shall have a sound, comprehensive and well-documented *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* management framework as part of their overall risk management system, which enables them to address *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* quickly, efficiently and comprehensively and to ensure a high level of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(1)|digital operational resilience]]*.
## 2.
The *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* management framework shall include at least strategies, policies, procedures, ICT protocols and tools that are necessary to duly and adequately protect all *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(6)|information assets]]* and *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(7)|ICT assets]]*, including computer software, hardware, servers, as well as to protect all relevant physical components and infrastructures, such as premises, data centres and sensitive designated areas, to ensure that all *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(6)|information assets]]* and *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(7)|ICT assets]]* are adequately protected from risks including damage and unauthorised access or usage.
## 3.
In accordance with their *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* management framework, financial entities shall minimise the impact of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* by deploying appropriate strategies, policies, procedures, ICT protocols and tools. They shall provide complete and updated information on *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* and on their *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* management framework to the competent authorities upon their request.
## 4.
Financial entities, other than *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(60)|microenterprises]]*, shall assign the responsibility for managing and overseeing *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* to a control function and ensure an appropriate level of independence of such control function in order to avoid conflicts of interest. Financial entities shall ensure appropriate segregation and independence of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* management functions, control functions, and internal audit functions, according to the three lines of defence model, or an internal risk management and control model.
## 5.
The *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* management framework shall be documented and reviewed at least once a year, or periodically in the case of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(60)|microenterprises]]*, as well as upon the occurrence of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(10)|major ICT-related incidents]]*, and following supervisory instructions or conclusions derived from relevant *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(1)|digital operational resilience]]* testing or audit processes. It shall be continuously improved on the basis of lessons derived from implementation and monitoring. A report on the review of the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* management framework shall be submitted to the competent authority upon its request.
## 6.
The *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* management framework of financial entities, other than *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(60)|microenterprises]]*, shall be subject to internal audit by auditors on a regular basis in line with the financial entities’ audit plan. Those auditors shall possess sufficient knowledge, skills and expertise in *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]*, as well as appropriate independence. The frequency and focus of ICT audits shall be commensurate to the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* of the financial entity.
## 7.
Based on the conclusions from the internal audit review, financial entities shall establish a formal follow-up process, including rules for the timely verification and remediation of critical ICT audit findings.
## 8.
The *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* management framework shall include a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(1)|digital operational resilience]]* strategy setting out how the framework shall be implemented. To that end, the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(1)|digital operational resilience]]* strategy shall include methods to address *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* and attain specific ICT objectives, by:
### (a)
explaining how the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* management framework supports the financial entity’s business strategy and objectives;
### (b)
establishing the risk tolerance level for *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]*, in accordance with the risk appetite of the financial entity, and analysing the impact tolerance for ICT disruptions;
### (c)
setting out clear information security objectives, including key performance indicators and key risk metrics;
### (d)
explaining the ICT reference architecture and any changes needed to reach specific business objectives;
### (e)
outlining the different mechanisms put in place to detect *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incidents]]*, prevent their impact and provide protection from it;
### (f)
evidencing the current *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(1)|digital operational resilience]]* situation on the basis of the number of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(10)|major ICT-related incidents]]* reported and the effectiveness of preventive measures;
### (g)
implementing *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(1)|digital operational resilience]]* testing, in accordance with Chapter IV of this Regulation;
### (h)
outlining a communication strategy in the event of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incidents]]* the disclosure of which is required in accordance with [[2022R2554_EN.14|Article 14]].
## 9.
Financial entities may, in the context of the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(1)|digital operational resilience]]* strategy referred to in paragraph 8, define a holistic ICT multi-vendor strategy, at *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(26)|group]]* or entity level, showing key dependencies on *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(19)|ICT third-party service providers]]* and explaining the rationale behind the procurement mix of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(19)|ICT third-party service providers]]*.
## 10.
Financial entities may, in accordance with Union and national sectoral law, outsource the tasks of verifying compliance with *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* management requirements to intra-*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(26)|group]]* or external undertakings. In case of such outsourcing, the financial entity remains fully responsible for the verification of compliance with the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* management requirements.
