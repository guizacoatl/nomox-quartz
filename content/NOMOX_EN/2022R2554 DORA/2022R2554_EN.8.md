# Article 8 - Identification

## 1.
As part of the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* management framework referred to in [[2022R2554_EN.6#1|Article 6(1)]], financial entities shall identify, classify and adequately document all ICT supported business functions, roles and responsibilities, the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(6)|information assets]]* and *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(7)|ICT assets]]* supporting those functions, and their roles and dependencies in relation to *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]*. Financial entities shall review as needed, and at least yearly, the adequacy of this classification and of any relevant documentation.
## 2.
Financial entities shall, on a continuous basis, identify all sources of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]*, in particular the risk exposure to and from other financial entities, and assess *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(12)|cyber threats]]* and ICT vulnerabilities relevant to their ICT supported business functions, *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(6)|information assets]]* and *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(7)|ICT assets]]*. Financial entities shall review on a regular basis, and at least yearly, the risk scenarios impacting them.
## 3.
Financial entities, other than *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(60)|microenterprises]]*, shall perform a risk assessment upon each major change in the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(2)|network and information system]]* infrastructure, in the processes or procedures affecting their ICT supported business functions, *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(6)|information assets]]* or *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(7)|ICT assets]]*.
## 4.
Financial entities shall identify all *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(6)|information assets]]* and *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(7)|ICT assets]]*, including those on remote sites, network resources and hardware equipment, and shall map those considered critical. They shall map the configuration of the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(6)|information assets]]* and *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(7)|ICT assets]]* and the links and interdependencies between the different *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(6)|information assets]]* and *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(7)|ICT assets]]*.
## 5.
Financial entities shall identify and document all processes that are dependent on *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(19)|ICT third-party service providers]]*, and shall identify interconnections with *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(19)|ICT third-party service providers]]* that provide services that support *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(22)|critical or important functions]]*.
## 6.
For the purposes of paragraphs 1, 4 and 5, financial entities shall maintain relevant inventories and update them periodically and every time any major change as referred to in paragraph 3 occurs.
## 7.
Financial entities, other than *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(60)|microenterprises]]*, shall on a regular basis, and at least yearly, conduct a specific *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* assessment on all *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(3)|legacy ICT systems]]* and, in any case before and after connecting technologies, applications or systems.
