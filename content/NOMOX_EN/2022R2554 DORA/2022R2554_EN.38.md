> Level 2 reference(s): [[2025R0420_EN.1]]# Article 38 - General investigations

## 1.
In order to carry out its duties under this Regulation, the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseer]]*, assisted by the joint examination team referred to in [[2022R2554_EN.40#1|Article 40(1)]], may, where necessary, conduct investigations of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(23)|critical ICT third-party service providers]]*.
## 2.
The *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseer]]* shall have the power to:
### (a)
examine records, data, procedures and any other material relevant to the execution of its tasks, irrespective of the medium on which they are stored;
### (b)
take or obtain certified copies of, or extracts from, such records, data, documented procedures and any other material;
### (c)
summon representatives of the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(23)|critical ICT third-party service provider]]* for oral or written explanations on facts or documents relating to the subject matter and purpose of the investigation and to record the answers;
### (d)
interview any other natural or legal person who consents to be interviewed for the purpose of collecting information relating to the subject matter of an investigation;
### (e)
request records of telephone and data traffic.
## 3.
The officials and other persons authorised by the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseer]]* for the purposes of the investigation referred to in paragraph 1 shall exercise their powers upon production of a written authorisation specifying the subject matter and purpose of the investigation.
That authorisation shall also indicate the periodic penalty payments provided for in [[2022R2554_EN.35#6|Article 35(6)]] where the production of the required records, data, documented procedures or any other material, or the answers to questions asked to representatives of the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(19)|ICT third-party service provider]]* are not provided or are incomplete.
## 4.
The representatives of the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(23)|critical ICT third-party service providers]]* are required to submit to the investigations on the basis of a decision of the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseer]]*. The decision shall specify the subject matter and purpose of the investigation, the periodic penalty payments provided for in [[2022R2554_EN.35#6|Article 35(6)]], the legal remedies available under Regulations (EU) No 1093/2010, (EU) No 1094/2010 and (EU) No 1095/2010, and the right to have the decision reviewed by the Court of Justice.
## 5.
In good time before the start of the investigation, the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseer]]* shall inform competent authorities of the financial entities using the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(21)|ICT services]]* of that *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(23)|critical ICT third-party service provider]]* of the envisaged investigation and of the identity of the authorised persons.
The *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseer]]* shall communicate to the JON all information transmitted pursuant to the first subparagraph.
