# Article 8 - Notification of significant cyber threats

## 1.
Financial entities that notify significant cyber threats to competent authorities in accordance with [[2022R2554_EN.19#2|Article 19(2) of Regulation (EU) 2554/2022]] shall use the template laid down in Annex III to this Regulation and follow the data glossary and instructions set out Annex IV to this Regulation.
## 2.
Financial entities shall ensure that the information contained in the notification of significant cyber threats is complete and accurate.
