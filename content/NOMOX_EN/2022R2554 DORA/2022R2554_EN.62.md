# Article 62 - Amendments to Regulation (EU) No 600/2014


Regulation (EU) No 600/2014 is amended as follows:
## (1)
[[2022R2554_EN.27g|Article 27g]] is amended as follows:
### (a)
paragraph 4 is replaced by the following:

‘4.

An APA shall comply with the requirements concerning the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(4)|security of network and information systems]]* set out in Regulation (EU) 2022/2554 of the European Parliament and of the Council


.

Regulation (EU) 2022/2554 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 14 December 2022 on *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(1)|digital operational resilience]]* for the financial sector and amending Regulations (EC) No 1060/2009, (EU) No 648/2012, (EU) No 600/2014, (EU) No 909/2014 and (EU) 2016/1011.’;"
### (b)
in paragraph 8, point (c) is replaced by the following:
the concrete organisational requirements laid down in paragraphs 3 and 5.’;
## (2)
[[2022R2554_EN.27h|Article 27h]] is amended as follows:
### (a)
paragraph 5 is replaced by the following:

‘5.   A CTP shall comply with the requirements concerning the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(4)|security of network and information systems]]* set out in Regulation (EU) 2022/2554.’.
### (b)
in paragraph 8, point (e) is replaced by the following:
the concrete organisational requirements laid down in paragraph 4.’;
## (3)
[[2022R2554_EN.27i|Article 27i]] is amended as follows:
### (a)
paragraph 3 is replaced by the following:

‘3.   An ARM shall comply with the requirements concerning the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(4)|security of network and information systems]]* set out in Regulation (EU) 2022/2554.’;
### (b)
in paragraph 5, point (b) is replaced by the following:
the concrete organisational requirements laid down in paragraphs 2 and 4.’.
