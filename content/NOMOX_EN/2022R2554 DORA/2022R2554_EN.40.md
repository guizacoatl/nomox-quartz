> Level 2 reference(s): [[2025R0420_EN.0]], [[2025R0420_EN.3]], [[2025R0420_EN.5]], [[2025R0420_EN.2]]# Article 40 - Ongoing oversight

## 1.
When conducting oversight activities, in particular general investigations or inspections, the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseer]]* shall be assisted by a joint examination team established for each *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(23)|critical ICT third-party service provider]]*.
## 2.
The joint examination team referred to in paragraph 1 shall be composed of staff members from:
### (a)
the ESAs;
### (b)
the relevant competent authorities supervising the financial entities to which the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(23)|critical ICT third-party service provider]]* provides *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(21)|ICT services]]*;
### (c)
the national competent authority referred to in [[2022R2554_EN.32#4|Article 32(4)]], point (e), on a voluntary basis;
### (d)
one national competent authority from the Member State where the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(23)|critical ICT third-party service provider]]* is established, on a voluntary basis.

Members of the joint examination team shall have expertise in ICT matters and in operational risk. The joint examination team shall work under the coordination of a designated *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseer]]* staff member (the ‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseer]]* coordinator’).
## 3.
Within 3 months of the completion of an investigation or inspection, the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseer]]*, after consulting the Oversight Forum, shall adopt recommendations to be addressed to the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(23)|critical ICT third-party service provider]]* pursuant to the powers referred to in [[2022R2554_EN.35|Article 35]].
## 4.
The recommendations referred to in paragraph 3 shall be immediately communicated to the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(23)|critical ICT third-party service provider]]* and to the competent authorities of the financial entities to which it provides *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(21)|ICT services]]*.
For the purposes of fulfilling the oversight activities, the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseer]]* may take into consideration any relevant third-party certifications and ICT third-party internal or external audit reports made available by the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(23)|critical ICT third-party service provider]]*.
