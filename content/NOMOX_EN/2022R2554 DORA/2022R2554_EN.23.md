# Article 23 - Operational or security payment-related incidents concerning credit institutions, payment institutions, account information service providers, and electronic money institutions


The requirements laid down in this Chapter shall also apply to *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(9)|operational or security payment-related incidents]]* and to *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(11)|major operational or security payment-related incidents]]*, where they concern *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(31)|credit institutions]]*, *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(35)|payment institutions]]*, *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(37)|account information service providers]]*, and *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(38)|electronic money institutions]]*.
