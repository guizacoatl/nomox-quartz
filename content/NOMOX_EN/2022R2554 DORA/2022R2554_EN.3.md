# Article 3 - Definitions


For the purposes of this Regulation, the following definitions shall apply:
## (1)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(1)|digital operational resilience]]*’ means the ability of a financial entity to build, assure and review its operational integrity and reliability by ensuring, either directly or indirectly through the use of services provided by *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(19)|ICT third-party service providers]]*, the full range of ICT-related capabilities needed to address the security of the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(2)|network and information systems]]* which a financial entity uses, and which support the continued provision of financial services and their quality, including throughout disruptions;
## (2)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(2)|network and information system]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(2)|network and information system]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.6|Article 6]], point 1, of Directive (EU) 2022/2555;
## (3)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(3)|legacy ICT system]]*’ means an ICT system that has reached the end of its lifecycle (end-of-life), that is not suitable for upgrades or fixes, for technological or commercial reasons, or is no longer supported by its supplier or by an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(19)|ICT third-party service provider]]*, but that is still in use and supports the functions of the financial entity;
## (4)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(4)|security of network and information systems]]*’ means *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(4)|security of network and information systems]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.6|Article 6]], point 2, of Directive (EU) 2022/2555;
## (5)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]*’ means any reasonably identifiable circumstance in relation to the use of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(2)|network and information systems]]* which, if materialised, may compromise the security of the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(2)|network and information systems]]*, of any technology dependent tool or process, of operations and processes, or of the provision of services by producing adverse effects in the digital or physical environment;
## (6)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(6)|information asset]]*’ means a collection of information, either tangible or intangible, that is worth protecting;
## (7)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(7)|ICT asset]]*’ means a software or hardware asset in the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(2)|network and information systems]]* used by the financial entity;
## (8)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incident]]*’ means a single event or a series of linked events unplanned by the financial entity that compromises the security of the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(2)|network and information systems]]*, and have an adverse impact on the availability, authenticity, integrity or confidentiality of data, or on the services provided by the financial entity;
## (9)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(9)|operational or security payment-related incident]]*’ means a single event or a series of linked events unplanned by the financial entities referred to in [[2022R2554_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], points (a) to (d), whether ICT-related or not, that has an adverse impact on the availability, authenticity, integrity or confidentiality of payment-related data, or on the payment-related services provided by the financial entity;
## (10)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(10)|major ICT-related incident]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incident]]* that has a high adverse impact on the *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(2)|network and information systems]]* that support *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(22)|critical or important functions]]* of the financial entity;
## (11)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(11)|major operational or security payment-related incident]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(9)|operational or security payment-related incident]]* that has a high adverse impact on the payment-related services provided;
## (12)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(12)|cyber threat]]*’ means ‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(12)|cyber threat]]*’ as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.2|Article 2]], point (8), of Regulation (EU) 2019/881;
## (13)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(13)|significant cyber threat]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(12)|cyber threat]]* the technical characteristics of which indicate that it could have the potential to result in a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(10)|major ICT-related incident]]* or a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(11)|major operational or security payment-related incident]]*;
## (14)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(14)|cyber-attack]]*’ means a malicious *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incident]]* caused by means of an attempt perpetrated by any threat actor to destroy, expose, alter, disable, steal or gain unauthorised access to, or make unauthorised use of, an asset;
## (15)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(15)|threat intelligence]]*’ means information that has been aggregated, transformed, analysed, interpreted or enriched to provide the necessary context for decision-making and to enable relevant and sufficient understanding in order to mitigate the impact of an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(8)|ICT-related incident]]* or of a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(12)|cyber threat]]*, including the technical details of a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(14)|cyber-attack]]*, those responsible for the attack and their modus operandi and motivations;
## (16)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(16)|vulnerability]]*’ means a weakness, susceptibility or flaw of an asset, system, process or control that can be exploited;
## (17)
‘threat-led penetration testing (TLPT)’ means a framework that mimics the tactics, techniques and procedures of real-life threat actors perceived as posing a genuine *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(12)|cyber threat]]*, that delivers a controlled, bespoke, intelligence-led (red team) test of the financial entity’s critical live production systems;
## (18)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(18)|ICT third-party risk]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(5)|ICT risk]]* that may arise for a financial entity in relation to its use of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(21)|ICT services]]* provided by *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(19)|ICT third-party service providers]]* or by subcontractors of the latter, including through outsourcing arrangements;
## (19)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(19)|ICT third-party service provider]]*’ means an undertaking providing *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(21)|ICT services]]*;
## (20)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(20)|ICT intra-group service provider]]*’ means an undertaking that is part of a financial *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(26)|group]]* and that provides predominantly *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(21)|ICT services]]* to financial entities within the same *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(26)|group]]* or to financial entities belonging to the same institutional protection scheme, including to their *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(27)|parent undertakings]]*, subsidiaries, branches or other entities that are under common ownership or control;
## (21)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(21)|ICT services]]*’ means digital and data services provided through ICT systems to one or more internal or external users on an ongoing basis, including hardware as a service and hardware services which includes the provision of technical support via software or firmware updates by the hardware provider, excluding traditional analogue telephone services;
## (22)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(22)|critical or important function]]*’ means a function, the disruption of which would materially impair the financial performance of a financial entity, or the soundness or continuity of its services and activities, or the discontinued, defective or failed performance of that function would materially impair the continuing compliance of a financial entity with the conditions and obligations of its authorisation, or with its other obligations under applicable financial services law;
## (23)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(23)|critical ICT third-party service provider]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(19)|ICT third-party service provider]]* designated as critical in accordance with [[2022R2554_EN.31|Article 31]];
## (24)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(24)|ICT third-party service provider established in a third country]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(19)|ICT third-party service provider]]* that is a legal person established in a third-country and that has entered into a contractual arrangement with a financial entity for the provision of *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(21)|ICT services]]*;
## (25)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(25)|subsidiary]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(25)|subsidiary]]* undertaking within the meaning of [[2022R2554_EN.2|Article 2]], point (10), and [[2013L0034_EN.22|Article 22 of Directive 2013/34/EU]];
## (26)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(26)|group]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(26)|group]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.2|Article 2]], point (11), of Directive 2013/34/EU;
## (27)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(27)|parent undertaking]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(27)|parent undertaking]]* within the meaning of [[2022R2554_EN.2|Article 2]], point (9), and [[2013L0034_EN.22|Article 22 of Directive 2013/34/EU]];
## (28)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(28)|ICT subcontractor established in a third country]]*’ means an ICT subcontractor that is a legal person established in a third-country and that has entered into a contractual arrangement either with an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(19)|ICT third-party service provider]]*, or with an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(24)|ICT third-party service provider established in a third country]]*;
## (29)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(29)|ICT concentration risk]]*’ means an exposure to individual or multiple related *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(23)|critical ICT third-party service providers]]* creating a degree of dependency on such providers so that the unavailability, failure or other type of shortfall of such provider may potentially endanger the ability of a financial entity to deliver *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(22)|critical or important functions]]*, or cause it to suffer other types of adverse effects, including large losses, or endanger the financial stability of the Union as a whole;
## (30)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(30)|management body]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(30)|management body]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (36), of Directive 2014/65/EU, [[2022R2554_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]], point (7), of Directive 2013/36/EU, [[2022R2554_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (s), of Directive 2009/65/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council


, [[2022R2554_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (45), of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014, [[2022R2554_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]], point (20), of Regulation (EU) 2016/1011, and in the relevant provision of the Regulation on markets in crypto-assets, or the equivalent persons who effectively run the entity or have key functions in accordance with relevant Union or national law;
## (31)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(31)|credit institution]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(31)|credit institution]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (1), of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council


;
## (32)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(32)|institution exempted pursuant to Directive 2013/36/EU]]*’ means an entity as referred to in [[2022R2554_EN.2#5|Article 2(5)]], points (4) to (23), of Directive 2013/36/EU;
## (33)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(33)|investment firm]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(33)|investment firm]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (1), of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (34)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(34)|small and non-interconnected investment firm]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(33)|investment firm]]* that meets the conditions laid out in [[2019R2033_EN.12#1|Article 12(1) of Regulation (EU) 2033/2019]] of the European Parliament and of the Council


;
## (35)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(35)|payment institution]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(35)|payment institution]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.4|Article 4]], point (4), of Directive (EU) 2015/2366;
## (36)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(36)|payment institution exempted pursuant to Directive (EU) 2015/2366]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(35)|payment institution]]* exempted pursuant to [[2015L2366_EN.32#1|Article 32(1) of Directive (EU) 2366/2015]];
## (37)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(37)|account information service provider]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(37)|account information service provider]]* as referred to in [[2015L2366_EN.33#1|Article 33(1) of Directive (EU) 2366/2015]];
## (38)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(38)|electronic money institution]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(38)|electronic money institution]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.2|Article 2]], point (1), of Directive 2009/110/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council;
## (39)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(39)|electronic money institution exempted pursuant to Directive 2009/110/EC]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(38)|electronic money institution]]* benefitting from a waiver as referred to in Article 9(1) of Directive 2009/110/EC;
## (40)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(40)|central counterparty]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(40)|central counterparty]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.2|Article 2]], point (1), of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012;
## (41)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(41)|trade repository]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(41)|trade repository]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.2|Article 2]], point (2), of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012;
## (42)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(42)|central securities depository]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(42)|central securities depository]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (1), of Regulation (EU) No 909/2014;
## (43)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(43)|trading venue]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(43)|trading venue]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (24), of Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (44)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(44)|manager of alternative investment funds]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(44)|manager of alternative investment funds]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.4#1|Article 4(1)]], point (b), of Directive 2011/61/EU;
## (45)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(45)|management company]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(45)|management company]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (b), of Directive 2009/65/EC;
## (46)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(46)|data reporting service provider]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(46)|data reporting service provider]]* within the meaning of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014, as referred to in [[2022R2554_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], points (34) to (36) thereof;
## (47)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(47)|insurance undertaking]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(47)|insurance undertaking]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.13|Article 13]], point (1), of Directive 2009/138/EC;
## (48)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(48)|reinsurance undertaking]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(48)|reinsurance undertaking]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.13|Article 13]], point (4), of Directive 2009/138/EC;
## (49)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(49)|insurance intermediary]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(49)|insurance intermediary]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (3), of Directive (EU) 2016/97 of the European Parliament and of the Council


;
## (50)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(50)|ancillary insurance intermediary]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(50)|ancillary insurance intermediary]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (4), of Directive (EU) 2016/97;
## (51)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(51)|reinsurance intermediary]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(51)|reinsurance intermediary]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (5), of Directive (EU) 2016/97;
## (52)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(52)|institution for occupational retirement provision]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(52)|institution for occupational retirement provision]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.6|Article 6]], point (1), of Directive (EU) 2016/2341;
## (53)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(53)|small institution for occupational retirement provision]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(52)|institution for occupational retirement provision]]* which operates pension schemes which together have less than 100 members in total;
## (54)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(54)|credit rating agency]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(54)|credit rating agency]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]], point (b), of Regulation (EC) No 1060/2009;
## (55)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(55)|crypto-asset service provider]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(55)|crypto-asset service provider]]* as defined in the relevant provision of the Regulation on markets in crypto-assets;
## (56)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(56)|issuer of asset-referenced tokens]]*’ means an *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(56)|issuer of asset-referenced tokens]]* as defined in the relevant provision of the Regulation on markets in crypto-assets;
## (57)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(57)|administrator of critical benchmarks]]*’ means an administrator of ‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(57)|critical benchmarks]]*’ as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]], point (25), of Regulation (EU) 2016/1011;
## (58)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(58)|crowdfunding service provider]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(58)|crowdfunding service provider]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.2#1|Article 2(1)]], point (e), of Regulation (EU) 2020/1503 of the European Parliament and of the Council


;
## (59)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(59)|securitisation repository]]*’ means a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(59)|securitisation repository]]* as defined in [[2022R2554_EN.2|Article 2]], point (23), of Regulation (EU) 2017/2402 of the European Parliament and of the Council


;
## (60)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(60)|microenterprise]]*’ means a financial entity, other than a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(43)|trading venue]]*, a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(40)|central counterparty]]*, a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(41)|trade repository]]* or a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(42)|central securities depository]]*, which employs fewer than 10 persons and has an annual turnover and/or annual balance sheet total that does not exceed EUR 2 million;
## (61)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(61)|Lead Overseer]]*’ means the European Supervisory Authority appointed in accordance with [[2022R2554_EN.31#1|Article 31(1)]], point (b) of this Regulation;
## (62)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(62)|Joint Committee]]*’ means the committee referred to in Article 54 of Regulations (EU) No 1093/2010, (EU) No 1094/2010 and (EU) No 1095/2010;
## (63)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(63)|small enterprise]]*’ means a financial entity that employs 10 or more persons, but fewer than 50 persons, and has an annual turnover and/or annual balance sheet total that exceeds EUR 2 million, but does not exceed EUR 10 million;
## (64)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(64)|medium-sized enterprise]]*’ means a financial entity that is not a *[[2022R2554_EN.3#(63)|small enterprise]]* and employs fewer than 250 persons and has an annual turnover that does not exceed EUR 50 million and/or an annual balance sheet that does not exceed EUR 43 million;
## (65)
‘*[[2022R2554_EN.3#(65)|public authority]]*’ means any government or other public administration entity, including national central banks.
