# Article 4 - Change of the membership in the joint examination team


Periodically, or where the Lead Overseer changes, or where material changes as specified in [[2025R0420_EN.2#2|Article 2(2)]] occur, the Lead Overseer, after having consulted the members of the joint examination team, shall assess the results of the members of the joint examination team. Both the nominating authorities and Lead Overseer shall use the results of that assessment to decide whether it is appropriate to change the membership of the joint examination team.
