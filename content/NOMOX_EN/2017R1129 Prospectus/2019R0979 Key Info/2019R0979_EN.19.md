# Article 19 - Upload of documents and accompanying data


When uploading any documents referred to in [[2017R1129_EN.25#6|Article 25(6) of Regulation (EU) 1129/2017]] into the notification portal, the competent authority shall ensure those documents are in a searchable electronic format that cannot be modified and are accompanied by the data relating to those documents as specified in the tables of Annex VII to this Regulation in a common XML format.
