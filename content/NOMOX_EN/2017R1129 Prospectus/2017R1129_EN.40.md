# Article 40 - Right of appeal


Member States shall ensure that decisions taken under this Regulation are properly reasoned and subject to a right of appeal before a tribunal.

For the purposes of [[2017R1129_EN.20|Article 20]], a right of appeal shall also apply where the competent authority has neither taken a decision to approve or to refuse an application for approval nor has made any request for changes or supplementary information within the time limits set out in [[2017R1129_EN.20#2|Article 20(2)]], (3), (6) and (6a) in respect of that application.
