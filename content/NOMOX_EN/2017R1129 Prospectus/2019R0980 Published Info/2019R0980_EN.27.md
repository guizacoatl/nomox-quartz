# Article 27 - Prospectus summary

## 1.
An overview section of a prospectus shall only use the term ‘*[[2019R0980_EN.1#1.|summary]]*’ if it complies with the requirements laid down in [[2017R1129_EN.7|Article 7 of Regulation (EU) 1129/2017]].
## 2.
Where the *[[2019R0980_EN.1#1.|summary]]* of a prospectus is to be supplemented in accordance with [[2017R1129_EN.23|Article 23 of Regulation (EU) 1129/2017]], the new information shall be integrated in the *[[2019R0980_EN.1#1.|summary]]* of that prospectus in a way that enables investors to easily identify the changes. The new information shall be integrated in the *[[2019R0980_EN.1#1.|summary]]* of the prospectus either by producing a new *[[2019R0980_EN.1#1.|summary]]* or by supplementing the original *[[2019R0980_EN.1#1.|summary]]*.
