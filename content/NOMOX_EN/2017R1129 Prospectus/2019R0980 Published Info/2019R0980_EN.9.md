# Article 9 - Registration document for secondary issuances of non-equity securities


A specific registration document for non-equity securities that is drawn up in accordance with [[2017R1129_EN.14|Article 14 of Regulation (EU) 1129/2017]] shall contain the information referred to in Annex 8 to this Regulation, unless it contains the information referred to in Annex 3 to this Regulation.
