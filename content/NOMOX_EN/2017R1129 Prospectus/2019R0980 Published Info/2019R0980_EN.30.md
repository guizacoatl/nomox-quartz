# Article 30 - EU Growth securities note for equity securities

## 1.
A specific securities note for equity securities drawn up in accordance with [[2017R1129_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) 1129/2017]] shall contain the information referred to in Annex 26 to this Regulation.
## 2.
By way of derogation from paragraph 1, the specific securities note for the securities referred to in paragraphs 1 and 2 of [[2019R0980_EN.19|Article 19]] and paragraphs 1 and 2 of [[2019R0980_EN.20|Article 20]] of this Regulation, where those securities are not shares or other transferrable securities equivalent to shares, shall be drawn up in accordance with [[2019R0980_EN.31|Article 31]] of this Regulation.
