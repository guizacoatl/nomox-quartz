# Article 16 - Securities note for wholesale non-equity securities


For non-equity securities as referred to in [[2019R0980_EN.8#2|Article 8(2)]] of this Regulation, the securities note shall contain the information referred to in Annex 15 to this Regulation, unless it contains the information referred to in Annex 14 to this Regulation or unless a specific securities note is drawn up in accordance with Articles [[2019R0980_EN.14|14]] or 15 of Regulation (EU) 2017/1129.
