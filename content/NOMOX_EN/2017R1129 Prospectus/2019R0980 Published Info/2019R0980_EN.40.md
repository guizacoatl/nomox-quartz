# Article 40 - Additional criteria for the scrutiny of the completeness, consistency and comprehensibility of the information contained in the prospectus


Where necessary for investor protection, the competent authority may apply criteria in addition to those laid down in Articles [[2019R0980_EN.36|36]], [[2019R0980_EN.37|37]] and [[2019R0980_EN.38|38]] for the purposes of scrutinising the completeness, comprehensibility and consistency of the information in the draft prospectus.
