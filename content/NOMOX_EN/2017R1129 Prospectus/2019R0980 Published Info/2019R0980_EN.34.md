# Article 34 - Supplements to the specific summary for the EU Growth prospectus


Where the specific *[[2019R0980_EN.1#1.|summary]]* of an EU Growth prospectus is to be supplemented in accordance with [[2017R1129_EN.23|Article 23 of Regulation (EU) 1129/2017]], the new information shall be integrated in the specific *[[2019R0980_EN.1#1.|summary]]* of that EU Growth prospectus in a way that enables investors to easily identify the changes. The new information shall be integrated in the specific *[[2019R0980_EN.1#1.|summary]]* of the EU Growth prospectus either by producing a new specific *[[2019R0980_EN.1#1.|summary]]* or by supplementing the original specific *[[2019R0980_EN.1#1.|summary]]*.
