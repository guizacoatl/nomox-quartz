# Article 31 - EU Growth securities note for non-equity securities


A specific securities note for non-equity securities that is drawn up in accordance with [[2017R1129_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) 1129/2017]] shall contain the information referred to in Annex 27 to this Regulation.
