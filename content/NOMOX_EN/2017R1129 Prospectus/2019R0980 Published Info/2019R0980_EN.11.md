# Article 11 - Registration document for non-equity securities issued by third countries and their regional and local authorities


By way of derogation from Articles [[2019R0980_EN.7|7]] and [[2019R0980_EN.8|8]], a registration document that is drawn up for non-equity securities issued by third countries or their regional or local authorities, shall contain the information referred to in Annex 10.
