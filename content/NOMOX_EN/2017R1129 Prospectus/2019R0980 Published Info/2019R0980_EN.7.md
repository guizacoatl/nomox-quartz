# Article 7 - Registration document for retail non-equity securities


For non-equity securities other than those referred to in [[2019R0980_EN.8#2|Article 8(2)]] of this Regulation, the registration document shall contain the information referred to in Annex 6 to this Regulation, unless it is drawn up in accordance with Articles [[2019R0980_EN.9|9]] and [[2019R0980_EN.14|14]] or 15 of Regulation (EU) 2017/1129 or contains the information referred to in Annex 1 to this Regulation.
