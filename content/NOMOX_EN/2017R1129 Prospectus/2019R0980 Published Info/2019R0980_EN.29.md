# Article 29 - EU Growth registration document for non-equity securities


A specific registration document for non-equity securities that is drawn up in accordance with [[2017R1129_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) 1129/2017]] shall contain the information referred to in Annex 25 to this Regulation.
