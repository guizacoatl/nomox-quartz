# Article 16 - Revision

## 1.
PRIIP manufacturers shall without undue delay revise the key information document where a review pursuant to [[2017R0653_EN.15|Article 15]] concludes that changes to the key information document need to be made.
## 2.
PRIIP manufacturers shall ensure that all sections of the key information document affected by such changes are updated.
## 3.
The PRIIP manufacturer shall publish the revised key information document on its website.
