# Article 15 - 1.


In accordance with [[2010R1094_EN.9#2|Article 9(2) of Regulation (EU) No 1094/2010]], EIOPA shall monitor the market for insurance-based investment products which are marketed, distributed or sold in the Union.
## 2.
Competent authorities shall monitor the market for insurance-based investment products which are marketed, distributed or sold in or from their Member State.
