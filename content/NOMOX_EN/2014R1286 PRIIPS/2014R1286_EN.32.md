# Article 32 - 1.


Management companies as defined in Article 2(1)(b) of Directive 2009/65/EC, investment companies as referred to in [[2014R1286_EN.27|Article 27]] thereof and persons advising on, or selling, units of UCITS as referred to in [[2014R1286_EN.1#2|Article 1(2)]] thereof shall be exempt from the obligations under this Regulation until
31 December 2022

.
## 2.
When a Member State applies rules on the format and content of the key information document, as laid down in Articles [[2014R1286_EN.78|78]] to 81 of Directive 2009/65/EC, to non-UCITS funds offered to retail investors, the exemption laid down in paragraph 1 of this Article shall apply to management companies, investment companies and persons advising on, or selling, units of such funds to retail investors.
