# Article 5 - General payment modalities

## 1.
All fees shall be payable in euros. The fees shall be paid as specified in Articles [[2020R1732_EN.6|6]] and [[2020R1732_EN.7|7]], and 8.
## 2.
Any late payments shall incur the default interest laid down in Article 99 of Regulation (EU, Euratom) 2018/1046 of the European Parliament and of the Council.
