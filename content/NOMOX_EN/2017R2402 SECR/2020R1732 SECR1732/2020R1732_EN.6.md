# Article 6 - Payment of registration fees


Payment of registration fees
## 1.
The registration fee and extension-of-registration fee referred to in [[2020R1732_EN.3|Article 3]] shall be paid in full at the time the securitisation repository submits its application for registration or extension of registration.
## 2.
ESMA shall not refund registration or extension-of-registration fees where an applicant withdraws its application for registration or extension of registration.
