# Article 4 - Annual supervisory fees for registered securitisation repositories and trade repositories that have extended their registration

## 1.
The annual supervisory fee for all registered securitisation repositories for year n shall be equal to the cost estimate for the supervision of those securitisation repositories’ activities as included in ESMA’s budget for that year.
## 2.
The annual supervisory fee to be paid by a securitisation repository for the year in which that securitisation repository was registered shall be equal to the registration fee due under [[2020R1732_EN.3|Article 3]], multiplied by the number of calendar days from the securitisation repository’s date of registration until the end of that year and divided by the total number of days in that year.

By way of derogation from the first subparagraph, where a securitisation repository is registered during the month of December, that repository shall not be required to pay an annual supervisory fee for the year in which it was registered.
## 3.
The annual supervisory fee for a given year n to be paid by a securitisation repository registered on or after 1 October of the preceding year shall be equal to the registration fee due under [[2020R1732_EN.3|Article 3]].
## 4.
The annual supervisory fee for a given year n to be paid by a securitisation repository registered before 1 October of the preceding year shall be equal to the annual supervisory fee referred to in paragraph 1 divided between all securitisation repositories registered before 1 October of that preceding year in proportion to the applicable turnover for each securitisation repository calculated in accordance with [[2020R1732_EN.2#3|Article 2(3)]].
## 5.
With the exception of the annual supervisory fee payable under paragraph 2, the annual supervisory fee shall never be less than EUR 30 000 .
