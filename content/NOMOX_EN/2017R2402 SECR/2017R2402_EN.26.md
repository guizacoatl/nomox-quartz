# Article 26 - Programme-level requirements

## 1.
All *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|ABCP transactions]]* within an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* shall fulfil the requirements of [[2017R2402_EN.24#1|Article 24(1)]] to (8) and (12) to (20).

A maximum of 5 % of the aggregate amount of the exposures underlying the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|ABCP transactions]]* and which are funded by the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* may temporarily be non-compliant with the requirements of [[2017R2402_EN.24#9|Article 24(9)]], (10) and (11) without affecting the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* status of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*.

For the purpose of the second subparagraph of this paragraph, a sample of the underlying exposures shall regularly be subject to external verification of compliance by an appropriate and independent party.
## 2.
The remaining weighted average life of the underlying exposures of an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* shall not be more than two years.
## 3.
The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* shall be fully supported by a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.25#2|Article 25(2)]].
## 4.
The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* shall not contain any *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(4)|resecuritisation]]* and the credit enhancement shall not establish a second layer of tranching at the programme level.
## 5.
The securities issued by an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* shall not include call options, extension clauses or other clauses that have an effect on their final maturity, where such options or clauses may be exercised at the discretion of the seller, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]*.
## 6.
The interest-rate and currency risks arising at *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* level shall be appropriately mitigated and any measures taken to that effect shall be disclosed. Except for the purpose of hedging interest-rate or currency risk, the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]* shall not enter into derivative contracts and shall ensure that the pool of underlying exposures does not include derivatives. Those derivatives shall be underwritten and documented according to common standards in international finance.
## 7.
The documentation relating to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* shall clearly specify:
### (a)
the responsibilities of the trustee and other entities with fiduciary duties, if any, to *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]*;
### (b)
the contractual obligations, duties and responsibilities of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]*, who shall have expertise in credit underwriting, the trustee, if any, and other ancillary service providers;
### (c)
the processes and responsibilities necessary to ensure that a default or insolvency of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(13)|servicer]]* does not result in a termination of servicing;
### (d)
the provisions for replacement of derivative counterparties, and the account bank at *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* level upon their default, insolvency and other specified events, where the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(14)|liquidity facility]]* does not cover such events;
### (e)
that, upon specified events, default or insolvency of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]*, remedial steps shall be provided for to achieve, as appropriate, collateralisation of the funding commitment or replacement of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(14)|liquidity facility]]* provider; and
### (f)
that the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(14)|liquidity facility]]* shall be drawn down and the maturing securities shall be repaid in the event that the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* does not renew the funding commitment of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(14)|liquidity facility]]* before its expiry.
## 8.
The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(13)|servicer]]* shall have expertise in servicing exposures of a similar nature to those securitised and shall have well-documented policies, procedures and risk-management controls relating to the servicing of exposures.
