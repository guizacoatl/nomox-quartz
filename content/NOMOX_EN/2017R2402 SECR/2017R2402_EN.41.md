# Article 41 - Amendment to Directive 2011/61/EU


[[2011L0061_EN.17|Article 17 of Directive 2011/61/EU]] is replaced by the following:

‘[[2017R2402_EN.17|Article 17]]
Where AIFMs are exposed to a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* that no longer meets the requirements provided for in Regulation (EU) 2017/2402 of the European Parliament and of the Council, they shall, in the best interest of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]* in the relevant AIFs, act and take corrective action, if appropriate.
