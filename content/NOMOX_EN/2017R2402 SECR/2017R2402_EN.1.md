# Article 1 - Subject matter and scope

## 1.
This Regulation lays down a general framework for *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]*. It defines *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* and establishes due-diligence, risk-retention and transparency requirements for parties involved in *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]*, criteria for credit granting, requirements for selling *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* to retail clients, a ban on re-*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]*, requirements for *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPEs]]* as well as conditions and procedures for *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* repositories. It also creates a specific framework for *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|simple, transparent and standardised]]* (‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]*’) *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]*.
## 2.
This Regulation applies to *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investors]]* and to *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originators]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsors]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lenders]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* special purpose entities.
