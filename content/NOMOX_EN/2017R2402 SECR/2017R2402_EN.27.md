## 1.
*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|Originators]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsors]]* shall jointly notify ESMA by means of the template referred to in paragraph 7 of this Article where a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* meets the requirements set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.19|19]] to 22, Articles [[2017R2402_EN.23|23]] to 26 or Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26a|26a]] to 26e (‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS notification]]*’). In the case of an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*, only the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* shall be responsible for the notification of that programme and, within that programme, of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|ABCP transactions]]* complying with [[2017R2402_EN.24|Article 24]]. In the case of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(10)|synthetic securitisation]]*, only the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* shall be responsible for the notification.

The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS notification]]* shall include an explanation by the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* of how the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* criteria set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.20|20]] to 22, Articles [[2017R2402_EN.24|24]] to 26 or Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26b|26b]] to 26e have been complied with.

ESMA shall publish the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS notification]]* on its official website pursuant to paragraph 5. *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|Originators]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsors]]* of a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* shall inform their competent authorities of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS notification]]* and designate amongst themselves one entity to be the first contact point for *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]* and competent authorities.
## 2.
The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]* may use the service of a third party authorised under [[2017R2402_EN.28|Article 28]] to assess whether a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* complies with Articles [[2017R2402_EN.19|19]] to 22, Articles [[2017R2402_EN.23|23]] to 26 or Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26a|26a]] to 26e.

However, the use of such a service shall not, under any circumstances, affect the liability of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]* in respect of their legal obligations under this Regulation. The use of such service shall not affect the obligations imposed on *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investors]]* as set out in [[2017R2402_EN.5|Article 5]].

Where the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]* uses the service of a third party authorised pursuant to [[2017R2402_EN.28|Article 28]] to assess whether a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* complies with Articles [[2017R2402_EN.19|19]] to 22, Articles [[2017R2402_EN.23|23]] to 26 or Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26a|26a]] to 26e, the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS notification]]* shall include a statement that compliance with the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* criteria was confirmed by that authorised third party.

The notification shall include the name of the authorised third party, its place of establishment and the name of the competent authority that authorised it.
## 3.
Where the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]* is not a credit institution or investment firm, as defined in points (1) and (2) of [[2013R0575_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]], established in the Union, the notification pursuant to paragraph 1 of this Article shall be accompanied by the following:
### (a)
confirmation by the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]* that its credit-granting is done on the basis of sound and well-defined criteria and clearly established processes for approving, amending, renewing and financing credits and that the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]* has effective systems in place to apply such processes in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.9|Article 9]] of this Regulation; and
### (b)
a declaration by the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]* as to whether credit granting referred to in point (a) is subject to supervision.
## 4.
The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* and, where applicable, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]*, shall immediately notify ESMA and inform their competent authority when a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* no longer meets the requirements set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.19|19]] to 22, Articles [[2017R2402_EN.23|23]] to 26, or Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26a|26a]] to 26e.
## 5.
ESMA shall maintain, on its official website, a list of all *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* which the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originators]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsors]]* have notified it of meeting the requirements set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.19|19]] to 22, Articles [[2017R2402_EN.23|23]] to 26, or Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26a|26a]] to 26e.

ESMA shall add each *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* so notified to that list immediately and shall update the list where the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* are no longer considered to be *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* following a decision of competent authorities or a notification by the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]*. Where the competent authority has imposed administrative sanctions in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.32|Article 32]], it shall notify ESMA thereof immediately. ESMA shall immediately indicate on the list that a competent authority has imposed administrative sanctions in relation to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* concerned.
## 6.
ESMA, in close cooperation with the EBA and EIOPA, shall develop draft regulatory technical standards specifying the information that the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]* are required to provide in order to comply with the obligations referred to in paragraph 1.

ESMA shall submit those draft regulatory technical standards to the Commission by 10 October 2021.

The Commission is empowered to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in this paragraph in accordance with Articles [[2010R1095_EN.10|10]] to [[2010R1095_EN.14|14]] of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010.
## 7.
In order to ensure uniform conditions for the implementation of this Regulation, ESMA, in close cooperation with the EBA and EIOPA, shall develop draft implementing technical standards to establish the templates to be used for the provision of the information referred to in paragraph 6.

ESMA shall submit those draft implementing technical standards to the Commission by 10 October 2021.

Power is conferred on the Commission to adopt the implementing technical standards referred to in this paragraph in accordance with [[2010R1095_EN.15|Article 15 of Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010]].
