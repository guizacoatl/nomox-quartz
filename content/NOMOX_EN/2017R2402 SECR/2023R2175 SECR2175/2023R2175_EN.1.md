# Article 1 - Definitions


For the purposes of this Regulation, the following definitions shall apply:
### (a)
‘synthetic form of retention’ means the retention of a material net economic interest through the use of derivative instruments;
### (b)
‘contingent form of retention’ means the retention of a material net economic interest through the use of credit support that ensures an immediate enforcement of the retention, including by guarantees, letters of credit, or similar forms of credit support.
