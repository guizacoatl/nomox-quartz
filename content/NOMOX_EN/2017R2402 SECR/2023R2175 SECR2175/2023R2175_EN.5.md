# Article 5 - Retention of the originator’s interest in a revolving securitisation or securitisation of revolving exposures


The retention of the originator’s interest of not less than 5 % of the nominal value of each of the securitised exposures as referred to in [[2023R2175_EN.6#3|Article 6(3)]], point (b), of Regulation (EU) 2017/2402 shall only be considered fulfilled where the retained credit risk of such exposures ranks

pari passu

with, or is subordinated to, the credit risk securitised in relation to the same exposures.
