# Article 13 - Transactions for which the retention requirement does not apply, as referred to Article 6(6) of Regulation (EU) 2017/2402


Transactions for which the retention requirement does not apply, as referred to in [[2017R2402_EN.6#6|Article 6(6) of Regulation (EU) 2402/2017]], shall include securitisation positions in the correlation trading portfolio which are either reference instruments as referred to in [[2023R2175_EN.338#1|Article 338(1)]], point (b), of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 or which are eligible for inclusion in the correlation trading portfolio.
