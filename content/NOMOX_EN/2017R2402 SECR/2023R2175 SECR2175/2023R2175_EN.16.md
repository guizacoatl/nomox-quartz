# Article 16 - Fulfilment of the retention requirement in securitisations of own issued debt instruments


Where an entity securitises its own issued debt instruments, including covered bonds as defined in [[2023R2175_EN.3|Article 3]], point (1), of Directive (EU) 2019/2162 of the European Parliament and of the Council


, and the underlying exposures of the securitisation comprise exclusively those own-issued debt instruments, the retention requirement in [[2017R2402_EN.6#1|Article 6(1) of Regulation (EU) 2402/2017]] shall be considered complied with.
