# Article 2 - Definitions


For the purposes of this Regulation, the following definitions apply:
## (1)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]*’ means a transaction or scheme, whereby the credit risk associated with an exposure or a pool of exposures is tranched, having all of the following characteristics:
### (a)
payments in the transaction or scheme are dependent upon the performance of the exposure or of the pool of exposures;
### (b)
the subordination of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(6)|tranches]]* determines the distribution of losses during the ongoing life of the transaction or scheme;
### (c)
the transaction or scheme does not create exposures which possess all of the characteristics listed in [[2013R0575_EN.147#8|Article 147(8) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]].
## (2)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|securitisation special purpose entity]]*’ or ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]*’ means a corporation, trust or other entity, other than an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]*, established for the purpose of carrying out one or more *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]*, the activities of which are limited to those appropriate to accomplishing that objective, the structure of which is intended to isolate the obligations of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]* from those of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*;
## (3)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*’ means an entity which:
### (a)
itself or through related entities, directly or indirectly, was involved in the original agreement which created the obligations or potential obligations of the debtor or potential debtor giving rise to the exposures being securitised; or
### (b)
purchases a third party’s exposures on its own account and then securitises them;
## (4)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(4)|resecuritisation]]*’ means *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* where at least one of the underlying exposures is a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]*;
## (5)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]*’ means a credit institution, whether located in the Union or not, as defined in point (1) of [[2013R0575_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]], or an investment firm as defined in point (1) of [[2014L0065_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2014/65/EU]] other than an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, that:
### (a)
establishes and manages an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|asset-backed commercial paper programme]]* or other *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* that purchases exposures from third-party entities, or
### (b)
establishes an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|asset-backed commercial paper programme]]* or other *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* that purchases exposures from third-party entities and delegates the day-to-day active portfolio management involved in that *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* to an entity authorised to perform such activity in accordance with Directive 2009/65/EC, Directive 2011/61/EU or Directive 2014/65/EU;
## (6)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(6)|tranche]]*’ means a contractually established segment of the credit risk associated with an exposure or a pool of exposures, where a position in the segment entails a risk of credit loss greater than or less than a position of the same amount in another segment, without taking account of credit protection provided by third parties directly to the holders of positions in the segment or in other segments;
## (7)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|asset-backed commercial paper programme]]*’ or ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*’ means a programme of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* the securities issued by which predominantly take the form of asset-backed commercial paper with an original maturity of one year or less;
## (8)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|asset-backed commercial paper transaction]]*’ or ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|ABCP transaction]]*’ means a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* within an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*;
## (9)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(9)|traditional securitisation]]*’ means a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* involving the transfer of the economic interest in the exposures being securitised through the transfer of ownership of those exposures from the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* to an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]* or through sub-participation by an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]*, where the securities issued do not represent payment obligations of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*;
## (10)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(10)|synthetic securitisation]]*’ means a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* where the transfer of risk is achieved by the use of credit derivatives or guarantees, and the exposures being securitised remain exposures of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*;
## (11)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investor]]*’ means a natural or legal person holding a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]*;
## (12)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investor]]*’ means an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investor]]* which is one of the following:
### (a)
an insurance undertaking as defined in point (1) of Article 13 of Directive 2009/138/EC;
### (b)
a reinsurance undertaking as defined in point (4) of Article 13 of Directive 2009/138/EC;
### (c)
an institution for occupational retirement provision falling within the scope of Directive (EU) 2016/2341 of the European Parliament and of the Council in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.2|Article 2]] thereof, unless a Member States has chosen not to apply that Directive in whole or in parts to that institution in accordance with Article 5 of that Directive; or an investment manager or an authorised entity appointed by an institution for occupational retirement provision pursuant to [[2016L2341_EN.32|Article 32 of Directive (EU) 2341/2016]];
### (d)
an alternative investment fund manager (AIFM) as defined in point (b) of [[2011L0061_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2011/61/EU]] that manages and/or markets alternative investment funds in the Union;
### (e)
an undertaking for the collective investment in transferable securities (UCITS) management company, as defined in point (b) of Article 2(1) of Directive 2009/65/EC;
### (f)
an internally managed UCITS, which is an investment company authorised in accordance with Directive 2009/65/EC and which has not designated a management company authorised under that Directive for its management;
### (g)
a credit institution as defined in point (1) of [[2013R0575_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]] for the purposes of that Regulation or an investment firm as defined in point (2) of Article 4(1) of that Regulation;
## (13)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(13)|servicer]]*’ means an entity that manages a pool of purchased receivables or the underlying credit exposures on a day-to-day basis;
## (14)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(14)|liquidity facility]]*’ means the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]* arising from a contractual agreement to provide funding to ensure timeliness of cash flows to *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]*;
## (15)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(15)|revolving exposure]]*’ means an exposure whereby borrowers’ outstanding balances are permitted to fluctuate based on their decisions to borrow and repay, up to an agreed limit;
## (16)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(16)|revolving securitisation]]*’ means a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* where the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* structure itself revolves by exposures being added to or removed from the pool of exposures irrespective of whether the exposures revolve or not;
## (17)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(17)|early amortisation provision]]*’ means a contractual clause in a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(15)|revolving exposures]]* or a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(16)|revolving securitisation]]* which requires, on the occurrence of defined events, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]*’ *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation positions]]* to be redeemed before the originally stated maturity of those positions;
## (18)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(18)|first loss tranche]]*’ means the most subordinated *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(6)|tranche]]* in a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* that is the first *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(6)|tranche]]* to bear losses incurred on the securitised exposures and thereby provides protection to the second loss and, where relevant, higher ranking *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(6)|tranches]]*.
## (19)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]*’ means an exposure to a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]*;
## (20)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]*’ means an entity which, itself or through related entities, directly or indirectly, concluded the original agreement which created the obligations or potential obligations of the debtor or potential debtor giving rise to the exposures being securitised;
## (21)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(21)|fully- supported ABCP programme]]*’ means an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* that its *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* directly and fully supports by providing to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]*(s) one or more liquidity facilities covering at least all of the following:
### (a)
all liquidity and credit risks of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*;
### (b)
any material dilution risks of the exposures being securitised;
### (c)
any other *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|ABCP transaction]]*-level and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*-level costs if necessary to guarantee to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investor]]* the full payment of any amount under the ABCP;
## (22)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(22)|fully supported ABCP transaction]]*’ means an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|ABCP transaction]]* supported by a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(14)|liquidity facility]]*, at transaction level or at *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* level, that covers at least all of the following:
### (a)
all liquidity and credit risks of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|ABCP transaction]]*;
### (b)
any material dilution risks of the exposures being securitised in the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|ABCP transaction]]*;
### (c)
any other *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|ABCP transaction]]*-level and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*-level costs if necessary to guarantee to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investor]]* the full payment of any amount under the ABCP;
## (23)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(23)|securitisation repository]]*’ means a legal person that centrally collects and maintains the records of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]*.

For the purpose of [[2017R2402_EN.10|Article 10]] of this Regulation, references in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.61|61]], [[2017R2402_EN.64|64]], [[2017R2402_EN.65|65]], [[2017R2402_EN.66|66]], [[2017R2402_EN.73|73]], [[2017R2402_EN.78|78]], [[2017R2402_EN.79|79]] and [[2017R2402_EN.80|80]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 to ‘trade repository’ shall be construed as references to ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(23)|securitisation repository]]*’;
## (24)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(24)|non-performing exposure]]*’ or ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(24)|NPE]]*’ means an exposure that meets any of the conditions set out in [[2013R0575_EN.47a#3|Article 47a(3) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]];
## (25)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(25)|NPE securitisation]]*’ means a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* backed by a pool of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(24)|non-performing exposures]]* the nominal value of which makes up not less than 90 % of the entire pool’s nominal value at the time of origination and at any later time where assets are added to or removed from the underlying pool due to replenishment, restructuring or any other relevant reason;
## (26)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(26)|credit protection agreement]]*’ means an agreement concluded between the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* and the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investor]]* to transfer the credit risk of securitised exposures from the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investor]]* by means of credit derivatives or guarantees, whereby the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* commits to pay an amount, known as a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(27)|credit protection premium]]*, to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investor]]* and the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investor]]* commits to pay an amount, known as a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(28)|credit protection payment]]*, to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* in the event that one of the contractually defined credit events occurs;
## (27)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(27)|credit protection premium]]*’ means the amount the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* has committed to pay to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investor]]* under the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(26)|credit protection agreement]]* for the credit protection promised by the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investor]]*;
## (28)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(28)|credit protection payment]]*’ means the amount the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investor]]* has committed to pay to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* under the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(26)|credit protection agreement]]* in the event that a credit event defined in the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(26)|credit protection agreement]]* occurs;
## (29)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(29)|synthetic excess spread]]*’ means the amount that, according to the documentation of a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(10)|synthetic securitisation]]*, is contractually designated by the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* to absorb losses of the securitised exposures that might occur before the maturity date of the transaction;
## (30)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(30)|sustainability factors]]*’ mean *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(30)|sustainability factors]]* as defined in point (24) of [[2019R2088_EN.2|Article 2 of Regulation (EU) 2088/2019]] of the European Parliament and of the Council;
## (31)
‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(31)|non-refundable purchase price discount]]*’ means the difference between the outstanding balance of the exposures in the underlying pool and the price at which those exposures are sold by the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]*, where neither the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* nor the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]* are reimbursed for that difference.
