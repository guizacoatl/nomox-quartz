# Article 19 - Simple, transparent and standardised non-ABCP traditional securitisation


*[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|Simple, transparent and standardised]]* non-ABCP *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(9)|traditional securitisation]]*
## 1.
*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(9)|Traditional securitisations]]*, except for *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programmes]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|ABCP transactions]]*, that meet the requirements set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.20|20]], [[2017R2402_EN.21|21]] and [[2017R2402_EN.22|22]], shall be considered to be *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]*.
## 2.
By 18 October 2018, the EBA, in close cooperation with ESMA and EIOPA, shall adopt, in accordance with [[2010R1093_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]], guidelines and recommendations on the harmonised interpretation and application of the requirements set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.20|20]], [[2017R2402_EN.21|21]] and [[2017R2402_EN.22|22]].
