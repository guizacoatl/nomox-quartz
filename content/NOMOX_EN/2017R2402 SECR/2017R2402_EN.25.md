# Article 25 - Sponsor of an ABCP programme

## 1.
The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* shall be a credit institution supervised under Directive 2013/36/EU.
## 2.
The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* of an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* shall be a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(14)|liquidity facility]]* provider and shall support all *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation positions]]* on an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* level by covering all liquidity and credit risks and any material dilution risks of the securitised exposures as well as any other transaction- and programme-level costs if necessary to guarantee to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investor]]* the full payment of any amount under the ABCP with such support. The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* shall disclose a description of the support provided at transaction level to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]* including a description of the liquidity facilities provided.
## 3.
Before being able to *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*, the credit institution shall demonstrate to its competent authority that its role under paragraph 2 does not endanger its solvency and liquidity, even in an extreme stress situation in the market.

The requirement referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph shall be considered to be fulfilled where the competent authority has determined on the basis of the review and evaluation referred to [[2013L0036_EN.97#3|Article 97(3) of Directive 2013/36/EU]] that the arrangements, strategies, processes and mechanisms implemented by that credit institution and the own funds and liquidity held by it ensure the sound management and coverage of its risks.
## 4.
The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* shall perform its own due diligence and shall verify compliance with the requirements set out in [[2017R2402_EN.5#1|Article 5(1)]] and (3) of this Regulation, as applicable. It shall also verify that the seller has in place servicing capabilities and collection processes that meet the requirements specified in points (h) to (p) of [[2013R0575_EN.265#2|Article 265(2) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]] or equivalent requirements in third countries.
## 5.
The seller, at the level of a transaction, or the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]*, at the level of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*, shall satisfy the risk-retention requirement referred to in [[2017R2402_EN.6|Article 6]].
## 6.
The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* shall be responsible for compliance with [[2017R2402_EN.7|Article 7]] at *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* level and for making available to potential *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]* before pricing upon their request:
### (a)
the aggregate information required by point (a) of the first subparagraph of [[2017R2402_EN.7#1|Article 7(1)]]; and
### (b)
the information required by points (b) to (e) of the first subparagraph of [[2017R2402_EN.7#1|Article 7(1)]], at least in draft or initial form.
## 7.
In the event that the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* does not renew the funding commitment of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(14)|liquidity facility]]* before its expiry, the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(14)|liquidity facility]]* shall be drawn down and the maturing securities shall be repaid.
