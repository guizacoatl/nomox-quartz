# Article 5 - Due-diligence requirements for institutional investors

## 1.
Prior to holding a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]*, an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investor]]*, other than the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]*, shall verify that:
### (a)
where the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]* established in the Union is not a credit institution or an investment firm as defined in points (1) and (2) of [[2013R0575_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]], the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]* grants all the credits giving rise to the underlying exposures on the basis of sound and well-defined criteria and clearly established processes for approving, amending, renewing and financing those credits and has effective systems in place to apply those criteria and processes in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]] of this Regulation;
### (b)
where the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]* is established in a third country, the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]* grants all the credits giving rise to the underlying exposures on the basis of sound and well-defined criteria and clearly established processes for approving, amending, renewing and financing those credits and has effective systems in place to apply those criteria and processes to ensure that credit-granting is based on a thorough assessment of the obligor’s creditworthiness;
### (c)
if established in the Union, the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]* retains on an ongoing basis a material net economic interest in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.6|Article 6]] and the risk retention is disclosed to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investor]]* in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.7|Article 7]];
### (d)
if established in a third country, the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]* retains on an ongoing basis a material net economic interest which, in any event, shall not be less than 5 %, determined in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.6|Article 6]], and discloses the risk retention to *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investors]]*;
### (e)
the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]* has, where applicable, made available the information required by [[2017R2402_EN.7|Article 7]] in accordance with the frequency and modalities provided for in that Article;
### (f)
in the case of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(24)|non-performing exposures]]*, sound standards are applied in the selection and pricing of the exposures.
## 2.
By derogation from paragraph 1, as regards *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(22)|fully supported ABCP transactions]]*, the requirement specified in point (a) of paragraph 1 shall apply to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]*. In such cases, the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* shall verify that the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]* which is not a credit institution or an investment firm grants all the credits giving rise to the underlying exposures on the basis of sound and well-defined criteria and clearly established processes for approving, amending, renewing and financing those credits and has effective systems in place to apply those criteria and processes in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]].
## 3.
Prior to holding a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]*, an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investor]]*, other than the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]*, shall carry out a due-diligence assessment which enables it to assess the risks involved. That assessment shall consider at least all of the following:
### (a)
the risk characteristics of the individual *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]* and of the underlying exposures;
### (b)
all the structural features of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* that can materially impact the performance of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]*, including the contractual priorities of payment and priority of payment-related triggers, credit enhancements, liquidity enhancements, market value triggers, and transaction-specific definitions of default;
### (c)
with regard to a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* notified as *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.27|Article 27]], the compliance of that *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* with the requirements provided for in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.19|19]] to 22 or in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.23|23]] to 26, and [[2017R2402_EN.27|Article 27]]. *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|Institutional investors]]* may rely to an appropriate extent on the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS notification]]* pursuant to [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]] and on the information disclosed by the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]* on the compliance with the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* requirements, without solely or mechanistically relying on that notification or information.

Notwithstanding points (a) and (b) of the first subparagraph, in the case of a fully supported *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investors]]* in the commercial paper issued by that *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* shall consider the features of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* and the full liquidity support.
## 4.
An *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investor]]*, other than the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]*, holding a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]*, shall at least:
### (a)
establish appropriate written procedures that are proportionate to the risk profile of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]* and, where relevant, to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investor]]*’s trading and non-trading book, in order to monitor, on an ongoing basis, compliance with paragraphs 1 and 3 and the performance of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]* and of the underlying exposures.

Where relevant with respect to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* and the underlying exposures, those written procedures shall include monitoring of the exposure type, the percentage of loans more than 30, 60 and 90 days past due, default rates, prepayment rates, loans in foreclosure, recovery rates, repurchases, loan modifications, payment holidays, collateral type and occupancy, and frequency distribution of credit scores or other measures of credit worthiness across underlying exposures, industry and geographical diversification, frequency distribution of loan to value ratios with band widths that facilitate adequate sensitivity analysis. Where the underlying exposures are themselves *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation positions]]*, as permitted under [[2017R2402_EN.8|Article 8]], *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investors]]* shall also monitor the exposures underlying those positions;
### (b)
in the case of a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* other than a fully supported *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*, regularly perform stress tests on the cash flows and collateral values supporting the underlying exposures or, in the absence of sufficient data on cash flows and collateral values, stress tests on loss assumptions, having regard to the nature, scale and complexity of the risk of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]*;
### (c)
in the case of fully supported *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*, regularly perform stress tests on the solvency and liquidity of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]*;
### (d)
ensure internal reporting to its management body so that the management body is aware of the material risks arising from the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]* and so that those risks are adequately managed;
### (e)
be able to demonstrate to its competent authorities, upon request, that it has a comprehensive and thorough understanding of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]* and its underlying exposures and that it has implemented written policies and procedures for the risk management of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation position]]* and for maintaining records of the verifications and due diligence in accordance with paragraphs 1 and 2 and of any other relevant information; and
### (f)
in the case of exposures to a fully supported *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*, be able to demonstrate to its competent authorities, upon request, that it has a comprehensive and thorough understanding of the credit quality of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* and of the terms of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(14)|liquidity facility]]* provided.
## 5.
Without prejudice to paragraphs 1 to 4 of this Article, where an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investor]]* has given another *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investor]]* authority to make investment management decisions that might expose it to a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]*, the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investor]]* may instruct that managing party to fulfil its obligations under this Article in respect of any exposure to a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* arising from those decisions. Member States shall ensure that, where an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investor]]* is instructed under this paragraph to fulfil the obligations of another *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investor]]* and fails to do so, any sanction under Articles [[2017R2402_EN.32|32]] and [[2017R2402_EN.33|33]] may be imposed on the managing party and not on the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(12)|institutional investor]]* who is exposed to the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]*.
