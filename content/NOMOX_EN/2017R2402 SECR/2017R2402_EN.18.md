# Article 18 - Use of the designation ‘simple, transparent and standardised securitisation’


*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|Originators]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsors]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPEs]]* may use the designation ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]*’ or ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|simple, transparent and standardised]]*’, or a designation that refers directly or indirectly to those terms for their *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]*, only where:
### (a)
the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* meets all the requirements set out in Section 1, 2 or 2a of this Chapter, and ESMA has been notified pursuant to [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]]; and
### (b)
the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* is included in the list referred to in [[2017R2402_EN.27#5|Article 27(5)]].

The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]* involved in a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* considered *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* shall be established in the Union.
