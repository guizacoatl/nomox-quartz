# Article 43 - Transitional provisions

## 1.
This Regulation shall apply to *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* the securities of which are issued on or after 1 January 2019, subject to paragraphs 7 and 8.
## 2.
In respect of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#9.|securitisations the securities of which were issued]]* before 1 January 2019, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originators]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsors]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPEs]]* may use the designation ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]*’ or ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|simple, transparent and standardised]]*’, or a designation that refers directly or indirectly to those terms, only where the requirements set out in [[2017R2402_EN.18|Article 18]] and the conditions set out in paragraph 3 of this Article are complied with.
## 3.
*[[2017R2402_EN.2#9.|Securitisations the securities of which were issued]]* before 1 January 2019, other than *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation positions]]* relating to an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|ABCP transaction]]* or an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*, shall be considered ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]*’ provided that:
### (a)
they met, at the time of issuance of those securities, the requirements set out in [[2017R2402_EN.20#1|Article 20(1)]] to (5), (7) to (9) and (11) to (13) and [[2017R2402_EN.21#1|Article 21(1)]] and (3); and
### (b)
they meet, as of the time of notification pursuant to [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]], the requirements set out in [[2017R2402_EN.20#6|Article 20(6)]] and (10), [[2017R2402_EN.21#2|Article 21(2)]] and (4) to (10) and [[2017R2402_EN.22#1|Article 22(1)]] to (5).
## 4.
For the purposes of point (b) of paragraph 3, the following shall apply:
### (a)
in [[2017R2402_EN.22#2|Article 22(2)]], ‘prior to issuance’ shall be deemed to read ‘prior to notification under [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]]’;
### (b)
in [[2017R2402_EN.22#3|Article 22(3)]], ‘before the pricing of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]*’ shall be deemed to read ‘prior to notification under [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]]’;
### (c)
in [[2017R2402_EN.22#5|Article 22(5)]]:
#### (i)
in the second sentence, ‘before pricing’ shall be deemed to read ‘prior to notification under [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]]’;
#### (ii)
‘before pricing at least in draft or initial form’ shall be deemed to read ‘prior to notification under [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]]’;
#### (iii)
the requirement set out in the fourth sentence shall not apply;
#### (iv)
references to compliance with [[2017R2402_EN.7|Article 7]] shall be construed as if [[2017R2402_EN.7|Article 7]] applied to those *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* notwithstanding [[2017R2402_EN.43#1|Article 43(1)]].
## 5.
In respect of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#9.|securitisations the securities of which were issued]]* on or after 1 January 2011 but before 1 January 2019 and in respect of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#9.|securitisations the securities of which were issued]]* before 1 January 2011 where new underlying exposures have been added or substituted after 31 December 2014, the due-diligence requirements as provided for in Regulation (EU) No 575/2013, Delegated Regulation (EU) 2015/35 and Delegated Regulation (EU) No 231/2013 respectively shall continue to apply in the version applicable on 31 December 2018.
## 6.
In respect of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#9.|securitisations the securities of which were issued]]* before 1 January 2019 credit institutions or investment firms as defined in points (1) and (2) of [[2013R0575_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]], insurance undertakings as defined in point (1) of Article 13 of Directive 2009/138/EC, reinsurance undertakings as defined in point (4) of Article 13 of Directive 2009/138/EC and alternative investment fund managers (AIFMs) as defined in point (b) of [[2011L0061_EN.4#1|Article 4(1) of Directive 2011/61/EU]] shall continue to apply [[2013R0575_EN.405|Article 405 of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013]] and Chapters I, II and III and Article 22 of Delegated Regulation (EU) No 625/2014, Articles [[2017R2402_EN.254|254]] and [[2017R2402_EN.255|255]] of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2015/35 and Article 51 of Delegated Regulation (EU) No 231/2013 respectively as in the version applicable on 31 December 2018.
## 7.
Until the regulatory technical standards to be adopted by the Commission pursuant [[2017R2402_EN.6#7|Article 6(7)]] of this Regulation apply, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originators]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsors]]* or the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]* shall, for the purposes of the obligations set out in [[2017R2402_EN.6|Article 6]] of this Regulation, apply Chapters I, II and III and Article 22 of Delegated Regulation (EU) No 625/2014 to *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* the securities of which are issued on or after 1 January 2019.
## 8.
Until the regulatory technical standards to be adopted by the Commission pursuant to [[2017R2402_EN.7#3|Article 7(3)]] of this Regulation apply, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originators]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsors]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPEs]]* shall, for the purposes of the obligations set out in points (a) and (e) of the first subparagraph of [[2017R2402_EN.7#1|Article 7(1)]] of this Regulation, make the information referred to in Annexes I to VIII of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2015/3 available in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.7#2|Article 7(2)]] of this Regulation.
## 9.
For the purpose of this Article, in the case of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* which do not involve the issuance of securities, any references to ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#9.|securitisations the securities of which were issued]]*’ shall be deemed to mean ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#9.|securitisations the initial securitisation positions of which are created]]*’, provided that this Regulation applies to any *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* that create new *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation positions]]* on or after 1 January 2019.
