# Article 7 - Format of the application

## 1.
A third party shall allocate a unique reference number to each document it submits to the competent authority as part of its application.
## 2.
A third party shall include a substantiated explanation in its application for any requirement of this Regulation considered non-applicable.
## 3.
The application referred to in [[2019R0885_EN.1|Article 1]] shall be accompanied by a letter signed by a member of the third party's management body confirming that:
### (a)
the submitted information is accurate and complete to the best of his or her knowledge, as of the date of the submission of the application;
### (b)
the applicant is neither a regulated entity as defined in point (4) of Article 2 of Directive 2002/87/EC


, nor a credit rating agency as defined in point (b) of [[2009R1060_EN.3#1|Article 3(1) of Regulation (EC) No 1060/2009]].
