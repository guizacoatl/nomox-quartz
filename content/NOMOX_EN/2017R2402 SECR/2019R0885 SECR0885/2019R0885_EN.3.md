# Article 3 - Corporate governance


Where the third party adheres to a corporate governance code of conduct for the appointment and role of the independent directors and the management of conflicts of interest, the application referred to in [[2019R0885_EN.1|Article 1]] shall identify that code and provide an explanation for any deviation by the third party s from that code.
