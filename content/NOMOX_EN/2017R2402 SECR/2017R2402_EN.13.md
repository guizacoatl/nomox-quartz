# Article 13 - Notification of ESMA decisions relating to registration or extension of registration

## 1.
Where ESMA adopts a decision as referred to in [[2017R2402_EN.12|Article 12]] or withdraws the registration as referred to in [[2017R2402_EN.15#1|Article 15(1)]], it shall notify the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(23)|securitisation repository]]* within five working days with a fully reasoned explanation for its decision.

ESMA shall, without undue delay, notify the competent authority as referred to in Article 11(1) of its decision.
## 2.
ESMA shall communicate, without undue delay, any decision taken in accordance with paragraph 1 to the Commission.
## 3.
ESMA shall publish on its website a list of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* repositories registered in accordance with this Regulation. That list shall be updated within five working days of the adoption of a decision under paragraph 1.
