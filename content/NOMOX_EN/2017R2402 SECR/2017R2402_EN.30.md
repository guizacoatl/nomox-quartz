# Article 30 - Powers of the competent authorities

## 1.
Each Member State shall ensure that the competent authority designated in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.29#1|Article 29(1)]] to (5) has the supervisory, investigatory and sanctioning powers necessary to fulfil its duties under this Regulation.
## 2.
The competent authority shall regularly review the arrangements, processes and mechanisms that *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originators]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsors]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPEs]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lenders]]* have implemented in order to comply with this Regulation.

The review referred to in the first subparagraph shall include:
### (a)
the processes and mechanisms to correctly measure and retain the material net economic interest on an ongoing basis in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.6#1|Article 6(1)]] and the gathering and timely disclosure of all information to be made available in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.7|Article 7]];
### (aa)
for exposures that are not part of an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(25)|NPE Securitisation]]*:
#### (i)
the credit-granting criteria applied to performing exposures in accordance with [[2017R2402_EN.9|Article 9]];
#### (ii)
the sound standards for selection and pricing applied to underlying exposures that are *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(24)|non-performing exposures]]* as referred to in the second subparagraph of [[2017R2402_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]];
### (b)
for *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* which are not *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* within an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*, the processes and mechanisms to ensure compliance with [[2017R2402_EN.20#7|Article 20(7)]] to (12), [[2017R2402_EN.21#7|Article 21(7)]], and [[2017R2402_EN.22|Article 22]];; and
### (c)
for *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* which are *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* within an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]*, the processes and mechanisms to ensure, with regard to *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|ABCP transactions]]*, compliance with [[2017R2402_EN.24|Article 24]] and, with regard to *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programmes]]*, compliance with [[2017R2402_EN.26#7|Article 26(7)]] and (8);
### (d)
for *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(25)|NPE securitisations]]*, the processes and mechanisms to ensure compliance with [[2017R2402_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]] preventing any abuse of the derogation provided for in the second subparagraph of [[2017R2402_EN.9#1|Article 9(1)]]; and
### (e)
for *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* on-balance-sheet *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]*, the processes and mechanisms to ensure compliance with Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26b|26b]] to 26e.
## 3.
Competent authorities shall require that risks arising from *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* transactions, including reputational risks, are evaluated and addressed through appropriate policies and procedures of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originators]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsors]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPEs]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lenders]]*.
## 4.
The competent authority shall monitor, as applicable, the specific effects that the participation in the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* market has on the stability of the financial institution that operates as *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investor]]* as part of its prudential supervision in the field of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]*, taking into account, without prejudice to stricter sectoral regulation:
### (a)
the size of capital buffers;
### (b)
the size of the liquidity buffers; and
### (c)
the liquidity risk for *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]* due to a maturity mismatch between their funding and investments.

In cases where the competent authority identifies a material risk to financial stability of a financial institution or the financial system as a whole, irrespective of its obligations under [[2017R2402_EN.36|Article 36]], it shall take action to mitigate those risks, report its findings to the designated authority competent for macroprudential instruments under Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 and the ESRB.
## 5.
The competent authority shall monitor any possible circumvention of the obligations set out in [[2017R2402_EN.6#2|Article 6(2)]] and ensure that sanctions are applied in accordance with Articles [[2017R2402_EN.32|32]] and [[2017R2402_EN.33|33]].
