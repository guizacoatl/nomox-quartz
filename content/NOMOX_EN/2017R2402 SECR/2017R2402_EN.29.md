# Article 29 - Designation of competent authorities

## 1.
Compliance with the obligations set out in [[2017R2402_EN.5|Article 5]] of this Regulation shall be supervised by the following competent authorities in accordance with the powers granted by the relevant legal acts:
### (a)
for insurance and reinsurance undertakings, the competent authority designated in accordance with point (10) of Article 13 of Directive 2009/138/EC;
### (b)
for alternative investment fund managers, the competent authority responsible designated in accordance with [[2011L0061_EN.44|Article 44 of Directive 2011/61/EU]];
### (c)
for UCITS and UCITS management companies, the competent authority designated in accordance with Article 97 of Directive 2009/65/EC;
### (d)
for institutions for occupational retirement provision, the competent authority designated in accordance with point (g) of Article 6 of Directive 2003/41/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council;
### (e)
for credit institutions or investments firms, the competent authority designated in accordance with [[2013L0036_EN.4|Article 4 of Directive 2013/36/EU]], including the ECB with regard to specific tasks conferred on it by Regulation (EU) No 1024/2013.
## 2.
Competent authorities responsible for the supervision of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsors]]* in accordance with [[2013L0036_EN.4|Article 4 of Directive 2013/36/EU]], including the ECB with regard to specific tasks conferred on it by Regulation (EU) No 1024/2013, shall supervise compliance by *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsors]]* with the obligations set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.6|6]], [[2017R2402_EN.7|7]], [[2017R2402_EN.8|8]] and [[2017R2402_EN.9|9]] of this Regulation.
## 3.
Where *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originators]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lenders]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPEs]]* are supervised entities in accordance with Directives 2003/41/EC, 2009/138/EC, 2009/65/EC, 2011/61/EU and 2013/36/EU and Regulation (EU) No 1024/2013, the relevant competent authorities designated according to those acts, including the ECB with regard to specific tasks conferred on it by Regulation (EU) No 1024/2013, shall supervise compliance with the obligations set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.6|6]], [[2017R2402_EN.7|7]], [[2017R2402_EN.8|8]] and [[2017R2402_EN.9|9]] of this Regulation.
## 4.
For *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originators]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lenders]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPEs]]* established in the Union and not covered by the Union legislative acts referred to in paragraph 3, Member States shall designate one or more competent authorities to supervise compliance with the obligations set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.6|6]], [[2017R2402_EN.7|7]], [[2017R2402_EN.8|8]] and [[2017R2402_EN.9|9]]. Member States shall inform the Commission and ESMA of the designation of competent authorities pursuant to this paragraph by 1 January 2019. That obligation shall not apply with regard to those entities that are merely selling exposures under an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* or another *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* transaction or scheme and are not actively originating exposures for the primary purpose of securitising them on a regular basis.
## 5.
Member States shall designate one or more competent authorities to supervise the compliance of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originators]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsors]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPEs]]* with Articles [[2017R2402_EN.18|18]] to 27, and the compliance of third parties with [[2017R2402_EN.28|Article 28]].

Member States shall inform the Commission and ESMA of the designation of competent authorities pursuant to this paragraph by 10 October 2021. Until the designation of a competent authority to supervise the compliance with the requirements set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26a|26a]] to 26e, the competent authority designated to supervise the compliance with the requirements set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.18|18]] to 27 applicable at 8 April 2021 shall also supervise the compliance with the requirements set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26a|26a]] to 26e.
## 6.
Paragraph 5 of this Article shall not apply with regard to those entities that are merely selling exposures under an *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* or other *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* transaction or scheme and are not actively originating exposures for the primary purpose of securitising them on a regular basis. In such a case, the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* or *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* shall verify that those entities fulfil the relevant obligations set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.18|18]] to 27.
## 7.
ESMA shall ensure the consistent application and enforcement of the obligations set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.18|18]] to 27 of this Regulation in accordance with the tasks and powers set out in Regulation (EU) No 1095/2010. ESMA shall monitor the Union *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* market in accordance with [[2014R0600_EN.39|Article 39 of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]] of the European Parliament and the Council and apply, where appropriate, its temporary intervention powers in accordance with [[2014R0600_EN.40|Article 40 of Regulation (EU) No 600/2014]].
## 8.
ESMA shall publish and keep up-to-date on its website a list of the competent authorities referred to in this Article.
