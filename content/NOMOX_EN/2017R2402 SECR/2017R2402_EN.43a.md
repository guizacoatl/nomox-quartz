# Article 43a - Transitional provisions for STS on-balance-sheet securitisations

## 1.
In respect of *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(10)|synthetic securitisations]]* for which the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(26)|credit protection agreement]]* has become effective before 9 April 2021, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originators]]* and *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPEs]]* may use the designation ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]*’ or ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|simple, transparent and standardised]]*’, or a designation that refers directly or indirectly to those terms, only where the requirements set out in [[2017R2402_EN.18|Article 18]] and the conditions set out in paragraph 3 of this Article are complied with at the time of the notification referred to in [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]].
## 2.
Until the date of application of the regulatory technical standards referred to in [[2017R2402_EN.27#6|Article 27(6)]], *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originators]]* shall, for the purposes of the obligation set out in [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]], make the necessary information available to ESMA in writing.
## 3.
*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|Securitisations]]* the initial *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation positions]]* of which were created before 9 April 2021 shall be considered to be *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* provided that:
### (a)
they met, at the time of the creation of the initial *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(19)|securitisation positions]]*, the requirements set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26b|26b]](1) to (5), (7) to (9) and (11) and (12), Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26c|26c]](1) and (3), [[2017R2402_EN.26e#1|Article 26e(1)]], the first subparagraph of [[2017R2402_EN.26e#2|Article 26e(2)]], the third and fourth subparagraph of [[2017R2402_EN.26e#3|Article 26e(3)]], and Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26e|26e]](6) to (9); and
### (b)
they meet, as of the time of notification pursuant to [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]], the requirements set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26b|26b]](6) and (10), Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26c|26c]](2) and (4) to (10), Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26d|26d]](1) to (5) and the second to seventh subparagraph of [[2017R2402_EN.26e#2|Article 26e(2)]], the first, second and fifth subparagraph of Article 26e(3) and Articles [[2017R2402_EN.26e|26e]](4) and (5).
## 4.
For the purposes of point (b) of paragraph 3 of this Article, the following shall apply:
### (a)
in [[2017R2402_EN.26d#2|Article 26d(2)]], ‘prior to the closing of the transaction’ shall be deemed to read ‘prior to notification under [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]]’;
### (b)
in [[2017R2402_EN.26d#3|Article 26d(3)]], ‘before the pricing of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]*’ shall be deemed to read ‘prior to notification under [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]]’;
### (c)
in [[2017R2402_EN.26d#5|Article 26d(5)]]:
#### (i)
in the second sentence, ‘before pricing’ shall be deemed to read ‘prior to notification under [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]]’;
#### (ii)
in the third sentence, ‘before pricing at least in draft or initial form’ shall be deemed to read ‘prior to notification under [[2017R2402_EN.27#1|Article 27(1)]]’;
#### (iii)
the requirement set out in the fourth sentence shall not apply;
#### (iv)
references to compliance with [[2017R2402_EN.7|Article 7]] shall be construed as if [[2017R2402_EN.7|Article 7]] applied to those *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisations]]* notwithstanding [[2017R2402_EN.43#1|Article 43(1)]].
