# Article 23 - Simple, transparent and standardised ABCP securitisation

## 1.
An *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(8)|ABCP transaction]]* shall be considered *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* where it complies with the transaction-level requirements provided for in [[2017R2402_EN.24|Article 24]].
## 2.
An *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* shall be considered *[[2017R2402_EN.2#1.|STS]]* where it complies with the requirements provided for in [[2017R2402_EN.26|Article 26]] and the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(5)|sponsor]]* of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(7)|ABCP programme]]* complies with the requirements provided for in [[2017R2402_EN.25|Article 25]].

For the purpose of this Section, a ‘seller’ means ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*’ or ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(20)|original lender]]*’.
## 3.
By 18 October 2018, the EBA, in close cooperation with ESMA and EIOPA, shall adopt, in accordance with [[2010R1093_EN.16|Article 16 of Regulation (EU) No 1093/2010]], guidelines and recommendations on the harmonised interpretation and application of the requirements set out in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.24|24]] and [[2017R2402_EN.26|26]] of this Regulation.
