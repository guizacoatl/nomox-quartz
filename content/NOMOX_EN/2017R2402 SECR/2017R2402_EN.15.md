# Article 15 - Withdrawal of registration

## 1.
Without prejudice to [[2012R0648_EN.73|Article 73 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012]], ESMA shall withdraw the registration of a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(23)|securitisation repository]]* where the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(23)|securitisation repository]]*:
### (a)
expressly renounces the registration or has provided no services for the preceding six months;
### (b)
obtained the registration by making false statements or by other irregular means; or
### (c)
no longer meets the conditions under which it was registered.
## 2.
ESMA shall, without undue delay, notify the relevant competent authority referred to in Article 11(1) of a decision to withdraw the registration of a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(23)|securitisation repository]]*.
## 3.
The competent authority of a Member State in which a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(23)|securitisation repository]]* performs its services and activities and which considers that one of the conditions referred to in paragraph 1 has been met, may request ESMA to examine whether the conditions for the withdrawal of registration of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(23)|securitisation repository]]* concerned are met. Where ESMA decides not to withdraw the registration of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(23)|securitisation repository]]* concerned, it shall provide detailed reasons for its decision.
## 4.
The competent authority referred to in paragraph 3 of this Article shall be the authority designated under [[2017R2402_EN.29|Article 29]] of this Regulation.
