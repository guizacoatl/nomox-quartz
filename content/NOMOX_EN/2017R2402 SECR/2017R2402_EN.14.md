# Article 14 - Powers of ESMA

## 1.
The powers conferred on ESMA in accordance with Articles [[2017R2402_EN.61|61]] to 68, 73 and 74 of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012, in conjunction with Annexes I and II thereto, shall also be exercised with respect to this Regulation. References to [[2017R2402_EN.81#1|Article 81(1)]] and (2) of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 in Annex I to that Regulation shall be construed as references to [[2017R2402_EN.17#1|Article 17(1)]] of this Regulation.
## 2.
The powers conferred on ESMA or on any official of or other person authorised by ESMA in accordance with Articles [[2012R0648_EN.61|61]] to [[2012R648_EN.63|63]] of Regulation (EU) No 648/2012 shall not be used to require the disclosure of information or documents which are subject to legal privilege.
