# Article 38 - Amendment to Directive 2009/65/EC


Article 50a of Directive 2009/65/EC is replaced by the following:

‘[[2017R2402_EN.50a|Article 50a]]
Where UCITS management companies or internally managed UCITS are exposed to a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* that no longer meets the requirements provided for in the Regulation (EU) 2017/2402 of the European Parliament and of the Council, they shall, in the best interest of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]* in the relevant UCITS, act and take corrective action, if appropriate.
