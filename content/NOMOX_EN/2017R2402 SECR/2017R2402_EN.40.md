# Article 40 - Amendment to Regulation (EC) No 1060/2009


Regulation (EC) No 1060/2009 is amended as follows:
## (1)
in recitals 22 and 41, in [[2017R2402_EN.8c|Article 8c]] and in point 1 of Part II of Section D of Annex I, ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|structured finance instrument]]*’ is replaced by ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation instrument]]*’;
## (2)
in recitals 34 and 40, in Articles [[2017R2402_EN.8|8]](4), 8c, 10(3) and 39(4) as well as in the fifth paragraph of point 2 of Section A of Annex I, point 5 of Section B of Annex I, the title and point 2 of Part II of Section D of Annex I, points 8, 24 and 45 of Part I of Annex III and point 8 of Part III of Annex III, ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|structured finance instruments]]*’ is replaced by ‘*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|securitisation instruments]]*’;
## (3)
in [[2017R2402_EN.1|Article 1]], the second subparagraph is replaced by the following:

‘This Regulation also lays down obligations for issuers and related third parties established in the Union regarding *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|securitisation instruments]]*.’
## (4)
in [[2017R2402_EN.3#1|Article 3(1)]], point (l) is replaced by the following:
“*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation instrument]]*” means a financial instrument or other assets resulting from a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* transaction or scheme referred to in [[2017R2402_EN.2#1|Article 2(1) of Regulation (EU) 2402/2017]] (*[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|Securitisation]]* Regulation);’
## (5)
[[2017R2402_EN.8b|Article 8b]] is deleted;
## (6)
in point (b) of [[2017R2402_EN.4#3|Article 4(3)]], point (b) of the second subparagraph of Article 5(6) and [[2017R2402_EN.25a|Article 25a]], the reference to [[2017R2402_EN.8b|Article 8b]] is deleted.
