# Article 26d - Requirements relating to transparency

## 1.
The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* shall make available data on static and dynamic historical default and loss performance such as delinquency and default data, for substantially similar exposures to those being securitised, and the sources of those data and the basis for claiming similarity, to potential *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]* before pricing. Those data shall cover a period of at least five years.
## 2.
A sample of the underlying exposures shall be subject to external verification prior to the closing of the transaction by an appropriate and independent party, including verification that the underlying exposures are eligible for credit protection under the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(26)|credit protection agreement]]*.
## 3.
The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* shall, before the pricing of the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]*, make available to potential *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]* a liability cash flow model which precisely represents the contractual relationship between the underlying exposures and the payments flowing between the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]*, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]*, other third parties and, where applicable, the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(2)|SSPE]]*, and shall, after pricing, make that model available to *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]* on an ongoing basis and to potential *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]* upon request.
## 4.
In the case of a *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(1)|securitisation]]* where the underlying exposures are residential loans or auto loans or leases, the *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* shall publish the available information related to the environmental performance of the assets financed by such residential loans, auto loans or leases, as part of the information disclosed pursuant to point (a) of the first subparagraph of [[2017R2402_EN.7#1|Article 7(1)]].

By way of derogation from the first subparagraph, *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originators]]* may, from 1 June 2021, decide to publish the available information related to the principal adverse impacts of the assets financed by the underlying exposures on *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(30)|sustainability factors]]*.
## 5.
The *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(3)|originator]]* shall be responsible for compliance with [[2017R2402_EN.7|Article 7]]. The information required by point (a) of the first subparagraph of [[2017R2402_EN.7#1|Article 7(1)]] shall be made available to potential *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]* before pricing upon request. The information required by points (b) to (d) of the first subparagraph of [[2017R2402_EN.7#1|Article 7(1)]] shall be made available before pricing, at least in draft or initial form. The final documentation shall be made available to *[[2017R2402_EN.2#(11)|investors]]* at the latest 15 days after the closing of the transaction.
## 6.
By 10 July 2021, the ESAs shall develop, through the Joint Committee of the European Supervisory Authorities, draft regulatory technical standards in accordance with Articles [[2017R2402_EN.10|10]] to 14 of Regulations (EU) No 1093/2010, (EU) No 1094/2010 and (EU) No 1095/2010 on the content, methodologies and presentation of information referred to in the second subparagraph of paragraph 4 of this Article, in respect of the sustainability indicators in relation to adverse impacts on the climate and other environmental, social and governance-related adverse impacts.

Where relevant, the draft regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph of this paragraph shall mirror or draw upon the regulatory technical standards developed in compliance with the mandate given to the ESAs in Regulation (EU) 2019/2088, in particular in [[2017R2402_EN.2a|Article 2a]], and [[2017R2402_EN.4#6|Article 4(6)]] and (7) thereof.

The Commission is empowered to supplement this Regulation by adopting the regulatory technical standards referred to in the first subparagraph in accordance with Articles [[2017R2402_EN.10|10]] to 14 of Regulations (EU) No 1093/2010, (EU) No 1094/2010 and (EU) No 1095/2010.
