## Question

**Submission Date:** 27-05-2024

Should the price field at position level be amended following a change in the
notional amount?
 

24-05-2024

 Original language

No, counterparties should not update the price field at position level after it is
reported initially (i.e., with action type ‘new’). The price field at position level should
reflect the price of the position when it is first reported.

In particular, in cases where the notional of the position varies (increase/decrease)
overtime due to factors such as, the inclusion of new trades into the position or the
termination of trades included in that position, the price field should not be updated.

## ESMA Answer

**Answer Date:** 24-05-2024

No, counterparties should not update the price field at position level after it is
reported initially (i.e., with action type ‘new’). The price field at position level should
reflect the price of the position when it is first reported.

In particular, in cases where the notional of the position varies (increase/decrease)
overtime due to factors such as, the inclusion of new trades into the position or the
termination of trades included in that position, the price field should not be updated.
